THE SEED IS THE WORD

visits as of today, 11 11, 11

We're just a few soul winners visiting street kids with healthy treats and the Gospel.

Some may think these street kids are surely hopeless but they're not! Not yet.

Six out of ten don't live with their mom but with some neighbor.

Eight out of ten don't even know their dad's name.

They roam the streets all day surrounded by older ones who teach them the wrong survival skills.

During the last ten years visiting street kids, I discovered how urgent it is to empower them before their childhood is gone.

Together, while they're still longing for righteousness we can sow the seed. As Jesus said, the seed is The Word. And some seed will fall on good ground.
