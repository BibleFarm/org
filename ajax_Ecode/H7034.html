H7034. qalah

II. [קָלָה‎] verb Niph`al belightly esteemed, dishonoured (|| form of קלל‎; compare Late Hebrew קְלנָא ᵑ7 קָלוֺן,‎ disgrace, shame); — Perfect3masculine singular consecutive וְנִקְלָה‎ Isa 16:14; Deut 25:3; Participle נִקְלֶה‎ Isa 3:5 2t.; — be lightly esteemed, held of little account, 16:14 (glory of Moab); וְנִקְלֶה רָשׁ אִישׁ‎ 1Sam 18:23; so (Participle as substantive) Isa 3:5 (opposed to נִכְבָּד‎), Prov 12:9 (opposed to מִתְכַּבֵּד‎); more positively, be dishonoured, degraded, Deut 25:3 (עֵינֶיךָ‎).

Hiph`il treat with contempt, dishonour, Participle וגו אָבִיו ׳מַקְלֶה‎ Deut 27:16 one dis-honouring [i.e. opposed to כַּבֵּד‎ of 5th Command].
