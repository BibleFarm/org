H7990. shallit

שַׁלִּיט‎ adjective having mastery, ruling; — absolute ׳שׁ‎ Ezra 7:24 +, emphatic אָ֯‎ Dan 2:15; masculine plural שְׁלִיטִין‎ Ezra 4:20, מִן‎- Dan 4:23; —

1 having, exercising, mastery:

a. of God, followed by ב‎, Dan 4:14; 4:22; 4:29; 5:21; absolute, of heavens 4:23

b. of kings Ezra 4:20 (ב‎).

2 as noun = ruler Dan 2:10, captain 2:15.

3 having authority to do a thing (Infinitive), hence impersonal = it is authorized, Ezra 7:24 (so often in Syriac; compare S-CA 11 + למבנה שליט אנת‎).
