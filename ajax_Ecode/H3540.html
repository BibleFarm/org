H3540. Kedorlaomer

כְּדָרְלָעֹמֶר‎ proper name, masculine king of Elam (= Babylon. Kudar-Laga[mar] (Lagamaru = proper name, of divinity), see PinchesVict. Inst. Jan. 20, 1896 SayPSBA, June 1896, 176 ScheilRev. Bibl. 1896, 600 f., compare SchrCOT) — Gen 14:1, 4, 5, 9, 17.
