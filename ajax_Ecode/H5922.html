H5922. al

עַל‎ preposition upon, over, on account of, above, to, against (Biblical Hebrew and General Aramaic id.); — suffix 1 singular עֲלַי‎; 2 masculine singular עליך‎ Kt (so Egyptian Aramaic, CookeNo. 77, A 5, 6, B 8, S-CPap. A 3, B 5 +), עֲלָךְ‎, Qr (עֲלַךְ ᵑ7‎, Dalm§ 47, 2) Dan 3:12 6t. Daniel; Ezra 7:18; 3masculine singular עֲלוֺהִי‎ (so Nabataean, Palmyrene; Egyptian Aramaic עלוי‎ S-CA 6 +) Dan 3:28 +, עֲלֹהִי‎ Ezra 6:11; 3feminine singular עליה‎ Kt (so Egyptian Aramaic, S-CD 24), עֲלַהּ‎ Qr (so ᵑ7‎) Dan 4:14; 5:21; 1plural עֲלֵינָא‎ Ezra 4:12, עֲלֵ֑ינָא‎ 4:18; 5:17 Baer (Gi עֲלֶ֑ינָא‎); 3 masculine plural עֲלֵיהֹם‎ (so Nabataean, CISii. 226; Egyptian Aramaic, S-CPap. F 5, 8+) 7:24, עֲלֵיהוֺן‎ 5:1, 3; —

1. a. upon, Dan 2:10, 28, 46; 5:5, 7; 6:11 + often, 4:26 מַלְכוּתָא עַלהֵֿיכַל‎ (2Sam 11:2); to be established מַלְכוּתִי עַל‎ 2 Samuel 4:33 (compare 2Chr 1:1; Biblical Hebrew II. 1) to lay an impost upon, Ezra 7:24; to trust on, Dan 3:28; after a verb of motion, עַל מְטָא‎ to come upon, 4:21; 4:25; of times, to pass over, 4:13; 4:20; 4:22; 4:29; Ezra 5:1 עֲלֵיהוֺן יִשְׂרָאֵל אֱלָהּ בְּשֻׁם‎ i.e. (named) over them.

b. idiomatic (as in Biblical Hebrew; see

II. 1 d) of the person who is the subject of an emotion or experience, Dan 5:9 עֲלוֺהִי שָׁנַיִן וְזִיוֺתִי‎ were changed upon him, 7:28; 6:19 עֲלוֺהִי נַדַּת וְשִׁנְּתֵהּ‎ (compare 2:1).

c. on accusative of (Biblical Hebrew 1f b), Dan 3:16 עַלדְּֿנָה מִּתְגָּם‎. So in עַלדְּֿנָה‎ on this accusative Ezra 4:15, 22; 5:17, עַלמָֿה‎ wherefore ? Dan 2:15; and דִּי דִּבְרַת עַל‎ (see ׳דְּ‎).

d. on behalf of (ib.

1f c), Ezra 6:17.

e. regarding, concerning (ib.

1f g), Dan 2:18; 5:14, 29; 6:6; 6:13; Ezra 7:14 +?

2 over, with verbs of ruling, appointing, etc., Dan 2:48-49, 3:12; 4:14 +, Ezra 4:20; 6:18.

3 in a comparative sense, above, beyond, Dan 3:19; 6:4.

4 expressing direction:

a. to, of a person, after a verb of motion (compare Biblical Hebrew

7c a a), Dan 2:24; 4:31; 4:33; 6:7; 6:16; Ezra 4:12, 23 + often; after to send (a letter, etc.) 4:11, 17, 18; 5:6 +, write 4:7, prophesy 5:1; of the direction of the mind (ib.

7c c), Dan 3:12; 6:14 (twice in verse); 6:15; compare Ezra 5:5 אֱל ׳וְעֵין עַל הֲוַת‎ (compare Jer 40:4 and אֶל‎ Psa 34:16).

b. against, Dan 3:19, 29; 5:23; 6:5; 6:6 +; Ezra 4:8; 7:23.

5 in such phrases as טְאֵב עַל, שְׁפַר‎ (to be) good or acceptable to (as in Syriac and late Hebrew: see Biblical Hebrew 8), Ezra 5:17 טָב מַלְכָּא עַל אִם‎, 7:18 (twice in verse); Dan 4:24 (יִשְׁמַּר‎), 6:15 (בְּאֵשׁ‎), 6:24 (טְאֵב‎). — compare the synopsis of meanings in Lzb340.
