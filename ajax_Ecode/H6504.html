H6504. parad

I. [מָּרַד‎] verb divide (Late Hebrew id., divide, separate; so Syriac in derived species and derivatives; Arabic be single, sole, also (Dozy) unfold, unroll): —

Qal Passive participle feminine plural מְּרֻדוֺת‎ Ezek 1:11 divided, i.e. spread, of wings.

Niph`al Perfect3plural נִפְרְדוּ‎ Gen 10:5, 32, נִפְרָ֑דוּ‎ 2Sam 1:23; Imperfect, יִמָּרֵד‎ Gen 2:10; Prov 19:4, etc.; Imperative masculine singular נָא הִמָּ֫רֶד‎ Gen 13:9; Infinitive construct לוֺט הִמָּֽרֶדֿ‎ 13:14; Participle נִפְרָד‎ Judg 4:11; Prov 18:1, נִמְּרָדִים‎ Neh 4:13; —

1 divide, separate (intransitive), Gen 2:10 (of river, dividing into branches); of one man separating from another, with מֵעַל‎ 13:9, 11, מֵעִם‎ 13:14 (all J), from others, מִן‎ Judg 4:11; reciprocal Gen 25:23 (J; of sons of Rebekah representing nations, with מִמֵּעַיִךְ‎ in construction praegn. [Ges§ 119ff], i.e. divided (hostile) from birth); absolute Prov 18:1; of peoples separating from (מִן‎) parent stock 10:5, 32 (P).

2 be divided, separated, 2Sam 1:23; Neh 4:13; of loss of friendship Prov 19:4.

Pi`el Imperfect3masculine singular יְפָרֵ֑דוּ‎ Hosea 4:14 make a separation, i.e. go apart (in company with [עִם‎] harlots, compare RSSemitic i. 436, 2nd ed. 455).

Pu`al Participle divided, הָעַמִּים בֵּין וּמְפֹרָד עַםמְ֗֗֗פֻזָּר‎ Est 3:8

Hiph`il Perfect3masculine singular הִפְרִיד‎ Gen 30:40; Imperfect יַפְרִיד‎ Ruth 1:17; Prov 18:18, 3masculine plural וַיַּפְרִדוּ‎ 2Kin 2:11; Infinitive construct suffix בְּהַפְרִידוֺ‎ Deut 32:8; Participle מַפְרִיד‎ Prov 16:28; 17:9; —

1 divide, separate Gen 30:40 (JE) Deut 32:8, of separating friends Prov 16:28; 17:9.

2 make a division, separation, between (בֵּין‎), 2Kin 2:11; Ruth 1:17; of parting disputants (with בֵּין‎) Prov 18:18.

Hithpa`el Perfect3plural וְהִתְמָּֽרְדוּ‎ Psa 22:15; Imperfect יִתְמָּֽרְדוּ‎ 92:10, רָ֑דוּ‎- Job 4:11; 41:9; — be divided, separated, from each other; of scales of crocodile (c. negative) 41:9, of bones, = be loosened at the joint, Psa 22:15 (figurative of helplessness); = be dispersed Job 4:11; Psa 92:10.
