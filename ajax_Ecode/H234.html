H234. azkarah

אַזְכָּרָה‎ noun feminine memorial-offering, only P (an Aramaic infinitive form; compare BaNB 90 Sta§ 224) — absolute ׳א‎ Lev 24:7; suffix אַזְכָּֽרָתָהּ‎ 2:2 5t.; — used of the frankincense burned for the shew-bread לאז ׳ללחם לי ׳אִשֶּׁה והיתה‎ 24:7; elsewhere always after הקטיר‎, only with suffix, referring to מִנְחָה‎ 2:2, 9, 16; 6:8; Num 5:26; of the meal used as חַטָּאת‎ by the very poor Lev 5:12; always connected with אִשֶּׁה‎, except 6:8; Num 5:26.
