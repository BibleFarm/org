H4324. Mikal

מִיכַל‎ proper name, feminine (according to Ol§ 277 f., contracted from מִיכָאֵל‎): — younger daughter of Saul, wife of David (ᵐ5‎ Μελχολ), 1Sam 14:49; 18:20, 27, 28; 19:11-12, 13, 17 (twice in verse); 25:44; 2Sam 3:13-14, 6:16 = 1Chr 15:29; 2Sam 6:20-21, 23; also 21:8 (ᵐ5‎ Μιχολ), but error for מֵרַב‎ (ᵐ5‎L Μεροβ).
