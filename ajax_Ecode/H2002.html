הַמְנִיכָא‎ Qr (Kt המינכָא‎, also [Gi] המוניכא‎, etc.)

noun [masculine] chain, necklace (Talmud הַמְנִכָּא‎ DWB 108; Syriac ; loan-word from Greek μανιάκης K§ 64, 4; or < Persian hämy¹n, girdle [diminutive would be hamy¹nak] (whence also Greek μαν., Bev DrDan 5:7) AndrM 60* (reading הַמְיָֽנְכָא‎); see also Behrmix LagGes. Abh. 40; Arm. Stud. 1420; see Krauss5f. (who compares also Talmud מָנְיָיק מוּנְיָיק,‎ necklace, bracelet, LevyNHWB iii. 52 b, 158 b D230)); — of gold, 5:7, 16, 29.
