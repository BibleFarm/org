H4586. Meunim

or Meinim מְעוּנִים‎ proper name, of a people Me`unim, Me`unites (usually connected with מָעוֺן‎, (see עון‎), but dubious, compare BuhlEdom. 41 f.); — a people South (SE ?) of Canaan (compare Buhll.c.); — ׳הַמּ‎2Chr 26:7 and 1Chr 4:41 Qr (Kt ᵐ5 המעינים,‎B τοὺς Μιναίους, ᵐ5‎L τ. Κιναίους; read also מֵהַמְּעוּנִים‎2Chr 20:1 (for מֵהָעַמּוֺנִים ᵑ0‎), so ᵐ5‎B A (ᵐ5‎L Αμμανιειμ), Ew Be Ke Ot Kau Kit; בְּנֵימְֿעוּנִים‎ (among Nethinim who went back with Zerubbabel) Ezra 2:50 = Neh 7:52 are perhaps descendants of those named above (2Chr 26:7). ᵐ5‎ οἱ Μιναῖοι, but against ׳מ‎ = Mineans (GlaserSkizze ii. 450 f. and others) see SprengerZMG xliv (1890), 505.
