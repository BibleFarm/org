H7793. Shur

III. שׁוּר‎ proper name, of a location southwest of Palestine, on east border of Egypt; — Gen 16:7 (J; שׁ ׳דֶּרֶךְ‎), 20:1 (E), towards Egypt 25:18 (J), 1Sam 15:7, שׁ֫וּרָה‎ 27:8; ׳מִדְּבַּרשֿׁ‎ Exod 15:22 (J). — Often supposed to denote properly the 'wall' or line of fortresses, built by Egyptian kings across isthmus of Suez; but dubious: compare DrHast.
