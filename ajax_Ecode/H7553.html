H7553. riqmah

רִקְמָה‎ noun feminine variegated stuff (woven or embroidered); — absolute ׳ר‎ Judg 5:30 +; suffix רִקְמָתֵךְ‎ Ezek 16:18, תָם‎- 26:16; dual רִקְמָתַיִם‎ Judg 5:30; plural רְקָמוֺת‎ Psa 45:15; — (piece of) variegated stuff, Judg 5:30 (twice in verse), for garments Ezek 16:10, 13; 27:16, 24; Psa 45:15; explicitly, ר ׳בִּגְדֵי‎ Ezek 16:18; 26:16; ׳ר‎ apparently = variegated work 27:7; figurative of variegated plumage 17:3; of variegated stone 1Chr 29:2.
