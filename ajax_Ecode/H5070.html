H5070. Nadab

נָדָב‎ proper name, masculine (generous, noble) —

1 eldest son of Aaron Exod 6:23; 24:1, 9; 28:1; Lev 10:1; Num 3:2, 4; 26:60-61, 1 Chronicles 5:29; 1Chr 24:1. ᵐ5‎ Ναδαβ (so 3, 4).

2 son of Jeroboam I 1Kin 14:20; 15:25, 27, 31. ᵐ5‎ Ναβατ, Ναβαθ, ᵐ5‎L Ναδαβ.

3 a Jerahmeelite 1Chr 2:28, 30.

4 a Gibeonite 1Chr 8:30; 9:36.
