H5947. alliz

[עַלִּיז‎] adjective exultant, jubilant (on form compare LagBN 110); — feminine singular עַלִּיזָה‎ jubilant city Isa 22:2 (|| הֹמִיָּה עִיר‎), 32:13, compare Zeph 2:15 and (city omitted) Isa 23:7; of person, plural עַלִּיזִים שְׁאוֺן‎ 24:8; construct גַּאֲוָתִי עַלִּיזֵי‎ 13:3, compare Zeph 3:11, see גַּאֲוָה‎.
