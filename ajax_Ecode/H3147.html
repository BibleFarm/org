H3147. Yotham

יוֺתָם‎ proper name, masculine (׳י‎ is perfect) —

1 king of Judah, son Azarish (ᵐ5‎ Ἰωαθαμ, Ἰωαθαν, Ἰωναθαν) 2Kin 15:5, 7, 30, 32, 36, 38; 16:1; 1Chr 3:12; 5:17; 2Chr 26:21, 23; 27:1, 6, 7, 9; Isa 1:1; 7:1; Hosea 1:1; Micah 1:1.

2 youngest son of Jerubbaal (Giddeon) Judg 9:5, 7, 21, 57 (ᵐ5‎ ut above; Manuscripts Ἰωθαμ, Ἰωαθαμ).

3 a descendent of Caleb 1Chr 2:47 (ᵐ5‎ Ἰωαθαμ).

יֵשׁוּעַ‎ see יְהוֺשׁוּעַ‎.
