H2267. cheber

חֶ֫בֶר‎ noun [masculine] company, association, spell — absolute חָ֑בֶר‎ Deut 18:11 2t.; construct חֶבֶר‎ Hosea 6:9; plural חֲבָרִים‎ Psa 58:6, suffix חֲבָרַיִךְ‎ Isa 47:9, 11 —

1 company, band of (bad) priests Hosea 6:9.

2 association, society (abstract), only in חָ֑בֶר בֵּית‎ Prov 21:9 = 25:24 house of association, i. e. shared with a contentious woman (house in common, see RVm VB).

3 spell :

a. as accusative of congnate meaning with verb with חָבַר‎ (q. v.) Deut 18:11; Psa 58:6.

b. alone, of Babylonian magic Isa 47:9, 11 (see Len Chald. Magic [1878]; Magie u. Wahrsage-kunst 1878; ScholzG-tzendienst u. Zauberwesen, 1877. 80 ff. SayTBA iii. 145 ff and Rel,. Bab 396 ff., 440 ff.).
