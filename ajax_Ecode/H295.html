H295. Achiezer

אֲחִיעֶ֫זֶר‎ proper name, masculine (my brother is help, compare Assyrian A—ulê°te, my brother is strength, DlPr 202)

1 a chief of Dan Num 1:12; 2:25; 7:66, 71; 10:25.

2 one of David's heroes 1Chr 12:3.
