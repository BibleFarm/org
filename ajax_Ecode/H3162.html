H3162. yachad

יַ֫חַד‎45 noun [masculine] unitedness. 1 as substantive only 1Chr 12:17 (peculiar) לֳי֑חַד לֵבָב עֲלֵיכֶֹם יִהְיֶהלִּֿי‎ I will have a heart toward you for unitedness, i.e. my heart shall be ready to become one with yours (compare v.12:38 אחד לב‎).

2 elsewhere always in accusative as

adverb in union, together (compare Arabic in accusative with suffix in his solitariness = alone) —

a. together, of community in action, place, or time (often combined, but one usually more prominent than the others); (1) in action, Job 38:7 אֵל כּוֺכְבֵי בְּרָןיַֿחַד‎ when the stars of God shouted together; Ezra 4:3 we together will build (opposed to you), with נִלְחַם‎ 1Sam 17:10, נִשְׁמַּט‎ Isa 43:26; (2) in place 1Sam 11:11 יָ֑חַד שׁנים בם נשׁארו ולא‎; 2Sam 10:15 יָחַ֑ד וַיֵּאָֽסְפוּ‎ Psa 2:2; 31:14; 88:18Psalm 1יַחַד שֶׁבֶת‎ Isa 50:8, as predicate Micah 2:12 אֲשִׂימֶנּוּ יַחַד‎; (3) in place and time at once. 2Sam 21:9 יָ֑חַד וַיִּמְּלוּ‎ and they perished together, 14:16 to destroy יחד בני ואת אתי‎; (4) of time alone (poetry) Isa 42:14 יחד וְאֶשְׁאַף אֶשֹּׁם‎ I will gasp and pant together, 45:8; Job 6:2; 17:16 **read נֵחָ֑ת‎ for יַחַ֑ת‎; יַחַד‎ here will then belong to (1).; Psa 141:10 אעבור עד אנכי יחד‎ while I at the same time pass on.

b. all together, altogether (poetic synonym of כֻּלָּם‎ but more forcible, suggesting often, especially with כל‎, all at once, as well as altogether), Isa 44:11 יחד יבשׁו יפחדו‎ (compare Psa 40:15), 62:10 יַחַד מֵהֶבֶל הֵמָּה‎ they are all together (made) of vanity, Isa 22:3 יחד נדדו קציניך כל‎ (with כל‎ also Psa 41:8; Job 34:15): often in poetry beginning a clause with emphasis, Deut 33:5; Job 3:18 שׁאננו אסירים יחד‎ 16:10; 19:12 גדודיו יבאו יחד‎, 24:4; 31:38; Psa 41:8; 98:8; Hosea 11:7 (with a negative), 11:8; in connection with a suffix, or object of a verb, Isa 27:4 יָ֑חַד אציתנה‎ I will burn it altogether, Psa 33:15 לִבָּם יַחַד הַיֹּצֵר‎ who formeth the hearts of them all together, 74:6; 74:8; Job 10:8 סָבִיב יַחַד‎ altogether round about (but read perhaps with ᵑ6 ᵐ5‎ Del Di תִסּוֺב אַחַר‎), 40:13.

c. together, in the sense of alike, the one as well as the other, Psa 49:3; 49:11 יאבדו ובער כסיל יחד‎ Job 21:26; 34:29. More frequent is

יַחְדָּו‎92 (Jer 46:12, 21; 49:3 יַחְדָּיו‎),

adverb together (properly (in) his or its unitednesses (compare Arabic alone; and for the form אַשְׁרָיו‎), but, the origin of the term. being forgotten, applied Generally as an adverb, without regard to number, Gender, or person: so Ges Ol§ 135c Sta§ 370a) — together:

a. of community in action, Psa 34:4 יחדו שׁמו ונרוממה‎ let us exalt his name together, Isa 52:9, with נוֺעַץ‎ take counsel, 45:21; Psa 71:10; 83:6; Neh 6:7; נִצָּה‎ to struggle Deut 25:11; in place, as with ישׁב‎ Gen 13:6; Deut 25:5, הלך‎ Gen 22:6, 8; Amos 3:3, בוא‎ Jer 3:18; Job 9:32, קרב‎ Isa 41:1, נפל‎ 2Sam 2:16, אכל‎ Judg 19:6; Jer 41:1, התקבץ‎ Josh 9:2, נאסף‎ Judg 6:33, etc.; Deut 22:10 יחדו ובחמור בשׁור‎ coupling pairs, and so strengthening וְ‎ (mostly in poetry), 22:11; Amos 1:15 יחדו ושׂריו הוא‎ (compare Jer 48:7 Qr [Kt יחד‎], 49:3) 6:11-12, י ונשׁים ׳שׂדות‎, 6:21 (accents), 13:14; 31:8, 13; 50:4, 33; Isa 41:19, compare 65:7; in time (rare: but see below), Psalm 4:9 in peace ואישׁן אשׁכבה יחדו‎ will I at once lie down and sleep (i.e. lie down and fall asleep immediately).

b) suggesting all at once, as well as all together, Exod 19:8 יחדו העם כל ויענו‎ (so, with כל‎, Isa 22:3; 31:3; Zech 10:4), Isa 1:28, 31 יחדו שׁניהם ובערו‎. Prefixed to a clause (like יחד‎), Deut 33:17 (compare יחד‎ 33:5), Isa 9:20; 11:7, 14; 31:3; 43:17; 45:16; 52:8; Jer 46:12; 51:38; Lam 2:8; Job 24:17 (strengthening לָמוֺ‎)
