H5057. nagid

נָגִיד‎ noun masculine leader (literally probably one in front), ruler, prince; — absolute ׳נ‎ 1Sam 9:16 31t.; construct נְגִיד (נְגִד)‎ 2Kin 20:5-6t.; plural נְגִידִים‎ Job 29:10 3t.; נְגִידֵי‎2Chr 35:8; — ruler, prince Psalm 76:13; Prov 28:16; Job 29:10; 31:37 (in simile). Especially

1 of king of Israel: of Saul לְנ ׳מָשַׁח עַל‎ (+ accusative of person), 1Sam 9:16 (subject prophet), compare of Solomon, subject people, 1Chr 29:22; 10:1 (subject ׳י‎); of David לְנ ׳צִוָּהוּ עַל‎ 1Sam 13:14; 25:30, ׳נ צִוָּהוּ עַל‎ 2Sam 6:21 (in these ׳י‎ subject), לנ ׳היה על‎ 5:2, נ ׳היה על‎ 7:8; 1Chr 11:2; 17:7; compare also 5:2; 2Chr 6:5; of David also נ ׳נָתַן ׳לְ‎ Isa 55:4; of Solomon נ ׳לִהְיוֺת על אֹתוֺ צִוָּה‎ 1Kin 1:35 (׳י‎ subject); of Jeroboam נ ׳נתן על‎ ( + accusative of person) 14:7 (׳י‎ subject); so 16:2 of Baasha; of Hezekiah ׳נְ עַָמִּי‎ 2Kin 20:5; ׳לְנ בְּאֶחָיו הֶעֱמִיד‎ ( + accusative of person) 2Chr 11:22 (of Abijah, subject Rehob.); of Judah 1Chr 28:4.

2 of a foreign ruler or prince Ezek 28:2; Dan 9:25-26,.

3 the title of some high official connected with the temple Jer 20:1 (of Pashhur) י ׳בבית נָגִיד פָקִיד וְהוּא‎ and he was prince-overseer in, etc. (for ׳פ‎ see 29:26); hence ׳נ האלהים בית‎ 1Chr 9:11; 2Chr 31:13; 35:8 (three ב ׳נגידי ׳ה‎, including the high-priest), Neh 11:11; of high-priest Dan 11:22 נ ׳בְּרִית‎.

4 ruler in other capacities (late): of tribe 1Chr 27:16; 2Chr 19:11; of Korahites 9:20; of Aaronite warriors 2 Chron 12:27; of division of army 1Chr 13:1; 27:4; 2Chr 11:11 (commandant of fortress), 32:21 (in Assyrian army: || שַׂר‎); of temple-treasuries 1Chr 26:24, compare 2Chr 31:12; ׳נ הבית‎28:7 (i.e. of palace?).

5 princely thimgs, plural abstract Prov 8:6.
