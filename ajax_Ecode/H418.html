H418. algummim

אֲלְגּוּמִים‎ noun [masculine] plural a tree (foreign & obscure) always with עֲצֵי‎, perhaps sandal-wood: — 2Chr 2:7 וְא בְּרוֺשִׁים אֲרָזִים ׳עֲצֵי‎ (from Lebanon); אַ ׳עֲצֵי‎9:10 (|| יְקָרָה אֶבֶן‎; both from Ophir); compare 9:11, see following
