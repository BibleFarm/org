Strong's: to braid, i.e. intermix; technically, to traffic (as if by barter); also or give to be security (as a kind of exchange)

H6148. arab

II. עָרַב‎ verb take on pledge, give in pledge, exchange (Late Hebrew go surety for (rare); Phoenician ערב‎ surety; Old Aramaic ערבא‎ pledge; עֲרַב ᵑ7‎, Syriac , go surety for; usually identified with I. ערב‎ — mix, exchange, pledge — but quite uncertain; compare BuhlLex LagBN 203); —

Qal Perfect3masculine singular ׳ע‎ Gen 44:32 4t.; 2 masculine singular עָרַבְתָּ‎ Prov 6:1; Imperfect1singular suffix אֶעֶרְבֶנּוּ‎ Gen 43:9; Imperative עֲרֹב‎ Psa 119:122, suffix עָרְבֵנִי‎ Isa 38:14, so Job 17:3 (but see below); Infinitive construct לַעֲרֹב‎ Ezek 27:9; Participle עֹרֵב‎ Prov 17:18, etc.; —

1 with accusative of person taken on pledge, i.e. go surety for the safety of, Gen 43:9 (J), 44:32 (J; + מֵעִם‎ person); for the debts of Prov 11:15; 20:16; 27:13; of God Isa 38:14 go surety for me, Psa 119:122, so Job 17:3 (si vera lectio, see עֵרָבוֺן‎); accusative of person omitted, ׳ע לְ‎ Prov 6:1 go surety to one (in behalf of another, || כַּמֶּ֑יךָ לַזָּר תָּקַע‎); with accusative of thing 22:26 (|| תֹּקְעֵיכָֿ֑ף‎).

2 give in pledge, with accusative of thing Neh 5:3 (i.e. mortagage), figurative ׳ע אֶתלִֿבּוֺ‎ Jer 30:21 so ׳ע רֵעֵהוּ לִפְנֵי עֲרֻבָּה‎ Prov 17:18 (|| כָּ֑ף תֹּקֵעַ‎).

3 exchange (in trade), with accusative of congnate meaning with verb מָעֲרָבֵךְ‎ Ezek 27:9, 27.
