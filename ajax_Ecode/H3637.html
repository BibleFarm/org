2 be put to shame, dishonoured, confounded, by judgments of ׳י‎, all || בושׁ‎: Jer 22:22 (מִן‎ of cause), Ezek 36:32 (id.), Jer 31:19 (כִּי‎ of cause), 8:12; Isa 41:11; 45:16 (+ בַּכְּלִמָּה הָלַךְ‎), Psa 35:4; 69:7; + חפר‎ 40:15; 70:3.
