H3641. Kalneh

כַּלְנֵ֑ה‎ (van d. H, so Norzi; Baer כַּלְנֶ֑ה‎) proper name, of a location in Babylonia, Gen 10:10 (J), ᵐ5‎ Ξαλαννη; DlPa 225 proposes identify. with Babylonian Kul-unu = Zirlab (conquered by Sargon in 710: COT10:10: Amos 6:2), but dubious, and site of Zirlab unknown.

כַּלְנֶה‎ Amos 6:2, probably = כַּלְנוֺ‎ Isa 10:9 proper name, of a location city (conquered by Assyria under Sargon ?) possibly = כַּנֶּה‎ (q. v.) Ezek 27:23, ᵐ5‎ omitted in Amos 6:2, Ξαλαννη Isa 10:9; perhaps = Kullani (WklGeschichte. Babylonian 225) i.e. (TomkinsPSBA Jan. 1883, 61) Kullanhou, near Aleppo, conquered by Tiglath-Pileser III in 738 (COTii, 195); or (Di) Kunulua (KG217 KBi, 107), southeast of Antioch (compare DrAmos 6:2).

כַּלְנֵה / כַּלְנׄו‎
原文音譯：hn kal-neh’; or kal-nay or kal-no’

詞類次數：專有名詞（3）

原文字根：字義溯源：甲尼，迦勒挪；那英勇的獵戶寧錄，他國的起頭乃是在示拿地的巴別，以力，亞甲，甲尼（創10:10）；在（賽10:9）提到迦勒挪的地方，那個編號是H3641（כַּלְנֵה‎ / כַּלְנׄו‎），和甲尼的編號一樣。比較（H3656（כַּנֵּה‎）=干尼）

出現次數：總共（3）；創（1）；賽（1）；摩（1）
