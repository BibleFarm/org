H83. eber

אֵ֫בֶר‎ noun [masculine] pinions (from strength, in poetry & figurative, plural in sense) as of dove Psa 55:7; eagle Isa 40:31; הָא ׳אֶרֶךְ‎ of king of Babylon under figure of eagle Ezek 17:3 (|| הַכְּנָפַיִם גְּדוֺל‎ of broad, overshadowing wings).
