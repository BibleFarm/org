H2491. chalal

I. חָלָל‎90 noun masculine pierced, ׳ח‎ absolute Deut 21:1 25t.; construct חֲלַל‎ Num 19:16; plural חֲלָלִים‎ 23:24 28t.; construct חַלְלֵי‎ Isa 22:2 21t.; suffix חֲלָלֶיךָ‎ Psa 69:27, etc. + 11 t. suffixes; —

1 pierced, fatally wounded Psa 69:27; Jer 51:52; Ezek 26:15; 30:24; Lam 2:12. Elsewhere

2 slain Num 19:18; 31:8, 19 (P), etc.; singular collective 2Sam 23:8, 18 = 1Chr 11:11, 20; Ezek 6:7; 30:11, etc., but usually plural 1Sam 17:52 (RV wounded) Jer 51:49, etc.; חֶרֶב חַלְלֵי‎ Isa 22:2; Jer 14:18; Ezek 31:17-18, 32:20-21, 25, 28, 29, 30, 31, 32; Zeph 2:12; Lam 4:9; רעב חַלְלֵי‎ 4:9; יהוה חַלְלֵי‎ Isa 66:16; Jer 25:33.

II. חָלָל‎ adjective profaned; — רָשָׁע חָלָל‎ (read construct חֲלַל‎ SS) profaned, dishonoured wicked one Ezek 21:30 (so AV Thes MV SS Co Or; but RV H" deadly wounded, Ew Sm fallen, slain); plural construct רְשָׁעִים חַלְלֵי‎ Ezekiel 21:34; feminine singular חֲלָלָה‎ of woman sexually dishonoured Lev 21:7, 14 (H; || זָנָה‎).
