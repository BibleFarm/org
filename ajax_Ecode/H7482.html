H7482. raam

רַ֫עַם‎ noun [masculine] thunder; — ascribed to ׳ר י ׳׃‎ absolute Isa 29:6, רַ֑עַם בְּסֵתֶר‎ Psa 81:8 (Baer Gi; i.e. thunder-cloud); suffix רַעַמְךָ קוֺל‎ 77:19; 104:7; construct גבורתו רַעַם‎ Job 26:14 (figurative of ׳י‎s display of might; opposed to שֵׁמֶץ‎); figurative of captains, ׳ר שָׂרִים‎ 39:25, i.e. thunderous shouting ( + תְּרוּעָה‎).
