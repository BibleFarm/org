H1060. bekor

בְּכוֺר‎122 noun masculine first-born — Gen 35:23 78t.; בְּכֹר‎ 25:13-14t.; suffix בְּכֹּרִי‎ 49:3 14t.; בְּכוֺרוֺ‎ 38:6 3t.; plural construct בְּכוֺרֵי‎ Neh 10:37 2t.; בְּכוֺרֵיהֶם‎ Psa 136:10; plural feminine בְּכֹרוֺת‎ Gen 4:4; Neh 10:37; בְּכֹרֹת‎ Deut 12:6, 17; 14:23; —

1 men and women:

a. individuals Gen 25:13 69t.

b. collective Num 3:46, 50; 8:16; 18:15.

c. plural Neh 10:37; Psa 135:8; 136:10.

d. בכור כל‎ Exod 11:5; 12:12, 29; 13:2, 13, 15 (twice in verse); 22:28; 34:2; Num 3:12 (3 t. in verse); 3:13, 40, 42, 43, 45; 8:17 (twice in verse); 8:18; 33:4; Psa 78:51; 105:36.

2 animals:

a. individuals Lev 27:26; Num 18:17; Deut 15:19 (twice in verse); 33:17.

b. collective בהמה בכור‎ Exod 11:5; 12:29; 13:15; Num 3:41; 18:15; הבכור כל‎ Deut 15:19.

c. plural בכרות‎ Gen 4:4; Deut 12:6, 17; 14:23; Neh 10:37.

3 figurative, noun of relation מות בכור‎ first-born of death Job 18:13 (deadly disease); דַלִּים בְּכוֺרֵי‎ first-born of the poor (the poorest) Isa 14:30; Israel is the first-born of Yahweh among the nations Exod 4:22 compare Jer 31:9; and the seed of David among dynasties Psa 89:28.
