H495. Ellasar

אֶלָּסָר‎ proper name, of a location Ellasar Gen 14:1, 9 (= Babylonian Larsa, modern Senkereh, approximately 28 miles northeast from Ur; compare LoftCS 240 f DlPa 223 f TieleGeschichte i. 86, COTon the passage).
