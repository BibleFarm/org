חֲמֹור‎ (chămôwr｜kham-ore'｜noun｜masculine｜(he)-ass)
[Heb] חֲמֹור‎ ETCBC: חֲמֹור‎ (subs｜he-ass) OSHL: h.dq.ab TWOT: 685a GK: H2789, H2790 Greek: Ἐμμόρ, ἐξαλείφω, ὄνος, ὑποζύγιον
Derivation: or (shortened) חֲמֹר‎; from חָמַר‎;
Strong's: a male ass (from its dun red)
KJV: (he) ass.

H2543. chamor

I. חֲמוֺר‎ noun [masculine] heap, absolute ׳ח‎ with dual חֲמֹרָתָ֑יִם‎ (Baer ׳חְִמֹרֹת‎), as if from חֲמֹרָה‎; — חֲמֹרָתָ֑יִם חֲמוֺר הַחֲמוֺר בִּלְחִי‎ Judg 15:16 with the ass's jawbone, a heap, two heaps, i.e. so many slain.

חֲמֹרֹתָ֑יִם‎ see I. חֲמוֺר‎. above

II. חֲמוֺר‎ noun masculineGen 45:23 (he)-ass (Late Hebrew id. (sometimes female, but this regularly חֲמוֺרָה‎); Aramaic הֲמָרָא‎ Arabic , Palmyrene חמרא‎ ReckendZMG 1888, 404; as imêru, SchrCot Gloss; — name from reddish colour) — absolute חֲמוֺר‎ 22:5 43t.; חֲמֹר‎ Exod 13:13; Deut 22:10; construct חֲמוֺר‎ Exod 23:5 4t.; הֲמֹר‎ Gen 49:14; suffix חֲמֹרְךָ‎ Deut 5:14; 28:31; חֲמֹרֶ֑ךָ‎ Exod 23:12; חֲמֹרוֺ‎ Gen 22:3 8t.; plural חֲמוֺרִים‎ Judg 19:10 9t.; חֲמֹרִים‎ Gen 12:16 16t.; suffix חֲמוֺרֵינוּ‎ Judg 19:19; חֲמֹרֵינוּ‎ Gen 43:18; חֲמוֺרֵיכֶם‎ 1Sam 8:16; חֲמֹרֵיהֶם‎ Gen 34:28 4t.; חֲמוֺרֵיהֶם‎ Josh 9:4; — ass (he-ass; אתון‎ = she-ass) found in all periods (collective only Gen 32:6; Isa 21:7); —

1 as (valuable) property, with oxen, sheep, camels, slaves, etc. Gen 12:16; 24:35; Genesis 30:48; 47:17; Exod 9:3 (all J) 20:17 (E), 21:33; 22:8 (twice in verse); 22:9; 23:4-5, 12; Num 16:15; Josh 6:21; 7:24 (all J E); note also Exod 13:13 = 34:20 (J E; firstlings belong to ׳י‎); Gen 36:24; Num 31:28, 30, 34, 39, 45 (all P); Deut 5:14, 18; 22:3-4, Judg 6:4; 1Sam 8:16; 12:3; 15:3; 22:19; 27:9; 1Chr 5:21; Ezra 2:67 = Neh 7:68 (Baer), Zech 14:15; collective Gen 32:5 (J; || שׁוֺר‎), שִׁפְחָה עֶבֶד, צאֹן,‎, but plural (similar ||) 34:28 (P), 47:17 (J); compare also ׳ח יְתוֺמִים‎ Job 24:3 the orphans' ass (|| אַלְמָנָה שׁוֺר‎); as knowing its master's crib Isa 1:3 (|| שׁוֺר‎); found in camp 2Kin 7:7, 10 (both || סוּסִים‎); as harnessed לַה ׳מֶתֶג‎ Prov 26:3 (|| סוּס‎).

2 used for riding :

a. by women Exod 4:20 (J), Josh 15:18 (JE) = Judg 1:14; 1Sam 25:20, 23, 42, compare 2Chr 28:15.

b. by men 2Sam 16:2; 17:23; 19:27; 1Kin 2:40; 13:13 (twice in verse); 13:23-24, 27, 28 (twice in verse); Isa 21:7; Zech 9:9.

c. חמורים צֶמֶד‎ a pair of asses 2Sam 16:1 (for burdens and for riding); for man and woman Judg 19:3, 10, 19, 21; carrying dead body 19:28, compare ׳ח‎ 1Kin 13:29.

3 beast of burden Gen 22:3, 5 (compare 22:6), 42:26-27, 45:23 (all E) 43:18, 24; 44:3, 13 (all J), Josh 9:4 (JE), 1Sam 25:18; 1 Chronicles 12:41; Neh 13:15(compare also ח ׳צֶמֶד‎ 2Sam 16:1 above); metaphor of Issachar ׳ח נָּ֑רֶם‎ Gen 49:14 (poem), an ass of (strong) bones. — ׳ח לֶחֶם‎ 1Sam 16:20 read probably חֲמִשָּׁה‎ five, or better עֲשָׂרָה‎ ten, see We Dr.

4 used in tillage Isa 32:20; not to be used in ploughing with an ox (שׁוֺר‎) Deut 22:10.

5 parts of body of ass mentioned are: ח ׳לְחִי‎ Judg 15:15-16, jawbone, Samson's weapon; ח ׳ראֹשׁ‎ 2Kin 6:25 eaten in famine; ה ׳בְּשַׂר‎ Ezek 23:20 Genital organ of ass (contemptuous simile)

6 ה ׳קְבוּרַת‎ Jer 22:19 burial of an ass, in figure of ignominious treatment of a corpse.
