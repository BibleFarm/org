H3096. Yahats

יַ֫הְצָה יַ֫הַץ,‎ proper name, of a location in Moab, site unknown (MI19, 20 יהץ‎) — יהץ‎ Isa 15:4; Jer 48:34; with ה‎ locative יָהְ֑צָה‎, on border of territory of Amorites (under Sihon) Num 21:23; Deut 2:32; also בְּיָהְ֑צָה‎ Judg 11:20; called יַהֲצָ֫ה‎ Josh 13:18 (assigned to Reuben); יַ֫הְצָה‎ 1Chr 6:63 (Levitical city in Reuben), Jer 48:21.

יהר‎ (√ of following; compare Late Hebrew יָהַר‎ Hithpa`el shew oneself haughty, adjective יָהִיר‎; Aramaic יְהַר‎ Pa`el be haughty, adjective יְהִירָא‎; compare Arabic be insane).
