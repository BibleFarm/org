H981. bata

or batah [בָּטָא בָּטָה,‎] verb speak rashly, thoughtlessly (Late Hebrew id., בטי‎) —

Qal Participle בּוֺטֶה‎ Prov 12:18 one that babbleth (opposed to חֲכָמִים לְשׁוֺן‎).

Pi`el Imperfect בִשְׁבוּעָה יְבַטֵּא‎ Lev 5:4 speak rashly, unadvisedly; so בִשְׂפָתָיו וַיְבַטֵּא‎ (of Moses) Psa 106:33; Infinitive בִשְׂפָתַיִם לְבַטֵּא‎ Lev 5:4 (P).
