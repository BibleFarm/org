[Heb] לְבֹונָה‎, לְבֹנָה‎ ETCBC: לְבֹנָה‎ (subs｜incense) OSHL: l.ak.ah TWOT: 1074d GK: H4227, H4247 Greek: λίβανος, λιβανωτός

KJV: (frank-) incense.

H3828. lebonah

I. לְבוֺנָה לְבֹנָה,‎ noun feminine frankincense (from white colour, compare LagBN 33; Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic id., לְבוּנְתָּא‎, Phoenician לבנת‎; Arabic (Lane3007); Ethiopic is from λιβανος); — ׳ל‎ Exod 30:24 18t.; לְבֹנָתָהּ‎ Lev 2:2, 16; — used in preparation of holy incense Exod 30:34 (|| סַמִּים‎); used as incense Jer 6:20, on meal-offering Lev 2:1-2, 15, 16; 6:8, compare 5:11; Num 5:15; also Jer 17:26; 41:5; Isa 43:23; 66:3; with shew-bread Lev 24:7; Neh 13:5, 9; 1Chr 9:29 (|| בְּשָׂמִים‎); burned as perfume (article of luxury) Song 3:6 (|| מֹר‎); הַלְּבוֺנָה גִּבְעַת‎ 4:6 (|| הַמּוֺר הַר‎); לְבוֺנָה עֲצֵי‎ 4:14 (|| אֲהָלוֺת מֹר, קִנָּמוֺן, קָנֶה, כַּרְכֹּם, נֵרְדְּ,‎); as tribute to Zion Isa 60:6 (|| זהב‎). — In Hexateuch only P.
