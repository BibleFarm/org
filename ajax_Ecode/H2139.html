H2139. Zakkur

זַכּוּר‎ proper name, masculine ᵐ5‎ Ζακχυρ, Ζακχουρ, etc.: —

1 a Reubenite Num 13:4 (P).

2 a Simeonite 1Chr 4:26.

3 Levites:

a. 1Chr 24:27.

b. an Asaphite 1Chr 25:2 (Σακχους), 25:10 (Ζαχχουθ), Neh 12:35 (Ζακχουρ), compare זִכְרִי‎ above 3c. c. 10:13; compare 13:13

4 companion of Ezra, Ezra 8:14 Qr (Kt זבוד‎, compare above).

5 contemporary of Nehemiah, Neh 3:2.
