H4581. maoz

or mauz or maoz or mauz מָעוֺז‎ noun masculineJudg 6:26 place or means of safety, protection (ᵑ0‎ suffixes as if from עזז‎, but probably erroneous, compare BuhlLex 13 Ges§ 85k); — ׳מ‎ absolute 6:26 +, construct Isa 30:2 +; suffix מָעוּזִי‎ 2Sam 22:33 (but read הַמְּאַזְּרֵנִי‎ as || Psa 18:33), מָעוּזִּי‎ 31:3 +, מָעֻזִּי‎ Isa 27:5 +, מָעוּזּוֺ‎ Psa 52:9 +, מָעֻזֹּה‎ Dan 11:10, etc. (read probably מְעוֺזִּי‎, etc.); plural מָעֻזִּים‎ 11:38; 11:39, suffix מָעֻזְּנֶיהָ‎ Isa 23:11, read מָעֻזֶּיהָ‎ (Ges§ 20o CheHeb.Hpt. and others; strike out dagesh forte in all, see above); —

1 literally place of safety Judg 6:26, hence (c. collateral idea of strength, as if from עזז‎ = fastness Isa 23:11, 14 (in 23:14 CheHeb.Hpt. proposes מְחוֺזְכֶם‎ your city), Ezek 24:25; 30:15; Dan 11:7, 10, 19, הַמּ ׳הַמִּקְדָּשׁ‎ 11:31, מָעֻזִּים מִבְצְרֵי‎ 11:39; ׳מ הַיָּם‎ i.e. harbour Isa 23:4, but strike out O1 Du CheHpt.; ׳מ מֵאוֺיֵב‎ Nahum 3:11; compare מ ׳עָרֵי‎ Isa 17:9.

2. a. figurative, of God (׳י‎) as refuge Nahum 1:7; Psa 27:1; 31:5; 37:39; 52:9; Isa 25:4 (twice in verse); Joel 4:16, ׳י וּמְנוּסִי וּמָעֻזִּי עֻזִּי‎ Jer 16:19, ׳מ מְשִׁיחוֺ יְשׁוּעוֺת‎ Psa 28:8 (|| עָזֿ‎); so מ ׳צוּר‎ Isa 17:10; Psa 31:3, מ ׳אֱלֹהֵי‎ 43:2; also בְּמָעֻזִּי יַחֲזֵק‎ Isa 27:5 let him lay hold of my refuge (me as refuge); cf . י ׳חֶדְוַת מָעֻזְּכֶם הִיא‎ Neh 8:10, ׳מ י דֶּרֶךְ ׳לַתֹּם‎ Prov 10:29.

b. of a heathen god, מָעֻזִּים אֱלוֺהַּ‎ Dan 11:38 (read מְחֹזִים‎ cities CheIsa 23:1,Heb.Hpt.).

3 figurative of human protection, ׳מ מַּרְעֹה‎ Isa 30:2-3,(both || מִצְרַיִם צֵּל‎); מ ׳אֶפְרַיִם ראֹשִׁי‎ Psa 60:9 = 108:9, i.e. my helmet; also (of angel) Dan 11:1 (|| מַחֲזִיק‎).

עֲוִיל‎ see II. עול‎. below
