H1626. gerah

II. גֵּרָה‎ noun feminine a weight, 20th part of shekel, gerah (Assyrian girû, ZehnpfBAS i. 506) — גֵּרָה‎ Exod 30:13 4t. — Only in definition of שֶׁקֶל‎, with number עֶשְׂרִים‎ 30:13; Lev 27:25; Num 3:47; 18:16; Ezek 45:12.
