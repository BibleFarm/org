H6094. atstsebeth

[עַצֶּ֫בֶת‎] noun feminine hurt, injury, pain; — עַצָּ֑בֶת‎ Prov 10:10 he that winketh with the eye causeth hurt (stirs up strife, etc.); construct עַצְּבַתלֵֿב‎ pain of heart 15:13 (opposed to שָׂמֵחַ לֵב‎); plural suffix עַצְּבֹתָ֫י‎ Job 9:28 my pains, so read perhaps also 7:15 (for עַצְמוֺתָ֫י ᵑ0‎); עַצְּבוֺתָם‎ Psa 16:4 (due to idolatry; > idols ᵑ9 ᵑ7‎ We and others); לְע ׳מְחַבֵּשׁ‎ 147:3 binding up their hurts.
