H6231. ashaq

עָשַׁק‎ verb oppress, wrong, extort (עֲשַׁק ᵑ7‎ = Biblical Hebrew, so Old Aramaic עשק‎; Syriac accuse, slander; Assyrian adjective eš‡u, strong; Arabic roughness, injustice); —

Qal Perfect ׳ע‎ Leviticus 5:21; Ezek 18:18; 2masculine singular suffix עֲשַׁקְתָּ֫נוּ‎ 1Sam 12:4, etc.; Imperfect3masculine singular suffix יַעַשׁקֻנִי‎ Psa 119:122; 2masculine singular תַּעֲשֹׁק‎ Lev 19:13 +, etc.; Infinitive construct עֲשֹׁק‎ Hosea 12:8, suffix עָשְׁקָם‎ 1Chr 16:21; Psa 105:14; Participle active עשֵׁק‎ Prov 14:31 +, feminine plural הָעשְׁקוֺת‎ Amos 4:1, etc.; passive עָשׁוּק‎ Deut 28:29 +, etc.; —

1 oppress, wrong (often by extortion, || גָּזַל‎), with accusative of person 1Sam 12:3-4, Micah 2:2; Leviticus 5:21; Lev 19:13; especially the poor and helpless Amos 4:1; Deut 24:14; Jer 7:6; Ezek 22:29; Zech 7:10; Prov 14:31; 22:16; 28:3; Eccl 4:1; also with accusative of congnate meaning with verb ׳ע גָּזֵל (וְ)גָזַל עשֶׁק‎ Ezek 18:18 practise extortion, 22:29, take by extortion Leviticus 5:23 and (with accusative of thing) Mal 3:5; absolute Hosea 12:8 practise extortion; Job 10:3 deal tyrannically (of God); participle oppressor, extortioner Jer 21:12; Psa 72:4; Eccl 4:1; passive participle as substantive the oppressed Psa 103:6; 146:7.

2 oppress a nation Isa 52:4, compare Psa 105:14 = 1Chr 16:21; Psa 119:122, participle act. oppressor 119:121, passive participle oppressed Jer 50:33, in predict. ׳ע וְגָזוּל‎ Deut 28:29, ׳ע וְרָצּוּץ‎ 28:23; Hosea 5:11.

3 dubious are: נָהָר יַעְשֹׁק‎ Job 40:23 a river oppresses him, i.e. rushes violently upon him (that is, the hippopotamus; Du יָשֹׁק‎, from שׁוּק‎; > Gunk Bu יִשְׁקַע‎ falls, sinks); נֶפֶשׁ בְּדַם עָשֻׁק‎ Prov 28:17 oppressed (? burdened) with the blood of a person.
