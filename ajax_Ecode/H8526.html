H8526. Talmay

תַּלְמַי‎ proper name, masculine 1. king of Geshur, father-in-law of David, 2Sam 3:3; 13:37; 1Chr 3:2.

2 a son of `Anak Num 13:22; Josh 15:14, תַּלְמָ֑י‎ Judg 1:10; Θομμει, Θολμει, Θαλαμειν, etc.
