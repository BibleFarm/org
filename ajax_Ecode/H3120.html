[pn] יָוָן‎ ETCBC: יָוָן‎ (nmpr｜[jawan, Greece]) OSHL: j.ba.ae TWOT: 855 GK: H751, H3430
Derivation: probably from the same as יַיִן‎; effervescing (i.e. hot and active);
Strong's: Javan, the name of a son of Joktan, and of the race (Ionians, i.e. Greeks) descended from him, with their territory; also of a place in Arabia

H3120. Yavan

ארנבן‎ proper name, of a location whence wine, so Co Ezek 27:19 for וְיָוָן וְדָן ᵑ0‎; compare Assyrian wine of Aranabanim.

יָוָן‎ proper name, of a people Ionia ns), Greece (Greek Ἰάονες (i.e. originally Ἰάονες), see also ᵐ5‎ below; Old Persian Yauna SpiegAPK 237; Assyrian Iamanu (Iavanu) DlPa 248 ff. COTGloss; Egyptian Y:e-v:a-n-(n)a, see WMM369f. compare also 24 ff.; see especially on this word StaDe Populo Javan 1880) — Ionians, as traders Ezek 27:13, ᵐ5‎ Ἑλλάς (named with מֶשֶׁךְ תּוּבַל,‎; in 27:12 תַּרְשִׁישׁ‎ in 27:14 תּוֺגַרְמָה‎); compare יְוָנִי‎ (adjective, of a people) below; as distant, Isa 66:19, ᵐ5‎ Ἑλλάς (with תּוּבַל לוּד, מּוּל, תַּרְשִׁישׁ,‎ and הָֽרְחֹקִים הָאִיִּים‎; list to be emended from ᵐ5‎ Staib. 5 ff.); called 'son' of Japhet Gen 10:2 (P) = 1Chr 1:5, and 'father' of כִּתִּים תַּרְשִׁישׁ, אֱלִישָׁא,‎ and רֹדָנִים‎ (so read for דֹדָנִים‎) Gen 10:4 (P) = 1Chr 1:7 (in these ᵐ5‎ Ιωυναν, see LagBN 84 Anm. **); addressed (personified) יָוָן בָּנַיִךְ‎ Zech 9:13 (opposed to צִיּוֺן בניך‎) ᵐ5‎ Ἕλληνες, and so in following; יָוָן מֶלֶךְ‎ Dan 8:21 (see וּפָרָ֑ס מָדַי מַלְכֵי‎ 8:20) = king of Greece i.e. kingdom over which Alexander ruled, whose dominion was afterward divided (see 8:22ff.); שַׂריָֿוָן‎ 10:20 of the guardian-angel of Greece (see מָּרָס שַׂר‎ va); יָוָן מַלְכוּת‎ 11:2 (v:b מָּרַֹס‎). — Ezek 27:19 וְיָוָן‎ is textual error, see וְדָן‎ above.
