H7522. ratson

רָצוֺן‎ noun [masculine] goodwill, favour, acceptance, will; — absolute ׳ר‎ Deut 33:23 +; construct רְצוֺן‎ Psa 145:19 +; suffix רְצוֺנִי‎ Isa 60:10, רְצֹנוֺ‎ Dan 8:4 +, etc.; —

1 goodwill, favour:

a. of God, Deut 33:16; Isa 60:10; Psalm 5:13; Psa 30:6; 30:8; Psalm 51:20; 89:18; 106:4; Prov 8:35; 12:2; 18:22; ר ׳עֵת‎ Isa 49:8; Psa 69:14; ר ׳יוֺם‎ Isa 58:5; ׳שְׁנַתרֿ‎ 61:2; || בְּרָכָה‎ Deut 33:23; opposed to תּוֺעֵבָה‎ Prov 11:1, 20; 12:22; 15:8.

b. of men: in General Prov 10:32; 11:27; 14:9; of kings 14:35; 16:13, 15; 19:12.

2 acceptance, of persons, offering sacrifice, יהוה לפני לרצון‎ Exod 28:38 (P); with ל‎ person for acceptance for him before ׳י‎; with suffix person לרצנוֺ‎ Lev 1:3; abbreviated לָכֶם לְרָצוֺן‎ 22:20, לרצנכם‎ 19:5; 22:19, 29; 23:11, לצֵּון‎ 22:21; Isa 56:7; Jer 6:20; Psalm 19:15 (of words; compare Hosea 14:3); רצון על‎ Isa 60:7 (read לְ‎, so Manuscripts, see Gi, also Du), רצון‎ Mal 2:13.

3 will, desire, pleasure:

a. of God, ר ׳עשׂה‎ do his will Ezra 10:11; Psa 40:9; 103:21; 143:10.

b. of man, כרצונוֺ עשׂה‎ do according to his will, exactly as he pleased, Dan 8:4; 11:3, 16, 36; Neh 9:24; Est 1:8; 9:5, compare Neh 9:37; desire Psa 145:16; 145:19; 2Chr 15:15; = self-will Gen 49:6 (poem).
