סָפַד‎ (çâphad｜saw-fad'｜verb｜wail)
[Heb] סָפַד‎ ETCBC: ספד‎ (verb｜lament) OSHL: o.cc.aa TWOT: 1530 GK: H6199 Greek: κλαίω, κόπτω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: properly, to tear the hair and beat the breasts (as Orientals do in grief); generally to lament; by implication, to wail
KJV: lament, mourn(-er), wail.
