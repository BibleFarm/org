זַרְזִיף‎ Psa 72:6 see זרף‎.

[זָרַף‎] verb probably drip (compare either Arabic shed tears, or Aramaic imber vehemens) —

Hiph`il יַזְרִיף‎ read in Psa 72:6 by Hup Bi Che (for זַרְזִיף‎) followed by אָ֑רֶץ‎ cause to drip, irrigate.

זַרְזִיף‎ noun [masculine] drop, dripping (Late Hebrew id.; on form (if Genuine) compare NöM 85, NS 191) — Psa 72:6 ז ׳כִּרְבִיבִים אָ֑רֶץ‎ as showers, a dripping of the earth, but see foregoing.
