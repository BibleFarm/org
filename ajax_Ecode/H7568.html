H7568. resheth

רֶ֫שֶׁת‎ noun feminineExod 27:5 net; — absolute רֶשֶׁת‎ Hosea 5:1 11t.; רָ֑שֶׁת‎ Prov 1:17; construct רֶשֶׁת‎ Exod 27:4; 38:4; suffix רִשְׁתִּי‎ Hosea 7:12 2t.; רִשְׁתּוֺ‎ Psa 10:9; 35:8; רִשְׁתָּם‎ Ezek 19:8; Psa 35:7; —

1 net:

a. for catching (birds, etc.) הָרָ֑שֶׁת מְזֹרָה חִנָּם‎ Prov 1:17 to no purpose is the net spread in the eyes of any bird; elsewhere figurative (1) of judgment of ׳י‎, all with פרשׁ‎: Hosea 7:12 (|| אוֺרִידֵם הַשָּׁמַיִם כְּעוֺף‎), Ezek 12:13; 17:20 (both || מְצוּדָתִי‎), 32:3 (of ׳י‎ catching Pharaoh, under figure of sea-monster; || II. חֵרֶם‎); (2) of nations capturing king of Israel under figure of lion 19:8 (with פרשׁ‎; || שַׁחְתָּם‎); (3) of priests and rulers (as entangling people in sin) Hosea 5:1 (|| מַּח‎).

b. spread for feet of man (figurative): (1) by ׳י‎ Lam 1:13 (with מָּרַשׁ‎); (2) by wicked (= plot) Prov 29:5 (with פרשׁ‎), Psa 140:6 (c. id.; || מַּךְ טָמַן‎), 10:9; 25:15, בְּרַגְלָיו בְּדֶשֶׁת שֻׁלַּח‎ Job 18:8 (|| שְׂבָכָה‎); elsewhere with טמן‎ Psa 9:16 (|| שַׁחַת‎), 31:5; 35:7 (רִשְׁתָּם שַׁחַת‎), 31:8; with הֵכִין‎ 57:7 (|| שִׁיחָה‎).

2 brazen network for altar of tabernacle הָרֶשֶׁת‎ Exod 27:4-5, ר מַעֲשֵׂה ׳מִכְבָּר נְחשֶׁת‎ 27:4 = 38:4 (all P). — On Infinitive רֶשֶׁת‎, see ירשׁ‎ above

רֶ֫שֶׁת‎ net, see ירשׁ‎.
