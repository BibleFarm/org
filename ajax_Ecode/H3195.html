H3195. Yetur

יְטוּר‎ proper name, masculine and

of a people (perhaps connected etymologically with טִירָה‎ see Gen 25:16) — a 'son' of Ishmael 25:15 = 1Chr 1:31 +? 5:19; Ἰτουραῖοί τε καὶ Ἄραβες Straboxvi, 2, 18, Ἰτουραίους JosephAnt. xiii. 11, 3 ed. Niese; they gave name to their region, which was, substantially, Anti-Lebanon, compare τῆς Ἰτουραίας καὶ Τραχωνίτιδος χώρας Luke 3:1; on יְטוּר‎ and (later) proper name, of a territory Ituraea, see especially GASmGeogr. 544 ff. and reff.

מוֺתָר‎ noun masculineProv 14:23 abundance, pre-eminence; —

1 abundance, plenty מוֺתָ֑ר‎ Prov 14:23; 21:5 (in both opposed to מַחְסוֺר‎).

2 pre-eminence, superiority, אָ֔יִן מִןהַֿבְּהֵמָה הָאָדָם מוֺתַר‎ Eccl 3:19 the superiority of man over beast is nothing.
