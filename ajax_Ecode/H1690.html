דְּבֵלָה‎ noun feminine lump of pressed figs, pressed (fig-) cake (Late Hebrew דְּבֵילָה‎, Aram id., , = Greek παλάθη; Arabic lump, large gobbet or mouthful; compare Assyrian dublu, foundation, & Hebrew synonym אֲשִׁישָׁה‎ DlHA 58) — דְּבֵלָה‎ 1Sam 30:12; construct דְּבֶלֶת‎ 2Kin 20:7 = Isa 38:21; plural דְּבֵלִים‎ 1Sam 25:18; 1Chr 12:40; — used as food 1Sam 25:18; 30:12; 1Chr 12:40; תְּאֵנִים דְּבֶלֶת‎, as application to boil, or eruption 2Kin 20:7 = Isa 38:21.
