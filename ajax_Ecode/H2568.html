1 without other numeral:

a. חָמֵשׁ‎ before feminine plural noun Gen 43:34 35t.; before collective noun ׳ח צאֹן‎ 1Sam 25:18; see also ׳ח בָּאַמָּה‎ five in cubit (s) = five cubits 1Kin 7:23; 1Chr 11:23; 2Chr 4:2; after noun (late) 2Kin 8:16 (לְיוֺרָם חָמֵשׁ בִּשְׁנַת‎ = in the fifth year) + 6 t. b. חֲמִשָּׁה‎ before masculine plural noun Gen 47:2 18t.; after noun Exod 36:38 36t. Numbers (all P); no noun expressed Gen 14:9 (only here with article) + 20 t.

c. חֲמֵשׁ‎ before noun (usually defined; except אֲלָפִים מֵאוֺת,‎, see 3, 4) Exod 26:3, 9; 36:10, 16 + Ezek 42:16 ׳ח אַמּוֺת‎ Kt, but Qr ׳ח מאות‎, see below

d. חַמְּלָכִים חֲמֵשֶׁת‎ Josh 10:5 5t.; ׳ח הָאֲנָשִׁים‎ Judg 18:7, 14, 17; ׳ח שׁקלים‎ Num 18:16; also, distributive, ׳ח ׳ח שְׁקָלִים‎ 3:47; ׳ח הַסְּרָנִים‎ Josh 13:3; Judg 3:3; 1Sam 6:18; ׳ח מ ׳בני‎ 2Sam 21:8.

e. = ordinal, ליורם חָמֵשׁ בִּשְׁנַת‎ 2Kin 8:16 the fifth (year) of Joram; לַחֹדֶשׁ בַּחֲמִשָּׁה‎ on the fifth (day) of the month Ezek 1:1-2, 8:1; 33:21; (so also 15th, 25th, etc.)

2 ׳ח‎ combined with עשׂרה עשׂר,‎ ten = fifteen:

a. עֶשְׂרֵה חֲמֵשׁ‎ (c. noun feminine) Gen 5:10; 7:20; Exod 27:14-15, 38:14-15, 2Kin 14:17, 23; 20:6; 2Chr 15:10; 25:25; Isa 38:5.

b. עָשָׂר חֲמִשָּׁה‎ (c.

noun masculine), (1) after noun Est 9:21; (2) before noun Exod 16:1 ( = ordinal fifteenth, so Lev 23:6 +) Hosea 3:2 +.

c. עָשָׂר חֲמֵשֶׁת‎ before noun 2Sam 19:18 +.

d. עָשָׂר חֲמֵשֶׁת‎, without noun = ordinal fifteenth 1Chr 24:14 4t. + Ezek 45:12 (read חֲמִשִּׁים‎ A Hi Co).

3 מֵאוֺת חֲמֵשׁ‎ = 500 Gen 5:30, 32 49t. + Ezek 42:16 Qr.

4 אֲלָפִים חֲמֵשֶׁת‎ = 5000 Num 31:32; Josh 8:12; Judg 20:45; 1Sam 17:5; Ezek 45:6; 48:15; 1Chr 29:7; 2Chr 35:9; Ezra 1:11; 2:69; — in 25, 000, 35, 000 etc. חֲמִשָּׁה‎ (usually without ׳א‎) precedes the 20, 000, 30, 000, etc.

5 with other numerals:

a. חָמֵשׁ‎ before larger numeral: (1) noun repeated Gen 5:6 4t.; (2) noun with 2nd number only, 5:17 8t. + Ezek 45:3 Kt; (3) noun with בְּ‎ with 2nd number 40:21 2t.

b. חָמֵשׁ‎ after larger number: (1) noun repeated Gen 25:7; (2) noun following ׳ח‎ (only) Judg 14:10 + (especially in expressions of age and duration); (3) noun before both numbers, stative absolute, 2Chr 3:15; stative construct 15:19.

c. no noun expressed, ׳ח‎ between other number, e.g. ח מאות ׳שֵׁשׁ ושׁבעים‎ Num 31:37.

d. חֲמִשָּׁה‎ before larger number, noun following larger number Num 1:25 22t.

e. ׳ח‎ follows, and (1) is followed by noun Judg 20:35 7t.; (2) noun precedes both 1Kin 7:3; Jer 52:30.

f. ׳ח‎ precedes, no noun expressed 1Kin 5:12.

g. ׳ח‎ follows, no noun expr Gen 18:28 +.

h. no noun expressed ׳ח‎ between other number Exod 38:28; Ezra 2:5.
