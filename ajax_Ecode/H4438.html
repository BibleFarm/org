2 reign: 40th year of reign of David 1Chr 26:31 compare 2Chr 3:2; 15:10, 19; 16:1, 12; 35:19; Jer 49:34; Dan 1:1; also 2Chr 29:19; of Babylonian and Persian kings Jer 52:31 (מ ׳בִּשְׁנַת‎ || 2Kin 25:27 מָלְכוֺ‎) Dan 2:1; 8:1; Ezra 4:5-6, (twice in verse); 7:1; 8:1; Neh 12:22; Est 2:16; compare מָּרָס מַלְכוּת עַדמְֿלֹח‎2Chr 36:20.
