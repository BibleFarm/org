H5692. uggah

עֻגָה‎ noun feminine disc or cake of bread; — absolute ׳ע‎ Hosea 7:8; 1Kin 17:13, construct עֻגַת‎ 19:6; Ezek 4:12; plural עֻגוֺת‎ Gen 18:6; Num 11:8; construct עֻגֹת‎ Exod 12:39; — bread-cake, made of סֹלֶת קֶמַח‎ Gen 18:6 (J), of קֶמַח‎ 1Kin 17:13, of manna Num 11:8 (J E; verb בִּשֵּׁל‎), of barley Ezek 41:2 (simile); unleavened, מַצּוֺת עֻגֹת‎ Exod 12:39 (E; verb אָפָת‎); ׳ע רְצָפִים‎ 1Kin 19:6 cake of hot-stones i.e. baked on them; metaphor ׳ע הֲפוּכָה כְּלִי‎ Hosea 7:8 Ehpraimitic source is a cake not turned (i.e. burnt, ruined). — On ׳ע‎ see BenzArchaeology 85 f. NowArchaeology i. 111 KennedyEncy. Bib. BREAD and synonyms, Ib.CAKE.
