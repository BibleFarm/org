H7225. reshith

[רֵאשִׁית‎] noun feminine beginning, chief (for *רְאֵשִׁית‎ NöGGA 1884, 1019, compare HolzHexateuch 465; Syriac ); — absolute ׳ר‎ Deut 33:21+, construct ׳ר‎ Gen 10:10+, רֵשִׁית‎ Deut 11:12; suffix רֵאשִׁיתוֺ‎ Eccl 7:8, רֵאשִׁתוֺ‎ Job 42:12, etc.; —

1.a beginning of kingdom Gen 10:10 (J), year Deut 11:12, reign Jer 26:1; 27:1; 28:1; 49:34;= first phase, step, or element in course of events Isa 46:10 (opposed to אַחֲרִית‎); of a thing (דָּבָר‎) Eccl 7:8 (opposed to id.); of sin Micah 1:13, strife Prov 17:14, wisdom Psa 111:10, knowledge Prov 1:7 thy beginning Job 8:7 +?his beginning 42:12 (both opposed to אַחֲרִית‎), ׳ר‎ before clause, ׳בְּר א ׳בָּרָא‎ Gen 1:1 in the beginning when god created (> absolute in the beginning God created); ׳ר אֹנִי‎ 49:3 (poem) beginning (first product) of my manly vigour (|| כֹּחִי בְּכֹרִי‎ so Deut 21:17; Psa 78:51 (both || בְּכוֺת‎ 105:36; compare ׳ר אֵל דֵּרְכֵי‎ Job 40:19 (of hippopotamus), ׳ר דַּרְכּוֺ‎ Prov 8:22 (of wisdom);=first season (of a tree) Hosea 9:10; גּוֺיִם׳ר‎ Num 24:20 (JE), first, earliest, of nations.

b. first of fruits Exod 23:19 (E), 34:26 (JE), Deut 26:2, 10; Ezek 44:30.a, of harvest Lev 23:10 (H), grain Deut 18:4; 2Chr 31:5 dough Num 15:20-21, (P), Ezek 44:30b Neh 10:38, wool Deut 18:4; of כָּלתְּֿבוּאָתֶ֑ךָ‎ Prov 3:9; Israel is ׳ר תְּבוּאָתֹה‎ Jer 2:3 (i.e. of ׳י‎s increase); ׳ר‎=first-fruits Lev 2:12 (P), Num 18:12 (P), Neh 12:44; ׳ר הָאָרֶץ‎ Ezek 48:14

2 first chief, Amos 6:1; ׳ר גְּבוּרָתָם‎ Jer 49:35 (of bows); so apparently ׳ר עַמּוֺן בְּנֵי‎ Dan 11:41, i.e. the principal part of them (Buhl Marti conjecture שְׁאֵרִית‎ after ᵑ6‎); ׳ר מַשְׁאוֺתֵיכֶם‎ Ezek 20:40, i.e. your chief oblations; ׳ר שְׁמָנִים‎ Amos 6:6, i.e. choice oils; = choice part, of land Deut 33:21, offering 1Sam 2:29, הַחֵרֶם‎ 15:21; absolute chief thing Prov 4:7 (of wisdom).
