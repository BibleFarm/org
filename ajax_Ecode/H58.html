אָבֵל‎ (ʼâbêl｜aw-bale'｜noun｜feminine｜a meadow)
[Heb] אָבֵל‎ ETCBC: אָבֵל כְּרָמִים‎ (nmpr｜Abel Keramim) OSHL: a.aj.ab TWOT: 7a GK: H64 Greek: Ἀβιληνή
Derivation: from an unused root (meaning to be grassy);
Strong's: a meadow
KJV: plain. Compare also the proper names beginning with Abel-.

H58. abel

II. אָבֵל‎ noun feminine 1. meadow (?) 1Sam 6:18 ᵑ0‎ but read אֶבֶן‎ compare 6:14; 6:15 ᵑ6 ᵐ5‎ We Dr.

2. proper name, of a location city in Northern Israel 2Sam 20:18, near Beth Maacah 20:14 = מַעֲכָה בֵּית אָבֵל‎ 20:15 (so also 20:14 Ew Th We Klo Dr), 1Kin 15:20; 2Kin 15:29; = ׳א מַיִם‎2Chr 16:4 (= Abil el †amµ, wheat-meadow northwest of Dan & south of Mutulleh RobBR III, 372).

3 הַשִּׁטִּים אָבֵל‎

proper name, of a location (= acacia-meadow) in lowlands of Moab Num 33:49; = שִׁטִּים‎ 25:1; Micah 6:5 (= Tel Kefrein (?) Tristr & MerrillPESoc. 4th Statement, 89).

4 כְּרָמִים אָבֵל‎

proper name, of a location (= vineyard-meadow) in Ammon Judg 11:33 (see Euseb. Ἀβελαμπελων).

5 מְחוֺלָה אָבֵל‎

proper name, of a location (= dance-meadow) Judg 7:22; 1Kin 4:12; Elisha's birthplace 19:16 (see Euseb. Ἀβελμαελαι).

6 מִצְרַיִם אָבֵל‎

proper name, of a location (= meadow of Egypt, i.e. fertile as Egypt ?) east of Jordan Gen 50:11 (where interpreted as if מ ׳אֵבֶל‎, so ᵑ9 ᵐ5‎; see Di).
