II. מְּנֵי‎ with prepositions: —

1 אֶלמְּֿנֵי‎:

a. to the presence of, Exod 23:17 pregnantly הָאָדוֺן אֶלמְּֿנֵי ֗֗֗ יֵרָאֶה‎ shall appear (coming) to the presence of (= before) ׳י‎ (|| 34:23 הָא ׳אֶתמְּֿנֵי‎); with יָצָא‎ = to confront2Chr 19:2.

b. to the front of, after a verb of motion, Lev 6:7 אֶלמְּֿנֵ ֗֗֗ אֹתָהּ ׳הַקְרֵב הַמִּזְבֵּחַ‎, 9:5; 16:2; Num 17:8; 20:10.

c. towards or on the front of (אֶל‎ = עַל‎), Ezek 41:4, 12, 15, 25; 42:2-3, 7, 10, 13; 45:7; 48:21.

d. on to the surface of, Lev 14:53 הַשָּׂדֶה אֶלמְּֿנֵי ֗֗֗ וְשִׁלַּח‎, Ezek 16:5.

2 אֶתמְּֿנֵי‎ close by (II. אֵת‎) the face or front of: hence

b. in front of, Gen 33:18. אֶתפֿ ׳וַיִּחַן הָעִיר‎, Lev 4:6 י ׳לִפְנֵי ׳אֶתפֿ הַקֹּדֶשׁ פָרֹכֶת‎ 4:17. — מְּנֵי מֵאֵת‎ from before, see II. אֵת‎ 4a. 3. בִּפְנֵי‎: (a) in the face of, mostly in particular phrases, with hostile import: thus, with יָרַק‎ to spit Num 12:14; Deut 25:9; התיצב‎ to make a stand 7:24; 11:25, and עמד‎ Josh 10:8, בְּפָנֶיךָ אִישׁ יַעֲמֹד לֹא‎ 21:42; 23:9; עָנָה‎ to answer (give evidence) against, Hosea 5:5 בפניו ישׂראל גאון וענה‎, 7:10; Job 16:8; בִּפְנֵיהֶם וְנָקֹטוּ‎ = feel loathing against their own selves, Ezek 6:9, so 20:43; 36:31. 4 לִפְנֵי‎, properly at the face or front of, the most General word for in the presence of, before: as Gen 18:22 אַבְרָהָם לִפְנֵי עֹמֵד‎, Exod 4:21; 11:10; 2Sam 2:14; Prov 17:18 + often; after a verb of motion, Gen 47:2 פ ׳לפני וַיַּצִּיגֵם‎ and set them before Pharaoh, 27:20 לפני י הִקְרָה ׳כִּי‎ literally made (it) to meet before me (compare 24:12), Exod 29:10; Prov 18:16 etc. In particular,
