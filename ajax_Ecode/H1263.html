H1263. Baruk

בָּרוּךְ‎ proper name, masculine (blessed)

1 friend and amanuensis of Jeremiah Jer 32:12-13, 16; 36:4-32; 43:3, 6; 45:1-2,.

2 a priest, son of Zabbai (Zaccai) Neh 3:20; 10:7.

3 son of Colhozeh, of the tribe of Judah Neh 11:5.
