גְּדוּלָּה‎ noun feminine greatness — 2Sam 7:21 3t.; גְּדֻלָּה‎ 1Chr 29:11; construct גְּדֻלַּת‎ Est 10:2; suffix גְּדוּלָּתוֺ‎ 1:4; גְּדֻלָּתוֺ‎ Psa 145:3; גְּדְלָּתִי‎ 71:21; גְדֻלָּו֯תְךָ‎ 145:6; plural intensive גְּדֻלּוֺת‎1Chr 17:19, 21; — chiefly late Hebrew

a. of Psalmist Psa 71:21; Mordecai Est 6:3; 10:2, king 1:4;

b. of God's greatness, as an attribute 1Chr 29:11; Psa 145:3; 145:6, or of his acts 2Sam 7:21 (compare Dr) 7:23 = 1Chr 17:19 (twice in verse); 17:21.
