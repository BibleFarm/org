H4532. Massah

III. מַסָּה‎ proper name, of a location where Israel tried ׳י‎ in the wilderness, Exod 17:7 (JE), Deut 6:16; 9:22; Psa 95:8 (all ᵐ5‎ (ὁ)πειρασμός), where Levi was tested Deut 33:8 (ᵐ5‎ πείρᾳ). **See GallKultstätten 32 interprets as place of trial (ancient judgment-place) and identification with Kadesh, called also מְרִיבָה (מֵי)‎ and מִשְׁמָּט עֵין‎.
