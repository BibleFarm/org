אחרין‎ Dan 4:5 Kt, דָּנִיֵּאל מַי קֳדָ עַל אחרין וְעַד‎; very dubious (see Commentaries); usually and unto the last (= at last) came in, etc.; but word in this sense unknown: Qr אָחֳרָן‎; read perhaps (Bev) אָחֳרָן וְעֹד‎ (or ? Kt אַחֲרִין‎; compare) and yet another came in before me.
