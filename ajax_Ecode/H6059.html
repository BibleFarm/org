עָנַק‎ (ʻânaq｜aw-nak'｜verb｜serve as necklace)
[Heb] עָנַק‎ ETCBC: ענק‎ (verb｜put around neck) OSHL: p.eb.ad TWOT: 1658c GK: H6735
Derivation: a primitive root; properly, to choke; used only as denominative from עָנָק‎,
Strong's: to collar, i.e. adorn with anecklace; figuratively, to fit out with supplies
KJV: compass about as a chain, furnish, liberally.
