H4074. Maday

מָדַי‎ proper name, of a people et

terr. Medes, Media (Old Persian Mâda SpAPK, Assyrian Madai COTGloss, and on Genesis 10.. 2 DlPar. 247); —

1 people, as 'son' of Japhet Gen 10:2 (P) = 1Chr 1:5; as hostile power Isa 13:17; 21:2; מָדַי זֶרַע‎ Dan 9:1; land and people, מָדַי מַלְכֵי‎ Jer 25:25 (+ עֵילָם מַלְכֵי‎), 51:11, 28; מ ׳מַלְכֵי וּפָרַס‎ Dan 8:20; compare וּמָדַי מָּרַס‎ Est 1:3, 14, 18, 19; 10:2; ᵐ5‎ Μαδαι.

2 of land alone = Media 2Kin 17:6; 18:11 (both מָדָ֑י עָרֵי‎).
