[כִּרְכָּרָה‎] noun feminine dromedary (according to Ki Thes; perhaps from constantly repeated undulating movement); — only plural absolute וּבַכִּרְכָּרוֺת‎ Isa 66:20 (+ וּבַמְּרָדִים וּבַצַּבִּים וּבָרֶכֶב בַּסּוּסִים‎; the whole list is a gloss according to Du CheIntr Is.).
