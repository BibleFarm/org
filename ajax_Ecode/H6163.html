H6163. Arabiy

עֲרָבִי‎ name, of a people of foregoing, steppe-dweller; — Isa 13:20; ׳כַּעֲרָבִ בַּמִּדְבָּ֑ר‎ Jer 3:2.

עַרְבִי‎ adjective, of a people Arabian (in strictly ethnographic sense, Nöl.c.); — ׳הָע‎ as substantive Neh 2:19 Geshem the Arabian, so 6:1; plural הָעַרְבִים‎ 4:1; 2Chr 21:16; 22:1; also (written as Late Hebrew) הערביים‎26:7 (Qr הָעַרְבִים‎), and even הָעַרְבִיאִים‎17:11.
