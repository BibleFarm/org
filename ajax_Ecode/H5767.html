H5767. avval

עַוָּל‎ noun masculine unjust, unrighteous one; — especially of oppressive ruler Zeph 3:5; Job 18:18; 27:7 (|| רָשָׁע‎), 29:17; 31:3 (|| אָ֑וֶן מֹּעֲלֵי‎); probably also 16:11 (for עֲוִיל ᵑ0‎, so Di; || רְשָׁעִים‎).
