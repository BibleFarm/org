H1576. gemul

גְּמוּל‎ noun masculine dealing, recompence, benefit — ׳ג‎ Psa 94:2 11t.; suffix גְּמֻלְךָ‎ Obadiah 15, etc.; plural suffix גְּמוּלָיו‎ 103:2; —

1 יָדַיִם גְּמוּל‎ dealing of the hands Judg 9:16; Prov 12:14 (לְ יָשׁוּב‎) Isa 3:11 (לְ יֵעָשֶׂה‎).

2 dealing, hence (from context) equivalent of dealing, recompence: with suffix Obadiah 15 בראשׁך ישׁוב גמלך‎, Joel 4:4; Joel 4:7 (הֵשִׁיב‎), so with לְ הֵשִׁיב‎ Psa 28:4; לְ שִׁלֵּם‎ 137:8; Prov 19:17; absolutely לְ גְּמוּל‎ (הֵשִׁיב‎) שִׁלֵם‎ Isa 59:18 (twice in verse); 66:6; Jer 51:6; Lam 3:64; עַל‎ Joel 4:4; Psa 94:2; Isa 35:4 אלהים ׳ג‎ the recompence of God.

3 benefit: גמוליו כל תשׁכחי אַל‎ Psa 103:2; עליו כגמול‎ according to the benefit (done) unto him2Chr 32:25
