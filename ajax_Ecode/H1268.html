H1268. Berothah

בֵּרוֺ֫תָה‎ proper name, of a location near Hamath Ezek 47:16 (contracted from בְּאֵרוֺתָה‎ ?), compare following; — hardly = Berytus (Beirût) with which form of name might agree see Steph. Byzant. and others in MoversPhen. ii. 1. 110 n.; perhaps Bereit¹n near Baalbek, see FurrerZPV viii. 34.

בֵּרֹתַי‎ proper name, of a location belonging to Hadadezer of Zoba 2Sam 8:8; perhaps = foregoing.
