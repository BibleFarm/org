H3515. kabed

כָּבֵד‎ adjective heavy; — ׳כ‎ Gen 41:31+ 34 t.; construct כְּבַד‎ Exod 4:10 (twice in verse); כֶּבֶד‎ Isa 1:4; plural כְּבֵדִים‎ Exod 17:12; construct כִּבְדֵי‎ Ezek 3:5-6, —

1. a. heavy, a burden Psa 38:5; hands (weary of holding up) Exod 17:12 (E), hair of head 2Sam 14:26, a corpulent old man 1Sam 4:18; עָוֺן כֶבֶד עַם‎ Isa 1:4 a people heavy with (the burden of) iniquity; a cloud charged with rain Exod 19:16 (E); a rock of large size Isa 32:2; oppressive, grievous, burdensome, a yoke 1Kin 12:4, 11 2Chr 10:4, 11; a famine Gen 12:10; 41:31; 43:1; 47:4, 13 (J); the vexation (כַּעַס‎) of a fool Prov 27:3; vehement, sore, of a mourning Gen 50:11(J).

b. massive, abundant, numerous, of a people עַם‎ Num 20:20 (J) 1Kin 3:9; army חיל‎ 2Kin 6:14; 18:14 = Isa 36:2 insect swarm Exod 8:20 (J).

c. heavy, dull of speech and tongue Exod 4:10 (twice in verse) (JE); of the לֵב‎, hard 7:14 (J).

d. hard, difficult, of a thing to be done Exod 18:18 (E) Num 11:14 (J); of a language to be understood Ezek 3:5-6,.

2 in the usage of J מְאֹד כָּבֵד‎ is frequent:

a. very oppressive, grievous, of hail Exod 9:18, 24; murrain 9:8; lamentation Gen 50:10.

b. very numerous, cattle Exod 12:38; army, מַחֲנֶה‎ Gen 50:9; locusts Exod 10:14; so חַיִל‎ 1Kin 10:2 2Chr 9:1.

c. very rich, במקנה‎ in cattle Gen 13:2.
