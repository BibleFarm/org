H271. Achaz

אָחָז‎ proper name, masculine (he hath grasped, abbreviation for יהואחז‎ (q. v.) compare Assyrian Ia-u-—a-zi (i.e. Ahaz) COT on 2Kin 16:8)

1 king of Judah, son of Jotham, father of Hezekiah 2Kin 15:38; 16:1-2, 5, 7, 8; 13t. 2Kings; Isa 1:1; 7:1, 3, 10, 12; 14:28; 38:18; 1Chr 3:13; 2Chr 27:9 8t. 2 Chronicles; Hosea 1:1; Micah 1:1.
