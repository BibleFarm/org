אֶ֫רֶז‎ noun masculineEzek 17:23 cedar; — feminine 17:22 ׳א הָרָמָה‎, but strike out ᵐ5 הרמה‎ Co; — (Arabic pine-tree, etc.; Ethiopic Syriac ) — ׳א‎ absolute Lev 14:4 19t., אָ֑רֶז‎ Job 40:17 6t.; plural אֲרָזִיס‎ Isa 9:9 35t.; construct אַרְזֵי‎ 2:13 5t.; suffix אֲרָזֶיךָ‎ Jer 22:17; Zech 11:1; אֲרָזָיו‎ Isa 37:24 = 2Kin 19:23; —

1 cedar-tree, (a) as growing Num 24:6; Psa 148:9; Isa 41:19; 44:14 compare Ezek 31:8; especially as growing on Lebanon 1Kin 5:13; 2Kin 14:9 2Chr 25:18 (both in fable of Jehoash); often לְּבָנוֺן (הַ) אַרְזֵי‎ Judg 9:15; Isa 2:13; 14:8; Psa 29:5 (twice in verse) (figurative) 104:16, compare 1 Kings 5:20; 2Kin 19:23 = Isa 37:24; Psa 92:13; Song 5:15; Ezek 27:5 (singular collective) Zech 11:1-2,(figurative); Song 1:17; Ezra 3:7; Jer 22:23 (figurative) Ezek 17:3 (figurative); (b) especially in simile, of outward power, stateliness & majesty Psa 80:11 (׳א אֵל‎) compare Ezek 31:3 (personified, but see Co on text), Amos 2:9; of individuals Jer 22:7; Psa 92:13; Ezek 17:22-23, compare other examples of figurative use, above; simile of straightness & strength Job 40:17 (tail of hippopotamus).

2 cedar-timber, cedar-wood for building, אֲרָזִים עֲצֵי‎ 1Sam 5:11; 1 Kings 5:22; 1 Kings 5:24; 1Kin 6:10; 9:11; 1Chr 14:1; 22:4 (twice in verse); 2Chr 2:7; Ezra 3:7; without עֵץ‎ 2Sam 7:2, 7; 1Kin 6:9, 15, 16, 18 (twice in verse); 6:20, 36; 7:2 (twice in verse); 7:3, 7, 11, 12; 10:27; 1Chr 17:1; 17:6; 2Chr 1:15; 2:3; 9:27 & Song 8:9; Isa 9:9; Jer 22:14-15, (compare also Isa 44:14; 1 Kings 5:20; Ezek 27:5; Ezra 3:7 above)

3 cedar-wood used in purifications, with עֵץ‎ Lev 14:4, 6, 49, 51, 52; Num 19:6 (all P).
