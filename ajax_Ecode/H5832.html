H5832. Azarel

עֲזַרְאֵל‎ proper name, masculine (El hath helped); — Οζρειηλ, Εζριηλ, etc.;

1 warrior of David 1Chr 12:7 (van d. H. v.12:6).

2 Levite musician 1Chr 25:18 (= עֻזִּיאֵל‎ 25:4), B Αζαρια, A Εζριηλ.

3 a Danite 1Chr 27:22, Αζαραηλ, Εζριηλ.

4 one with foreign wife Ezra 10:41.

5 priestly name:

a. Neh 11:13.

b. Neh 12:36.
