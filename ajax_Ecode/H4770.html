H4770. marbeq

מַרְבֵק‎ noun [masculine] stall (literally tying-place); — מ מִתּוֺךְ ׳עֲגָלִים‎ Amos 6:4 calves out of the stall (where they were fattened); ׳עֵגֶלמֿ‎ 1Sam 28:24 i.e. a stall-fed, fatted, calf; so מ ׳כְּעֶנְלֵי‎ Malachi 3:20 (simile of prosperity), Jer 46:21 (simile of well-fed and arrogant mercenaries).
