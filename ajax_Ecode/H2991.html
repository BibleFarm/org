H2991. Yibleam

יִבְלְעָם‎ proper name, of a location a city of Manasseh in West-Jordan land Josh 17:11 (J E; but ׳יִב‎ here dubious, see BuRS 13 f.), Judg 1:27; 2Kin 9:27; so read also 15:10 for קָבָלעָֿם ᵑ0‎ (ᵐ5‎L ἐν Ιεβλααμ; see Klo); = בִּלְעָם‎ 1Chr 6:55, q. v.; read יִבְלְעָם‎ also in Josh 21:25 (|| 1Chr 6:55), for ᵐ5 רִמּוֺן, גַּתּ ᵑ0‎ Ιεβαθα (ᵐ5‎L after ᵑ0‎ Γεθρεμμων, see Di; it lay about 13 miles east of north from Samaria, three-fifths of the way to Jezreel; modern ruin Bel`ame BdPal 228, compare SchultzZMG iii. 49; (Old Egyptian Y-b-ra-±a-mu WMM195).
