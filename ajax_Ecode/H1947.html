Derivation: feminine active participle of הָלַל‎;
Strong's: folly
KJV: madness.

H1947. holelah

[הוֺלֵלָה‎] noun feminine madness (on text see below), only plural הוֺלֵלוֺת הֹלֵלוֺת,‎, and only Ecclesiastes: Eccl 1:17 and I set my heart (לִבִּי וָאֶתְּנָה‎) to know wisdom, and to know madness and folly (וְשִׂכְלוּת הֹלֵלוֺת‎), compare הוֺלֵלוֺת‎ 2:12 (|| id.); ה וְהַסִּכְלוּת כֶּסֶל רֶשַׁע ׳וְלָדַעַת‎ 7:25; i.e. to know folly to be madness; possibly read חוֺלֵלוּת‎, compare סִכְלוּת‎ & following; the moral evil of it is specifically recognized in 9:3 (|| רָע‎).
