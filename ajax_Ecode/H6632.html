[Heb] צָב‎ ETCBC: צָב‎ (subs｜lizard), צָב‎ (subs｜wagon) OSHL: r.ae.ab, r.ad.ab TWOT: 1866a 1867a GK: H7369, H7370
Derivation: from an unused root meaning to establish;
Strong's: a palanquin or canopy (as a fixture); also a species of lizard (probably as clinging fast)
KJV: covered, litter, tortoise.

H6632. tsab

I. צָב‎ noun [masculine] litter; — absolute ׳צ‎, in צָב עֶגְלֹת‎ Num 7:3, probably litter-wagons, i.e. wagons covered, like palanquin; plural צַבִּים‎ Isa 66:20 litters (on Vrss compare DiNu).

II. צבב‎ (√ of following; compare Arabic cleave to ground; Late Hebrew צָב‎ = Biblical Hebrew, Syriac in Lexicons; Arabic is a large lizard, see especially Seetzen:Reisen iii. 436 f f.).

II. צָב‎ noun [masculine] lizard, as unclean, Lev 11:29.
