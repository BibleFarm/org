H5945. elyown

I. עֶלְיוֺן‎22 adjective 1 high; — masculine singular ׳ע‎ Deut 26:19; 28:1; 1Kin 9:8 (? reading הָע הַזֶּה ׳הַבַּיִת‎ as ᵐ5‎L Benz) = 2Chr 7:21, of ׳י‎ Psa 97:9.

2 upper Bethhoron, ׳ע‎ Josh 16:5 העליון‎ 1Chr 7:24; 2Chr 8:5, the upper (opposed to lower), of house Neh 3:25; compare 2Kin 15:35; 2Chr 23:20 5t., + Gen 40:17 (uppermost basket); feminine singular הָעֶלְיוֺנָה‎ the upper pool 2Kin 18:17 = Isa 36:2; 7:3; the highest side-chamber (צֵלָע‎) Ezek 41:7; feminine plural הָעֶלְיוֺנֹת‎ 42:5 the upper chambers (לְשָׁכוֺת‎).

3 of Davidic king exalted above monarchs Psa 89:28 (compare Deut 28:1, above).

II. עֶלְיוֺן‎ noun masculine Highest, Most High (probably = foregoing); —

1 name of God Num 24:16; Deut 32:8; Psa 18:14 = 2Sam 22:14; Psa 9:3; 21:8; 46:5; 50:14; 73:11; 77:11; 78:17; Psalm 83:19; 87:5; 91:1; 91:9; 92:2; 107:11; Isa 14:14; Lam 3:35, 38; with other divine names: עליון אל‎ Gen 14:18-19, 20, 22 (see Di) Psa 78:35; עליון יהוה‎ Psalm 7:18; 47:3; עליון אלהים‎ 57:3; 78:56.

5 of rulers, either monarchs or angel-princes, עֶלְיוֺן בְּנֵי‎ = אלהים‎ Psa 82:6.

[עֶלְיוֺן‎] adjective id. (Biblical Hebrew id.); — plural of God, עֶלְיוֺנִין קַדִּישֵׁי‎ (double plural, Buhl, as sometimes Biblical Hebrew, Ges§ 124q Köii. 1. 438 f) Dan 7:18, 22, 25, 27.
