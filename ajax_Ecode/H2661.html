חֲפֹר‎ (chăphôr｜khaf-ore'｜noun｜feminine｜mole)
[Heb] חֲפֹר‎, פֵּרֹות‎, חֲפַרְפָּרָה‎ ETCBC: פֵּרֹות‎ (subs｜[uncertain]) OSHL: h.es.af TWOT: 714a GK: H2923, H7249
Derivation: from חָפַר‎; a hole; only in connection with פֵּרָה‎, which ought rather to be joined as one word, thus חַפַרְפֵּרָה‎; by reduplication from חָפַר‎;
Strong's: a burrower, i.e. probably a rat
KJV: [phrase] mole.
