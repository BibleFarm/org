מוּל‎ (mûwl｜mool｜noun｜masculine｜(over) against)
[Heb] מוּל‎, לְמֹואל‎, מֹואל‎ ETCBC: מוּל‎ (subs｜front) OSHL: m.ay.aa TWOT: 1160 GK: H4346, H4568, H4578 Greek: ἐγγύς, μέρος, συναντάω
Derivation: or מֹול‎; (Deuteronomy 1:1), or מֹואל‎; (Nehemiah 12:38), or מֻל‎; (Numbers 22:5), from מוּל‎;
Strong's: properly, abrupt, i.e. a precipice; by implication, the front; used only adverbially (with prepositional prefix) opposite
KJV: (over) against, before, (fore-) front, from, (God-) ward, toward, with.

I. מוֺל מוּל,‎ ( Deut 1:1), מוֺאל‎ ( Neh 12:38)

substantive and

preposition front, in front of (derivatives dubious; according to Ol§ 223 c Sta§ 378 a, for מְאוֺל‎ from אוּל‎ to be in front: see further Köii. 300 f. HptBAS i. 172): —

1. substantive only 1Kin 7:5 (si vera 1.: see StaZAW 1883, 151) אֶלמֶֿחֱזָה מֶחֱזָה וּמוּל‎ and the front of (one) window was towards (the other) window, and Neh 12:38 לְמוֺאל‎ = in the opposite direction (but read probably לִשְׂמאוֺל‎, compare לַיָּמִין‎ 12:31). 2 as

preposition, Deut 1:1; 2:19 עמון בני מוּל‎ in front of the Ammonites, 3:29 (= 4:46; 34:6) פעור בית מוּל‎, 11:30; Josh 18:18; 19:46 יָפוֺ מוּל‎ 1Sam 14:5 (twice in verse); figurative Exod 18:19 האלהים מול לעם אַתָּה הֱיֵה‎ in front of God, i.e. representing God to them. With prefixes: —

a. אלמֿול‎ towards the front of, 1Sam 17:30 וַיִּסֹב אַחֵר אֶלמֿוּל מֵאֶצְלוֺ‎ to the front of another; on the front of, Exod 34:3 (pregnantly) ההר אלמֿול וְאַליִֿרְעוּ‎, Joel Exodus 8:33 (twice in verse); 9:1; 22:11 (see WAWJPhoenician xiii. 117 ff.); specifically אֶלֿ מְּנֵי מוּל‎ towards or on the forefront of, 26:9; 28:25 (|| 39:18), 39:37; Lev 8:9; Num 8:2-3, 2Sam 11:15 set ye Uriah המלחמה פני אלמֿול‎.

b. מִמּוּל‎: — (a) from the front of, Micah 2:8 (text dubious: read probably מֵעַל‎). (b) of position, off (see מן‎

1c) the front of, Lev 5:8 עָרְמּוֺ מִמּוּל‎ off the front of its neck, i.e. close in front of it, Num 22:5 מִמֻּלִי יושׁב והוא‎ close in front of me, 2Sam 5:23 (1Chr 14:14) להם ובאת בכאים ממול‎ come to them off the front of the mulberry-trees, 1Kin 7:39 (2Chr 4:10) נֶגֶב מִמּוּל‎; מָּנָיו מִמּוּל‎ on the forefront of, Exod 28:27 (|| 39:20).
