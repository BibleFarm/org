H446. Eliab

אֱלִיאָב‎ proper name, masculine (God is father, compare אֲבִיאֵל‎)

a. prince of Zebulon Num 1:9; 2:7; 7:24, 29; 10:16.

b. prince of Reuben Num 16:1, 12; 26:8-9, Deut 11:6.

c. brother of David 1Sam 16:6; 17:3, 28 (twice in verse); 1Chr 2:13; 2Chr 11:18 (compare אֱלִיהוּ‎ 1Chr 27:18).

d. a Kohathite 1Chr 6:12 = אֱלִיאֵל‎ v.6:19 compare אֱלִיהוּ‎ 1Sam 1:1.

e. a Gadite 1Chr 12:9. feminine Levite singer 15:18, 20; 16:5.
