אַחְזַי אֲחַזְיָה, אֲחַזְיָ֫הוּ,‎

proper name, masculine (Yah(u) hath grasped; ᵐ5‎ Ὀχοζ(ε)ιας 1Kin 22:52 etc., compare LagBN 53) **compare Arabic VIII, + = adopt, hence perhaps ׳י‎ hath adopted.

1 king of Israel, son of Ahab 1Kin 22:40, 50, 52; 2Kin 1:18; 2Chr 20:37 (אֲחַזְיָהוּ‎); + 2Kin 1:2; 2Chr 20:35 (אֲחַזְיָה‎).

2 king of Judah, son of Joram 2Kin 8:24-25, 9:21; 10:13; 11:1; 12:19; 13:1; 14:13; 1Chr 3:11; 22:1 15t. 2Kings, 1Chronicles (all אֲחַזְיָהוּ‎); 2Kin 9:16, 23, 27, 29; 11:2 (אֲחַזְיָה‎).

3 אַחְזַי‎ a priest Neh 11:13 (for which יַחְזֵרָה‎ 1Chr 9:12 see SmListen, Tafel).
