יְשׁוּעָה‎ noun feminine salvation; — ׳יְשׁ‎ Psa 119:155 18t.; יְשׁוּעָ֫תָה‎ 3:3; Jonah 2:10; יְשֻׁעָ֫תָה‎ Psa 80:3; construct יְשׁוּעַת‎ Exod 14:13 4t.; suffix יְשֻׁעָתִי‎ Job 30:15; יְשׁוּעָתִי‎ Psa 62:2 12t., + 25 t. suffixes; plural יְשׁוּעוֺת‎ 2Sam 22:51 6t.; יְשׁוּעֹת‎ 2Sam 42:12 3t.; יְשֻׁעוֺת‎ 2Sam 53:7; —

1 welfare, prosperity: ישׁעתי עברה כעב‎ Job 30:15 as a cloud my prosperity passed away.

2 deliverance: לישׁועה לי והיתה‎ and thou wilt be to me for deliverance 2Sam 10:11 = 1Chr 19:12.

3 salvation by God, primarily from external evils, but often with added spiritual idea: Gen 49:18 (poem), Isa 33:2; 52:7, 10; 59:11; 60:18; Jonah 2:10; Psa 3:3; Psalm 3:9; 14:7 = Psalm 53:7; 22:2; 35:3; 62:2; 69:30; 70:5 ( = תשׁועה‎ 40:17), 78:22; 80:3; 91:16; 96:2 = 1Chr 16:23, Psa 106:4; 140:8; Job 13:16; with verbs of rejoicing בישׁועה‎ 1Sam 2:1; Isa 25:9; Psa 9:15; 13:6; 35:9; חוֺמוֺת יָשִׁית יְשׁועָה‎ Isa 26:1 salvation will he set as walls; compare phrase ישׁ ׳אֱלֹהֵי‎ Psa 88:2, ישׁ ׳אֵל‎ Isa 12:2, ישׁ ׳צוּר‎ Deut 32:15 (song), Psa 89:27, compare וְישׁוּעָתִי צוּרִי‎ 62:3; 62:7; ישׁ ׳יוֺם‎ Isa 49:8; הישׁ ׳מַעַיְנֵי‎ 12:3 wells of salvation; plural יְשׁוּעוֺת‎ (intensive), of ׃י מָּנַי ׳יְשׁוּעוֺת‎ Psa 42:6; the salvation of my face (person; ᵑ0 פניו‎ error, ᵑ6 ᵐ5 פני‎), Psalm 42:12; 43:5; ישׁועות כּוֺס‎ 116:13 cup commemorating saving acts (of drink-offering); further 119:123; 119:155; 119:166; 119:174; || צְדָקָה‎ Isa 51:6, 8; 56:1; 62:1; Psa 98:2; 98:3; הָאָרֶץ עַדקְֿצֵה יְשׁוּעָתִי‎ Isa 49:6 my salvation unto the ends of the earth; יְשׁוּעָתֶ֑ךָ בְּכָלאגּוֺיִם‎ Psa 67:3 among all nations thy salvation.

4 victory: with עָשָׂה‎ work victory 1Sam 14:45; Isa 26:18: elsewhere of victories wrought by Yahweh for his people Exod 15:2 (song) Isa 12:2; Hab 3:8; Psa 20:6; 21:2; 21:6; 44:5; 68:20; 118:14; 118:15; 118:21; phrases: יְשׁוּעוֺת פעל‎ do victories 74:12; יְשׁוּעֹת חֹסֶן‎ Isa 33:6 store of victories; יְשׁוּעוֺת מִגְדּוֺל‎ 2Sam 22:51 Qr tower of victories (Kt מַגְדִּיל‎ and so || Psalm 18:51); יְשׁוּעוֺת מָעוֺז‎ Psa 28:8 stronghold of victories; י אֶתיְֿשׁוּעַת ׳רְאוּ‎ Exod 14:13 (J) see the victory of Yahweh, 2Chr 20:17; יְשׁוּעָה כּוֺבַע‎ Isa 59:17 helmet of victory; בִּישׁוּעָה עֲנָוִים יְפָאֵר‎ Psa 149:4 he will beautify the meek with victory. — compare the synonym תְּשׁוּעָה‎ below
