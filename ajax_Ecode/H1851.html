דַּק‎ adjective thin, small, fine — absolute masculine ׳דּ‎ Exod 16:14 4t.; דָּ֑ק‎ Lev 13:30; feminine דַּקָּה‎ 16:12; 1Kin 19:12; plural feminine absolute דַּקּוֺת‎ Gen 41:6 2t.; דַּקֹּת‎ 41:24; construct דַּקּוֺת‎ 41:3, דַּקֹּת‎ 41:4; —

1 thin, of kine ׳דּ בָּשָׂר‎ Gen 41:3 compare 41:4 (⅏‎ has in both רַקּוֺת‎ compare ᵑ0‎ 41:19; 41:20; 41:27); of ears of corn 41:6; 41:7; 41:23; 41:24 (all E); thin, shrunk, withered, of man Lev 21:20 (H).

2 small, fine, of the manna כַּכְּפֹר דַּק מְחֻסְמָּס דַּק‎ Exod 16:14; of incense Lev 16:12, hair 13:30 (all P); of dust דַּק אָבָק‎ Isa 29:5; in simile of isles in hand of יִטּוֺל כַּדַּק אִיִּים ׳י,‎ 40:15 (דַּק‎ = substantive fine thing, fine dust); once of a low whisper דַקָּה דְּמָמָה קוֺל‎ 1Kin 19:12.
