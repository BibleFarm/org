H5682. Abarim

עֲבָרִים‎ proper name, of a location 'Abarîm (properly regions beyond river or sea); — usually הָע ׳הַר‎ Num 27:12; Deut 32:49 ( = הַלנְֿבוֺ‎; both P), and הָע ׳הָרֵי‎ Deuteronomy 33:47 (נְבוֺ לִפְנֵי‎), Deuteronomy 33:48 (P); alone only מֵעֲבָרִים צַעֲקִי‎ Jer 22:20; the mountainous district in northwest Moab, just northeast of Dead Sea, GASmGeogr. 548 and EBi BuhlGeogr. 122; ᵐ5‎ Αβαρειμ(+?), but τὸ ἐν τῷ πέραν Num 33:47, εἰς τὸ πέραν τῆς θαλάσσης Jer 22:20. — See also below עִיִּים‎.

עֲבָרִים‎
