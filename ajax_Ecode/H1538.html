H1538. gulgoleth

גֻּלְגֹּ֫לֶת‎ noun feminine skull, head, poll (person) (on formation compare BaNB 205; Aramaic גּוּלְגַּלְתָּא‎) — absolute ׳ג‎ 2Kin 9:35 3t.; suffix גֻּלְגָּלְתּוֺ‎ Judg 9:53; 1Chr 10:10; plural suffix גֻּלְגְּלֹתָם‎ Num 1:2 5t.; —

1 skull, as broken by a stone Judg 9:53; as severed from body 2Kin 9:35; 1Chr 10:10.

2 head, poll (in counting, taxing, etc.; only P and late) לַגּ ׳עֹמֶר‎ Exod 16:16 an omer for every man; compare 38:26; Num 1:2, 18, 20, 22; 3:47; 1Chr 23:3, 24.
