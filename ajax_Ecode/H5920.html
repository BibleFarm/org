עָ֑ל עַל,‎ (compare Köii. 261 f.)

I. substantive height (poetry) Hosea 7:16 עָֹל לֹא יָשׁוּבוּ‎ they return (but) not upwards (i.e. not God-wards), 11:7 יִקְרָאֻהוּ וְאֶלעַֿל‎ they (the prophets) call it (the people) upwards, (but) none striveth to rise; as adverb accusative, 2Sam 23:1 the man עָ֔ל הֻ֣קַּם‎ (that) is raised up on high. With מֵעָ֑ל מִן,‎, מֵעָ֑ל (ה)שׁמים‎ (the) heavens above Gen 27:39; 49:25; Psa 50:4 (opposed to מִתַּחַת‎ Exod 20:4. In prose מִמַּעַל‎).
