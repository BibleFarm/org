H436. elon

I. אֵלוֺן‎ noun [feminine] terebinth (= אֵלָה‎) — ׳א‎ construct Gen 12:6 4t.; plural construct אֵלוֺנֵי‎ 13:18 3t. — terebinth (or other tall tree, compare below on 1Sam 10:3; Judg 4:5), marking shrine, & hence used in topographic designations; ׳א מוֺרֶה‎ (teacher's terebinth) Gen 12:6 (see Di) so Deut 11:30; ׳א מְעוֺנְנִים‎ (eonjurors' tereb.) Judg 9:37; distinguished by owner or ruler ׳א מַמְרֵא‎ Gen 13:18; 14:13; 18:1; by neighbouring town (ב)צענים‎ Judg 4:11; compare ׳א בְּצַעֲנַנִּים‎ Josh 19:33 (editions אַלּוֺן‎, but see Norzi Baer Di.); ׳אֵ בִּשְׁכֶם אֲשֶׁר מֻצָּב‎ Judg 9:6 (compare אֵלָה‎ Gen 35:4); ׳א תָּבוֺר‎ 1Sam 10:3 read ׳א דְּבוֺרָה‎ (& compare בָּכוּת אַלּוֺן‎ Gen 35:8, & דְּבוֺרָה תֹּמֶר‎ Judg 4:5) compare EwGeschichte iii. 31 Th Di Gen 35:8 (see also below אַלּוֺן‎).
