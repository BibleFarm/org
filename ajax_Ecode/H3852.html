H3852. lehabah

or lahebeth לֶהָבָה‎ noun feminine id.; — ׳ל‎ Num 21:28 13t., לַהֶ֫בֶת‎ absolute Job 15:30; construct Ezek 21:3; לַבַּת‎ Exod 3:3 (= לַהְבַּת‎; Samaritan להבת‎, see Di); plural לָהָבוֺת‎ Psa 105:32; construct לַהֲבוֺת‎ 29:7; —

1 flame, in poetry (|| or + אֵשׁ‎, except Psa 29:7; Dan 11:33); of fire consuming (לָהַט‎) Dathan, etc. Psa 106:18 (compare Num 16:35); wasting (לָהַט‎) mountains Num 83:15; consuming (בָּעֵר אָכַל,‎) chaff (in simile) Isa 5:24; of lightning (with hail) ל ׳אשׁ‎ Psa 105:32 (compare Exod 9:24), Psa 29:7 ׳ל אֵשּׁ‎ (on text of see compare Che); simile of judgment, ל ׳אֵשׁ‎ Lam 2:3 (verb אָכַל‎) Hosea 7:6 (verb כָּעֵר‎) Isa 47:14 ל ׳(מִיַד ׅ‎; compare 10:17; Obadiah 18; ׳ל ׳שַׁלְה‎ Ezek 21:3; figurative of drought Joel 1:19, of locusts' ravages 2:3 (verb לָהַט‎ in both); of war Num 21:28; Jer 48:4 (both verb אָכַל‎); compare Dan 11:33 ׅ‎ || (חֶרֶב‎; of calamity Isa 43:2; symbolic ל אֵשׁ ׳נֹגַהּ‎ of ׳י‎s presence 4:5 (compare Exod 13:21); אֵשׁ לַבַּת‎ 3:2.

2 point, head of spear 1Sam 17:7.
