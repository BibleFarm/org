a. common drink, for refreshment Gen 14:18 (E?) 27:25 (J), Judg 19:19; 2Sam 16:2; Amos 5:11; 9:14; Hosea 14:8; Job 1:13, 18; Dan 1:5 +, tonic Prov 31:6; article of commerce Ezek 27:18; Neh 13:15; among supplies in strongholds 2Chr 11:11; as making merry 2Sam 13:28; Zech 9:15; 10:7; Est 1:10; Eccl 9:7; 10:19; Psa 104:15.
