H3059. Yehoachaz

יְוֺאָחָז יְהוֺאָחָז,‎ proper name, masculine (׳י‎ hath grasped; ᵐ5‎ Ἰωαχας, & Ἰωαχαζ; compare also אֲהַזְיָהוּ‎, & cuneiform Ia-u-—a-zi for Ahaz, see אָחָז‎, and compare further JägerBAS i. 467 f.) —

1 יְהוֺאָהָז‎ king of Judah, son of Josiah 2Kin 23:30-31, 34; 2Chr 36:1, = יוֺאָהָז‎ 36:2; 36:4 (apparently = יוֺהָנָן‎ 1Chr 3:15).

2 יְהוֺאָחָז‎ king of Israel, son of Jehu 2Kin 10:35; 13:1, 4, 7, 8, 9, 10, 22, 25 (twice in verse); 14:8; 14:17; 2Chr 25:17, 25 = יוֺאָחָז‎ 2Kin 14:1.

3 יְהוֺאָחָז‎ king of Judah, son of Jehoram of Judah 2Chr 21:17; 25:23 = אֲחַזְיָהוּ‎ (אֲחַזְיָה‎)

2, q. v.

4 יוֺאָחָז‎ father of יוֺאָח‎ Josiah's chronicler 2Chr 34:8.
