זַלְעָפָה‎ noun feminine raging heat (on formation compare GesLbg 863 Ol§ 196 a. 1. 2) — ׳ז‎ Psa 119:53; plural absolute זִלְעָפוֺת‎ 11:6; construct זִלְעֲפוֺת‎ Lam 5:10; —

1 of fever heat of famine ׳ז רָעָב‎ Lam 5:10.

2 of burning wind ז ׳רוּח‎ Psa 11:6 (in figurative of ׳י‎s judgment, with גָּפְרִית אֵשׁ,‎).

3 figurative of zeal ׳ז אֲחָזַתְנִי‎ Psa 119:53.
