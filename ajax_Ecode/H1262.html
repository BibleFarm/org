בָּרָה‎ (bârâh｜baw-raw'｜verb｜eat)
[Heb] בָּרָה‎, בָּרָא‎ ETCBC: ברה‎ (verb｜[uncertain]), ברה‎ (verb｜eat) OSHL: b.cz.aa TWOT: 281 GK: H1346, H1356, H1357 Greek: βρῶσις, ἐκλέγομαι, ἐσθίω, συνεσθίω, ψωμίζω
Derivation: a primitive root; to select; also (as denominative from בָּר‎) to feed; also (as equivalent to בָּרַר‎) to render clear (Ecclesiastes 3:18)
Strong's: to select; to feed; to render clear
KJV: choose, (cause to) eat, manifest, (give) meat.
