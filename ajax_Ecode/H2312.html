חֵ֑דֶק‎ noun [masculine] brier (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic חִדְקָא‎ Löwp. 147, No. 104; compare Arabic a kind of night-shade, see Lane532); — כַּחֵדֶק טוֺבָם‎ Micah 7:4 the best of them (si vera lectio) is like the brier (|| מִמְּסוּכָה יָשָׁר‎); חָ֑דֶק כִּמְשֻׂכַת עָצֵל דֶּרֶךְ‎ Prov 15:19 the way of a sluggard is like a brier-hedge.
