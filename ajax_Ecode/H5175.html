a. as biting, Amos 5:19; Eccl 10:8, in spite of charm (לַחַשׁ‎) 10:11 [compare Ecculs 12:13], Jer 8:17 (+ צִפְעֹנִים‎; figurative of enemies); so הַשְׁרָפִים הַנְּחָשִׁים‎ Num 21:6 (deadly; J E; compare JacobArab.
