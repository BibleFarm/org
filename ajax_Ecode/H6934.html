H6934. Qadmiel

קַדְמִיאֵל‎ proper name, masculine Καδμιηλ, ᵐ5‎L Κεδμιηλ; Levite name (El is the ancient one; compare Sabean אלקדם‎ DHMZMG xxxvii (1883), 374); — Ezra 2:40 = Neh 7:43; Ezra 3:9; Neh 9:4-5, 10:10; 12:8, 24.

קָדְקֹד‎ see II. קדד‎.
