H7810. shochad

שֹׁ֫חַד‎ noun masculine present, specifically bribe; — ׳שׁ‎ absolute Isa 1:23 22t.; — bribe, usually to pervert justice, object of לָקַח‎ Exod 23:8 a (E), = Deut 16:19a, 10:17; 1Sam 8:3; 2Chr 19:7; Psa 15:5; Prov 17:23; + infinitive, take bribe to do something, Deut 27:25; Ezek 22:12; שׁ ׳אָהַב‎ Isa 1:23; compare also 5:23; 33:15; Exod 23:8b (E) = Deut 16:19b, Micah 3:11; Psa 26:10; Prov 17:8; 21:14; ׳אָהֳלֵישֿׁ‎ Job 15:34, i.e. abode of bribe-givers; ׳שׁ‎ Prov 6:35 (|| כֹּפֶר‎) is hush-money, or (possibly) legal compensation (compare Toy); ׳שׁ‎ elsewhere (with שָׁהַ‎) of bribing king to take sides 1Kin 15:19; 2Kin 16:8, compare Isa 45:13 (|| מְחִיר‎).
