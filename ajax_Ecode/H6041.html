עָנִי‎ (ʻânîy｜aw-nee'｜adjective｜poor)
[Heb] עָנִי‎ ETCBC: עָנִי‎ (adjv｜humble) OSHL: p.dv.ae TWOT: 1652d GK: H6714 Greek: ἀδικία, ἀδύνατος, ἀσθενής, ἐκπίπτω, κάκωσις, ναῦς, πένης, πενιχρός, πρᾷος, πραΰς, πτωχός, ταπεινός
Derivation: from עָנָה‎; (practically the same as עָנָו‎, although the margin constantly disputes this, making עָנָו‎ subjective and objective);
Strong's: depressed, in mind or circumstances
KJV: afflicted, humble, lowly, needy, poor.

H6041. ani

עָנִי‎ adj poor, afflicted, humble; — ׳ע‎ Deut 24:12 51t.; plural עֲיִּים‎ Isa 3:15 6t.; construct עֲנִיֵּי‎ 10:2 4t. (see also Kt and Qr below עָנָו‎ above); suffix עֲנִיֶּיךָ‎ Psa 72:2; 74:19, עֲנִיֶּ֑ךָ‎ Deut 15:11, עֲנִיָּו‎ Isa 49:13; feminine singular עֲנִיָּה‎ 51:21; 54:11 ( + 10:30 ᵑ0‎, but read עֲנִיהָ‎, see I. ענה‎); —

1 poor, needy, || אביון‎ Deut 15:11; 24:14. 15 Prov 31:20, || יתום‎ Job 24:9; Exod 22:24 (E), Deut 24:12; having right to gleanings Lev 19:10; 23:22 (H); ע ׳יְמֵי‎ Prov 15:15, compare 14:21 (Kt).

2 poor and weak, oppressed by the rich and powerful Isa 3:14-15, 32:7 (Qr), 58:7; 29:12; 36:6, 15; Prov 30:14; Eccl 6:8; Ezek 18:17; Zech 7:10; Job 24:4, also Amos 8:4 (Qr); || דל‎ Job 34:28; Prov 22:22; עַמִּי עֲנִיֵּי‎ Isa 10:2; ואביוך עני‎ Job 24:14 Prov 31:9 Jer 22:16; Ezek 16:49; 18:12; 22:29.

3 poor, weak, afflicted Israel, or pious in Israel afflicted by wicked nations or the wicked in Israel itself Psa 10:2; 10:9 (twice in verse); 14:6 (dubious, compare 53:6) 102:1 (or proper name), Isa 14:32; Hab 3:14; of Zion, עֲנִיָּה‎ Isa 51:21; 54:11; ע ׳שׁד‎ Psa 12:6; וע ׳יחיד‎ 25:16; ואביון עני‎ 35:10; 37:14; Psalm 40:18 = Psalm 70:6; 74:21; 86:1; 109:16; 109:22, plural Isa 41:17; וְכוֺאֵב עני‎ Psa 69:30; ׳ע וָרָשׁ‎ 82:3; ע ׳עֱנוּת‎ 22:25; ׳ע וְגֹוֵעַ‎ 88:16; || דל‎ Isa 26:6; Zeph 3:12; ׳ע וּנְכֵהרֿוּחַ‎ Isa 66:2; — Zech 11:7, 11 see II. כְּנַעֲנִי‎; — God does not forget them Psa 9:13 (Kt); 9:19 (Qr); 10:12 (Kt) 74:19, but has compassion on them Isa 49:13, saves Psa 34:7, delivers 35:10, and bestows various favours 68:11; 140:13, the king also judges 72:2; 72:4, and delivers 72:12.

4 humble, lowly, Zech 9:9 (victorious king); opposed to לֵצִים‎ Prov 3:34 (Kt); opposed to גֵּאִים‎ 16:19 (Kt); עני עם‎ opposed to עינים רמות‎ Psa 18:28 = 2Sam 22:28.
