H6806. tsaad

[צַ֫עַד‎] noun masculine2Sam 6:13 step, pace; — absolute צָ֑עַד‎ Prov 30:29; suffixצַעֲדוֺ‎ Jer 10:3; Prov 6:9, etc.; plural construct צַעֲדֵי‎ Job 18:7; suffix צְעָדַי‎ 31:4, etc.; —

1 literally step, pace, 2Sam 6:13, תַּחְתֵּנִי צַעֲדִי תַּרְחִיב‎ 22:37 = Psa 18:37, i.e. givest me firm footing, צ ׳מֵטִיבֵי‎ Prov 30:29 = making a good step, stately in march; Lam 4:18 (|| לָ֑כֶת‎).

2 step, steps, in figure of course of life, fortune: singular צ יֵצַר ׳לֹא‎ Prov 4:12; || דֶּרֶךְ‎ Jer 10:23; Prov 16:9; Job 14:16; 31:4; 34:21, אוֺנוֺ צַעֲדֵי‎ 18:7 (compare Prov 4:12 above); אַגִּידֶנּוּ מִסְמַּרעָֿדַי‎ Proverbs 31:37; of harlot 5:5 figurative, יִתְמֹ֑כוּ צְעָדֶיהָ שְׁאוֺל‎.
