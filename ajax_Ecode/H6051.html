H6051. anan

I. עָנָן‎87 noun masculineExod 19:16 cloud-mass, cloud; — ׳(הֶ)ע‎ absolute 19:9 +; construct עֲנַזִ‎ Hosea 6:4 +; suffix עֲנָֽנְךָ‎ Num 14:14, עֲנָנוֺ‎ Job 26:9; 37:15; plural עֲנִים‎ Jer 4:13; —

1 cloud-mass:

a. especially of theophanic cloud (58 t.), chiefly at Exodus in J E (less often P), usually הֶע ׳עַמּוּד‎ Exod 13:21-22, + (see עַמּוּד‎), but also ׳ע‎ alone 34:5; compare 14:20 (J), Num 10:34; 11:35; 14:14 (all J E); ׳עַבהֶֿע‎ Exod 19:9, ׳ע כָּבֵד‎ 19:16 (E); ׳ע‎ in P 16:10; 24:15 23t.; also Deut 1:33; 4:11; 5:19; Psa 78:14; 105:39; in temple 1Kin 8:10-11, 2Chr 5:13-14, compare Ezek 1:4; 10:3-4, hence in General Psa 97:2, as symbol of protection Isa 4:5; as a barrier Lam 3:44; compare ׳וע רַגְלָיו אֲבַק‎ Nahum 1:3.

b. of rain-bow cloud Gen 9:13-14, (twice in verse); 9:16 (P), Ezek 1:28 (simile).

c. עֲנַןבֹּֿקֶר‎ Hosea 6:4; 13:3 (simile of transitoriness; on phenomenon in Palestine see ChaplinPEQ, 1883, 19), compare Isa 44:22; Job 7:9 (both id.); simile of invasion Ezek 38:9, 16, plural Jer 4:13.

d. poetic in various connection Job 26:8-9, 38:9; with אוֺר‎ as thunder-cloud 37:11, 15.

e. symbolic of gloom Ezek 30:18; 32:7; ע ׳יוֺם וַעֲרָפֶל‎ Zeph 1:15; Ezek 34:12; Joel 2:2, compare Ezek 30:3 (all of day of ׳ע‎).

2 ׳ע קְטֹרֶת‎ Lev 16:13 (P) cloud of incense, so Ezek 8:11 (strike out ᵐ5 ׳ע‎ Co Berthol, not Toy).
