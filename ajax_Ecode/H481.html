[Heb] אָלַם‎ ETCBC: אלם‎ (verb｜bind) OSHL: a.dr.ab TWOT: 102 GK: H519, H520 Greek: δεσμεύω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to tie fast; hence (of the mouth) to be tongue-tied
KJV: bind, be dumb, put to silence.

H481. alam

[אָלַם‎] verb bind (perhaps compare Assyrian [al¹mu], almattu, fortress Dl in ZimBP 114 & in BDEzek.:xi; Arabic be in pain, Aramaic retain anger).

Niph`al (be bound =) be dumb, Perfect3feminine singular נֶאֱלָ֑מָה‎ Isa 53:7; וְנֶאֱלַמְתָּ֫‎ Ezek 3:26, etc.; Imperfect2masculine singular תֵּאָלֵם‎ 24:27; 3feminine plural תֵּאָלַמְנָה‎ Psa 31:19. — 1 be dumb, i.e. silent 39:3 ׳נ דוּמִיָּה‎ (|| הֶחֱשֵׁיתִי‎); compare 39:10 (|| אֶפְתַּחמִּֿי לֹא‎); simile of sheep Isa 53:7 (|| id.); be dumb, i.e. unable to speak Ezek 3:26 (result of אֶלחִֿכָּ֑ךָ אַדְבִּיק וּלְשׁוֺנְךָ‎) compare 24:27 עוֺד תֵאָלֵם וְלֹא וּתְדַבֵּר ֗֗֗ מִּיךָ יִמָּתַח‎; 33:22 עוֺד נֶאֱלַמְתִּי וְלֹא מִּי וַיִּפְתַּח‎; also Dan 10:15 (compare 10:16); be made dumb subject שָׁ֑קֶר שִׂפְתֵי‎ lying lips Psa 31:19 (|| לִשְׁאוֺל יִדְּמוּ‎ 31:18).
