יָעָה‎ (yâʻâh｜yaw-aw'｜verb｜sweep together)
[Heb] יָעָה‎ ETCBC: יעה‎ (verb｜sweep) OSHL: j.cc.aa TWOT: 879 GK: H3589
Derivation: a primitive root; apparently
Strong's: to brush aside
KJV: sweep away.

H3261. yaah

יָעָה‎ verb sweep together (with collateral idea of carrying away) (Arabic is collect, gather) —

Qal Perfect וְיָעָה‎ consecutive Isa 28:17 subject בָּרָד‎ with accusative (ב ׳ויעה כָזָב מַחְסֵה‎). **Compare also Arabic vessel Gen 42:25 Saad, compare BaldenspergerPEF 1904, 53, who compares Modern Arabic wa±â ().
