Strong's: a height; also a sieve

H5299. naphah

I. [נָפָה‎] noun feminine sieve or other winnowing implement, Di Du Schwinge, CheHpt fan (as swung); — only construct שָׁוְא בְּנָ֫פַת גוֺיִם לַהֲנָפָה‎ Isa 30:28 to swing nations in a sieve of worthlessness.

II. [נָפָה‎] noun feminine height; — only construct in combination דּוֺר נָפַת‎ Josh 12:23 = ׳נ דּאֹר‎ 1Kin 4:11, plural construct ד ׳נָפוֺת‎ Josh 11:2; compare II.דּוֺד‎, and Di11:2; see also [נֶפֶת‎].
