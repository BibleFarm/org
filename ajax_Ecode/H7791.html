H7791. shur

II. שׁוּר‎ noun [masculine] wall (Aramaic שׁוּרָא‎, ); — Gen 49:22 (poem in J), 2Sam 22:30 = Psa 18:30; plural suffix שׁוּרֹתָם‎ Job 24:11 their walls De Di De, compare Bu; but see שׁוּרָה‎ infra; שָׁרוֺתֶיהָ‎ Jer 5:10 = its walls, according to Vrss AV RV Gf Gie, read then ׳שֻׁ‎, but see id.

[שׁוּרָה‎] noun feminine probably row of olives or vines (so Du Buhl and others; compare Late Hebrew שׁוּרָה‎, Jewish-Aramaic שׁוּרְתָא‎ row, whence modern Arabic as loan-word, compare VogelstLand wirthschaft 41 f.); — plural suffix שׁוּרֹתְם‎ Job 24:11 (al. II. שׁוּר‎); Bu and Du strike out suffix; here also Jer 5:10 (reading ׳שֻׁ‎) Du Buhl Dr, her (vine-)rows, but see II. שׁוּר‎.
