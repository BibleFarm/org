H2970. Yaazanyahu

or Yaazanyah יַאֲזַנְיָ֫הוּ‎ proper name, masculine (Yahweh heareth) — so 2Kin 25:23; Ezek 8:11; יַאֲזַנְיָה‎ Jer 35:3; Ezek 11:1; contracted (וִ)יזַנְיָהוּ‎ Jer 40:8, (וִ)יזַנְיָה‎ 42:1 —

1 a JudÊan 2 Kings 25:33; Jer 40:8.

2 an elder of Israel, son of Shaphan Ezek 8:11.

3 son of Jeremiah Jer 35:3.

4 a leading JudÊan, son of Azur Ezek 11:1.

5 a leading JudÊan Jer 42:1 (= עֲזַרְיָה‎ 43:2).
