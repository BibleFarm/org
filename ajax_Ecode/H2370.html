חֲזָה‎ verb see, behold (see Biblical Hebrew); —

Pe`al Perfect3masculine singular ׳ח‎ Dan 4:20; 5:1-2;masculine singular תֲוַיְתָ‎ 2:43 +, תָה‎- 2:41.2: 41; 1singular חֲזֵית‎ 2:46 +; 2 masculine plural תֲוֵיתוּן‎ 2:8;

a. accusative of person Dan 3:25, with ל‎ 3:27, accusative of thing 5:5.

4 passive = customary (seemly) Dan 3:19 (so ᵑ7‎ Lev 5:10 and elsewhere, see Dr).
