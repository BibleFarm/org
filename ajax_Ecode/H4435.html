H4435. Milkah

מִלְכָּה‎ proper name, feminine ᵐ5‎ Μελχα (= מַלְכָּה‎ ? NöZMG xlii. 1888. 484 compare Phoenician proper name, of deities מלכת‎; compare GrayProp. N. 116); —

1 wife of Nahor Gen 11:29 (twice in verse); 22:20, 23; 24:15, 24, 47 (all J).

2 daughter of Zelo-phehad (of Manasseh), Num 26:33; 27:1; 36:11; Josh 17:3 (all P). — Perhaps original town-name, Grayl.c.
