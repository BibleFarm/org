H1835. Dan

דָּן‎ proper name, masculine (judge) —

1 son of Jacob and Bilhah Gen 30:6; 35:25; Exod 1:4; Josh 19:47; Judg 18:29; 1Chr 2:2.

2 the tribe of Dan, דָן מַטֵּה‎ Exod 31:6; 35:34; 38:23; Lev 24:11; Num 1:39; 13:12; Josh 21:5, 23 (all P); דָן בְּנֵי‎ Gen 46:23; Num 1:38; 2:25; 7:66; 10:25; 26:42; 34:22; Josh 19:40, 47 (twice in verse); 19:48 (all P) Judg 1:34; 18:2, 16, 22, 23, 25, 26, 30; דָן מַחֲנֵה‎ Num 2:25, 31 (P) — on Judg 13:25; 18:12 see מַחֲנֶה‎; דָּן מִשְׁמְּחֹת‎ Num 26:42 (P); דָּן‎ alone in poetry Gen 49:16-17, Deut 33:22 (twice in verse); Judg 5:17, elsewhere Num 1:12 (P) Deut 27:13; 1Chr 27:22; 2Chr 2:13; Ezek 48:1-2, 32.

3 a city on northern frontier of Israel at one of the sources of the Jordan, originally לַיִשׁ‎ Judg 18:29 (compare 18:7); = לֶשֶׁם‎ Josh 19:47; captured by a colony of Danites; named Dan, and made a sacred place with rites of worship that lasted until the exile (Judg 18:30); = Tell el Kâdî, RobBR iii. 351, 358 BdPa1 265. This place is frequently referred to: Gen 14:14; Deut 34:1; 1Kin 12:29-30, 15:20; 2Kin 10:29; 2Chr 16:4; Jer 4:15; 8:16; Amos 8:14; read also וּבְדָן‎ for וְכֵן‎ 2Sam 20:18 ᵐ5‎ Ew We compare Dr; and see the phrase שֶׁבַע וְעַדבְּֿאֵר מִדָּן‎ Judg 20:1; 1Sam 3:20; 2Sam 3:10; 17:11; 24:2, 15; 1Kin 5:5; given by Chronicles וְעַדדָּֿן שֶׁבַע מִבְּאֵר‎ 1Chr 21:2; 2Chr 30:5. (Ezek 27:19 see ודן‎.)

Note. — יַעַן דָּ֫נָה‎ 2Sam 24:6. As no such place is known, the text is usually regarded as corrupt, and, ᵐ5‎ being uncertain, is variously changed: (1) יַעַר דָּ֫נָה‎ to Dan in the wood, Thes after ᵑ9‎ silvestria. (2) (וַיָּסֹבּוּ‎) וְעִיּוֺן דָּ֫נָה‎ to Dan and Ijon Klo (compare 1Kin 15:20). (3) (סָֽבֲבוּ‎) וּמִדָּן דָּן‎ We Dr compare ᵐ5‎.
