H4189. moshekah

or mosheketh [משֶׁ֫כֶת‎] noun feminine cord; — only plural construct כְּסִיל משְׁכוֺת‎ Job 38:31 the cords of Orion, i.e. probably those by which (according to some legend) he is dragged along in the sky (compare Di).
