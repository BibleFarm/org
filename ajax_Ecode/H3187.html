H3187. yachas

[הִתְיַחֵשׂ‎] verb Hithpa`el denominative enroll oneself or be enrolled by Genealogy (Late Hebrew often in Pi`el (also Hithpa`el), Aramaic Pa`el (Ithpa`al)) — Perfect3masculine plural הִתְיַחֲשׂוּ‎ 1Chr 5:17; 9:1; Infinitive construct הִתְיַחֵשׂ‎ 5:1 5t.; suffix הִתְיִחֲשָׂם‎ 4:33 7t.; Participle plural הַמִּתְיִחֲשִׂים‎ Ezra 2:62; Neh 7:64; — be enrolled by Genealogy 1Chr 5:17; 9:1; Neh 7:5; perhaps also ׳לְהִתְי‎2Chr 12:15 (tr. to 11:16 according to Hi Be, see VB); לְהִתְי ׳וְלֹא לַבְּכֹרָה‎ 1Chr 5:1 but he is not to be enrolled in the place of first born; followed by בְּ‎ (among, of, consisting in) 7:40, without לְ‎ 7:7; Ezra 8:1; ׳הִתְי הַכֹּהֲנִים‎2Chr 31:17; Infinitive = Genealogical enrolment 1Chr 4:33 these were their dwellings and they had (לָהֶם‎) genealogical enrolment, compare 7:5, 9; ׳בְּהִתְי‎ 5:7 at the enrolment, of. (לְ‎) their Generations; הִתְיַחֲשָׂם בְּחַצְרֵיהֶם הֵמָּה‎ 9:22 as for them, in their villages was their enrolment; nearly = genealogical list2Chr 31:15 (לִזְכָרִים‎; i.e. the males whose names were in the list), so Ezra 8:3, compare 2Chr 31:18-19, (all followed by בְּ‎ among, of) Participle plural with article הַמִּתְיַחֲשִׂים כְתָבָם בִּקְשׁוּ‎ Ezra 2:62 = Neh 7:64 they sought their writing (their book), namely the enrolled, i.e. their Genealogical record.
