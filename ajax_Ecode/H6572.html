H6572. parshegen

or pathshegen מַּרְשֶׁ֫גֶן‎ noun masculine copy (loan-word from Perssian through Aramaic, see Biblical Aramaic); — construct ׳פ הַנִּשְׁתְּוָן‎ Ezra 7:11, compare מַּתְשֶׁגֶן‎.

מַּתְשֶׁ֫גֶן‎ noun masculine copy ( = מַּרְשֶׁנֶן‎ q. v.); — construct ׳מּ (הַ)כְּתָב‎ Est 3:14; 4:8; 8:13.
