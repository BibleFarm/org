H6738. tsel

צֵל‎ noun masculinePsa 144:4 shadow; — ׳צ‎ absolute 2Kin 20:9 +, construct Gen 19:8 +; suffix צִלְךָ‎ Psa 121:5, צִלּוֺ‎ Ezek 31:6 +, also צִלְלוֺ‎ Job 40:22, etc.; plural צְלָלִים‎ Song 2:17; 4:6; construct צִלְלֵי‎ Jer 6:4; —

1 shadow on dial 2Kin 20:9-10, (twice in verse); 20:11 || Isa 38:8; צִלְלֶיעָֿ֑רֶב‎ Jer 6:4, compare Song 2:17; 4:6; of mountain Judg 9:36.

2 shadow, shade, as protection (from sun): of branches, Judg 9:15 (in allegory), Hosea 4:13; Job 40:22; Jonah 4:5-6, and (in figurative) Hosea 14:8; Ezek 17:23; 31:6, 12, 17; Psa 80:11; Song 2:3 (of refreshment, delight); of rock Isa 32:2 (simile); ׳צ קֹרָתִי‎ Gen 19:8 shadow of my roof; of cloud Isa 25:4 and (figurative of ׳י‎s protection) 4:6; 25:4, so probably 16:3; ׳צ כְּנָפֶיךָ‎ shadow of thy wings, figurative of ׳י‎'s protection Psa 17:8; 36:8; 57:2; 63:8; shade, in General, Job 7:2, figurative of ׳י‎ Psa 121:5; hence = protection, defence, of city (wall) Jer 48:45; of ׳י‎ Num 14:9 (JE), Psa 91:1 (see also proper name בְּצַלְאֵל‎), compare יָדוֺ צֵל‎ Isa 49:2 (covering prophet, under figure of sword), 51:16 (covering people); of human ally 30:2-3, of king Lam 4:20; also of wisdom Eccl 7:12, money 7:12. — בְּצִלָּהּ‎ Isa 34:15 apparently reference to קִמּוֺז‎, but improbable; read perhaps בֵּצֶיהָ‎ her eggs Du CheHpt (CheHeb Hpt transpose וְדָֽגְרָה וּבָֽקְעָה‎).

3 shadow as symbol of transitoriness of life Job 8:9; 14:2; Psa 144:4 (עוֺבֵר כְּצֵל‎), Eccl 6:12; 8:13; נָטוּי כְּצֵל‎ of an individual Life near its end Psa 102:12, So כִּנְטוֺתוֺ כְּצֵל‎ 109:23; in simile of emaciated members, כַּצֵּל וִיצֻרַי‎ Job 17:7.
