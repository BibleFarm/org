H2438. Chiram

חִירָם‎ proper name, masculine Hiram (Phoenician חרם‎; abbreviated from foregoing) — ׳ח‎ 2Sam 5:11 18t. 1Kings + Kt 1Chr 14:1; 2Chr 4:11; 9:10 (all Qr חוּרָם‎); חִירוֺם‎ 1 Kings 5:24; 1 Kings 5:32; 1Kin 7:40; also חוּרָם‎ 1Chr 8:5 9t. Chronicles —

1 king of Tyre, contemporary of David & Solomon 2Sam 5:11; 1Kin 5:15 (twice in verse); 5:16; 1 Kings 5:21; 1 Kings 5:22; 1 Kings 5:24; 1 Kings 5:25 (twice in verse); 1 Kings 5:26; 1 Kings 5:32; 9:11 (twice in verse); 9:12, 14, 27; 10:11-12, 1Chr 14:1; 2Chr 2:2; 2:10; 2:11; 8:2, 18; 9:10, 21.

2 an artificer of Tyre, sent by Hiram the king 1Kin 7:13, 40 (twice in verse); 7:45; 2Chr 2:12; 4:11 (twice in verse); 4:16.

3 a Benjamite 1Chr 8:5.
