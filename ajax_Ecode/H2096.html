H2096. zohar

זֹ֫הַר‎ noun [masculine] shining, brightness (Arabic , Late Hebrew זְהוֺר‎, compare Aramaic זִיהֲרָא‎); — absolute Ezek 8:2 ז ׳כְּמַרְאֵה‎ like an appearance of shining (explained, vb, as resembling amber, חַשְׁמַל‎ compare 1:27); also construct Dan 12:3 see foregoing.
