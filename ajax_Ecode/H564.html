H564. Immer

אִמֵּר‎ proper name, masculine 1. a priest assigned to David's time 1Chr 24:14 perhaps =

2 ancestor of priest 1Chr 9:12 compare Neh 11:13; Ezra 2:37; 10:20; Neh 7:40.

3 priest in Jeremiah's time Jer 20:1.

4 father of Zadok Neh 3:29.

5. proper name, of a location (?) Ezra 2:59 = Neh 7:61.
