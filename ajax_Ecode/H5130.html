H5130. nuph

I. [נוּף‎] verb move to and fro, wave, besprinkle (Late Hebrew Hiph`il, Pilpal wave, blow, fan, compare נָפָה‎ sift, נָפָה‎ noun sieve (as Biblical Hebrew); ᵑ7‎ Aph`el as Biblical Hebrew; Syriac bend, wave, shake; Di compare Ethiopic quadril. distil, drop like dew; sift); —

Qal Perfect1singular וגו מֹר מִשְׁכָּבִי ׳נַ֫פְתִּי‎ Prov 7:17 I have besprinkled my couch with myrrh, etc.

Po`lel Imperfect3masculine singular וגו הַר יָדוֺ ׳יְנֹפֵף‎ Isa 10:32 he brandisheth his hand toward the mountain (on accusative הַר‎ compare Ges§ 118f. DaSynt. § 69, R 2).

Hiph`il Perfect3masculine singular הֵנִיף‎ Josh 8:31 +, 2 masculine singular הֵנַ֫פְתָּ‎ Exod 20:25 (Ges§ 72k), וְהֵנַפְתָּ֫‎ 29:24 3t.; 1 singular הֲנִיפֹ֫תִי‎ Job 31:21; Imperfect וַיָּ֫נֶף‎ Lev 8:27; Num 8:21, suffix וַיְנִיפֵ֫הוּ‎ Lev 8:29; 2masculine singular תָּנִיף‎ Deuteronomy 23:26 2t., etc.; Imperative masculine plural הָנִ֫יפוּ‎ Isa 13:2; Infinitive construct הָנִיף‎ 10:15 2t.; לַהֲנָפָה‎ 30:28 (Ges§ 72z BaNB 90, see also הֲנָחָה‎), etc.; Participle מֵנִיף‎ 19:16; Zech 2:13, etc.; — swing, wield, wave: —

1 wield, move tool to and fro in using it, with עַלֿ‎ of material: עָלֶיהָ הֵנַפְתָּ חֵרְבְּךָ כִּי‎ Exod 20:25 (E), i.e. over stone, so Deut 27:5; Josh 8:31 (D), and Deuteronomy 23:26 a sickle thou shalt not wield over the standing grain of thy neighbour; absolute מְנִיפוֺ‎ Isa 10:15 against him that wieldeth it (i.e. a saw), and וְאֶתמְֿרִימוֺ שֵׁבֶט כְּהָנִיף‎ 10:15 (read אֶתֿ‎) like a rod's wielding him that lifteth it.2.Shake or wave the hand;

a. wave hand אֶלהַֿמָּקוֺם‎ 2Kin 5:11, in healing ceremony (i.e. probably toward sanctuary KmpKau; > toward the spot [where leprosy appears], so most).

b. with עַלֿ‎, shake or brandish against, Isa 11:15; 19:16; Zech 2:13 (all of ׳י‎) Job 31:21 [compare Ecclus 12:18 (without עַל‎) in mockery].

c. wave hand, as a signal, Isa 13:2.

3 swing to and fro בְּנָפַת‎,in a sieve, Isa 30:28 (figurative, of nations).

4 Oft. technical term in P (H), of rite in which originally the priest lifted his share of offering and waved it, i.e. moved it toward altar and back, in token of its presentation to God and its return by him to priest: in H, י לִפְנֵי אֶתהָֿעֹמֶר ׳וְהֵנִיף‎ Lev 23:11a compare 23:11b; 23:12, also 23:20 (on text see Di Dr-WhHpt); in P, י לִפְנֵי ׳תְּנוּפָה אֹתוֺ וְהֵנַפְתָּ֫‎ Exod 29:26, so Lev 7:30; 8:29; 10:15 (object omitted) Num 6:20; thus also Levites are set apart for service of the priests 8:11 (Di strike out see ), 8:13; 8:21, compare 8:14 (י ׳לִפְנֵי‎ omitted); but same phr, of entire lamb, with oil, Lev 14:12, 24, and of offerings which were burnt, entirely Exod 29:24 (compare 29:25), Lev 8:27 (compare 8:28), or in part Num 5:25 (י לִפְנֵי הַמִּנְחָה ׳הֵנִיף‎), — in these the original significance seems lost; so, clearly, of contributions for tabernacle, = offer לְי זָהָב תְּנוּפַת ׳הֵנִיף‎ Exod 35:22. — compare תְּנוּפָה‎ below

5 Shed abroad (si vera lectio) תָּנִיף נְדָבוֺת גֶּשֶׁם‎ Psa 68:10 bounteous rain thou didst shed abroad [compare Ecclus 43:17c], but verb not wholly suitable; Lag Gr proposes תַּטִּיף‎, yet this also questionable.
