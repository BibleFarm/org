H8558. tamar

I. תָּמָר‎ noun masculine palm-tree, date-palm, Phoenix dactylifera; — ׳ת‎ Joel 1:12; in simile of prosperity Psa 92:13, of (graceful) stature Cant 7:8, compare (figurative) Song 7:9; plural תְּמָרִים‎ in oasis Exod 15:27 (J), || Num 33:9 (P); ת ׳כַּמֹּת‎ Lev 23:40 (H) palm-branches, compare ת ׳עֲלֵי‎ Neh 8:15; הַתְּמָרִים עִיר‎ = Jericho Deut 34:3 (JE), 2Chr 28:15; Judg 1:16; 3:13. — See PostHast.
