טָרִי‎ (ṭârîy｜taw-ree'｜adjective｜fresh)
[Heb] טָרִי‎ ETCBC: טָרִי‎ (adjv｜fresh) OSHL: i.bl.ab TWOT: 824a GK: H3269
Derivation: from an unused root apparently meaning to be moist;
Strong's: properly, dripping; hence, fresh (i.e. recently made such)
KJV: new, putrefying.

H2961. tari

[טָרִי‎] adjective fresh, only feminine singular טְרִיָּה‎ fresh, טְרִיָּה לְחִיחֲֿמוֺר‎ Judg 15:15 the fresh jawbone of an ass, i.e. not yet dry and brittle; ט ׳מַכָּה‎ Isa 1:6 fresh, raw wound, not yield healed or even treated (so Ges Ew Brd Di Du; festering De Che).
