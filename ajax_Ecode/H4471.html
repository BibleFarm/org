H4471. Mamre

מַמְרֵא‎ proper name, masculine and

of a location Mamre, only in Genesis:

1 in J only מַמְרֵא אֵלֹנֵי‎ terebinths of Mamre (proper name, masculine?), Gen 13:18 (defined by בְּחֶבְרוֺן אֲשֶׁר‎), 18:1 (in both the dwelling-place of Abraham).

2. proper name, masculine in Gen 14:13 (מ ׳אֵלֹנֵי הָאֱמֹרִי‎), compare 14:24 (where ally of Abraham)

3. proper name, of a location in P: near Abraham's place of burial Gen 23:17; 25:9; 49:30; 50:3; apparently identified with Hebron 23:19; 35:27, compare Di. — ᵐ5‎ Μαμβρη.
