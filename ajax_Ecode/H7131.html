H7131. qareb

קָרֵב‎ verbal adjective approaching; — ׳ק‎ 1Sam 17:41 +; plural קְרִבִים‎ Ezek 40:46; 45:4; — approaching:

1 as predicate, וְק ׳הֹלֵח אֶלדָּֿוִד‎ 1Sam 17:14, לַמִּלְחָמָה קְרֵבִים אַתֶּס‎ Deut 20:8; abs וְק ׳הָלוֺח‎ 2Sam 18:25; c, article as substantive, 1Kin 5:7 (+ אֶלוֺשֻׁלְחָן‎), Numbers 17:28 (twice in verse) (+ י ׳מִשְׁכַּן אֶלֿ‎).

2 with article approaching ׳י‎ temple or tabernacle, for service, with לְשָׁרֵת‎ Exodus 40:46 (+ ׳אֶליֿ‎), Ex 45:4; absolute הַקּ ׳הַוָּר‎ Num 1:51; 3:10, 38; 18:7.
