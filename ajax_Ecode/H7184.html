H7184. qasah

or qasvah [קַשְׂוָה‎, Köii. 1, 165] noun feminine a kind of jug, jar, utensil of tabernacle and (Chronicles) temple; — plural קְשָׂוֺת‎ Exod 37:16; 1Chr 28:19; construct הַנָּ֑סֶח קְשׂוֺת‎ Num 4:7 jars of the drink-offering; suffix (of table) Exod 25:29.
