IV. אִי‎ adverb not (frequently in Rabb, as אֶפְשַׁר אִי‎ impossible; and in Ethiopic the ordinary negative; compare Phoenician אי‎ CISi, 3, 5, and in איבל‎165; 18; 167; 11; Assyrian ai) Job 22:30 אִינָֿקִי‎ the non-innocent. **22:30: read perhaps אֵל‎ (Me), or אֱלֹהַּ‎ (Ley), with וְתִמָּלֵט‎ in verse b.
