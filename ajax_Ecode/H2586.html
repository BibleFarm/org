H2586. Chanun

חָנוּן‎ (favoured; compare Assyrian –anunu, king of Gaza COT on 2Kin 15:29); —

1 an Ammonite king 2Sam 10:1-2, 3, 4; 1Chr 19:2 (twice in verse); 19:3-4, 6.

2 Jewish chiefs, contemporaries of Nehemiah:

a. Neh 3:18.

b. Neh 3:30.
