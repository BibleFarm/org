סֶלַע‎ (çelaʻ｜seh'-lah｜noun｜masculine｜crag)
[Heb] סֶלַע‎ ETCBC: סֶלַע‎ (subs｜rock) OSHL: o.bq.ab TWOT: 1508a GK: H6152 Greek: κρημνός, πέτρα, στερέωμα
Derivation: from an unused root meaning to be lofty;
Strong's: a craggy rock, literally or figuratively (a fortress)
KJV: (ragged) rock, stone(-ny), strong hold.

H5553. sela

I. סֶ֫לַע‎61 noun masculineJudg 6:20 crag, cliff, synonym צוּר‎; — absolute ׳ס‎ 6:20 +, סָ֑לַע‎ Num 20:10 +; construct סֶ֫לַע‎ 1Sam 23:28; suffix סַלְעִי‎ 2Sam 22:2 +, סַלְעוֺ‎ Isa 31:9; plural סְלָעִים‎ 2:21 +; —

1 literally cliff, crag Judg 6:20; Isa 2:21; 7:19; Amos 6:12; Num 20:8 (twice in verse); 20:10 (twice in verse); 20:11(compare Neh 9:15; Psa 78:16, and contrast the צוּר‎ of Exod 17:6), Num 24:21; Deut 32:13 (not elsewhere Hexateuch), + 10 t.; הַסּ ׳שֵׁן‎ 1Sam 14:4 (twice in verse) tooth of the crag, i.e. sharp crag, so Job 39:28; excavated in cliff as place of burial Isa 22:16; abode of wild animals, ׳יַעֲלֵיסֿ‎ 22:1, compare Psa 104:18; Prov 30:26, of birds Job 39:28; Song 2:14 (in figurative), compare Jer 48:28, so of Edom Obadiah 3 = 49:16; compare (Num 24:21 above and) Isa 42:11; particular cliffs are: ׳ס עֵיטָם‎ Judg 15:8, 11 compare 15:13, ׳ס (הָ)רִמּוֺן‎ 20:45, 47 (twice in verse); 21:13 ׳ס הַמַּחְלְקוֺת‎ 1Sam 23:28 compare 23:25; see also II.סֶלַע‎.

2 figurative, especially סַלְעִי‎ of ׳י‎ (only Psalms), Psa 18:3 = 2Sam 22:2; Psa 31:4; 42:10; 71:3; of Assyrian god Isa 31:9 (probably; compare צוּר‎ Deut 32:31, 37); ס ׳צֵל כָּבֵד‎ Isa 32:2 (simile of protecting care); figurative of security Psa 40:3 (feet on cliff), מִשְׂגַּבּוֺ סְלָעִים מְצָדוֺת‎ Isa 33:16; symbol of obstinacy מִסּ פְנֵיהֶם ׳חִזְּקוּ‎ Jer 5:3; ס ׳צְחִיחַ‎ (bare cliff, literally glare of [the] cliff), in figure of openness, flagrancy Ezek 24:7-8, of razed city 26:4, 14 (only here Ezekiel); in figure of fall of Babylonian Jer 51:25.
