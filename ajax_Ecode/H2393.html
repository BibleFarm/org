H2393. chezqah

[חֶזְקָה‎] noun feminine strength, force (strictly Infinitive form from חזק‎ see Ges§ 45. 1 b) —

1 of urgency of ׳י‎s hand in prophetic inspiration הַיָד֑ בְּחֶזְקַת‎ Isa 8:11 (compare חָזַק‎ Ezek 3:14).

2 of royal power כְּחֶזְקָתוֺ‎2Chr 12:1; 26:16 (|| חָזַק‎ 26:15), Dan 11:2.
