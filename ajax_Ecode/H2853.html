Hoph`al Infinitive absolute הָחְתֵּל‎ be swathed, swaddled; — חֻתַּלְתְּ לֹא וְהָחְתֵּל הֻמְלַ֫חַתְּ לֹא וְהָמְלֵחַ‎ Ezek 16:4 and not at all wast thou rubbed with salt, and not at all wast thou swaddled, of Jerusalem under figure of infant.
