H2354. Chur

II. חוּר‎ proper name, masculine (meaning dubious; compare Nabataean proper name חורו‎ Nö in Eutp. 45) —

1 ᵐ5‎ Ωρ, named with Aaron as compan. of Moses Exod 17:10, 12; 24:14 (all E).

2 ᵐ5‎ Ωρ, a Judahite Exod 31:2 = 35:30, compare 38:22 (all P) 1Chr 2:19-20, 50 (on divinity of verse see Be VB); also 4:1, 4; 2Chr 1:5.

3 ᵐ5‎ Ουρ, a king of Midian Num 31:8; Josh 13:21 (both P).

4 ᵐ5‎L Εουρ (B omitted) head of fam. of returned exiles Neh 3:9.

5 בֶּןחֿוּר‎ officer of Solomon 1Kin 4:8, see below בֵּן‎ p. 122.

III. חוּר‎ = חֻר‎ see below III. חרר‎.
