[Heb] בָּשָׂר‎ ETCBC: בָּשָׂר‎ (subs｜flesh) OSHL: b.dj.ab TWOT: 291a GK: H1414 Greek: ἄνθρωπος, δόξα, κρέας, σάρκινος, σάρξ, σπέρμα, σῶμα, χρώς
Derivation: from בָּשַׂר‎;
Strong's: flesh (from its freshness); by extension, body, person; also (by euphemistically) the pudenda of aman
KJV: body, (fat, lean) flesh(-ed), kin, (man-) kind, [phrase] nakedness, self, skin.

H1320. basar

בָּשָׂר‎266 noun masculine flesh (compare Arabic skin, Syriac , Assyrian bišru, blood-relation, DlAssyrian Stud. i. 143, compare Pr. 170, Sabean תורם בשר‎ flesh of bulls) — Gen 2:21 126t.; construct בְּשַׂר‎ 17:11 40t.; suffix בּשָׂרִי‎ etc. Psa 16:9 96t.; plural בְּשָׂרִים‎ Prov 14:30; — 1 of the body:

a. of animals Gen 41:2-19 Exod 21:28; 22:30; Num 12:12 (E) 11:4-33 (JE) Gen 9:4 + (P 30 t.) Deut 12:15 + (D 9 t.) Judg 6:19-21 1Sam 2:13, 15; 1Kin 17:6; 19:21; Job 31:31; 41:15; Psa 50:13; Prov 23:20; Isa 22:13; 44:16, 19; 65:4; 66:17; Jer 7:21; 11:15; Ezek 4:14 6t. Dan 10:3; Hosea 8:13; Micah 3:3; Hag 2:12; Zech 11:9, 16.

b. of men Gen 40:19 (E) 2:21; Exod 4:7 (J) Lev 12:3; 13:2 16t. 26:29 (P) Deut 28:53, 55; 32:42; Judg 8:7; 1Sam 17:44; 1Kin 4:34; 5:10; 5:14; 6:30; 1 Kings 9:36; Job 2:5; 4:15; 6:12; 7:5; 10:11; 13:14; 19:20, 22; 21:6; 33:21, 25; Psa 27:2; 38:4; 38:8; 79:2; 102:6; 109:24; Prov 4:22; 5:11; Isa 9:19; 17:4; 49:26; Jer 19:9; Lam 3:4; Ezek 32:5; 37:6, 8; 39:17-18, Dan 1:15; Zech 14:12. The flesh of the body is contrasted with stone Ezek 11:19; 36:26.

2 flesh for the body itself (especially in P): ייסך לא אדם בשׂר על‎ upon the body of man it shall not be poured Exod 30:32 (P); בשׂרו על ילבשׁ בד מכנסי‎ linen drawers shall he put on his body Lev 6:3; 16:4 (P); בשׂרו על שׂק וישׂם‎ 1 Kings 2;:27; בשׂרו את ורחץ‎ and he shall bath his body 14:9; 15:13, 16; 16:24, 26, 28; 17:16; 22:6; Num 19:7-8, (P); בכשׂרכם תתנו לא לנפשׁ שׂרט‎ ye shall not put any cutting for any one in your body Lev 19:28 compare 21:5 (P); בשׂרם כל על תער והעבירו‎ and they shall pass a razor over all their body Num 8:7 (P). Ecclesiastes uses בשׂר‎ only in this sense Eccl 2:3; 4:5; 5:5; 11:10; 12:12; elsewhere this usage only in poetry; the body antithetical to נפשׁ‎ Job 14:22; Isa 10:18; Psa 63:2; לב‎ 16:9; 84:3; Prov 14:30 (only here emphatic plural = entire body Bö§ 695, Leiblichkeit De); מִבְּשָׂרִי‎ apart from my body, in disembodied state Job 19:26; בשׂרי מפחדך סמר‎ my body trembleth for fear of thee Psa 119:120.

3 make organ of Generation (euphemism): עָרְלַתְכֶם בְּשַׂר‎ Gen 17:11, 14, 23, 24, 25 (P); ערוה בשׂר‎ Exod 28:42 (P), but בשׂר‎ Gen 17:14; Lev 15:2-19 (P) Ezek 16:26; 23:20; 44:7, 9.

4 flesh for kindred, blood-relations: מבשׂרי ובשׂר מעצמי עצם‎ bone of my bone and flesh of my flesh Gen 2:23 (J); אחד לבשׂר והיו‎ and they shall become one flesh 2:24 (J); ובשׂרי עצמי‎ 29:14 (J) Judg 9:2; 2Sam 5:1; 19:13; 19:14; 1Chr 11:1; בשׂר‎ with suffix in same sense Gen 37:27 (J) Neh 5:5; Isa 58:7, for which בשׂרו שְׁאֵר‎ near of kin, man or woman Lev 18:6; 25:49 (both H; 25:49 || מִשְׁמָּחָה‎, compare RSk149).

5 man over against God as frail or erring Gen 6:3 (J) Psa 56:5; 78:39; eyes of flesh Job 10:4; arm of flesh 2Chr 32:8; Jer 17:5; horses are flesh not spirit Isa 31:3

6 the phrase ׃כָּלבָּֿשָׂר‎

a. all living beings Gen 6:17, 19; 7:21; 9:11, 15, 16, 17; Lev 17:14; Num 18:15 (P) Job 34:15; Psa 136:25.

b. animals Gen 7:15-16, 8:17 (P).

c. mankind Gen 6:12, 18; Num 16:22; 27:16 (P) Deut 5:28; Psa 65:3; 145:21; Isa 40:5-6, 49:26; 66:16, 23, 24; Jer 12:12; 25:31; 32:27; 45:5; Ezek 21:4; 21:9; 21:10; Joel 3:1; Zechariah 2:17; compare אישׁ בשׂר כל‎ Job 12:10.
