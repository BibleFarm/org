H6218. asor

עָשׂוֺר‎, and (Exod 12:3) עָשׂר‎ noun [masculine] a ten, decade; —

1 usually of days:

a. a period of ten days Gen 24:55 (J).

b. in phrase, לַחֹדֶשׁ (עָשׂר) בֶּעָשׂוֺר‎ on the tenth of the month Lev 25:9 (H), Ezek 20:1; 24:1; 40:1; Exod 12:3; Lev 16:29; 23:27; Num 29:7; Josh 4:19 (all P), 2Kin 25:1 = Jer 52:4, 12 (seventh day in || 2Kin 25:8).

2 of strings of instrument: ע ׳נֵבֶל‎ harp, a ten (-stringed one) Psa 33:2; 144:9; ׳ע‎ alone 92:4 upon a ten (-stringed instrument).
