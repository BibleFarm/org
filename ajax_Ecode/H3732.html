H3732. Kaphtori

[כַּפְתֹּרִי‎] adjective, of a people, only plural as substantive כַּפְתֹּרִים‎ Cretans; Gen 10:14 (J) = 1Chr 1:12; as expellers of the 'Awwim' from their homes about Gaza, Deut 2:23.

I. כַּר‎ basket-saddle, see below כור‎.
