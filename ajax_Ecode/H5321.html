H5321. Naphtali

נַפְתָּלִי‎ proper name, masculine and of a tribe Naphtali, Νεφθαλει (μ), etc. (interpreted as my wrestling Gen 30:8; perhaps originally crafty, cunning one); —

1 second son of Jacob and Bilhah Gen 30:8 (J), 35:25; 46:24 (P), 1Chr 2:2, compare Gen 49:21 (poem).

2 as a tribe of Israel:

a. Judg 1:33; 4:10; 5:18; Deut 33:23 (twice in verse) +; so (Hexateuch, only P) נ ׳בְּנֵי‎ Judg 4:6; Num 1:42 6t., נ ׳מַטֵּה‎ 2:29; 1Kin 7:14 5t., נ בְּנֵי ׳מַטֵּה‎ Num 10:27; 34:28; Josh 19:39; נ ׳שַׁעַר‎ Ezek 48:34 (in new Jerusalem); נ ׳אֶרֶץ‎ 1Kin 15:20; 2Kin 15:29, compare Isaiah 8:23; נ ׳קֶדֶשׁ‎ Judg 4:6; נ ׳הַר‎ Josh 20:7; נ ׳גְּבוּל‎ Ezek 48:4, נ ׳יָ֫מָּה‎ 48:3; נ ׳עָרֵי‎2Chr 16:4.
