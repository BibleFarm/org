H8602. taphel

I. תָּפֵל‎ adjective tasteless, unseasoned; — absolute ׳ת‎, as substantive Job 6:6; figurative of unsatisfying prophecies Lam 2:14 ( + שָׁוְא‎).

II. תָּפֵל‎ noun [masculine] whitewash; — applied to wall, in Ezekiel's vision Ezek 13:10-11, 14, 15; figurative of false prophet 22:28.
