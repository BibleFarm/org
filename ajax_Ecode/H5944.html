[Heb] עֲלִיָּה‎ ETCBC: עֲלִיָּה‎ (subs｜upper room) OSHL: p.cs.ah TWOT: 1624f GK: H6608 Greek: ἐκκεντέω
Derivation: feminine from עָלָה‎;
