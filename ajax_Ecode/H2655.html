1 of man, with accusative Psa 34:13; 35:27; Mal 3:1; with infinitive Neh 1:11; absolute הֶחָפֵץ‎ whosoever would 1Kin 13:33; אתה חפץ אם‎ if thou pleasest 21:6; חֲפֵצָה נֶפֶשׁ‎ willing soul 1Chr 28:9; plural construct before nouns absolute Psa 35:22; 40:15 = 70:3; לְכָלחֶֿפְצֵיהֶם דְּרוּשִׁים‎ studied of all who take pleasure in them 111:2.
