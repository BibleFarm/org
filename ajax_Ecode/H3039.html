H3039. yadid

[יָדִיד‎] adjective (poetry) beloved (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic ) — construct יְדִיד‎ Deut 33:12; suffix לִידִידִי‎ Isa 5:1 (twice in verse); Jer 11:15; לִידִידוֺ‎ Psa 127:2; plural suffix יְדִידֶיךָ‎ 60:7; 108:7; feminine יְדִידֹת‎ 45:1; יְדִידוֺת‎ 84:2; —

1 my beloved, used by prophets of ׳י‎ under figure of husbandman Isa 5:1 (twice in verse); so my (thy, his) beloved Jer 11:15; Psa 60:7 = 108:7Psalm 2; beloved, of Benjamin as beloved by ׳י‎ Deut 33:12.

2 lovely, מִשְׁכְּנוֺתֶיךָ מַהיְּֿדִידוֺת‎ Psa 84:2 how lovely are thy habitations !

3 feminine plural as abstract substantive יְדִידֹת שִׁיר‎ Psa 45:1 = a song of love.
