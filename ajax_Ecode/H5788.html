H5788. ivvarown

עִוָּרוֺן‎ noun [masculine] blindness; — only in phrase (subject ׳י‎), smite (הִכָּה‎) with blindness: figurative of 'blind incapacity' (Dr) ׳בְּע‎ Deut 28:28; of smiting horses ׳בַּע‎ Zech 12:4, i.e. with blind staggers, making them helplessly wild.

עַוֶּ֫רֶת‎ noun feminine id.; — of sacrifice animals, abstract for concrete, Lev 22:22 (H).
