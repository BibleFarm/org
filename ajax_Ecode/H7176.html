H7176. qereth

[קֶ֫רֶת‎] noun feminine town, city (late poetry) (compare Phoenician חדשת קרת‎ proper name, of a location in Cyprus, also = Carthage, and perhaps Cirta, Tigranocerta; compare Thes); — always קָ֑רֶת‎; — of Job's city, Job 29:7; indefinite Prov 8:3; 9:3, 14; city as a corporate body of men 11:11.
