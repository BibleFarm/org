H4024. Migdol

מִגְדּוֺל‎ noun masculine tower, 2Sam 22:51 Qr (Kt מגדיל‎ = Psalm 18:51 מַגְדִּל‎).

מִגְדֹּל‎ proper name (מִגְדּוֺל‎ only Jer 46:14) fortified city on the northeast border of Egypt Exod 14:2; Num 33:7; Jer 44:1; 46:14; Ezek 29:10; 30:6; Coptic meschtôl (Champoll.L'Égypte sous les Pharaons ii. 79), Egyptian makθel EbGS 522.
