H2683. chetsen

[חֵ֫צֶן‎] noun masculine bosom of a garment (Arabic , Ethiopic also Syriac bosom (= *), see HoffmZMG 1878, 753 LagM ii. 361 ff.; BN 46 f.; on this word and following see BaNB 120; ZMG 1889, 183) — מְעַמֵּר וְחִצְנוֺ קוֺצֵר כַמּוֺ מִלֵּא שֶׁלּאֹ‎ Psa 129:7 (grass) with which a reaper filleth not his hand, nor a binder his bosom.
