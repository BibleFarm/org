[דֵּעַ‎] noun [masculine] knowledge, opinion (late) — only suffix דֵּעִי‎ Job 32:6 3t., and plural דֵּעִים‎ 37:6; all in speech of Elihu.

1 knowledge, ד ׳תְּמִים‎ Job 37:16 one perfect in knowledge (of God).

2 judgement, opinion Job 36:3; דֵּעִי חַֹוּת‎ 32:6 to declare my opinion, so 32:10; 32:17.
