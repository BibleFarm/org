H3954. leqesh

לֶ֫קֶשׁ‎ noun [masculine] probably after-growth, after-math, i.e. spring-crop (compare GASm12Proph. i. 109); — only (2 t.) Amos 7:1, הַמֶּלֶךְ׃ נִּזֵּי אַחַר לֶקֶשׁ וְהִנֵּה הַלָּ֑קֶשׁ עֲלוֺת בִּתְחִלַּת‎. — > Other interpretations are: late-rain growth (from מַלְקוֺשׁ‎ q. v.), HoffmZAW iii. (1883) 116; leaf-and-herb-gathering in spring, We after KleinZPV iv. 1881, 83; Anderlindviii. 1886, 62.
