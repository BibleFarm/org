H4339. meshar

[מֵישָׁר‎] noun masculine evenness, uprightness, equity; — only plural: מֵשָׁרִים‎ Prov 1:3; elsewhere מֵישָׁרִים‎ Isa 26:7 17t.; —

1 evenness, level Isa 26:7, of path of righteous (in the future), figurative for free from difficulties; smoothness, of the flow of wine, ׳בְּמ‎ Prov 23:31; ׳למ‎ Cant 7:10.

2 in ethical sense, uprightness, equity, as taught in the school of wisdom Prov 8:6, || צֶדֶק‎ 1:3; 2:9; of government ׳(ב)מ‎ Psa 9:9; 58:2; 75:3; 96:10; 99:4; of speech Isa 33:15; Prov 23:16; of Yahweh's promises Isa 45:19; מ ׳רצה‎ 1Chr 29:17 have pleasure in equity; מ ׳עשׂה‎ Dan 11:6 make an equitable arrangement.

3 adverb rightly, with חזה‎ Psa 17:2; אהב‎ Song 1:4.
