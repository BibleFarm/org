H8292. sheruqah

or sheriqah [שְׁרִיקָה‎] noun [feminine] hissing, perhaps also whistling, piping; — plural absolute (intensive) שְׁרִיקֹת‎ Jer 18:16 Qr hissing, in derision, + שַׁמָּה‎ (see שְׁרֵקָה‎), Kt שרוקת‎; construct עֲדָרִים שְׁרִקוֺת‎ Judg 5:16 as signal hissings (or whistlings, pipings) for flocks.

שׁרר‎ (√ of [שָׁרִיר‎], שְׁרִירוּת‎, and perhaps of other words following; Old Aramaic שר‎ be firm sound, Aramaic שְׁרַר‎ be firm, hard, be firm, consistent, substantial, truthful, strengthen confirm : Aramaic and Late Hebrew שָׁרִיר‎ firm, hard; firmness, strength, constancy).
