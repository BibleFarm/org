H7916. sakir

שָׂכִיר‎ adjective hired; — absolute ׳שׂ‎ Exodus 14:45 +, feminine שְׂכִירָה‎ Isa 7:20; construct שְׂכֹיר‎ Lev 25:53; suffix שְׂכִירְךָ‎ 25:6; plural suffix שְׂכִרֶיהָ‎ Jer 46:21; —

1 hired, of beast Exod 22:14 (E), razor Isa 7:20 (figurative).

2 usually as substantive hireling, hired labourer Deut 15:18; 24:14; Lev 19:13; 22:10; 25:6, 40, 53 (all H), Exod 12:45; Lev 25:50 (both P), Mal 3:5; Job 7:1-2, 14:6; שׂ ׳כִּשְׁנֵי‎ Isa 16:14 years like a hireling's years (reckoned strictly), so 21:16; mercenaries Jer 46:21.
