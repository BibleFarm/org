שַׁעַטְנֵז‎ noun masculine mixed stuff (perhaps of Egyptian origin; Kn derives from Coptic saht, woven, + nudj, false (PeyronLex 224. 133), and thinks originally שַׁעַטְנֹז‎; ᵐ5‎ has κίβδηλος spurious); — a kind of cloth forbidden for garments; defined Deut 22:11 by יַחְדָּו וּפִשְׁתִּים צֶמֶר‎, and Lev 19:19 (H) by כִּלְאַיִם‎.
