H447. Eliel

אֱלִיאֵל‎ proper name, masculine (El is God, or my God is God) only Chronicles

a. two or three of David's chiefs 1Chr 11:46-47, 12:11.

b. chief of Manasseh 1Chr 5:24.

c. two chiefs of Benjamin 1Chr 8:20, 22.

d. chief of the Hebronites 1Chr 15:9, 11.

e. a chief Kohathite 1Chr 6:19 = אֱלִיאָב‎ v.6:12 compare אֱלִיהוּ‎ 1Sam 1:1. feminine a Levite2Chr 31:13.
