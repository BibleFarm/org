מַלְכוּ‎ noun feminine royalty, reign, kingdom; — absolute ׳מ‎ Dan 2:39 +; construct מַלְכוּת‎ Ezra 4:24 +; emphatic תָא‎- Dan 2:37 +, תָה‎- 2:44; suffix תִי‎- 4:15 +, הֵתּ‎- Daniel 3:33 +, תָךְ‎- 4:23 +; plural construct מַלְכְוָת‎ 7:27; emphatic וָתָא‎- 2:44; 7:23; —

1 royalty, kingship, kingly authority: Dan 4:23; 4:28; 4:33; מ ׳הֵיכַל‎ 4:26, מ ׳בֵּית‎ 4:27, כָּרְסֵא‎ 5:20.

2 organized (world-) kingdom: Dan 2:39 (twice in verse); 2:40-41, 42, 44; 7:23 (twice in verse); 7:24, 27; ׳מ אֲנָשָׁא‎ 4:14; 4:22; 4:29; 5:21; of specific kings 2:37; 5:18, 28; 6:1; of God Daniel 3:33; 7:27 (both ׳מ עָלַם‎), 2:44 (twice in verse); Daniel 3:33; 4:31; 6:27; 7:27; of Messiah 7:14 (twice in verse); of saints 7:18 (twice in verse); 7:22, 27.

3 realm (territorial) Dan 4:15; 4:33b; 5:7, 11, 16, 29; 6:2 (twice in verse); 6:4; 6:8; 6:27; Ezra 7:13, 23; metonymy 6:5 +?

4 reign, time of reigning Dan 5:26; Daniel 6:29 (twice in verse); Ezra 4:24; 6:15.
