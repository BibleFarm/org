3 of Abraham Gen 23:6, and heads of Ishmaelite tribes 17:20; 25:16.

4 rulers of the congregation, הָעֵדָה נְשִׂיאֵי‎ Exod 16:22; Josh 9:15, 18; 22:30, ׳הַנּ בָּעֵדָה‎ Exod 34:31, compare 35:27; Lev 4:22; Josh 9:18b; 9:19, 21 (twice in verse); 17:4; 22:14 (3 t. in verse); 22:32 (all P); especially

5 tribal chiefs and representatives, according to the religious organization of P, Num 1:16, 44; 2:3 57t. Numbers (especially chapters 2, 7, 34; note הַלֵּוִי נְשִׂיאֵי נְשִׂיא‎ 3:32); compare 1Chr 2:10; 4:38; 5:6; 7:40; 2Chr 1:2, also 1Kin 8:1 (probably gloss, omitted by ᵐ5‎ Benz), 2Chr 5:2.

a. of Zedekiah (for מֶלֶךְ‎ see Böhmer8K 1900, 112 ff.), Ezek 7:27; 12:10, 12; 21:30, + 19:1 (read singular ᵐ5‎ Ew Hi Co Berthol Toy).

d. foreign princes Ezek 26:16; 27:21; 30:13; 32:29; 38:2-3, 39:1, 18.

7 leader of exiles returning under Cyrus, according to Ezra 1:8.

8 non-Israel chiefs in P: Gen 34:2; Num 25:18; Josh 13:21.
