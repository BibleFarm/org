H2380. chazuth

חָזוּת‎ noun feminine vision, conspicuousness (on formation compare LgBN 202) — absolute ׳ח‎ Isa 21:2; Dan 8:5, 8; construct חָזוּת‎ Isa 29:11; suffix חָזוּתְכֶם‎ 28:18; —

1 vision, oracle of a prophet הֻגַּדלִֿי קָשָׁה חָזוּת‎ Isa 21:2 a hard vision is declared to me; הֶחָתוּם הַסֵּפֶר כְּדִבְרֵי הַּכֹּל חָזוּת לָכֶם וַתְּהִי‎ 29:11 the vision (prophecy) of the whole is become to you as the words of a sealed writing; תָקוּם לאֹ אֶתשְֿׁאוֺל חָזוּתְכֶם‎ 28:18 your agreement with Sheol will not stand, so Thes MV De Che Di and others after Vrss, but Ew§ 156 e renders oracle, and Siegf. Hellseherei, see חֹזֶה‎

2. 2. conspicuousness in appearance: הָזוּת קֶרֶן‎ a conspicuous horn Dan 8:5 +? 8:8; 8:21; so אַרְבַּע חָזוּת‎ 8:8, but < read אֲחֵרוֺת‎ others, as ᵐ5‎ Gr Bev.
