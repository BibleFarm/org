[Heb] עָשַׁת‎ ETCBC: עשׁת‎ (verb｜be smooth?), עשׁת‎ (verb｜think) OSHL: p.gh.aa, p.gg.aa TWOT: 1716 1717 GK: H6950, H6951 Greek: διασώζω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: probably to be sleek, i.e. glossy; hence (through the idea of polishing) to excogitate (as if forming in the mind)
KJV: shine, think.
