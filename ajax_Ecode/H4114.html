H4114. mahpekah

[מַהְמֵּכָה‎] noun feminine overthrow — always construct מַהְמֵּכַת‎; & always of overthrow of Sodom, Gomorrah etc., except Isa 1:7 ׳מ זָרִים‎, where read סְדֹם‎ (Ew Che RSProph. 345 Di and others; also LagSemitic i. 3); yet even so probably gloss, compare StuJPTh 1877, 714; ׳מ סְדֹם‎ Deut 29:22; Jer 49:18; and with force of verbal noun, governing accusative, ׳מ אתסֿדם אלהים‎ Isa 13:19; Jer 50:40; Amos 4:11. compare also הָפַךְ‎ 1b, הֲפֵכָה‎.
