סִיר‎ (çîyr｜seer｜noun｜masculine｜pot｜thorn)
[Heb] סִיר‎, סִירָה‎ ETCBC: סִיר‎ (subs｜pot), סִירָה‎ (subs｜hook) OSHL: o.az.at, o.az.as TWOT: 1489 1490 GK: H6105, H6106 Greek: ἄκανθα, ἀκάνθινος, σκόλοψ, χαλκίον
Derivation: or (feminine) סִירָה‎; or סִרָה‎; (Jeremiah 52:18), from a primitive root meaning to boil up;
Strong's: a pot; also a thorn (as springing up rapidly); by implication, a hook
KJV: caldron, fishhook, pan, (wash-)pot, thorn.
