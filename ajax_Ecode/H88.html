H88. Oboth

אֹבֹת‎ proper name, of a location (water-skins) station of Israel in wilderness Num 21:10; Numbers 34:43; not yet determined, probably on eastern skirts of Idumea not far from Moab; accusative to Wetzst in DeCt 168 Wêba, in the Arabah, but identification not probably; compare Di on 21:10.

אוד‎ (be curved, bent, also transitive burden, oppress, compare Arabic ).
