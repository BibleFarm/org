H2532. chemdah

חֶמְדָּה‎ noun feminine id. — ׳ח‎ Hosea 13:15 8t.; construct חֶמְדַּת‎ 1Sam 9:20 3t.; suffix חֶמְדָּתִי‎ Jer 12:10; חֶמְדָּתֵךְ‎ Ezek 26:12; חֶמְדָּתָם‎ Dan 11:8; — desire of Israel 1Sam 9:20 followed by לְ‎ before object of desire; ח ׳בְּלֹא‎ = without desire2Chr 21:20 (i.e. he lived as no one desired), see Öttli ᵐ5‎ (not ᵐ5‎L) ᵑ9‎; concrete: ׳כלחֿ ישׂראל‎ 1Sam 9:20 and for whom is all that is desirable in Israel? so ᵑ9 ᵐ5‎ RV Dr; (>AV and on whom is all the desire of Israel?); compare ח ׳ובאו כלהֿגוים‎ Hag 2:7 (i.e. the desirable, precious things of all nations); elsewhere after construct as חֶמֶד‎; especially ח ׳כלי‎ Hosea 13:15; Nahum 2:10; Jer 25:34; 2Chr 32:27; 36:10; Dan 11:8; ח ׳שְׂכִיּוֺת‎ Isa 2:16, ח ׳אֶרֶץ‎ Psa 106:24; Jer 3:19; Zech 7:14, compare ח ׳חֶלְקַת‎ Jer 12:10; ח ׳בתי‎ Ezek 26:12; ׳ח נשׁים‎ Dan 11:37, apparently reference to some object of idolatrous worship, perhaps Adonis = Tammuz Ew Bev (Astarte, Meinh).
