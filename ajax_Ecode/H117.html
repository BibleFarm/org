H117. addir

אַדִּיר‎ adjective majestic — ׳א‎ Psa 8:2 +; feminine אַדֶּ֫רֶת‎ Ezek 17:8 ? (see below) etc. —

1 majestic (wide, lofty) of waters of sea Exod 15:10; Psa 93:4; a ship Isa 33:21; a tree Ezek 17:23; a vine 17:8 (אַדֶּרֶת גֶּפֶן‎, so Fü; or ׳א‎ abstract noun see below אַדֶּרֶת‎ below); also figurative of kings Psa 136:18; nations Ezek 32:18; gods 1Sam 4:8; of ׳י‎ Psa 93:4; 76:5; of name of ׳י‎ 8:2; Psalm 8:10.

2 substantive majestic one, of nobles, chieftains, etc., Judg 5:13, 25; Nahum 2:6; 3:18; Jer 14:3; 25:34 (הַצּאֹן אַדִּירֵי‎ figurative so): 35.36; 30:21; Zech 11:2; 2Chr 23:20; Neh 3:5; 10:30; of ׳י‎ Isa 10:34; 33:21; of servants of ׳י‎ Psa 16:3 (= priests ? compare 1Chr 24:5 & see Che).
