H6473. paar

[מָּעַר‎] verb open wide (the mouth) (Late Hebrew rarely = Biblical Hebrew, usually open bowels, go to stool; compare Jewish-Aramaic מְּעַר‎; Arabic and Syriac = Biblical Hebrew, chasm); —

Qal Perfect of voracious greed, figurative 3feminine singular מִּיהָ וּפָֽעֲרָה‎ Isa 5:14 (of Sheôl), 3 plural בְּפִיהֶם עָלַי מָּֽעֲרוּ‎ (Ges§ 119q) Job 16:10; of eager desire (good sense), מָּֽעֲרוּ מִּיהֶם‎ 29:23, מִּיפָֿעַרְתִּי‎ Psa 119:131.
