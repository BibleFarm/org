H6486. pequddah

מְּקֻדָּה‎32 noun feminine oversight, mustering, visitation, store; — absolute ׳פ‎ Hosea 9:7; 1Chr 23:11; construct מְּקֻדַּת‎ Num 3:32 +; suffix מְּקֻדָּתוֺ‎ Num 109:8, etc.; plural מְּקֻדּ(וֺ)ת‎ Jer 52:11 +; —

1 visitation:

a. = punishment Hosea 9:7; Isa 10:3; Micah 7:4; Ezek 9:1; Num 16:29 (P), especially in מְּקֻדָּתָם עֵת(שְׁנַתׅ‎ Jer 8:12; 10:15 6t. Jeremiah (compare עֵת‎ with מָּקַד‎ 6:15; 49:8; 50:31), הַמּ ׳בֵּית‎ = prison 52:11.

b. gracious visitation, providence Job 10:12.

2. a. oversight, charge Num 4:16 (P), also 3:36 (P; redund.), 1Chr 26:30; = office2Chr 23:18.

b. overseer (abstract for concrete), 2Kin 11:8; Ezek 44:11 (with אֶל‎ of thing), Num 3:32 (P); collective = magistracy Isa 60:17 (LagBN 151), compare 2Chr 24:11.

c. class of officers 1Chr 23:11; 24:3, 19.

d. charge = thing entrusted Num 4:16 (P).

3 mustering2Chr 17:14; 26:11.

4 store, things laid up, Isa 15:7; Psa 109:8 (perhaps; Hup WeSkizzen vi. 184 and others office).
