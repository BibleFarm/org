תֹּם‎ noun [masculine] completeness, integrity ; — תֹּם‎ absolute Prov 10:20 +; construct Job 4:6; Psa 78:72, construct also תָּםֿ‎ Prov 13:6 4t.; suffix תֻּמִּי‎ Psa 26:1 11t. suffix; plural תֻּמִּים‎ Exod 28:30 3t.; suffix תֻּמֶּיךָ‎ Deut 33:8 ; —

1 completeness, fullness : כְּתֻמָּם‎ Isa 47:9 according to their full measure; תֻּמּוֺ בְּעֶצֶם‎ Job 21:23 in his very completeness.

2 innocence, simplicity: לתמם הלכים‎ 2Sam 15:11; לְתֻמּוֺ בַּקֶּשֶׁת מֶשַׁךְ‎ 1Kin 22:34 2Chr 18:33, i.e. without definite aim.

3 integrity: לֵבָב תֹּם‎ integrity of mind Gen 20:5-6, (E), 1Kin 9:4; Psa 78:72; 101:2; תָּםדֶּֿרֶךְ‎ Prov 13:6; Job 4:6; בַּתֹּם (הת)הלך‎ Prov 10:9, בְּתֻמּוֺ‎ 19:1; 20:7; 28:6, בְּתֻמִּי‎ Psa 26:1; 26:11; תֹם הֹלְכֵי‎ Prov 2:7; וָישֶׁר תֹּם‎ Psa 25:21; בְּתֻמִּי‎ 41:13; כְּתֻמִּי‎ 7:9; לַתֹּם מָעוֺז‎ Prov 10:29.

4 תֻּמִּים‎ in וְתֻמִּים אוּרִים‎ (meaning dubious, see, אורים‎) Deut 33:8; Exod 28:30 (P) Lev 8:8 (P), Ezra 2:63 = Neh 7:65; + 1Sam 14:41 (for תָּמִים‎) We Dr Bu HPS; also Hosea 3:4 (for תרפים‎ CheJQ. July 1899, 564 and 4:5 (תֻּמֶּ֑יךָ‎ for אִמֶּ֑ךָ‎) RubenJQ. April 1899, 44 Chel.c.
