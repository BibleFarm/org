מִּסֵּחַ‎ adjective lame; — absolute ׳פ‎ 2Sam 9:13 +; plural מִּסְחִים‎ 5:6 +; — as attributive Lev 21:18 (+עִוֵּר‎); predicate 2Sam 19:27, ׳פ רַגְלָיו שְׁתֵּי‎ 9:13 lame as to both his feet; compare also (of animal) Deut 15:21 (+עִוֵּר‎); as substantive 2Sam 5:6, 8 (twice in verse); Jer 31:8; Mal 1:8, 13; Job 29:15 (all + עִוֵּר‎), also Isa 33:23; 35:6; Prov 26:7.
