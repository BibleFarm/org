H2186. zanach

I. זָנַח‎ verb reject, spurn (perhaps compare Assyrian zinû, be angry, especially of gods Gu§ 105 ZimBP 23 ff.) —

Qal Perfect ׳ז‎ Hosea 8:3, 5; Lam 2:7; זָנַחְתָּ‎ Psa 44:10 2t.; suffix זְנַחְתָּ֑נִי‎ 43:2; זְנַחְתָּנוּ‎ 60:3; 60:12; 108:12; זְנַחְתִּים‎ Zech 10:6; Imperfect יִזְנַח‎ Psa 77:8; Lam 3:31; תִּזְנַח‎ Psa 44:24 2t.; — reject, Israel rejects good Hosea 8:3; Samaria's calf rejects her 8:5 (others make ׳י‎ subject; WeKl. Proph. reads אזנת‎ I reject), elsewhere God rejects people Psa 43:2; 60:3; 77:8; 88:15; Zech 10:6; לָנֶצַח‎ Psa 44:24; 74:1; לעולם‎ Lam 3:31; ז בצבאתינו תצא ׳ולא‎ Psa 44:10; 60:12; 108:12; מִשָּׁלוֺם נַפְשִׁי‎ Lam 3:17; king Psa 89:39; altar Lam 2:7.
