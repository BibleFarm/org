H6578. Perath

מְּרָת‎ proper name, masculine, of a river Eupharates, Ευφρατης (Assyrian Purattu DlPa.169 ff., Whence Old Persian, Ufrâtu, SpiegAPK 211); — the greatest river of West Asia; ׳פ‎ Gen 2:14. פ ׳נְהַר‎ 15:18; (both secondary phrases in J), פ ׳נְהַר‎ also 2Sam 8:3 (Qr, see נָהָר‎), Jer 46:2, 6, 10; Deut 1:7; 11:24; Josh 1:4 (D), 2Kin 23:29; 24:7; 1Chr 5:9; 18:3; נהר‎ omitted Jer 13:4-5, 6, 7 (where Ew Hi Marti, after SchickZPV iii. ll, think of some other מְּרָת‎, but Gf Gie and most defend Euphrates) 51:63; 2Chr 35:20 (compare Gen 2:14 above); name not certainly attested before D Jeremiah; on earlier הַנָּהָר‎ for this river see ׳נ‎.

מֹּרָת‎ Gen 42:22 see [ מָּרָה‎].
