גְּוִיָּה‎ noun feminine body, corpse — absolute ׳ג‎ Nahum 3:3; construct גְּוִיַּת‎ Judg 14:8 2t.; suffix גְּוִיָּתוֺ‎ 1Sam 31:10; Dan 10:6, גְּוִיָּתֵנוּ‎ Gen 47:18, גְּוִיָּתָם‎ Nahum 3:3; plural absolute גְּוִיּוֺת‎ Psa 110:6; suffix גְּוִיֹּתֵנוּ‎ Neh 9:37, גְּוִיֹּתֵיהֶם‎ Ezek 1:23, גְּוִיֹּתֵיהֶ֑נָֿה‎ 1:11; —

1 living human body Gen 47:18 (singular, of many persons), compare plural Neh 9:37; of man in Daniel's vision Dan 10:6 (body apart from extremities); also of the living creatures in Ezekiel's vision Ezek 1:11, 28.

2 dead body, corpse, carcass:

a. of man 1Sam 31:10 (so originally in || 1Chr 10:10 see WeSm compare Dr) 10:12; 10:12 (singular of several persons), Psa 110:6; collective Nahum 3:3 (twice in verse);

b. of lion Judg 14:8-9,.
