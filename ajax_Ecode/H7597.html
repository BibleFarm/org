H7597. Shealtiel

שְׁאַלְתִּיאֵל‎ proper name, masculine (I have asked (him) of God; on accusative compare (late) Psa 137:3); — first son of Jehoiachin and uncle of Zerubbabel according to 1Chr 3:17 (compare 3:18; 3:19); but Zerubbabel's father Hag 1:1; 2:23; Ezek 3:2, 8; Neh 12:1 (attempts at explaining in Be Kit Benz). — Σαλαθιηλ.

שַׁלְתִּיאֵל‎ proper name, masculine (= שְׁאַלְתִּיאֵל‎ Thes Buhl); — father of Zerubbabel Hag 1:12, 14; 2:2; Σαλαθιηλ.
