conjunction or (whether aut or vel).

1 Gen 24:49; 31:43; Exod 4:11; Song 2:9; Lev 13:24; Num 5:30 + often (especially in laws); sometimes implying a preference, nearly = or rather Gen 24:55 עָשׂוֺר אוֺ יָמִים‎ a few days or ten Judg 18:19; 1Sam 29:3. Prefixed to the first as well as to the second alternative (rare) either (whether) ... or Lev 5:1; 13:48, 51; = or, if not Ezek 21:15 (si vera lectio) Ke Mal 2:17; Job 16:3; 22:11.

2 introducing a sentence, especially a particular case under a General principle, or = or if Exod 21:31 וג יִגַּח ׳אוֺבֵֿן‎ or if he gore a son, etc. 21:36; Lev 4:23, 28 (see Di) Leviticus 5:21; Leviticus 5:22; 25:49b; Num 5:14; 2Sam 18:13 or if I had dealt falsely against his life, then, etc., Ezek 14:17, 19 or if I send, etc.

3 if perchance, 1Sam 20:10 if perchance thy father answer thee with something hard, Lev 26:41.

4 once, with the jussive (as in Arabic with the subjunctive see Dr§ 175) = except: Isa 27:5 I would burn them together, בְּמָעֻזִּי יַחֲזֵק אוֺ‎ or else let him take hold (= except he take hold) of my strong-hold, etc.
