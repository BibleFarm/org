קָפַץ‎ (qâphats｜kaw-fats'｜verb｜draw together)
[Heb] קָפַץ‎ ETCBC: קפץ‎ (verb｜draw together) OSHL: s.cm.aa TWOT: 2051 GK: H7890 Greek: συνάγω, συνέχω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to draw together, i.e. close; by implication, to leap (by contracting the limbs); specifically, to die (from gathering up the feet)
KJV: shut (up), skip, stop, take out of the way.
