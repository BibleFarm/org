H8267. sheqer

שֶׁ֫קֶר‎113 noun masculine deception, disappointment, falsehood; — ׳שׁ‎ Exod 23:7 64t.; שָׁ֑קֶר‎ 5:9 42t.; plural שְׁקָרִים‎ Psa 101:7 3t.; suffix שִׁקְרֵיהֶם‎ Jer 23:22; —

1 deception, what deceives, disappoints, and betrays one : a molten image Jer 10:14 = 51:17; gift Prov 25:14; grace of person 31:30, etc.; שׁ ׳אֹ֫רַח‎ Psa 119:104; 119:128; שׁ ׳דֶּ֫רֶח‎ 119:29; בשׁ ׳בטח‎ Jer 13:25; שׁ ׳נחל‎ 16:19, compare also Psa 7:15; 33:17; 119:118; Isa 28:15; לַשֶּׁקֶר‎ for disappointment, in vain 1Sam 25:21; Jer 3:23.

2 deceit, fraud, wrong : שׁ (ל) ׳עשׂה‎ do or practise fraud, wrong Hosea 7:1; Jer 6:13; 8:8, 10; with בְ‎, 2Sam 18:13; שׁ ׳מְּעֻלַּת‎ Prov 11:18; שׁ ׳לֶ֫חֶם‎ 20:17 food got by fraud; שׁ ׳זֶ֫רע‎ Isa 57:4; שׁ ׳עֵט‎ Jer 8:8; שׁ ׳ילד‎ Psa 7:15 (|| עמל‎); שׁ קשׁת לשׁון ׳דרךְ‎ Jer 9:2; ׳בְּשׁ‎ 3:10 (|| בגד‎), adverb שׁקד‎ fraudulently, wrongfully Psa 35:19; 38:20; 69:5; 119:78; 119:86.

3 injurious falsehood, in testimony, especially in courts : שֶׁקֶר עַד‎ Exod 20:16 (E) Deut 19:18; Psa 27:12; Prov 6:19; 14:5; 25:18; שׁקרים עֵד‎ 12:17; 19:5, 9; שׁ ׳ענה‎ Deut 19:18 testify falsehood; שׁ ׳שְׁבֻעַת‎ Ze 8:17 false oath; לשׁ ׳נשׁבע‎ swear falsely Jer 5:2 5t.; שׁ על ׳נשׁבע‎ Leviticus 5:22; שׁ ׳דבּר‎ Micah 6:12 + (see [ דָּבַר‎], דָּבָר‎); שׁ י ׳אִמְרֵ‎ Isa 32:7; שׁ ׳שׂפת(י)‎ Psa 31:19; 120:2; Prov 10:18; 17:7; ׳שׁ טפל‎ Job 13:4; Psa 119:69, compare 119:163; absolute false ! 2Kin 9:12; Jer 37:14.

4 falsity, of false or self-deceived prophets: שׁ ׳נכּא‎ prophesy falsely Jer 14:14; 23:25-26, 29:21; + ל‎ person 27:10; 20:14, 16; ׳נ ׳לַשּׁ‎ 20:15; ׳נ ׳בשׁ‎ 5:31; 20:6; 29:9; שׁ ׳מוֺרֶה‎ Isa 9:14; Hab 2:18; שׁ ׳חזה‎ Zech 10:2; שׁ ׳חֲזוֺן‎ Jer 14:14, etc., (+ 12 t.); שׁ ׳רוּח‎.

5 lie, falsehood, in General: שׁ ׳דבר‎ Psa 101:7 (|| רְמִיָּה‎); שׁ ׳דְּבַר‎ Prov 13:5; of מִלִּים‎ Job 36:4; שׁ ׳לְשׁוֺן‎ false tongue Prov 6:17; 12:19; 21:6; 26:28; Psa 109:2; of lips Prov 12:22; שׁ ׳אִישׁ‎ 17:4 liar.
