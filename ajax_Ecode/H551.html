H551. omnam

אָמְנָם‎ adverb (= אֻמְנָם‎) verily, truly, in asseverations 2Kin 19:17 (= Isa 37:18) Job 19:4-5, 34:12; 36:4; Ruth 3:12, also ironical Job 9:2; 12:2.
