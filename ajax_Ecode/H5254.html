1 test, try (synonym בחן‎), absolute 1Sam 17:39 (a sword); with בְּ‎ Judges 7:39; Eccl 2:1; with accusative Dan 1:12, 14; accusative + בְּ‎ 1Kin 10:1 2Chr 9:1; Eccl 7:23.

3 test, try, prove, tempt [but not in modern sense of the word: see DrDeut 6:16; Psalms 453, 483]

b. Israel tests, or tries God: Exod 17:2, 7; Num 14:22 (J), Deut 6:16; Psa 78:18; 78:41; 78:56; 95:9; 106:14; so Ahaz, Isa 7:12.
