(מֵיטָב‎) noun [masculine] the best, only construct מֵיטַב‎ and as superlative; the best of a thing: כָּרְמוֺ ׳מ וּמ ׳שָׂדֵהוּ‎ Exod 22:4 (JE) the best of his field and the best of his vineyard (choicest fruit); ׳מ הַצּאֹן‎ the best of the sheep 1Sam 15:9, 15; ׳בְּמ הָאָרֶץ‎ in the best of the land Gen 47:6, 11 (P).
