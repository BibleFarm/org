לָאט‎ Judg 4:21, etc., see לוּט‎.

לָאט לָּט,‎ noun [masculine] secrecy, mystery; — absolute לָט‎ 1Sam 18:22 2t., לָאט‎ Judg 4:21; plural suffix לָטֵיהֶם‎ Exod 7:22 2t.; לַהֲטֵיהֶם‎ 7:11; — always with ׃בְּ‎

1 בַּלָּט‎ = secretly 1Sam 18:22; 24:5; Ruth 3:7; so בַּלָּאט‎ Judg 4:21.

2 בְּלָטֵיהֶם‎ with their mysteries = enchantments (i. e. of מִצְרַיִם חַרְטֻמֵּי‎) Exod 7:22; 8:3; 8:14 = בְּלַהֲטֵיהֶם‎ 7:11 (all P).
