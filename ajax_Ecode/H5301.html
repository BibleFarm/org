נָפַח‎ (nâphach｜naw-fakh'｜verb｜breathe)
[Heb] נָפַח‎ ETCBC: נפח‎ (verb｜blow) OSHL: n.ej.aa TWOT: 1390 GK: H5870 Greek: ἐμφυσάω, καίω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to puff, in various applications (literally, to inflate, blow hard, scatter, kindle, expire; figuratively, to disesteem)
KJV: blow, breath, give up, cause to lose (life), seething, snuff.

H5301. naphach
