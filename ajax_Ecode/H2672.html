H2672. chatsab

or chatseb [חָצַב‎] חָצֵב‎ verb hew, hew out, cleave (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic חֲצַב‎; SI4. 6 participle plural החצבם‎; compare Phoenician מחצב‎ = Hebrew id.) —

Qal Perfect חָצֵב‎ Isa 5:2; חָֽצְבָה‎ Prov 9:1; חָצַבְתִּי‎ Hosea 6:5, etc.; Imperfect וַיַּחְצֹב‎2Chr 26:10; 2 masculine singular תַּחְצֹב‎ Deut 8:9; Infinitive construct לַחְצֹב‎ Jer 2:13; לַחְצוֺב‎ 1Chr 22:2; Participle active חֹצֵב‎ Isa 10:15 4t.; construct חֹצְבִי‎ 22:16; חֹצְבִים‎ 1Chr 22:2-3t.; חֹצְבֵי‎ 2Kin 12:13; passive חֲצוּבִים‎ Deut 6:11; Neh 9:25; —

1 hew out, (dig), wine-vat יֶקֶב‎ Isa 5:2; sepulchre (קֶבֶר‎) 22:16 (twice in verse); cisterns בֹּרוֺת‎ Deut 6:11 (twice in verse); 2Chr 26:10; Neh 9:25, compare Jer 2:13; of mining תַּת ׳מהרריה נחשׁת‎ Deut 8:9 out of its mountains thou mayst hew out copper.

2. a. hew stone 1Chr 22:2; metaphor of pillars Prov 9:1 (subject wisdom); elsewhere participle, hewer of stone 2Kin 12:13; 1Chr 22:2, 15 (see SI4.6above); probably 1 Kings 5:29; 2Chr 2:1; 2:17 (בָּהָר׳ח‎ see Be), compare 24:12; Ezra 3:7.

b. apparently hew wood Isa 10:15 ׳ח בוֺ‎ (i.e. בְּגַרְזֶן‎).

3 metaph. hew in pieces Hosea 6:5 figurative of ׳י‎ attacking people by agency of prophets (|| הֲרַגְתִּים‎); divide, cleave subject ׳י‎s voice Psa 29:7 object אשׁ להבות‎, i.e. the thunder of his voice sends forked lightnings (but on text compare Che and critical note)

Niph`al Imperfect יֵחָצְבוּן‎ be cut, hewn, graven (words on rock) Job 19:24.

Pu`al Perfect חֻצַּבְתֶּם‎ Isa 51:1 figurative, hewn out of rock (of Israel's origin; || נֻקַּר‎).

Hiph`il Participle מַחֲצֶ֫בֶת‎ Isa 51:9 hew in pieces = Qal 3, figurative of destroying רהב‎ = Egypt (compare Psa 89:11).
