H1965. hekal

[הֵיכַל‎] noun masculine palace, temple (Assyrian loan-word; Biblical Hebrew; also Palmyrene היכלא‎ and הכלא‎ Lzb259 SAC43); — emphatic הֵיכְלָא‎ Ezra 4:14 +; construct הֵיכַל‎ Dan 4:26; suffix לִי הֵיכְּ‎ 4:1, לֵהּ‎- 6:19; —

1 place of king Dan 4:1; 4:26; 5:5; 6:19; Ezra 4:14 (see מְלַח‎).

2 temple:

a. in Jerusalem Dan 5:2-3, Ezra 5:14 a.; 5:15; 6:5 (twice in verse).

b. in Babylon Ezra 5:14 b.c.
