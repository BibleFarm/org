עֲרַב‎ noun [masculine] desert-plateau, steppe (compare CheIntr. Is. 129); — בַּעֲרַב בַּעֲרָ֑ב‎ (si vera lectio) Isa 21:13 (twice in verse) in the steppe (of what we know as North Arabia); but in vb Vrss Lo Che GuKau and others בָּעֶרֶב‎ in the evening.
