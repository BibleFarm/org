דַּל‎ adjective low, weak, poor, thin (especially common in Wisdom literature and poetry) — דַּל‎ Lev 14:21 8t., דָּ֑ל‎ Exod 23:3 17t. + שָדּל‎ Psa 82:3; דַּלִּים‎ Amos 2:7 17t.; דַּלּוֺת‎ Gen 41:19; — weak, thin, of kine 41:19 (E); of Amnon 2Sam 13:4; weak, of family of Saul 3:1 (opposed to חָזֵק‎) compare Judg 6:15; reduced, poor (opposed to rich) Lev 14:21(P) Ruth 3:10 (opposed to עָשִׁיר‎) Jer 5:4 (opposed to הגדולים‎, 5:5) Prov 28:15; Zeph 3:12 (וָדָ֑ל עָנִי עַם‎); mostly substantive, a poor (man), the poor Exod 23:3 (JE); opposed to עָשִׁיר‎ 30:15 (P) Prov 10:15; 22:16; 28:11; opposed to הוֺן‎ 19:4; || אביון‎ 1Sam 2:8; Job 5:16; Psa 72:13; 82:4; 113:7; Prov 14:31; Isa 14:30; 25:4; Amos 4:1; 8:6; compare also Job 20:10, 19; Prov 22:9; Jer 39:10; reduced, weak, helpless (|| עני‎) Job 34:28; Psa 82:3(|| also יתום‎), Prov 22:22; 10:2; 11:4; 26:6; Amos 2:7; || אלמנה‎ Job 31:16; opposed to שׁוֺעַ‎ 34:19; — compare also Psa 41:2; Prov 19:17; 21:13; 28:3, 8; 29:7, 14.
