H44. Abiezer

אֲבִיעֶ֫זֶר‎ proper name, masculine (my father is help) — = אִיעֶזֶר‎ Num 26:30 —

1 a Manassite, called 'son' of Gilead Num 26:30 (compare Di) Josh 17:2; Judg 6:34; 8:2; and son of Gilead's sister 1Chr 7:18.

2 a Benjamite, a warrior of David 2Sam 23:27; 1Chr 11:28; 27:12.
