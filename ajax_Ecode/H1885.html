H1885. Dathan

דָּתָן‎ proper name, masculine (compare Assyrian Datana ShlmObelisk, 1. 161) a Reubenite, son of Eliab Num 16:1, 12, 24, 25, 27 (twice in verse); 26:9 (twice in verse); Deut 11:6; Psa 106:17.
