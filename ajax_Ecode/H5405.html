H5405. nashath

נָשַׁת‎ verb be dry, parched; —

Qal Perfect3feminine singular נָשָׁ֑תָּה בַּצָּמָא לְשׁוֺנָם‎ Isa 41:17 (on dagesh forte affect. see Ges§ 20i); also figurative גְבוּרָתָם נָָֽשְׁתָה‎ Jer 51:30, their might is dry, i.e. fails.

Niph`al Perfect3plural consecutive מֵהַיָּ֑ם וְנִשְּׁתומַֿיִם‎ Isa 19:5 and water shall be dried up from the sea; compare Imperfect מַיּם יַנָָּֽתְשׁוּ‎ Jer 18:14, where read probably יִנָָּֽשְׁחוּ‎ (Gf Gie and others).
