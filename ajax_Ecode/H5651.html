II. עֶ֫בֶד‎ proper name, masculine (servant of God = עַבְדְּאֵל‎); —

1 father of Gaal Judg 9:26, 28, 30, 31, 35 (GFM, after HollenbThLz 1891, 371 [compare BuIb. 1892, 63] עֹבֵד‎, as ᵐ5‎L; see also GrayProp. N. 184, 272); ᵐ5‎ Ιωβηλ, A ᵐ5‎L Αβεδ.
