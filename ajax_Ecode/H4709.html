H4709. Mitspah

מִצְמָּה‎38 proper name, of a location (properly outlook-point or +?; with article (exceptHos Josh 5:1)); —

1 in Gilead, explanation as name given to Laban's cairn, north of Jabbok Gen 31:49 (on text see Ball Holz; ᵐ5‎ ἡ ὅρασις).

2 probably south of Jabbok, Judg 10:17 (ἡ σκοπιά), 11:11, 34 (Μασσηφα; so ᵐ5‎L 10:17), Hosea 5:1 (ἡ σκοπιά); apparently = II. מִצְמֶּה‎

3, BuhlGeogr. 262 (מֶּה‎-, מָּה‎-, seem to interchange); site unknown; see conjectures in Buhll.c., compare GASmGeogr. 586.

3. near Hermon, הַמּ ׳אֶרֶץ‎ Josh 11:3, Μασευμαν, Μασσηφα(θ), = בִּקְעַתמִֿצְמֶּה‎ 11:8, Μασσωχ, Μασσηφα; see BuhlGeogr. 240.

4 old sacred place in Benjamin, Μασσηφα, etc.; — ׳הַמּ‎ Judg 20:1 +, הַמִּצְמָּ֫תָה‎ 1Sam 7:5 +; — Judg 20:1, 3; 21:1, 5, 8; 1Sam 7:5 7t. 1 Samuel 7; 1 Samuel 10; 1Kin 15:22 2Chr 16:6; 2Kin 25:23, 25; Jer 40:10 12t. 40, 41; Neh 3:7, 15, 19; = הַמִּצְמֶּה‎ Josh 18:26; — modern Nabî Samwîl, 5 miles northwest of Jerusalem, see BuhlGeogr. 167 f..
