H3409. yarek

יָרֵךְ‎ noun feminineNum 5:27 thigh, loin, side, base (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic יַרְכָּא‎; Zinjirli ירך‎ loin; Assyrian arku, arkâtu, back, rear, hereafter DlHWB 242; Arabic hip) — absolute יָרֵךְ‎ Judg 15:8 6t.; construct יֶ֫רֶךְ‎ Gen 24:9 11t. + 2Sam 3:27 (see below); suffix יְרֵכִי‎ Gen 24:2; 47:29; יְרֵכֵךְ‎ Num 5:21; יְרֵכוֺ‎ Gen 32:26 6t.; יְרֵכָהּ‎ Exod 25:31+ 3t.; dual יְרֵכַיִם‎ 28:42; suffix יְרֵכַיִךְ‎ Cant 7:2; —

1 thigh,

a. outside of thigh, where sword was worn, עַליְֿרֵכוֺ חֶרֶב שִׂים‎ Exod 32:27 (E); יְמִינוֺ עַליֶֿרֶךְ אֹתָהּ וַיַחְגֹּר‎ Judg 3:16 and he girded it upon his right thigh, 3:21; Psa 45:4; Song 3:8; הַיָּרֵךְ כַּף‎ Genesis 32:33 hollow of the thigh, Gen 32:26 (twice in verse); Genesis 32:33 (all J); יְרֵכַיִךְ חַמּוּקֵי‎ Cant 7:2 the roundings of thy thighs; וְעַדיְֿרֵכַיִם מִמָּתְנַיִם‎ Exod 28:42 (P); || בֶּטֶן‎ Num 5:21-22, 27 (P); עַליְֿרֵכוֺ צֹלֵעַ‎ Gen 32:32 (J), limping upon his thigh; עַליָֿרֵח סָפַק‎ Jer 31:19 and (with אֶלֿ‎) Ezek 21:17, smite upon, thigh, in token of consternation; יָרֵךְ עַלֿ שׁוֺק אוֺתָם וַיַּךְ‎ Judg 15:8 and he smote them, hip upon thigh, a great slaughter.

b. thigh = loins, as seat of procreative power (RSK. 34; Semitic i, 360 (380)) ירך יֹצְאֵי‎ those proceeding from the loins of any one Gen 46:26; Exod 1:5 (both P), Judg 8:30; hence ירך תַּחַת יַד שִׂים‎ place the hand under thigh, in taking oath Gen 24:2, 9 (J), 47:29.

2 side (flank) of altar 2Kin 16:14; Lev 1:11 (P); of tabernacle Exod 40:22, 24; Num 3:29, 35 (all P); also הַשַּׁעַר יֶרֶךְ‎ (for הַשּׁ ׳תּוֺךְ ᵑ0‎) ᵐ5‎ Th We Dr Bu Kit.

3 base (loins) of candlestick Exod 25:31; 37:17; Num 8:4 (all P).
