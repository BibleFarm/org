גָּזִית‎ noun feminine a cutting, hewing — גָּזִית‎ Exod 20:25 10t., always absolute; — hewing, ג ׳אַבְנֵי‎ = hewn stones (building-stones) 1 Kings 5:31; 1Chr 22:2; Ezek 40:42 (for tables), compare ג כְּמִדּוֺת יְקָרוֺת ׳אֲבָנִים‎ 1Kin 7:9, 11 (but Klo strike out as gloss, compare ᵐ5‎); also without אבני‎ = hewn stones Exod 20:25 (altar) Amos 5:11 (ג ׳בָּֽתֵּי‎) Isa 9:9 (opposed to לְבֵנִים‎); ג ׳טוּרֵי‎ 1Kin 6:36, ג ׳טוּרִים‎ 7:12; also Lam 3:9 בְּג דַּרְכִּי ׳גָּדַר‎.
