H5689. agab

[עָגַב‎] verb have inordinate affection, lust (compare Arabic wonder, admire, beloved; and (on sens. obsc. in Late Hebrew) LevyNHWB iii. 616); —

Qal Perfect3feminine singular עָֽגְבָה‎ Ezek 23:7, 9, 12; Imperfect3feminine singular וַתַּעְגַּב‎ 23:5, + 23:16 Kt (Qr וַתַּעְגְּבָה‎), וַתַּעְגְּבָּה‎ 23:20 (Ew§ 191 c Ol§ 228 b); Participle plural עֹגְבִים‎ Jer 4:30; — lust after (עַלֿ‎), only in figure of relations of Samaria and Jerusalem (personified) with foreigners Ezek 23:5, 9, 12, 16, 20, so with accusative 23:7; participle as substantive paramours Jer 4:30.
