אוֺצָר‎ noun masculineProv 15:16 treasure, store, treasury, storehouse (so Aramaic, also ) — אוֺצָר‎ 15:16 +; construct אוֺצַר‎ Josh 6:19 +; suffix אוֺצָרוֺ‎ Deut 28:12 2t.; plural אוֺצָרוֺת‎ Joel 1:17 +; אֹצָרוֺת‎ Prov 21:6 +; construct אוֺצְרוֺת‎ Micah 6:10 +; אֹצְרוֺת‎ Job 38:22 +; plural suffix אוֺצְרֹתָֽי׃‎ Deut 32:34; Isa 39:4; אֹצְרֹתָֽי׃‎ Deut 32:34; אוֺצְרוֺתֶיךָ‎ Jer 17:3; אוֺצְרֹתָם‎ Isa 30:6; אֹצְרֹתֵיהֶם‎ Prov 8:21, etc. —

1 treasure(s), Generally plural (gold, silver, costly utensils, etc.) Josh 6:19, 24; Isa 2:7; 30:6; 45:3 (׳א חשֶׁח‎, i.e. concealed, hoarded) Hosea 13:15; Jer 15:13; 17:3; 20:5; 48:7; 49:4; 51:13; 1Kin 14:26 (twice in verse); 2Kin 24:13 (twice in verse), perhaps also Isa 39:2, 4 = 2Kin 20:13, 15; wealth Prov 15:16; 21:6, 20; gathered for temple-building (singular) ׳א י ׳בֵּית‎ 1Chr 29:8, ׳א הַמְּלָאכָה‎ Ezra 2:69 = Neh 7:70, also 7:69 (compare 1Chr 26:27).

2 store, supply of food, drink, etc., 2Chr 11:11; 1Chr 27:27-28, figurative ׳א רֶשַׁע‎ Micah 6:10; Prov 10:2; י ׳יִרְאַת אוֺצָרוֺ הִיא‎ Isa 33:6.

3 אוֺצָר בֵּית‎

a. treasure-house Neh 10:39 compare Dan 1:2;

b. storehouse, magazine Mal 3:10; & without בית‎ a. treasure-house or chamber, treasury 1Kin 7:51; 15:18 (twice in verse); 2Kin 12:19; 14:14; 16:8; 18:15; Jer 38:11; 50:37 (?) 1Chr 9:26; 26:20 (twice in verse); 26:22, 24, 26; 28:12 (twice in verse); 2Chr 5:1; 16:2 (?) 32:27 compare Ezek 28:4; b. storehouse Joel 1:17; Prov 8:21; 1Chr 27:25 (twice in verse); Neh 13:12-13,

c. magazine of weapons, figurative of Yahweh's armoury Jer 50:25;

d. storehouses of God for rain, snow, hail, wind, sea Deut 28:12; Job 38:22 (twice in verse); Jer 10:13; 51:16; Psa 135:7; 33:7.
