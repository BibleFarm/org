כֹּוס‎ (kôwç｜koce｜noun｜either masculine or feminine｜cup｜owl)
[Heb] כֹּוס‎, כֹּוס‎ ETCBC: כֹּוס‎ (subs｜cup), כֹּוס‎ (subs｜owlet) OSHL: k.aw.an, k.aw.am TWOT: 965 966 GK: H3926, H3927 Greek: ποτήριον, φιάλη
Derivation: from an unused root meaning to hold together;
Strong's: a cup (as a container), often figuratively, a lot (as if a potion); also some unclean bird, probably an owl (perhaps from the cup-like cavity of its eye)
KJV: cup, (small) owl. Compare כִּיס‎.
