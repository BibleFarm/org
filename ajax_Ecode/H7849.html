H7849. shatach

[שָׁטַח‎] verb spread, spread abroad (Late Hebrew id.; Arabic spread out; so Ethiopic Aramaic שְּׁטַח‎ ); —

Qal Perfect3plural suffix consecutive וּטָחוּם לַשֶּׁמֶשׁ‎ Jer 8:2 they shall spread them abroad, etc., accusative of many separate things, so Imperfect3feminine singular וַקתִּשְׁטַךְ‎ 2Sam 17:19 (עַל‎ location; distinguished from מָּרַשׂ‎ spread covering); 3 masculine plural + infinitive absolute שָׁטוֺחַ לָהֶם וַיִּשְׁטְחוּ‎ Num 11:32 (J) they spread (that is, the quails) for themselves all abroad round about the camp (i.e. to dry them; ᵑ9‎ siccaverunt; compare Di Gray); Participle לַנּוֺיִם שֹׁטֵחַ‎ Job 12:23 expanding the nations (compare ל‎ 3b).

Pi`el Perfect1singular כַמָּ֑י אֵלֶיךָ שִׁטַּחְתִּי‎ Psa 88:10 I have spread out unto thee my palms.
