II. [צוּק‎] verb pour out, melt (|| יצק‎); —

Qal Perfect3plural (according to Thes and others) צָקוּן‎ Isa 26:16, but see below; Imperfect3masculine singular נְחוּשָׁה יָצוּק וְאֶבֶן‎ Job 28:2 (read perhaps יָצוּקוּ‎, see Bu) and stone (ore, men) melt it into copper; מַּלְגֵישָֿׁ֑מֶן עִמָּדִי יָצוּק צוּר‎ 29:6 the rock used to pour out beside me streams of oil. — Isa 26:16 (as above) must mean they poured out (uttered) a whisper (לַחַשׁ‎; i. e. prayer); but form most improbable; Koppe Gr Di Du and others propose לַחַשׁ צְקוֺן‎ (from assumed צָקוֺן‎, I. צוק‎) = constraint of (like that of) magic; < Houb Kit ׳וגו בְלַחַץ צָֽעֲקוּ‎, or CheHpt לָנוּ מוּסָֽרְךָ כִּי מִלַּחַץ צָעַקְנוּ‎.
