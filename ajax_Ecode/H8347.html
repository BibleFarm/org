H8347. Sheshak

שֵׁשַׁךְ‎ proper name, of a territory = בָּבֶל‎ (Jerome Rabb and modern; by Atbaš (׳בש׳את‎), i.e. disguising name by substituting last letter of alphabet for first, next last for second, etc., compare Thes); — Jer 51:41 and hence (perhaps gloss) 25:26 (see Gie).
