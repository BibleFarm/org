H709. Argob

אַרְגֹּב‎ proper name

1. location (heap, or region of clods; 'glede' GASmG551); — always א ׳חֶבֶל‎ Deut 3:4, 14; 1Kin 4:13, ׳ח ׳הָא‎ Deut 3:13; = measured region of Argob; some well-defined district of Bashan (apparently identification with יָאִיר חַוֺת‎ 3:14 [where ׳ח ׳י‎ in Bashan, so Josh 13:30], but this a harmonistic correction; ׳ח ׳י‎ in fact in Gilead [Num 32:41; Judg 10:4, and distinguished from אַרְגֹּב‎ 1Kin 4:13 compare Deut 3:13); exact location dubious, GASml.c. and especially Dr3:4; BuhlGeogr.18 thinks of ‚uwet, south of Upper Yarmuk, a border district between Bashan and Gilead. — Αρσοβ; 1Kin 4:13 Ερεβαταμ, ᵐ5‎L Πασαβαν, A Ερσαβ.

2. masculine 2Kin 15:25, Αρσοβ dubious; see Klo Kit Benz Bur.
