H6467. poal

מֹּ֫עַל‎37 noun masculineProv 20:11 doing, deed, work (in poetry and late, nearly = מַעֲשֶׂה‎); — ׳פ‎ absolute Hab 1:1; Psa 44:2, construct Deut 33:11 +; suffix מָּעֳלוֺ‎ 32:4 +, מֹּעֲלוֺ‎ Isa 1:31 (Köii.1, 35, 493 Ges§ 93q, see below), Jer 22:13; מָּעָלְכֶם‎ Isa 41:24, etc.; plural מְּעָלִים‎ 2Sam 23:20; 1Chr 11:22; —

1 deed, thing done:

a. of God, מָּעֳלוֺ‎ his work in providence Deut 32:4; Isa 5:12; Job 36:24; in deliverance Psa 44:2 (as accusative of congnate meaning with verb), 77:13 5t.; in judgment 64:10; 95:9, as accusative of congnate meaning with verb Hab 1:5.

b. of false gods Isa 41:24.

c. of men, daily toil Job 24:5; Psa 104:23; specific act Ruth 2:12; ׳פ יָדָיו‎ of sacrificing Deut 33:11; plural of achievements 2Sam 23:20 = 1Chr 11:32; action as having moral quality Job 34:11; Prov 20:11; 24:12, 29, evil implied Jer 25:14; 50:29; Job 36:9; Psa 28:4, ׳פ כַּמָּיו‎ 9:17; ׳פ חָמָס‎ Isa 59:6; good action Prov 21:8.

2 work, thing made:

a. by hands of God Isa 45:9, 11 (of men).

b. by man Isa 1:31 (i.e. probably his idol Ges Checomm. Di and others; > read בַּעֲלוֺ‎ LagSemitic i. 5 StaZAW iii (1888), 12f.).

3 wages of work Jer 22:13; Job 7:2.

4 acquisition of treasures Prov 21:6.
