H6068. Anathoth

עֲנָתוֺת‎ and (1Kin 2:26) עֲנָתֹת‎ proper name Αναθωθ;

1. location (compare goddess Anat, reference below foregoing); — Isa 10:30; 1Kin 2:26; Jer 1:1; 11:21-22, 32:7-8, 9; Josh 21:18 (P), 1Chr 6:45; Ezra 2:23 = Neh 7:27; 11:32; modern `Anâtâ, approximately 3 miles north-northeast from Jerusalem, compare BuhlGeogr. 175 GASmGeogr. 315.

2. masculine a. 1Chr 7:8.

b. Neh 10:20.
