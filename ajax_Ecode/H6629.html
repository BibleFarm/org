[Heb] צֹאן‎ ETCBC: צֹאן‎ (subs｜cattle) OSHL: r.ab.ab TWOT: 1864a GK: H7366 Greek: ἀμνός, κτῆνος, λαός, ποίμνιον, προβατικός, πρόβατον
Derivation: or צאֹון‎; (Psalm 144:13), from an unused root meaning to migrate;
Strong's: a collective name for a flock (of sheep or goats); also figuratively (of men)
KJV: (small) cattle, flock ([phrase] -s), lamb ([phrase] -s), sheep(-cote, -fold, -shearer, -herds).

צאֹן‎273 collective noun feminineGen 30:36 (possibly masculine 30:39 a +, KöSynt. § 247 g, but dubious, 30:39b with verb feminine plural, as usually; in 1Sam 15:14 join הַוֶּה‎ with קוֺל‎; see further AlbrZAW xv (1895), 316 ff.) small cattle, sheep and goats, flock, flocks; — ׳צ‎ absolute Gen 4:2 +; construct 29:9 +; suffix צאֹנוֺ‎ 4:4 +; צאֹנֵנוּ‎ Exod 10:9, צאֹונֵוּ‎ Psa 144:13; as plural צאֹנֵינוֺ‎ Neh 10:37 van d. H. Gi (not Baer; very late, si vera lectio), etc.; —

1 literally

a. small cattle, usually of sheep and goats in one flock (RobBR i. 477) Gen 30:31-32, +; sheep specified also 21:28 (הַצּ ׳כִּבְשׂת‎), 31:28 (צ ׳אֵילֵי‎) +; sheep only 1Sam 25:2 (+עִוִּים‎); goats specified Gen 27:9; Jer 50:8; ׳צ‎ as yielding (1) animals for food Amos 6:4 (בָּרִים‎), 1Sam 14:32; 2Sam 12:4; Neh 5:18 +; ׳צ מַאְַבָל‎ Psa 44:12 (in simile); (2) wool Gen 31:19 + (see [ גָּזַז‎], גֵּז‎); (3) milk, צ ׳חֲלֵב‎ Deut 32:14 (? goats' milk, compare Prov 27:27); (4) sacrificial victims Gen 4:4 (J), Lev 1:2, 10; 3:6; Num 15:3 (all P), 22:40 (E), Deut 12:6, 17, 21 + often; so קָדָשִׁים צאֹן‎ Ezek 36:38 a, compare בְּכוֺרֵי וְצאֹנֵינוּ בְקָרֵינוּ‎ Neh 10:37; in General as property (often + בָּקָר‎, etc.) Gen 12:16; 13:5; 24:35; Job 1:3; 42:12 + often (see also II. [עַשְׁתָּרוֺת‎]); gift Gen 20:14; 21:27; 38:17 (goat), Deut 15:14; 2Chr 17:11; booty 1Sam 15:9, 15 + .

b. ׳צ‎ sometimes of a definite number of animals (i.e. as plural of שֶׂה‎, etc.): two Isa 7:21, four Exodus 21:37 (larger numbers see 1Sam 25:2, 5, 18; Num 31:32, 36; Neh 5:18; Job 1:3; 42:12 above). — See also בֵּן‎ 7b; עֵדֶר נָוֶה, מַרְבֵּץ, מִכְלָה, גְּדֵרָה,‎; שַׁצֵר רֹצֶה, כֶלֶב,‎.

2 simile of multitude Ezek 36:37-38, compare Psa 107:41; of children Job 21:11; of dead, with death as shepherd Psa 49:15; of (shepherdless) Israel Num 27:17; 1Kin 22:17 2Chr 18:16; Zech 10:2; 13:7, wandering in sin Isa 53:6; of Israel led by ׳י‎ Psalm 77:21; Psa 78:52; 80:2, compare Zech 9:16 (on text see We Now); Ezek 34:12; of Israel in distress, ׳כְּצ טִבְחָת‎ Psa 44:23, compare (of wicked) ׳צ לְטִבְתָה‎ Jer 12:3; of scattered Babylonian Isa 13:14.

3 metaphor of multitude, ׳צ אָרָם‎ Ezek 36:38; of Israel 2Sam 24:17 = 1Chr 21:17; Jer 23:1-2, 3; Ezek 24:5, and especially sustained figurative 34:2, 317t. 34, also Zech 11:7, 17; ׳צ אֹבְדוֺת‎ Jer 50:6, ׳צ תִּפְאַרְתֵךְ‎ 13:20; of Israel under ׳י‎s care, (וֺ‎-, יִ֯‎) ׳צ מַרְעִיתֶ֑ךָד‎ Psa 74:1; 79:13; 100:3; Ezek 34:31, also ׳צ יָדוֺ‎ Psa 95:7(|| מַרְעִיתוֺעַםֿ‎; read perhaps יָדוֺ צַם ׳צ, מַרְעִיתוֺ‎, so Che and others); ׳צ נַחֲלָתֶךָ‎ Micah 7:14; also in combinations: הַצֵּ ׳אַדִּירֵי‎ Jer 25:34-35, 36, ׳צ חַהֲרֵגָה‎ Zech 11:4, 7 (compare Psa 44:23), הַצּ ׳עֲנִיֵּי‎ 44:7; 44:11 (all of Israel); of Edom צּ הַ ׳צְעִירֵי‎ Jer 49:20; 50:45.
