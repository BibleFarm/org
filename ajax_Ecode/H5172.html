Pi`el practise divination, divine, observe signs (denominative from נָהָשׁ‎ according to NöZeitschr. f. Völkerpsychol. i (1860), 413 BauSemitic Rel. i. 287 LagBN 188 GerberVerb. 1 practise divination Gen 44:5 (J; by means of cup, i.e. by hydromancy), with implied power to learn secret things 44:15; condemned by prophetic writer 2Kin 21:6 2Chr 33:6 (+ וְיִדְּעֹנִים אוֺב וְעָשָׂח עוֺנֵן‎), 2Kin 17:17 (|| קְסָמִים וַיִּקְסְמוּ‎); forbidden in D and H: Deut 18:10 ( + וּמְכַשֵּׁף מְעוֺנֵן קְסָמִים קֹסֵם‎), Lev 19:26 (H; || תְּעוֺנֵנ֑וּ‎).

2 observe the signs or omens Gen 30:27 (j); so probably יְנַחֲשׁוּ‎ 1Kin 20:33 now the men were observing the signs.
