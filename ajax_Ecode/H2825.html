חֲשֵׁכָה‎ noun feminine darkness (chiefly in poetry) — ׳ח‎ Gen 15:12 2t., so read also probably Micah 3:6 (for חָֽשְׁכָה ᵑ0‎); חֲשֵׁיכָה‎ Psa 139:12; construct חֶשְׁכַּת‎ 18:12; plural חֲשֵׁכִים‎ Isa 50:10; — darkness, opposed to light (אוֺרָה‎) Psa 139:12; supernatural, ׳ח גדולה‎ Gen 15:12 (JE); מַיִם חֶשְׁכַּתֿ‎ Psa 18:12 in theoph. (but || 2Sam 22:12 חַשְׁרַתמֿים‎ a mass of water); figurative = lack of understanding Psa 82:5; = distress Isa 8:22 (|| צָרָה‎), 50:10.
