H6794. tsinnuwr

צִנּוֺר‎ noun masculine pipe, spout, conduit (so Late Hebrew); — absolute בַּצִּנּוֺר וְיִגַּע‎ 2Sam 5:8 (i.e., si vera lectio, of Jerusalem, but very dubious, compare Dr HPS); plural suffix צִנּוֺרֶיךָ קוֺל‎ Psa 42:8 the sound of thy (water-) spouts figurative, of sluices of heaven opened, compare מֶּלֶג אֲרֻבֶּה,‎.
