she-goat; — absolute ׳ע‎ Gen 15:9 6t.; plural עַזִּים‎ 27:9 65t.; suffix עִזֶּיךָ‎ 31:38; —

1 as property Gen 30:32-33, 35 (J), 31:15, 38 (E), 1Sam 25:2.

2 as food: עִזִּים (גְּדִי) גְּדָיֵי‎ kid(s) of she-goats Gen 27:9, 16 (J) + 7 t., עִזִּים שְׂעִיר‎ 37:31 (J) a buck of goats, he-goat, עִזִּים שֵׂה‎ Deut 14:4 a (n individual) goat; compare Lev 7:23 (P; fat forbidden); also ע ׳חֲלֵב‎ Prov 27:27 goats' milk.

3 as sacrificial victim, chiefly P:

a. in General Lev 22:27 (H), Num 15:11.

b. in בְּרִית‎ Gen 15:9 (J E; compare 15:18).

c. מֶּסַח‎ Exod 12:5 (שֶׂה‎), עִזִּים בְּנֵי‎2Chr 35:7.

d. עוֺלָה‎ Lev 22:19 (H), 1:10; Num 28:30 (ע ׳שְׂעִיר‎).

e. שְׁלָמִים זֶבַח‎ Lev 3:12; 17:3 (H; compare 17:5), אִשֶּׁה‎ Num 18:17 (ע ׳בְּכוֺר‎).

f. ע ׳שְׂעִיר חַטָּאת,‎ Ezek 43:22; 45:23; Lev 4:23 3t. Leviticus; Num 7:16 19t. Numbers; עִזִּים שְׂעִירַת‎ a single she-goat Lev 4:28; 5:6; זִּים עְפִרֵי‎2Chr 29:21 he-goats.

4 עִזִּים‎ = goats' hair, as material 1Sam 19:13, 16; Exod 25:4 6t. P (35:26 object of טָווּ‎!).

5 in simile עִזִּים חֲשַׂפֵי‎ 1Kin 20:27 (see [ חָשִׂיף‎]); הָע ׳כְּעֵדַר‎ Song 4:1; 6:5, of flowing, undulating hair.

6 הָע ׳צְפִיר‎ Du Song 8:5, 8 he-goat in vision.
