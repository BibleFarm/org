חָלַם‎ (châlam｜khaw-lam'｜verb｜be healthy｜dream)
[Heb] חָלַם‎ ETCBC: חלם‎ (verb｜dream) OSHL: h.cu.aa, h.cv.aa TWOT: 662 663 GK: H2730, H2731 Greek: ἐμπίπτω, ἐνυπνιάζομαι, ἐνύπνιον, ἐνύπνιον, ὁράω, παρακαλέω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: properly, to bind firmly, i.e. (by implication) to be (causatively to make) plump; also (through the figurative sense of dumbness) to dream
KJV: (cause to) dream(-er), be in good liking, recover.

H2492. chalam

I. [חָלַם‎] verb be healthy, strong (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic Ethpe`el be made healthy, strong, Aph`el causative, also derivatives; חֲלַם‎ press firmly together) —

Qal Imperfect בְנֵיהֶם יַחְלְמוּ‎ Job 39:4 their young are healthy.

Hiph`il Imperfect2masculine singular וְתַחֲלִימֵנִי‎ Isa 38:16 and restore me to health (|| וְהַחֲיֵ֫ינִי‎).

II. חָלַם‎ verb dream (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic , חֲלַם‎; Ethiopic Arabic , dream, experience an emission of the seminal fluid, attain to puberty) —

Qal Perfect ׳ח‎ Gen 42:9; Dan 2:1, חָלַמְתִּי‎ Gen 37:9 3t., חָלָ֑מְתִּי‎ 37:6 2t., etc., + 3 t. Perfect; Imperfect יַחֲלֹם‎ Isa 29:8 5t., יַחְלְמוּ‎ Job 39:4, וַיַּחַלְמוּ‎ Gen 40:5, יַחֲלֹמ֑וּן‎ Joel 3:1, נַחַלְמָה‎ Gen 41:11; Participle חוֺלֵם‎ Deut 13:4,חֹלֵם‎ Gen 41:1; Deut 13:2; 13:6, חֹלְמִים‎ Psa 126:1; — dream:

a. of ordinary dreams of sleep Isa 29:8 (twice in verse); Psa 126:1.

b. of dreams with prophetic meaning: of Jacob Gen 28:12, Joseph 37:5-6, 9 (twice in verse); 37:10; 42:9, of Pharaoh and his servants 40:5, 8; 41:1, 5, 11 (twice in verse); 41:15 (all E, not elsewhere in Hexateuch); of Midianite Judg 7:13, Nebuchad. Dan 2:1, 3, old men in latter days Joel 3:1.

c. of dreams of false prophets Deut 13:2; 13:4; 13:6; Jer 23:25 (twice in verse).

Hiph`il Participle, מַחְלְמִים‎ dream (of false prophets, with accusative of congnate meaning with verb) Jer 29:8 (but read חֹלְמִים‎ [מ‎ by dittograph from אַתֶּם‎], Hi Gf Gie Gr).
