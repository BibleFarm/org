H552. umnam

אֻמְנָם‎ adverb (from אֹמֶן‎ by affix ָ  ם‎) verily, truly, indeed, always in interrogative Gen 18:13 (J), elsewhere הַאֻמְנָם‎ Num 22:37 (E) 1Kin 8:27; 2Chr 6:18; Psa 58:2.
