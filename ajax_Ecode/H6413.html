מְּלֵיטָה‎ noun feminine escape (on form compare BaNB 144, 166); — absolute ׳פ‎ Gen 32:9 +, מְּלֵטָה‎ Exod 10:5 +; constructמְּלֵיטַת‎ 2Kin 19:30 +; —

1 escape, deliverance Gen 45:7 (E) Jer 25:35.

2 elsewhere probably always escaped remnant:

a. of green things (from locusts) Exod 10:5 (J) Joel 2:3.

b. of person Gen 32:9 (J) Judg 21:17; Isa 15:9; Jer 50:29; 1Chr 4:43; 2Chr 12:7; 20:24; 30:6; Ezra 9:8; Dan 11:42; + מִמְּנֵי‎ 2Sam 15:14.
