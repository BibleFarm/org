לָהַהּ‎ (lâhahh｜law-hah'｜verb｜amaze｜languish)
[Heb] לָהַהּ‎, יָלַהּ‎, לָהָה‎, לִהְלֵהַּ‎ ETCBC: יָלַהּ‎ (verb｜be confused), להה‎ (verb｜be crazy) OSHL: l.ao.aa, l.ao.ab TWOT: 1079 GK: H3532, H4263, H4264, H4271 Greek: ἐκλείπω
Derivation: a primitive root meaning properly, to burn, i.e. (by implication)
Strong's: to be rabid (figuratively, insane); also (from the exhaustion of frenzy) to languish
KJV: faint, mad.
