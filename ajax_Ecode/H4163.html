H4163. motsaah

[מוֺצָאָה‎] noun feminine only plural:

a. Micah 5:1 מוֺצָאוֺתָיו‎ (compare future ruler out of Bethlehem), his origin.

b. 2Kin 10:27 Qr מוֺצָאוֺת‎ places of going out to, i.e. a privy (compare Arabic ; German Abtritt), euphemistically for Kt מַחֲרָאוֺת‎.
