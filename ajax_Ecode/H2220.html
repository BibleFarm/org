1 arm,

a. literally, of a man Judg 15:14; 16:12; 2Sam 1:10; Isa 9:19; 17:5 (probably accusative instrumental), Deut 33:20; Zech 11:17 (twice in verse); Cant 8:6; Dan 10:6; Ezekiel 13:30 (strike out Co); ׳ז כֹּחוֺ‎ Isa 44:12 (of a smith, forging); ׳ז חֲשׂוּפָה‎ Ezek 4:7 (of prophet); in figure of ׳י‎ teaching Ephraim to walk Hosea 11:3; יָדָיו זְרֹעֵי‎ Gen 49:24 arms of his hands, i.e. arms that by their strength enable him (Joseph) to draw the bow; זְרֹעָיו בֵּין אֶתיְֿהוֺרָם וַיַּךְ‎ 2Kin 9:24, i.e. his arms seen from behind, his shoulders.
