H2598. chanukkah

חֲנֻכָּה‎ noun feminine dedication, consecration, as a matter of usage only P and late (Late Hebrew חֲנוּכָּה‎ Feast of Dedication; so Aramaic חֲנוּכְּתָא‎) — abs ׳ח‎ Neh 12:27; construct חֲנֻכַּת‎ Num 7:10 6t.; dedication of wall of Jerusalem Neh 12:27 (twice in verse), by sacrifices and processions, with music; of altar in temple 2Chr 7:9; of 'the house' Psa 30:1 (title), i.e. the temple (re-dedication by Judas Maccabaeus, 1Macc 4:52ff.), see Ol Bae and others (opposed to De), and especially CheOP 234. 247; = dedication-offering for altar in tabernacle Num 7:10 (accusative with וַיַּקְרִיבוּ‎), compare הַמִּזְבֵּחַ לַחֲנֻכַּת אֶתקָֿרְבָּנָם יַקְרִיבוּ‎ 7:11, also 7:84; 7:88 (all P; see Di 7:20).
