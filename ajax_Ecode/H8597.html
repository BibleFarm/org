H8597. tipharah

תִּפְאָרָה‎ noun feminine beauty, glory; — ׳תּ‎ Isa 28:5; Jer 48:17; elsewhere absolute and construct תִּפְאֶרֶת‎ Isa 3:18 20t.; תִּפְאָ֑רֶת‎ Prov 28:12 6t.; suffix תִּפְאַרְתִּי‎ Isa 46:13 20t. suffix; —

1 beauty, finery Isa 3:18; of garments 52:1; jewels Ezek 16:17, 39; 23:26, compare 2Chr 3:6; flock Jer 13:20; a man Isa 44:13; city of Samaria 28:1, 4; diadem 28:5.

2 glory:

a. of rank: apparel of high priest Exod 28:2, 40 (P); ת ׳עֲטֶרֶת‎ crown of glory Prov 4:9; 16:31; Isa 62:3; Jer 13:18; Ezek 16:12; 23:42; greatness of monarch Est 1:4; house of David and inhabitants of Jerusalem Zech 12:7 (twice in verse).

b. of renown ולת ׳לְשֵׁם‎ Deut 26:19; 1Chr 22:5; Jer 13:11; 33:9.

c. attribute of ׳י‎ Psa 71:8; 1Chr 29:11; ת ׳שֵׁם‎ Isa 63:14; 1Chr 29:13; ׳ת עֹז‎ Psa 89:18; ת ׳זְרוֺעַ‎ Isa 63:12; hence ׳ת‎ in ׳י‎s sanctuary Psa 96:6, תִּפְאַרְתִּ ׳בֵּית‎ Isa 60:7; compare 63:15 (of heavenly temple), 64:10; ׳ת‎ as ׳י‎s gift to Israel 46:13; compare 60:19 (also Psa 89:18 above); of future fruit of land 4:2; designation of ark of ׳י‎ 78:61.

3. a. honour of nation Israel Lam 2:1.

b. glorying, boasting, of individual, Isa 20:5; Prov 17:6; 19:11; 20:29; 28:12; warrior Judg 4:9; monarchs Isa 10:12; nations 13:19; Ezek 24:25, ת ׳מַקֵּל‎ rod (sceptre) of glorying Jer 48:17 (others 1).
