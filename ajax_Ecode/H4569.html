Derivation: or feminine מַעֲבָרָה‎; from עָבַר‎;
Strong's: a crossing-place (of a river, a ford; of a mountain, a pass); abstractly, a transit, i.e. (figuratively) overwhelming

H4569. ma

'abar [מַעֲבָר‎] noun [masculine] ford, pass, passing; — only construct:

1 יַבֹּק מַעֲבַר‎ Gen 32:23 (J) the ford of (the) Jabbok.

2 pass, מִכְמָשׂ מַעֲבַר‎ 1Sam 13:23 the pass of M.

3 passing, sweep, מַטֶּה כָּלמַֿעֲבַר‎ Isa 30:32 every sweep of the rod (עָבַר‎ 4c).

מַעְבָּרָה‎

noun feminine ford, pass, passage; — absolute ׳מ‎ Isa 10:29; plural מַעְבָּרוֺת‎ 16:2; Jer 51:32, מַעְבְּרוֺת‎ absolute Josh 2:7; 1Sam 14:4; construct Judg 3:28 2t.; —

1 ford, only plural Josh 2:7 (JE), Judg 3:28; 12:5-6, Isa 16:2; so also 1Sam 13:7 (for עָֽבְרוּ ᵑ0‎) We Dr Kit Löhr; + מַעְבֶּרֶת‎ 1Kin 7:46 2Chr 4:17, see סֻכּוֺת‎

2 pass (wady, ravine), 1Sam 14:4; Isa 10:29.

3 passage in defensive works of Babylonian Jer 51:32.
