רְבִיעִי‎ masculine, רְבִיעִית‎

feminine, adjective ordinal number fourth; — masculine רְבִיעִי‎ Gen 2:14 29t., רְבִעִי‎ 1Chr 24:8; 26:11; plural רְבִיעִים‎ 2Kin 15:12, רְבִעִים‎ 10:30; feminine absolute רְבִיעִית‎ 1Kin 6:1 3t., רְבִיעִת‎ Lev 19:29, רְבִעִית‎ 1Kin 6:37; 6t. + 6:33 (see below); construct רְבִיעִית‎ Num 15:5, רְבִיעִת‎ Exod 29:40 4t., רְבִעִית‎ Num 15:4 2t.; — fourth, usually ׳הָֽר‎:

1. masculine Gen 2:14; 15:16 (JE), also (especially of days, months, sons, etc.) 1:19; Exod 28:20; 39:13; Num 7:30; 29:23; Josh 19:17 (all P), Judg 19:5; Jer 39:2; 52:6; 1Chr 27:7b; 2Chr 20:26; Ezra 8:33; noun omitted 2Sam 3:4 = 1Chr 3:2; 2:14; 3:15; 8:2; 12:10; 23:19; 24:8, 23; 25:11; 26:2, 4, 11; 27:7a; Ezra 10:14; Dan 11:2; that is חֹדֶשׁ‎ Ezek 1:1; Zech 8:19; plural in רְבִ(י)עִים בְּנֵי‎ sons of fourth (ones), i.e. to the fourth Generation, 2Kin 10:30; 15:12 (compare [רִבֵּעַ‎], and [Old Aramaic N¢rab] רבע בני‎ Cook107).

2. feminine of years, 1Kin 6:1, 37; 2Kin 18:9; Jer 25:1; 28:1; 36:1; 45:1; 46:2; 51:59; Zech 6:8; Lev 19:24 (H); רְבִעִית מֵאֵת‎ 1Kin 6:33, read ᵑ9 ᵐ5 רְבֻעוֺת מְזֻזֹת‎ Th Sta Kmp Kit Benz Bur.

3 רְבִיעִית‎ Ezek 48:20 four square (read possibly רְבוּעָה‎).

4 עִ(י)ת רְבִ(י)‎ as noun = fourth part, construct, ׳ר הַהִין‎ Lev 23:13 (H), Exod 29:40; Num 15:4-5, 28:5, 7, 14; ׳ר הִיּוֺם‎ Neh 9:3a; so absolute (הַיּוֺם‎ omitted) 9:3 b.
