H3599. kis

כִּיס‎ noun masculineProv 1:14 bag, purse (perhaps kindred with כּוֺס‎, q. v.; Arabic (probably from Persian Lane2640); Ethiopic Late Hebrew כִּיס‎; Aramaic כִּיסָא‎, , Palmyrene כיסה מן‎ from his purse = at his own expense, Vogp. 13 and others) — bag, in which were carried:

a. weights (see אֶבֶן‎ 5) Deut 25:13; Micah 6:11; Prov 16:11;

b. money, i.e. a purse Isa 46:6; Prov 1:14.
