H3038. Yeduthun

יְדֻתוּן יְדוּתוּן,‎ proper name, masculine usually יְדוּתוּן‎; יְדֻתוּן‎2Chr 5:12; 35:15; יְדיּ֯תוּן‎ Psa 39:1; 77:1; Nehemiah 11:37; 1Chr 16:38; chief of one of the three choirs of the temple (only Chronicles & Psalm-titles) 9:16; 16:38, 41, 42; 25:3 (twice in verse); 25:6; 2Chr 5:12; the king's seer 35:15; his descendants formed one of the perpetual temple choirs יְדוּתוּן בְּנֵי‎ 1Chr 16:42; 25:1, 3; 2Chr 29:14; בֶּןיְֿדיּ֯תוּן‎ Neh 11:17. In Psalm-titles ידותון על‎ Psa 62:1; 77:1 (Qr); לִידיּתוּן‎ 39:1 (לְ‎ error for עלֿ‎), all = after the manner of (the choir of) Yeduthun (musical term according to RSOTJC 422, 2nd ed. 143). — see also LagOr ii. 16 ff. and proper name הֵימָן אָסָף,‎ (sub אמן‎), אֵיתָן‎ (sub יתן‎).
