H1417. gedud

or gedudah II. [גְּדוּד‎, or גְּדוּדָה‎] noun [masculine or feminine] furrow, cutting —

1 furrow, plural defective גְּדוּדֶ֑הָ‎ Psa 65:11 (|| תְּלָמֶיהָ‎). 2 plural גְּדֻדֹת‎, cuttings upon hands, compare גדד‎ Hithpo`el

2 sign of mourning Jer 48:37 (|| שׂק‎).
