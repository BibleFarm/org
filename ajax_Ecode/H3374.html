H3374. yirah

יִרְאָה‎ noun feminine fear; — ׳יר‎ Ezek 30:13 6t.; construct יִרְאַת‎ Gen 20:11 27t.; suffix יִרְאָתִי‎ Jer 32:40; יִרְאָתוֺ‎ Exod 20:20; יִרְאָֽתְךָ‎ Job 4:6 3t.; יִרְאָתֶ֑ךָ‎ Psa 5:8 2t.; —

1 fear, terror Isa 7:25; Ezek 30:13; ׳י גְדוֺלָה‎ great fear Jonah 1:10, 16; || מַּחַד‎ Deut 2:25; || רעד(הׅ‎ Psa 2:11; 55:6.

2 a terror = object of terror, || גֹּבַהּ‎ Ezek 1:18 (strike out Co).

3 fear of God, reverence, piety אֱלֹהִים יִרְאַת‎ Gen 20:11 (E), 2Sam 23:3; Neh 5:15; ׳יר שַׁדַּי‎ Job 6:14; ׳יר אֱלֹהֵינוּ‎ Neh 5:9; ׳יר יהוה‎2Chr 19:9; Isa 11:3; 33:6; Psa 34:12; Prov 10:27; 14:26-27, 15:16; 19:23; 22:4; 23:17; || the knowledge (of God) 1:29; 2:5; Isa 11:2; is the beginning of wisdom Psa 111:10; Prov 9:10, and knowledge 1:7; the instruction of wisdom 15:33, is to hate evil 8:13, and it involves departing from evil 16:6; ׳י אֲדֹנָי‎ is wisdom Job 28:28; יִרְאָה‎ is defined by context 15:4; with suffixes these are usually object, fear of him Exod 20:20 (E), of thee Psa 5:8; 90:11; 119:38; Isa 63:17, of me Jer 32:40, but subject, thy fear (i.e. thy religion) Job 4:6; 22:4.

4 יהוֺה יִרְאַת‎ Psa 19:10 the fear of ׳י‎ = the law as revered.
