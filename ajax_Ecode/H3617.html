H3617. kalah

כָּלָה‎ noun feminine completion, complete destruction, consumption, annihilation; always this form; —

1 completion, but only as adverb, and dubious: completely, altogether Gen 18:21 (J; read כֻּלָּם‎ Ol Kau Buhl), Exod 11:1 (si vera lectio)

2 complete destruction, consumption, annihilation:

a. almost always by God, especially כ ׳עָשָׂה‎ Isa 10:23 (+ וְנֶחֱרָצָה‎), Nahum 1:8 (2 accusative, si vera lectio; BuhlZAW 1885, 181 proposes בְּקָמָיו‎ for מְקוֺמָהּ‎ see IdHWB 12 KauAT, Text Krit. Erläut. p. 65; see also מָקוֺם‎ below קום‎), 1:9; Jer 4:27; Neh 9:31 (2 accusative); + אֵת‎ person = with, pregnant sense, = in dealing with (compare II. אֵת‎ 1d), Zeph 1:18 (׳כ נִבְהָלָה אַךְ‎), Jer 5:18; 30:11 = 46:28 (אֹתְָךָ‎ = אִתְּךָ‎), Ezek 11:13; 20:17 (אוֺתָם‎ = אִתָּם‎); + בְּכָלגּֿוֺיִם‎ Jer 30:11; 46:28; without ׳כ ׃עשׂה שָׁמַעְתִּי וְנֶחֱרָצָה‎ Isa 28:22, ׳עַדכֿ ׳וְנ‎ Dan 9:27 (on both compare Isa 10:23; Zeph 1:18); לְכָלָה‎ for annihilation Ezek 13:13 (si vera lectio, see Co), לְכ לְהַשְׁחִית ׳וְלֹא‎2Chr 12:12.

b. by men, תַּעֲשׂוּ ׳וְכ אַלֿ‎ Jer 5:10; ׳וְכ בְיָדוֺ‎ Dan 11:16 and (i.e. with) annihilation in his hand.
