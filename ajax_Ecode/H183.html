H183. avah

[אָוָה‎] verb incline, desire (compare Stem 2).

Pi`el Perfect אִוָּה‎ Psa 132:13; אִוְּתָה‎ Micah 7:1 +, etc.; Imperfect3feminine singular תְּאַוֶּה‎ Deut 12:20 4t. — desire subject usually נֶפֶשׁ‎, object fruit Micah 7:1 (in metaphor); flesh (to eat) 1Sam 2:16, compare Deut 12:20 (followed by infinitive בָּשָׂר לֶאֱכֹל‎); food & drink 14:26; of king desiring rule, נַפְשֶׁ֑ךָ אֲשֶׁרתְּֿאַוֶּה בְּכֹל וּמָלַכְתָּ‎ 2Sam 3:21; 1Kin 11:37; object evil (רַע‎) Prov 21:10; once object ׳י‎ Isa 26:9 בַּלַּיְלָה אִוִּיתִךָ נַפְשִׁי‎; of God וַיַּעַשׂ אִוְּתָה וְנַפְשׁוֺ‎ Job 23:13; as desiring Zion for dwelling-place (late, only cases without נפשׁ‎) Psa 132:13; 132:14;
