H173. Oholibamah

אָהֳלִיבָמָה‎ proper name Oholibama (tent of the high place)

1. feminine wife of Esau Gen 36:2, 5, 14, 18, 25.

2. masculine an Edomite chief Gen 36:41; 1Chr 1:52.
