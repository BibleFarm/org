שָׂבְעָה‎, [שִׂבְעָה‎] noun feminine satiety; — absolute ׳שׂ‎ Isa 56:11 +, construct שִׂבְעַתֿ‎ Ezek 16:49, sf, שָׂבְעָתַךְ‎ 16:28; —

1 as to food, especially לְשׂ ׳אָכַל‎ eat to satiety, one's fill, Isa 23:18; Ezek 39:19; ׳לְשׂ‎ also Isa 55:2 (figurative); ׳שׂ‎ of dogs 56:11 (figurative); לֶֿחֶםָ׳שׂ‎ leading to arrogance, as sin of Sodom Ezek 16:49 (+ גָּאוֺם‎, etc.).

2 to carnal desire Ezek 16:28 (figurative).
