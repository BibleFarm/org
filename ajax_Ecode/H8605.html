H8605. tephillah

תְּפִלָּה‎77 noun feminine prayer; — ׳ת‎ Isa 1:15 +, construct תְּמִּלַּת‎ Psa 80:5 +; suffix תְּפלָּתִי‎ 4:2 +, etc.; plural תְּפִלּוֺת‎ 72:20; —

1. a. prayer, 1Kin 8:38 2Chr 6:29; Isa 1:15; Psa 35:13; 80:5 +.

b. pray a prayer, ׳הִתְפ ׳ת‎ 2Sam 7:27; 1Kin 8:54 (compare 8:28; 8:29 2Chr 6:19-20,); בַּתּ ׳דִּבֶּר‎ Dan 9:21; specifically of intercession, ת ׳נשׂא‎, with בְּעַד‎ in behalf of 2Kin 19:4 = Isa 37:4; Jer 7:16; 11:14.

c. ׳ת אֶלאֱֿלֹהִים‎2Chr 33:18, לְאֵל‎ Psa 42:9, compare 69:14; with verb + לפני‎ 88:3; 141:2; ׳תּ תְּקַדְּמֶ֑ךָּ‎ 88:14.

d. ת ׳בית‎ Isa 56:7 (twice in verse) house of prayer (temple); with verb + ׳אֶליֿ‎ + אלהֿיכל‎ Jonah 2:8; with verb + קדשׁו למעון‎2Chr 30:27.

e. hear prayer ת (אֶתֿ) ׳שׁמע‎ 1Kin 8:45, 49 2Chr 6:35, 39; 1Kin 9:3; Psa 4:2; 39:13 9t.; אֶלתֿ ׳שׁמע‎ 1Kin 8:28-29, 2Chr 6:19-20, Neh 1:6; Dan 9:17; ת ׳פנהאל‎ 1Kin 8:28 2Chr 6:19; Psa 102:18; לת קַשֻּׁבוֺת ׳אָזְנֶיךָ‎2Chr 6:40; 7:15, compare Neh 1:11 (אֶל‎), ׳ת הַקְשִׁיבָה‎ Psa 61:2; ת בְּקוֺל ׳הִקְשִׁיבּ‎ 66:19; ת ׳הַאֲזִינָה‎ 17:1; 55:2; 86:6; ת ׳לקח‎ 6:10; compare 66:20; 102:18b.

2 in Psalm-titles, poetic, liturgical prayer Psa 17:1; 86:1; 90:1; 102:1; 142:1; Hab 3:1; of Davidic Psalter, דוד תפלות‎ Psa 72:20.
