H3809. la

or lah II. לָהֵן‎ conjunction except, but (from לָא‎ and הֵן‎ 'not if' (compare Hebrew לא אם‎ Gen 24:38); so Nabataean and Egyptian Aramaic להן‎ Cooke217, 219, 241 S-CPap. C 6, 7 +; lâinnî, however SocArab. Dial. see Marokko 44, Anm. 103; אִילָּהֵין ᵑ7‎ 32:28; 43:3 and elsewhere); —

1 after a negative (= Biblical Hebrew אִם כִּי‎, q. v.):

a. except Dan 2:11; 3:28; 6:6; 6:8.

b. but (German sondern) Dan 2:30.

2 without a negative: however, but Ezra 5:12.
