H5159. nachalah

נַחֲלָה‎224 noun feminine possession, property, inheritance (originally gift; Late Hebrew = Biblical Hebrew); — ׳נ‎ Num 18:23 +; נַחֲלָת‎ Psa 16:6 (Ges§ 90g), read נַחֲלָתִי‎ Ew SS We Köii. 1, 425; construct נַחֲלַת‎ Josh 13:23; suffix נַחֲלָתִי‎ Ruth 4:6; נַחֲלַתְכֶם‎ Psa 105:11; plural נְחָלוֺת‎ Isa 49:8, נְחָלֹת‎ Josh 19:51;

1 property:

a. in Canaan given by ׳י‎ to Israel, ישׂראל נחלת‎ Judg 20:6; ׳נ יעקב‎ Isa 58:14, especially נַחֲלָה לְךָ נֹתֵן אֲשֶׁר‎ Deut 4:21; 15:4; 19:10; 20:16; Deuteronomy 21:28; 24:4; 25:19; 26:1 (all D); עמי את הנחלתי אשׁר הנחלה‎ Jer 12:14.

b. distributive among tribes: נ ׳נתן לְ‎ Num 16:14; 36:2; Josh 14:3 (restore לְ‎), 17:4, 14; 19:49 (all P); לנ ׳נתן לְ‎ Deut 29:7; Josh 11:23; 14:13 (all D2); נחלה נָָֽחֲלוּ‎17:6(J); בּנ ׳תֵּחָלֵק‎ Num 26:53, 56 (P); בּנחלה נפל‎ 34:2 (P), Judg 18:1; Ezek 47:22; בנ ׳הפיל‎ Josh 13:6; 23:4 (D2), Ezek 45:1; 47:22, also 47:22 (Co reads ב‎ for מ‎).

c. nations as possession of the people, Psa 111:6; of the king 2:8.

d. Levites have no property in the land Num 18:23-24, Deut 10:9; 12:12; their possession is ׳י‎ Num 18:20 (P); ׳י נחלתו הוּא‎ Deut 10:9; 18:2; Josh 13:14, 33 (all D); the tithes Num 18:21, 24, 26 (P).

e. ׳י‎ takes Israel as his property, 1Sam 10:1; 1Kin 8:53; 2Kin 21:14; Isa 19:25; Micah 7:18; Psa 33:12; 68:10; 106:5; נחלה עם‎ Deut 4:20 (D2); וְנַחֲלָֽתְךָ עַמְּךָ‎ 9:26, 29 (D), 1Kin 8:51; || עַם‎ Isa 47:6; Joel 2:17; Joel 4:2; Micah 7:14; Psa 28:9; 78:62; 78:71; 94:5; 94:14; 106:40; י ׳נחלת‎ 1Sam 26:19; 2Sam 20:19; 21:3; ׳נ אלהים‎ 14:16; נ חבל ׳יעקב‎ Deut 32:9; נ ׳שׁבט‎ Jer 10:16 = 51:19, Psa 74:2; נ ׳שׁבטי‎ Isa 63:17; also, the holy mountain Exod 15:17 (song), temple Jer 12:7; Psa 79:1, land Jer 2:7; 12:8-9, 16:18; 50:11.

2 portion, share:

a. assigned by God, Isa 54:17; Psa 37:18; 127:3; elsewhere || חֵלֶק‎ Job 20:29; 27:13; 31:2.

b. by choice, || נ ׳לא ישׁי בבן לנו חֵלֶק,‎ 2Sam 20:1 we have no share in the son of Jesse = 1Kin 12:16 2Chr 10:16.

3 inheritance (45 t.) אָבִּינוּ בְּבֵית וְנַחֲלָה חֵלֶק‎ Gen 31:14 (E) portion or inheritance in the house of our father; נ ׳העביר לְ‎ Num 27:7-8, (P) cause the inheritance to pass unto; נ ׳נתן לְ‎ 27:9-10, 11 (P), Job 42:15; אבות נחלת‎ Num 36:3, 8; 1Kin 21:3-4, Prov 19:14; ׳נ תהיה לבניו‎ Ezek 46:16.
