רֵאשׁ‎ noun masculineDan 7:6 head (Egyptian Aramaic, S-CPap.; Palmyrene רש‎; רֵישׁ ᵑ7‎, Syriac ; Biblical Hebrew ראֹשׁ‎); — construct ׳ר‎ 7:1; emphatic הָ֯‎ 2:38; suffix רֵאשׁי‎ 4:2 +, חָ֯‎ 2:28, הֵּ֯‎ 2:32 +, הַּ֯‎ 7:20, הֵ֯וֺן‎ 3:27; plural absolute רֵאשִׁין‎ 7:6, suffix רָאשֵׁיהֹם‎ (K§§ 53, Anm. b); 63 GuHpt on the passage) Ezra 5:10; —

1 head of man Dan 3:27, compare 7:9; in vision: of image 2:32, 38, beast 7:6, 20.

2 head as seat of visions, רֵאשָׁךְ הֶזְוֵי‎, etc., Dan 2:28; 4:2; 4:7; 4:10; 7:1, 15.

3 = chief, ׳בְּר‎ Ezra 5:10 in the capacity of their chiefs.

4 sum, essential content, of matters Dan 7:1 (NesMM 40 beginning).
