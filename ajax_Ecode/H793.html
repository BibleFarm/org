H793. eshed

אֶ֫שֶׁד‎ noun [masculine] foundation, bottom, lower part (slope) (Assyrian išdu compare LotzTP 186; > others from אֲשַׁד‎, pour, Sabean אסד‎ = מסקי (משׁקה)‎ DHMZMG 1883, 8, whence fall, slope; compare Di Num 21:15) ׳א הַנְּחָלִים‎ the bottom of the ravines 21:15.
