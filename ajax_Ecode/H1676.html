H1676. daah

דָּאָה‎ noun feminine a bird of prey, possibly kite (ᵑ9‎ Saad BoHieroz. ii. 191 Di Lev 11:14; Aramaic דַּיְתָא‎, ; Late Hebrew דַּיָּה‎ of different birds of prey; name probably from flying, swooping), 11:14 forbidden as food; compare also Deut 14:13 והַדַּיָּה ואתהָֿאַיָּה וְהָרָאָה‎, where for הראה‎ read הַדָּאָה‎ & strike out הַדַּיָּה‎; so ᵐ5 ⅏‎; compare Di l.c.

ר‎ Reš 20th letter; = 200 in post-Biblical Hebrew. רָאָה‎ Deut 14:13 textual error; see דָּאָה‎
