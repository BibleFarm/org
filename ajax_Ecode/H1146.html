H1146. binyan

בִּנְיָן‎ noun masculine structure (loan-word = image u%navailable according to LagBN 205) applied

a. to enclosing wall of Ezekiel's temple Ezek 40:5;

b. to rear-building of same Ezek 41:12 (twice in verse); 41:15, but read probably בִּנְיָה‎ q. v.; so Sm Co;

c. apparently to whole temple Ezek 42:1, 10 (Co reads הבית‎); compare 42:5 (strike out Co); — Sm refers 42:1 to enclosing wall, see

a.
