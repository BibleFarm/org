שָׁכַם‎ (shâkam｜shaw-kam'｜verb｜start)
[Heb] שָׁכַם‎ ETCBC: שׁכם‎ (verb｜rise early) OSHL: v.dd.ag TWOT: 2386 GK: H8899 Greek: ἀνίστημι, ἐξανίστημι, ὀρθρίζω, ὀρθρινός
Derivation: a primitive root; properly, to incline (the shoulder to a burden); but used only as denominative from שְׁכֶם‎;
Strong's: literally, to load up (on the back of man or beast), i.e. to start early in the morning
KJV: (arise, be up, get (oneself) up, rise up) early (betimes), morning.

H7925. shakam

[שָׁכַם‎] verb Hiph`il denominative start, rise, early (properly load backs of beasts for day's journey); — Perfect3masculine singular consecutive וְהִשְׁכִּים‎ 2Sam 15:2; Job 1:5, etc.; Imperfect3masculine singular וַיַּשְׁכֵּם‎ Gen 19:27 +, 2 masculine singular תְּשְׁכִּים‎ Judg 9:33, etc.; Imperative הַשְׁכֵּם‎ Exod 8:16 +; Infinitive absolute הַשְׁכֵּם‎ Jer 7:25 +, כֵּים‎- 44:4; Prov 27:14, erroneous אַשְׁכֵּים‎ Jer 25:3 (Ges§ 53 K); Participle מַשְׁכִּים‎ Hosea 6:4 +, etc., — rise early, make an early start, of journey (Hexateuch only J E); לְדַרְכְּכֶם‎ Judg 19:9 (+ מָחָר‎), compare Cant 7:13; + verb of going Gen 19:2 (J), standing 2Sam 15:2 (compare Dr), encamping Judg 7:1, etc.; + infinitive 1Sam 15:12 (compare Dr), 29:11; 2Kin 6:15 (לָקוּם‎), קוּם מַשְׁכִּימֵי‎ Psa 127:3 (opposed to שֶׁבֶת מְאַחֲרֵי‎); usually with בַּבֹּקֶר‎ Gen 19:27 (J; אֶלהַֿמָּקוֺם‎), 1Sam 29:10 (twice in verse) + 27 t., + (hyperbole) יִרְדֹּ֑פוּ בַבּ ׳מַשְׁכִּימֵי שֵׁכָר‎ Isa 5:11 (|| בֵנֶּשֶׁף מְאַחֲרֵי‎); with מִמָּחֳרָת‎ Exod 32:6 (E), Judg 6:28 3t.; with הַשַּׁחַר בַּעֲלוֺת‎ Josh 6:15 (J), 1 Samuel 19:26; וַיֵּצְאוּ וַיַּשְׁכִּימוּ וַיְמַהֲרוּ‎ 8:14 (J) they made a quick and early start; and went out; Infinitive absolute (Ges§ 113s) as adverb ׳הַשׁ וְהַעֲרֵב‎ 1Sam 17:16 at early morning and at evening; especially of ׳י‎, in Jeremiah: וְדַבֵּר ׳הַשׁ‎ = speaking early and often Jer 7:13; 35:14 and (of Jeremiah) 25:3, compare 11:7; 32:33; often ׳הַשׁ וְשָׁלוֺחַ‎ = sending early and often 7:25; 24:4; 26:5; 29:19; 35:14; 44:4, also 2Chr 36:15; so verb finite הִשְׁחִיתוּ הִשְׁכִּימוּ‎, Zeph 3:7 = with eagerness they corrupted (Ges§ 120g); participle מַשְׁ ׳כַּטַּל הֹלֵךְ‎ Hosea 6:4 like the dew departing early, 13:3 (Gesib.). — 1Sam 9:26 read וַיִּשְׁכַּב‎, see שׁכב‎
