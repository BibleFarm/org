שִׁלּוּחַ‎ (shillûwach｜shil-loo'-akh｜noun｜masculine｜sending away)
[Heb] שִׁלּוּחַ‎, שִׁלּוּחִים‎ ETCBC: שִׁלּוּחִים‎ (subs｜act of sending) OSHL: v.dl.ah TWOT: 2394b GK: H8933
Derivation: or שִׁלֻּחַ‎; from שָׁלַח‎;
Strong's: (only in plural) a dismissal, i.e. (of a wife) divorce (especially the document); also (of a daughter) dower
KJV: presents, have sent back.

H7964. shilluchim

שִׁלּוּחִים‎ noun [masculine] plural sending away, parting gift; —

1 שִׁלוּחֶיהָ אַחר‎ Exod 18:2 (J) after she had been sent away.

2 שִׁלֻּחִים‎ 1Kin 9:16 he gave (city) as parting gift (i.e. dowry) to his daughter; compare figurative שִׁלּוּחִים‎ Micah 1:14 thou shalt give a pearting gift (= say farewell to, lose) Morešeth of Gath.
