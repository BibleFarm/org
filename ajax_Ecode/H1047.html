H1047. Beth

Peor מְּעוֺר בֵּית‎ proper name, of a location (= ׳בּ פ ׳בַּעַל‎, compare below בעל‎) east of Jordan Deut 3:29, in land of Amorites 4:46 compare Josh 13:20 (where assigned to Reuben); in land of Moab Deut 34:6.
