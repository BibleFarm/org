H200. ivveleth

אִוֶּ֫לֶת‎25 noun feminine folly. — absolute Prov 12:23 +; construct 14:8 +; suffix אִוַּלְתִּי‎ Psa 38:6, etc.; — folly, special product of כְּסִילִים‎ (see כֶּסֶל‎) Prov 12:23 + (12 t.); with מְּתָאִים‎ 14:18; with אֱוִלִיים‎ only 16:22; 27:22 for alliteration. It is bound up in mind of boy, to be removed only by rod of discipline 22:15; ׳א‎ personified tears down house built by נָשִׁים חַכְמוֺת‎ 14:1; it is contrasted with תְּבוּנָה‎ 14:29; 15:21.
