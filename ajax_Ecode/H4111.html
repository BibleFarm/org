H4111. Mahalalel

מַהֲלַלְאֵל‎ proper name, masculine **read מְהַלֶּלְאֵל‎ GrayProp. N. 201. (praise of God, compare יְהַלֶּלְאֵל‎ above); —

1 great-grandson of Seth according to Genealogy of P, Gen 5:12-13, 15, 16, 17; 1Chr 1:2.

2 a man of Judah Neh 11:4.
