H569. Amraphel

אַמְרָפֶל‎ proper name, masculine king of Shinar Gen 14:1, 9(probably = חמרבי‎, –ammurabi of Babylon, who reigned approximately , compare SchrSBA 1887, xxxi (June 23) COTii. 296 f. Dl in DeGen 1887, Excursus).
