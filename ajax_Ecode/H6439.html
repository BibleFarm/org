H6439. Penuel

מְּנוּאֵל‎ proper name (face of God, compare GFM JU 8, 8; late Assyrian Panîli HilprUniv. Pa. Exp. ix. 68); — Φανουηλ, but in Gen εἶδος (τοῦ) θεοῦ:

1. location east of Jordan, near Jabbok Gen 32:32; Judg 8:8 (twice in verse); 8:9, 17; 1Kin 12:25 = מְּנִיאֵל‎ Gen 32:31: site unknown; see especially PaineBib.Sac.1878, 481 ff.

2. masculine a. in Judah 1Chr 4:4.

b. in Benjamin 1Chr 8:25 Qr (Kt פניאל‎).
