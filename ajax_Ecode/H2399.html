חֵטְא‎ noun masculineDeut 15:9 sin (on formation see Lag BN 142) — ׳ח‎ Lev 19:17 15t.; suffix חָטְאוֺ‎ 24:15 5t., חֲטָאָו֯‎ Lam 3:39, חֶטְאָם‎ Lev 20:20; plural חֲטָאִים‎ Eccl 10:4; construct חֲטָאֵי‎ 2Kin 10:29; Ezek 23:49; suffix חֲטָאַי‎ Gen 41:9, חֲטָאָ֑י‎ Isa 38:17; Psa 51:11, חֲטָאֵינוּ‎ Dan 9:16; Psa 103:10, חֲטָאֵיכֶם‎ Isa 1:18; —

1 sin:

a. against man Gen 41:9 (E) Eccl 10:4.

b. elsewhere ag. God, absolute Isa 31:7; Hosea 12:9; ׳ח חָטָא‎ Lam 1:8 compare Deut 19:15; 2Kin 10:29; ׳ח מות משׁפט‎ sin worthy of death Deut 21:22 = ׳ח מות‎ 22:26; with בְּ‎, because of, Dan 9:16; God is entreated: hide thy face from my sins Psa 51:11; and it is said in faith, thou wilt cast behind thy back all my sins Isa 38:17.

2 guilt of sin: ב חטא היה‎ sin (the guilt of sin) come on one Deut 15:9; 21:22; 23:22; 23:23; 24:15; מת בחטאו‎ in his sin (guilt) die Num 27:3 (P); בחטאו אישׁ‎ each for his own sin Deut 24:16 = 2Kin 14:16 2Chr 25:4; אמי יחמתני בחטא‎ Psa 51:7 in sin (condition of sin and guilt) my mother conceived me; כשׁנים חטאיכם אם‎ Isa 1:18 though your sins be like scarlet (in guilt); לנו עשׂה כחטאינו לא‎ Psa 103:10 not according to our sins (their guilt) hath he done to us.

3 punishment for sin: ח ׳נשׂא‎ bear sin (its punishment) Lev 20:20; 24:15 (H), Num 9:13; 18:22 (P); with עַל‎, bear sin because of Lev 19:17; 22:9 (H) Num 18:32 (P); bear sins of idols (in worshipping idols) Ezek 23:49; the ideal servant of Yahweh bore the sins of many Isa 53:12; without נשׂא‎, only Lam 3:39 חטאו על גבר‎ (wherefore doth) a man (complain) for the punishment of his sin ?
