H5983. Ammon

עַמּוֺן‎106 proper name, of a people Ammon, ᵐ5‎ Αμμαν, Αμμων (Assyrian Bît Ammanu COTGlossary); always (except 1Sam 11:11 [where however ᵐ5‎ reads ע ׳בְּנֵי‎], Psa 83:8, which have ׳ע‎ alone) עַמּוֺן בְּנֵי‎, connected by J with בֶּןעַֿמִּי‎ son of Lot Gen 19:38 (and meaning of name doubtless sought herein by J; compare II. עַם‎); people apparently akin to Israel, but usually hostile, dwelling east of Jordan, northeast of Moab, between Arnon and Jabbok: Num 21:24 (twice in verse) (JE), Deut 2:19 (twice in verse); 2:37; 3:11, 16; Josh 12:2; 13:10 (D), 13:25 (P), Judg 3:13; 10:6 25t.
