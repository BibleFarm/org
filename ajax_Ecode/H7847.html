H7847. satah

[שָׂטָה‎] verb turn aside (Aramaic סְטָא‎, id.; שְׂטָא‎ stray; Ethiopic III. be seduced, etc.; cf DrJPhil. xi (1882), 205); —

Qal Perfect2feminine singular שָׂטִית‎ Num 5:19-20, Imperfect3feminine singular תִּשְׂטֶה‎ 5:12; 5:29, jussive 3masculine singular יֵשְׂטְ‎ Prov 7:25; Imperative masculine singular שְׂטֵה‎ 4:15; — turn aside, of unfaithful wife Num 5:12 (absolute), + אִישׁ תַּחַת‎, i.e. while married, 5:19 (+ טֻמְאָה‎ adverb accusative), 5:20; 5:29 (all P); of youth, from (מֵעַל‎) way of evil woman Prov 4:15, toward (אֶל‎) her ways 7:25.
