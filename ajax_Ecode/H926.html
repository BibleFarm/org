בָּהַל‎ (bâhal｜baw-hal'｜verb｜tremble)
[Heb] בָּהַל‎ ETCBC: בהל‎ (verb｜disturb) OSHL: b.ak.aa TWOT: 207 GK: H987 Greek: ἐξέρχομαι, παραλύω, παρίημι, σπεύδω, σπουδάζω, σπουδή, ταράσσω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to tremble inwardly (or palpitate), i.e. (figuratively) be (causative, make) (suddenly) alarmed or agitated; by implication to hasten anxiously
KJV: be (make) affrighted (afraid, amazed, dismayed, rash), (be, get, make) haste(-n, -y, -ily), (give) speedy(-ily), thrust out, trouble, vex.
