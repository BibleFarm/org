H8095. Shimon

שִׁמְעוֺן‎44 proper name, masculine (compare proper name שמעון‎ in Palmyrene Lzb378; meaning doubtful; Rs and others compare said to mean offspring of hyena and wolf; see HoggEBiv. 4531); —

1 second son of Jacob and Leath, Συμεωη: Gen 29:33 (name explanation from שָׁמַע‎), 35:23; Exod 1:2 14t. (associated especially with Levi Gen 34:25, 30; 49:5).

2 tribal name Judg 1:3 (twice in verse); 1:17; Num 1:6 +; so שׁ ׳גְּנֵי‎ 1:22 7t., שׁ ׳מַטֵה‎ 1:23; 2:12; 13:5; שׁ גְנֵי ׳מַטֵּה‎ 10:19; 34:20; Josh 19:1, 8; 21:9; 1Chr 6:50.

3 post-exilic Jew with foreign wife Ezra 10:31.
