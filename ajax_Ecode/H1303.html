H1303. barqanim

בַּרְקָנִים‎ noun masculine plural briers (so Vrss Ki and others, also Stu q. v., Be, v unknown; compare || קוֺצִים‎; > JDMich, Thes and others threshing-sledges, furnished with sharp (glittering) stones) Judg 8:7 וְאֶתהַֿבּ הַמִּדְבָּר אֶתקֿוֺצֵי ׳אֶתבְּֿשַׂרְכֶם וְדַשְׁתִּ֫י‎ (see also דושׁ‎) and I will thresh your flesh together with the thorns of the wilderness and the briers; 8:16 סֻכּוֺת אַנְשֵׁי אֵת בָּהֶם הַבּ וְאֶת הַמִּדְבָּר וְאֶתקֿוֺצֵי הָעִיר אֶתזִֿקְנֵי ׳וַיִּקַּח וַיֹּדַע‎ (read וַיָּדָשׁ‎ for ᵐ5 וַיֹּדַע‎ BuRS 114; compare Stu Be) and he took the thorns of the wilderness and the briers and threshed, etc.
