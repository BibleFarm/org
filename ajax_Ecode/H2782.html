I. חָרַץ‎ verb cut, sharpen, decide (Late Hebrew id., cut in, decide, Aramaic in derivatives; Assyrian —arâƒu, dig, decide, —ariƒu, trench, ZehnpfBAS i. 502; Phoenician חרץ‎ decision, HoffmAGG xxxvi, May 1889, 11) —

Qal Perfect3masculine singular ׳ח‎ Josh 10:21; חָרָ֑צְתָּ‎ 1Kin 20:40; Imperfect יֶחֱרַץֿ‎ Exod 11:7; 2masculine singular תֶּחֱרָ֑ץ‎ 2Sam 5:24; Passive participle חָרוּץ‎ Isa 10:22; Lev 22:22; plural חֲרוּצִים‎ Job 14:5 (see also חָרוּץ‎ below); —

3 decide 1Kin 20:40 (absolute); so participle pass ימיו חרוצים‎ Job 14:5 his days are determined, fixed (|| חדשׁיו מִסְמַּר‎), Isa 10:22 ח ׳כִּלָּיוֺן‎; act with decision 2Sam 5:24.
