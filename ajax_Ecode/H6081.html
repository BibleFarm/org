H6081. Epher

עֵ֫פֶר‎ proper name, masculine Αφερ, etc.; —

1 'son' of Midian Gen 25:4; 1Chr 1:33 (ᵐ5‎L Γοφερ).

2 in Judah 1Chr 4:17 (Α Γαφερ).

3 in Manasseh 1Chr 5:24.
