H3369. yaqosh

[יָקשׁ‎] verb lay a bait or lure (see מוֺקֵשׁ‎), then Generally lay snares (see also קוֺשׁ נקשׁ,‎) —

Qal Perfect יָק֫שְׁתִּי‎ Jer 50:24; יָָֽקְשׁוּ‎ Psa 141:9; Participle plural יוֺקְשִׁים‎ 124:7; — lay snares, figurative of devices of wicked 141:9 (with accusative of congnate meaning with verb); of ׳י‎s plan to destroy Babylon Jer 50:24 נלכדר וגם לך יקשׁתי‎; Participle as substantive bait-layers, fowlers Psa 124:7 (compare 141:9) simile

Niph`al Perfect נוֺקַ֫שְׁתָּ‎ Prov 6:2; וְנוֺקְשׁוּ‎ consecutive Isa 8:15; 28:13; Imperfect2masculine singular תִּוָּקֵשׁ‎ Deut 7:25; be caught by a bait, ensnared, in business entanglements Prov 6:2; in disastrous consequences of idolatry Deut 7:25 (|| נלכד‎); of those ensnared by ׳י‎s plans (followed by ונלכדו‎) Isa 8:15; 28:13.

Pu`al Participle יוּקָשִׁים‎ (for מְיוּקָשִׁים‎, unless this should be read: Ges§ 52. R. 6; Sta§ 220): entrapped, in circumstances of life Eccl 9:12.
