H6914. Qibroth

Hattaavah הַתַּאֲוָה קִבְרוֺת‎ proper name, of a location Μνήμαγα (τῆς) ἐπιθυμίας: a station in wilderness (the graves of desire, compare Num 11:34 and תַּאֲוָה‎ above); — 11:34-35, הַתּ ׳קִבְרֹת‎ 33:16-17, Deut 9:22.
