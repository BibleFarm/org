H4846. merorah

[מְרֹרָה‎] noun feminine bitter thing, gall, poison; construct מְרוֺרַת‎ Job 20:14; suffix מְרֹרָתוֺ‎ 20:25; plural מְרֹרֹת‎ Deut 32:32, מְרֹרוֺת‎ Job 13:36; —

1 gall, i.e. seat of gall, gall-bladder Job 20:25 (|| גֵּוָה‎ back; compare מְרֵרָה‎ Job 16:30).

2 = poison, ׳מ מְּתָנִים‎ Job 20:14.

3 מְרֹרוֺת‎ bitter thing Job 13:26 (of God's edict of judgment).

4 plural abstract = bitterness; ׳מ אַשְׁכְּלֹת‎ Deut 32:32 clusters of bitterness (|| רוֺשׁ עִנְּבְי‎).
