H5268. Nisrok

נִסְרֹךְ‎ proper name, of a divinity Assyrian god, worshipped by Sennacherib, 2Kin 19:37 = Isa 37:38; ᵐ5‎ Εσδραχ, A Εσθραχ, ᵐ5‎L Ασραχ(Κ); ᵐ5‎ Νασαραχ, א‎ Ασαρακ, A Ασαραχ (Isaiah); JosAnt. x. 1, 5 Αρασκη; no such god in cuneiform inscriptions; see views in MeinhJesaiaerzählungen (1898) on the passage; corrupt form of Nusku (= נסוך‎) according to HalJas. xiii (1879), 387 = Mél. de. Crit. 177 Muss-ArnJBL xi.1 (1892), 86; Hebraica. vii. 89 R. 17; Nusku is a solar diety, compare JastrRel. Bab. 220 f.
