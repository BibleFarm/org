H1459. gav

[גַּו‎] noun masculine midst (Egyptian Aramaic, Nabataean, Palmyrene גו‎; גָּיו ᵑ7‎; especially construct מִגּוֺ לְגוֺ, בְּגוֺ,‎; so Syriac ; compare Biblical Hebrew נֵּו‎); — construct גּוֺא‎ (so Nabataen CookeNo. 94) Dan 3:26 +, 7:15 editions, Gi גּוֺ‎ (Baer גּוֺא‎, see on 3:6); suffix 3masculine singular גַּוֵּהּ‎ Ezra 5:7, 3feminine singular גַּוַּהּ‎ 4:15; 6:2; — midst, always with prepositions:

a. בְּגוֺא‎ (= Hebrew בְּתוֺךְ‎), Dan 3:25 גוּרָא בְּגוֺא‎, 4:7; 7:15; Ezra 4:15; 5:7 בְּגַוֵהּ כְּתִיב וְכִדְנָה‎, 6:2.

b. לְגוֺא‎ Dan 3:6, 11, 15, 21, 23, 24.

c. מִןגּֿוֺא‎ Dan 3:26.

גֵּרָה‎ see גאה‎.
