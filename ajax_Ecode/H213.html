[אוּץ‎] verb press, be pressed, make haste —

Qal Perfect אָץ‎ Josh 10:13; 17:15; אַ֫צְתִּי‎ Jer 17:16 Participle אָץ‎ Prov 19:2-3t. Proverbs; אָצִים‎ Exod 5:13; —

1 press, hasten (transitive but object not expressed) Exod 5:13 (E).

2 (intransitive) be pressed, confined, narrow Josh 17:15 הַראֶֿפְרַיִם לְךָ אָץ כִי‎.

3 hasten, make haste, followed by ל‎ + Infinitive Josh 10:13 לָבוֺא אָץ לֹא‎ (J, of sun); Prov 28:20 ׳א לְהַעֲשִׂיר‎; followed by מִן‎ Jer 17:16 א ׳לֹא מֵרֹעֶה‎; followed by ב‎ (of particular in which one hastens) Prov 19:2 (בְּרגְלַיִם‎); 29:20 (בִּדְבָרִים‎); compare also 21:5 לְמַחְסוֺר אַח וְכָלאָֿץ‎.

Hiph`il Imperfect וַיָּאִיצוּ‎ Gen 19:15; תָּאִיצוּ‎ Isa 22:4 — hasten (transitive) followed by ב‎ Gen 19:15 (J); followed by ל‎ + Infinitive לְנַחֲמֵנִי ׳אַלתָּֿא‎ Isa 22:4. (May be Qal Imperfect, & verbי׳ע‎.)
