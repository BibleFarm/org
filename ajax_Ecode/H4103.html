מְהוּמָה‎ noun feminine tumult, confusion, disquietde, discomfiture — absolute ׳מ‎ Deut 7:23 7t.; construct מְהוּמַת‎ 1Sam 5:11; Ezek 22:5; plural absolute מְהוּמוֺת‎2Chr 15:5, מְהוּמֹת‎ Amos 3:9; —

1 tumult, confusion Amos 3:9 (|| עֲשׁוּקִים‎ oppression), disturbance, turmoil (opposed to peace) 2Chr 15:5 (|| verb המם‎ 15:6), compare Ezek 22:5; disquietude (of mind) Prov 15:16; especially tumult & confusion, panic, as due to divine judgment ׳מְהוּמַתיֿ‎ Zech 14:13 compare Ezek 7:7; also 1Sam 5:9 and מָוֶת מְהוּמַת‎ 5:11.

2 discomfiture, (due to ׳י‎) in war 1Sam 14:20 (compare 14:15; 14:22); Deut 7:23 (accusative of congnate meaning with verb with הום‎ q. v.), 28:20 (|| מִגְעֶרֶת מְאֵרָה,‎); compare מ ׳יוֺם‎ 1Sam 22:5 (|| מְבוּכָה מְבוּסָה,‎) a day of discomfiture & down-treading & confusion.
