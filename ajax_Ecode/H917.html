H917. Bedan

בְּדָן‎ proper name, masculine (= עַבְדָּן‎ ?).

1 a judge of Israel 1Sam 12:11; but rd probably בָּרָק‎, so ᵑ6 ᵐ5‎ We, compare Dr (see Judg 4:6f.).

2 a Manassite 1Chr 7:17.
