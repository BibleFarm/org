H2526. Cham

I. חָם‎ proper name, masculine person and

location (compare Sabean חם‎ Hal243) — ᵐ5‎ Ξαμ: —

1 Ham, 2nd son of Noah Gen 5:32; 6:10; 7:13; 9:18 (twice in verse); 1Chr 1:4; called father of Canaan Gen 9:22 and of various peoples 10:1, 6, 20; 1Chr 1:8, compare שָׁם הַיּשְׁבִים מִןחָֿם‎ 4:40; these peoples were inhabitants of southern lands, or related historically or politically to such inhabitants; see מּוּט מִצְרַיִם, כְּנַעַן, כּוּשׁ,‎, etc.

2 in late Psalms a name (collective) for Egyptians: — ׳אָֽהֳלֵיחֿ‎ Psa 78:51; ׳אֶרֶץחֿ‎ 105:23; 105:27; 106:22 (all || מִצְרַיִם‎, except 105:27). — On historical significance, and attempts at etymology see DiGen. ed. 6, chap.10, especially pp. 165, 168 f., HomSV i. 427 BuUrg. 323 WiedÄgypt. Geschichte. i. 22.
