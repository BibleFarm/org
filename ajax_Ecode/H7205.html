H7205. Reuben

רְאוּבֵן‎71 proper name, masculine Reuben, Ρουβην[ιν] (be-hold a son! but Gen 29:32 makes = בְּעָנְיִי רָאָה‎ (!); JosAnt. i. 19, 7 and others Ρουβηλος, ᵑ6‎ , ׳ר‎ then perhaps = Arabic , lion, compare Di); —

1 eldest son of Jacob and Leah Gen 29:32; 30:14 11t. Genesis; Exod 1:2; 1Chr 2:1; as head of family or clan Exod 6:14 (twice in verse); Num 1:20; 26:5 (all P), 1Chr 5:1, 3, compare בֶּןרְֿא אֱלִיאָב ׳בְּנֵי‎ Deut 11:6.

2 as name of clan or tribe Judg 5:15-16, Deut 27:13; Josh 18:7 ( +גָּד‎; both D), Deut 33:6 (poem), Ezek 48:6-7, 31; Num 1:5 (P); so ר ׳מַטֵּה‎ 1:21; 13:4; Josh 20:8; 21:7 (all P), 1Chr 6:48; 6:63, ר ׳מַחֲנֵה‎ Num 2:10, 16; 10:18 (all P); ר ׳בְּנֵי‎, as tribe, Numbers 16:l b 32:37 (both J E), 2:10; 7:20; 26:5; Josh 13:23 (twice in verse) (all P), also (+ Gad and often Manasseh) Num 32:1-2, 6, 25 (JE), Josh 4:12 (D), Num 32:29, 31, 33; Josh 22:9 11t. 22 (all P), compare 1Chr 5:18; ׳בֶּןרֿ‎ as tribe Josh 15:6; 18:7 (both P), ר ׳בְנֵיֿ מַטֵּה‎ 13:15 (P).
