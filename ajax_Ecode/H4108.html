H4108. mahlek

[מַהֲלָךְ‎] noun masculineNeh 2:6 walk, journey, going (late); construct מַהֲלַךְ‎ Ezek 42:4 2t.; suffix מַהֲלָֽכְךָ‎ Neh 2:6; plural מַהְלְכִים‎ Zech 3:7; —

1 walk 'of 10 cubits' breadth before the chambers' in Ezekiel's temple Ezek 42:4 (compare Da).

2 journey from Babylonian to Jerusalem Neh 2:6; of dimension of Nineveh, a journey of three days Jonah 3:3 i.e. diameter or length, compare 3:4; (according to Herodotsee 58 one day's journey = 150 stadia, = length of Nineveh according to Diodii.3).

3 plural goings, i.e. free access (VB) Zech 3:7.
