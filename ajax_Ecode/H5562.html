H5562. Samgar

Nebo נְבוֺ סַמְגַּר‎ proper name, masculine an officer of Nebuchadnezzar according to ᵑ0‎ Jer 39:3 +?Šumgir Nabu (SchrCOT), but probably textual error; Gie (plausibly) takes סמגר‎ as corrupted dittogram of מג רב‎, and joins נבו‎ to following, נבושׂרסכים‎ = נְבוּשַׁזְבַּן‎, compare 39:13 and ᵐ5‎ Σαμαγωθ (Σαμαγαδ, Σαμαγαρ, etc.) καὶ Ναβουσαχαρ.
