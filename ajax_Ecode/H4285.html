H4285. machshak

מַחְשַׁךְ‎ noun masculine dark place (poetry) — absolute ׳מ‎ Isa 29:15; 42:16; מַחְשָׁ֑ךְ‎ Psalm 88:19; plural מַחֲשַׁכִּים‎ Psa 88:7; 143:3 = Lam 3:6 construct מַחֲשַׁכֵּי‎ Psa 74:20; — dark place:

a. = hiding-place אֿרץ׳מח‎ Psa 74:20.

b. dark region, in which men may lose their way Isa 42:16.

c. = grave, or שׁאול‎ Psalm 88:19 (see Che); of dark place of God's wrath (like She' ô l) Psa 88:7; compare 143:3 = Lam 3:6.

d. = secret place, secrecy (of plots) Isa 29:15.
