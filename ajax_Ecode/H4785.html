H4785. Marah

מָרָה‎ proper name, feminine of bitter spring in Sinaitic peninsula Exod 15:23 (twice in verse) (J), Num 33:8-9, (P), + ה‎ locative מָרָ֑תָה‎ Exod 15:23 (J); probably modern `Ain Hawwâraiun. (compare RobBR i. 67) PalmerDesert i. 40 EbGS 125 f.)
