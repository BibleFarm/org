H3062. Yehudain

[יְהוּדַי‎] name, of a people Jew (Biblical Hebrew יְהוּדִי‎); — plural absolute יְהוּדָאִין‎ (K§ 61, 6)) Dan 3:12; emphatic יְהוּדָיֵא‎ (Ib.ib.) 3:8; Ezra 4:12, 23; 5:1, 5; 6:7 (twice in verse); 6:8, 14.
