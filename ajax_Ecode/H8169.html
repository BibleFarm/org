H8169. Shaalbim

שַׁעַלְבִים‎ proper name, of a location (perhaps haunt of foxes; Arabic , Assyrian šêlibu, fox); — in (original) territory of Dan, Judg 1:35 (compare GFM, on etymology and site), 1Kin 4:9, Θαλαβεειν, Σαλαβειμ, Judges also αἱ ἀλώπεκες, as doublet, or (A ᵐ5‎L) alone; = שַׁעֲלַבִּין‎ Josh 19:42, Σα(α)λαβειν.

שַׁעֲלַבִּין‎ see foregoing.
