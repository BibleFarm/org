H2809. Cheshbon

II. חֶשְׁבּוֺן‎ proper name, of a location of the city of Sihon king of the Amorites Num 21:26-27, 28, 30, 34; 32:3 (all E); Deut 1:4; 2:24, 26, 30; 3:2, 6; 4:46; 29:6; Josh 9:10; 12:2, 5; 13:10, 21, 27 (all D) Judg 11:19; Jer 48:45; Neh 9:22; captured by Israel who dwelt in it Num 21:25 (E) Judg 11:26; rebuilt by Reuben Num 32:37 (E), given to Reuben at the division of the land Josh 13:17 (P); on the border of Gad, 13:26 (P), it subsequently fell to Gad and was assigned to Levites out of that tribe 21:39 (P) 1Chr 6:66; the Moabites gained possession of it Isa 15:4; 16:8-9, and subsequently the Ammonites Jer 48:2, 34, 45; 49:3; it was celebrated for its fish ponds Cant 7:5. — Modern „usbân, SeetzenReisen i. 407 RobBR i. 551 BDPal 191 SurveyEP 8.
