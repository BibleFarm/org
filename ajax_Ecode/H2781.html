H2781. cherpah

חֶרְמָּה‎ noun feminine reproach — ׳ח‎ Gen 34:14 35t.; construct חֶרְמַּת‎ Josh 5:9 15t.; suffix חֶרְמָּֽתְךָ‎ Psa 74:22 etc. + 17 t. suffixes; plural חֲרָפוֺת‎ 69:11; Dan 12:2; construct חֶרְמּוֺת‎ Psa 69:10. —

1 taunt of enemy 1Sam 17:26; so also 25:39; reproach cast upon another, scorn, contumely Nehemiah 3:36; Neh 5:9; Psa 69:20; 69:21; 71:13; 89:51; 119:22; Prov 18:3; Isa 51:7; Ezekiel 21:33; ח ׳שׁמע‎ Jer 51:51; Zeph 2:8; Lam 3:61; ח ׳נתן על‎ Jer 23:40; ח ׳נשׂא על‎ Psa 15:3 (slander); ח ׳נשׂא‎ Jer 31:19; Ezek 36:15; Micah 6:16 (reproach); ׳ח על נשׂא‎ bear reproach for Psa 69:8; Jer 15:15; Zeph 3:18; ח ׳השׁיב‎ Hosea 12:15; Nehemiah 3:36; ח ׳חרף‎ Psa 69:10; 79:12; 89:51; ח ׳הסיר מעל‎ 1Sam 17:26; Isa 25:8; ח ׳העביר‎ Psa 119:39; reproaches against God 69:10 (חוֺרְפֶיךָ חֶרְמּוֺת‎), 74:22; 79:12.

2 reproach which rests upon one, condition of shame, disgrace:

a. sexual 2Sam 13:13; Isa 47:3; Ezek 16:57; Prov 6:33.

b. barrenness of womb Gen 30:23 (E) Isa 4:1; widowhood 54:4.

c. hunger Ezek 36:30; disease Job 19:5.

d. ritual, uncircumcision Gen 34:14 (P) Josh 5:9 (JE).

e. injuries from enemies Lam 3:30; 5:1; Neh 1:3; 2:7; Job 16:10; Dan 11:18 (twice in verse).

3 a reproach, the object of reproach, the person of thing reproached אָדָם חֶרְמַּת‎ a reproach of man Psa 22:7; ׳ח נבל‎ 39:9; ח ׳היה ׳ל‎ become an object of reproach to 31:12; 79:4; 89:42; 109:25; Ezek 5:15; (ל לח ׅ׳(היה)‎ Isa 30:5; Jer 6:10; 20:8; 42:18; 44:8, 12; 49:13; Psa 69:11; Dan 9:16; 12:2; ח ׳נתן ׅ׳(ל‎ Ezek 22:4; Joel 2:19; Psa 78:66; לח ׳נתן‎ Jer 24:9; 29:18; Ezek 5:14; Josh 2:17; ח ׳שׂים ׳ל‎ Psa 44:14; ח ׳שׂים על‎ 1Sam 11:2.
