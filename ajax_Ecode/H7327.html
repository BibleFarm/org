H7327. Ruth

רוּת‎ proper name, feminine Ruth (for רְעוּת‎, i.e. friendship, Syriac , Thes LagBN 84, 156; Or. ii. 41 Köii. 1. 481); — Moabitess, ancestress of David, Ruth 1:4, 14, 16, 22; 2:2, 8, 21, 22; 3:9; 4:5, 10, 13; Ρουθ.
