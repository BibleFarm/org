Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to divide, i.e. (reflex.) be solitary

H909. badad

I. [בָּדַד‎] verb be separate, isolated (Arabic cause to withdraw; II. separate, disunite, Gen 11:8 Saad; IV. divide into parts; x. go alone, act independently), only Participle בּוֺדֵד‎ Isa 14:31 (of straggler in army), Hosea 8:9 לוֺ בוֺדֵד מֶּרֶא‎ a wild-ass (simile of Ephraim) going alone for itself (i.e. willfully: see below לְ‎), Psa 102:8 (of bird sitting solitarily).
