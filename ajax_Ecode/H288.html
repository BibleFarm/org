H288. Achimelek

אֲחִימֶ֫לֶךְ‎ proper name, masculine (brother of Melek, Phoenician חמלכת חמלך,‎; see also אחיהו‎ above)

1 priest in Saul's (David's) time 1Sam 21:2 (twice in verse); 21:3; 21:9; 22:14, 16 (אֲחִימֶ֑לֶךְ‎ 1Chr 24:31; Psa 52:2 = (title); son of Ahitub 1Sam 22:9, 11 (compare12); 22:20; father of Abiathar 23:6; 30:7; so also 2Sam 8:17 ᵑ6‎ Ew We Th Dr; where אחימ ׳בןאֿביתר ᵑ0‎; whence id. wrongly 1Chr 24:6, compare 24:4 (׳אֲח אִיתָמָר מִבְּנֵי‎) & 18:16 (where read ׳אֲח‎ for ׳אֲב‎).

2 a Hittite 1Sam 26:6.
