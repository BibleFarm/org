אַלְמֹנִי‎ (ʼalmônîy｜al-mo-nee'｜adjective｜some one)
[Heb] אַלְמֹנִי‎ ETCBC: אַלְמֹנִי‎ (subs｜stranger) OSHL: a.dr.al TWOT: 107 GK: H532
Derivation: from אַלְמֹן‎ in the sense of concealment;
Strong's: some one (i.e. so and so, without giving the name of the person or place)
KJV: one, and such.

H492. almoni

אַלְמֹנִי‎ adjective some one, a certain (name unspoken); א מְּלֹנִי ׳מְקוֺם‎ 1Sam 21:3; 2Kin 6:8; ׳פ ׳א‎ alone, of person, = such-an-one, so-and-so Ruth 4:1.
