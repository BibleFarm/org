Derivation: probably from an unused root meaning to coil;

H5919. akshub

עַכְשׁוּב‎ noun masculine usually asp, viper (so ᵑ9 ᵐ5‎; perhaps conject. from sense and || נָחָשׁ‎), but perhaps = (? corrupt for) עַכָּבִישׁ‎ spider (q. v.; compare ᵑ7‎on the passage); — ע ׳חֲמַת‎ Psa 140:4 a viper's (? spider's) venom is under their lips (see especially Che and references).

עַל‎ see עֹל עלה‎ see III. עלל‎.
