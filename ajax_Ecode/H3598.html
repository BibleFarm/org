H3598. Kimah

כִּימָה‎ noun feminine perhaps Pleiades (see above; Late Hebrew id.; ᵑ6 כִּימָא, ᵑ7‎ ) — as made by ׳י‎, Amos 5:8 (|| כְּסִיל‎), Job 9:9 (|| תֵמָן חַדְרֵי כְּסִיל, עָשׁ,‎); and under his control, כ ׳מַעֲדַנּוֺת‎ 38:31 (|| כְּסִיל משְׁכוֺת‎). — (SternJüd. Zeitschr. f. Wissen. u. Leben. iii.((1864-65) 258 ff. Nö in SchenkelBL HoffmZAW iii. 107 ff. interpret עשׁ‎ as Pleiades, and כימה‎ as Sirius).
