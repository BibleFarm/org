H7713. sederah

[שְׂדֵרָה‎] noun feminine 1. row, rank of soldiers in line.

2 architectural term. (probably for סְדֵרָה‎, see above and compare Ba§ 92 a); — only plural absolute שְׂדֵרוֺת‎ 2Kin 11:8; 2Chr 23:14, רֹת‎- 1Kin 6:9; 2Kin 11:15; —

1 rows, ranks, 2Kin 11:8, and 11:15 2Chr 23:14 (where thought to be gloss by Benz, on account of מִבַּיִת‎, within).

2 technical term of building, בָּאֲרָוִים וּשְׂדֵרֹת גֵּבִים‎ 1Kin 6:9, meaning unknown.
