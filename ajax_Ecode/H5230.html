H5230. nakal

[נָכַל‎] verb be crafty, deceitful, knavish (Assyrian nakâlu, be crafty, cunning; נְכֵל ᵑ7‎ be crafty, Pa`el deceive; Syriac deceive, plot; compare perhaps act faithlessly, faithless, PräAmhar. Spr. 90); —
