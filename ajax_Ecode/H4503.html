מִנְחָה‎ (minchâh｜min-khaw'｜noun｜feminine｜gift)
[Heb] מִנְחָה‎ ETCBC: מִנְחָה‎ (subs｜present) OSHL: m.cn.ab TWOT: 1214a GK: H4966 Greek: δῶρον, θυσία, μάννα, ὁλοκαύτωμα, προσφορά
Derivation: from an unused root meaning to apportion, i.e. bestow;
Strong's: a donation; euphemistically, tribute; specifically a sacrificial offering (usually bloodless and voluntary)
KJV: gift, oblation, (meat) offering, present, sacrifice.
