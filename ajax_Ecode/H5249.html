H5249. Nimrim

נִמְרִים‎ proper name, of a location in Moab (compare Sabean נמרן‎ attributive of Bar‡m, DHMZMG xxix (1875), 592, see also CISiv. No. 103, p. 169; wholesome water Lane2853, also Wel.c.); — only נִמְרִים מֵי‎ Isa 15:6; Jer 48:34; probably = Βηνναμαρειμ LagOnom. 284, 33, in Wady Numêre at southeast end of Dead Sea, BuhlGeogr. 272.

נִמְרִים‎
原文音譯：~yrIm.nI nim-reem’

詞類次數：專有名詞（2）

出現次數：總共（2）；賽（1）；耶（1）
