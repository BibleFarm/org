H5000. naveh

נָאוֶה‎ adjective comely, seemly; — ׳נ‎ masculine Song 2:14 3t.; feminine נָאוָה‎ Psa 33:1 4t., נָוָה‎ Jer 6:2; —

1 comely, beautiful, of woman Song 1:5; 6:4 (|| יָפָה‎) of face 2:14 (מַרְאֶה‎) so נָוָה‎ Jer 6:2 (figurative of Jerusalem; || מְעֻנָּגָה‎); of mouth Song 4:3 (|| שְׂפָתוֺת‎).

2 seemly, of praise תְּהִלָּה‎ Psa 33:1; 147:1; subject שְׂפַתיֶֿתֶר‎ Prov 17:7 (compare Song 4:3); תַּעֲנוּג‎ Cant 19:10; כָּבוֺד‎ Cant 26:1. So also Psa 93:5 Masora (anomalous feminine) see [נאה‎] above

נְאוֺת‎ see II. [ נָוָה‎] below II. נוה‎.

נאם‎ √ of following; compare groan, sigh; perhaps also Late Hebrew נוּם‎ speak).
