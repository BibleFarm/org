H4822. Meremoth

מְרֵמוֺת‎ proper name, masculine 1. priest, time of Zerubbabel Neh 12:3 (= מְרָיוֺת‎, q. v., 12:15) ᵐ5‎L Μαρειμωθ.

2 priest, time of Ezra and Nehemiah, Ezra 8:33; Neh 3:4, 21; 10:6 (probably same). ᵐ5‎ Μερειμωθ, Μεραμωθ, etc.

3 one of those who had taken strange wives Ezra 10:36. ᵐ5‎ Ιεραμωθ, A Μαρεμωθ.
