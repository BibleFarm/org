H4406. millah

מִלָּה‎ noun feminine word, thing; — absolute ׳מ‎ Dan 2:9 +, construct מִלַּת‎ 2:10 +; emphatic 2:5 +, ָ  א‎, 2:8 +; plural absolute מִלּין‎ 7:1, 25, construct מִלֵּי‎ 5:10, emphatic מִלַּיָּא‎ 7:11, 16; —

1 word, utterance Dan 4:28; 6:15, plural 5:10; 7:11, 25; in bad sense, ׳מ וּשְׁחִיתָה כִדְבָח‎ 2:9, רַבְרְבָתָא מִלַּיָּא‎ 7:11 (compare רַבְרְבָן‎ alone 7:8; 7:20); word of God's judgment 4:30; = command 2:5, 8; 3:22, 28; 6:13.

2 thing, affair, matter: Dan 2:10 (twice in verse); 2:11, 15, 17, 23; 5:15, 26; 7:28 (twice in verse), plural 7:1; 7:16.
