H3795. kathith

כָּתִית‎ adjective beaten; — only in combination כָּתִית שֶׁמֶן‎ beaten oil, i.e. oil made by beating or pounding the olives in a mortar; especially fine and costly (compare LevyNHWB ii. 443 below כָּתִית‎); — 1 Kings 5:25; Exod 29:40; Num 28:5; כ זָךְ זַית ׳שֶׁמֶן לַמָּאוֺר‎ Exod 27:20 pure beaten olive oil for the lamp = Lev 24:2 (Hexateuch only H, P).
