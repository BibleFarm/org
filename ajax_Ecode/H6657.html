H6657. Tsedad

[צְדָד‎] proper name, of a location on northern border of Canaan, with ה‎ locative, צְדָ֫דָה‎ Num 34:8 (Σαραδακ, Samaritan צרדה‎), Ezek 47:15 (Σελδαμμα), read probably צְרָ֫דָה‎; perhaps Khirbet ‚era-da-, north of Abil, east of Merj 'Ajûn toward Hermon (latitude approximatly 33° 25' north, longitude approximately 35° 35' east), so van KasterenRev. Bibl., 1895, 30, compare BuhlGeogr. 67.
