[רָדַם‎] verb Niph`al be in, or fall into, heavy sleep (Late Hebrew id.; compare Arabic stop up (door, gap, etc.), whence perhaps be deaf (stopped up) to sounds, etc.); — Perfect3masculine singular נִרְדַּם‎ Judg 4:21; 1singular נִרְדַּמְתִּי‎ Dan 8:8; Imperfect3masculine singular וַיֵּרָדֵם‎ Jonah 1:5; Participle נִרְדָּם‎ Prov 10:5 3t.; — be or fall fast asleep: Judg 4:21; Jonah 1:5 (after וַיִּשְׁכַב‎), 1:6 (participle, Ges§ 120b DaSynt. § 70 (a)); participle as substantive בַּקָּצִיר נִרְדָּם‎ Prov 10:5; of sleep of death Psa 76:7; stunning effect of awe and dread Dan 8:18; 10:9 (both + אָ֑רְצָה‎ [וּפָנַי‎] עַלמָּֿנַי‎.
