H737. aruchah

אֲרֻחָה‎ noun feminine meal, allowance (of food) absolute Jer 40:5; construct יָרָק אֲרֻחַת‎ Prov 15:17 a portion of herbs, i.e. a slender meal; elsewhere of allowance given to captive king Jehoiachin, ׳א תָּמִיד‎ a continual allowance 2Kin 25:30 = Jer 52:34; אֲרֻחָתוֺ‎ ib. = id.
