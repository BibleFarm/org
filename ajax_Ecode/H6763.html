צֵלָע‎ (tsêlâʻ｜tsay-law'｜noun｜feminine｜rib)
[Heb] צֵלָע‎ ETCBC: צֵלָע‎ (subs｜side) OSHL: r.cf.ab TWOT: 1924a GK: H7521 Greek: μάστιξ, μέρος, ξύλον, πέρας, πλευρά
Derivation: or (feminine) צַלְעָה‎; from צָלַע‎;
Strong's: a rib (as curved), literally (of the body) or figuratively (of a door, i.e. leaf); hence, a side, literally (of a person) or figuratively (of an object or the sky, i.e. quarter); architecturally, a (especially floor or ceiling) timber or plank (single or collective, i.e. a flooring)
KJV: beam, board, chamber, corner, leaf, plank, rib, side (chamber).
