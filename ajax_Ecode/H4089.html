H4089. Madmannah

מַדְמַנָּה‎ 1. proper name, of a location city of southern Judah Josh 15:31; location dubious, Onomasticon Μηδεβηνα = Μηνοεις near Gaza LagOnom. 279, 2nd ed. 276; compare Minyay south of Gaza RobBR i. 602; on other proposed identification compare Di.

2. proper name, masculine descendant of Caleb 1Chr 2:49.
