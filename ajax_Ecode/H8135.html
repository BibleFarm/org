1 human, Num 35:20 (P), Ezek 23:29; 35:11; Prov 10:12, 18; 15:17; 26:26; Psa 109:5; Eccl 9:1, 6; שׂ ׳דִּבְרֵי‎ Psa 109:3; emphatic, חָמָס שִׂנְאַת‎ 25:19, שׂ ׳תַּכְלִית‎ 139:22 (against ׳י‎s foes), also (of sexual revulsion) ׳שׂ גְּדוֺלָה‎ 2Sam 13:15 (all accusative of congnate meaning with verb).
