H2918. tirah

[טִירָה‎] noun feminine encampment, battlement (from idea of surrounding, enclosure; Syriac sheepfold, ἔπαυλις) — construct טִירַת‎ Song 8:9; טִירָתָם‎ Psa 69:25; plural טִירוֺת‎ Ezek 46:23; suffix טִירֹתָם‎ Num 31:10; Gen 25:16; טִירוֺתָם‎ 1Chr 6:39; טִירוֺתֵיהֶם‎ Ezek 25:4; —

1 encampment, especially of circular encampment of nomad tribes, mentioned with חצר‎ Gen 25:16; with ערים‎ Num 31:10; Ezek 25:4 (|| מִשׁכניהם‎); encampment (poetry) = habitation Psa 69:26 (|| אהליהם‎); more Generally, 1Chr 6:39 (|| מושׁבות‎).

2 in metaphor ׳ט כָּ֑סֶף‎ Song 8:9 a battlement of silver.

3 row of stones, only plural טִירוֺת‎ Ezek 46:23, virtually plural of טוּר‎ 1, q. v.
