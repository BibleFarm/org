H4038. maggal

מוֺלֶ֫דֶת‎ noun feminine kindred, birth, offspring; — ׳מ‎ construct Lev 18:9, 11; suffix מוֺלַדְתִּי‎ Gen 24:4 3t.; etc.; plural suffix מוֺלְדוֺתַיִךְ‎ Ezek 16:4; מֹלְדֹתַיִךְ‎ 16:3; —

1 kindred Gen 12:1 (|| אביך בית ארצך,‎), 24:4 (|| ארץ‎), Num 10:30 (|| id.), Gen 31:3 (|| אבותיך ארץ‎), 43:7 (all J); || עַם‎ Est 2:10, 20; 8:6; especially מ ׳אֶרֶץ‎ land of one's kindred Gen 11:28; 24:7 (both J), 31:13 (E), Jer 22:10; 46:16; Ezek 23:15; Ruth 2:11.

2 plural circumstances of birth, birth (figurative of origin of Jerusalem) וּמ ׳מְכֹרֹתַיִךְ הַכְּנַעֲנִי מֵאֶרֶץ‎ Ezek 16:3, compare 16:4.

3 (female) offspring, one born Lev 18:9 (twice in verse), begotten 18:11 (all H); collective = issue, offspring הוֺלַ֫דְתָּ אֲשֶׁר מוֺלַדְתְּךָ‎ Gen 48:6 (P).

מַגָּל‎ noun [masculine] sickle (Arabic (yet possibly Aramaic loan-word Frä133), Aramaic ); — with תָּפַשׂ‎ handle, wield Jer 50:16 (|| זָרַע‎); with שָׁלַח‎ Joel 4:13 figurative of judgement.
