נֶכֶד‎ (neked｜neh'-ked｜noun｜masculine｜progeny)
[Heb] נֶכֶד‎ ETCBC: נֶכֶד‎ (subs｜progeny) OSHL: n.dj.ab TWOT: 1363a GK: H5781 Greek: ὄνομα, σπέρμα
Derivation: from an unused root meaning to propagate;
Strong's: offspring
KJV: nephew, son's son.

H5220. neked

נֶ֫כֶד‎ noun [masculine] progeny, posterity, always with נִין‎ in standing phrase (Ecclus 41:5; 47:22c; both with נין‎); — absolute נ וְלֹא נִין ׳לֹא בְּעַמּוֺ‎ Job 18:19, compare וְנ וְנִין ׳וּשְׁאָר שֵׁם‎ Isa 14:22; suffix וּלְנֶכְדִּי וּלְנִינִי לִי‎ Gen 21:23 to me and to my offspring and to my posterity.
