H1131. Binnuwy

בִּנּוּי‎ proper name, masculine 1. head of a family that returned with Zerubbabel Neh 7:15 (= בָּנִי‎ || Ezra 2:10 & perhaps 10:34 compare SmListen 14; but BeRy proposes here בִּגְוַי‎).

2 a Levite of Ezra's time Ezra 8:33; Neh 10:10 (properly = בָּנִי‎ 8:7, בֻּנִּי‎ 9:4) 12:8; compare 3:24 (בַּוַי‎, textual error 3:18) see BeRy; also Ezra 2:40 = Neh 7:43 לִבְּנֵי‎ read perhaps לְבִנֻּי‎ compare SmListen 16 but BeRy otherwise; compare further GrGeschichte.ii.2, 389.

3 Israelites of Ezra's time,

a. Ezra 10:30;

b. Ezra 10:38.
