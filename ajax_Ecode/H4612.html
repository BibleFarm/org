H4612. maamad

[מַעֲמָד‎] noun [masculine] office, function, service; —

1 station, office, post (מַעֲמָֽדְךָ‎) Isa 22:19 (|| מַצָּֽבְךָ‎), עַלמַֿעֲמָדָם‎2Chr 35:15 at their post.

2 office, function 1Chr 23:28.

3 service, probably specifically waiting at table (strictly mode of standing), construct מְשָֽׁרְתָיו מַעֲמַד‎ 1Kin 10:5 2Chr 9:4.
