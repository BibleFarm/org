H7612. sheth

שֵׁאת‎ noun feminine doubtful word; perhaps (for *שֶׁאֶת‎) din of battle (= שָׁאוֺן‎); Thes and others devastation, compare שְׁאִיָּה‎; either meaning suits ׳הַשּׁ‎ Lam 3:47 (|| הַשָּׁ֑בֶר‎; but Wecompare Hex. 2, 351, from II. שׁוא‎, desolation); contracted (according to Thes and most) שֵׁת‎, in שֵׁת בְּנֵי‎ Num 24:17 (J) sons of (battle-)din (warriors, = שָׁאוֺןבְּנֵי‎ in || Jer 48:45); Vrss take שֵׁת‎ here as proper name; Gray proposes שֵׂת‎ = שְׂאֵת‎, pride, or reading as Jeremiah compare Wecomp. Hex.351.
