H4983. Mattanyah

or Mattanyahu מַתַּנְיָה מַתַּנְיָ֫הוּ,‎ proper name, masculine (compare GrayProp. N. 294, No. 82); — ᵐ5‎ Μαθθας, Μανθανιας, Ματθανιας, etc.; —

1 מַתַּנְיָה‎, last king of Judah, name changed to Zedekiah 2Kin 24:17.

2 מַתַּנְיָ֫הוּ‎, Levite name:

a. son of Heman 1Chr 25:4.

b. Asaphite 1Chr 25:16.

c. id., 2Chr 29:13 (Hezekiah's time).

3 מַתַּנְיָה‎, Levite name:

a. Asaphite 1Chr 9:15; Neh 11:17, 22; 12:8, 35, perhaps = 2b, c (but much confusion and uncertainty; e.g. ׳מ‎ is of Hezekiah's time 2Chr 29:13, and 4th Generation before Nehemiah, Neh 11:12; 12:35; while contemporary of Zerubbabel 12:8 and of Nehemiah 11:17).

b. other Levites: (1) 2Chr 20:14, (2) Neh 12:25, (3) 13:13.

4 מַתַּנְיָה‎, name of several who took strange wives:

a. Ezra 10:26.

b. Ezra 10:27.

c. Ezra 10:30.

d. Ezra 10:37.
