H1100. beliyyaal

בְּלִיַּ֫עַל‎27 noun [masculine] worthlessness (compound בְּלִי‎ not, without and יַעַל‎ worth, use, profit) — ׳בּ‎ Deut 13:14 20t.; בְּלִיָּ֑עַל‎ Psa 101:3 5t.; — the quality of being useless, good for nothing.

1 abstract הבליעל אַנְשֵׁי (ה)בליעל, אִישׁ‎, worthless, good-for-nothing, base fellows 1Sam 25:25; 2Sam 16:7; 20:1; 1Kin 21:13; Prov 16:27; = ׳בֶּןבֿ‎ 1Sam 25:17, ב ׳בְּנֵי‎ Deut 13:14; Judg 19:22; 20:13; 1Sam 2:12; 10:27; 1Kin 21:10, 13; 2Chr 13:7; ב ׳בַּת‎ 1Sam 1:16 (drunken woman); ב ׳עֵד‎ base witness Prov 19:28; ב ׳דְּבַר‎ base, wicked thing Psa 41:9 (yet compare 3 below), 101:3 (add probably also 1Sam 29:10, so ᵐ5‎ We Dr); ׳דָּבָרב֗֗֗‎ (elliptical and in apposition) Deut 15:9.

2 concrete elliptical of ב ׳אישׁ‎ 2Sam 23:6; Job 34:18; וּבְלִיַּעַל רַע כָּלאִֿישׁ‎ 1Sam 30:22; בְּלִיַּעַל אָדָם‎ Prov 6:12.

3 ruin, destruction: so Psa 41:9 according to De Che and others, but see above; ב ׳יֹעֵץ‎ counsellor of ruin Nahum 1:11; ׳ב‎ alone a man of ruin, destroyer 2:1; ב ׳נַחֲלֵי‎ floods of destruction (|| שְׁאוֺל‎) 2Sam 22:5 = Psa 18:5.
