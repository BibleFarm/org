H224. Urim

אֻרִים‎ noun [masculine] plural region of light, East Isa 24:15 (so Ges MV Ew De Di RV; Lo Hi Kn Che read איים‎ = coasts, so Cdd. ᵐ5‎; — ᵐ5‎ mostly omit ᵑ9‎ in doctrina, thinking of Urim, see BrMP 297).

אוּרִים‎ noun masculine Urim (plural intensive, mostly with article the Urim, and mostly joined with תֻּמִּים‎ q. v.) הֲסִידֶךָ לְאִישׁ וְאוּרֶיךָ תֻּמֶּיךָ‎ Deut 33:8 thy Thummim and thy Urim has the man of thy favour, i.e. the Levite tested at Massah & Meribah; וְהִתֻּמִּים הָאוּרִים‎ were put into the הִמִּשְׁמָּט חשֶׁן‎ of the high priest Exod 28:30; Lev 8:8 (P); this חשֶׁן‎ (q. v.) was a little bag or pouch worn on breast of high, priest, to hold the Urim & Thummim; the name ׳ח הַמִּשְׁמָּט‎ was given because of decisions made by that which was within it; thus, Eleazar was to inquire of ׳י‎ for Joshua הָאוּרִים בְּמִשְׁמַּט‎ Num 27:21 (P); Saul prayed אוּרִים הָבָה‎ opposed to תֻּמִּים הבה‎, 1Sam 14:41 ᵐ5‎, so We Dr (ᵑ0‎ omits former, and reads תָּמִים‎ in latter); ׳י‎ did not answer Saul בָּאוּרִים‎ or by dreams or prophets 28:6; post-exilic Jews reserved difficult questions until there stood up a priest וּלְתֻמִּים לְאוּרִים‎ Ezra 2:63; Neh 7:65 (here alone without article) These passages give little information; ᵐ5‎ δήλωσις καὶ ἀλήθεια, ᵑ9‎ doctrina et veritas, Symm φωτισμοὶ καὶ τελεώτητες; JosAnt. iii. 8. 9 thinks of the twelve gems of face of bag as giving decision by shining; PhiloVit. Mos. iii thinks of two small images (׳א‎ & ׳ת‎), probably embroidered in the cloth of the bag, like oracle-images of Egypt (Diod1, 48, 75 AelianVar. Hist.14, 34); EwGeschichte iii, 309; Antiq. 295, of two stones of different colours for sacred lot, on this see Dr 1Sam 14:41 where אוּרִים ᵐ5‎ & תֻּמִּים‎ as object of give, δος = הָבָה‎; compare הִמִּילוּ‎ 14:42, וַיִלָּכֵד‎ 14:41 (used of taking by lot 10:20; Isa 7:14, 16); see also We & RSOTJC Lect x. N 4; BährSymbolik, of one thing within bag, a sacred pledge to high priest of the enlightenment & perfection he would receive from ׳י‎ when called to make sacred decisions; KaEx 544 sees the sacred pledge in the twelve sacred gems themselves, that stimulate priest to self-sacrifice & perfect sanctification.

אוּר / אוּרִים / אֻרִים‎
原文音譯：~yrIWa oo-reem’

詞類次數：名詞（7）

原文字根：光

字義溯源：烏陵；意為：光，火焰；為（H217（אוּר‎）=火焰）的複數，而H217（אוּר‎）又出自（H215（אֹור‎）*=使發光）烏陵和土明是放在大祭司決斷的胸牌裏（出28:30；利8:8）有的聖經學者說，烏陵與土明是二塊方形石，一白一黑，白的表明：是；黑的表明：否；藉此來解答他們在耶和華面前的求問；至於實際情形，不得而知，（拉2:63）與（尼7:65）不過是說明烏陵與土明在被擄回來後己無可使用了，可能在被擄那一段時日被毀損了。烏陵共用了七次，五次是與土明同時用，二次是單獨用（民27:21；撒上28:6）
