H5578. Sansannah

סַנְסַנָּה‎ proper name, of a location in southern Judah; — Josh 15:31, ᵐ5‎ Σεθεννακ, A Σανσαννα, ᵐ5‎L Σεενακ; probably = סוּסָה חֲצַר‎ 19:5, חֲצַרסֿוּסִים‎ 1Chr 4:31; conjectures on location (near Gaza), see in Dion the passage BuhlGeogr. 163 and references (Simsum, northeast of Gaza, compare by J. SchwarzDas heilig. Land (1852), 72 van d. VeldeMem, 346, is too far north).
