H6175. arum

עָרוּם‎ adjective crafty, shrewd, sensible; —

1 crafty, ׳ע‎ as predicate, of serpent Gen 3:1; plural as substantive עֲרוּמִים מַחְשְׁבוֺת‎ Job 5:12, ע ׳לְשׁוֺן‎ 15:5.

2 in Proverbs, in good sense (opposed to מֶּתִי כְּסִיל, אֱוִיל,‎); as attributive עָרוּם אָדָם‎ Prov 12:23 a shrewd or sensible man; ׳ע‎ as substantive = id., 12:16; 13:16, so ע ׳חָכְמַת‎ 13:8; = prudent man 14:15; 22:3; 27:12; plural עֲרוּמִים‎ 14:18.
