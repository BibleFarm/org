יָחִיל‎ adjective

verb. waiting, so Thes SS Buhl, but very dubious, — only לְ וְדוּמָם וְיָחִיל טוֺב‎ Lam 3:26 good is it that one be waiting and that silently (but see דוּמָם‎) for the salvation of ׳י‎; construct. with ו‎ and adjective hardly possibly; Ew Ke Lohr Köii. 407 and others regard as verb (from חול‎); read perhaps וְיֹחִיל‎ Hiph`il Imperfect from יחל‎ (compare 3:24).
