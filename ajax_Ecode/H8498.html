תְּכוּנָה‎ (tᵉkûwnâh｜tek-oo-naw'｜noun｜feminine｜arrangement)
[Heb] תְּכוּנָה‎ ETCBC: תְּכוּנָה‎ (subs｜fixed place) OSHL: k.aw.ak TWOT: 964e GK: H9414
Derivation: feminine passive participle of תָּכַן‎;
Strong's: adjustment, i.e. structure; by implication, equipage
KJV: fashion, store.

H8498. tekunah

תְּכוּנָה‎ noun feminine arrangement, preparation, fixed place; —

1 arrangement, disposition תְּכוּנָתוֺ‎ Ezek 43:11, i.e. the arrangement of the house (strike out Co with ᵐ5‎ Manuscripts)

2 preparation, לַתְּכוּנָה קֶצֶה וְאֵין‎ Nahum 2:10 and no end to the preparation (i.e. things prepared, supply, store).

3 fixed place, i.e. dwelling-place, of God (= מָכוֺן‎) תְּכוּנָתוֺ‎ Job 23:3.
