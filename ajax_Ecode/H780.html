H780. Ararat

אֲרָרַט‎ proper name, of a territory Ararat (Assyrian Urar‰u COTGloss, compare LagArmen. Stud. § 100) — ׳א‎ Jer 51:27; אֲרָרָ֑ט‎ Gen 8:4 3t. — a district in Eastern Armenia between the river Araxes & lakes Van & Oroomiah, compare KGF; to this probably reference in 2Kin 19:37 = Isa 37:38 א ׳אֶרֶץ‎; also Gen 8:4 א ׳הָרֵי‎, where the ark rested; used perhaps with wider reference Jer 51:27 א ׳מַמְלְכוֺת‎ (|| אַשְׁכְּנַז מִנִּי,‎).
