H2801. charath

[חָרַת‎] verb grave, engrave, only

Qal Passive participle הַלֻּחֹת עַל חָרוּת‎ Exod 32:16 (E), engraved upon the tablets. (by finger of God), but from absence of || in cognate languages (Arabic is perforate, bore, slit) probably error for חָרוּשׁ‎ (Jer 17:1).
