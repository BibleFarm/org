H6116. atsarah

or atsereth עֲצֶ֫רֶת עֲצָרָה,‎ noun feminine assembly (? as confined, held in); — absolute עֲצָרָה‎ Isa 1:13 2t.; עֲצֶ֫רֶת‎ Deut 16:8 3t., עֲצָ֑רֶת‎2Chr 7:9; construct עֲצֶ֑רֶת‎ Jer 9:1; plural suffix עַצְרֹתֵיכֶם‎ Amos 5:21; —

1 sacred assembly, rejected by ׳י‎, Isa 1:13 and (at Bethel, etc.) Amos 5:21; for Baal 2Kin 10:20; Deut 16:8 (last day of Maƒƒoth), Lev 23:36 (P), Num 29:35 (P), Neh 8:18 (all of day after feast of booths), compare 2Chr 7:9 [Late Hebrew of feast of weeks, עֲצַרְתָּא ᵑ7‎ JosAnt. iii. 10, 6 Ἀσάρτα]; occasional, Joel 1:14; 2:15.

2 assemblage, company (in General), ׳ע בֹּגְדִים‎ Jer 9:1 an assemblage of deceivers (Gr עֲדַת‎; Che חֶבְרַת‎).
