H1533. Gilboa

גִּלְבֹּעַ‎ proper name, of a location Gilboa` (derivation unknown), mountain-ridge at southeast end of plain of Jezreel, where Saul & Jonathan were killed; usually with article הַגּ ׳הַר‎ 1Sam 31:1, 8; 2Sam 1:6; בַּגִּלְבֹּעַ הָרֵי‎ 1:21 (David's lament); ׳בַּגּ‎ alone 1Sam 28:4; 2Sam 21:12; without article גִּלְבֹּעַ הַר‎ 1Chr 10:1, 8 (|| 1Sam 31:1, 8 above); — modern Jebel Fu‡û±a BdPal 244.
