H4511. Minnith

מִנִּית‎ proper name, of a location in Ammonite territory, site unknown (4 Roman miles from Heshbon Euseb.Onom. 253, 33; compare BuhlGeogr. 266); — Judg 11:33 (ᵐ5‎ ἄχρις Ἀρνών, A εἰς Σεμωειθ, ᵐ5‎L Σεμενειθ); מ ׳חִטֵּי‎ Ezek 27:17 wheat of Minnîth (compare wheat from Ammon 2Chr 27:5) so Berthol, but dubious; Co conjectures וּנְכאֹת חִטִּים‎ wheat and storax.

[מְנֻסָה‎] see מְנוּסָה‎ below נוס‎.
