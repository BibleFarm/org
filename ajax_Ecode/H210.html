H210. Uphaz

אוּפָז‎ ( proper name, of a location unknown & dubious) whence came gold, מֵא ׳זָהָב‎ Jer 10:9; א ׳ֶכּתֶם‎ Dan 10:5; so Thes 1Kin 10:18 מוּפָז זָהָב‎ (מוּפָז‎ = ׳מֵא‎); but ᵐ5‎ δόκιμος, & 2Chr 9:17 טָהוֺר‎, whence MV Klo make מוּפָז‎ Hoph`al Participle from פזז‎ q. v. Klo reads אוֺפִיר‎ (q. v.) for אוּפָז‎ Jer 10:9; Dan 10:5, in view of אוֺפִיר כֶּתֶם‎ Isa 13:12. **Corrupt, see SiegfThLz, Nov. 7, 1883, 530; he explained ׳מֵא‎ Jer 10:9 as error for מוּפָז‎ 1Kin 10:18, and ׳א‎ Dan 10:5 as borrowed from Jer 10:9; in 10:9 Gie reads מֵאוֺפִיר‎, Du either this or מוּפָז‎ (see פזז‎); read מֵאוֺפִיר‎ perhaps also Dan 10:5 (Dr).
