H672. Ephrath

or Ephrathah אֶפְרָ֫תָה‎ proper name (see Sta§ 308 d. 1; 342 d. 2; Morgenl. Forsch. 215) — might in several cases, e.g. Gen 35:16, be אֶפְרָת‎ + ה‎ locative, but see Ruth 4:11; —

1. proper name, of a location place near Bethel, where Rachel died & was buried Gen 35:16, 19; 48:7 (אֶפְרָת‎ only 48:7 where ה‎ perhaps dropped on accusative of ה‎ following (Ol); in last two passages לחם בית הוא‎ is a gloss, see Di; compare 1Sam 10:2).

2 id., a name of Bethlehem Micah 5:1; Ruth 4:11; compare perhaps אֶפְרָתָ֑ה כָּלֵב‎ 1Chr 2:24 (ᵐ5‎ ἦλθεν Ξαλὲβ εἰς Ἐφράθα).

3 id. Psa 132:6, perhaps applied to district where Kirjath Jearim lay, on the border of Judah & Benjamin, compare De Che.

4. proper name, feminine אֶפְרָת‎ name given to wife of Caleb 1Chr 2:19 = אֶפְרָתָ֑ח‎ 2:50; 4:4.

1）以法他就是伯利恆（創35:19）
