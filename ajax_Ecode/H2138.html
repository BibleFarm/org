H2138. zakur

[זָכוּר‎] noun masculine male, i. q. זָכָר‎, but only with suffix; — collective כָּלזְֿכוּרְךָ‎ Exod 23:17 = 34:23 (both J E), = Deut 16:16, of attendance at feasts; כָּלזְֿכוּרָהּ‎ (of city refusing terms of peace) 20:13 commanded to be slain (opposed to הַטַּף הַנָּשִׁים,‎, etc., 20:14).
