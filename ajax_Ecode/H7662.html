H7662. shebaq

[שְׁבַק‎] verb leave, let alone (שְׁבַק ᵑ7‎, Syriac ; compare Biblical Hebrew, p. 990); —

Pe`al Infinitive לְמִשְׁבַּק‎ Dan 4:23 leave roots, so Imperative masculine plural שְׁבֻ֫קוּ‎ 4:12; 4:20 (both with ב‎ location); Ezra 6:7 let alone, with ל‎ accusative of thing.

Hithpe`el Imperfect3feminine singular תִּשְׁתְּבִק‎ Dan 2:44 be left (of kingdom, + ל‎ of a people).
