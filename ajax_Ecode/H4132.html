H4132. mot

מוֺט‎ noun [masculine] shaking, pole, bar of yoke; — absolute ׳מ‎ Num 4:10 5t.; suffix מֹטֵ֫הוּ‎ Nahum 1:13; —

1 a shaking; tottering (c. negative, of security) ׳מ לֹאיִֿתֵּן לַצַּדִּיק‎ Psa 55:23; לַמ נָתַן ׳לֹא רַגְלֵנוּ‎ 66:9, compare 121:3.

2 pole, or bar for carrying (named from springing motion) Num 4:10, 12 compare especially 13:23.

3 bar of yoke Nahum 1:13 (מֵעָלַיַךְ מֹטֵהוּ אֶשְׁבֹּר‎), figurative of oppression. compare following.
