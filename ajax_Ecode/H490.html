אַלְמָנָה‎54 noun feminine widow (Mishna id., Assyrian almattu Dl in ZimBP 114) — ׳א‎ Gen 38:11 +; no construct; plural אַלְמָנוֺת‎ Exod 22:23 +; plural suffix אַלְמְנוֺתָיו‎ Job 27:18, etc. — widow 1Kin 17:20; אַ ׳אִשָּׁה‎ 2Sam 14:5 (|| אִישִׁי וַיָּ֫מָת‎) 1Kin 7:14; 11:26; 17:9-10, Gen 38:11 (J; living in father's house) compare Lev 22:13 (H; || גְּרוּשָׁה‎); 21:14 (H), where widow forbidden as wife of high priest, like זֹנָה חֲלָלָה, גְּרוּשָׁה,‎ compare Ezek 44:22 (3 t. in verse) (id. of all priests, except widow of priest); Num 30:10 (P; of widow's vow, || גְּרוּשָׁה‎); אַלְמָנוֺת נְשֵׁיכֶם‎ = ye shall be slain Exod 22:23 (|| יְתֹמִים בְּנֵיכֶם‎) compare Psa 109:9; Jer 15:8; 18:21; Ezek 22:25, also Lam 5:3; (simile); of those snatched away by pestilence תִבְכֶּינָה לֹא אַלְמְנוֺתָיו‎ Job 27:15; by sword, id., Psa 78:64; ᵐ5‎ We read חַיּוֺת אַלְמָנוֺת‎ 2Sam 20:3 (see אַלְמָנוּת‎ below) of imprisoned concubines; figurative of Jerusalem Lam 1:1; Babylon Isa 47:8; especially widow as helpless, exposed to oppression & harsh treatment (often || יָתוֺם‎, & גֵּר‎); 1:23; 10:2; Job 22:9; 24:3; 31:16; Psa 94:6; Mal 3:5; harshness forbidden, & care for them enjoined Exod 22:21 (E) Deut 14:29; 16:11, 14; 24:17, 19, 20, 21; 26:12-13, 27:19; Isa 1:17; Jer 7:6; 22:3; Zech 7:10, compare Job 29:13; under especially care of God Deut 10:18; Jer 49:11; Prov 15:25; Psa 68:6; 146:9; once of severity of judgment in not sparing widow Isa 9:16. (׳אַל‎ 13:22 see below אַרְמוֺן‎; Ezek 19:7 see ib. & also below מעון‎.)
