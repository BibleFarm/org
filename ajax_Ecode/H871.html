H871. Atharim

אֲתָרִים‎ proper name, of a location only Num 21:1 הָא ׳דֶּרֶח‎; so ᵐ5‎, perhaps (Di) name of a caravan-route, compare vestige, footprint; others (after ᵑ6 ᵑ7‎) translate way of the spies (compare 13:22); but הָאֲתָרִים‎ for הַתָּרִים‎ is highly improbable, and a locality would hardly receive its designation from the spies.
