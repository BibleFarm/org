עֵדוּת‎19 and עֵדֻת‎27

noun feminine testimony; — ׳ע‎ absolute Exod 16:34 +, construct Psa 19:8 +; plural suffix עֵדְוֺתֶיךָ‎ 119:14 +, עֵדְוֺתָיו‎ Jer 44:23 4t.; —

1 testimony of the Ten Words on the tables as a solemn divine charge, הע ׳לֻחֹת‎ Exod 31:18; 32:15; 34:29 (P), ׳הע‎ 25:16, 21; 40:20 (P); ark as containing tables, הע ׳אֲרוֺן‎ 25:22; 26:33-34, 30:6, 26; 39:35; 40:3, 5, 21; Num 4:5; 7:89 (all P), Josh 4:16 (J E; Kue הברית‎ [against him Di], so Benn Steuern), לע ׳הארן‎ Exod 31:7 (P); abbreviated הע ׳לִפְנֵי‎ 16:34; 30:36; Numbers 17:19; Numbers 17:25 (P); הע ׳עַל‎ 27:21 (= ׳הע‎ Lev 24:3), Lev 30:6; 16:13 (P); tabernacle as containing ark of testimony, הע ׳משׁכן‎ tabernacle of the testimony Exod 38:21; Num 1:50, 53 (twice in verse); 10:11 (P); so הע ׳אֹהֶל‎ 9:15; Numbers 17:22; Numbers 17:23; 18:2 (P) 2Chr 24:6.

2 the code of law in General (late), as a testimony of God: Psa 19:8; 119:88; 122:4; || תּוֺרָה‎ 78:5; || חֹק‎ 81:6. In titles, ע ׳שׁוּשַׁן עַלֿ‎ 60:1, ע ׳אֶלשֹֿׁשַׁנּ֑ים‎ 80:1 = melody whose first line compare law as testimony to a choice flower. Special laws of the code are denoted by plural; + חֻקּוֺת‎ etc. 1Kin 2:3; 2Kin 23:3 2Chr 34:31 (+ חקים‎ etc.), Jer 44:23; חֻקִּים‎ etc. 1Chr 29:19; מִצְוֺת‎ Neh 9:34; plural of contents of covenant 2Kin 17:15; plural elsewhere Psa 119:14; 119:31; 119:36; 119:99; 119:111; 119:129; 119:144; 119:157. See also III.[[עֵדָה‎]. — For הָעֵדוּת‎ 2Kin 11:12 2Chr 23:11 read probably הַצְּעָדוֺת‎ (compare 2Sam 1:10 We and others).
