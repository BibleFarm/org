H4732. Miqloth

מִקְלוֺת‎ proper name, masculine 1. a Benjamite 1Chr 8:32 (read perhaps also 8:31 near the end; so ᵐ5‎, compare 9:37), 9:37-38, — ᵐ5‎ Μακαλωθ, Μακελλωθ, etc.

2 an officer of David according to 1Chr 27:4 (omitted by ᵐ5‎B; ᵐ5‎L Μακελλωθ).
