H8559. Tamar

II. תָּמָר‎25 proper name

1. feminine

a. daughter-in-law of Judah Gen 38:6, 11 (twice in verse); 38:13, 24; 1Chr 2:4; Ruth 4:12.

b. daughter of David, sister of Absalom 2Sam 13:1, 211t. 13, 2Chr 3:9.

c. daughter of Absalom 2Sam 14:27.

2. location Ezek 47:19; 48:28; 1Kin 9:18 Kt ( > Qr תַּדְמֹר‎ q. v.), so read also || 2Chr 8:4 (for תַּדְמֹר ᵑ0‎); site not certain, compare BuhlG 184. — תָּמָר בַּעַל‎ see בַּעַל‎.
