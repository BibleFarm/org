מָה‎ (mâh｜maw｜interrogative pronoun｜what?)
[Heb] מָה‎, לָמָּה‎, מַי‎, שַׁלָּמָה‎, מַּזֶה‎, מִזֶּה‎ ETCBC: מָה‎ (prin｜what), לָמָה‎ (inrg｜why) OSHL: m.an.aa TWOT: 1149 GK: H4342, H4537, H4768, H8975 Greek: αἴτημα, ἱκανός, ἱκανός, λίαν, ποῖος, τίς, τίς, τίς, τίς, λαμά
Derivation: or מַה‎; or מָ‎; or מַ‎; also מֶה‎; a primitive particle;
Strong's: properly, interrogative what? (including how? why? when?); but also exclamation, what! (including how!), or indefinitely what (including whatever, and even relatively, that which); often used with prefixes in various adverbial or conjunctive senses
KJV: how (long, oft, (-soever)), (no-) thing, what (end, good, purpose, thing), whereby(-fore, -in, -to, -with), (for) why.
