הִגָּיֹון‎ (higgâyôwn｜hig-gaw-yone'｜noun｜masculine｜resounding music)
[Heb] הִגָּיֹון‎, הִגָּיׄון‎ ETCBC: הִגָּיֹון‎ (subs｜groaning) OSHL: e.aj.ad TWOT: 467c GK: H2053 Greek: ᾠδή
Derivation: intensive from הָגָה‎;
Strong's: a murmuring sound, i.e. a musical notation (probably similar to the modern affettuoso to indicate solemnity of movement); by implication, a machination
KJV: device, Higgaion, meditation, solemn sound.

H1902. higgayon

הִגָּיוֺן‎ noun masculine resounding music, meditation, musing; — ׳ה‎ absolute Psa 9:17; 92:4; construct הֶגְיוֺן‎ Psalm 19:15; suffix הֶגְיוֺנָם‎ Lam 3:62; —

1 resounding music; בְּכִנּוֺר חִגָּיוֺן עֲלֵי‎ Psa 92:4 with sounding music upon the lyre (Che); compare ׳ה סֶ֑לָה‎ 9:17 (a musical direction, see סלה‎).

2 meditation, musing לִבִּי וְהֶגְיוֺן אִמְרֵיפִֿי‎ Psalm 19:15 the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart; also in bad sense = plotting עָלַי וְהֶגְיוֺנָם קָמַי שֹׂפְתֵי‎ Lam 3:62; the lips of those rising against me, and their imagining against me (|| מַחְשְׁבֹתָם‎ 3:61; compare I.הָגָה‎ 3b).

הִגָּיׄון‎
原文音譯：!AyG"hi hig-gaw-yone’

詞類次數：名詞（4）

原文字根：鴣鴣聲

字義溯源：喃喃之聲，意即：音樂符號，（也許是哀婉動人的曲子，表示節奏莊嚴）幽雅的聲音，示意：一種陰謀，計謀，計劃，意念，考慮，嚴肅的聲音，希迦庸（此編號的音譯）源自（H1897（הָגָה‎）*=低聲怨言）的加強
