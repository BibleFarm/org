H2892. tohar

טֹּ֫הַר‎ noun [masculine] purity, purifying —

1 purity, לָטֹּ֫הַר הַשָּׁמַיִם כְּעֶצֶם‎ Exod 24:10 (JE), as the body of heavens for purity.

2 suffix טָהֳרָהּ יְמֵי‎ Lev 12:4, 6 the days of her purifying (menstruation).

[טֳהָר‎] (see Ges§ 10. 2. B), or [טְהָר‎] (Ol§ 173 b Bö§ 386),

noun [masculine] clearness, lustre (dubious word; below טֹהַר‎ Hup Pe SS; so Thes, reading מִטָּהֳרוֺ‎) — only מִטֳּהָרוֺ הִשְׁבַּתָּ‎ Psa 89:45; De Hup Pe Sch thou hast made (him) to cease from his lustre; ᵑ9 ᵑ6‎ made his lustre to cease, so AE Ki and others reading noun מִטְהָרוֺ‎ or מִטְּהָרוֺ‎ (dagesh forte according to Ges§ 20. 2. 2 b), and SchrSK 1868, 642 who reads טְהָרוֺ הִשְׁבַּתָּה‎; Gr proposes עטרתוֺ‎ his crown; Bae מִיָּדוֺ מַטֶּה‎ sceptre from his hand.
