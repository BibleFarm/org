[Heb] אֹור‎ ETCBC: אֹור‎ (subs｜light) OSHL: a.cd.ab TWOT: 52a GK: H240 Greek: ἀσχημοσύνη, δέρμα, δερμάτινος, ἥλιος, ἡμέρα, μεσημβρία, σῶμα, φέγγος, φῶς, φωτισμός, χρώς
Derivation: from אֹור‎;
Strong's: illumination or (concrete) luminary (in every sense, including lightning, happiness, etc.)
KJV: bright, clear, [phrase] day, light (-ning), morning, sun.

H216. or

אוֺר‎121 noun masculineGen 1:3 (feminineJob 36:32; Jer 13:16) light (Assyrian urru = ûru Dlw). — absolute Gen 1:3 +; construct Judg 16:2 +; suffix אוֺרִי‎ Psa 27:1, etc.; plural אוֺרִים‎ 136:7; —

1 light as diffused in nature, light of day Gen 1:3-4, 5 (P) Job 3:9; 38:19 +.

2 morning light, dawn, הַבֹּקֶר אוֺר‎ light of the morning Judg 16:2; 1Sam 14:36; 25:34, 36; 2Sam 17:22; 2Kin 7:9; Micah 2:1; ׳א בֹּקֶר‎ 2Sam 23:4 (poem of David); ׳עַדהָֿא‎ Judg 19:26 (compare עַדהַֿבֹּקֶר‎ 19:25); לָאוֺר‎ Job 24:14; הַיּוֺם מַחֲצִית עַד מִןהָֿאוֺר‎ from dawn till mid-day, Neh 8:3 compare Prov 4:18.

3 light of the heavenly luminaries; ׳א הַלְּבָנָה‎, || ׳א הַחַמָּה‎ moonlight & sunlight Isa 30:26; אוֺר כּוֺכְבֵי‎ stars of light Psa 148:3; אוֺר מְאוֺרֵי‎ luminaries of light Ezek 32:8; אוֺרִים‎ = מְאוֺרִים‎ Psa 136:7; so עֲלֵיאֿוֺר‎ in sunshine Isa 18:4; the sun itself Job 31:26.

4 daylight רְשָׁעִים אוֺר‎ light of the wicked Job 38:15 (their work-day being the night); אוֺר יוֺם‎ a day of light Amos 8:9 (= a clear, sunshiny day).

5 lightning Job 36:32; 37:3, 11, 15 compare Hab 3:11.

6 light of lamp Prov 13:9; Jer 25:10; of crocodile's hot breath Job 41:10.

7 light of life ׳א חַיִּים‎ Job 33:30; Psalm 56:14; compare אוֺר‎ 3:16, 20.

8 light of prosperity Job 22:28; 30:26; Psa 97:11; Lam 3:2.

9 light of instruction אוֺר וְתוֺרָה מִצְוָה נֵר‎ Prov 6:23 the commandment is a lamp and instruction a light; so the Messian. servant is גּוֺיִם אוֺר‎ Isa 42:6; 49:6; compare ׳א עַמִּים‎ 51:4; the advent of Messiah is shining of great light 9:1 (twice in verse).

10 light of face מָּנִים אוֺר‎ = bright, cheerful face (of men) Job 29:24; betokening king's favour Prov 16:15 (compare ׳א עֵינַיִם‎ Psa 38:11); of God = his shining, enlightening, favouring face 4:7; 44:4; 89:16.

11 Yahweh is ׳א יִשְׂרָאֵל‎ Isa 10:17, as source enlightenment & prosperity; light & salvation Psa 27:1; light to guide Micah 7:8 compare Psa 43:3; everlasting light of Zion, instead of sun & moon Isa 60:19-20, house of Jacob is to walk in his light 2:5.
