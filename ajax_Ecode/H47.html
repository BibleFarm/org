H47. abbir

אַבִּיר‎ adjective mighty, valiant — ׳א‎ Job 34:20 + Isa 10:13 Kt (Qr כַּבִּיר‎); construct id. 1Sam 21:8; plural אַכִּירִים‎ Job 24:22 +; — mighty (always = substantive & in poetry except 1Sam 21:8).

1 men Job 24:22 (= violent) 34:20; 1Sam 21:8 (read ׳א הָרָצִים‎ Gr Dr, compare 22:17; but LagProbebibel הָעֲיָרִים אֹבִיל‎ see Id.BN 45; Klo גִּבּוֺר‎), Lam 1:15; לֵב אַבִּירֵי‎ stout of heart Isa 46:12 (= obstinate) Psa 76:6.

2 angels Psalm Psa 78:25 (compare 103:20).

3 animals; bull, simile of king of Assyrian Isa 10:13 (read כְּאַבִּיר‎ & see Di); elsewhere plural; metaphor for enemies בָשָׁן אַבִּירֵי‎) Psa 22:13 (|| מּרים‎); for princes 68:31; for Edomites Isa 34:7; hence even as sacrificial Psa 50:13 (|| עַתּוּדִים‎); of horses Jer 8:16; 47:3; 50:11. **Judg 5:22; Jer 46:15 (but 46:15 many MSS. ᵐ5‎ Aq Symm Theod ᵑ9‎ many moderns read אַבִּירֶ֑ךָ‎ thy bull, i.e. Apis).

אַבְרָהָם‎ see אַבְרָם‎ below II. אבה‎.
