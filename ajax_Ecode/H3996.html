[Heb] מָבֹוא‎ ETCBC: מָבֹוא‎ (subs｜entrance) OSHL: b.ap.ac TWOT: 212b GK: H4427 Greek: ὁδός
Derivation: from בֹּוא‎; (with or without שֶׁמֶשׁ‎)
Strong's: an entrance (the place or the act); specifically sunset or the west; also (adverb with preposition) towards
KJV: by which came, as cometh, in coming, as men enter into, entering, entrance into, entry, where goeth, going down, [phrase] westward. Compare מֹובָא‎.

מָבוֺא‎ noun masculineJer 38:14 entrance, a coming in, entering; — ׳מ‎ absolute 38:14 3t. + Ezek 42:9 Kt (Qr מֵבִיא‎ wrong, compare Co); construct מְבוֺא‎ Deut 11:30 12t.; suffix מְבוֺאוֺ‎ Psa 104:19 2t.; מְבֹאוֺ‎ 50:1; מבואך‎ 2Sam 3:25 Kt (Qr מוֺבָאֶ֑ךָ‎ inexplicable compare Dr); plural construct מְבוֺאֵי‎ Ezek 26:10, מְבוֺאֹת‎ 27:3; —

1 entrance, i.e. place or way of entrance, into a city Judg 1:24-25, 1Chr 4:39; Prov 8:3 (׳מ מְּתָחִים‎); into buildings 2Kin 11:16; 16:18; 1Chr 9:19 (הַמָּבוֺא שֹׁמְרֵי‎), 2Chr 23:13, 15; Jer 38:14; Ezek 42:9; 46:19; see also יָם מְבוֺאֹת‎ entrance of (the) sea, gate of the sea, of situation of Tyre 27:3.

2 entering, act of entrance, by violence, in storm of city Ezek 26:10; coming in or together, making a crowd, עָ֑ם כִּמְבוֺא‎ 33:31 simile of eagerness to hear Yahweh's word; in phrase וְאֶתמֿבואך אֶתמֿוֺצָאֲךָ‎ 2Sam 3:25 thy going out and thy coming in, mode of entering temple, or the people who enter Ezek 44:5; particularly of sunset הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ מְבוֺא‎ Psa 104:19; = west Deut 11:30; Josh 1:4; 23:4 הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ מְבוֺא הַגָּדוֺל הַיָּם‎ = Mediterranean, Zech 8:7 הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ מְבוֺא אֶרֶץ‎ (|| מִזְרָח אֶרֶץ‎), Mal 1:11; Psa 50:1; 113:3 sunset = west, opposed to sunrise = east; in phrase from east to west, i.e. over the whole earth, everywhere on earth.
