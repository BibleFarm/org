H7904. shakah

[שָׁכָה‎, so most] verb Hiph`il Participle as adjective in הָיוּ מַשְׁכִּים מוּזָנִים סוּסִים‎ Jer 5:8 usually, horses... roaming at large they have become (compare Ethiopic ); — but meaning lustful needed; Aq Theod ἔλκοντες, Jerome trahentes (that is, genitalia), i.e. משְׁכִים‎; read with Arnheim Du Dr מַאֲשָׁכִים‎, i.e. fed stallions (literally growing אֲשָׁכִים‎, Lev 21:20; compare מַפְרִים מַקְרִין,‎).
