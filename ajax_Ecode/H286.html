H286. Achilud

אֲחִילוּד‎ proper name, masculine (according to Thes = יְלוּד אֲחִי‎ child's brother (?))

1 father of Jehoshaphat, David's chronicler 2Sam 8:16; 20:24; 1Kin 4:3; 1Chr 18:15.

2 father of Baana, officer of Solomon 1Kin 4:12.
