H1431. gadal

גָּדַל‎115 verb grow up, become great (Aramaic גְּדַל‎ (Ithpe`el), twist, twine, Arabic twist a cord, make firm, strong, become strong, so Late Hebrew) —

Qal Perfect ׳ג‎ etc. Gen 38:14 14t.; suffix גְּדֵלַנִי‎ Job 31:18; Imperfect יִגְדַּל‎ etc. Psa 35:27 34t.; —

1 grow up,

a. child Gen 21:8, 20; 25:27; 38:11, 14; Exod 2:10-11, (JE) Judg 11:2; 13:24; Ruth 1:12; 1Sam 2:21; 3:19; 1Kin 12:8, 10 ( = 2Chr 10:8, 10) 2Kin 4:18; כְאָב גְּדֵלַנִי‎ he grew up to me as to a father Job 31:18;

b. lamb 2Sam 12:3.

2 become great,

a. in extent, wealthy Gen 26:13 (twice in verse); 41:40 (JE) Jer 5:27;

b. in value. בְּעֵינָ֑י נֶפֶשׁ גָָּֽדְלָה י, ׳בְּעֵינֵי‎ prized by 1Sam 26:24 (twice in verse);

c. in intensity, grief Job 2:13, mourning Zech 12:11, punishment Lam 4:6, trespass Ezek 9:6;

d. in sound, loud cry Gen 19:13 (J);

e. in importance, of a king Eccl 2:9; 1Kin 10:23 ( = 2Chr 9:22) Dan 8:9-10, (under figure of horn), chief Gen 24:35; 48:19 (J), Messiah Micah 5:3, Jerusalem Ezek 16:7;

f. of God 2Sam 7:22; Psa 104:1, his works 92:6, his power Num 14:17 (J).

3 to be magnified,

a. house of David Zech 12:7;

b. Yahweh Psa 35:27; 40:17; 70:5; Mal 1:5, his name 2Sam 7:26 ( = 1Chr 17:24).

Pi`el Perfect גִּדַּל‎ Josh 4:14; Est 3:1; גִּדֵּ֔ל‎ Isa 49:21; גִּדֵּ֑לָה‎ 51:18, etc.; Imperfect יְגַדֵּל‎ 44:14 8t.; Imperative גַּדְּלוּ‎ Psa 34:4; Infinitive גַּדֵּל‎ Num 6:5 3t.; Participle מְגַדְּלִים‎ 2Kin 10:6 (מְגַדְּלוֺת‎ Song 5:15 ᵑ7 ᵐ5‎ Hi Bö De); —

1 cause to grow, e.g. hair Num 6:5 (P), plants Jonah 4:10; Isa 44:14; Ezek 31:4; Song 5:13; bring up children 2Kin 10:6; Isa 1:2; 23:4; 49:21; 51:18; Dan 1:5; Hosea 9:12.

2 make great, powerful Gen 12:2 (J) Josh 3:7; 4:14 (D) 1Kin 1:37, 47; 1Chr 29:12, 25; 2Chr 1:1; Est 3:1; 5:11; 10:2.

3 magnify,

a. man Job 7:17;

b. God Psa 34:4; 69:31.

Pu`al Participle plural מְגֻדָּלִים‎ brought up Psa 144:12.

Hiph`il Perfect הִגְדִּיל‎ Psa 41:10 +, etc.; Imperfect יַגְדִּיל‎ Isa 42:21; Dan 8:25, etc.; Infinitive הַגְדִּיל‎ 1Chr 22:8; Amos 8:5; Participle מַגְדִּיל‎ Psalm 18:51 ( = מִגְדֹּיל‎ in || 2Sam 22:51); plural מַגְדִּילִים‎ Psa 35:26; —

1 make great, e.g. shekel Amos 8:5, pile for fire Ezek 24:9, joy Isa 9:2, counsel 28:29, wisdom Eccl 1:16, works 2:4, house of Yahweh 1Chr 22:5; the heel Psa 41:10 either lifted high (Ges), or (compare De Now) gave me insidiously a great fall; compare ׳ג מִּיךָ‎ Obadiah 12 i.e. utter proud words (see רחב‎ Hiph`il)

2 magnify, salvation Psalm 18:51, so || 2Sam 22:51 (Kt מַגְדִּיל‎; > Qr מִגְדֹּל‎), mercy Gen 19:19 (J), teaching Isa 42:21, the word of Yahweh Psa 138:2.

3 do great things לַעֲשׂוֺת הִגְדִּיל‎,

a. in a good sense, of God Psa 126:2; 126:3; Joel 2:21, also pregnant without Infinitive 1Sam 12:24.

b. in bad sense, of 'the northern one' Joel 2:20, also pregnant without Infinitive Lam 1:9; Zeph 2:8, 10; Dan 8:4, 8, 11, 25; with עַל‎, of enemies Psa 35:26; 38:17; 55:13; Job 19:5; Jer 48:26, 42; Ezek 35:13 of speaking ׳ג בְּפִיכֶם עָלַי‎.

c. also, with Infinitive implied, wept greatly 1Sam 20:41.

Hithpa`el Perfect וְהִתְגַּדִּלְתִּ֫י‎ Ezek 38:23 I will magnify myself, shew myself great and powerful (of God); Imperfect יִתְגַּדֵּל‎, with עַל‎in a bad sense, magnify oneself against Isa 10:15; Dan 11:36; יִתְגַּדָּ֑ל‎ 11:37.
