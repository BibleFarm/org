H402. oklah

אָכְלָה‎17 noun feminine food, eating (with some verbal force, compare DrJPh xi. 217) only P, & Ezekiel; always לְאָכְלָה‎,

1 especially in phrase like לְאָ יִהְיֶה ׳לָכֶם‎ Gen 1:29; 6:21; 9:3; Lev 11:39; so 25:6 לְאָבְלָה לָכֶם הָאָרֶץ שַׁבַּת וְהָיְתָה‎ compare לְא ׳נָתַן‎ Gen 1:30; Exod 16:15.

2 devouring, by wild beasts, only figurative of ravaged people Ezek 29:5; 34:5, 8, 10; 39:4, compare 35:12.

3 consumimg, in fire Ezek 15:4, 6, of fire-sacrifice of children 23:37; figurative of judgment of ׳י‎ Ezekiel 21:37; (compare also infinitive of אָכַל‎).
