H138. Adoniyyahu

אֲדֹנִיָּ֫הוּ (אֲדֹיִּנָה)‎ proper name, masculine (my Lord is Yahweh, compare Phoenician אדנשמש אדנבעל, אדנאשמן,‎ etc., in Assyrian Aduniba' al SchrKB II, 172)

1 fourth son of David 1Kin 1:8 11t., 2:13 5t. (= אֲדֹנִיָּה‎ 2Sam 3:4; 1Kin 1:5, 7, 18; 2:28; 1Chr 3:2).

2 a Levite2Chr 17:8.

3 a chief of the people Neh 10:17 (= אֲדֹנִיקָם‎ 7:18; Ezra 2:13; compare 8:13).
