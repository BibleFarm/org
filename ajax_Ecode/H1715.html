H1715. dagan

דָּגָן‎ noun masculine corn, grain (of cereals) (Late Hebrew id., Samaritan see Thes.; compare Phoenician דגן ארצת‎) — דָּגָן‎ Gen 27:28-29t.; construct דְּגַן‎ Psa 78:24; suffix דְּגָנִי‎ Hosea 2:11, דְּגָָֽנְךָ‎ Deut 7:13; 12:17; 14:23; 18:4, דְּגָנֶ֑ךָ‎ 11:14, דְּגָנֵךְ‎ Isa 62:8, דְּגָנָם‎ Psa 4:8; 65:10 — corn, with תִּירוֺשׁ‎ must, q. v., Gen 27:28, 37 (both J) Psa 4:8; Isa 62:8; Hosea 2:11; 7:14; 9:1; Zech 9:17; compare also Num 18:27 (P; here, for מִןהַֿיָּ֑קֶב מְלֵאָה תירושׁ,‎), Lam 2:12 (where יַיִן‎), Hosea 14:8 (where גֶּפֶן‎ & יֵין לְבָנוֺן‎); ותירושׁ דגן ארץ‎ of land of Canaan Deut 33:28; of land of captives (words of the Rabshak) 2Kin 18:32 = Isa 36:17 (other products, also, named in both); usually with תירושׁ‎ & יצהר‎ Num 18:12; Deut 7:13; 11:14; 12:17; 14:23; 18:4; 28:51; 2Chr 31:5 (+דבשׁ‎) 32:28; Neh 5:11; Nehemiah 10:40; 13:5, 12; Jer 31:12; Hosea 2:10; Hosea 2:24; Joel 1:10; 2:19; Hag 1:11; also Joel 1:17 Me; (compare 2Kin 18:32; Isa 36:17 above); alone, rare and late Neh 5:2-3, 10; Psa 65:10; Ezek 36:29; דְּגַןשָֿׁמַיִם‎ Psa 78:24; — of the above the following refer to firstfruits offered to the priests Num 18:12 (compare 18:27) Deut 18:4; 2Chr 31:5; to tithe Deut 12:17; 14:23; Neh 13:5, 12; to both Generally Nehemiah 10:40; to loaning on usury 5:10-11,.
