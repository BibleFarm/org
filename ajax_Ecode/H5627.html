H5627. sarah

סָרָה‎ noun feminine a turning aside, defection, apostasy, withdrawal; — always absolute ׳ס‎; —

1 defection, used apparently of any moral or legal offence Deut 19:16, so probably Isa 59:13 (+ שָׁ֑קֶר דִּבְרֵי עשֶׁק,‎).

2 apostasy, Deut 13:6; Isa 1:5; 31:6; Jer 28:16; 29:32.

3 בִּלְתִּי סָרָה‎ non-withdrawal Isa 14:6 (compare verb, 14:25; Amos 6:7).

סִרָה‎ see הַסּ ׳בּוֺר‎ above יסור‎, only in יסורי‎ Jer 17:13 Kt, but see Qr סור‎
