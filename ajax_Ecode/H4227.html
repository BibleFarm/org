H4227. machabath

מַחֲבַת‎ noun feminine flat plate, pan, or griddle for baking, only P and late; absolute ׳מ‎ Lev 2:5 2t.; מַחֲבַ֑ת‎ 7:9; construct מַחֲבַת‎ Ezek 4:3; —

1 used in preparing the (baken) mincha, preceded by עַלֿ‎ Lev 2:5; 6:14; 7:9 (all P), compare 1Chr 23:29.

2 ׳מ בַּרְזֶל‎ Ezek 4:3 a plate of iron, signifying an iron wall, in prediction of siege of city.
