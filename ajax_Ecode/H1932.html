הוּא‎ (hûwʼ｜hoo｜personal pronoun｜he)
[Arc] הוּא‎, הִיא‎ ETCBC: הִיא‎ (prps｜she), הוּא‎ (prps｜he) OSHL: xe.af.aa TWOT: 2693 GK: H10200, H10205
Derivation: (Aramaic) or (feminine) הִיא‎; (Aramaic), corresponding to הוּא‎
Strong's: {he (she or it); self, or (especially with the article) the same; sometimes (as demonstrative) this or that; occasionally (instead of copula) as or are}
KJV: [idiom] are, it, this.

H1932. hu

or hi הוּא‎14 masculine הִיא‎7

feminine pronoun of 3 singular he, she, it (= Biblical Hebrew הִיא הוּא,‎; Zinjirli and Palmyrene (once) הא‎, Palmyrene (usually), Nabataean, Egyptian Aramaic הו‎, feminine ׳ה‎, Lzb257, S-CPap. A 1, 12+; A 4, C 9 +. The plural is אִנּוּן‎, q. v.); — he, she, it, Dan 2:21 ֗֗֗ מְהַשְׁנֵא וְהוּא‎ and he will change, etc., 2:22; 2:44; 6:5; 7:7, 24; Ezra 5:8; Dan 2:32 צַלְמָא הוּא‎ that image (nom. pend. ['hanging' noun clause]); 4:19 הוּא אַנְתָּה‎ thou art it. Resuming the subject with emphatic (Biblical Hebrew 2b) 2:47; 6:17; resuming the subject in predication (ib. 3b) 2:20 הִיא דִּילֵֿהּ וּגְבוּרְתָּא חָכְמְתָא‎ it is his, 2:28. Anticipating the subject (ib. 4a) 2:9 דָֽתְכוֺן הִיא חֲדָא‎; with a pronoun (ib. 4b) 2:38 ֗֗֗ הוּא אַנְתְּה‎ thou art the head of gold 5:13; 3:15 ֗֗֗ אֱלָהּ הוּא מָן‎ who is the god . . . ? (so plural אִנּוּן‎ Ezra 5:4); Ezra 4:27 ֗֗֗ הִיא דָא הֲלָא‎ (ib. 4b γ); Ezra 6:27 ֗֗֗ הוּא דִּי‎ who is . . . (ib. 2c), 6:15 (so ֗֗֗ אִנוּן דִּי‎ Dan 7:17). [compare in ᵑ7‎ Exod 14:25; 15:11; 2Sam 20:19; 24:17; Psa 43:2; 63:4; 66:3 +.] Affirming existence (Biblical Hebrew 6b) Dan 4:21. compare Dr §§ 198-201, K§ 87, 3.
