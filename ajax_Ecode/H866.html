H866. ethnah

אֶתְנָה‎ noun feminine hire of a harlot; — אֿ‎ absolute, Hosea 2:14 (in figurative of Israel as adulteress); — on אֶתְנַן‎ (which We Now Marti read here also) see II. תנן‎.

אֶתְנַן‎ noun masculineEzek 16:34 hire of harlot ( = אֶתְנָה‎); — absolute ׳א‎ 16:34, 41, אֶתְנָ֑ן‎ Hosea 9:1 +; construct אֶתְנַן‎ Deut 23:19 +; suffix אֶתְנַנָּהּ‎ Isa 23:18, נַָּ֯הֿ‎ 23:17; plural suffix אֶתְנַנֶּיהָ‎ Micah 1:7a (We proposes אֲשֵׁרֶיהָ‎, compare Now Marti); — harlot's hire, Deut 23:19 (׳א זוֺנָה‎); elsewhere in figure: of idolatrous Israel Hosea 9:1, Jerusalem Ezek 16:31, 34 (twice in verse); 16:41, Tyre Isa 23:17-18, of costly idols of Samaria Micah 1:7 b. 7 c, compare 1:7 a (see above).
