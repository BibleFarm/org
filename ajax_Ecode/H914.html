H914. badal

[בָּדַל‎] verb be divided, separate (Arabic change, substitute, Mishna בָּדַל‎ divide, Syriac Ithpe`el, Ethpe`el) —

Hiph`il divide, separate; Perfect הִבְדִּיל‎ Num 16:9; Deut 10:8; suffix וְהִבְדִּילוֺ‎ consecutive 29:20; 3feminine singular וְהִבְדִּילָה‎ Exod 26:33; וְהִבְדַּלְתָּ‎ Num 8:14; הִבְדִּילוּ‎ Ezek 22:26, etc. Imperfect יַבְדִּיל‎ Lev 1:17 +; וַיַּבְדֵּל‎ Gen 1:4, 7; 1Chr 25:1; suffix יַבְדִּילַנִי‎ Isa 56:3; וָאַבְדִּל‎ Lev 20:26; וָאַבְדִּילָה‎ Ezra 8:24, etc.; Infinitive absolute הַבְדֵּל‎ Isa 56:3; construct לְהַבְדִּיל‎ Gen 1:14 +; Participle מַבְדִּיל‎ 1:6; מַבְדִּילִים‎ Isa 59:2; —

1 divide, separate, subject God Gen 1:4 followed by וּבֵין‎....בֵּין‎ between light and darkness, compare 1:7; subject heavenly bodies 1:14; 1:18; subject firmament followed by בֵּיןלְ֗֗֗‎ 1:6 (all in P's source); Exod 26:33 of the מָּרֹכֶת‎ in tabernacle, followed by לָכֶם בֵּיןוּ֗֗֗בֵין֗֗֗‎; of iniquities, separating men from God לְבֵין ֗֗֗ בֵּין‎ Isa 59:2.

2 separate, set apart, followed by מִן‎, of ׳י‎, setting off Israel from other peoples Lev 20:24 (H) Num 16:9 (P); Lev 20:26 followed by also לִי לִהְיוֺת‎; compare 1Kin 8:53 ׳הִבְדּ לְנַחֲלָה לְךָ‎ separate them to thyself as an inheritance; of Moses, setting apart Levites, followed by מִתּוֺח‎ Num 8:14; of separating an individual from the people, i.e. excluding him Deut 29:20, followed by also לְרָעָה‎; followed by מֵעַל‎ Isa 56:3 (twice in verse), i.e. excluding him; compare also Neh 13:3 followed by מִן‎; of setting apart (forbidding) beasts as unclean לְטַמֵּא לָכֶם‎ Lev 20:25; of setting apart the tribe of Levi וגו ׳לָשֵׂאת‎ Deut 10:8 (subject ׳י‎); compare also (hum. subject) 1Chr 25:1, followed by לַעֲבֹדָה‎, 2Chr 25:10 followed by Infinitive; similarly Ezra 8:24; even followed by participle Ezek 39:14 they shall set apart men עֹבְרִים‎, i.e. to pass through, or, men that shall pass through (RV); so of setting apart cities, accusative Deut 4:41; compare 19:2, 7.

3 make a distinction between clean and unclean, holy and profane, followed by בֵּיןוּ֗֗֗בֵין‎ Lev 20:25 (H); 10:10; 11:47 (both P); followed by בֵּיןלְ֗֗֗‎ Ezek 22:26; 42:20.

4 divide into parts Lev 1:17; 5:8 (P), prohibited in case of fowls offered in sacrifice.

Niph`al Perfect נִבְדְּלוּ‎ 1Chr 12:8; Ezra 9:1; Imperfect יִבָּדֵל‎ 10:8; וַיִּבָּדֵל‎ 1Chr 23:13; וַיִּבָּֽדְלוּ‎ Ezra 10:16; Neh 9:2; Imperative הִבָּֽדְלוּ‎ Num 16:21; Ezra 10:11; Participle נִבְדָּל‎ 6:21; Neh 10:29. 1 (reflexive of Hiph`il 2)

a. separate oneself from people of the land, heathen, and their practices, also from non-Jewish wives, followed by מִן‎ Ezra 6:21; 9:1; 10:11; Neh 9:2; absolute in same sense Ezra 10:16 (compare StaG. ii. 199 & 179 n. WlPharisäer u. Sadd. 76).

b. withdraw from (Moses & Aaron from Israel) Num 16:21 (P), followed by מִתּוֺח‎.

c. separate oneself unto (אֶל‎) David 1Chr 12:8.

d. Neh 10:29 combines a. & c. separate oneself הָאֱלֹהִים אֶלתּֿוֺרַת הָֽאֲרָצוֺת מֵעַמֵּי‎.

2 passive, be separated.

a. be excluded from the people, followed by מִן‎ Ezra 10:8.

b. be set apart, of Aaron, followed by לְהַקְדִּישׁוֺ‎ 1Chr 23:13. — On הבדיל‎, & distinction from הפריד‎ see DrJPhoenician xi. 219.
