H2518. Chilqiyyahu

or Chilqiyyah חִלְקִיָּה חִלְקִיָּהוּ,‎ proper name, masculine (my portion is ׳י‎) ᵐ5‎ usually Ξελκ(ε)ιας, Ξελχιας; —

1 father of Eliakim, Hezekiah's officer, חלקיהו‎ 2Kin 18:18, 26; Isa 22:20; 36:3, 22; חלקיה‎ 2Kin 18:37.

2 high priest, Josiah's time, חלקיהו‎ 2Kin 22:4, 8, 14; 23:4, 24; 2Chr 34:9, 14, 15 (twice in verse); 34:18, 20, 22; חלקיה‎ 2Kin 22:8, 10, 12; 1 Chronicles 5:39 (twice in verse); 1Chr 9:11; 2Chr 35:8; Ezra 7:1, compare also Neh 11:11.

3 חלקיהו‎ father of Jeremiah Jer 1:1. — The following are all ׃חלקיה‎

4 Levites:

a. 1Chr 6:30.

b. 1Chr 26:11.

5 a priest, contemp. of Ezra Neh 4:8; 12:7, 21.

6 father of Gemariah, an ambassador of Zedekiah to Nebuchadrezzar Jer 29:3.
