H1507. Gezer

II. גֶּ֫זֶר‎ proper name, of a location (portion) Levitical city on border of Ephraim Josh 10:33; 12:12; 21:21; 1Kin 9:16; 1Chr 6:52; 7:28; 20:4; גָּ֑זֶר‎ Josh 16:3, 10; Judg 1:29 (twice in verse) compare 2Sam 5:25; 1Kin 9:15, 17; with ָ  ה‎ locative גָּ֑זְרָה‎ 1Chr 14:16; — cuneiform Gazri, BezTell el-Amarna Tabl. BM. 148; modern Tell-Jezer, approximately 18 miles north of west from Jerusalem, south of the Jaffa road; Gann in PEF1873, 78 f.; 1875, 74 f. compare BdPal 17.
