6 ׳בּ י ׳לִפְנֵי‎ of penitent weeping Judg 20:23 (compare Be; see also 21:2) Deut 1:45; 2Kin 22:19; 2Chr 34:27, compare also Num 25:6; joined with fasting Judg 20:26; 2Sam 12:21-22, compare Ezra 10:1; so of weeping in anxious entreaty Hosea 4:5; on participle as proper name Judg 2:1, 5 see בֹּכִים‎ below
