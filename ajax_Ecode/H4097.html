H4097. midrash

[מִדְרָשׁ‎] noun [masculine] study, exposition, midrash, only construct מִדְרַשׁ‎ (late; common in Late Hebrew, in sense of imaginative exposition or didactic story) — הַמְּלָכִים סֵפֶר מִדְרַשׁ‎ midrash of the book of Kings2Chr 24:27; עִדּוֺ הַנָּבִיא מִדְרַשׁ‎ midrash of the prophet Iddo13:22. These were probably of a didactic character, compare DrIntr 497.
