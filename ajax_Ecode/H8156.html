H8156. shasa

[שָׁסַע‎] verb divide, cleave (Late Hebrew Pi`el tear spart; ᵑ7‎Jer Pa`el Lam 3:11 tear in pieces (for HCT מִּשֵּׁחַ‎)); —

Qal Participle (with accusative of congnate meaning with verb שֶׁ֫סַע‎): masculine singular מַּרְסָה שֶׁסַע וְשֹׁסַע‎ Lev 11:7 (P; on ׳שֹׁ‎ compare LagBN 84) cleaving the cleft of the hoof, i.e. having cloven hoof, so feminine singular שֹׁסַ֫עַת‎ 11:3, compare Deut 14:6; שֹׁסַ֫עַת אֵינֶנָּה שֶׁסַע‎ Lev 11:26 (P); passive הַשְּׁסוּעָה הַמַּרְסָה‎ Deut 14:7.

Pi`el tear in two, a lion, kid, Imperfect, Infinitive construct הַגְּדִי כְּשַׁסַּע וַיְשַׁסְּעֵהוּ‎ Judg 14:6; a bird (by, or at, its wings), Perfect3masculine singular consecutive אֹתוֺ וְשִׁסַּע‎ Lev 1:17 (P); imperfect3masculine singular (בַּדְּבָרִים אֶתאֲֿנָשָׁיו‎) וַיְשַׁסַּע‎ 1Sam 24:8 gives too violent a meaning; probably corrupt for word = restrain, or the like Dr HPS, compare Bu Now (hesitantly).

שֶׁ֫סַע‎ noun [masculine] cleft; — as accusative of congnate meaning with verb with [ שָׁסַע‎], q. v.; absolute Lev 11:26, construct 11:3; 11:7; Deut 14:6.
