[Heb] נְחֹשֶׁת‎ ETCBC: נְחֹשֶׁת‎ (subs｜bronze), נְחֹשֶׁת‎ (subs｜menstruation) OSHL: n.cw.ab, n.cx.ab TWOT: 1349a 1350a GK: H5733, H5734 Greek: πέδη, χάλκεος, χαλκός
Derivation: for נְחוּשָׁה‎;
Strong's: copper, hence, something made of that metal, i.e. coin, a fetter; figuratively, base (as compared with gold or silver)
KJV: brasen, brass, chain, copper, fetter (of brass), filthiness, steel.

I. נְח֫שֶׁת‎137 noun masculine1Kin 7:45 (compare AlbrZAW xvi (1896), 108; feminine1 Chronicles 18:8) copper, bronze (Late Hebrew = Biblical Hebrew; Phoenician נחשת‎; Palmyrene נחשא‎ Lzb322; Syriac , נְחָשָׁא ᵑ7‎, Arabic , Ethiopic (so also Old Ethiopic DHMEpigr.Denkm.aus Abess.(1894),52); WMMAs.u.Eur.127 cites Egyptian teµ(µ)ost, copper (= *tenµost), as loan-word from נְחשֶׁת‎ + feminine article); — ׳נ‎ absolute Gen 4:22 +; construct Exod 38:29; suffix נְחֻשְׁתִּי‎ Lam 3:7, נְחֻשְׁתֵךְ‎ Ezek 16:36, נְחֻשְׁתָּהּ‎ 24:11, נְחֻשְׁתָּם‎ 2Kin 25:13 2t.; dual נְחֻשְׁתַּ֫יִם‎ Judg 16:21 6t.; —

1 copper, as ore Deut 8:9, compare Zech 6:8; worked by artificer Gen 4:22 (J) 1Kin 7:14 (twice in verse); 2Chr 24:12; material — doubtless hardened with alloy, probably tin, making bronze, compare NowArchaeology i. 243 BenzArchaeology 214 — of armour 1Sam 17:5-6, 1Kin 14:27, utensils 2Kin 25:14 = Jer 52:18; Lev 6:21; Num 17:4 + very often, altar 2Kin 16:14-15, Exod 38:30; 39:39, etc., and other objects Num 21:9 (twice in verse) (JE), 2Kin 18:4; 1Kin 4:13; 7:15ff. +, especially in description of tabernacle and temple; cast 7:14; polished מְמֹרָט‎ 7:45, מָרוּק‎2Chr 4:16 (compare Lev 6:21); and shining מֻצְהָב‎ Ezra 8:27. On ׳נ קָלָל‎ Ezek 1:7; Dan 10:6 see קלל‎; ׳נ‎ as spoil of war 2Sam 8:8; 2Kin 25:13 = Jer 52:17, 20 etc.

2 fetters of copper or bronze, usually dual Judg 16:21; 2Sam 3:34; 2Kin 25:7 = Jer 39:7; 52:11; 2Chr 36:6, also 33:11; singular only Lam 3:7 (figurative of oppression).

3 as less in value than gold but more than wood Isa 60:17 (twice in verse); figurative of worthless people Jer 6:28 (+ בַּרְזֶל‎), id. (as dross of silver) Ezek 22:18 (+ עוֺפֶרֶת בַּרְזֶל, בְּדִיל,‎). — On 16:36 see IV. נחשׁ‎.

4 figurative of pitiless sky Deut 28:23.
