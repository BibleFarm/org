H890. boshah

בָּאְשָׁה‎ noun feminine (stinking things) stinking or noxious weeds, Job 31:40 בָאְשָׁה שְׂעֹרָה וְתַחַתֿ חוֺחַ יֵצֵא חִטָּה תַּחַת‎ instead of wheat may there spring forth bramble, and instead of barley stinking weeds (compare Assyrian bîšu ZehnpfBAS i. 633).
