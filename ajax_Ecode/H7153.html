H7153. Qiryath

Arba or Qiryath Haarba אַרְבַּע קִרְיַת‎ proper name, of a location older name of Hebron; πόλις Αρβο(κ), Καρ(ι)αθαρβοκ, etc. (probably = fourfold city (otherwise Josh 14:15; 15:13; 21:11), see especially GFM); — 14:15 (J E D), Judg 1:10b (׳ק אַרְבַּ֑ע‎), also Gen 23:2; Josh 15:13, 54; 20:7; 21:11 (all P); ׳ק ׳הָא‎ Gen 35:27 (P) Neh 11:25. — see חֶבְרוֺן‎.
