H8480. Tachath

II. תַּ֫חַת‎ proper name

1. masculine a. Levite 1Chr 6:9; 6:22.

b. Ephraimite 1Chr 7:20 (twice in verse). — Θααθ, Κααθ, etc.

2. location station in wilderness Num 33:26-27, Κατααθ.
