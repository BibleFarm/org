II. גָּלִיל‎ noun masculine cylinder, rod, circuit, district; —

1 cylinder, rod, only plural construct כֶסֶף גְּלִילֵי‎ Est 1:6 of support of rich hangings (|| שֵׁשׁ עַמּוּדֵי‎); & בַּתַּרְשִׁישׁ מְמֻלָּאִים זָהָב גְּלִילֵי‎ Song 5:14, simile of (? fingers of) hands: cylinders of gold set with topaz.

2 circuit, district, on northern border of Israel in Naphtali (see below), population largely heathen (Late Hebrew id. Aramaic גְּלִילָא‎, ) — הַגּוֺיִם גְּלִיל‎ Isaiah 8:23; elsewhere =

proper name, of a location always with article Josh 20:7; 21:32; 1Chr 6:61 all בַּגּ ׳קֶדֶשׁ‎; הַגּ ׳אֶרֶץ‎ 1Kin 9:11, so also ᵐ5‎ Josh 12:23 (compare Di) for גִּלְגַּל‎ q. v.; הַגָּלִ֫ילָה‎ Galilee, 2Kin 15:29 (|| נַפְתָּלִי אֶרֶ֖‎) Ew§ 173 h n Sta§ 308 a 2 Ges§ 90 f.
