H5798. Uzza

or Uzzah עֻזָּא‎ proper name, masculine Οζα, Αζα :

1 driver of the ark 2Sam 6:3 = עֻזָּה‎ 6:6; 6:7; 6:8 = עֻזָּא‎ 1Chr 13:7, 9, 10, 11.

2 in גַּןעֻֿזָּא‎ burial-place of Manasseh and Amon 2Kin 21:18, 26 (׳ע‎ perhaps = עֻזִּיָּה‎, compare StaG i. 569, ii. 679 Kit).

3 Benjamite name 1Chr 8:7.

4 head of family of returned exiles Ezra 2:49 = Neh 7:51.

עֻזָּה‎ proper name, masculine 1. Levite name 1Chr 6:14, Οζ(ι)α, Αζα.

2 see עֻזָּא‎.
