[נָתַח‎ verb only

Pi`el cut up, cut in pieces, divide by joints (Dr1811, 7; Late Hebrew id., Pi`el; rare); —

Pi`el Perfect נִתַּח‎ Lev 8:20, וְנִתַּח‎ consecutive 1:6, 12; Imperfect3masculine singular וַיְנַתַּח‎ 1Kin 18:33, suffix וַיְנַתְּחֵ֫הוּ‎ 1Sam 11:7, וַיְנַתְּחֶ֫הָ‎ Judg 19:29, etc.; — cut up, in pieces, with accusative of animals 1Sam 11:7, of woman Judg 20:6 (all these to be sent about as token for assembling warriors; on significance of the practice compare RSSem i. 383; 2nd ed. 402); elsewhere usually + לִנְתָחִים‎, cut up into pieces, of woman 19:29; of sacrificial animals 1Kin 18:23, 33 (accusative only), Exod 29:17; Lev 1:6, 12; 8:20 (all P).
