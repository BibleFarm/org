H207. Ono

אוֺנוֺ‎ proper name, of a location (vigorous, for אונון‎) city in Benjamin Ezra 2:33; Neh 7:37; 11:35; 1Chr 8:12; valley of same name Neh 6:2; probably Kefr `Anâ, northwest of Lydda, Survey11, 251.
