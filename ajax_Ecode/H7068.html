H7068. qinah

קִנְאָה‎ noun feminine ardour, zeal, jealousy (from colour produced in face by deep emotion); — absolute ׳ק‎ Num 5:14 +; construct קִנְאַת‎ Isa 9:6; suffix קִנְאָתִי‎ Num 25:11 +, etc.; plural קְנָאֹת‎ 5:15, 18, 25, 29; —

1 ardour of jealousy of husband Prov 6:34; 27:4; ק ׳רוּחַ‎ jealous disposition Num 5:14 (twice in verse); 5:30 (P); offering for jealousy, ק ׳מנחת‎ 5:15.18.25 (P); הק ׳תּוֺרַת‎ 5:29 (P); of rivalry Eccl 4:4; 9:6; Ephraim against Judah Isa 11:13; ardent love, || אחכה‎ Song 8:6.

2 ardour of zeal:

a. of men for God Num 25:11 (twice in verse)(P) 2Kin 10:26; for the house of ׳י‎ Psa 69:10.

b. of God for his people, especially in battle Isa 42:13; 63:15; Zech 1:14; 8:2; ק ׳מְעִיל‎ Isa 59:17; ׳ק ׳י וצת תעשׂה‎ 9:6; 37:32 = 2Kin 19:31.

3 ardour of anger:

a. of men against adversaries Psa 119:139; Job 5:2 (|| כעשׂ‎), Prov 14:30 (opposed to מַרְמֵּא לֵב‎).

b. of God against men, || חֵמָה‎ Ezek 5:13; 16:38, 42; 23:25; 36:6; || עֶבְרָה‎ 38:19; || אַף‎ Deut 29:19; Ezek 35:11; + אֵשׁ‎ Isa 26:11; Ezek 36:5; Zeph 1:18; 3:8; Psa 79:5; הַק ׳סֵמֶל הַמַּקְנֶה‎ Ezek 8:3 the anger-image provoking to anger; ׳ס ׳הק‎ alone 8:5.
