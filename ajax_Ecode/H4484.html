H4484. mene

מְנֵא‎ noun [masculine] maneh, mina, a weight (see Biblical Hebrew; Old Aramaic מנה‎ SAC75 Lzb313); — absolute Dan 5:25 (twice in verse); 5:26, see ClGann:JAs Juillet-Août, 1886, 36 ff. NöZA i. 414 f. Dr and others; its connection in Daniel with מְנָה‎ is due to word-play; see also תְּקֵל מְּרֵס,‎.
