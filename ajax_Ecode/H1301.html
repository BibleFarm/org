H1301. Baraq

בָּרָק‎ proper name, masculine (lightning-flash, compare Punic Barcas, surname of Hamilcar, compare NeposHamilcar 1, 1; Sabean ברקם‎ DHMZMG, 1875, 592; Palmyrene ברק‎ VogPalm. 76) son of Abinoam, & leader of Israel Judg 4:6, 8, 9, 10, 12, 14 (twice in verse); 4:15-16, 22; 5:1, 12, 15.

בְּרַק‎ compare ברק בני‎.
