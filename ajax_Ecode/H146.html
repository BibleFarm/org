H146. Addar

אַדָּר‎ proper name, masculine (noble ?).

1 son of Bela, grandson of Benjm. 1Chr 8:3 (perhaps error — compare Be — for אַרְדְּ‎ q. v. Num 26:40; Gen 46:21).

2 city in Judah Josh 15:3 (אַדָּ֫רָה‎); = אַדָּר חֲצַר‎ (q. v.) Num 34:4.
