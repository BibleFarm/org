H1078. Bel

בֵּל‎ proper name, masculine a chief Babylonian deity (Babylonian Bêlu = בַּעַל‎, lord; Bêl regarded as older form than בַּעַל‎ by HptHebraica. i. 178; BAS i. 17) = Merodach (compare מרדך‎), tutelary god of Babylon (to be distinguished from older Bêlu, one of ancient Babylonian triad) Jer 50:2 (|| מְרֹדַךְ‎) 51:44; Isa 46:1 (|| נְבֹו‎) — both writers of Babylonian period; — on Bel see COT Gen 11:4; Judg 2:11; SayRel. Babylonian 103, 110 JenKosmologie 24, 134, 307, 391.
