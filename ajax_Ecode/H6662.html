H6662. tsaddiq

צַדִּיק‎206 adjective just, righteous; — absolute ׳צ‎ Gen 6:9+; plural צַדִּיקִים‎ Exod 23:8+, etc.; —

1 just, righteous, in government:

a. of Davidic king 2Sam 23:3; צֶמַהּ‎ Jer 23:5; Zech 9:9 (|| victorious).

b. of judges, Ezek 23:45; Prov 29:2 (see Toy; Kau questions this meaning in all these).

c. of law, צ ׳מִשְׁמָּטִים‎ Deut 4:8.

d. of God Deut 32:4; Psa 119:137; 129:4; Job 34:17, opposed to Pharaoh Exod 9:27 (J); in discrimination Jer 12:1; Zeph 3:5; Psa 7:10; 7:12; 11:7; condemnation 2Chr 12:6; Dan 9:14; Lam 1:18; Ezra 9:15; Neh 9:33; redemption Isa 45:21; Psa 116:5; keeping promises Neh 9:8; in all his ways Psa 145:17.

2 just in one's cause, right: Exod 23:7-8, (E) Deut 16:19; 25:1; 1Kin 8:32 2Chr 6:23; Isa 5:23; 29:21; Job 32:1; 36:7; Prov 17:15, 26; 18:5, 17; 24:24; Amos 2:6; 5:12; Hab 1:4, 13; right in law, not under penalty 2Sam 4:11; 1Kin 2:32; innocent of specific offence 2Kin 10:9; with מְן‎ compare 1Sam 24:18.

3 just, righteous, in conduct and character:

a. towards God Gen 7:1; 18:23-24, (twice in verse); 18:25 (twice in verse); 18:26, 28 (J) 20:4 (E), Hab 2:4; Mal 3:18.

b. in General, ethically: Psalm 5:13; Psa 7:10; 11:3; 11:5 21t. Psalms (+below), Prov 2:20; 3:33; 4:18; 9:9 56t. Proverbs; Eccl 3:17 7t. Ecclesiastes; Isa 3:10; 57:1 (twice in verse); Jer 20:12; Lam 4:13; Ezek 3:20-21, (twice in verse) + 12 t. Ezekiel; Hosea 14:10; || תמים‎ Gen 6:9 (P) Job 12:4; || נָקִי‎ 17:9; 22:19; 27:17; Psa 94:21; || לֵב יִשְׁרֵי‎ 32:11; Psalm 64:11; 97:11.

4 righteous, as justified and vindicated by ׳י‎, especially servant of ׳י‎ Isa 53:11, so his people, usually plural 60:21; Psa 33:1 8t. Psalms (see also above); צ ׳אָהֳלֵי‎ 118:15 tents of the righteous; צ ׳גּוֺרַל‎ 125:3; צ ׳עֲדִת‎ 1:5; צ ׳דֶּרֶךְ‎ 1:6; singular collective 34:20, 22; Psalm 75:11; Isa 24:16; 26:7 (twice in verse); צַדִּיק גּוֺי‎ 26:2 (|| אֱמֻנִים שֹׁמֵר‎).

5 right, correct, Isa 41:26 (compare אֱמֶת‎ 43:9); lawful צ ׳שְׁבִי‎ 49:24 EV; but read עָרִיץ‎.
