עַתִּיק‎ adjective advanced, aged (Biblical Hebrew (as Aramaism), √עתק‎, q. v.); — construct (מַיָּא‎-) ׳ע יוֺמִין‎ one advanced, aged, in days Dan 7:9, 13, 22 (compare Syriac Ecclus 25:4 and elsewhere, PS3011).
