H5058. neginah

or neginath [נְגִינָה‎] noun feminine music; — absolute נְגִינַת‎ Psa 61:1 (see below); suffix נְגִינָתִי‎ 77:7; נְגִינָתָם‎ Job 30:9 2t.; plural נְגִינוֺת‎ Psa 4:1 6t.; נְגִינָתַי‎ Isa 38:20; תָ֑י‎- Hab 3:19; —

1 music (of stringed instrument) accusative of congnate meaning with verb Isa 38:20; as occupation Lam 5:14; especially בִּנְגִינוֺת‎ in Psalm-titles; Psa 4:1; 6:1; 54:1; 55:1; 67:1; 76:1; so Hab 3:19; compare עַלנְֿגִינַת‎ Psa 61:1 (read perhaps plural Ges§ 80f.).

2 song (with string accompare?) Psa 77:7; subject of mocking song Job 30:9 (|| מִלָּה‎), Psa 69:13 (׳נ שֵׁכָר שׁוֺתֵי‎), Lam 3:14 (|| שְׂחֹק‎).
