שָׁם‎ (shâm｜shawm｜adverb｜there)
[Heb] שָׁם‎ ETCBC: שָׁם‎ (advb｜there) OSHL: v.dv.aa TWOT: 2404 GK: H9004 Greek: ἐκεῖ, ἐκεῖθεν, ἐκεῖσε
Derivation: a primitive particle (rather from the relative pronoun, אֲשֶׁר‎);
Strong's: there (transferring to time) then; often thither, or thence
KJV: in it, [phrase] thence, there (-in, [phrase] of, [phrase] out), [phrase] thither, [phrase] whither.

H8033. sham

שָׁם‎ adverb there, thither (Arabic there, compare then; Biblical Aramaic תַּמָּה‎, Egyptian Aramaic תמה‎ S-CPap. A 4, J 6, תַּמָּן ᵑ7‎ (with demonstrative n, compare אֱדַיִן‎ beside אֲזַי‎), Syriac ; ZenjHad. 8 (Cooke159) שם‎); —

1. a. there Gen 2:8, 12; 11:2, 7, 31 + often; placed early in sentence for emphasis, Micah 4:10 תִּנָּצֵ֑לִי שָׁם‎, Nahum 3:15; Ezek 32:22, 24; Psa 104:26 +, ֗֗֗ וְשָׁם‎ Gen 41:12; Exod 15:27; Num 13:22, 33 +, וְשָׁם ֗֗֗ שָׁם‎ Exod 15:25; Deut 12:14; Isa 27:10; Job 3:17 +, ֗֗֗ שָׁם וְהִנֵּה‎ Gen 29:2, ֗֗֗ שָׁם כִּי‎ 11:9; 21:31; 35:7; 2Sam 1:21; 15:21 +, ֗֗֗ שָׁם אַךְ‎ Isa 34:14-15, ֗֗֗ שָׁם גַּם‎ 23:12; 57:7; Psa 139:10; resuming a preceding 'where,' Job 39:30 הוּא שָׁם חֲלָלִים בַּאֲשֶׁר‎, Judg 5:27; Jer 22:12; Eccl 11:3, compare 1:7. Repeated, שָׁם ֗֗֗ שָׁם‎ here ... there Isa 28:10, 13. In poetry, pointing to a spot in which a scene is localized vividly in the imagination, Psa 14:5 פחד פחדו שׁם‎ there feared they a fear! Psalm 36:13, compare 48:7; 66:6; 132:17; Hosea 6:7; Zeph 1:14. — Hab 3:4 read probably (We Now) שָׂם‎ the flashes at his side made he the hiding of his power; Eccl 3:17 שָׁם‎ = with God, in the Divine plan or scheme: but read perhaps שָׂם‎ Hi De and others

b. preceded by the rel., שָׁם אֲשֶׁר‎ where, Gen 2:11; Exod 20:21; 1Sam 9:10 +, usually with one or more words between, Gen 13:3, 14; 19:27; Exod 29:42; 2Sam 11:16; 2Kin 23:27 + often; after שָׁם הוּא בַּאֲשֶׁר בַּאֲשֶׁר,‎ Gen 21:17. With אשׁר‎ omitted (the relative being indicated by construct, Ges§ 130 c, d) Ezek 39:11 ׳בְּיִשְׂר קֶבֶר שָׁם מְקוֺם‎ a place where a grave (may be) in Israel, but ᵑ7 ᵐ5‎ Co Or and others read שֵׁם‎ a place of renown.

2 thither, after verbs of motion ( = שָׁ֫מָּה‎, see

3), Deut 1:37 שָׁם תָבאֹ לֹא‎, Judg 19:15; 21:10; 1Sam 2:14; 9:6 and elsewhere; שָׁם ֗֗֗ אֲשֶׁר‎ whither, 1Kin 18:10; 21:18; 2Kin 1:4, 6, 16; Isa 20:6, more often in Jeremiah, Ezekiel, as Jer 8:3; 22:27; 23:3, 8; 45:5 +, Ezek 4:13; 12:16; 36:20, 22 +; so שֶׁשָּׁם‎ Psa 122:4.

3 with ה‎ locative, שָׁ֫מָּה‎ (sh¹mm¹h):

a. after verbs of motion, thither, Gen 19:20 שָׁ֫מָּה לָנוּס‎ to flee thither, 19:22; 20:13; 24:6; 29:3; Deut 1:38 + often; = into it, therein (of a basket, pit, etc.), Exod 16:33 הָעֹמֶר מְלֹא וְתֶןשָֿׁ֫מָּה‎, 21:33 שָׁמָּה וְנָפַל‎, 30:18; Judg 8:25; 2Kin 12:10; pregnantly, after a verb implying motion, as to bury, Gen 23:13 שָׁ֫מָּה אֶתמֵֿתִי וְאֶקְבְּרָה‎, 25:10; 49:31; 50:5 (all P), נוֺעַד‎ to meet Exod 29:42-43, 30:6, 36; Numbers 17:19 (all P), Josh 2:16; 7:3; Jer 13:7; Joel 4:7, perhaps Song 8:5 (? c).

b. שָׁ֫מָּה ֗֗֗ אֲשֶׁר‎ whither Gen 20:13; Num 33:54; Deut 4:5, 14 + often; where 2Kin 23:8; 1Chr 4:41.

c. more rarely, i. q. שָׁם‎ there, Gen 43:30 שָׁ֫מָּה וַיֵּבְךְ‎, Josh 2:1; Judg 16:27; 2Kin 4:11; 9:16; Hosea 2:17; Isa 22:18; 34:15; 65:9; Jer 18:2; 27:22; Ezek 23:3; 32:29-30, (32:22; 32:24; 32:26 שָׁם‎), 48:35 ׳י שָׁ֫מָּה‎, Psa 76:4; 122:5; Eccl 3:16.

4 מִשָּׁם‎ from there, thence:

a. often after verbs of departing, taking, etc., Gen 2:10; 11:8-9, 12:8; 18:16; 24:7; 42:2, etc., Num 13:23; 21:12; 22:41 מִשָּׁם וַיַּרְא‎, Deut 4:29 מִשָּׁם וּבִקַּשְׁתֶּם‎, 30:4; Jer 37:12; Hosea 2:17; Amos 9:2-3, 4; Job 39:29 +. Peculiarly, Gen 49:24 יִשְׂר אֶבֶן רֹעֶה ׳מִשָּׁם‎ Ges De RVm from there [from heaven], (from) the Shepherd (׳י‎), the Stone (Rock) of Israel, Ew Di Sta from there (where is) the Shepherd of (רֹעֵה‎) the (Bethel-)stone of Israel; but both forced: text dubious; Gunk by the name (מִשֵּׁם‎; so ᵑ6‎) of the Shepherd, etc. (as Ew Di).

b. מִשָּׁם ֗֗֗ אֲשֶׁר‎ whence Gen 24:5; Num 23:13 +.

c. expressing origin, Gen 3:23 the ground מִשָּׁם לֻקַּח אֲשֶׁר‎, 10:14 whence (= from whom) came forth the Philistines, Judg 19:18 אָנֹכִי מִשָּׁם‎, 1Sam 10:12 מִשָּׁם אִישׁ‎, Isa 65:20.

d. of the mass, from which something is taken or made, Gen 27:9; 30:32 (of צאֹן‎), Lev 2:2 זגו מִשָּׁם ׳וְקָמַץ‎ (from the meal, etc., of a מנחה‎), 1Kin 17:13 קְטַנָּה עֻגָה מִשָּׁם לִי עֲשִׁי אַךְ‎ i.e. from the meal and oil, 2Kin 7:2, 19 תאֵֹכל׃ לֹא וּמִשָּׁם‎, Ezek 5:3.
