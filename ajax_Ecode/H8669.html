H8669. teshuqah

[תְּשׁוּקָה‎] noun feminine longing; — of woman for man, תְּשׁוּקָתֵח אֶלאִֿישֵׁח‎ Gen 3:16 (J); of man for woman, תְּשׁוּקָתוֺ וְאֵלַי לְדוֺדִי אֲנִי‎ Cant 7:11; of beast to devour, figurative תְּשׁוּקָתוֺ אֵלֶיךָ‎ 4:7 (J). (ᵐ5‎ ἀποστροφή Genesis, ἐπιστροφή Canticles, whence NesMarg. 6 proposes תְּשׁוּבָתֵךְ‎ 3:16, which BallHpt reads in all; but how explain the unusual and striking word in MT?).
