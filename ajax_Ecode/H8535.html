H8535. tam

תָּם‎ adjective complete — ׳ת‎ Gen 25:27 10t.; suffix תַּמָּתִי‎ Song 5:2; 6:9 (late, except Gen 25:27); —

1 complete, perfect: in beauty Song 5:2; 6:9; of physical strength Psa 73:4 (read תָּם לָ֑מוֺ‎ Ew De DrSam. xxxi Che Bae and others).

2 sound, wholesome: תָּם אישׁ‎, of Jacob as tent-dweller, opposed to Esau, hunter, Gen 25:27 (J; De leutseliger Mann, Di ruhig, still, AV RV plain man; ׳ת‎ here possibly redactional, = 3).

3 complete, morally innocent, having integrity: תָּם אִישׁ‎ Job 1:8; 2:3; ׳ת‎ alone 1:1; 8:20; 9:20-21, 22; Prov 29:10; Psa 37:37; 64:5. — תַּמִּים‎ see [תָּאם‎]. p. 1060f
