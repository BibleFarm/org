H160. ahabah

אַהֲבָה‎ noun feminine love (= Infinitive of אהב‎ q. v.) — absolute ׳א‎ Prov 10:12 18t.; construct אַהֲבַת‎ Jer 2:2-3t.; suffix אַהֲבָתִי‎ Psa 109:4; 109:5; אַהֲבָֽתְךָ‎ 2Sam 1:26; אַהֲבָתוֺ‎ Isa 63:9; Zeph 3:17; אַהֲבָתָהּ‎ Prov 5:19; אַהֲבָתָם‎ Eccl 9:6 — love, especially Wisdom Literature & late.

1 human (to human object) absolute Eccl 9:1, 6 (both || שִׂנְאָה‎) so Prov 10:12; 15:17; compare 27:5; see also 17:9; of man toward man Psa 109:4; 109:5; love for one's self (נַפְשׁוֺ‎) 1Sam 20:17; between man & woman Song 2:4-5, 5:8; 8:6-7, (twice in verse); Prov 5:19 compare also 2Sam 1:26 (׳א נָשִׁים‎); personified Song 2:7; 3:5; 7:7; 8:4; compare figurative use 3:10; of mere sexual desire 2Sam 13:15; figurative of Jerusalem's love to ׳י‎ Jer 2:2 (׳א כְּלוּלֹתַיִח‎), & of love of adulter. Jesus. 2:33.

2 God's love to his people Hosea 11:4 (א ׳עֲבֹתוֺת‎) Jer 31:3; Isa 63:9; Zeph 3:17.
