H2332. Chavvah

I. חַוָּה‎ proper name, feminine (life, see Di Gen 3:20; > WeProl. 1886, 322; Skizzen iii. 217 after Nö who suggests serpent as possible meaning, compare Arabic ; but RSK 177 'mother of every µayy,' see II. חַי‎ below חיה‎) — 3:20 and the man called his wife חַוָּ֑ה‎, because she was כָּלחָֽֿי׃ אֵם‎; compare ׳ח אִשְׁתּוֺ‎ 4:1 (both J). — II. חַוָּה‎ village, see below below II. חוה‎.
