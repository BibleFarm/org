H222. Uriel

אוּרִיאֵל‎ proper name, masculine (flame of El or my light is El, see אֵל‎ below אלה‎; compare Phoenician ארמלך‎ in Assyrian Urumilki (SenTaylor Cylind.ii.50, compare COT2Kin 18:13))

1 chief of Levitical line of Kohath, in time of David 1Chr 6:9; 15:5, 11.

2 maternal grandfather of Abijah2Chr 13:2.
