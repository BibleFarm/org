1 rather seld. (royal) palace (so almost always in Assyrian); of Ahab 1Kin 21:1, of king of Babylon 2Kin 20:18 = Isa 38:17; 2Chr 36:7, compare Dan 1:4, and plural עֹנֶג הֵיכְלֵי‎ Isa 13:22 luxurious palaces; at Nineveh ׳וְהַה נָמוֺג‎ Nahum 2:7; not clearly defined are מֶלֶךְ הֵיכַל‎ Psa 45:16 and שֵׁן הֵיכְלֵי‎ 45:9; הֵיכָלוֺת‎ Hosea 8:14 (in Northern Israel); see also הַהוּא בַּיּוֺם הֵיכָל שִׁירוֺת וְהֵילִילוּ‎ Amos 8:3 and palace-songs shall be howlings in that day; compare also (probably) of Phoenician palaces Joel 4:5; quite General מֶלֶךְ הֵיכְלֵי‎, Prov 30:28, & הֵיכָל תַּבְנִית‎ Psa 144:12 (simile of well-proportioned daughters); — usual term for royal palace in Hebrew is הַמֶּלֶךְ בֵּית‎, see בית‎.

2 of palace of God considered as king, = house of God or of ׳י‎, temple (compare ׳הַה‎ Isa 6:1 e. below where prophet sees עַלכִּֿסֵּא ישֵׁב אדני‎):

a. of early, pre-Solomonic house at Shiloh י ׳הֵיכַל‎ 1Sam 1:9; 3:3 (= י ׳בֵּית‎ 1:7; 3:15; מוֺעֵד אֹהֶל מֶּתַח‎ 2:22 omitted by ᵐ5‎ We Klo Dr) with doors (דלתות‎, 3:15) & a doorpost (מזוזה‎ 1:9) (compare 2Sam 22:7 = Psa 18:7 e below).

b. of Solomon's temple: specifically the hall or nave of the temple (the holy place, distinguished from the דְּבִּיר‎ the inner sanctuary, the Holy of Holies, and with this included in the more General term י ׳בֵּית‎ or הַבַּיִת‎, 1Kin 6:1-2,+ often) 6:17 (compare 6:19; 6:20 & 6:2) also 6:5; 6:33; 7:50 2Chr 4:22; further 4:7-8, Ezek 8:16 (twice in verse) הַבַּיִת הֵיכַל עַלמְּֿנֵי הָאוּלָם‎ 1Kin 6:3; הַהֵיכָל אֻלָם‎ 7:21 || הַהֵיכָל עַלמְּֿנֵי‎2Chr 3:17; י ׳הֵיכַל‎ 2Kin 18:16; also in wide sense (apparently = י ׳בֵּית‎), י ׳הֵיכַל‎ 23:4; 24:13 (|| י ׳בֵּית‎), Jer 7:4 (3 t. in verse) (|| י ׳בֵּית‎ 7:2), 24:1 see also הֵיכָלוֺ נִקְמַת‎ 50:28; 51:11 (both || י ׳נִקְמַת‎); 2Chr 26:16; 27:2, compare 29:16 (|| י בֵית ׳מְּנִ֫ימָה‎; opposed to ב ׳חֲצַר ׳י‎), in all three י ׳הֵיכַל‎.

c. hall or nave of Ezekiel's temple, the holy place (see above) (included in הַבַּיִת‎, the General term embracing the whole group of buildings) Ezek 41:1, 4, 15, 21, 23, 25 (compare 41:20) 42:8.

d. general designation of 2 nd temple, הַהֵיכָל‎ Zech 8:9 (|| י ׳בֵּית צְבָאוֺת‎); oftener י ׳הֵיכַל‎ Hag 2:15, 18; Zech 6:12-13, 14, 15; Ezra 3:6, 10, הֵיכָלוֺ‎ Mal 3:1; Psa 27:4; יִשְׂרָאֵל אֱלֹהֵי ליהוה הֵיכָל בּוֺנִים‎ Ezra 4:1; (הַ)הֵיכָל‎ Neh 6:10 (twice in verse) (|| הָאֱלֹהִים בֵּית‎), 6:11; Isa 44:28, compare also 66:6. — הֵיכָל‎ occurs occasionally in Psalms, without conclusive evidence, in all cases, to what temple reference is had; apparently distinguished from י ׳בֵּית‎ (as below b) Psa 5:8 (קָדְשְׁךָ הֵיכַל‎; see בֵּיתֶ֑ךָ‎ 5:7); perhaps also 68:30 (distinguished from Holy of Holies by De Che; yet verse obscure); but no distinction evident 79:1; 138:2; Jonah 2:5; 2:8 (all קָדְשְׁךָ הֵיכַל‎); compare הֵיכָל֑ךָ קְדשׁ‎ Psa 65:5 (|| בֵּיתֶ֑ךָ‎); as containing the worshipping assembly 48:10.

e. of the heavenly temple where ׳י‎ sits enthroned Isa 6:1; his abode in the heavens 2Sam 22:7 = Psa 18:7; 11:4 (|| שָׁמַיִם‎; compare קָדְשׁוֺ זְבֻל‎ Isa 63:15, || id.), 29:9; קָדְשׁוֺ מֵהֵיכַל אֲדֹנָי‎ Micah 1:2, compare Hab 2:20 קָדְשׁוֺ בְּהֵיכַל ויהוה‎.
