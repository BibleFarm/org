תָּלָה‎ (tâlâh｜taw-law'｜verb｜hang)
[Heb] תָּלָה‎ ETCBC: תלה‎ (verb｜hang) OSHL: w.as.aa TWOT: 2512 GK: H9434 Greek: κρεμάννυμι, σταυρόω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to suspend (especially to gibbet)
KJV: hang (up).

H8518. talah

תָּלָה‎ verb hang (Late Hebrew id.; Assyrian Pi`el tullû, hang up; Arabic let down, dangle (a rope, etc.); Aramaic תְּלָא‎, hang; perhaps Old Aramaic תלה‎ id., Lzb386; Ethiopic in derived sense, be devoted, addicted, to); —

Qal Perfect3masculine singular ׳ת‎ Gen 40:22 +; 2 masculine singular וְתָלִ֫יתָ‎ Deut 21:22 consecutive; 3 plural תָּלוּ‎ Est 8:7; 9:14 suffix תלום‎ 2Sam 21:12 Kt (Qr see תלא‎), etc.; Imperfect3masculine singular suffix וַיִּתְלֵם‎ Josh 10:26, etc.; Imperative masculine plural suffix תְּלֻהוּ‎ Est 7:9; Infinitive construct לִתְלוֺת‎ Ezek 15:3; Est 6:4; Participle active תֹּלֶה‎ Job 26:7; passive תָּלוּי‎ Deut 31:23 2t., plural תְּלוּיִם‎ Josh 10:26; — hang:

1 hang up any object: accusative of hands and feet of slain, + עַלהַֿבְּרֵכָה‎ 2Sam 4:12; תָּלוּי‎ of Absalom בָּאֵלָה‎ in the terebinth 18:10; ׳ת‎ with accusative of thing, + עַל‎ Isa 22:24; Ezek 15:3; Song 4:4; Psa 137:2; subject ׳ת עַלבְּֿלִימָה אֶרֶץ ׳י,‎ Job 26:7.

2 specifically put to death by hanging, with accusative of person Gen 40:22; 41:13 (both E), 2Sam 21:12 (compare תלא‎), Est 9:14; absolute תָּלוּי‎ Deut 21:23 one hanged; accusative of person + עַלעֵֿץ‎ Gen 40:19 (E), Deut 21:22; Josh 8:29; 10:26 (twice in verse) (all J E), Est 5:14; 6:4; 7:9-10, 8:7; 9:13, 25.

Niph`al Perfect3plural נִתְלוּ בְּיָדָם שָׂרִים‎ Lam 5:12 princes have been hung up by their hands; Imperfect3masculine plural עַלעֵֿץ וַיִּתָּלוּ‎ Est 2:23 they were hanged on a tree; + 3 masculine singular with apocope וַיִּתָּל‎ 2Sam 18:9 (for וַיּתַֻּן‎ see נתן‎ Hoph`al).

Pi`el hang up for display, with accusative of thing: Perfect3plural תִּלּוּ‎ Ezek 27:10 (ב‎ location), 27:11 (עַלאחוֺמוֺתַיִךְ‎).
