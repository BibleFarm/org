H5787. ivver

עִוֵּר‎ adjective blind; — only absolute ׳ע‎ Exod 4:11 +, plural עִיְרִים‎ 2Sam 5:6 +, feminine עִוְרוֺת‎ Isa 42:7; (with article הָעִוֵּר‎ Deut 28:29, elsewhere ׳הַע‎ Baer Ginsb; van d. H. לָעִוֵּר‎ Job 29:15, הָעִוְרִים‎ 2Sam 5:6, 8; Isa 42:18); — blind, usually as substantive = the blind:

1 literally, physically blind:

a. of men Exod 4:11 (J; opposed to מִּקֵּחַ‎), 2Sam 5:6, 8 (twice in verse); Lev 19:14 (H), Deut 27:18; Jer 31:8; Job 29:15; in simile Deut 28:29; Zeph 1:17; Isa 59:10, compare Lam 4:14 (apposition); as adjective attribute ע ׳אִישׁ‎ Lev 21:18 (H).

b. of sacrifice animal Deut 15:21; Mal 1:8. 2 figurative,

a. of the helpless, groping Isa 29:18; 35:5; 42:16; Psa 146:8; as adjective attribute, עִוְרוֺת עֵינַיִם‎ Isa 42:7.

b. of the dull, unreceptive, Isa 42:18-19, (3 t. in verse) (but 42:19 c read חֵרֵשׁ‎ q. v.); as adjective attributive עִוֵּר עַם‎ 43:8; as predicate 56:10.
