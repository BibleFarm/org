H5988. Ammiel

עַמִּיאֵל‎ proper name, masculine (my kinsman is God, GrayProp. N. 254); — ᵐ5‎ Αμος(με)ιηλ:

1 east Jordan name 2Sam 9:4-5, 17:27.

2 a Danite Num 13:12 (P).

3 David's father-in-law 1Chr 3:5 (= אֱלִיעָם‎ 2Sam 11:3).

4 son of Obed Edom 1Chr 26:5.
