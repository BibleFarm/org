מָקֹור‎ (mâqôwr｜maw-kore'｜noun｜masculine｜spring)
[Heb] מָקֹור‎ ETCBC: מָקֹור‎ (subs｜well) OSHL: s.az.ab TWOT: 2004a GK: H5227 Greek: ἔξοδος, πηγή, ῥύσις, ὕδωρ
Derivation: or מָקֹר‎; from קוּר‎;
Strong's: properly, something dug, i.e. a (general) source (of water, even when naturally flowing; also of tears, blood (by euphemism, of the female pudenda); figuratively, of happiness, wisdom, progeny)
KJV: fountain, issue, spring, well(-spring).
