[Heb] חֶרֶס‎, חַרְסָה‎ ETCBC: חֶרֶס‎ (subs｜scabies), חֶרֶס‎ (subs｜sun) OSHL: h.gj.ad, h.fx.ab TWOT: 759b 748a GK: H3063, H3064, H3066
Derivation: or (with a directive enclitic) חַרְסָה‎; from an unused root meaning to scrape; also perhaps from the mediating idea of חַרְסוּת‎
