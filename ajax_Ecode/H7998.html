H7998. shalal

שָׁלָל‎75 noun masculine1Sam 30:16 prey, spoil, plunder, booty; — absolute ׳שׁ‎ Gen 49:27 +; construct שְׁלַל‎ Judg 5:30 +, suffix שְׁלָלֵךְ‎ Zech 14:1, שְׁלַלְכֶם‎ Isa 33:4, etc.; —

1 prey, of Benjamin as wolf Gen 49:27 (poem in J; with חִלֵּק‎).

2 booty, spoil of war, of all kinds (often || בַּז‎): including garments, gold and silver Josh 7:21 (J), ornaments Judg 8:24-25, fabrics and women 5:30 a, b, flocks and herds 1Sam 30:20; Jer 49:32; 2Chr 15:11, + persons Deut 20:14 (twice in verse), garments, food and drink 2Chr 28:15, etc. (58 t.; among these) הֶעָרִים שְׁלַל‎ Deut 2:25; 2Sam 12:30; 1Chr 20:2 and (+ cattle in addition) Deut 3:7; Josh 8:27; 11:14 (both RD), compare (also of city) Isa 8:4; Deut 13:17 (twice in verse); Zech 14:1 and (+ cattle) Josh 8:2 (RD), but including person and cattle Deut 20:14, compare 20:14; figurative of entire nation Jer 50:10; Ezek 7:21; Zech 2:13; ׳שׁ‎ in symbolic proper name וגו שָׁלָל ׳מַהֵר‎ Isa 8:1, 3, see above p.555:a; ׳שׁ‎ in metaphor of future majesty of י ׳עֶבֶד‎ 53:12; לְשׁ נַפְשׁוֺ ׳וְהֳיְתָהאלּוֺ‎ i.e. life shall be spared, Jer 21:9; 38:2; 39:18, compare 45:5.

3 private plunder Isa 10:2 (שְׁלָלָם אַלְמָנוֺת‎), Prov 1:13 and (perhaps in current saying) 16:19.

4 = gain Prov 31:11. — שׁ ׳לְצַוִּארֵי‎ Judg 5:30 c is clearly wrong; meaning dubious (see especially GFM); plausible conjectures are שׁ ׳לְצַוָּארַי‎ for my neck as spoil, ReussBr and others, and שֵׁגָל לְצַוְּארֵי‎ for the neck of the queen Ew Be Kit and others (but שֵׁגָל‎ elsewhere late); Now strike out שָׁלָל‎ and reads לְצַוָּארָיו‎.
