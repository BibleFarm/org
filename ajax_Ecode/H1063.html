H1063. bikkurah

בִּכּוּרָה‎ noun feminine first ripe fig, early fig (regarded as a delicacy) (Löw391; compare Arabic , Spanish albacora, Moorish bokkôre) Micah 7:1; Hosea 9:10; suffix בִּכּוּרָהּ‎ = בִּכּוּרָתָהּ‎ (read בִּכּוּרָה‎ Di), Isa 28:4; plural בַּכֻּרוֺת‎ Jer 24:2 **probably a different word, plural abstract = early ripeness, compare Du.
