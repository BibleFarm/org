H5160. Nachaliel

נַחֲלִיאֵל‎ proper name, of a location (= valley — or plam — of El); — station of Israel east of Dead Sea Num 21:19 (twice in verse) (JE), possibly (if = valley) one of main tributaries of Arnon, e.g. W. Wâle (see BlissPEF 1895, 204, 215).
