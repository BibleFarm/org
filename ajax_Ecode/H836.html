H836. Asher

אָשֵׁר‎ proper name, masculine Asher (happy one, Felix, compare Phoenician אשרשלח‎, which however may contain (god) Asshur or Osiris, compare BaeRel 161).

1 son of Jacob and Zilpah Gen 30:13; 35:26; 46:17; Exod 1:4; Num 26:46; 1Chr 2:2.

2 the tribe Gen 49:20; Num 1:13; Deut 27:13; 33:24 (twice in verse); Josh 17:10-11, 19:34; Judg 1:31; 5:17; 6:36; 7:23; 1Kin 4:16; 1Chr 12:36; 2Chr 30:11; Ezek 48:2-3, 34; אָשֵׁר בְּנֵי‎ Num 1:40; 2:27; 7:72; 10:26; 26:44, 47; 34:27; Josh 19:24, 31; 1Chr 7:30, 40; אָשֵׁר מַטֵּה‎ Num 1:41; 2:27; 13:13; Josh 21:6, 30; 1Chr 6:47; 6:59.

3. proper name, of a location city east of Shechem Josh 17:7.
