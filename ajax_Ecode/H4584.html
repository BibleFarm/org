H4584. Maon

II. מָעוֺן‎ proper name

1. location in Judah Josh 15:55 (P), 1Sam 25:2 (ᵐ5‎ Μααν, Μαων), now Ma`în (BuhlGeogr. 163 and references), 8 miles south of Hebron; hence מ ׳מִדְבַּר‎ 23:24-25, (twice in verse), so read also 25:1 (for פארן ᵑ0‎) ᵐ5‎ Th We Dr Bu Klo Kit Löhr; compare

2. masculine in Judah 1Chr 2:45 (twice in verse), ᵐ5‎ Μεων, Μαων.

3. of a people Judg 10:12 named with Sidonians and Amalek as ancient foes of Israel; possibly intended by writer as = מְעוּנִים‎ q. v.; many read מִדְיָן‎ (ᵐ5‎B A ᵐ5‎L Μαδιαμ); see GFM. — מְעוֺן‎ see מְעוֺן בַּעַל בֵּית‎; 1Chr 4:41 see מְעוּנִים‎.
