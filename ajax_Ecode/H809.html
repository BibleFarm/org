H809. ashishah

אֲשִׁישָׁה‎ noun feminine (pressed) raisin-cake — ׳א‎ 2Sam 6:19; 1Chr 16:3 distributed, with other viands, to people; especially as stimulating, plural אֲשִׁישׁוֺת‎ Song 2:5; more explicitly עֲנָבִים אֲשִׁישֵׁי‎ Hosea 3:1 used in sacrificial feasts (compare RSOTJC Lect. xi. n. 7); חֲרֶשֶׂת קִיר אֲשִׁישֵׁי‎ Isa 16:7, i.e. the raisin-cakes which were an article of trade at Qir-hareseth (taken by Thes and others = foundations, i.e. foundations exposed by ruin, from [אָשִׁישׁ‎] with such a meaning).

אֵ֫שֶׁת‎ see אִשָּׁה‎ below III. אנשׁ‎.
