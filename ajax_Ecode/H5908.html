H5908. akkabish

עַכָּבִישׁ‎ noun masculine spider (עַכָּבִיתָא עַכּוּבִיתָא, ᵑ7‎; whence Arabic as loan-word; Late Hebrew עַכְשׁוּב‎ probably tarantula, according to LevyNHWB iii.648); — ע ׳בֵּית‎ Job 8:14 i.e. web, so probably 27:18 (reading ׳ע‎ for עָשׁ ᵑ0‎); compare ע ׳קוּרֵי‎ Isa 59:5 filaments of a spider.
