קֶדֶם‎ (qedem｜keh'-dem｜adverb｜eastward｜front)
[Heb] קֶדֶם‎, קֵדֶם‎, קֵדְמָה‎ ETCBC: קֶדֶם‎ (subs｜front) OSHL: s.al.ad, s.al.ab TWOT: 1988a GK: H7710, H7711, H7714 Greek: αἰών, ἀνατολή, ἀρχαῖος, ἀρχή, ἔμπροσθεν, ἥλιος
Derivation: or קֵדְמָה‎; from קָדַם‎;
Strong's: the front, of place (absolutely, the fore part, relatively the East) or time (antiquity); often used adverbially (before, anciently, eastward)
KJV: aforetime, ancient (time), before, east (end, part, side, -ward), eternal, [idiom] ever(-lasting), forward, old, past. Compare קִדְמָה‎.

H6924. qedem

קֶ֫דֶם‎ noun [masculine] front, east, aforetime; — absolute׳ק‎ Deut 33:27 +, קֶ֑קֶם‎ Judg 8:10 +; construct קֶדֶם‎ Prov 8:22; once plural construct קַדְמֵי‎ 8:23; —

1 location

a. front, ׳מִקּ‎ Isa 9:11 from the front (i.e. East), in front (opposed to מֵאָחוֺר‎), וָק ׳אָחוֺר‎ Psa 139:5 behind and before; ׳ק אֶהֳלֹךְ‎ Job 23:8 I go forward (opposed to אָחוֺר‎)

b. East: ׳הַרהַֿקּ‎ Gen 10:30 (J) mount of the East; ׳בְּנֵיקֿ‎ = dwellers in the east, tribes east or northeast of Canaan 29:1 (E), Judg 6:3, 33; 7:12; 8:10; 1Kin 5:10; Isa 11:14; Jer 49:28; Ezek 25:4, 10; Job 1:3; ק ׳הַרְרֵי‎ Num 23:7, see Di Sachau: Reise (1883), 159 ff.165 ff. Dr in Hast.PETHOS; ק ׳אֶרֶץ‎ Gen 25:6 (J); מִקֶּדֶם מָֽלְאוּ‎ Isa 2:6 they are full from the east (insert probablyמִקְסָם קֶסֶם,‎, or < קֹסְמִים‎ before ׳מִקּ‎, compare Lo De Du CheHpt Di-Kit); elsewhere ׳מִקּ‎ =eastward: (I) in the east Gen 2:8 (2) on the eastern (side) 12:8 (J) Zech 14:4; ׳מִקּ לְ‎ on the east of Gen 3:24 (J), 12:8 (J), Numbers 34:(P), Josh 7:2 (JE), Judg 8:11; Ezek 11:23 = Jonah 4:5; -3eastward, with verb of motion Gen 11:2; 13:11 (both J).

2 temporal, ancient time, aforetime:

a. ק ׳הַרְרֵי‎ Deut 33:15 ancient mountains, compare ק ׳אֱלֹהֵי‎ 33:27, ׳מַלְכֵיקֿ‎ Isa 19:11, שְׁמֵיקֿ ׳בִּשְׁמֵי‎ Psa 68:34.

b. ק ׳כִּימֵי‎ Psa 44:2 in ancient days; compare ק ׳מִימֵי‎ from of old Isa 23:7; Micah 7:20; Lam 1:7; 2:17; Isa 37:26 = ק ׳לְמִימֵי‎) 2Kin 19:25 (see מִן‎ 9b (2)); ק ׳כִּימֵי‎ Isa 51:9; Jer 46:26; ק ׳יַרְחֵי‎ Job 29:2 months of old.

c. ׳מִקּ‎ from of old Micah 5:1; Isa 45:21; 46:10; Hab 1:12; Neh 12:46; Psa 77:12 ׳מִנִּיק‎ 7; 8:2 springing out of, derived from, ancient times (compare Dr); מִקּ ׳יָמִים‎ 77:6; 143:5 days from of old.

d. ׳ק‎ alone, as adverb anciently, of old Psa 74:2; 119:152; ׳כְּק‎ Jer 30:20; Lam 5:21; ק ׳ישֵׁב‎ Psa 55:20 he that sitteth (enthroned from) of old.

e. beginning, מִקַּדְמֵיאָֿ֑רֶץ‎ Prov 8:23 from the beginnings of the earth; ׳ק מִפְעָלָיו‎ 8:22 the beginning of his works (|| רֵאשִׁית‎).
