H3000. Yeberekyahu

יְבֶרֶכְיָ֫הוּ‎ proper name, masculine (= יְבָרֶכְיָהוּ‎ s. Yah blesseth Ges§ 27, 3) father of a Zechariah in Isaiah's time Isa 8:2; usually in abbreviated form as following: בֶּרֶכְיָה‎

1 son of Zerubbabel 1Chr 3:20.

2 a Levite gruard of the ark 1Chr 9:16; 15:23.

3 father of Meshullam, one of Nehemian's chiefs Neh 3:4, 30; 6:18.

4 father of the prophet Zechariah Zech 1:1 = בֶּרֶכְיָהוּ‎ 1:7; בֶּרֶכְיָהוּ‎ also

5 father of Asaph 1Chr 6:24; 15:17.

6 Ephraimite chief2Chr 28:12.
