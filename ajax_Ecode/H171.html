H171. Oholiab

אָהֳלִיאָב‎ proper name, masculine Oholiab (Father's tent, compare Phoenician אהלמלך אהלבעל,‎; Sabean אהלאל אהלעתֿתר,‎ chief assistant of Bezalel in construction of tabernacle, etc. Exod 31:6; 35:34; 36:1-2, 38:23.
