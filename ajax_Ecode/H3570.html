II. כּוּשׁי‎ proper name, masculine ᵐ5‎ Ξουσει —

1 Jer 36:14 great-grandfather of יְהוּדִי‎, q. v. (perhaps original appellation Cushite).

2 father of prophet Zephaniah Zeph 1:1.
