H7621. shebuah

שְׁבֻעָה שְׁבוּעָה,‎ noun feminine (Josh 2:17 read הַזּה‎ AlbrZAW xvi(1896), 116) oath, curse; — absolute ׳שְׁבוּ‎ 9:20 +, ׳שְׁבֻ‎ Gen 26:3 +; construct שְׁבֻעַת‎ Exod 22:10 +; suffix שְׁבֻעָתִי‎ Gen 24:8 etc.; plural שְׁבֻעוֺת‎ absolute Ezek 21:28, construct Hab 3:9; [Jer 5:24; Ezek 45:21 see שָׁבוּעַ‎]; — oath [as accusative of congnate meaning with verb with הִשָּׁבַע‎ etc. Num 30:3 + (in rel.cl.) 5t.]:

1 of man:

a. attesting innocence Exod 22:10 (E), friendship 2Sam 21:7, promise 1Kin 2:43 (after שָׁמַר‎ keep; all י ׳שְׁבֻעַת‎ oath to to (׳י‎); innocence also Num 5:21 a; friendship Neh 6:18 (׳בַּעֲלֵישׁ לוֺ‎), promise Lev 5:4; of vow (|| נדר‎) Num 30:3; 30:11; 30:14 (אִסָּר שְׁבֻעַת‎), Josh 9:20 (all P), Neh 10:30 (|| אָלָה‎), Eccl 9:2, of covenant with ׳י‎2Chr 15:15, אֱלֹהִים שְׁבוּעַת‎ Eccl 8:2 (probably of oath of allegiance to king), שֶׁקֶר שְׁבֻעַת‎ Zech 8:17; of threat Judg 21:5; 1Sam 14:26 (י ׳שְׁבֻעַת ᵐ5‎); keep, discharge the oath is הַשּׁ ׳הֵקִים‎ Gen 26:3 (J), Jer 11:5; מִשׁ ׳נָקָה‎ be free, exempt from an oath Gen 24:8 (J), compare Josh 2:17, 20.

b. = curse, Isa 65:15 and (|| אָלָה‎) Num 5:21 (P), Dan 9:11.

2 oath of ׳י‎, in promise, Deut 7:8 (שָׁמַר‎ in rel. clause), || covenant (בְּרִית‎), לְיִצְחָק שְׁבוּעַתוֺ‎ Psa 105:9 = 1Chr 16:16. — מַטּוֺת שְׁבֻעוֺת‎ Hab 3:9 very dubious: AV oaths of the tribes; oaths, rods of the word, Hi-St; sworn (passive participle שְׁבַע‎) were the rods ( = chastisements) of (thy) word, Ges Hi RVm; heptads of spears Ew, compare Da; text probably corrupt, Now proposes (אַשְׁמָּֽתְךָׅ שִׂבַּעְתָּ‎ thou hast sated with shafts thy quiver; Marti (after ᵐ5‎Manuscripts) thy bow was satiated with shafts; GASm thou gluttest (?) thy shafts. — Ezek 21:28 see √.
