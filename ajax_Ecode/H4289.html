מַחְתָּה‎ noun feminine fire-holder, censer, snuffdish; — absolute ׳מ‎ Lev 16:12; Num 17:11; suffix מַחְתָּתוֺ‎ Lev 10:1+ 4 t.; plural absolute מַחְתּוֺת‎ Num 16:6 4t.; מַחְתֹּת‎ 16:17 3t.; construct מַחְתּוֺת‎ 17:3; 17:4; suffix מַחְתֹּתָיו‎ Exod 27:3; מַחְתֹּתֶיהָ‎ 25:38 2t; —

1 snuff-holder, snuff-dish (NowArchäol. ii, 63. 65) of gold Exod 25:38; 37:23; Num 4:9 (all P).

2 bronze utensils, fire-pans, belonging to altar of burnt-offerings Exod 27:3; 38:3, compare Num 4:14 (all P); fire-pans of gold 1Kin 7:50 2Chr 4:22; 2Kin 25:15 = Jer 52:19.

3 censer, Lev 10:1; 16:12; Num 16:6, 17 (4 t. in verse); 16:18; 17:2; 17:3; 17:4 (of bronze), 16:11 (all P).
