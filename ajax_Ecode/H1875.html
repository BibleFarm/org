דָּרַשׁ‎162 verb resort to, seek (compare Arabic rub over, efface (a site), tread (wheat), figurative read repeatedly, study; beat (a path), discuss, Pa`el practise in; Late Hebrew search out (a meaning), expound) —

Qal Perfect 1Chr 10:14; Imperfect יִדְרשׁ‎ Psa 10:4 +, אֶדְרְשָׁה‎ 1Sam 28:7, נִדְרְשָׁה‎ 1Kin 22:7 +, יִדְרְשׁוּן‎ Isa 55:2; Imperative דְּרָשֿׁ‎ 1Kin 22:5 +; Infinitive absolute דָּרשׁ‎ Lev 10:16; Deut 23:22; construct דְּרשׁ‎ 22:2 + לִדְרָשֿׁ‎ Ezek 14:7, לְדַרְיוֺשׁ‎ (scribal error for לִדְרוֺשׁ‎ Ew§ 239a Ol§ 245 g) Ezra 10:16; Participle דֹּרֵשׁ‎ Deut 11:12 10t., דּוֺרֵשׁ‎ 1Chr 28:9 3t.; passive דְּרוּשִׁים‎ Psa 111:2, דְּרוּשָׁה‎ Isa 62:12; synonym בקשׁ‎; —

1 (tread a place,) resort to, frequent, with religious object, with accusative of location Amos 5:5; 2Chr 1:5, לְ‎ local Deut 12:5.

2 seek, consult, inquire of:

a. accusative אֱלֹהִים ׳י,‎ Gen 25:22 (J) Exod 18:15 (E) 1Sam 9:9; 1Kin 22:8; 2Kin 3:11; 8:8; 22:13, 18; 1Chr 15:13; 21:30; 2Chr 18:7; 34:21; Psa 24:6; 78:34; Jer 21:2; 37:7; Ezek 20:1, 3; the ark of God 1Chr 13:3; word of Yahweh 1Kin 22:5 (= 2Chr 18:4) compare 1Kin 14:5; with בְּ‎ Yahweh 1Chr 10:14; 2Chr 34:26; Ezek 14:7; his word 2Kin 1:16; with אֶלאֵֿל אֶל,‎ Job 5:8; their God Isa 8:19, Messianic king 11:10; י ׳מֵעַלסֵֿפֶר‎ out of the book of ׳י‎ 34:16 (in gloss, according to CheJQ Jan. 1892, 332); with מֵאוֺתוֺ‎ of a לי ׳נָבִיא‎ 1Kin 22:7 (= 2Chr 18:6) compare (above) 18:8; 2Kin 3:11 and 1Kin 14:5 (מֵעִמְּךָ‎).

b. heathen gods and necromancers, הַדֹּרֵשׁ‎ the inquirer Ezek 14:10; with אוֺב בַּעֲלַת בְּ,‎ 1Sam 28:7, בָּאוֺב‎ 1Chr 10:13, זְבוּב בְּבַעַל‎ 2Kin 1:2-3, 6, 16; with אֶלהָֿאֹבוֺת אֶל,‎ Isa 8:19, אֶלהַֿמֵּתִים‎ Deut 18:11, אֶלהָֿאֱלִילִים‎ Isa 19:3.

3 seek deity in prayer and worship:

a. the true God (compare בקשׁ‎ 3) י ׳דרשׁ‎ Deut 4:29; Hosea 10:12; Amos 5:4, 6; Isa 9:12; 31:1; 55:6; 58:2; 65:10; Jer 10:21; 29:13; Zeph 1:6; Lam 3:25; 1Chr 16:11 (= Psa 105:4) 28:9; 2Chr 12:14; 14:3; 14:6; 15:2, 12; 16:12; 22:9; 26:5; Psa 9:11; 22:27; 34:5; 34:11; 119:2; 119:10; (ה)אלהים דרשׁ‎2Chr 19:3; 26:5; 30:19; Psa 14:2 (= 53:3) 69:33; ד ׳אֲדֹנָי‎ 77:3; with לְ‎, only in Chronicler, ליהוה‎ 1Chr 22:19; 2Chr 15:13; 20:3; Ezra 6:21; לאלהים‎2Chr 17:4; 31:21; 34:3; Ezra 4:2; DrIntr. 503 finds in Chronicles weakened meaning, revere.

b. seek heathen deities, with accusative Jer 8:2; 2Chr 25:15, 20; with לְ‎, Deut 12:30; 2Chr 17:3.

4. a. seek, with idea of demanding, require (בקשׁ‎ is seek, simply), with accusative lost sheep Deut 22:2; Ezek 34:6, 8, 11; goat Lev 10:16 (twice in verse) (P); with אחר‎, after green fodder Job 39:8.

b. inquire, investigate (a matter), absolute Judg 6:29; Deut 13:15; 17:4, 9; 19:18; with accusative י ׳דּוֺרֵשׁ כָּללְֿבָבוֺת‎ all hearts Yahweh searches 1Chr 28:9; הַמּוֺפֵת לִדְרשׁ‎ to inquire into the wonder2Chr 32:31; with לְ‎, to inquire about 2Sam 11:3; Job 10:6; followed by עַל‎ person + infinitive 2Chr 24:6 apply to the Levites to bring, etc., compare 31:9 (עַל‎ both person and thing).

5 ask for, require, demand, with accusative of thing Ezek 20:40; + מִן‎ person Deut 23:22; Micah 6:8; exact, often with collateral idea of avenging, מֵעִם‎ Deut 18:19, so with מִיַּד‎ Ezek 34:10, מִיַּד דָּם דָּרַשׁ‎ Gen 9:5 (P) Ezek 33:6; accusative only, דמים דֹּרֵשׁ‎ Psa 9:13, ׳ד רִשְׁעוֺ‎ 10:15; absolute 10:4; 10:13; 2Chr 24:22.

6 seek with application, study, follow, practise, absolute by wisdom Eccl 1:13; with accusative ׳ד מִשְׁמָּט‎ study or practise justice Isa 1:17; 16:5; י ׳מַעֲשֵׂי‎ Psa 111:2; וְאַלרַֿע טוֺב‎ Amos 5:14; commands of God Psa 119:45; 119:94; 119:155; 1Chr 28:8; the law Ear 7:10; ׳ד שׁלום‎ seek or study the peace, welfare of any one Deut 23:7; Jer 29:7; Ezra 9:12, לשׁלום‎ Jer 38:4, ׳ד טוב‎ Est 10:3, ׳ד רעה‎ Prov 11:27; Psa 38:13 (compare בקשׁ‎ 2, where this use is more common).

7 seek with care, care for, אתהּ דֹּרֵשׁ אלהיך יהוה אשׁר ארץ‎ land which Yahweh thy God careth for Deut 11:12; אֱלוֺהַּ אַליִֿדְרְשֵׁהוּ‎ let not Eloah care for it Job 3:4; יִד לֹא ׳אוֺתָךְ‎ Jer 30:14; צֶמֶר דָּֽרְשָׁה‎ Prov 31:13; with לְ‎ Jer 30:17; לְנַפְשִׁי דּוֺרֵשׁ אֵין‎ no one careth for me Psa 142:5; but in both these לְ‎ perhaps belongs to אֵין‎, compare || מַכִּיר לִי אֵין‎ 142:5, also Lam 4:4 etc.; דְּרוּשָׁה‎ cared for (of Jerusalem) Isa 62:12.
