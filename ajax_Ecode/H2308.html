חָדַל‎ verb cease (Sabean חדל‎ be negligent, SabDenkm76) —

Qal Perfect חָדַל‎ Gen 18:11 4t.; וְחָדַל‎ consecutive Num 9:13; Psa 49:9; 2masculine singular וְחָדַלְתָּ֫‎ Exod 23:5; plural הָֽדְּלוּ‎ Judg 5:6 4t.; חָד֑לּוּ‎ (compare Köi, 243) 5:7; 1Sam 2:5; 1plural חָדַלְנוּ‎ Jer 44:18; Imperfect יֶחְדַּל‎ Deut 15:11 + (Job 10:20 Kt יחדל‎ jussive > Qr וַחֲדָ֑ל‎ Imperative); אֶחְדָּ֑ל‎ Judg 15:7 3t.; וְאַחְדְּלָה‎ Job 16:6; plural יֶחְדָּ֑לוּ‎ Ezek 2:5 2t.; יֶחְדָּלוּ֔ן‎ Exod 9:29; וַיַּחְדְּלוּ‎ Gen 11:8; Exod 9:33, נֶחְדָּ֑ל‎ 1Kin 22:15, etc.; Infinitive construct חֲדֹל‎ 1Sam 12:23; Imperative חֲדַל‎ Exod 14:12 +, etc.; חִדְלוּ‎ Isa 1:16; 2:22; חֲדָ֑לוּ‎ Zechariah 11:22; —

1 cease, come to an end Exod 9:29, 33, 34 (J), Isa 24:8; impersonal לְשָׂרָה לִהְיוֺת חָדַל‎ Gen 18:11 (J); cease to be Deut 15:11 (followed by מִן‎), and in poetry Judg 5:6-7, (twice in verse); 1Sam 2:5; Job 14:7; = be lacking, fail 19:14; Prov 10:19.

2 cease, leave off, followed by infinitive with לְ‎ Gen 11:8 (J), 41:49 (E), Num 9:13 (P), 1Sam 12:23; 23:13; Jer 44:18; 51:30; Deut 23:23; Psa 36:4; Prov 19:27; Ruth 1:18; infinitive alone Isa 1:16; so also Hosea 8:10, where read וְחָֽדְלוּ‎ for ᵐ5 וַיָּחֵלּוּ‎ Che We; followed by infinitive with מִן‎ Exod 23:5 (JE), 1Kin 15:21 2Chr 16:5; followed by substantive with מִן‎ (cease regarding) Isa 2:22 (but Che Du and others treat see as interpolation; omitted by ᵐ5‎), 1Sam 9:5; Prov 23:4; followed by מֵאלהים‎2Chr 35:21, i.e. leave off provoking God; Exod 14:12 (J), followed by מִמֶּנּו‎ desist from us, let us alone; Job 7:16; followed by accusative 3:17 ׳ח רֹגֶז‎; 14:6 have rest (i.e. cease from being troubled, see Di; compare 14:1); no complem. expressed, cease, forbear Judg 15:7; 20:28; Amos 7:5; 1Kin 22:6, 15 2Chr 18:5, 14; Jer 40:4; 41:8; Zech 11:12; Psa 49:9; Job 16:6; 2Chr 25:16; specifically of refusal to hear Ezek 2:5, 7; 3:11, 27 (all opposed to שָׁמַע‎).
