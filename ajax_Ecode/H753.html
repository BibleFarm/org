אֹ֫רֶךְ‎94 noun [masculine] length — only singular, ׳א‎ absolute Exod 27:1 +; construct Gen 6:15 +; אָרְכּוֺ‎ Exod 25:10 +; אָרְכָּם‎2Chr 3:11; אָרְכָּן‎ Ezek 42:11, etc. —

a. length of ark Gen 6:15 (P), of land of Canaan 13:17 (J); most often of ark & other measurements in tabernacle & temple Exod 25:10, 17; 26:2, 8; 27:1, 9 + (22 t. Exodus, P), 1Kin 6:2-3, 20 + (13 t. Kings & Chronicles), Ezek 40:7, 11, 18 + (41 t. Ezekiel), etc.

b. of time ׳א יָמִים‎ Deut 30:20; Job 12:12; Psa 21:5; 23:6; 91:16; 93:5; Prov 3:2, 16; Lam 5:20.

c. ׳א אַמַּיִם‎ forbearance, self-restraint, Prov 25:15. — (Ezek 31:7 ᵑ6 ᵐ5‎ Co read רֹב‎ for אֹרֶךְ‎, compare 31:5 where Co strike out verb ארך‎; 41:22 read probably ᵐ5 אדנו‎ Sm, or אדניו‎ Co = base.)
