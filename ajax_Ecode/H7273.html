H7273. ragli

רַגְלִי‎ adjective on foot; &; ר ׳אִישׁ‎ (after number) footmen, especially foot-soldiers, Judg 20:2; 2Sam 8:4 = 1Chr 18:4; 19:18 (מָּרָשִׁים‎ in || 2Sam 10:18); elsewhere ׳ר‎ as substantive, usually collective, of Israel at Exodus. Exod 12:37; Num 11:21 (both J E); = foot-soldiery 1Sam 4:10; 15:4; 2Sam 10:6; 1Kin 20:29; 2Kin 13:7; plural noun רַגְלִים‎ footmen, men on foot Jer 12:5 (opposed to הַסּוּסִים‎).
