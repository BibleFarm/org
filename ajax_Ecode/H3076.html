H3076. Yehochanan

יוֺחָנָן יְהוֺחָנָן,‎ proper name, masculine ᵐ5‎ mostly Ἰωαναν, Ἰωναν (׳י‎ hath been gracious, compare חֲנַנְאֵל אֶלְחָנָן‎ & references; also Phoenician בעליחן יחנבעל‎) — 1. a. ׳יְהוֺ‎ a priest Neh 12:13;

b. high priest Ezra 10:6 = יוֺחָנָן‎ Neh 12:22-23, apparently = יוֺנָתָן‎ 12:11 (twice in verse) (ᵐ5‎ Ἰωναθαν).

2 ׳יְהוֺ‎ Levite Neh 12:42.

3 ׳יְהוֺ‎ son of Tobiah Neh 6:18 (ᵐ5‎ Manuscripts Ἰωναθαν).

4 ׳יְהוֺ‎ porter in David's time 1Chr 26:3 (ᵐ5‎ Ἰωνας, ᵐ5‎L Ἰωναθαν).

5 ׳יְהוֺ‎ a Judaite captain 2Chr 17:15 apparently also 23:1.

6 ׳יְהוֺ‎ an Ephraimite 2Chr 28:12.

7 ׳יְהוֺ‎ an Israelite, Ezra's time Ezra 10:28.

8 ׳יוֺ‎ (& so all following) a Jewish captain, after fall of Jerusalem 2Kin 25:23; Jer 40:8, 13, 15, 16; 41:11, 13, 14, 15, 16; 42:1, 8; 43:2, 4, 5

9 eldest son of king Josiah 1Chr 3:15 ᵐ5‎L Ἰωαχας (apparently = יְתוֺאָחָז‎ 2Kin 23:30 + ).

10 a post-exilic prince of the line of David 1Chr 3:24.

11 father of Azariah, priest in Solomon's time 1 Chronicles 5:35; 1 Chronicles 5:36.

12 two of David's mighty men:

a. a Benjamite 1Chr 12:5;

b. a Gadite 1Chr 12:13.

13 a returning exile Ezra 8:12.
