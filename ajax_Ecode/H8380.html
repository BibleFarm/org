H8380. taom

[תּוֺאָם‎ (Köii. 1. 69 תְּאֹם‎)]

noun masculine twin (Late Hebrew id.; compare Phoenician proper name, masculine תאם‎, Greek Θωμᾶς = δίδυμος John 20:24); — plural absolute of two boys תּוֺמִם‎ Gen 25:24 (Ges§ 23f.) תְּאוֺמִים‎ 38:27 (both J); of animals, construct צְבִיָּה תְּאוֺמֵי‎ Cant 4:5, צ ׳תָּאֳמֵי‎ Cant 7:4.
