H3575. Kuth

or Kuthah כּוּת כּוּתָה,‎ proper name, of a location whence king of Assyria (Sargon) transported colonists into Northern Israel, מִכּוּתָה‎ 2Kin 17:4, אַנְשֵׁיכֿוּת‎ 17:30; Babylonian Kûtû, Kûtê, modern Tel-Ibrahim, approximately 20 miles northeast from Babylon, see COT17:24 DlPar 217 M-AJBL 1892, xi. 169.
