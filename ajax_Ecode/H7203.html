H7203. ro

'eh I. רֹאֶה‎ noun [masculine] seer, with article ׳הָר‎ (except 2Sam 15:27); — old name for נָבִיא‎ 1Sam 9:9b, used of Samuel 9:9 a.; 9:11; 9:18; 9:19 and (as archaism) 1Chr 9:22; 26:28; 29:29; of חֲנָנִי‎2Chr 16:7, 10; הֲרוֺאֶה‎ apparently of Zadok 2Sam 15:27, but corrupt (conjecture in We Dr); plural (with preposition) לָרֹאִים‎ Isa 30:10 the seers, as a class.

II. רֹאֶה‎ noun [masculine] prophetic vision בָּר ׳שָׁגוּ‎ Isa 28:7 they reel in (their) vision (|| מְּלִילִיָּת מָּקוּ‎).
