H4768. marbith

מַרְבִּית‎ noun feminine increase, great number, greatness; — ׳מ‎ absolute Lev 25:37; construct 1Sam 2:33 + 2t.; suffix מַרְבִּיתָם‎ 1Chr 12:30 (van d. H. v.12:29); —

1 increase, concrete:

a. of family 1Sam 2:33.

b. increment, interest, usury, Lev 25:37 (H; of food-stuffs, || נֶשֶׁךְ‎, of money; ׳מ‎ of money Egyptian Aramaic, opposed to ראש‎ principal, CookeNorth-Semitic Inscr. 404).

2 great number: of people 2Chr 30:18; with suffix = the greater part of them, 1Chr 12:30 (see above).

3 greatness, of wisdom 2Chr 9:6.
