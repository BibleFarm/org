נְהִי‎ noun [masculine] wailing, lamentation, mourning song; — ׳נ‎ absolute Amos 5:16 5t.; וָנֶהִ֑י‎ Jer 9:9; — wailing, at ׳י‎s judgement Micah 2:4 as accusative of congnate meaning with verb; Jer 9:9 (c.עַלֿ נָשָׂא‎, + ׳בְּכִ‎, || קִינָה‎ ), 9:17 (c, id.,|| דִּמְעָה‎), 9:18 (נ ׳קוֺל‎ ), 31:15 (|| תַּמְרוּרִים בְּכִי,‎); mourning song נ ׳יוֺדְעֵי‎ Amos 5:16 of professional mourners, skilled in mourning song; compare Jer 9:19, where taught (|| קִינָת‎), also הִי‎ Ezek 2:10 (|| אֵכֶל‎, מִסְמֵּד‎ q. v.
