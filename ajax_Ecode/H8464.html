H8464. tachmas

תַּחְמָס‎ noun [masculine] name of male ostrich, according to Boii.830 Thes (Thes derives from violence of this bird, compare Arabic violence, also ostrich; other conjectures are: owl ᵑ9 ᵐ5‎; swallow, Saad: see also Kn in Di) — mentioned as unclean Lev 11:16 (P) Deut 14:15.
