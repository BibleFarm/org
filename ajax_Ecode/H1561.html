H1561. gelel

or gel [גֵּל‎] noun [masculine] dung (ball of dung, Arabic etc., dung of camels etc., compare Aramaic גַּלָּא‎; on form see Ew§ 255 b Ol§ 150. 163) — suffix גֶּלֲלוֺ‎ Job 20:7, plural construct הָאָדָם‎ (צֵאַת‎) גֶּלֲלֵי‎ Ezek 4:12, 15; — human dung used as fuel 4:12, 15 ׳ג הָאָדָם (צֵאַת)‎; perhaps also Job 20:7, but compare Di, who thinks no reference to fuel, & De who thinks of cattle-dung; (see on Arabic word Wetzst in De). — See also II.גָּלָל‎.
