III. [אהל‎] noun [masculine] odorific tree, aloe (?) (? loan-word from Sanskrit aguru, agaru, dialect aghil, compare WilsonSkr. Dict.; M. Müller in PuseyDaniel 2d ed. p. 647 f; aloÎxylon agallochum (compare SigismundAromata, Leipz. 1884, p. 38 f, MV cite KondrackiBeitr. z. Kenntn. d. 1 plural אֲהָלִים‎ trees planted by ׳י‎ Num 24:6 (|| ארזים‎ perhaps error for אֵילִים‎ compare Di.

2 aloes, as sweet-smelling; perfume for bed, אֲהָלִים‎ Prov 7:17 (|| קִנָּמוֺן מֹר,‎); for garments אֲהָלוֺת‎ Psa 45:9 (|| קְצִיעוֺת מֹר,‎); of bride, under figure of odorific tree Song 4:14 (|| בְשָׂמִים כָּלרָֿאשֵׁי מֹר,‎).
