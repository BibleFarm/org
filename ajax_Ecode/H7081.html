H7081. qesem

קֶ֫סֶם‎ noun [masculine] divination; — absolute ׳ק‎ Num 23:25+, קָ֑סֶם‎ Ezek 21:26; construct קֶסֶם‎ 13:6; plural קְסְמִים‎ Deut 18:10 +; —

1 of the nations : Balaam, Num 23:23 (poem in J E; || נַחַשׁ‎; with ב‎ against; as accusative of congnate meaning with verb ׳לִקְאָםקֿ‎ Ezek 21:26; ׳ק‎ as instrument of divination בִּימִינוֺ‎ 21:27; so of elders of Moab and Midian, בְּיָדָם קְסָמִים‎, Num 22:7(E). — Isa 2:6 see [קָסַם‎].

2 of false prophets ׳ק כָּזָב‎ Ezek 13:6 (but see [קָסַם‎]); ׳ק‎ as accusative of congnate meaning with verb 13:23 (Co Berthol Krae כָּזָב‎ as 13:9; Ezekiel 21:34; Ezekiel 22:38); ׳ק צֱלִיל‎ (so Gf for וֶאֱלִו֯ל ᵑ0‎ Jer 14:4 (all || שׁוא חזה‎ or שׁקר‎) קְסָמִים קֹסֵם‎); prohibited Deut 18:10; 2Kin 17:17; reprobated 1Sam 15:23 (poem; || תרפים‎).

3 in good sense מֶלֶךְ ׳ק שִׂפְתֵי עַל‎ Prov 16:10 (king's lips as oracle).
