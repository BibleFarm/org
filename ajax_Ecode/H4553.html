מִסְמֵּד‎ noun masculineGen 50:10 wailing; — absolute ׳מ‎ Amos 5:16 11t.; construct מִסְמַּד‎ Micah 1:11 2t.; suffix מִסְמְּדִי‎ Psa 30:12; — wailing:

1 for dead, Gen 50:10 (J; as accusative of congnate meaning with verb), Zech 12:10 (with עַלֿ‎; compare 12:11; 12:11 below).

2 for calamity experienced Amos 5:16 (twice in verse) (|| אֵבֶל‎), vAmos 5:17; Micah 1:8 (׳מ כַּתַּנִּים‎, || אֵבֶל‎), 1:11; Jer 48:38; Ezek 27:31, anticipated Jer 6:26; Est 4:3.

3 in contrition Isa 22:12 (+ בְּכִי‎), Joel 2:12 (+ בְּכִי צוֺם,‎), compare Zech 12:11 (twice in verse).

4 in General, Psa 30:12 (opposed to מָחוֺל‎, compare Eccl 3:4).
