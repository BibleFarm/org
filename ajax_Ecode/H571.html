[Heb] אֶמֶת‎, אֱמֶת‎ ETCBC: אֶמֶת‎ (subs｜trustworthiness) OSHL: a.dy.am TWOT: 116k GK: H622 Greek: ἀλήθεια, ἀληθεύω, ἀληθής, ἀληθινός, δίκαιος, δικαιοσύνη, ἐλεημοσύνη
Derivation: contracted from אָמַן‎;
Strong's: stability; (figuratively) certainty, truth, trustworthiness
KJV: assured(-ly), establishment, faithful, right, sure, true (-ly, -th), verity.

H571. emeth

אֱמֶת‎ noun feminine firmness, faithfulness, truth (contracted for אֱמֶנֶת‎ from אָמֵן‎) Gen 24:48 106t.; suffix אֲמִתְּךָ אֲמִתּוֺ,‎ Psa 91:4 18t.

1 reliability, sureness: אֱמֶת דֶּרֶח‎ sure way Gen 24:48 (J); אמת שׂכר‎ sure reward Prov 11:18; אמת אות‎ sure token Josh 2:12 (J); אמת זֶרַע‎ Jer 2:21.

2 stability, con-tinuance: וֶאֱמֶת שָׁלוֺם‎ peace and stability Isa 39:8 (= 2Kin 20:19) Est 9:30; Jer 33:6; Zech 8:19, compare אמת שׁלום‎ Jer 14:13.

3 faithfulness, reliableness: (α) of men אֱמֶת אִישׁ‎ faithful man Neh 7:2; אמת אנשׁי‎ Exod 18:21 (E); בֶּאֱמֶת הָלַח‎ walk in faithfulness, faithfully 1Kin 2:4; 3:6; 2Kin 20:3; Isa 38:3 compare 1Sam 12:24; of בַּאֲמִתֶּ֑ךָ ׳י,‎ Psa 25:5; 26:3; 86:11; האמת עיר‎ Zech 8:3; of men Psa 45:5; 51:8; 2Chr 31:20; 32:1; Prov 29:14; Isa 10:20; 42:3; 48:1; ׳בא ובתמים‎ Judg 9:16, 19; Josh 24:14; וֶאֱ ׳חֶסֶד‎ mercy and faithfulness Prov 3:3; 14:22; 16:6; 20:28; Hosea 4:1, & the phrase ואמת חסד עשׂה‎ Gen 24:49; 47:29; Josh 2:14 (J) 2Sam 15:20. (b) an attribute of God Psa 54:7; 71:22; Isa 38:18-19, 61:8; אמת נתן‎ Micah 7:20; אמת עשׂה‎ Ezek 18:9; Neh 9:33; ואמת חסד עשׂה‎ Gen 32:11 (J) 2Sam 2:6; ואמת חסד רב‎ abundant in mercy and faithfulness Exod 34:6 (J) Psa 86:15; these attributes are also associated 40:11; 40:12; 61:8; 115:1; 138:2; Isa 16:5; Gen 24:27 (J); they are messengers of God to men Psa 57:4; 85:11; 89:15 compare 43:3; י ארחות ׳כל ואמת חסד‎ 25:10; the faithfulness of God endureth for ever 117:2; he keepeth it for ever 146:6; it reacheth unto the skies 57:11; 108:5; it is shield & buckler 91:4; he is אמת אל‎ 31:6 = אמת אלהי‎2Chr 15:3; אֱמֶת‎ is also associated with the divine ישׁר‎ Psa 111:8; צֶדֶק‎ 85:12; צְדָקָה‎ Zech 8:8; מִשְׁמָּט‎ Psa 111:7; Jer 4:2; & salvation Psa 69:14.

4 truth (a) as spoken: אמת דבר‎ speak truth 1Kin 22:16; 2Chr 18:15; Jer 9:4; Zech 8:16; Psa 15:2; א ׳דִּבְרֵי‎ Eccl 12:10; הַדָּבָר אֱמֶת‎ the thing is certainly true Deut 13:15; 17:4; אֱמֶת אִמְרֵי‎ = אמת אמרים‎ Prov 22:21; אמת הגה‎ 8:7; א ׳הגיד‎ Dan 11:2, אֲמִתְּךָ‎ Psa 30:10; אמת שׂפת‎ Prov 12:19; אֱמֶת‎ it is true Isa 43:9; אתכם האמת‎ whether truth is with you Gen 42:16 (E) compare Isa 59:14-15,. (b) of testimony and judgment אֱמֶת עֵד‎ true witness Prov 14:25; ונאמן אמת עד‎ Jer 42:5; אמת משׁפט‎ Ezek 18:8; Zech 7:9; אמת שָׁפַט‎ 8:16. (c) of divine instruction אֱמֶת כְּחָב‎ Dan 10:21; אמת תורת‎ Mal 2:6 compare Neh 9:13; אמת תורתך‎ Psa 119:142; י ׳דבר אמת בְּפִיךָ‎ 1Kin 17:24 compare 2Sam 7:28; אמת י ׳משׁפטי‎ Psa 19:10; אמת דברך ראשׁ‎ 119:160; אמת מצותיך‎ 119:151. (d) truth as a body of ethical or religious knowledge Dan 8:12; באמתך להשׂכיל‎ 9:13.

5. adverb in truth, truly Psa 132:11; אמת אלהים יהוה‎ Yahweh is God in truth, truly Jer 10:10; elsewhere בֶּאֱמֶת‎ Judg 9:15; Psa 145:18; Jer 26:15; 28:9; 32:41.
