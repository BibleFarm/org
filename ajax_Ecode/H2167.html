זָמַר‎ (zâmar｜zaw-mar'｜verb｜make music)
[Heb] זָמַר‎ ETCBC: זמר‎ (verb｜sing) OSHL: g.bq.aa TWOT: 558 GK: H2376 Greek: ὑμνέω, ψάλλω
Derivation: a primitive root (perhaps identical with זָמַר‎ through the idea of striking with the fingers); properly, to touch the strings or parts of a musical instrument, i.e.
Strong's: play upon it; to make music, accompanied by the voice; hence to celebrate in song and music
KJV: give praise, sing forth praises, psalms.
