H6875. tsori

or tseri or tsori צֳרִי‎ and (Gen 37:25) (וּ)צְרִי‎ noun [masculine] a kind of balsam, as merchandise 37:25 (J), Ezek 27:17, gift Gen 43:11 (J), medicament Jer 8:22; 46:11; 51:8 (for national disaster, in fig,); — ThDyerM'Lean in Ency. Bib.Balm think a resin, like (not necess.=) gum of mastic-tree, pistacia lentiscus (otherwise PostHastings DB).
