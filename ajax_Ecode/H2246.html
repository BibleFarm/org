H2246. Chobab

חֹבָב‎ proper name, masculine (compare Phoenician proper name חב‎; WeSkizzen iii, 171. 217 suggests comparison with serpent) — son of Reuel, חֹתֵן‎ of Moses (see חתן‎), Num 10:29 (J E), Judg 4:11; insert ׳ח‎ probably also 1:16, see GFM.
