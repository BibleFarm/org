H1283. Beriah

בְּרִיעָה‎ proper name, masculine 1. a son of Asher Gen 46:17 (twice in verse); Num 26:44-45, 1Chr 7:30-31,.

2 son of Ephraim 1Chr 7:23 (where explanation as if from בְּרָעָה‎).

3 a Benjamite בְּרִעָה‎ 1Chr 8:13, בְּרִיעָה‎ 8:16.

4 a Levite 1Chr 23:10-11,.
