H3088. Yehoram

יֹרָם יוֺרָם, יְהוֺרָם,‎

proper name, masculine (׳י‎ is exalted; compare מַלְכִּירָם‎; further, Phoenician רמבעל בעלרם,‎; Sabean אלרם‎ DHMZMG 1876, 686) —

1 ׳יְהוֺ‎ (ᵐ5‎ Ἰωραμ) king of Judah, son of Jehoshaphat 1Kin 22:51; 2Kin 1:17; 8:16, 25, 29; 12:19; 2Chr 21:1, 3, 4, 5, 9, 16; 22:1, 6, 11; = ׳יוֺ‎ 2Kin 8:21, 23, 24; 11:2; 1Chr 3:11.

2 ׳יְהוֺ‎ (ᵐ5‎ Ἰωραμ) king of Israel son of Ahab 2Kin 1:17; 3:1, 6; 9:15, 17, 21 (twice in verse); 9:22-23, 2Chr 22:5-6, 7; = ׳יוֺ‎ 2Kin 8:16, 25, 28 (twice in verse); 8:29 (twice in verse); 9:14 (twice in verse); 9:16 (twice in verse); 9:29; 2Chr 22:5, 7.

3 ׳יְהוֺ‎priest in time of Jehoshaphat 2Chr 17:8 (ᵐ5‎ Ἰωραμ, Ἰωραν).

4 ׳יוֺ‎ son of Tou, king of Hamath 2Sam 8:10 (ᵐ5‎ Ἰεδδουραν) probably error for הֲדוֺרָם‎ || 1Chr 18:10.

5 יֹרָם‎a Levite 1Chr 26:25 (ᵐ5‎ Ἰωραμ).
