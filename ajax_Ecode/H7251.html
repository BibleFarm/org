רָבַע‎ (râbaʻ｜raw-bah'｜verb｜squared)
[Heb] רָבַע‎ ETCBC: רבע‎ (verb｜be square) OSHL: t.al.ae TWOT: 2107 GK: H8062
Derivation: a primitive root (rather identical with רָבַע‎ through the idea of sprawling 'at all fours' (or possibly the reverse is the order of deriv.); compare אַרְבַּע‎); properly, to be four (sided); used only as denominative of רֶבַע‎;
Strong's: to be quadrate
KJV: (four-) square(-d).

H7251. raba

[רְבַע‎] verb denominative, only Passive participle squared, square; —

Qal Passive participle רָבוּעַ‎ square, of altar Exod 27:1; 30:2; 37:25; 38:1, compare Ezek 43:16; breastplate 30:2; 39:9; plural רְבֻעִים‎, of doorways, etc. [on text see Kit Benz] 1Kin 7:5; feminine singular רְבֻעָה‎ as substantive = a square Ezek 41:21 (accusative Co Toy, omit מְזוּזַת‎, and joining ׳ר‎ to 41:20; Krae reads רְבֻעֹת מְזוּזתֹ‎, compare 1Kin 6:33, רְבִייְִ‎ 2).

Pu`al Participle square, מְרֻבַּע‎ Ezek 45:2, of piece of land; feminine singular מְרֻבָּ֑עַת‎ 40:47, of court; feminine plural מְרֻבָּעוֺת‎ 1Kin 7:31 (opposed to עֲנֻלּוֺת‎), of borders of bases (compare Sabean רבעתם‎, a kind of square building, SabDenkm31).
