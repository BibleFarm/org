KJV: Asshurim, Ashurites.

H805. Ashuwriy

אַשּׁוּרִם‎ proper name, of a people, plural an Arab tribe traced back to Abraham & Keturah Gen 25:3 compare Di.

אֲשׁוּרִי‎ adjective, of a people ׳הָא‎ as collective noun 2Sam 2:9, but read perhaps הַגְּשׁוּרִי‎ q. v., compare also We Dr; Köh Klo and others read הָאָֽשֵׁרִי‎ & compare Judg 1:32.
