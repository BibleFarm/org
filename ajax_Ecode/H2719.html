חֶ֫רֶב‎411 noun feminineIsa 21:15 sword (as weapon; Aramaic חַרְבָּא‎, , Zinjirli חרב‎; Arabic dart, javelin) — absolute חֶ֫רֶב‎ Gen 3:24 +; חָ֑רֶב‎ Exod 17:13 +; construct חֶ֫רֶב‎ Deut 33:29 +; suffix חַרְבִּי‎ Exod 15:9 13t.; חַרְבְּךָ‎ Gen 27:40 7t.; חֵרְבֶּ֑ךָ‎ 1Sam 17:33; Psa 17:13; חַרְבּוֺ‎ Num 22:23-24t.; חַרְבְּכֶם‎ Jer 2:30; Ezek 33:26; חַרְבָּם‎ Psa 37:15; 44:4; plural חֲרָבוֺת‎ Isa 21:15 5t.; construct חַרְבוֺת‎ Josh 5:2 2t.; suffix חַרְבוֺתָיו‎ Ezek 26:9; חַרְבוֺתָם‎ Isa 2:4-5t.; חַרְבֹתֵיהֶם‎ Micah 4:3 2t.; —

1. a. sword, as weapon of war Gen 48:22 (E), Judg 7:14, 20; 1Sam 21:9, and so in all periods; two-edged (short) sword ׳ח אָרְכָּהּ גֹּמֶד פֵיוֺ֖ת שְׁנֵי וְלָהּ‎ Judg 3:16, compare ׳ח מִּיּוֺת‎ Prov 5:4 (in simile), ׳ח מִּיפִיּוֺת‎ Psa 149:6; see also ח ׳צוּר‎ edge of sword 89:44.

b. gird on sword = הח ׳חָגַר‎ 1Sam 17:39; 25:13 (3 t. in verse); Psa 45:4 (יָרֵך עַל ׳ח ׳הח‎), ירך על שִׂים‎ Exod 32:27, compare especially 2Sam 20:8.

c. draw the sword הח ׳הֵרִיק‎ Exod 15:9; Lev 26:33; Ezek 5:2, 12; 12:14; 28:7, הח ׳הוציא‎ 21:8; 21:10, הח ׳פתח‎ Psa 37:14; Ezekiel 21:33, נטשׁ‎ Isa 21:15 (Gr Che לטשׁ‎ see below); usually הח ׳שׁלף‎ Num 22:23, 31; Josh 5:13; Judg 3:22; 8:10, 20; 9:54; 1Sam 17:51; 31:4 = 1Chr 10:4; 21:16, and as characteristic of warriors ח ׳שֹׁלֵף‎ Judg 20:2, 15, 17, 25, 35, 46; 2Sam 24:9; 2Kin 3:26; 1Chr 21:5 (twice in verse); אֶלהַֿח יָדוֺ ׳וַתִּדְבַּק‎ 2Sam 23:10, insert also in || 1Chr 11:13 (Dr).

d. whet, sharpen the sword הח ׳לטשׁ‎ Psa 7:13 (compare Isa 21:15 above), but also שׁנן‎ Deut 32:41 compare Psa 64:4, הוּחַדָּה‎ Ezek 21:14; 21:16.

e. put up the sword into the sheath הח ׳הֵשִׁיב אֶלנְֿדָנָהּ‎ 1Chr 21:27 (sheath of sword is elsewhere תַּעַר‎ 1 Samuel 7:51; 2Sam 20:8; Jer 47:6; Ezek 21:8).

f. slay with sword ׳בח הרג‎ Josh 10:11; 13:22 +; in אנשׁים ימותו‎ 1Sam 2:33, insert בְּחֶרֶב‎ before ᵐ5 אנשׁים‎ We Dr; rarely בח ׳הכה‎ Josh 11:10; 2Kin 19:37 = Isa 37:38; very often לְפִיחֿ ׳הִכָּה‎ smite according to the mouth of the sword i.e. as the sword can devour (2Sam 2:26; 11:25) = without quarter Num 21:24 (E) Deut 13:16; a 20:13; Josh 11:11-12, 14 (all D) Judg 18:27; 21:10; 1Kin 10:25; Job 1:15, 17; also with העיר‎ as sole object Josh 8:24; 10:28, 30, 32, 35, 37, compare 10:39; 19:47; Judg 1:8, 25; 20:37; 1Sam 22:19 (twice in verse); 2Sam 15:14; לפיחֿ ׳הרג‎ Gen 34:26 (J); לפיחֿ ׳החרים‎ Deut 13:16b (including הָעִיר‎ in object), Josh 6:21; 1Sam 15:8, לפיחֿ ׳חלשׁ‎ Exod 17:13 (E); לפיחֿ ׳נפל‎ Josh 8:24; Judg 4:16; י ׳ל֗֗֗פיחֿ׳וַיָּהָם‎ 4:15; — (׳לפיחֿ‎ is chiefly in Joshua and Judges (21 t.), in Hexateuch only J E D; but Job post-exilic).

g. הח ׳אכלה‎ etc., of the sword as devouring: — בָשָׂר חַרְבִּי תּאֹכֵל‎ Deut 32:42 (poetry), 2Sam 2:26; 11:25; 18:8; Isa 1:10; Jer 2:30; 46:10, 14; Nahum 2:14.

h. slain by sword is ׳חֲלַלחִֿ‎ Num 19:16 (P) and frequently Ezekiel, ׳חַלְלֵיחֿ‎ Ezek 31:17-18, 32:21, 25, 28, 29, 30, 32; 35:8 ח ׳מְחֻלְלֵי‎ Ezekiel 35:26 < Co ׳חלליחֿ‎ (on text of 32:20, 22, 31 see Co).

i. ח ׳שְׁבֻיוֺת‎ Gen 31:26 are captives of (taken by) sword.

j. sword frequently agent of ׳י‎ Judg 7:20; Isa 34:6; Jer 12:12; 47:6 etc., — so Deut 28:22, but read חֹרֶב‎ see below I. חרב‎ (compare on other hand 1Sam 17:47); note especially Gen 3:24 הַמִּתְהַמֶּכֶת הח ׳לַהַט‎; in poetry ח ׳לַהַכ‎ Nahum 3:3, ח ׳בְּרַק‎ Deut 32:41.

k. ׳ח‎ figurative of tongue Psa 57:5; compare 59:8; figurative of violence war, etc., Gen 27:40; in simile Prov 5:4 (of grievous end of dealings with strange women).

2 knife ׳ח צֻרִים‎ flint knives for use in circumcision Josh 5:2-3,(see WeSkizzen iii, 166); Ezek 5:1-2,הַגַּלָּבִים ׳ח תַּעַר חַדָּה‎ (according to Co use of sword as razor is significant).

3 of tools used in hewing stone עליה הֵנַפְתָּ חַרְבְּךָ‎ Exod 20:25; בח ׳יִתֹּץ‎ Ezek 26:9, according to most = tools, axes, 'steel' (Co ' Eisen '), but possibly swords as implement ready to hand; 2Chr 34:6 Qr בְּחַרְבֹתֵיהֶם‎ with their tools; read perhaps בְּחָרְבֹתֵיהֶם‎ in their ruins, Be, who compare Psa 109:10; see חָרְבָּה‎ below II. חרב‎ (ᵑ9 ᵐ5‎ omit חרב‎).
