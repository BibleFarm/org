H3892. lach

לַח‎ adjective moist, fresh, new; — ׳ל‎ Gen 30:37; Ezek 21:3; לָ֑ח‎17:24; plural לַחִים‎ Num 6:3 2t.; —

1 moist, fresh, of trees, fruit, etc.; לַח לִבְנֶה‎ Gen 30:37 (J); ל ׳עֵץ‎ Ezek 17:24; 21:3; grapes ל ׳עֲנָבִים‎ Num 6:3.

2 new, of cords, ל ׳יְתָרִים‎ Judg 16:7-8, probably made of fresh sinews of animal (compare Syriac PS1652; GFMon the passage)
