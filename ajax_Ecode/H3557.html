[כּוּל‎] verb comprehend, contain (Late Hebrew, Aramaic id., measure, measure out, of dry or liquid measure; Syriac Aph`el id.; Arabic measure grain) —

Qal Perfect only Isa 40:12 הָאָרֶץ עֲפַר בְּשָׁלִשׁ מִיכָּ֗֗֗ל‎ who hath comprehended the dust of the earth in a shalish-measure?

Pilpel Perfect כִּלְכֵּל‎ 2Sam 19:33; suffix כִּלְכְּלָם‎ 1Kin 18:4; 2masculine singular suffix כִּלְכַּלְתָּם‎ Neh 9:21, etc.; Imperfect יְכַלְכֵּל‎ Zech 11:16 +, etc.; Infinitive construct כַּלְכֵּל‎ Jer 20:9 +, etc.; Participle מְכַלְכֵּל‎ Mal 3:2; — sustain, maintain, contain:

1 sustain, support, nourish, followed by accusative of person Gen 45:11; 50:21 (both E), 2Sam 19:33; 19:34; 20:3; 1Kin 4:7 a (v:b absolute), 5:7; 17:4, 9; Neh 9:21; Ruth 4:15; Psa 55:23; Zech 11:16 (of sheep); followed by accusative of person + of thing (food) Gen 47:12 (J), 1Kin 18:4, 13.

2 contain, followed by accusative of person 1Kin 8:27; 2Chr 2:5; 6:18 (heavens cannot contain ׳י‎); hold in, restrain Yahweh's word within one, Jer 20:9.

3 support, endure, sickness ׳יְכַל מַחֲלֵ֑הוּ אִישׁ רוּחַ‎ Prov 18:14; Mal 3:2 (object בּוֺאוֺ אֶתיֿוֺם‎); of sustaining a cause, in court, ׳יְכַל בְּמִשְׁמָּט דְּבָרָיו‎ Psa 112:5.

1 contain, followed by accusative (of liquid) 1Kin 7:26, 38; 2Chr 4:5; in figure Jer 2:13, absolute Ezek 23:32; (of burnt-offering) 1Kin 8:64; 2Chr 7:7; hold in (wrath of ׳י‎, object not expressed, subject Jeremiah) Jer 6:11 (opposed to שָׁפַךְ‎).

2 sustain, endure, followed by accusative Amos 7:10; Jer 10:10; Joel 2:11.
