I. סוּפָה‎ noun feminine storm-wind (that makes an end ?); — absolute ׳ס‎ Isa 5:28 +; סוּמָּ֫תָה‎ Hosea 8:7 (Ges§ 90f.); suffix סוּפָֽתְךָ‎ Psa 83:16; plural סוּפוֺת‎ Isa 21:1; — strom-wind, ס בְּיוֺם ׳כְּסַעַר‎ Amos 1:14; Nahum 1:3 (|| שְׂעָרָה‎), Isa 17:13 (|| רוּחַ‎), Job 37:9; Isa 21:1, as driving chaff Job 21:18 (verb גָּנַב‎; in simile), simile of rushing chariots Isa 5:28; 66:15; Jer 4:13, of ruin Prov 1:27; compare 10:25; symbolic of ׳י‎s judgments, וְס יִזְרָ֖עוּ ׳רוּחַ יִקְצֹ֑רוּ‎ Hosea 8:7; Psa 83:16 (|| סַעַר‎), Job 27:20 (verb גָּנַב‎), Isa 29:6 ( + מְעָרָה‎).
