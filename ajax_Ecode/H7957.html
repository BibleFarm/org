H7957. shalhebeth

שַׁלְהֶ֫בֶת‎ noun feminine flame (Aramaic שַׁלְהוֺבִיתָא‎ ) — in שׁ ׳לַהֶבֶת‎ Ezek 21:3, of judgment; so שַׁלְהָ֑בֶת‎ Job 15:30; שַׁלְהֶבֶתְיָה אֵשׁ רִשְׁמֵּי‎ Song 8:6 i.e. Yahweh-flame = powerful flame (> JägerBAS i.471 JastrJBL xiii (1894), 111 explains יָֿה‎ here as encl. particle)
