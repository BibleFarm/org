noun masculineGen 39:14 sound, voice; — absolute קוֺל‎ 4:10 + (הַקֹּל‎ 27:22; 45:16); construct קוֺל‎ 3:8 + (וְקֹל‎ Exod 19:16, לְקֹל‎ 4:8 (twice in verse)); suffix קוֺלִי‎ 1Sam 26:17 +, קוֺלְךָ‎ Judg 18:25 +, etc. (קֹלִי‎ Gen 22:18, קֹלֶ֑ךָ‎ Exod 3:18, etc.); plural (usually of thunder) קֹלֹת‎ 9:23 4t., קוֺלֹת‎ 20:18 (Gi 20:15), קֹלוֺת‎ 1Sam 2:17 5t.; —

1 sound: especially

a. of human voice Josh 6:10; 2Kin 7:10; in speech Gen 27:22 (twice in verse); 1Sam 1:13; 24:17; 26:17 (twice in verse); recognized (הִכִּיר‎) 26:17; Jer 18:3; ק ׳יְפֵה‎ Ezek 33:32 pleasant of voice, compare Song 2:14 (twice in verse); 8:13; in singing Exod 32:18; 2Sam 19:36; Isa 52:8, calling Judg 9:7, shouting Exod 32:17; 1Sam 4:6 (twice in verse); 1Kin 1:40; Ezra 3:13, rejoicing Jer 7:34 (4 t. in verse) = 16:9; 25:10; 33:11, praise Psa 66:8; 26:7; Joel 2:10, laughter Jer 30:19, weeping Gen 21:16; Judg 2:4; 1Sam 11:4; Ruth 1:9, 14; Ezra 3:13, outcry Gen 39:15, 18; 1Sam 4:14; Jer 8:19, distress, lament 9:18; Ezek 27:30; Zech 11:3; Psa 102:6, supplication 28:2; 28:6; 31:23; 86:6; adverb ׳ק אֶחָד‎ with one voice Exod 24:3; 2Chr 5:13, ׳ק גָּדוֺל‎ loud voice 2Sam 15:23 + (compare ׳בְּק ׳ג‎ Gen 39:14; 1Sam 28:12 +), ׳ק רָם‎ Deut 27:14; ק ׳נָשָׂא‎ Gen 21:16 + (see נָשָׂא‎ 1b (5)), ק ׳הֵרִים‎ 39:15 + (see דום‎ Hiph`il); on ׳ק‎ as independent noun, followed by subject + verb of calling Psa 3:5; 27:7; 142:2 see Ges§ 144m

b. of ׳י‎ Gen 3:8, 10; Isa 6:8; Exod 19:19; 1Kin 19:3; Micah 6:9; Ezek 10:5 (compare also 2b) +; adverb ׳ק גָּדוֺל‎ Deut 5:19; Ezek 9:1; ׳ק דַקָּה דְּמָמָה‎ 1Kin 19:12.

c. of seraph Isa 6:4.

d. of angel Dan 8:16 (׳ק אָדָם‎ i.e. in human speech, compare Bev); ׳ק דְּבָרָיו‎ 10:6, 9 (twice in verse).

e. of animals: bleating of sheep 1Sam 15:14, lowing of cattle 15:14; Jer 9:9, neighing of horses 8:16, roaring of lion Amos 3:4; Jer 2:15 +, hissing of serpent 46:22 (in simile), singing of birds Nahum 2:8; Zeph 2:14; Psa 104:12; Song 2:12; Eccl 12:4.

f. exclamation, at beginning of clause, a sound of...! = hark! Gen 4:10; Isa 13:4; 40:3; 52:8; Jer 4:15; 10:22; 50:28; Song 2:8; 5:2 (Ges§ 146b).

2 sound:

a. of instrument, especially ׳ק (הַ)שֹּׁפָר‎ Exod 9:16; 20:18 (Gi 20:15) Amos 2:2; 1Kin 1:41 +, וגו ׳חֲצֹצְרוֺת‎2Chr 5:13, כִּנּוֺר‎ Ezek 26:13, עוּנָב‎ Job 21:12; of מַּעֲמֹנִים‎ on high priest's robe Exod 28:35.

b. thunder-clap or peal (compare Dr 1Sam 12:17), י ׳קוֺל‎ (or קוֺלוֺ‎) Amos 1:2; Isa 30:30-31, Jer 10:13 = 51:16, Joel 2:11; Joel 4:16; Psa 18:14 = 2Sam 22:14; Psa 29:3; 29:7; 46:7; Job 37:4 (compare 1Sam 7:10; Psa 77:18; 77:19; 104:7), so especially plural absolute (see above) Exod 9:23, 28, 29, 33, 34; 19:16; 20:18 (Gi 20:15), 1Sam 7:10; 12:17-18, Job 28:26 = 38:25.

c. רַגְלֶיהָ קוֺל‎ 1Kin 14:6 compare 2Kin 6:32, and (probably) Gen 3:8; also ׳ק (הַ)צְּעָדָה‎ 2Sam 5:24 = 1Chr 14:15; of runners 2Kin 11:13; 2Chr 23:12.

d. stamping of hoofs Jer 47:3; Ezek 26:10 ( +chariots, etc.), compare 1Kin 1:41.

e. of chariots Nahum 3:2; Joel 2:5; wheels Ezek 3:13; whip Nahum 3:2.

f. of sea, and great waters Hab 3:10; Ezek 1:24; 43:2; Psa 93:3; 93:4, compare Jer 50:42 (in simile); ׳ק צִנּוֺרֶיךָ‎ Psa 42:8.

g. earthquake Ezek 37:7.

h. a fall Jer 49:21; Ezek 26:15; 31:16.

i. of a multitude 1Sam 4:14; Isa 13:4; Ezek 23:42.

j. din of war Exod 32:17; Jer 50:22, 46; 51:55; Lam 2:7.

k. of wings Ezek 1:24; 3:13; 10:5.

l. flame Joel 2:5; crackling of thorns Eccl 7:6; rustling of leaves Lev 26:36.

m. millstones Jer 25:10; Eccl 12:4.

3 of articulate speech, thing said:

a. (1) often of human advice, command, entreaty Gen 3:17; 4:23; Exod 3:18; 4:1, 9; Deut 1:45; 21:18 (twice in verse); 21:20; 1Sam 2:25; 8:7, 9; 2Sam 12:18; ׳ק דִּבְרֵיכֶם‎ Deut 1:34; 5:25; even of written words 2Kin 10:6; so also ׳ק הָאֹת‎ Exod 4:8 (twice in verse) voice (teaching) of the sign. (2) of a report Gen 45:16; Eccl 10:20. (3) of a proclamation Exod 36:6 (P), 2Chr 24:9; 30:5; 36:22 = Ezra 1:1; 10:7; Neh 8:15. (4) ׳ק אָלָה‎ Lev 5:1 utterance of adjuration.

b. often (especially Deuteronomy, Jeremiah) of words of ׳י‎ Gen 22:18; 26:5; Exod 5:2; 15:26; 19:5; Deut 8:20; 9:23; 13:15; Deuteronomy 13:19 +, Judg 2:2, 20; 6:10; 1Sam 12:14-15, 15:1, 19, 20, 22; Jer 3:13, 25; 7:23 +; of י ׳מַלְאַךְ‎ Exod 23:21-22,. — קֹל‎ Jer 3:9 see below קלל‎.

קֹל‎ noun [masculine] lightness, frivolity (so Vrss Ki Gie; Gf thinks = קָלוֺן‎; > = voice); — זְנוּנָהּ מִקֹּל‎ Jer 3:9. — קֹל‎ voice, see קוֺל‎.
