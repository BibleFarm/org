קָדִים‎ (qâdîym｜kaw-deem'｜noun｜masculine｜East)
[Heb] קָדִים‎ ETCBC: קָדִים‎ (subs｜east) OSHL: s.al.ah TWOT: 1988d GK: H7708 Greek: ἀνατολή, βίαιος, ἐναντίον, ἐναντίος, καύσων, νότος, πνεῦμα
Derivation: or קָדִם‎; from קָדַם‎;
Strong's: the fore or front part; hence (by orientation) the East (often adverbially, eastward, for brevity the east wind)
KJV: east(-ward, wind).

H6921. qadim

קָדִים‎69 noun masculineHosea 13:15 East, east wind; — absolute ׳ק‎ Gen 41:6 +; with ה‎ locative קָדִ֫ימָה‎ Ezek 11:1 +, קָדִ֫מָה‎ 48:4 +; —

1 (apart from Ezekiel) East, especially (הַ)קּ ׳רוּחַ‎ wind of the East, east wind, often as violent and scorching, from desert on southeast, = Arabic sirocco ( eastern), compare DrAmos 4:9: Exod 10:13 (twice in verse) (JE), 14:21 (J), Jer 18:17 (simile), Jonah 4:8; Psa 48:8; also ׳ק‎ (רוּחַ‎ omitted) = east wind Gen 41:6, 23, 27 (E), Hosea 12:2; 13:15; Isa 27:8; Job 15:2; 27:21; 38:24; Psa 78:26. — קָדִימָה‎ Hab 1:9 is dubious, usually eastward, i.e. forward, but text probably corrupt, compare We Now Da, GASm proposes מַקְדִּים‎ (see also [מְגַמָּה‎] p. 169 above). 2 in Ezekiel (52 t.):

a. East הַקּ ׳רוּחַ‎ east wind Ezek 17:10; 19:12; 27:26; east side 42:16 (+ north, south, west); הַקּ ׳שַׁעַר‎ 40:44 (+ north, south); ק ׳מְּאַת‎ 47:18, + west 48:6-7, 8, compare 48:1 (הַיָּם ׳פ ׳ק‎; but read ᵐ5 ׳מִמּ ׳ק פ ׳יְעַד יָמָּה‎ Co Toy and others), + north, south, west, 48:16; קּ הַ ׳דֶּרֶךְ‎ i.e. toward the east 40:10 7t., ׳מִדּ ׳הַקּ‎ 43:2; ׳לַקּ‎ 40:23 eastward (+ North) 41:14; ׳מֵהַקּ‎ 42:9 on the East.

b. קָדִ֫ימָה‎ = East: הַקּ ׳דֶּרֶךְ‎ Ezek 40:6; קָדִימָה גְּבוּל‎ 45:7; 48:21 (+ west); קָדִימָה מְּאַת‎ 47:18, + west 48:3 8t., + north, south, west, 48:32.

c. as adverb קָדִ֫ימָה‎ eastward Ezek 11:1; 44:1; 47:1; + west 45:7 (strike out Berthol Krae), 48:18, + north, south, west, 48:10; 48:17; also (הַ)קָּדִים‎ eastward 40:19; 43:17; 46:1, 12; 47:1, 3, + north 47:2.
