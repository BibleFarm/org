H2394. chozqah

חָזְקָה‎ noun feminine strength, force, violence, always בְּחָזְקָה‎ with strength, force, etc.; —

1 of oppression Judg 4:3 forcibly, violently; capture by force 1Sam 2:16; Ezek 34:4 of ruling ׳בְּח וּבְמָּ֑רֶךְ‎ with force and with rigour.

2 rebuke severely, sharply Judg 8:1; cry mightily, insistently Jonah 3:8. — לֲחָזְקָה‎ 2Kin 12:13 see חָזַק‎
