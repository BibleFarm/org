H7677. shabbathon

שַׁבָּתוֺן‎ noun masculine sabbath observance, sabbatism; — ׳שׁ‎ Exod 16:23 10t. P; usually phrase שַׁבָּתוֺן שַׁבַּת‎ sabbath of sabbatic observance; —

1 of weekly sabbath Exod 31:15; 35:2; Lev 23:3; Exod 16:23 (קֹדֶשׁ שַׁבַּת שַׁבָּתוֺן‎, probably transposed).

2 day of atonement Lev 16:31; 23:32.

3 sabbatical year Lev 25:4; compare שַׁבָּתוֺן שְׁנַת‎ 25:5.

4 ׳שׁ‎ alone of feast of trumpets Lev 23:24, and of first and eighth days of feast of tabernacles 23:39; 23:39.
