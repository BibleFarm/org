H3841. Libnah

לִבְנָה‎ proper name, of a location 1. city in southwest Judah, exact site unknown, captured by Joshua, according to D, Josh 10:29 (twice in verse); 10:31-32, 39; 12:15 (all D), 15:42 (P); Levitical city 21:13 (P) = 1Chr 6:42; further 2Kin 8:22 2Chr 21:10; 2Kin 19:8 = Isa 37:8; 2Kin 23:31; 24:18 = Jer 52:1; ᵐ5‎ Λεβνα. See LagOnom. 274, 135, 26; 2nd ed. 273 Λεβνα; BuhlGeogr. 193.

2 station of Israel in wilderness, between מָּ֑רֶץ רִמֹּן‎ and רִסָּה‎ Num 33:20-21, (perhaps = לָבָן‎ Deut 1:1); ᵐ5‎ Λεμωνα, A ᵐ5‎L Λεβωνα.
