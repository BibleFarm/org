H8203. Shephatyah

or Shephatyahu שְׁפַטְיָה(וּׅ‎ proper name, masculine (׳י‎ judged, vindicated); — usu, Σαφατια(ς):

1 יָה‎-:

a. fifth son of David 2Sam 3:4 = 1Chr 3:3 (Σαβατεια, A Σαφαθια, ᵐ5‎L Σαφατιας).

b. contemporary of Jeremiah Jer 38:1 (B Σαφανιας).

c. man of Judah Neh 11:4.

d. chiefs of families of restoration:-1 Ezra 2:4 = Neh 7:9; Ezra 8:8; -2 2:57 = e. Benjamite 1Chr 9:8.

2 יָהוּ‎-:

a. son of Jehosh.2Chr 21:2.

b. hero of David 1Chr 12:5.

c. Simeonite 1Chr 27:16.
