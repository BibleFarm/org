מִשְׁנֶה‎ (mishneh｜mish-neh'｜noun｜masculine｜double)
[Heb] מִשְׁנֶה‎ ETCBC: מִשְׁנֶה‎ (subs｜second) OSHL: v.em.ad TWOT: 2421c GK: H5467 Greek: διαδέχομαι, διάδοχος, διπλοῦς
Derivation: from שָׁנָה‎;
Strong's: properly, a repetition, i.e. a duplicate (copy of a document), or a double (in amount); by implication, a second (in order, rank, age, quality or location)
KJV: college, copy, double, fatlings, next, second (order), twice as much.

H4932. mishneh

מִשְׁנֶה‎ noun [masculine] double, copy, second; — absolute ׳מ‎ Gen 41:43 +; construct מִשְׁנֵה‎ Jer 16:18 3t., so read also Deut 15:18 (van d. H., > נֶה‎- Gi), Jer 17:18 (Gie Buhl and others); suffix מִשְׁנֵהוּ‎ 1Sam 8:2 +; plural מִשְׁנִים‎ 1Chr 15:18 (1Sam 15:9; Ezra 1:10 see below); —

1 (the) double: מ ׳כֶּסֶף‎ Gen 43:12 (J) money of double amount, compare ׳מ כֶּסֶף‎ 43:15 (J), a double amount (in) money (Ges§ 131q); מ ׳לֶחֶם‎ Exod 16:22 (P); ׳וגו אֲשֶׁר׳מ עַל‎ 16:5 (P) the double above what they gathered daily; שָׂכִיר שְׂכַר מִשְׁנֵה‎ Deut 15:18 (see Dr); ׳מ עֲוֺנָם‎ Jer 16:18 the double of their guilt, 17:18 (see above); a double portion Isa 61:7 (twice in verse); Zech 9:12; Job 42:10.

2 written copy of law Deut 17:18; Josh 8:32 (D).

3 second in order

a. second, rank, הַמּ ׳מִרְכֶּבֶת‎ Gen 41:43 (J), הַמּ ׳רֶכֶב‎2Chr 35:24; of person one second in rank, priest next to high-priest, ׳(הַ)מ‎ 2Kin 23:4; 25:18 = Jer 52:24; לְמ ׳אֶהְיֶהלְּֿךָ‎ 1Sam 23:17, הַמֶּלֶךְ מִשְׁנֵה‎2Chr 28:7, ׳מ לַמֶּלֶךְ‎ Est 10:3; 1Chr 15:18; 16:5; 2Chr 31:12; ׳מ מֵאֶחָיו‎ Neh 11:17. — 1Sam 15:9 read (compare ᵑ7 ᵑ6‎) הַמַּשְׁמַנִּים‎ (Neh 8:10) Th, הַשְּׁמַנִּים‎ or הַשְּׁמֵנִים‎ We Dr Bu, etc. (Ezek 34:16); ׳מִשׁ‎ Ezra 1:10 is apparently corruption of a numeral.

b. second in age, 1Sam 8:2; 17:13; 2Sam 3:3; 1Chr 5:12.

3 second quarter, district, of city, הַמִּשְׁנֶה‎ 2Kin 22:14 2Chr 34:22; Zeph 1:10, compare מִשְׁנֶה הָעִיר‎ Neh 11:9 over the city, namely, the second quarter.
