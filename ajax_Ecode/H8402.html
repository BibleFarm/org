H8402. Tibni

תִּבְנִי‎ proper name, masculine rival of Omri (WeIsrael u. Jüd. Geschichte., 3rd ed., 70, compare Sidon. Thabnit); — 1Kin 16:21-22, (twice in verse); Θαμνει, ᵐ5‎L Θαβεννει.
