H3667. Kna

'an I. כְּנַ֫עַן‎90 proper name, masculine and

of a territory Canaan (ᵐ5‎ Ξανααν, Phoenician כנען‎ = Phoenicia; Ξνα = כנע‎, Hecataeus, see MüllerFr.Hist.Gr.i,17 and others; Egyptian Ka-n-±-na WMMAs.u.Europa,205 ff.; Tel Amarna Kina—na, Kina——i, etc., BezBM Tablets, 150 WklTA 39; etymology dubious GFMPAOS Oct. 1890. lxvii ff.; see also GASmGeogr. 4 f. BuhlGeogr. § 42); — 1 as

proper name, masculine, son of Ham Gen 9:18, 22, 25, 27; 10:15 (as ancestor of Canaanites and Phoenicians; all J); 10:6 (P); 1Chr 1:8, 13 (from Gen 10:6, 15).

2. a. land, west of Jordan, into which Hebrews came, and where they settled, subduing the inhabitants; כְּנַעַן מַלְכֵי‎ Judg 5:19, כ ׳מלך‎ 4:2, 23, 24 (twice in verse); hence כ ׳מַמְלְכוֺת‎ Psa 135:11; כ ׳ישְׁבֵי‎ Exod 15:15 (song in E); compare כ ׳מִלְחֲמוֺת‎ Judg 3:1; כ ׳עֲצַבֵּי‎ Psa 106:38 idols of Canaan, i.e. of the former inhabitants; especially כ ׳אַרְצָה כ, (ֿ) ׳אֶרֶץ‎ Gen 44:8; 46:31; 47:1, 4, 13, 14, 15; 50:5, 13 (all J), 35:6; 42:5, 7, 13, 29, 32; 45:17, 25; Josh 24:3 (all E), Gen 11:31; 12:5 (twice in verse); 13:12 (opposed to הַכִּכָּר עָרֵי‎ vb), 16:3; 17:8 (all P) + 40 t. P, Judg 21:12; 1Chr 16:18 = Psa 105:11; also כ ׳הָאָרֶץ‎ Num 34:2 (P); כ ׳בְּנוֺת‎ Gen 28:1 daughters of Canaan = women of the land, so 28:6; 28:8; 36:2 (all P); כנען‎ is personified Hosea 12:8 = apostate Israel; כ ׳שְׂפַת‎ Isa 19:18 = the Hebrew language (without evil implication).

b. the coast, especially Phoenicia Isa 23:11; compare ׳כ מְּלִשְׁתִּים אֶרֶץ‎ Zeph 2:5.

II. כְּנָ֫עַן‎ noun [masculine] merchant (s) (because Canaanites, especially Phoenicians, were traders); — כְּנַעַן כָּלעַֿם‎ Zeph 1:11; כ ׳אֶרֶץ‎ Ezek 16:29 (omitted by ᵐ5‎ B and others Co), 17:4 a land of merchants; compare כנעני‎ near the end.
