H7071. Qanah

קָנָה‎ proper name

1 of wady between Ephraim and Manass, ק ׳נַחַל‎ Josh 16:8; 17:9 (both P), Κα(ρα)να, Καναι, etc.; identification by Rob BR iii. 135. with Wady Kânah, south and southwest of Nablûs, compare BuhlGeogr. 101, 105.

2. loc in Asher, Josh 19:28, Καν(θ)α(ν), etc.; probably †âna, southeast from Tyre RobBR ii 455 f. Buhl299, Egyptian Ka°nô WMMAs.u.Eur.181, and perhaps Tel Amarna †anû.
