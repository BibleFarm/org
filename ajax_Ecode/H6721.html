H6721. Tsidon

צִידוֺן‎ and (Gen 10:15, 19; 49:3) צִידֹן‎ proper name, of a location Sidon, Σιδων, ancient Phoenician city, on coast north of Tyre (in Assyrian ‚idum( n)u, COTGloss; Tel Amarna ‚iduna, Phoenician צדן‎, Old Aramaic צידן‎; in Egyptian D±-(d)u-na, WMMAs.u.Eur.184); — 'first-born' of Canaan 10:15 = 1Chr 1:13; northern limit of Canaanite Gen 10:19, compare 49:13 defined as ׳צ רַבָּה‎ Josh 11:8 (so 19:28 below); also Judg 1:31; 10:6 (צ ׳אֱהֵֹי‎), 18:28; 1Kin 17:9; named with Tyre Josh 19:28 (compare 19:29), 2Sam 24:6 (compare 24:7), Isa 23:2, 4 and בַּתצֿ ׳בְּתוּלַת‎ 23:12 (compare 23:5 etc.), Jer 25:22; 27:3; 47:4; Ezek 27:8; 28:21-22, (compare 28:2; 28:12), Zech 9:2; Joel 4:4. — See PietschmPhöniz. 54 ff.
