H2104. Zuzim

זוּזִים‎ proper name, of a people an ancient trans-Jordanic people, dwelling in הָם‎ Gen 14:5; otherwise unknown; abode apparently in (later) land of Ammon, between Bashan and Moab; commonly supposed = זַמְזֻמִּים‎ q. v., in that case perhaps text error in one case or the other, compare NöUntersuchungen z. Kritik d. A. T. 162. — (ᵐ5‎ ἔθνη ἰσχυρά). — On proper name, of a location Ζιζα, , between Bosra and Lejûn, compare Kn Di on the passage, TristrMoab 182 ff.
