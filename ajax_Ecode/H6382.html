H6382. pele

מֶּ֫לֶא‎ noun masculine wonder (Late Hebrew id.; as unusual, extraordinary); — ׳פ‎ Exod 15:11 6t.; suffix מִּלְאֲךָ‎ Psa 89:6; מִּלְאֶ֑ךָ‎ 77:12; 88:13, plural מְּלָאִים‎ Lam 1:9; feminine plural מְּלָאוֺת‎ Psa 119:129; Dan 12:6; —

1 wonder: extraordinary, hard to be understood, God's dealings with His people Isa 29:14; the testimonies of the Law Psa 119:129; יוֺעֵץ מֶּלֶא‎ Isa 9:5 marvel of a counsellor (Baer מֵּלֶא‎), wonderful counsellor (of Messianic king); plural as adverb accusative פ ׳וַתֵּרֶד‎ Lam 1:9 she (Jerusalem) hath come down marvelously.

2 wonder: of God's acts of judgment and redemption Psa 77:12; 88:13; 89:6; פ ׳עשׂה‎ Exod 15:11; Isa 25:1; Psa 77:15; 78:12; 88:11; of extraordinary trials הפ ׳קֵץ‎ Dan 12:6.
