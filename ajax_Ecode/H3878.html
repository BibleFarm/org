1. a. Levi, son of Jacob and Leah, as individual, Gen 29:34; 34:25, 30; 35:23; 49:5 (all J); 35:23; 46:11; Exod 1:2; 6:16; 16:16; Num 3:17; 16:1 (all P); 1 Chronicles 21; 1Chr 5:22; 6:1; 6:4; 6:23; 6:28; 6:32; 23:6; Ezra 8:18; so ׳בַּתלֿ‎ [אֶתֿ‎] Exod 2:1 (E), Num 26:59 (P).

b. as head of a family of descendants, in phrase לֵוִי בֵּית‎ Exod 2:1 (E), and (with reference to tribe; late) Numbers 17:23, ל בֵּית ׳מִשְׁמַּחַת‎ Zech 12:13.

c. often ל ׳בְּנֵי(ֿ)‎ Exod 32:26, 28 (E), Josh 21:10 (P), ל ׳בְּנֵי(ֿ)‎ with tribal reference Num 3:15; 4:2; 18:21 (charged with service of tabernacle, and hence to receive tithes; all P); as priests לֵוִי בְּנֵי הַכֹּהֲנִים‎ Deut 21:5; 31:9, compare Mal 3:3, and ל מִבְּנֵי הָיוּ ׳לֹא‎ 1Kin 12:31; from the ל ׳בְּנֵי‎ the Zadokites are selected as priests Ezek 40:46; according to 1Chr 9:18; 23:24, 27 ל ׳בְּנֵי‎ are subordinate officials in temple (compare 23:28f.), compare 24:20; sharply distinguished from priests Ezra 8:15; Neh 12:23 (compare 12:22); so also in later stratum of story of Korah's revolt Num 16:7-8, 10 (P2).

2 as name of tribe, ל ׳שֵׁבֶט‎ Deut 18:1 (priestly tribe), ל ׳מַטֵּה‎ Num 1:49 (in charge of tabernacle), 3:6; 18:2 (ministers unto Aaron); לֵוִי‎ alone = (tribe of) Levi Deut 27:12; 33:8 (earlier poem), Num 26:58 (P), Ezek 48:31; Mal 2:4 (priestly tribe, compare 2:1), 1Chr 21:6; 27:17; ל ׳מַטֵּה‎ =rod of (the tribe of) Levi Numbers 17:18 (P); also (no inheritance, because charged with service of tabernacle) Deut 10:9.
