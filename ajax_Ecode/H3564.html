H3564. kuwr

כּוּר‎ noun [masculine] smelting-pot or furnace (Arabic ; Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic id., ; Ethiopic ) — pot or furnace for smelting metals, but always in metaphor or simile of human sufferings in punishment or discipline: iron (figurative of Egypt as place of bondage) ׳מִכּ הַבַּרְזֶל‎ Deut 4:20; Jer 11:4, כ ׳מִתּוֺךְ ׳הַבּ‎ 1Kin 8:51; gold ׳כ לַזָּהָב‎ Prov 17:3 = 27:21 (|| מַצְרֵף‎); various metals Ezek 22:18, 20, 22; once עֹ֑נִי בְּכוּר‎ Isa 48:10 in a smelting-pot of affliction.
