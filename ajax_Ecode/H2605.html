H2605. Chanan

חָנָן‎ proper name, masculine (gracious; compare Sabean חנן‎, Phoenician חנא‎) —

1 one of the warriors of David 1Chr 11:43.

2 one of the heads of families of Nethinim Ezra 2:46 = Neh 7:49.

3 names of Levites Neh 8:7; 10:11; 13:3.

4 chiefs of the people:

a. Neh 10:23.

b. Neh 10:27.

5 Benjamite name 1Chr 8:23, 38; 9:44 (compare proper name, of a location חָנָן בֵּית‎, Mishna חָנָן כְּפַר‎).

6 הָאֱלֹהִים אִישׁ‎ head of a prophetic guild, חָנָן בְּנֵי‎ Jer 35:4.
