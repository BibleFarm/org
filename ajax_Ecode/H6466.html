פָּעַל‎ (pâʻal｜paw-al'｜verb｜do)
[Heb] פָּעַל‎ ETCBC: פעל‎ (verb｜make) OSHL: q.ce.aa TWOT: 1792 GK: H7188 Greek: ἐνεργέω, ἐπάγω, ἐπιτελέω, ἐργάζομαι, ἔργον, καταρτίζω, κατεργάζομαι, ποιέω, πράσσω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to do or make (systematically and habitually), especially to practise
KJV: commit, (evil-) do(-er), make(-r), ordain, work(-er).

H6466. paal

מָּעַל‎57 verb do, make (in poetry for עָשָׂה‎) (Late Hebrew מּוֺעֵל‎ workman, compare Jewish-Aramaic; Phoenician פעל‎ = Biblical Hebrew (often); Arabic id.; Old Aramaic; פעל‎; Syriac in derived species and derivatives ); —

Qal Perfect3masculine singular Deut 32:27 +, אֵל פָ֫עַל‎ Num 23:23, 2masculine singular מָּעַלְתָּ‎ Exod 15:17 +, etc.; Imperfect3masculine singular יִפְעַל‎ Job 22:17 +; 2 masculine singular תִּפְעָלֿ‎ 35:6, etc.; Infinitive construct לְפָעְָלָם‎ 37:12 (see below); Participle active מֹּעֵל‎ Psa 15:2+, plural construct מֹּעֲלֵי‎ 5:6 +, etc.; —

1 do:

a. of God, with accusative (abstract) Num 23:23; Deut 32:27; Job 33:29, יְשׁוּעוֺת‎ Psa 74:12; + ל‎ person 31:20; 68:29; Isa 26:12; Job 22:17; with accusative of congnate meaning with verb מֹּעַל‎ Psa 44:2; Hab 1:5; accusative omitted Isa 43:13, compare 41:4.

b. of men, with accusative Psa 11:3; Job 11:8, + ל‎ person (God) 7:20, + ב‎ person (God) 35:6; especially of doing evil, שָׁ֑קֶר‎ Hosea 7:1, עָוֶל‎ Job 34:32, עַוְלָה‎ 36:23; Psa 58:3; 119:3, אָוֶן‎ Prov 30:20, especially אָוֶן מֹּעֲלֵי‎ Hosea 6:8; Isa 31:2; Job 31:3; 34:8, 22; Psa 5:6 15t. in Psalms, Prov 10:29; 21:15, רָע מֹּעֲלֵי‎ Micah 2:1; rarely of doing right, with accusative מִשְׁמָּטוֺ‎ Zeph 2:3, צֶדֶק‎ Psa 15:2.

c. of clouds, with accusative Job 37:12 (disregarding ֑֯‎).

2 make;

a. of God, with accusative concrete Exod 15:17; + ל‎ of thing Prov 16:4, ל‎ of 2nd object Psa 7:16; מֹּעֲלִי‎ Job 36:3 my Maker.

b. of man, with accusative concrete Isa 44:12, 15; accusative omitted 44:12; Psa 7:16. — Isa 1:31 see מֹּעַל‎.
