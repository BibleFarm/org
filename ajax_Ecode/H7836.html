שָׁחַר‎ (shâchar｜shaw-khar'｜verb｜look early)
[Heb] שָׁחַר‎ ETCBC: שׁחר‎ (verb｜look for) OSHL: v.cp.ac TWOT: 2369 GK: H8838 Greek: ἐπιμελῶς, ζητέω, ὀρθρίζω, πρώϊμος
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: properly, to dawn, i.e. (figuratively) be (up) early at any task (with the implication of earnestness); by extension, to search for (with painstaking)
KJV: (do something) betimes, enquire early, rise (seek) betimes, seek diligently) early, in the morning).
