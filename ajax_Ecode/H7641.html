שִׁבֹּל‎ (shibbôl｜shib-bole｜noun｜feminine｜ear｜flowing stream)
[Heb] שִׁבֹּל‎, שִׁבֹּלֶת‎ ETCBC: שִׁבֹּלֶת‎ (subs｜grain), שִׁבֹּלֶת‎ (subs｜stream) OSHL: v.as.ad, v.as.ac TWOT: 2316b 2316c GK: H8672, H8673 Greek: καλάμη, κλάδος, στάχυς
Derivation: or (feminine) שִׁבֹּלֶת‎; from the same as שֹׁבֶל‎;
Strong's: a stream (as flowing); also an ear of grain (as growing out); by analogy, a branch
KJV: branch, channel, ear (of corn), (water-)flood, Shibboleth. Compare סִבֹּלֶת‎.

H7641. shibbol

or shibboleth I. שִׁבֹּ֫לֶת‎ noun feminine flowing stream; — ׳שׁ‎ absolute Psa 69:3, as test of dialect Judg 12:6 (compare סִבֹּלֶת‎ and GFM); construct ׳שׁ מַיִם‎ Psa 69:16, ׳שׁ הַנָּהָר‎ Isa 27:12.

II. שִׁבֹּ֫לֶת‎ noun feminine ear of grain; — ׳שׁ‎ absolute Job 24:21 +; plural שִׁבֳּלִים‎ Gen 41:5 +, construct שִׁבֳּלֵי‎ Zech 4:12; — ear, Gen 41:5-6, 7: 41:7; 41:22; 41:23; 41:24 (twice in verse); 41:26-27, Isa 17:5 (twice in verse); Ruth 2:2; Job 24:24; transf. spike (Pusey), end of olive bough Zech 4:12.
