H1179. Baal

Chermon חֶרְמוֺן בַּ֫עַל‎ proper name, of a location Judg 3:3; 1Chr 5:23, a city so named as seat of the worship of Baal. 'The crest of Hermon is strewn with ruins and the foundations of a circular temple of large hewn stones,' TristrTpg, compare on sacredness, Euseb. LagOnom 217: possibly = גד בעל‎ q. v.
