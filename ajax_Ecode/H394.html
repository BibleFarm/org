H394. akzari

אַכְזָרִי‎ adjective cruel, always in this form; — Prov 12:10; 17:11; Jer 6:23; 50:42, also 30:14 (read א ׳מוּסָר‎, for מוּסַר ᵑ0‎, Gf Gie); as substantive Prov 5:9; 11:17, perhaps also, in apposition with י ׳יוֺם‎, Isa 13:9.
