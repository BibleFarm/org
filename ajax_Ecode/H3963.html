H3963. lethek

לֶ֫תֶךְ‎ noun [masculine] a barley-measure (Late Hebrew id., a corn-measure); only in וְל ׳חֹמֶרשְֿׂעֹרִים שְׂעֹרִים‎ Hosea 3:2; according to ᵑ9‎ = 1/2 כֹּר‎ = 1/2 חֹמֶר‎ (q. v.), compare Mishna (LevyNHWB ii. 531), but this tradition is uncertain (compare NowArchaeology i. 203); ᵐ5‎ νέβελ οἴνου, for ׳ל ׳שׂ‎.
