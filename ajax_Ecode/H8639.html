H8639. tardemah

תַּרְדֵּמָה‎ noun feminine deep sleep; — absolute ׳ת‎ Gen 2:21 +; construct תַּרְדֵּמַת‎ 1Sam 26:12; — deep sleep, usually with נָפַל‎ + עַל‎ person, and usually by supernatural agency: י ׳וַיַמֵּל ׳א ׳ת עַל‎ Gen 2:21 (J; ᵐ5‎ ἔκστασις), ׳ת עַל נָָֽפְלָח‎ 15:12 (J; ᵐ5‎ id.), Job 4:13 = 33:15 (ᵐ5‎ [δεινὸς], φόβος), so י ׳תַּרְדֵּמַת‎ 1Sam 26:12 (ᵐ5‎ θάμβος, result of slothfulness (על‎ and person omitted) Prov 19:15; figurative for insensibility of spirit, י עֲלֵיכֶם ׳נָסַךְ ת ׳רוּחַ‎ Isa 29:10.
