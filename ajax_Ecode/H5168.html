נָחְ֑נוּ נַ֫חְנוּ,‎ pronoun 1 plural we (abbreviated from אֲנַחְנוּ‎; compare the forms , נַחְנָא‎, just cited; also Assyrian nîni) Gen 42:11; Exod 16:7-8, Num 32:32; Lam 3:42. (In 2Sam 17:12 נַחְנוּ‎ is 1 plural perfect Qal from נוּחַ‎.)
