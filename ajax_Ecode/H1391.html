H1391. Gibon

גִּבְעוֺן‎ proper name, of a location — ׳ג‎ Josh 9:17 +; with ָ  ה‎ locative; גִּבְע֫וֺנָה‎ 2Sam 2:12 compare 1Kin 3:4; — Levitical city in tribe of Benjamin; formerly inhabited by Hivites Josh 9:17; 10:2, 4, 5, 10, 12, 41; 11:19; 18:25; 21:17; 2Sam 2:12-13, (pool of Gibeon) 2:16; 3:30; 20:8; Jer 28:1; 41:12 (great waters which are in Gibeon) 41:16; 1Chr 14:16; see especially ג ׳אַנְשֵׁי ג, ׳יוֺשְׁבַי‎ Josh 9:3, (called Hivites 9:7) 10:1, 6; 11:19; Neh 3:7, so ג ׳בְּנֵי‎ 7:25 (= גִבָּר בְּנֵי‎ Ezra 2:20, see גִּבָּר‎); compare also as proper name, masculine 1Chr 8:29 גִבְעוֺן אֲבִי יָָֽשְׁבו וּבְגִבְעוֺן‎ = 9:35; compare ג ׳מִדְבַּר‎ 2Sam 2:24, בְּג ׳עֵמֶק‎ Isa 28:21; it was the site of great Bamah 1Kin 3:4-5, 9:2, where was 'the tabernacle of Yahweh in the high place' according to 1Chr 16:39; compare 21:20; 2Chr 1:13 (twice in verse).
