H4713. Mitsri

מִצְרִי‎ adjective, of a people Egyptian; — masculine singular ׳מ‎ Gen 39:1 16t.; feminine singular מִעְרִית‎ 16:1 3t.; masculine plural מִצְרִים‎ 12:12 5t.; feminine plural מִצְרִיֹּת‎ Exod 1:19; —

1 adjective מ ׳אִישׁ‎ = an Egyptian Gen 39:1; Exod 2:11, 19; Lev 24:10; 1Sam 30:11; 2Sam 23:30; הַמּ ׳הָאִישׁ‎ 1Chr 11:23; מ ׳נָעָר‎ 1Sam 30:13, מ ׳עֶבֶד‎ 1Chr 2:34; מִצְרִית שִׁפְחָה‎ Gen 16:1, הַמִּצְרִיֹּת כַּנָּשִׁים‎ Exod 1:19.

2 with article = substantive the Egyptian Gen 39:2, 5; Exod 2:12, 14; 2Sam 23:30 (twice in verse); 1Chr 11:23 (twice in verse); so once without article מִצְרִי‎ Deut 23:8; הַמִּצְרִי‎ once (late) collective= the Egyptians Ezra 9:1 (where with Canaanitish peoples); plural = the Egyptians, הַמִּצְרִים‎ Gen 12:12, 14; 43:22; Deut 26:6; Josh 24:7; feminine singular הַמִּצְרִית הָגָּר‎ Hagar the Egyptian woman Gen 16:3; 21:9; 25:12.
