H2530. chamad

חָמַד‎ verb desire, take pleasure in (Aramaic חֲמַד‎ (not in Syriac); Arabic praise, eulogize, appove of; Sabean חמדם‎ in gratitude [praise] there-for DHMZMG 1875, 595; 1876, 686) —

Qal Perfect ׳ח‎ Psa 68:17; Prov 12:12; חָֽמְדוּ‎ 1:22; וְחָֽמְדוּ‎ consecutive Micah 2:2; חֲמַדְתֶּם‎ Isa 1:29; Imperfect יַחְמֹד‎ Exod 34:24; 2masculine singular תַּחְמֹד‎ 20:16 (twice in verse) + 3t. (including Prov 6:25 jussive); suffix וָאֶחְמְדֵם‎ Josh 7:21; וְנֶחְמְדֵ֫הוּ‎ Isa 53:2; Passive participle חֲמוּדוֺ‎ Psa 39:12; Job 20:20, חֲמוּדֵיהֶם‎ Isa 44:9; — desire:

a. in bad sense of inordinate, ungoverned, selfish desire, followed by accusative Exod 20:17 (twice in verse) (E) = Deut 5:18 (5:18 b has תִּתְאַוֶּה‎), Exod 34:24 (JE) Deut 7:25; Josh 7:21 (JE) Micah 2:2; Prov 12:12; of lustful desire 6:25.

b. = take pleasure in, of idolatrous tendency Isa 1:29, so participle plural חֲמוּדֵיהֵם‎ 44:9 their delightful things (things in which they delight, see also 44:11 and BevJPhoenician xvii. 1888. p. 126); חָֽמְדוּ לָצוֺן לֵצִים‎ Prov 1:22 how long do scorners delight in scoring.

c. less often in good sense, said of God א חָמַד ׳הָהָר לְשִׁבְתּוֺ‎, poetic Psa 68:17; object the suffering servant of ׳י‎, Isa 53:2 no beauty in him, that we should desire him (choose him, be drawn toward him); passive participle collective חֲמוּדוֺ‎ Psa 39:12 his desired things, i. e. chosen, choice, desirable (see Niph`al); so probably Job 20:20.

Niph`al Participle נֶחְמָד‎ Gen 2:9 2t.; הַנֶּחֱמָדִים‎ Psa 19:11; — desirable: לְמַרְאֶה נ ׳עץ‎ Gen 2:9, ׳נ לְהַשְׂכִּיל‎ 3:6, ׳הַנֶּח מִזָּהָב‎ Psa 19:11 which are more desirable than gold; נֶחְמָד אוֺצָר‎ Prov 21:20 desirable, i.e. costly treasure.

Pi`el Perfect1singular delight greatly ח ׳בְּצִלּוֺ וְיָשַׁ֫בְתִּי‎ Song 2:3 in his shadow I greatly delighted and sate, i.e. sate with great delight.

[חֲמוּדָה‎] noun feminine desirableness, preciousness — Plural absolute חֲמוּדוֺת‎ Dan 9:23; חֲמוּדֹת‎ Ezra 8:37; חֲמֻדוֺת‎2Chr 20:25 4t.; חֲמֻדֹת‎ Gen 27:15; construct חֲמֻדוֺת‎ Dan 11:43; — desirable, choice things i.e. garments Gen 27:15 (|| בגדים‎); compare Dan 11:38 (|| יקרה אבן‎), 11:43 (מִצְרַיִם חֲמֻדוֺת‎); = precious things Ezra 8:27 (as gold); ח ׳כלי‎2Chr 20:25; ח ׳לֶחֶם‎ Deut 10:3; of man only ׳אִישׁחֿ‎ Dan 10:11, 19, and, without ׳ח אָ֫תָּה אישׁ,‎ 9:23 thou art a precious treasure (on construction see Dr§ 189, 2; see Bev and compare negative חמד‎ Isa 53:2).
