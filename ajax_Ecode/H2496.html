H2496. challamish

חַלָּמִישׁ‎ noun masculine flint; — חַלָּמִישׁ‎ Deut 8:15 3t.; construct חַלְמִישׁ‎ 32:13; — flint הַח ׳צוּר‎ rock of flint = flinty rock, whence water flowed for Israel in desert 8:15, and (no צור‎) Psa 114:8; worked by miners Job 28:9; hyperb. oil out of ׳ח צוּר‎ Deut 32:13 flint of rock = rocky flint (poetry); — in simile of firmness כַּחַלָּמִישׁ מָּנַי שַׂמְתִּי עַלכֵּֿן‎ Isa 50:7.
