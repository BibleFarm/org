H2717. charab

I. [חָרֵב‎] verb be dry, dried up (compare יָבֵשׁ‎) (Aramaic חֲרוֺב‎ be dry, חוּרְבָּא‎ drought); —

Qal Perfect3plural חָֽרְבוּ‎ Gen 8:13 (twice in verse), Isa 19:6; Imperfect3masculine singular יֶחֱרַב‎ Hosea 13:15 2t., וַיֶּחֱרָ֑ב‎ Psa 106:9; Imperative feminine singular חֳרָ֑בִי‎ Isa 44:27; — be dry, dried up:

1 of ground, be freed from waters of flood ׳ח חָאֲדָמָה מְּנֵי‎ Gen 8:13b (J).

2 of waters, be dried up, taken away; of waters of flood Gen 8:13 a (P); of Nile וְיָבֵשׁ יֶחֱרַב וְנָהָר‎ Isa 19:5 (|| מֵהַיָּם וְנִשְּׁתוּמַֿיִם‎), see same combination Job 14:11 (|| מִנִּייָֿם מַיִם אָֽזְלו‎); Isa 19:6 (|| דָּֽלֲלוּ‎) 44:27 (|| הוֺבִישׁ‎); of Red Sea Psa 106:9; figurative of Ephraim's freshness and vigour Hosea 13:15.
