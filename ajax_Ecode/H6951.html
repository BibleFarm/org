קָהָל‎123 noun masculineEzek 38:15 assembly, convocation, congregation; — absolute ׳ק‎ Jer 31:8 +; construct קְהַל‎ Exod 12:6 +; suffix קְהָלָ֑ךָ‎ Ezek 38:13, etc.; plural suffix קְהָלֶיךָ‎ 38:7; —

1 assembly specially convoked:

a. for evil counsel, || סוד‎ Gen 49:6 (poem); ׳ק מרעים‎ Psa 26:5; for civil affairs Prov 5:14 (see Toy), 26:26; Job 30:28 (Di publice).

b. for war or invasion, Num 22:4 (E), Judg 20:2; 21:5, 8; 1Sam 17:47; Ezek 16:40; 38:7 (plural only here), + 8 t. Ezekiel; ׳ק גדול‎ (|| רב חיל‎) 38:15; ׳ק רב‎ (|| גדול חיל‎) 17:17; 38:4; ׳ק גוים‎ Jer 50:9; ׳ק עמים‎ Ezek 23:24; 32:3.

c. company of returning exiles, Jer 31:8; Ezra 2:64 = Neh 7:66.

d. for religious purposes, to hear words of ׳י‎ at Horeb Deut 5:19, הק ׳ביום‎ 9:10; 10:4 (gloss, not in ᵐ5‎), 18:16; word of Jeremiah Jer 26:17; 44:15; for feasts, fasts and worship 2Chr 20:5; 30:25 (twice in verse); Neh 5:13; Joel 2:16; Psa 107:32; ׳ק רב‎ 22:26 4t.; ק֗֗֗ ׳וַיֵּאָֽסְפו מְאֹד לרב‎2Chr 30:13; ׳ק גדול‎ 1Kin 8:65 2Chr 7:8; (ה)ק ׳בתוך‎20:14; Psa 22:23.

2 congregation, as organized body:

a. of Israel: ׳ק יהוה‎ Micah 2:5; Num 16:3; 20:4 (P), 1Chr 28:8; (׳י‎) ׳באבק‎ Deut 23:2; 23:3 (twice in verse); 23:4 (twice in verse); 23:9; Neh 13:1; Lam 1:10; ק ׳כל ישׂראל‎ Deut 31:30; Josh 8:35 (D)Lev 16:17 (P) 1Kin 8:14 (twice in verse); 8:22, 55 2Chr 6:3 (twice in verse); 6:12-13, 1Kin 12:3; 1Chr 13:2; ק ׳כל ישׂראל עדת‎ Exod 12:6 (ᵐ5‎ reads בני‎ before ׳יִשׂ‎), Num 14:5 (P; ᵐ5‎ συναγωγή; ᵑ0‎ probably conflation); הק עם ׳כל‎ Lev 16:33; הק ׳כל‎ Exod 16:3 (P) 1Chr 13:4 12t. Chronicles; ׳הק‎ Lev 4:13-14, 21 8t. Numbers (P) 8 t. 2 Chronicles

b. restored community in Jerusalem Ezra 10:12, 14; Neh 8:2, 17; ׳ק הֵגּוֺלָה‎ Ezra 10:8; later the better part of it, ׳ק חסידים‎ Psa 149:1.

c. of angels, ׳ק קדשׁים‎ Psa 89:6.

d. more General: company, assembled multitude, ׳ק גוים‎ Gen 35:11 (P), ׳ק עמים‎ 28:3; 48:4 (P), ׳ק רפאים‎ Prov 21:16.
