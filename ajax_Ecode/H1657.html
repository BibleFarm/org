(P has land רעמסס‎ 47:11 etc., compare Di 45:10.

2 הַגּשֶׁן ארץ‎ Josh 11:16 (D), גּשֶׁן אֶרֶץ‎ 10:41 (D), גּשֶׁן‎ 15:51 (P) (ᵐ5‎ in all Γοσομ) a city named with חֹלֹן‎ & גִּלֹה‎ compare Di. — not clearly located, but on southern border of Judah.
