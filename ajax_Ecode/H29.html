[pn] אֲבִיָּה‎, אֲבִיָּהוּ‎ ETCBC: אֲבִיָּה‎ (nmpr｜Abijah), אֲבִיָּהוּ‎ (nmpr｜Abijah) OSHL: a.ae.ai GK: H31, H32 Greek: Ἀβιά
Derivation: or prolonged אֲבִיָּהוּ‎ ; from אָב‎ and יָהּ‎; father (i.e. worshipper) of Jah;
Strong's: Abijah, the name of several Israelite men and two Israelitesses
KJV: Abiah, Abijah.

H29. Abiyyah

אֲבִיָּ֫הוּ‎ proper name, masculine & feminine (Yah(u) is (my) father) — so 2Chr 13:20-21, = אֲבִיָּם‎ 1Kin 14:31; 15:1, 7 (twice in verse); 15:8 (ᵐ5‎ Ἀβιου, Ἀβια); = אֲבִי‎ 2Kin 18:2 (ᵐ5‎ Ἀβου, Ἀβουθ); = אֲבִיָּה‎ 1Sam 8:2 22t. —

1 king of Judah, son & successor of Rehoboam 1Kin 14:31; 15:1, 7 (twice in verse); 15:8; 1Chr 3:10; 2Chr 11:20, 22; 12:16; 13:1-2, 3, 4, 15, 17, 19, 20, 21, 22; 2 Chronicles 13:23.

2 2nd son of Samuel 1Sam 8:2; 1Chr 6:13.

3 son of Jerob. I 1Kin 14:1.

4 son of Becher, a Benjamite 1Chr 7:8.

5 head of a priestly house 1Chr 24:10.

6 id. Neh 10:8; 12:4, 17.

7 wife of Hezron 1Chr 2:24.

8 mother of Hezekiah 2Kin 18:2; 2Chr 29:1.
