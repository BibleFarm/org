אֵזוֺר‎ noun masculineIsa 5:27 waistcloth (Late Hebrew id., Arabic , compare RSJQ Jan. 1892, 289 ff.) — ׳א‎ absolute Job 12:18 8t; construct 2Kin 1:8 4t.; — of skin 1:8 (prophet.); of linen Jer 13:1 (see Che) compare 13:2; 13:4; 13:6; 13:7; 13:7; 13:10; 13:11 (symbolic of Israel & Judah cleaving to ׳י‎); of Assyrian warriors' waistcloth,'girdle of loins' Isa 5:27; in wall-images of Chaldeans, א ׳חֲגוֺרֵי בְּמָתְנַיִם‎ Ezek 23:15 (compare 2Kin 1:18 see below אָזַר‎) so figurative of Yahweh's power over kings בְּמָתְנֵיהֶם אֵזוֺר וַיֶּאְסֹר‎ Job 12:18 (׳א‎ = fetter, compare Di; but according to RSl.c. reference to slaves' garment); metaphor of righteousness & faithfulness Isa 11:5 (twice in verse) (חֲלָּצָיו׳א ׳א, מָתְנָיו‎).
