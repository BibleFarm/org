H356. Elon

II. אֵלוֺן‎ proper name, masculine (= id. compare אֵלָה‎ proper name)

1 a son of Zebulun Gen 46:14; Num 26:26.

2 אֵילֹן‎ Gen 26:34; אֵיִלוֺן‎ 36:2 a Hittite, father-in-law of Esau.

3 אֵילוֺן‎ Judg 12:11-12, a judge of Israel, of tribe of Zebulun

4. proper name, of a location אֵילוֺן‎ Josh 19:43 a town of Dan; so 1Kin 4:9 (where read ׳א ח ׳וּבֵית‎ Th Klo).
