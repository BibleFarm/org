H6103. atslah

עֵצְלָה‎ noun feminine sluggishness; — ׳י‎ Prov 19:15; dual intensive עֲצַלְתַּיִם‎ Eccl 10:18 double (i.e. great) sluggishness (so Thes De and others; read then perhaps עֵצְלָתַיִם‎; > from עָצֵל‎ a sluggish pair, i.e. hands Ew and others), but improbable; || יָדַיִם שִׁפְלוּת‎, whence Bi conjectures עֵצְלוּת‎, Siegf יָדַיִם עֵצְלַת‎.
