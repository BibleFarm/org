H1075. Bikri

בִּכְּרִי‎ proper name, masculine (youthful) —

1 a Benjamite 2Sam 20:1-2, 6, 7, 10, 13, 21, 22; only in phrase בֶּןבִּֿכְּרִי שֶׁבַע‎. 2 perhaps

adjective, of a people plural with article הַבִּכְּרִים‎ 2Sam 20:14 the Bichrites (i.e. family of Sheba`), הַבֵּרִים ᵑ0‎; compare ᵐ5‎ ἐν Ξαρρει = (ב)כרי‎; so Klo Dr.
