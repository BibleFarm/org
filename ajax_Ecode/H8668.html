H8668. teshuah

תְּשׁוּעָה‎ ( תְּשֻׁעָה‎ 2Sam 19:3) noun feminine deliverance, salvation = יְשׁוּעָה‎ (formed by false analogue, as if from √ שׁוע‎, in sense of ישׁע‎; most assign it to שׁוע‎, but no sufficient evidence for existence of such a √; compare Köii. 200) — absolute ׳ת‎ 1Sam 11:9 19t.; construct Jer 3:23 5t.; suffix תְּשׁוּעָתִי‎ Isa 46:13 2t.; תְּשׁוּעָֽתְךָ‎ Psa 40:11 2t.; תְּשׁוּעָתֶ֑ךָ‎ 40:17; 71:15; —

1 deliverance, usually by God, through human agency, especially from oppression 1Sam 11:9, and in battle 1Chr 19:12 (הוֺשׁיע‎ in || clause), = victory Judg 15:18 (ת ׳נָתַתָּ וגו ׳בְּיַד‎), 2Kin 5:1, ת ׳עשׂה ׳י‎ 1Sam 11:13 ׳י‎ wrought a great victory, so 19:5; 2Sam 19:3; 23:10, 12; 1Chr 11:14 (as accusative of congnate meaning with verb after הושׁיע‎), Prov 21:31; Psa 33:17 (נושׁע‎ in || 33:16); ת ׳הַנּוֺתֵן למלכים‎ 144:10; so in phrase ׳חֵץתֿ ליהוה‎ 2Kin 13:17, ׳חֵץתֿ בַּאֲרָם‎ 13:17; of national deliverance, from exile, ת יִשְׂרָאֵל ׳נוֺשַׁע עולמים‎ Isa 45:17 (DaSynt.:§ 67 b), 46:13 (|| צְדָקָה‎), 46:13 (with נתן‎; || תִּפְאֶרֶת‎); in General of national success and prosperity Jer 3:23; Prov 11:14 = 24:6; of deliverance from personal trouble Psa 37:39; Lam 3:26 (י ׳תְּשׁוּעַת‎) or of national deliverance under figure of personal Psa 40:11 (|| אֱמוּנָָֽתְךָ‎), 40:17; 71:15 (|| צְדָקָח‎), so ׳י תְּשׁוּעָתִי‎ Psalm 38:23; contrasted with ׳י‎s deliverance is אָדָם תְּשׁוּעַת‎ deliverance of (through) man Psalm 60:13 = 108:13, compare 146:3.

2 more exclusively spiritual in sense, = salvation, appear to be תְשׁוּעָתִי אֱלֹהֵי‎ Psa 51:16; 119:41 (|| חֲסָדֶךָ‎), Psalm 51:81, and ת יִלְבְּשׁוּ ׳כֹּהֲנֶיךָ‎2Chr 6:41 (from יֵשַׁע אלברשׁ כהניה‎ Psa 132:16).
