H6552. Pirathon

מִּרְעָתוֺן‎ proper name, of a location in Ephraim (? = height; compare Sabean proper name יהפרע‎ he makes lofty Osl.c.); — Judg 12:15, Φαραθωμ, A ᵐ5‎L Φρααθων; perhaps modern Far±atâ, approximately 6 miles WSM. from Nablus, compare BuhlGeogr. 206 (GASmGeogr.353 proposes top of Wady Farah northeast from Nablus).
