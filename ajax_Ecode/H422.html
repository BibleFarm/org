H422. alah

II. אָלָה‎ verb swear, curse (compare I. אלה‎ ? so Thes LagOr ii. 3) —

Qal Perfect 1Kin 8:31 ( = 2Chr 6:22 all Vrss Th Bö Bä Kp reading וְאָלָה‎); אָלִית‎ Judg 17:2; Infinitive absolute אָלֹה‎ Hosea 4:2; אָלוֺת‎ 10:4.

1 swear, take oath before God 1Kin 8:31 ( = 2Chr 6:22); in covenants Hosea 4:2; 10:4 (falsely).

2 curse Judg 17:2.

Hiph`il Imperfect וַיּאֶֹל‎ 1Sam 14:24; Infinitive לְהַאֲלֹתוֺ‎ 1Kin 8:31 2Chr 6:22 adjure, put under oath.
