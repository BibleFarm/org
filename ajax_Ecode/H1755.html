1 period, age, Generation, mostly poetic:

a. of duration in the past, former age(s) עוֺלָמִים דּוֺרוֺת‎ Isa 51:9 (|| ימי קדם‎); וָדֹר דֹּר שְׁנוֺת‎ Deut 32:7 (song; || עולם ימות‎); וָדֹר בְּדֹר‎ Psa 90:1 (compare 90:2); דּוֺרוָֿדוֺר מוֺסְדֵי‎ Isa 58:12 (|| עולם חרבות‎); וָדוֺר דּוֺר שֹׁמֲמוֺת‎ 61:4 (|| id.)

b. usually of duration to come, future age(s), דֹּר לְדֹר‎ Exod 3:15 (E; || לעולם‎) compare Prov 27:24 Kt (Qr וָדוֺר לְדוֺר‎; || id.); דֹּר מִדֹּר‎ Exod 17:16 (E), = לָדוֺר מִדּוֺר‎ Isa 34:10; וָדֹר לְדֹר‎ Psa 10:6; 33:11 (|| לעולם‎) 49:12; 79:13; 85:6; 102:13; 119:90; 135:13; 146:10; Joel 4:20 (all || id.); 89:2 (|| עולם‎) 89:5 (|| עדעֿולם‎) 106:31 (|| id.) Isa 34:17 (|| id.); Psa 77:9 (|| לנצח‎); וָדֹר עַדדֹּֿר‎ 100:5 (|| לעולם‎) Isa 13:20 (|| לנצח‎) Jer 50:39 (|| id.); וָדֹר בְּכָלדֹּֿר‎ Jeremiah 45:18 (|| ועד לעולם‎) compare Est 9:28; וָדֹר דֹר כְּמוֺ שְׁנוֺתָיו‎ Psa 61:7 compare וָדוֺר דּוֺר שְׁנֵי‎ Joel 2:2 (|| העולם‎); as modifying phrase וָדוֺר דּוֺר מְשׂוֺשׂ‎ Isa 60:15 (|| עולם נאון‎); further דּוֺרִים דּוֺר‎ Psa 72:5; דּוֺרִים לְדוֺר יְשׁוּעָתִי‎ Isa 51:8 (|| לעולם‎).

c. apparently including both past and future שְׁנוֺתֶיךָ דּוֺרִים בְּדוֺר‎ Psa 102:25; וָדֹר דּוֺר בְּכָל‎ 145:13; וָדוֺר לְדוֺר‎ Lam 5:19.

2 of men living at a particular time (period, age), generation, as transitory בָּא וְדוֺר הֹלֵךְ דּוֺר‎ Eccl 1:4: specifically

a. in the present, and (or) the past Gen 7:1 (J) Exod 1:6 (P) Num 32:13 (JE) Deut 1:35; 2:14; Judg 2:10 (twice in verse); Isa 53:8 (compare Che critical note) Jer 2:31; also Psa 95:10; Job 8:8; Isa 41:4.

b. Judg 3:2 (present & future); compare plural דֹּרֹתָיו‎ Gen 6:9 (i.e. his own Generation and those immediately contiguous, before and after).

c. especially of a future Generation Gen 15:16 (JE), with numeral, compare Deut 23:3; 23:4; 23:9; also 29:21; Psa 48:14; 71:18; 78:4; 78:6; 102:19; 109:13; Joel 1:3 compare Psa 22:31; Psalm 22:32 (Checritical note); of a succession of Generations מַעֲשֶׂיךָ יְשַׂבַּח לְדוֺר דּוֺר‎ 145:4; וָדוֺר בְּכָלדּֿוֺר‎ Est 9:28; usually plural עוֺלָם לְדֹרֹת‎ Gen 9:12 (P); with number דּוֺר לְאֶלֶף‎ Psa 105:8 (|| עולם‎) = 1Chr 16:15 (|| לעולם‎); Deut 7:9; see further, of posterity, דֹּרוֺת אַרְבָּעָה‎ Job 42:16; usually plural with suffix Gen 17:7, 9, 12; Exod 12:14, 17, 42; 16:32-33, 27:21; 29:42; 30:8, 10, 21, 31; 31:13, 16; 40:15; Lev 3:17; 6:11; 7:36; 10:9 (all P); 17:7; 21:17; 22:3; 23:14, 21, 31, 41, 43; 24:3; 25:30 (all H); Num 9:10; 10:8; 15:14-15, 21, 23, 38; 18:23; 35:29; Josh 22:27-28, (all P).

3 generation characterized by quality or condition, class of men: עִקֵּשׁ דּוֺר‎ crooked Generation Deut 32:5 (song) compare 32:20; Psa 78:8 (twice in verse); Jer 7:29; of different classes of wicked, Prov 30:11-12, 13, 14 compare Psa 12:8; of the righteous, as a class 14:5; 24:6; 73:15 (דּוֺר בָּנֶיךָ‎) 112:2 (compare 22:31 ᵑ0‎, but < Che joins לדור‎ to Psalm 22:32 compare above below 2c); so also אֲבוֺתָיו דּוֺר‎ i.e. the dead 49:20 (so most; yet see below below 4).

4 dwelling-place, habitation Isa 38:12 compare דּוּר‎ verb, so Saad Ki Ges De Che RVm (Ew life || חַיַּי‎); so also Psa 49:20 De Witt, compare CheOP 479.

דָּר‎ noun [masculine] generation (Biblical Hebrew דּוֺר‎); — absolute in phrase וְדָו עִםדָּֿר‎ Daniel 3:33; Dan 4:31 (is) with Generation and Generation, i.e. endures Generation after Generation.
