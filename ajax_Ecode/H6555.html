H6555. parats

I. מָּרַץ‎ verb break through (Late Hebrew id.; Jewish-Aramaic מְּרַץ‎ id.; perhaps Assyrian parâƒu (see DlHWB); Arabic cut, slit, also hit); —

Qal Perfect3masculine singular מָּרַץ‎ 2Sam 5:20 +; 2 masculine singular suffix מְּרַצְתָּנוּ‎ Psa 60:3, etc.; Imperfect3masculine singular יִפְרֹץ‎ Exod 1:12; 19:22, יִרְְָץֿ‎ v 24, זַיִּפְרֹץ‎ Gen 30:30 +, etc.; Infinitive absolute מָּרֹץ‎ Isa 5:5; construct מְּרֹץ‎2Chr 31:5, מְּרוֺץ‎ Eccl 3:3; Participle active מֹּרֵץ‎ Micah 2:13; Eccl 10:8; passive feminine singular מָּרוּצָה‎2Chr 32:5; Prov 25:28; masculine plural מְּרֻצִים‎ Neh 4:1 + מָּרוּצִים הֵם‎ 2:13 Qr (> Kt המפרוצים‎ ); — break through:

1 break or burst out, from womb Gen 38:29 (J; with accusative of congnate meaning with verb); from enclosure Micah 2:13.

2 break through, down (from without), with accusative גָּדֵר‎ Isa 5:5; Psa 80:13; 89:41; Eccl 10:8; חוֺמָה‎ Nehemiah 3:35; 2Chr 26:6, compare passive participle Neh 2:13; 2Chr 32:5; with בְּחוֺמָה‎ = make a breach in 2Kin 14:13 2Chr 25:23; מְּרוּצָה עִיר‎ Prov 25:28; absolute, opposed to בָּנָה‎ Eccl 3:3; הַמֹּרֵץ‎ Micah 2:13; הַמְּרוּצִים‎ Neh 4:1 the part broken down.

3 break into, with accusative י ׳בֵּית‎2Chr 24:7.

4 break open, a mining shaft Job 28:4.

5 break up, break in pieces, with accusative מַעֲשֶׂיךָ‎2Chr 20:37 (|| אֳנִיּוֺת וַּיִּשָּֽׁבְרוּ‎).

6 break put (violently) upon, of ׳י‎ in sudden judgment, with accusative of person 2Sam 5:20 = 1Chr 14:11; Psa 60:3; c, ב‎ person Exod 19:22, 24 (J), I Chron 15:13, so of plague Psa 106:29; of ׳י‎, with accusative of congnate meaning with verb מֶּרֶין‎ + ב‎ person 2Sam 6:8 = 1Chr 13:11; with accusative of congnate meaning with verb + suffix Job 16:14.

7 use violence absolute Hosea 4:2.

8 break over [limits], increase, לָרֹב וַיִפְרֹץ‎ Gen 30:30 (J), 1Chr 4:38; + ה‎ locative Gen 28:14 (J); absolute 30:43 (J), Exod 1:12 (J; + רָבָה‎), Hosea 4:10; Isa 54:3, בָּאָרֶץ מָּרַץ‎ Job 1:10.

9 burst open, intransitive, of wine-vats Prov 3:10 (with accusative of material תִּירוֺשׁ‎).

10 spread, i.e. become known, of הַדָּבָר‎2Chr 31:5. — 1Chr 13:2 is probably corrupt, see Kau, who (after SS) conjectures נֶחֱרָצָה‎; ׳פ‎ in 2Chr 11:23 is apparently distributive (with מִן‎ partitive) but dubious. — 1Sam 28:23; 2Sam 13:25, 27; 2Kin 5:23 see פצר‎.

Niph`al Participle נִפְרָ֑ץ חָזוֺן אֵין‎ 1Sam 3:1 no vision spread abroad (i.e. General, or frequent, compare Qal 10; || י ׳דְּבַר יָקָר הָיָה‎, but text dubious.

Pu`al Participle feminine מְפֹרָ֑צֶת‎ Neh 1:3 (of wall).

Hithpa`el Participle plural הַמִּתְמָּֽרְצִים‎ 1Sam 25:10 slaves who break away, each from (מִמְּנֵי‎) his master.
