H2674. Chatsor

חָצֹר חָצוֺר,‎ proper name, of a location (LagBN 47 f.) — ᵐ5‎ Ασωρ, etc.;

1 a royal city of northern Canaan Josh 11:1 מֶלֶךְחֿ ׳יָבִין‎, compare 12:19, and Judg 4:2, 17; 1Sam 12:9; Josh 11:10 (twice in verse); 11:11, 13; 19:36; 2Kin 15:29; חָצֹר‎ 1Kin 9:15.

2 places in the Negeb:

a. Josh 15:23.

b. c. Josh 15:25 חָצוֺר הִיא חֶצְרוֺן וּקְרִיּוֺת חֲדַתָּה וחָצוֺר‎.

3 חָצוֺר‎ Neh 11:33 a Benjamite town, probably = חָצוֺר בַּעַל‎ q. v.

4 נְבוּכַדְרֶאצַּו֯ר הִכָּה אֲשֶׂר חָצוֺר מַמְלְכוֺת‎ Jer 49:28, compare 49:30; 49:33, an Arabian locality, see WinerBW Hazor Gf Gie. — ח ׳עֵין‎ Josh 19:37, see below עַיִן‎.
