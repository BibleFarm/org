H5219. nekoth

נְכאֹת‎ noun feminine a spice, (usually understood of tragacanth gum, exuding from astralagus bush, DiGen 27:25 and references; NowArchaeology i. 64 (and 249); compare also Löw§ 24; but = Arabic = summitas rubicunda sive flores plantae (i.e. a certain plant, Frey), according to HomAufsätze 4); — absolute ׳נ‎ 37:25 ( + לּט צְרִי,‎ as merchandise), 43:11 (+ id., also שְׁקֵדִים בָּטְנִים, דְּבַשׁ,‎, as gift).

נכד‎ (√ of following, meaning uncertain; Ethiopic = gens, stirps, [compare Samaritan נגד‎ Gen 21:23]).
