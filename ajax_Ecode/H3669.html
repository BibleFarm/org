[pn] כְּנַעַנִי‎, כִּנְעָן‎, כְּנַעֲנִי‎ ETCBC: כְּנַעֲנִי‎ (adjv｜Canaanite), כִּנְעָנִי‎ (subs｜tradesman) OSHL: k.cd.ae, k.cd.af TWOT: 1002a 1002b GK: H4048, H4050, H4051

Strong's: a Kenaanite or inhabitant of Kenaan; by implication, a pedlar (the Canaanites standing for their neighbors the Ishmaelites, who conducted mercantile caravans)
