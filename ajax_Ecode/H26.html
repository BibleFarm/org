H26. Abigayil

אֲבִיגַ֫יִל‎ proper name, feminine (my father is joy (?) originally אבגל‎? (אֲבִגֵל‎? compare ᵑ0‎ below & NöZMG 1883, 537 Anm2).

1 wife of Nabal, then of David 1Sam 25:14, 23, 39, 40, 42; 27:3; 30:5; 2Sam 2:2; 1Chr 3:1; = אֲבִגַיִל‎ 1Sam 25:3, 36 אֲבִו֯גַיִל‎ 25:18, אֲבִיגָל֑‎ 25:32, אֲבִיגַ֯לִ‎ 2Sam 3:3.

2 sister of David 1Chr 2:16-17, = אֲבִיגַל‎ 2Sam 17:25.

אבוגיל‎ Kt 2Sam 25:18 see אֲבִיגַיִל‎ below II. אבה‎.
