H7171. qerets

קֶ֫רֶץ‎ noun masculine dubious word, apparently nipping, concrete nipper usually interpreted (since Hi) of a nipping or stinging insect (compare Aramaic קָרוֺצָא‎, Arabic biting insect); e.g. gadfly (compare ThesAdd. 111), figurative of Nebuchadr. ׳ק בָּא מִצָּפוֺן‎ Jer 46:20 a gadfly cometh from the north; ᵑ9‎ (Aq Symm) stimulator, i.e. קֹרֵץ‎; Thes and others, more Generally, excidium.
