H4993. Mattithyah

or Mattithyahu מַתִּתְיָה מַתִּתְיָ֫הוּ,‎ proper name, masculine (compare GrayProp. N. 294, No. 81); — ᵐ5‎ Ματταθιας, etc; —

1 a Levitical musician, מַתִּתְיָ֫הוּ‎ 1Chr 15:18, 21; 25:3, 21 = מַתִּתְיָה‎ 16:5.

2 מַתִּתְיָה‎, Levitical name 1Chr 9:31.

3 ׳מ‎, attendant of Ezra (also Levite ?) Neh 8:4.

4 one of those who took strange wives Ezra 10:43.
