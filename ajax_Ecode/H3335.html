[Heb] יָצַר‎, יׄוצֵר‎ ETCBC: יצר‎ (verb｜shape), יֹוצֵר‎ (subs｜potter) OSHL: j.cv.aa TWOT: 898 GK: H3450, H3670 Greek: γίνομαι, ἵστημι, κατασκευάζω, κεραμεύς, κέραμος, κτίζω, πλάσσω, ποιέω, συντάσσω
Derivation: probably identical with יָצַר‎ (through the squeezing into shape); (compare יַצַע‎);

1 of human activity:

a. of a potter who forms out of clay a vessel Isa 29:16; 41:25; Jer 18:4 (twice in verse); 18:6 (twice in verse); 1Chr 4:23; Lam 4:2; Zech 11:13 (twice in verse) (these last two according to Thes SS et al. error for אוֺצָר‎); (ה)י ׳כְּלִי‎ potter's vessel 2Sam 17:28; Psa 2:9; Jer 19:11; הי ׳חֹמֶר‎ potter's clay Isa 29:16; י ׳נֵבֶל‎ 30:14; י ׳בַּקְבֻּק‎ Jer 19:1 הי ׳בית‎ 18:2-3,.

b. of a carver of wood, graven images Isa 44:9-10, 12; Hab 2:18 (twice in verse).

c. frame, devise in the mind ׳י עָמָל‎ Psa 94:20.

2 of divine activity:

a. (as a potter) forming Adam out of עפר‎ from אדמה‎ Gen 2:7-8, (J), beasts and birds, also from אדמה‎ 2:19 (J); Israel as a people Isa 27:11; 43:1, 21; 44:21; 45:9 (twice in verse); 45:11; 64:7, even from the womb 44:2, 24; the servant of Yahweh from the womb 49:5; of the formation of the individual man 43:7; Jeremiah in the womb Jer 1:5; the eye of man Psa 94:9; the locust Amos 7:1; Leviathan Psa 104:26; the dry land 95:5; the earth Isa 45:18 (twice in verse); the mountains Amos 4:13; הַכֹּל‎ Jer 10:16 = 51:19. Figurative perhaps lost sight of in some of the above, and quite certainly in the forming of light Isa 45:7, of summer and winter Psa 74:17, the רוּחַ‎ of man Zech 12:1, and the לב‎ of men Psa 33:15.

b. figurative for frame, pre-ordain, plan (in divine purpose), of a situation מֵרָחוֺק‎ Isa 22:11; of an occurrence קֶדֶם מִימֵי‎ 37:26 = 2Kin 19:25; אֶעֱשֶׂנָּה אַף יָצַרְתִּי‎ I have planned, I will also do it 2Ki 46:11; ׳י על רעה‎ Jer 18:11; ׳י לַהֲכִינָהּ‎ devised it to establish it 33:2.
