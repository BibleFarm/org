H7823. shachis

or sachish סָחִישׁ‎ noun [masculine] grain that shoots up of itself in 2nd year (√ unknown); — || סָפִיחַ‎, 2Kin 19:29 (= שָׁחִיס‎ || Isa 37:30); on use of this for food, compare Straboxi. 4, 3 (of Albanians).
