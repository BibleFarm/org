H529. emun

אֵמֻן‎ noun [masculine] trusting, faithfulness (on formation compare Ges§ 84a R. 12). 1. 1 בָּם אֵמֻן לֹא בָּנִים‎ children in whom there is no trusting Deut 32:20 (poetry) 2. אֱמוּנִים‎ plural abstract faithfulness; א ׳צִיר‎ messenger of faithfulness, trusty messenger Prov 13:17; א ׳עֵד‎ faithful witness 14:5; compare א ׳אִישׁ‎ 20:6; א ׳שֹׁמֵר‎ keeping faithfulness Isa 26:2, perhaps also Psa 31:24 א ׳נצד‎ see I. [ אָמַן‎].
