H1009. Beth

Arebel ַארְבֵֿאל בֵּית‎ proper name, of a location Hosea 10:14, perhaps Arbel near Pella (east of Jordan) Jerome Euseb. in LagOnom. 88, 2nd ed. 123; 214, 2nd ed. 236, compare Now on the passage; but in Galilee RobBR ii. 399, modern Irbid, compare FurrerZPV ii. 1879, 57 f.; BdPal 257.
