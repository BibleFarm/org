H6870. Tseruyah

צְרוּיָה‎ proper name, feminine Σαρουια: mother of Abishai, Joab and Asahel; — ׳צ‎ 1Sam 26:6 +, צְרֻיָה‎ 2Sam 14:1; 16:10; 23:37; — according to 1Chr 2:16 a she was David's sister; called יוֺאָב אֵם‎ 2Sam 17:25; elsewhere after construct בֶּןֿ‎ (בְּנֵי‎): ׳בֶּןצֿ‎ of Abishai 1Sam 26:6; 2Sam 16:9; 18:2; 19:22; 21:17; 1Chr 18:12; of Joab 2Sam 2:13; 8:16 = 1Chr 18:15; 2Sam 14:1; 23:18 and 23:37 = 1Chr 11:39; 1Kin 1:7; 2:5, 22; 1Chr 11:6; 26:28; 27:24; plural of all three 2Sam 2:18; 1Chr 2:16; Abishai and Joab 2Sam 3:39; 16:10; 19:23.
