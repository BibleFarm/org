H6290. Paran

מָּארָן‎ proper name, of a location Paran, Φαραν (compare proper name פארן‎ Sinaitic Inscription); — usually פ ׳מִדְבַּר‎ home of Ishmael Gen 21:21 (E), traversed by Israel in Exodus Num 12:16 (E), 10:12; 13:2, 26 (all P); it lay northeast from (the traditional) Sinai, with Gulf of Akaba and the ± Arabah as its east border; in the same region was פ ׳הַר‎ Deut 33:2; Hab 3:3, — perhaps coast-range of mountains along west shore of Gulf of Akaba; ׳פ‎ alone 1Kin 11:18 (twice in verse) (between Midian and Egypt); Deut 1:1 (location dubious). — 1Sam 25:1 see II. מָעוֺן‎ below עון‎. — See RobBR i, 177 f. PalmerDesert of Exodus, 284 ff., Di DrDt.
