H1381. Gebal

גְּבָל‎ proper name, of a location (= Arabic , Γεβαληνη) mountainous region south of Dead Sea, ᵑ7‎ Seir, compare JosAnt, ii. 1, 2; ix. 9, 1; Psa 83:8 ועמלק ועמון גבָל‎; — modern Jibâl; Seetzen:ii. 357 BurckhTravels 401 RobBR ii. 154.

גבן‎ (probably be curved, contracted, coagulated; Syriac Pa`el coagulate; Arabic is be timid, perhaps from shrinking, cowering).
