b. כְּפַעַםבְּֿפַעַם‎ as time on time, i.e. as formerly, as usual, Num 24:1 (E) Judg 16:20; 20:30-31, 1Sam 3:10 (compare Dr) 20:25.

c. בַּמַּעַם (גַּם) הַוּאֹת‎ at this repetition (this time) also Exod 8:28; 9:14 (J) + 3 t.; בַּמּ ׳גַּם הַהִוא‎ Deut 9:19; 10:10.

d. הַמַּעַם‎ (Hexateuch only J): (1) = this once Gen 18:32, הַמּ ׳אַךְ‎ Exod 10:17; Judg 6:39, ׳רַקהַֿמּ‎ 6:39, הַמּ ׳אַךְ הַזֶּה‎ 16:28 (הַזֶּה‎ dubious, see above); (2) = now at lenth Gen 2:23; 29:34-35, 30:20; 46:30; Judg 15:3; 16:18, compare also Exod 9:27 now at length (it is clear that) I have sinned.

e. מָּ֑עַם ֗֗֗ מַּעַם‎ Prov 7:12 now ... now, at one time ... at another.
