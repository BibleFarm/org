אָדֹם‎ adjective red — ׳א‎ Isa 63:2; Zech 1:8 + Gen 25:30 (twice in verse), see below; אָדוֺם‎ Song 5:10; feminine אֲדֻמָּה‎ Num 19:2, plural אֲדֻמִּים‎ 2Kin 3:22 2t.; — ruddy, red, of man Song 5:10; horse Zech 1:8 (twice in verse) (|| שָׂרֹק‎ compare Assyrian DlW 87) 6:2 (|| שָׁחֹר‎); heifer Num 19:2; water 2Kin 3:22 (׳א כַּדָּם‎); compare as substantive red, redness on garment Isa 63:2; הָאָדֹם‎ = the (red) lentils Gen 25:30 (twice in verse), but read הָאֱדֹם‎ see below; compare also מַעֲלֶה‎.
