עָשָׂר‎ (ʻâsâr｜aw-sawr'｜noun｜either masculine or feminine｜ten)
[Heb] עָשָׂר‎, עֶשְׂרֵה‎, עֲשֶׂרֶת‎ ETCBC: עֶשְׂרֵה‎ (subs｜-teen), עָשָׂר‎ (subs｜-teen) OSHL: p.gb.ac TWOT: 1711b GK: H6925, H6926, H6930
Derivation: for עֶשֶׂר‎;
Strong's: ten (only in combination), i.e. -teen; also (ordinal) -teenth
KJV: (eigh-, fif-, four-, nine-, seven-, six-, thir-) teen(-th), [phrase] eleven(-th), [phrase] sixscore thousand, [phrase] twelve(-th).

H6240. asar

or esreh עָשָׂר‎203, עֶשְׂרֵה‎144

noun ten, only after units to make numbers 11-19, both cardinal and ordinal; עֶשְׂרֵה‎ with

noun feminine, עָשָׂר‎ with

noun masculine, thus:

1 eleven:

a. עָשָׂר אַחַד‎ (with masculine noun) Gen 32:23 2t.

b. עֶשְׂרֵה אַחַת‎ (with feminine noun) 2Kin 23:36 7t.; also = eleventh 1Kin 6:38; 2Kin 9:29.

c. עָשָׂר עַשְׁתֵּי‎ (with masculine noun) Num 29:20; ordinal Deut 1:3 7t.

d. עֶשְׂרֵה עַשְׁתֵּי‎ (with feminine noun) Exod 26:7 4t.; ordinal Jer 1:3-4t.

2 twelve:

a. עָשָׂר (ם) שְׁנֵי‎ (with masculine noun) Gen 35:22 80t. ordinal 1Kin 19:19 12t.

b. עֶשְׂרֵה שְׁתֵּי(ם)‎ (with feminine noun) Exod 24:4 31t.; ordinal 2Kin 8:25 6t.

3 thirteen:

a. עָשָׂר שְׁלשָׁה‎ (with masculine noun) Num 29:14 2t.; ordinal Est 3:12 7t.

b. עֶשְׂרֵה שְׁלשׁ‎ (with feminine noun) 1Kin 7:1 9t.; ordinal Gen 14:4 2t.

4 fourteen:

a. עָשָׂר אַרְכָּעָה‎ Gen 46:22 13t.; ordinal Exod 12:6 17t.

b. עֶשְׂרֵה אַרְבַּע‎ Gen 31:41 5t.; ordinal 2Kin 18:13 3t.

5 fifteen:

a. עָשָׂר חֲמִשָּׁה‎ Hosea 3:2-3t.; ordinal Exod 16:1 14t.

b. עָשָׂר חֲמֵשֶׁת‎ Judg 8:10; 2Sam 19:18.

c. עֶשְׂרֵה חֲמֵשׁ‎ 2Kin 14:17 9t.; ordinal 14:23; 2Chr 15:10. — See also עֶשֶׂר‎

2d. 6. sixteen:

a. עָשָׂר שִׁשָּׁה‎ Exod 26:25 6t.; ordinal 1Chr 24:14 2t.

b. עֶשְׂרֵה שֵׁשׁ‎ Gen 46:18 13t.

7 seventeen:

a. עָשָׂר שִׁבְעָה‎ 1Chr 7:11 2t.; ordinal Gen 7:11 3t.

b. עֶשְׂרֵה שְׁבַע‎ Gen 37:2 4t.; ordinal 1Kin 22:52; 2Kin 16:1. — See also עֶשֶׂר‎

3d. 8. eighteen:

a. עָשָׂר (שְׂמֹנַת) שְׁמֹנָה‎ Gen 14:4 10t.; ordinal 1Chr 24:15; 25:25.

b. עֶשְׂרֵה שְׁמֹנֶה‎ Judg 3:14 6t.; ordinal 1Kin 15:1 8t.

9 nineteen:

a. עָשָׂר תִּשְׁעָה‎ 2Sam 2:30; ordinal 1Chr 24:16; 25:26.

b. עֶשְׂרֵה תְּשַׁע‎ Gen 11:25; Josh 19:38; ordinal 2Kin 25:8 = Jer 52:12. These numbers usually take singular of the nouns most often used, חֹדֶשׁ יוֺם, שָׁנָה,‎, compare (sometimes) אַמָּךְ אִישׁ,‎, etc.; otherwise plural; rarely (late usage) they follow noun, which is then usually plural, e.g. Num 29:30; Josh 15:51 (P), Ezra 8:35 (twice in verse); Est 3:13; 8:5, etc. (even עֶשְׂרֵה שְׁתֵּים שָׁנִים‎ Nah 5:14), but שָׁנָה‎ precedes ordinal 1Kin 6:38, שְׁנַת‎ 15:1 8t., so יוֺם‎ (c. ordinal) 2Chr 29:17 5t. Esther 9; also יוֺם‎ and שָׁנָה (שְׁנַת)‎ both precede and following ordinal Num 7:72, 78; 2Kin 8:25; 9:29; 14:23; 16:1; Jer 32:1; 2Kin 25:8 = Jer 52:12. — See also Köii. 1. 211 ff. DaSynt. § 37 a, also R. 1, 2. Ges§§ 92d, e, 134, and especially HernerSynt. d. Zahlw-rter im AT {1893}; on form in הֵ֯‎ (prob for יַ֯‎), Ol§ 110 Köii. 1, 427 WSG 138.
