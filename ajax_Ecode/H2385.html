H2385. chaziz

[חֲזִיז‎] noun [masculine] thunder-bolt, lightning flash (Late Hebrew חֲזִיז‎; Aramaic חֲזִיזָא‎ shining cloud) — only construct חֲזִיז‎ and plural חֲזִיזִים‎; קֹלוֺת לַחֲזִיז וְדֶרֶךְ‎ Job 28:26 and a way for thunder-bolts = 38:25; ׳י חֲזִיזִים עשֶֹׁה‎ Zech 10:1 (|| גֶּשֶׁם מְטַרֿ‎).
