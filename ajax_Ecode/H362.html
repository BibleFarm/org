H362. Elim

[אֵילִים‎], always אֵילִם‎ proper name, of a location (= place of terebinths or other great trees, see Di Gen 14:6 & Exod 15:27 (12 fountains & 70 palms)) 2nd station of Israel in desert after passing sea 16:1 (twice in verse); Num 33:9-10, אֵי֫לִמָה‎ Exod 15:27; Num 33:9; probably = Wady Gharandel compare RobBR i. 100, 105.
