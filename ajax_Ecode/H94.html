H94. Agur

אָגוּר‎ proper name, masculine (perhaps hireling, Arabic , Aramaic אֲגִירָא‎, see PS, Assyrian agîru, compare HptBAS i. 124; others gatherer, from I. אגר‎) son of יָקֶה‎, an author of proverbs Prov 30:1.
