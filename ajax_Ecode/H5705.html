עַד‎35 preposition and conjunction even to, until (Biblical Hebrew III. עַד‎; so ᵑ7‎, Syriac, Old Aramaic, Nabataean, Palmyrene, Egyptian Aramaic (Lzb336 S-CA 5 +)); —

1 preposition:

a. of space, even to Dan 7:13; עַדכָּֿא‎ 7:28.

b. of amount, up to Ezra 7:22 (4 t.).

c. of time, until Dan 2:20; 6:15; 6:27; 7:18 (twice in verse); 7:26; Ezra 4:24; 5:16 וְעַדכְּֿעַן‎; = our against 6:15 (compare Biblical Hebrew, p. 724:a top); to (the end of) = during Dan 6:8 תְּלָתִין יוֺמִין עַד‎, 6:13; 7:12, 25. On עַדאָֿחֳרָן‎ 4:5 see אָחֳרָן‎; and on דִּי עַדדִּֿבְרַת‎ 4:14 see [ דִּבְרָה‎].

2 conjunction:

a. עַדדִּֿי‎ until, followed by perfect (of past time) Dan 2:34; 4:30; 5:21; 7:4, 9, 11, 22; followed by imperfect (of future) 2:9; 4:20; 4:22 (twice in verse); 4:29. — 6:25 וג שְׁלִטוּ דִּי עַד ֗֗֗ מְטוֺ ׳לָא‎ up to the time that . . . , i.e. ere that.

b. עַד‎ alone, Ezra 4:21; 5:5.
