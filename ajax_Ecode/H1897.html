[Heb] הָגָה‎ ETCBC: הגה‎ (verb｜mutter) OSHL: e.aj.aa TWOT: 467 GK: H2047
Derivation: a primitive root (compare הָגִיג‎);
Strong's: to murmur (in pleasure or anger); by implication, to ponder
KJV: imagine, meditate, mourn, mutter, roar, [idiom] sore, speak, study, talk, utter.

H1897. hagah

I. הָגָה‎ verb moan, growl, utter, speak, muse (only poetry) (onomatopoetic; Late Hebrew הָגָה‎ muse, speak, spell a word, so Aramaic הֲגָא‎; muse, especially Ethpa`al; Arabic satirize, insult, scold, also spell (borrowed meaning)) —

Qal Perf.2masculine singular וְהָגִ֫יתָ‎ consecutive Josh 1:8; 1singular הָגִ֫יתִי‎ Psa 143:5, וְהָגִיתִי‎ consecutive 77:13; Imperfect יֶהְגֶּה‎ Job 27:4 8t.; 3 feminine singular תֶּהְגֶּה‎ Psa 35:28 2t., אֶהְגֶּה‎ 63:7; Isa 38:14, יֶהְגּוּ‎ Psa 2:1 + 2t., תֶּהְגּוּ‎ Isa 16:7; נֶהְגֶּה֑‎ 59:11; Infinitive absolute הָגוֺ‎ 59:11; —

1 of inarticulate sounds:

a. growl, of lion growling over prey, followed by על‎ Isa 31:4.

b. groan, moan, in distress (like dove), absolute, Isa 38:14; 59:11 (נֶהְגֶּה הָגוֺ‎); sigh for (לְ‎) in sorrow, mourning, moan for 16:7 (|| לְ יְיֵלִיל‎), so also Jer 48:31.

2 utter, followed by accusative of thing, Psa 38:13; subject לָשׁוֺן‎ Job 27:4 (|| דִּבֶּר‎) Psa 35:28; 71:24; Isa 59:3; subject מֶּה‎ Psa 37:30; Prov 8:7 — compare also below Po. below; speak (absolute) Psa 115:7 (בְּ‎ instrumental)

3. a. (soliloquize) meditate, muse, with בְּ‎ of thing, Josh 1:8; Psa 1:2; 63:7; 77:13; 143:5; with accusative Isa 33:18, subject לֵב‎.

b. imagine, devise, with accusative Psa 2:1; Prov 24:2 (subject לֵב‎); with Infinitive 15:28 (subject id.)

Po. Infinitive absolute only דִּבְרֵישֶֿׁקֶר מִלֵּב וְהֹגוֺ הֹרוֺ‎ Isa 59:13 a conceiving and an uttering, out of the heart, lying words (|| וְסָרָה עשֶׁק דַּבֵּר‎); — on form compare Köi. 555; but read rather וְהָגוֺ הָרוֺ‎

Qal Infinitive absolute compare Di; BaNB 77 retains ᵑ0‎ & explanation as Qal Infinitive passive
