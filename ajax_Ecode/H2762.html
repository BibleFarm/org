חֲרַכִּים‎ noun [masculine] plural lattice or other opening through which one may look (Aramaic ᵑ7 חֲרַכָּא‎ Josh 2:15 and elsewhere=Hebrew חַלּ֖וֺן‎; but Late Hebrew חֲרָךְ‎ is an opening smaller than a window) — only מִןהַֿח ׳מֵצִיץ‎ Song 2:9 peeping in at the lattice (|| מִןהַֿחַלֹּנוֺת מַשְׁגִּיחַ‎).
