H2900. Tobiyyahu

or Tobiyyah טוֺבִיָּה טוֺבִיָּ֫הוּ,‎ proper name, masculine (Yah(u) is my good) —

1 טוֺבִיָּהוּ‎ a Levite, assigned to reign of Jehoshaphat 2Chr 17:8, not ᵐ5‎; ᵐ5‎L Τωβιας, elsewhere ᵐ5 טוֺבִיָּה‎ Τωβια(ς).

2 Ammonite adversary of Nehem. Neh 2:10, 19; Nehemiah 3:35; 4:1; 6:1, 12, 14, 17 (twice in verse); 6:19; 13:4, 7, 8.

3 head of a family of returning exiles of doubtful lineage Ezra 2:60 = Neh 7:62.

4 a chief of returning exiles Zech 6:10, 14, ᵐ5‎ χρησίμων(+?)αὐτῆς, i.e. טוֺבֶיהָ‎.
