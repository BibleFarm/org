[חָקַר‎] verb search (Late Hebrew id., Niph`al passive, Aramaic חֲקַר‎) —

Qal Perfect3masculine singular suffix וַחֲקָרוֺ‎ consecutive Prov 18:17; חֲקָרָהּ‎ Job 28:27; וְחָֽקַרְתָּ֫‎ Deut 13:15 etc.; Imperfect יַחְקֹר‎ Job 13:19; יַחֲקָרֿ‎ Psa 44:22; יַחְקְרֶנּוּ‎ Prov 28:11; אֶחְקֹר‎ 1Sam 20:12; תַּחְקְרוּן‎ Job 32:11; נַחְקֹ֑רָה‎ Lam 3:40 etc.; Imperative חָקְרֵ֫נִי‎ Psa 139:23; חִקְרוּ‎ Judg 18:2; Infinitive חֲקֹר‎ 2Sam 10:3 2t.; לַחְקוֺר‎ Prov 23:30; לְחָקְרָהּ‎ Judg 18:2; —

1 search (for), followed by accusative Prov 23:30; Job 32:11; absolute הֵיטֵיב וְשָׁאַלְתָּ֫ וְחָקַרְתָּ֫ וְדָרַשְׁתָּ֫‎ Deut 13:15; literal Ezek 39:14 (i.e. for bones unburied).

2. a. search through, explore, literally, object הארץ‎ Judg 18:2 (|| רַגֵּל‎) 18:2, 1Chr 19:3 (|| id.); city העיר‎ 2Sam 10:3 (|| id.), of mining Job 28:3; search out a subject or matter 5:27; 28:27; Prov 25:2; Psa 44:22.

b. search a man, find out his sentiments (AV sound him) 1Sam 20:12.

c. of ׳י‎ searching man, with accusative Psa 139:1 (|| ידע‎), 139:23 (|| id.) Job 13:9, compare Jer 17:10 (object לֵב‎).

d. of examining thoroughly, so as to expose weakness in a case Job 29:16; Prov 18:17, compare 28:11; so of self-examination ׳ח דרכים‎ Lam 3:40 (|| חפשׂ‎).

Niph`al Perfect נֶחְקַר‎ 1Kin 7:47; 2Chr 4:18; Imperfect יֵחָקֵר‎ Jer 46:23; יֵחָֽקְרוּ‎ 31:37 be searched out, found out, ascertained, 1Kin 7:47; 2Chr 4:18 (weight of the bronze used in temple utensils), of forest Jer 46:23; searched out, 31:37 subject ארץ מוסדיֿ‎
