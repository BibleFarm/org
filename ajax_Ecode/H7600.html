H7600. shaanan

שַׁאֲנָן‎ adjective at ease, secure (BaNB § 143a Ges§ 84 b k); — absolute׳שׁ‎ Isa 33:20; plural שַׁאֲנִּים‎ Amos 6:1 +, שַׁאֲנַנּוֺת‎ Isa 32:9, 11, 18; —

1 at ease, secure: שׁ ׳נָוֶה‎ Isa 33:20 ,secure habitation (of Jerusalem), compare שׁ ׳מְנוּחוֺת‎ 32:18 (|| מִבְטָחִים‎).

2 as substantive, one at ease, free from misfortune, Job 12:5.

3 at ease, with collateral idea of careless, wanton, arrogant, Amos 6:1; Isa 32:9, 11; Zech 1:15; Psa 123:4.

4 as substantive abstract = arrogance: suffix שַׁאֲנַנְךָ‎, 2Kin 19:28 (|| הִתְרַגֶּזְךָ‎) = Isa 37:29, but < שְׁאוֺנְךָ‎ thine uproar, BuZAW xii (1892), 36 Gr Che Marti Kit (perhaps), Bur. compare שׁלאנן‎.
