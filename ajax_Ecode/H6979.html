noun [masculine] might (?); — so read (according to most) for קַוקָֿו‎ in phrase ק ׳גּוֺי‎ Isa 18:2, 7 i.e. a mighty nation (compare Arabic strength; see (on reduplication) Ges§ 123e); > RV 'meting out conquered lands, literally 'of line, line' (11. קַו‎).

I. [קוּר‎] verb bore, dig (Arabic () cut a round hole in, scoop out); —

Qal Perfect1singular קַרְתִּי‎ 2Kin 19:24 I have dug (that is, a well)= Isa 37:25 (Meinh conjectures כָּרִתִי‎). —

Hiph`il, Pilpel, see I, II. קרר‎.

II. [קָרַר‎] verb Pilpel tear down (?) (Late Hebrew קִרְקֵר‎ tear down wall); — only Participle קִר מְקַרְקַר‎ Isa 22:5 usually (men are) tearing down wall (s), but see I. קִיר‎
