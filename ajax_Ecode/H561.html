H561. emer

[אִמְרָה‎] noun feminine utterance, speech, word (in poetry, mostly singular collective, compare plural verb Psa 119:103, but here read probably Pluraln. compare ᵐ5‎ Ol Hi De Che) — construct אִמְרַת‎ Isa 5:24 +; אִמְרָתִי‎ Gen 4:23 + etc.; Plural absolute אֲמָרוֺת‎ Psa 12:7; construct אִמֲרוֺת‎ ib.; — utterance, speech Gen 4:23 (song of Lamech, || קוֺל‎); so Isa 28:23; 32:9; Deut 32:2 (|| לֶקַח‎), Psa 17:9; Isa 29:4 (twice in verse) (|| also דִּבֶּד‎); especially saying(s), words(s) of ׳י‎ (command & promise) Deut 33:9 (|| בְּרִית‎); Isa 5:24 (|| תּוֺרָה‎) 2Sam 22:31 = Psa 18:31; 12:7; 105:19 (|| דָּבָר‎; esp 119:11; 119:38; 119:41 16t.; 138:2; 147:15 (|| דָּבָר‎), Prov 30:5.
