H4574. maadan

or maadannah [מַעֲדָן‎] noun [masculine] dainty (food), delight only plural; — מֶלָךְ מַעֲדַגֵּי‎ Gen 49:20 royal dainties; לְמַעֲדַנִּים הָאֹכְלִים‎ Lam 4:5 those who ate (according to dainties) daintily; more Generally לְנַפְשֶׁ֑ךָ מַעֲדַנִּים וְיִתֵּן‎ Prov 29:17 he shall give delight to thy soul (|| וִינִידֶ֑ךָ‎). — מַעֲדַנּוֺת‎, also below ענד‎.
