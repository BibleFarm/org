1 go around, surround, encompass:

a. go around, with accusative of city, Josh 6:3 compare 6:11 (accusative omitted), Psa 48:13 (all || סָבָב‎); also (figurative) אֶתֿ הַזְּעָקָה הִקִּ֫יפָה‎ Isa 15:8 the cry hath gone about the border of Moab.

b. surround, encompass, enclose, usually with עַלהָֿעִיר וַיַּקִּ֫פוּ ׃עַלֿ‎ 2Kin 6:14 and they closed in upon the city (beleaguered it); with עַל‎ person 11:8 (+ סָבִיב‎), Psa 17:9; 88:18 (|| סָבַב‎), + accusative of thing Job 19:6 he hath closed his net in upon me compare Lam 3:5; less often with accusative of person Psa 22:17 (|| סָבַב‎), 2Chr 23:7 (|| 2Kin 11:8; + סָבִיב‎), also of inanimate things 1Kin 7:24 2Chr 4:3 (both + סָבִיב‎, || סָבַב‎).

2 make the round, i.e. complete the circuit ׳הִקּ הַמִּשְׁתֶּה יְמֵי‎ Job 1:5 when the days of feasting had completed their circuit.

3 make round, round off (literally): חַקּ ׳לֹא ראֹשְׁכֶם מְּאַת‎ Lev 19:27 (|| זְקָנֶ֑ךָ מְּאַת אֵת תַשְׁחִית לֹא‎), of heathen rite.
