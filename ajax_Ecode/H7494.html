רַ֫עַשׁ‎ noun masculineJer 10:22 quaking, shaking; — ׳ר‎ absolute 1Kin 19:11 +, construct Nahum 3:2; Job 41:21; —

1 specifically earthquake, Amos 1:1; Zech 14:5; 1Kin 19:11 (twice in verse); 19:12; figurative of ׳י‎s judgement Isa 29:6; Ezek 38:19; compare (in vision) ׳ר גָּרוֺל קוֺל‎ 3:12-13, probably also 37:7 (ᵐ5‎ Thes Co Krae; > rustling, rattling, Hi-Sm Toy); hence hyperbole of shaking of earth by tramping of warriors Isa 9:4, by war-chariots Jer 47:3, compare 10:22, or wheels Nahum 3:2.

2 quaking, trembling, of person Ezek 12:18.

3 shaking, quivering, of horse eager to start Job 39:24 (+ רֹגֶז‎), of dart 41:21 (al. rushing sound).
