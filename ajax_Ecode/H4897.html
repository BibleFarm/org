H4897. meshi

מֶ֫שִׁי‎ noun [masculine] a costly material for garments, according to Rabb silk, (so NowArchaeology i. 124); whether this is based on actual tradition is dubious (etymology unknown); — absolute ׳מ‎ Ezek 16:13 (|| שֵׁשׁ‎), מֶ֑שִׁי‎ 16:10 (|| id.) See, further Thes Sm Da Berthol. ᵐ5‎ τρίχαπτα.
