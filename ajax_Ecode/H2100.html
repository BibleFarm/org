[זוּב‎] verb flow, gush (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic דִּיב דּוּב,‎, , all used often in sense 4 below; Arabic (medial ) melt, dissolve, flow) — only

Qal Imperfect יָזוּב‎ Lev 15:25 etc.; Participle זָב‎ Jer 49:9f. זָבָ֫ה‎ Lev 15:19 etc.; —

1 flow, gush (poetry), of water from the smitten rock Psa 78:20 (|| שָׁטַף‎), 105:41 (|| הָלַךְ‎), Isa 48:21 (|| נָזַל‎).

2 Participle frequently in וּדְבַשׁ חָלָב זָבַת (אֶרֶץ)‎, always of promised land, Exod 3:8, 17; 13:5 (all J) 33:3; Num 13:27; 14:8; 16:13-14, (all J E) Deut 6:3; 11:9; 26:9, 15; 27:3; 31:20; Josh 5:6 (D) Lev 20:24 (H) Jer 11:5; 32:22; Ezek 20:6, 15 (in last two insert ᵐ5 ארץ‎ Co); עִמְקֵךְ זָב‎ Jer 49:4 (fertile valley of Ammon).
