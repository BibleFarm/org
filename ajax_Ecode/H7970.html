noun indeclinable thirty; —

1 before singular noun: אַמָּה‎ Gen 6:15 3t., +, with בָּאַמָּה‎ Exod 26:8 3t.; שָׁנָה‎ 2Sam 5:4 12t. (age of paternity, of Levitical or royal duty, etc.), אִישׁ‎ Judg 14:19 3t., etc.; before plural noun בָּנִים‎ 10:4; 12:9, אֲנְשִׁים‎ Jer 38:10 (read here probably שְׁלשָׁהּ‎ Ew Hi Gf and others), less common words Ezek 40:17; Ezra 1:9-10, etc.; after plural noun Gen 32:16, ׳שׁ מְּעָמִים‎ Ezek 41:6; ׳שׁ אֶלֶף‎ 1Chr 23:3 +, ׳שׁ אֲלָפִים‎ Ezra 2:35; Neh 7:38; ׳שׁ‎ following units Gen 11:12 14t., precedes 1Kin 22:42 29t.; ׳שׁ‎ following 100 (1000, etc.) Gen 5:5 15t., precedes 5:3 21t.
