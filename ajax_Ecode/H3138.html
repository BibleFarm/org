KJV: first rain, former (rain).

H3138. yoreh

יוֺרֶה‎ noun [masculine] early rain, which falls in Palestine from the last of October until the first of December, opposed to מַלְקוֺשׁ‎: Deut 11:14; Jer 5:24; Hosea 6:3 (where ᵑ0‎ makes ׳יו‎ Participle, or Hiph`il Imperfect, but see We); compare also I. מוֺרֶה‎. See further RobBr i, 429 f. ChaplinPEF 1883, 8 ff. KleinZPV iv, 72 f.
