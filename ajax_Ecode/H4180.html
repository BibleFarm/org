H4180. morash

[מוֺרָשׁ‎] noun [masculine] a possession; — construct קִמֹּד מוֺרַשׁ‎ Isa 14:23 a possession for bitterns; מוֺרָשֵׁיהֶם אֵת יַעֲקֹב בֵּית וְיָרְשׁוּ‎ Obadiah 17 and the house of Jacob shall possess their possessions (but read perhaps מורישׁיהם‎ their dispossessors, ᵑ7 ᵑ9 ᵐ5‎ and others); figurative לְבָבִי מוֺרָשֵׁי‎ Job 17:11, i.e. my cherished thoughts (|| זִמּוֺתַי‎), but figurative is questionable, see Di, who derives from ארשׁ‎ desire, so Buhl.
