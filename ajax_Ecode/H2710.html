חָקַק‎ (châqaq｜khaw-kak'｜verb｜cut in)
[Heb] חָקַק‎ ETCBC: חקק‎ (verb｜engrave) OSHL: h.ff.aa TWOT: 728 GK: H2980 Greek: ἄρχων, ἀφορίζω, βασιλεύς, γράφω, διατάσσω, ἐρευνάω, ἡγέομαι, ἰσχυρός
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: properly, to hack, i.e. engrave (Judges 5:14, to be a scribe simply); by implication, to enact (laws being cut in stone or metal tablets in primitive times) or (gen.) prescribe
KJV: appoint, decree, governor, grave, lawgiver, note, pourtray, print, set.

H2710. chaqaq

[חָקַק‎] verb cut in, inscribe, decree (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic חֲקַק‎, Zinjirli חקק‎; Phoenician id. Participle Hiph`il; Arabic be just, right, obligatory, also make or decide to be just, etc.; justness, truth, necessity, obligation; Ethiopic adjective moderate, sufficient; compare also חקה‎) —

Qal וְחַקּוֺתָ֫‎ Ezek 4:1; suffix חַקֹּתִיךְ‎ Isa 49:16; Infinitive suffix בְּחֻקּוֺ‎ Prov 8:27; בְּחוּקוֺ‎ 8:29 (assimilated to בְּשׂוּמוֺ‎); חֻקָּהּ‎ Isa 30:8; Participle חֹקְקִי‎ 22:16 (archaic case ending Ges§ 90. 3 a); plural חֹקְקִים‎ 10:1; construct חוֺקְקֵי‎ Judg 5:9 (possibly Po`el = מחוקקי‎ with מְ‎ omitted, see Ges§ 52 (2), note 6); Participle Pass. plural חֲקֻקִֿים‎ Ezek 23:14 (Co חקוקים‎); —

1 cut in, with בְּ‎, Isa 22:16, of a dwelling-place = tomb, in a rock.

2 cut in or on, upon, engrave, inscribe, with על‎ on roll of a book Isa 30:8; representation of city on brick (as in Babylonia) Ezek 4:1; images on a wall 23:14; figurative of Zion's walls on palms of ׳י‎ Isa 49:16.

3 trace, mark out, a circle, with על‎ over the face of deep Prov 8:27; with accusative, foundations of earth 8:29.

4 of a law, engrave, inscribe (on a tablet), figurative for enact, decree און חקקי חקקים‎ Isa 10:1 (|| עמל כתב‎); ישׂראל חוֺקקֵי‎ poetic = commanders (see Po`el) Judg 5:9 (|| מחקקים‎ 5:14).

Po`el Imperfect יְחוֺקְקוּ‎ Prov 8:15; Participle מְחֹקֵק‎ Gen 49:10; Num 21:18; Deut 33:21; suffix מְחֹקְקִי‎ Psa 60:9; 108:9; מְחֹקְקֵנוּ‎ Isa 33:22; plural מְחֹקְקִים‎ Judg 5:14; — inscribe (as a law), enact (poetry): with accusative צֶדֶק‎ Prov 8:15 elsewhere participle

a. prescriber of laws, hence (as sovereign authority in a warlike clan) commander Deut 33:21 (of warlike tribe of Gad), Judg 5:14; Isa 33:22 (of ׳י‎; || מַלְכֵּנוּ שֹׁפְטֵנוּ,‎).

b. commander's staff Gen 49:10 (|| שֵׁבֶט‎), Num 21:18 (both poetry), Psa 60:9 = 108:9.

Pu`al Participle מְחֻקָּק‎ that which is decreed Prov 31:5 (late).

Hoph`al Imperfect יֻחָ֑קֿוּ‎ Job 19:23 inscribed in (בְּ‎) a book.
