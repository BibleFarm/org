II. בַּר‎ adjective pure, clean, Job 11:4; Psa 24:4; בָּר‎ Prov 14:4; plural construct בָּרֵי‎ Psa 73:1; feminine בָּרָה‎ 19:9; Song 6:9-10, —

1 pure, clear: לבב בר‎ pure in heart Psa 24:4; לבב ברי‎ 73:1; a pure damsel Song 6:9-10, man Job 11:4, commands of God Psa 19:9.

2 clean: בָּר אֵבוּס‎ crib is clean Prov 14:4.

3 perhaps adverb נַשְּׁקובַֿר‎ kiss purely, of sincere homage Psa 2:12 but compare I. בַּר‎, p. 135.
