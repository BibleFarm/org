H1052. Beth

Shean שְׁאָן בֵּית‎ proper name, of a location in Manasseh, west of Jordan (place of quiet) 1Kin 4:12 (twice in verse); בֵּיתשְֿׁאָן‎ Josh 17:11, 16; Judg 1:27; 1Chr 7:29; = שָׁ֑ן בֵּית‎ 1Sam 31:10, 12; בֵּיתשַֿׁן‎ 2Sam 21:12; — modern Beisân (Scythopolis), Late Hebrew בֵּישָׁן‎ RobBR iii. 329 BdPal 224 Surveyi. 101 f.
