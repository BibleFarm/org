[חָפַשׂ‎] verb search (ᵑ7‎Jer חֲפַס‎ dig, seek; Palestinian, Syriac dig (Schw); perhaps Assyrian êppêšu, êtpêšu, sensible, LyonSargontexte 65) —

Qal Imperfect2masculine singular suffix תַּחְמְּשֶׂ֫נָּה‎ Prov 2:4; יַחְמְּשׂוּ‎ Psa 64:7; נַחְמְּשָׂה‎ Lam 3:40; Participle חֹפֵשׂ‎ Prov 20:27; — search, search out, figurative;

1 search for, object בִּינָה‎ etc. Prov 2:4 (|| בקשׁ‎).

2 = think out, devise, with accusative עולות‎ unjust acts Psa 64:7 (compare also sub. Pu`al)

3 search = test, Lam 3:40 (object דרכינו‎; || חקר‎), Prov 20:27.

Niph`al Perfect נַחְמְּשׁוּ‎ Obadiah 6 subject עֵשָׂו‎ collective; searched out = exposed and plundered (|| מַצְמּוּנָיו נִבְעוּ‎).

Pi`el Perfect וְחִמַּשְׂתִּ֫י‎ 1Sam 23:23; וְחִמְּשׂוּ‎ 1Kin 20:6; Imperfect וַיְחַמֵּשׂ‎ Gen 31:35 2t.; אֲחַמֵּשׂ‎ Amos 9:3; Zeph 1:12; Imperative חַמְּשׂוּ‎ 2Kin 10:23; —

1 search through, with accusative, a house 1Kin 20:6, Jerusalem (metaphor) Zeph 1:12; no object expressed 2Kin 10:23.

2 search for:

a. a person with accusative 1Sam 23:23.

b. a thing, with accusative Gen 44:12 (J; Joseph searching for his cup); 31:35 (E; no object expressed, Laban looking for his teraphim); Amos 9:3 (׳י‎ searching for evildoers), Psa 77:7 (soul searching to understand ׳י‎s dealings with his servants).
