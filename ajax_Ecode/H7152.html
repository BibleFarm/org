H7152. Qeriyyoth

קרִיּוֺת‎ proper name, of a location (formed as plural intensive from קִרְיָה‎?); —

1 in Judah Josh 15:25 (αἱ πόλεις; whence, as supposed, Judas קְרִיּוֺת אִישׁ‎), perhaps †aryatên, approximately 12 miles south of Hebron, BuhlGeogr. 182.

2 in Moab, Καριωθ, αἱ πόλεις; MI13 קרית‎: Jer 48:24, ׳הַקּ‎ 48:41; Amos 2:2; perhaps = Ραββαθ Μωαβ = Μοαβ = Αρεοπολις (Euseb., see LagOnom. 277, 60 and others), modernRabba, approximately 11 miles south of Arnon, compare BuhlGeogr. 270.
