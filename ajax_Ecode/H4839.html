H4839. maraq

מָרָק‎ (van d. H מָרַק‎) noun masculine juice stewed out of meat, or broth (on form compare LagBN 50); — בַּמָּרוּר שָׂם וְהַמָּרָק‎ Judg 6:19 (see GFM); שְׁפוֺךְ וְאֶתהַֿמָּרָק‎ 6:20; construct מְרַק‎ Isa 65:4 Qr, so Vrss (Kt פרק‎) broth of unclean things.
