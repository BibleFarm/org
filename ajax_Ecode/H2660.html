H2660. Chepher

I. חֵ֫פֶר‎ proper name, masculine 1. a Manassite Num 26:32-33, 27:1; Josh 17:2-3, ᵐ5‎ Οφερ.

2 a man of Judah 1Chr 4:6; ᵐ5‎ Ηφαλ, ᵐ5‎L Αφερ.

3 one of David's heroes, according to 1Chr 11:36 (but on text see Be VB and Dr 2Sam 23:36).

II. חֵ֫פֶר‎ proper name, of a location 1. Canaanitish town, with a king, named just before Aphek Josh 12:17, exact site unknown.

2 1Kin 4:10 in Judah (ח ׳ארץ‎); — on ח ׳גִּ֫תָּה‎ Josh 19:13, הַח ׳גַּת‎ 2Kin 14:25, see גַּת‎ below יגן‎.
