H623. Asaph

אָסָף‎ proper name, masculine (gatherer, compare Phoenician proper name, feminine אספת‎)

1 father of יוֺאָח‎ the recorder 2Kin 18:18, 37 = Isa 36:3, 22.

2 one of David's chief musicians, a Levite, son of Berechiah 1Chr 6:24 (twice in verse) (|| הֵימָן‎ v.6:18, אֵיתָן‎ v.6:29 see these names); 15:17 (|| Heman & Ethan, all called) הַמְּשֹׁרֲרִים‎ 15:19; see also 16:5 (twice in verse); 16:7 compare 16:37 (|| Heman & Jeduthun 16:41; 16:42); 25:9, named with Heman & Jeduthun also 25:6; 2Chr 5:12; 35:15; Psa 50:1; Psalm 73:1-83:18 are ascribed to Asaph; compare also וְאָסָף דָוִיד בִּימֵי‎ Neh 12:46; he is called also הַחֹזֶה‎2Chr 29:30, compare עַליְֿדֵיהַמֶּלֶךְ הַנִּבָּא אָסָף‎ 1Chr 25:2; repeated mention is made also of sons of Asaph בְּנֵיאָֿסָף‎, sons, descendants, and pupils, or those who sang and played after his manner 25:1 (|| Heman & Jeduthun) 25:2; 25:2; called הַמְּשֹׁרֲרִים‎2Chr 35:15; Ezra 2:41; Neh 7:44; 11:22, compare Ezra 3:10; performing service of purification, Hezekiah's time 2Chr 29:13; one of them prophesying 20:14; — 1Chr 26:1 for אָסָף בְּנֵי‎ read אֶבְיָסָף בְּנֵי‎, compare 9:19; on זִכְרִי‎ 9:15, זַבְדִּי‎ Neh 11:17, זַכּוּר‎12:35, called בֶּןאָֿסָף‎, see these names.

3 keeper of king's park Neh 2:8.
