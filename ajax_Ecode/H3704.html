H3704. keseth

[כֶּ֫סֶת‎] noun feminine band, fillet (= charm or amulet according to Ehpraimitic source Syriac, φυλακτήρια according to ὁ Ἑβραῖος of Hexapl., compare RSJPh xiii, 286; in Late Hebrew כסת‎ usually = cushion, bolster, pillow), only plural עַלֿ כְּסָתוֺת מְתַמְּרוֺת‎ Ezek 13:18 women sewing bands upon elbows; with suffix 2 feminine plural כִּסְתוֺתֵיכֶ֫נָה‎ 13:20.
