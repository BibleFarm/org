H7285. regesh

[רֶ֫גֶשׁ‎] noun [masculine] throng; — בִּרָ֑נֶשׁ נְהֵלֵךְ‎ Psa 55:15 used to walk in the throng (compare הָמוֺן‎ 42:5, also to אלהים בית‎).

[רִגְשָׁה‎] noun feminine throng; — construct אָ֫וֶן מֹּעֲלֵי רִגְשַׁת‎ Psa 64:3; so (of worshippers, compare רֶגֶשׁ‎) 68:28, reading רִגְשָׁתָם‎ for רֹגְמָתָם‎, Hup Pe Bi Che Bae Dr.
