חָרִיף חָרִף,‎ proper name, masculine (compare Arabic autumn, see חֹרֶף‎ above) —

2 חָרִיף‎ one of those sealed Neh 10:20 ᵐ5‎ Αρειφ(α), ᵐ5‎L Αρηφ.
