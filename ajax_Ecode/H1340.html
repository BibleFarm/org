H1340. Bath

-shua בַּתשֿׁוּעַ‎ proper name, feminine (?) (daughter of opulence?) —

1 wife of David, mother of Solomon, etc. 1Chr 3:5 = בַּתשֶֿׁ֫בַּע‎ q. v.; We Klo בַּתשֶֿׁ֫וַע‎ compare Be; but probably textual error. see Dr on 2Sam 11:3.

2 wife of Judah 1Chr 2:3 ׳שֿׁ׳ב הַכְּנַעֲנִית‎, RV Bathshua but in || Gen 38:2, 12 not a proper name (compare V:2).
