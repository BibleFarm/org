גָּלוּת‎ noun feminine exile — Isa 20:4 9t.; גָּלֻת‎ Obadiah 20 (twice in verse); suffix גָּלוּתִי‎ 45:13, גָּלוּתֵינוּ‎ Ezek 33:21; 40:1 (Qames unchangeable); —

1 absolute exile, 2Kin 25:27; Jer 52:31; Ezek 1:2; 33:21; 40:1; Amos 1:6, 9; Obadiah 20 (twice in verse).

2 collective exiles, כושׁ גלות‎ Isa 20:4, יהודה גלות‎ Jer 24:5; 28:4; 29:22; 40:1, ישׁלח גלותי‎ he shall let my exiles (Yahweh's) go free Isa 45:13; see LagArm. Stud. § 445. **Amos 1:6; 1:9 compare Obad 20 (twice in verse) and Jer 13:19 (ᵐ5‎ Du Co and others).
