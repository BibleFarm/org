H8191. shaashuim

[שַׁעֲשֻׁעִים‎], שׁוּעִים‎- noun [masculine] plural intensive delight (BaNB 206); — absolute שׁוּעִים‎- Jer 31:20; Prov 8:30; suffix שׁוּעָיו‎- Isa 5:7, usually שֻׁעַי‎- Prov 8:31; Psa 119:24, שֻׁעָ֑י‎- 119:77 3t.; — delight, in phrase שׁ ׳נְטַע‎ Isa 5:7 the planting of his delight (in which he took delight), so שׁ ׳יֶלֶד‎ Jer 31:20 (|| יַקִּיר בֵּן‎); ׳שׁ אֶתֿ‎ Prov 8:31 my delight (was) with the sons of men; elsewhere = object of delight, of wisdom 8:30; God's law, etc., Psa 119:24; 119:77; 119:92; 119:143; 119:174.
