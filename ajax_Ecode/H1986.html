[הָלַם‎] verb smite, hammer, strike down (compare Phoenician מהלם‎ below below מַהֲלֻמוֺת‎); —

Qal Perfect3feminine singular וְהָֽלְמָה‎ consecutive Judg 5:26; 3plural הָֽלְמוּ‎ 5:22; Isa 16:8; suffix הֲלָמוּנִי‎ Prov 23:25; Imperfect3masculine singular suffix יֶהֶלְמֵנִי‎ Psa 141:5; 3masculine plural יַהֲלֹמ֑וּן‎ 74:6; Participle active מָּ֑עַם ה֫וֺלָם‎ Isa 41:7 (compare Ges§ 29, 3 b); passive construct הֲלוּמֵי‎ 28:1; — smite (with hammer), followed by accusative of person Judg 5:26 (fatally; || מָחַץ מָחַק,‎); also figurative of correction, discipline Psa 141:5 (|| הוֺכִּיחַ‎); of intoxicating, wine שְׂרוּקֶּיהָ הָֽלְמוּ גוֺיִם בַּעֲלֵי שִׂבְמָה גֶּפֶן‎ Isa 16:8 the vine of Sibma, its choice branches smote down lords of nations (so Hi De Che Brd Di and others, RVm; lords subject according to Ges Ew and others AV RV); also יַיִן הֲלוּמֵי‎ smitten down by wine 28:1; compare further Prov 23:35; followed by accusative of thing ה֫וֺלָםמָּֿ֑עְם‎ Isa 41:7 one striking an anvil; smite down, or strike off Psa 74:6 (object פִתּוּחִים‎ carved work); also absolute of horse' hoofs Judg 5:22. — On וַהֲלֹם‎ 1Sam 14:16 see הֲלֹם‎.
