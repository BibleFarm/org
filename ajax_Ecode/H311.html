H311. achar

[אחר‎] plural with אַחֲרֵי‎, preposition after (Cappadocian and Egyptian Aramaic אחר‎ afterwards (LzbEph.i. 361 S-Cpap C 8), probably Nabataean אחר‎ after (Lzb212 SAC16); Egyptian Aramaic אחריכם‎ (S-CPap. c 8+); ᵑ7‎ (sometimes) אֲחוֺרֵי‎; Biblical Hebrew אַחַר‎); — דְנָה אַחֲרֵי‎ after this Dan 2:29, 45; with suffix אַחֲרֵיהֹן‎ 7:24. Usu. Aramaic synonym בָּאתַר‎, q. v.
