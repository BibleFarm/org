H5955. oleloth

עוֺלֵלוֺת‎ noun feminine plural intensive gleaning (going over a second time); — gleaning (of grapes and olives; לֶקֶט‎ of grain), always figurative of remnant: absolute ׳ע‎ Isa 17:6 (|| זַיִת כְּנֹקֶף‎), 24:13 (simile; || id., + בָּצִיר‎), Jer 49:9 (compare בֹּצְרִים‎ va) = Obadiah 5 (id.); construct בָּצִיר עֹלִלֹת‎ Micah 7:1; figurative of warlike achievement אֶפְרַיִם עֹלְלוֺת‎ Judg 8:2 (opposed to בָּצִיר‎).
