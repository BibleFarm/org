H769. Arnon

אַרְנֹן אַרְנוֺן,‎ proper name, of a river Arnon, wady & stream in Moab (MI ארנן‎, Thes Add Rob Ges MV derive from רנן‎, i.e. the rushing, roaring stream) — אַרְנוֺן‎ Num 21:13 (twice in verse) + 12 t., אַרְנֹן‎ 21:14 10t. — called boundary between Moab & Amorites 21:13; Judg 11:18, 22 compare גְּבוּלאַרְנֹן‎ Num 22:36; often אֵרְנֹן נַחַל‎ Deut 2:24, 36; 3:8, 12, 16; 4:48; 2Kin 10:33; ׳נ אַרְנוֺן‎ Josh 12:1-2, 13:9, 16; אַרְנוֺן הַנְּחָלִים‎ Num 21:14, i.e. the stream-ravines that unite to form Arnon, compare Di; also אַרְנֹן בָּמוֺת‎ heights of Arnon 21:28; אַרְנוֺן מעברות‎ fords of Arnon Isa 16:2; elsewhere Num 21:13, 24, 26; Judg 11:13, 18, 26; synonym of Moab Jer 48:20; (compare TristrMoab 125 f.; modern Môjib).
