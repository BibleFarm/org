H5822. ozniyyah

עָזְנִיָּה‎ noun feminine (unclean) bird of prey, apparently akin to vulture (NowArchaeology i. 84, 116), named + מֶּרֶס נֶשֶׁר,‎ Deut 14:12; Lev 11:13 (H); = osprey TristrNHB 184 DrDeut 14:12 (others vulture DiLev 11:13) (√ unknown; conjectures see in Di; perhaps foreign word).
