בָּרַר‎ (bârar｜baw-rar'｜verb｜purify)
[Heb] בָּרַר‎, בָּרוּר‎ ETCBC: ברר‎ (verb｜purge), תבר‎ (verb｜[uncertain]), בָּרוּר‎ (adjv｜pure) OSHL: b.dg.aa TWOT: 288 GK: H1359, H1405, H1406 Greek: ἁγιάζω, ἅγιον, ἀφορίζω, διακρίνω, ἐκλέγομαι, παρασκευάζω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to clarify (i.e. brighten), examine, select
KJV: make bright, choice, chosen, cleanse (be clean), clearly, polished, (shew self) pure(-ify), purge (out).

H1305. barar

[בָּרַר‎] verb purify, select (compare Assyrian barâru, be shining, in derivatives ZimBP 46. 73 BelserBAS ii. 154; Arabic pious, kind, true; he was pious, good, virtuous, honest) —

Qal Perfect בָּרוֺתִי‎ Ezek 20:38; Infinitive suffix לְבָרָם‎ Eccl 3:18, Ges§ 67 R, 3; compare לָבוּר‎ 9:1 (si vera lectio) as metaplastic form, but on text see בּוּר‎ above; Participle passive masculine בָּרוּר‎ Job 33:3-4t.; feminine בְּרוּרָה‎ Neh 5:18; Zeph 3:9; —

1 purge out, purify: הַמֹּרְדִים מִכֶּם וּבָרוֺתִי‎ and I will purge out from among you the rebels Ezek 20:38; בְּרוּרָה שָׂפָה‎ a purified lip Zeph 3:9; מִלֵּל֑וּ בָּרוּר‎ utter in a pure, sincere manner Job 33:3.

2 choose, select, only Participle and in Chronicler: chosen, valiant men 1Chr 7:40; porters 9:22; musicians 16:41; sheep Neh 5:18.

3 cleanse, make shining, polish, passive participle בָּרוּר חֵץ‎ polished arrow Isa 49:2 (compare De & Jer 51:11 below)

4 test, prove Eccl 3:18 הָאֱלֹהִים לְבָרָם‎ that God may prove them (RV); — on 9:1 see בּוּר‎.

Niph`al הִבָּרוּ‎ Isa 52:11; Participle נָבָר‎ 2Sam 22:27 (= Psa 18:27); — purify oneself:

a. ceremonially, the bearers of the sacred vessels Isa 52:11.

b. morally 2Sam 22:27 = Psa 18:27.

Pi`el Infinitive לְבָרֵר‎ Dan 11:35 purify (|| לַלְבֵּן לִצְרוֺף,‎).

Hiph`il 1. Infinitive לְהָבַר‎ Jer 4:11 purify, cleanse.

2 Imperative הַחִצִּים הָבֵרוּ‎ Jer 51:11 polish arrows (see 3 above).

Hithpa`el 1. Imperfect יִתְבָּרֲרוּ‎ Dan 12:10 purify oneself.

2 תִּתְבָּרַר‎ Psa 18:27 = תִּתָּבָר‎ 2Sam 22:27 shew oneself pure, just, kind.
