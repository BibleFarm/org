H8553. Timnah

תִּמְנָ֫תָה תִּמְנָה,‎ proper name, of a location (probably = portion, territory); — (חִמְנָה‎ Josh 15:10, 57; 2Chr 28:18; תִּמְנָ֫תֶה‎ [as nominative see GFMJudg 14:1] Josh 19:43; Judg 14:1-2, 5; with ה‎ locative תִּמְנָ֫תָה‎ Gen 38:12-13, 14; Judg 14:1, 5; ᵐ5‎ Θαμνα, Θαμναθα): —

1 place in hill-country of Judah Gen 38:12-13, 14. probably = Josh 15:57. Site unknown (compare DiGen 38:12 RobBR ii. 17 n.).

2 place on border of Judah Josh 15:10; 19:43 (assigned to Dan), Judg 14:1 (twice in verse); 14:2, 5 (twice in verse); 2Chr 28:18. Modern Tibneh, approximately 15 miles south of west from Jerusalem compare RobBR ii.17 BuhlGeogr. 196.
