אַחַר‎ properly substantive the hinder or following part (compare the plural)

1. adverb a. of place, behind, twice Gen 22:13 (many MSS. ᵑ6 ᵐ5 ⅏‎ Ol Ew read אֶחָד‎ see Di) Psa 68:26.

b. of time, afterwards Gen 10:18; 18:5; 24:55; 30:21; Judg 19:5 +; וְאַחַר‎ in laws of P, as Lev 14:8, 19; 15:28; 22:7; Num 5:26 and elsewhere

2. preposition a. of place, behind, after Exod 3:1; 11:5; 2Kin 11:6; Song 2:9; Isa 57:8: אַחַר הָלַח‎ to go after, follow Gen 37:17; 2Kin 13:2; 23:3; Isa 65:2; Ezek 13:3; Job 31:7; אַחַר חָיָה‎ 1Sam 12:14; מֵאַחַר‎ from after 2Sam 7:8; Psa 78:71; Isa 59:13.

b. of time, after Gen 9:28; Lev 25:15 al; הַדְּבָרִים אַחַר הָאֵלֶּה‎ after these things Gen 15:1; 22:1; 39:7; 40:1; 1Kin 17:17; 21:1; Ezra 7:1; Est 2:1; 3:1; כֵּן אַחַר‎ Lev 14:36; Deut 21:13; 1Sam 10:5; זֶה אַחַר‎ (late) 2Chr 32:9; followed by infinitive Num 6:19; Jer 40:1; 1Chr 2:24; Job 21:3; אַחַר עַד‎ till after Neh 13:19.

c. Neh 5:15 strangely: Ew RV besides; but text probably corrupt, see Be Ry. 3

conjunction אֲשֶׁר אַחַר‎ after that Ezek 40:1; and without אשׁר‎ Lev 14:43; Jer 41:16; Job 42:7. As preposition & conjunction the plural אַחֲרֵי‎ is much more frequently, which in any case must be used before suffixes.

Plural only construct אַחֲרֵי‎ with suffix אַחֲרֶיךָ אַחֲרַי,‎, etc.

1. substantive hinder part 2Sam 2:23 הַחֲנִית בְּאַחֲרֵי‎ with the hinder end of the spear.

2. preposition a. of place, behind, after Gen 18:10 אַחֲרָיו וְהוּא‎ and it behind him Deut 11:30; Judg 5:14; 1Sam 14:13; 21:10; Hosea 5:8 אַחֲרֶיךָ‎ Behind thee! (sc. Look or The foe is); with a verbas הִבִּיט‎ to look Gen 19:17; 1Sam 24:9, ראֹשׁ הֵנִיעַ‎ to shake the head 2Kin 19:21 ( = Isa 37:22), especially verbs expressing or implying motion, as בָּא‎ to enter in (see Dr 2Sam 20:14) הִשְׁלִיח רָדַף, מָּנָה, מִלֵּא, הָלַח, הָיָה, דָּלַק, דָּבֵק, בִּעֵר,‎ (see these words).

b. of time, after Gen 9:9 אַחֲרֵיכֶם זַרְעֲכֶם‎ your seed after you; similarly 17:7-10, 19; 35:12; 48:4; Exod 28:43; Num 25:13 all P (also Deut 1:8; 4:37; 10:15; 1Sam 24:22; 2Sam 7:12 ||), & with בָּנָיו‎ Gen 18:19 J ( + בֵּיתוֺ‎) Exod 29:29 P Lev 25:46 H Deut 4:40; 12:25, 28; Jer 32:18, 39; 1Chr 28:8; Prov 20:7, דּוֺרוֺתֵינוּ‎ Josh 22:27, בֵּיתוֺ‎ Job 21:21; Exod 10:14; Judg 10:3 etc.; with infinitive Gen 5:4 הוֺלִידֹוֺ אַחֲרֵי‎ after his begetting Sheth, 13:14; 14:17; 18:12; 25:11 + often; אַחֲרֵיכֵֿן‎ afterwards 6:4 (אשׁר כן אחרי‎ = afterwards, when, compare 2Chr 35:20) Gen 15:14; 23:18; 25:26; 32:21; 41:31; 45:15 etc.; אחריכֿן ויהי‎ as a formula of transition chiefly in 2Samuel (2Sam 2:1; 8:1 || 10:1 || 13:1; 21:18 ||) compare 1Sam 24:6; Judg 16:4; 2Kin 6:24; 2Chr 20:1; 24:4 ; in late Hebrew זֹאת אַחֲרֵי‎ Job 42:16; Ezra 9:10; 2Chr 21:18 ( + כָּלֿ‎35:20 (do.); compare Aramaic דְנָה אַחֲרֵי‎ Dan 2:29, 45, and דְּנָה בָּאתַר‎ 7:6-7,. — The local (metaphor) and temporal senses blend Psa 49:14 יִרְצוּ בְּפִיהֶם וְאַחֲרֵיהֶם‎ & after them (i.e. following, imitating them) men applaud their speech, compare Job 21:33.

3. conjunction אֲשֶׁר אַחֲרֵי‎ after that, with the finite verb Deut 24:4; Josh 7:8; 9:16; 23:1; 24:20 +; without אשׁר‎ Lev 25:48; 1Sam 5:9 . (The most common construction of אחרי‎ is as a preposition with the infinitive construct) Josh 2:7 יָצְאוּ כַּאֲשֶׁר אַחֲרֵי‎ must be an error, either for אשׁר אחרי‎ or for כאשׁר‎ alone (notice אחריהם‎ twice in the same verse); 2Sam 24:10 read סָפַר אַחֲרֵי‎ see Dr.

4 with other prepositions: —

a. מֵאַחֲרֵי‎57, 1Chr 17:7 מִןאַֿחֲרֵי‎ (|| 2Sam 7:8 מֵאַחַר‎); (α) from behind Gen 19:26; 2Sam 2:23; from after i.e. from following after, usually with שׁוּב‎ or סוּר‎ 1Sam 24:2; 2Sam 2:22, 26, 30; 11:15; Josh 22:16, 18, 23, 29; 1Sam 15:11 +; with other verbs of motion, as עָלָה‎ 14:46; 2Sam 20:2, נַעֲלָה‎ 2:27, לָקַח‎ Amos 7:15; 1Chr 17:7, זָנָה‎ Hosea 1:2; pregnantly Isa 30:21 thy ears shall hear a word מֵאַחֲרֶיךָ‎ coming from behind thee, Jer 9:21 (that is נָפַל‎, see va) 1Sam 13:7 ᵐ5‎L We Dr מֵאַחֲרָיו חָֽרְדוּ‎ (β) denoting position (מן‎ = off, on the side of; see מן‎ behind Exod 14:19 (twice in verse); Josh 8:2, 4, 14; 1Kin 10:19; Neh 4:7 (לְ מֵאַחֲרֵי‎) 2Chr 13:13 b, (γ) of time (rare) Deut 29:21; Eccl 10:14; כֵן מֵאַחֲרֵי‎ 2Sam 3:28; 15:1; 2Chr 32:23.

b. אֶלאַֿחֲרֵי‎ 2Sam 5:23; 2Kin 9:18 אֶלאַֿחֲרַי סֹב‎ 9:19; Zech 6:6.

c. עַלאַֿחֲרֵי‎ Ezek 41:15 beside, at the back of.
