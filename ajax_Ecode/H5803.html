H5803. Azgad

עַזְגָּד‎ proper name, masculine Ασγαδ, Αζγαδ (Gad is mighty; GrayProp. N. 145); —

1. a. head of family returning with Zerubbabel Ezra 2:12 = Neh 7:17.
