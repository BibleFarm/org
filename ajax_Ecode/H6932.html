H6932. Qedemoth

קְדֵמֹת‎ proper name, of a location in Reuben (Moab) Κεδ(α)μωθ, Καδημωθ, etc.; Josh 13:18, מ֑וֺת‎ 21:37; 1Chr 6:64; hence קְדֵמוֺת מִדְבַּר‎ Deut 2:26 of contiguous desert; — ׳ק‎ lay north of upper Arnon; not identified; BuhlGeorge. 268 conjecture Umm-er-raƒâƒ, approximately 10 miles east-northeast from Dibon (compare TristrMoab 140ff. BdPal. 3 (1898), 177).
