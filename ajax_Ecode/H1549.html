H1549. gillayon

גִּלָּיוֺן‎ noun masculine table, tablet (Talmud גִּלְיוֺן‎, the empty margin of page or roll, see LagGN 1881, 403, compare BN 199) — עָלָיו וּכְתֹב גָּדוֺל גִּלָּיוֺן קַחלְֿךָ‎ take thee a great tablet and write upon it Isa 8:1; plural הַגִּלְֹינִים‎ tablets of polished metal, mirrors 3:23 ᵑ9 ᵑ7‎ Ges Che Di De; but transparent garments, gauzes, ᵐ5‎ Ew (compare Arabic fine garment).
