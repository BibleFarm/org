H774. Arpad

אַרְמַּד‎ proper name, of a location city in northern Syria (Assyrian Arpadda DlPa 275) — ׳א‎ Isa 10:9 elsewhere אַרְמָּ֑ד‎; approximately 15 miles north of Aleppo, modern Tel Erfâd; in OT only as conquered by Assyria, always named with Hamath, etc. 2Kin 18:34; 19:13 = Isa 36:19; 37:13 (on ᵐ5‎ Ῥαφέθ = Ἀρφάθ compare LagBN 78), also 10:9; Jer 49:23; (compare NöZMG 1871, 258 Kiepib. 655.)
