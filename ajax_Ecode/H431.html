H431. alu

אֲלוּ‎ Interjection lo! (derivation uncertain: usually regarded as by-form of אֲרוּ‎, q. v.; compare Egyptian Aramaic הלו‎, CookeNo.73, RES492 B = S-CM b); — וַאֲלוּ‎ Dan 2:31 וַאֲלוּ הֲוַיְתָ חָזֵי‎, 4:7; 4:10; 7:8 always in description of a vision.
