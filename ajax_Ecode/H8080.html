I. [שָׁמֵן‎] verb grow fat (Late Hebrew Hiph`il id., also שֶׁמֶן‎ Arabic be fat; Aramaic especially derived species and derivatives; שְׁמֵין‎ adjective fat: Assyrian šammu, fat; Phoenician (Punic) שמן‎ oil, so Palmyrene שמנא‎ Lzb378; on relation of meanings Frä147); —

Qal Perfect2masculine singular שָׁמַ֫נְתָּ‎ Deut 32:15 and Imperfect3masculine singular וַיִשְׁמַן‎ 32:15 both of Israel (prosperous and arrogant) under figure of fat beast; so Perfect3plural שָֽׁמְנוּ‎ Jer 5:28 (of wicked).

Hiph`il Imperfect3masculine plural וַיַשְׁמִינוּ‎ Neh 9:25 were sated and shewed fatness (inner causative); Imperative masculine singular הַשְׁמֵן‎ Isa 6:10 make fat (dull, unreceptive), הַוֶּה הָיָם לֵב‎.
