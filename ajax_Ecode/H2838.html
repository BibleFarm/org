H2838. chashuq

[חָשׁוּק‎] noun [masculine] fillet or ring clasping (binding) a pillar of the tabernacle, only plural suffix חֲשֻׁקֵיהֶם‎ Exod 27:10 5t.; חֲשׁוּקַיהֶם‎ 38:12, 17; those of the pillars at door of tabernacle (הָאֹהֶל‎) were overlaid with gold 36:38; those of the pillars of the court with silver 27:10-11, 38:10-11, 12, 17, 19 (all P), see Di on 27:10; > Thes and most who understand of connecting-rods, joining tops of pillars, from which curtains were hung.
