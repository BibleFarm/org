H7410. Ram

רָם‎ proper name, masculine

1. a. an ancestor of David, Ruth 4:19 (twice in verse), brother of Jerachmeel 1Chr 2:9-10,.

b. son of J. 1Chr 2:25, 27. — Αρραν, Ραμ, etc.

2 name of Elihu's family, Job 32:2 (Ραμ[α], Αραμ), dubious, compare Bu.
