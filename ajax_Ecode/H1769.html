H1769. Dibon

דִּיבוֺן דִּיבֹן,‎ proper name, of a location —

1 city in Moab (MI21; 28 דיבן‎, compare ib.1 adjective, of a people הדיבני‎) — דִּיבֹן‎ Num 21:30; 32:3, 34 (built up or at by Gad) Isa 15:2; in territory of Gad, hence גָּד֑ דִּיבֹן‎ Num 33:45-46, (compare on these verses & 32:34 MI10f.); also דִּיבוֺן‎ Josh 13:9, 17; Jer 48:22; דִּיבוֺן בַּת ישֶׁבֶת‎ 48:18; = דִּימוֺן‎ Isa 15:9, see also דִימוֺן מֵי‎ 15:9 = Arnon, compare Che & HptZA 1887, 268; — modern Dîbân, north of Arnon, compare Seetzen:Reisen i. 409 TristrLand of Moab 132 ff. BdPal 193.

2 דִּיבֹן‎ Neh 11:25 place in Judah, toward south = דִּימוֺנָה‎ Josh 15:22, compare Hptl.c.; conjectures by Kn Ke (compare Di) to be Tell ed Dheib (van de VeldeMem. 252) called also Ehdeib (RobBR ii. 102).
