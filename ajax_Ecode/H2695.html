H2695. Chetsro

חֶצְרַי חֶצְרוֺ,‎ ( proper name, masculine compare Sabean proper name, masculine חצֿר‎ DHMEpigr. Denkm. 56) — one of David's heroes, called the Carmelite, חצרו‎ 2Sam 23:35 Kt (חֶצְרַי‎ Qr; ᵐ5‎ Ασαραι) = חֶצְרוֺ‎ 1Chr 11:37 ᵐ5‎ Ησεραι, Ασαραι, Εσρει.
