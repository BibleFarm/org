H3907. lachash

[לָחַשׁ‎] verb whisper, charm (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic לְחַשׁ‎ ; Ethiopic ; whisper, see RSJPh xiv. 1885, 122, possibly originally serpent-charming); —

Pi`el Participle plural מְלַחֲשִׁים קוֺל‎ Psa 58:6 voice of whisperers (i.e. serpent-charmers; || תֲבָרִים חוֺבֵר‎).

Hithpa`el whisper together, Imperfect יִתְלַחֲשׁוּ‎ with עַל‎ Psa 41:8; Participle plural מִתְלַחֲשִׁים‎ 2Sam 12:19.
