H5037. Nabal

II. נָבָל‎ proper name, masculine (on popular etymology see 1Sam 25:25 below); — a churlish man of Carmel, whose widow David married 25:3-4, 5, 9, 10, 14, 19, 25 (twice in verse) (עִמּוֺ וּנְּבָלָה שְׁמוֺ נָבָל כֶּןהֿ֔וּא כִּשְׁמוֺ‎), 25:26 10t. 1Samuel + 2Sam 2:2; 3:3. ᵐ5‎ Ναβαλ.
