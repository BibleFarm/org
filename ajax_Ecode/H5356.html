נִקָּיוֺן‎ noun [masculine] innocency; — ׳נ‎ Psa 26:6; 73:13; נִקָּיֹן‎ Hosea 8:5; construct נִקְּיוֺן‎ Amos 4:6; נִקְיֹן‎ Gen 20:5; —

1 freedom from guilt, innocency: ׳נ כַּמַּי‎ Gen 20:5 (RJE); בנ ׳אֶרְחַץ דַּמָּ֑י‎ Psa 26:6 I will wash my hands in innocency = 73:13.

2 freedom from punishment Hosea 8:5.

3 in physical sense, שִׁנַּיִם נִקְיוֺם‎ cleanness of teeth (without food) Amos 4:6 (but read perhaps after קַהֲיוֺן ᵑ7 ᵑ6 ᵐ5‎ bluntness, so LagBN 201).
