H8441. toebah

תּוֺעֵבָה‎117 noun feminine abomination; — absolute ׳ת‎ Gen 43:32 21t.; תֹּעֵבָה‎ Jer 44:4; construct תּוֺעֲבַת‎ Gen 46:34 +; plural תּוֺעֵבוֺת‎ Ezek 8:6 +; construct תּוֺעֲבוֺת‎ 6:11 +, etc.; suffix תֹּעֲבֹתָיוֺ‎2Chr 36:8, etc.; —

1 ritual sense:

a. Israel's sacrifices, ׳ת מִצְרַיִם‎ (genitive object) Exod 8:22 (twice in verse) (E), compare Gen 46:34 (J); ׳ת למצרים‎ 43:32 (J); ׳ת לְ‎ of physical repugnance Psa 88:9.

b. to God and his people: ׳ת‎ of unclean food Deut 14:3; worshipper of idols Isa 41:24, compare Jer 2:7; various objectionable acts: ׳ת י ׳לפני‎ Deut 24:4; ׳ת ׳י‎ 7:25 (חֵרֶם‎, compare 7:26), 17:1; 18:12; 22:5; 23:19; 27:15; offering of children (עשׂה‎) 12:31, compare Jer 32:35, also plural 2Kin 16:3 2Chr 28:3; Deut 18:9, 12 ( + witchcraft); idolatrous practices (sometimes with other illegal acts) (כ)ת ׳עשׂה‎ 13:15; 17:4; Ezek 16:50; 18:12; Mal 2:11 (intermarriage with idolaters), compare Jer 44:4; usually with plural noun Deut 20:18; 1Kin 14:24; 2Kin 21:2, 11; 2Chr 33:2; 36:8; Jer 7:10; 44:22; Ezek 8:6 12t. Ezekiel; compare תועבות‎2Chr 36:14; Ezra 9:1, 11, 14; Ezek 5:9 18t. Ezekiel; of idols Deut 32:16; (תועבה‎) 2Kin 23:13; Isa 44:19 (compare Deut 27:15); idolatrous objects 2Chr 34:33; || שׁקּוצים‎ Jer 16:18 (RJE) Ezek 5:11; 11:18, 21, (ת ׳צַלְמֵי‎) 7:20; || גלולים‎ 14:6, compare 6:9; ת ׳גִּלּוּלֵי‎ 16:36.

2 ethical sense:

a. c. Genitive object of man, ׳ת שְׂפָתַי‎ Prov 8:7, ׳ת מלכים‎ 16:12, ׳ת צדיקים‎ 29:27 a, ׳ת כסילים‎ 13:19, ׳ת רָשָׁע‎ 29:27 b, ׳ת לְאָדָם‎ 24:9.

b. to God and his people: ׳ת‎ of sacrifice of wicked Prov 21:27 (compare 15:8), his prayers 28:9 compare Isa 1:13 (׳ת לְ‎), probably also Jer 6:15 = 8:12 (of various kinds of wickedness); unchastity Lev 18:22; with עשׂה‎ 20:13 (H) Ezek 22:11; 33:26; compare תועבות‎ Lev 18:26-27, 29 (compare 18:30; H), also Prov 26:25; ׳ת ׳י‎ Deut 25:16; Prov 3:32 10t. Proverbs, compare 6:16.
