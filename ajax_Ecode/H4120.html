H4120. meherah

מְהֵרָה‎ noun feminine haste, speed; — usually as adverb. accusative hastily, quickly: Josh 8:19; 10:6; 23:16 (all J E), Num 17:11 (P), Deut 11:17; Judg 9:54; 2Sam 17:16, 18, 21; Jer 27:16; Eccl 8:11 (in all these cases after verb); before verb (poetry) Isa 5:26 (|| קַל‎), Psa 31:3; 37:2; Isa 58:8; Joel 4:4 (|| קַל‎); so also 1Sam 20:38 ׳מ הוּשָׁה‎ (compare Dr); 2Kin 1:11 ׳מ רֵ֑דָה‎; — but perhaps read מַהֲרָה‎, i.e. Pi`el Imperative of מהר‎ (compare 1Sam 23:27); with preposition (late): ׳בִּמ‎ in haste = quickly Eccl 4:12; ׳עַדמֿ‎ id. Psa 147:15.
