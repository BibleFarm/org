H8487. Teman

II. תֵּימָן‎ proper name (masculine and)

of a location a northern district of Edom, poetic often = Edom Amos 1:12 (|| אֱדוֺם‎ vAmos 1:11, and בָּצְרָה‎), Obadiah 9 (|| הַרעֵֿשָׂו‎), Jer 49:7, 20; Ezek 25:13 (all || אֱדוֺם‎); Hab 3:3 (|| הַרמָּֿארָן‎); probably also Josh 12:3; 13:4 (both D); in Gen 3:6 (P) as name of an Edomite chief, son of אֱלִיפַז‎ and grandson of Esau 3:11 = 1Chr 1:36; 1:42 = 1:53; so תֵּו֯מָן‎ Gen 36:15. — See further BuhlEdomiter 30.
