H1650. Geshur

גְּשׁוּר‎ proper name, masculine (bridge ? or land of bridges ? compare WetzstHauran, 82).

1 of people, = הַגְּשׁוּרִי‎ Josh 13:13 compare 1Chr 2:23.

2 of land גְּשׁוּר מֶלֶךְ‎ 2Sam 3:3; 13:37 compare 13:38 (but text in disorder compare ᵐ5‎ We Dr) 14:32; 15:8 ׳בִּגְ בַּאֲרָם‎ 1Chr 3:2, גְּשׁ֫וּרָה‎ 2Sam 14:23; a territory east of the upper Jordan, exact limits unknown; according to GutheZPV xii. 1889, 232 f. between Gilead and Hermon, in Jaulân; according to Furrerib. xiii. 1890, 198 = the Ledjah, district east of Jaulân, with town Jisre; against him Gutheib. 285 f.
