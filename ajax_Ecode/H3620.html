H3620. Kelub

כְּלוּב‎ proper name, masculine 1. a name in Judah 1Chr 4:11, ᵐ5‎ Ξαλεβ (according to WeGentib. 20, Hist. 218 = חֶזְרוֺן בֶּןֿ כָּלֵב‎; read also for כַּרְמִי‎ 4:1).

2 father of one of David's officers, according to 1Chr 27:26 (ᵐ5‎ Ξοβουδ, A Ξαλουβ, ᵐ5‎L Ξαλουβ).
