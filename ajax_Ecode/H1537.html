H1537. Gilgal

II. גִּלְגָּל‎ proper name, of a location (= (sacred) circle of stones; compare Di on Josh 5:9, where word-play on roll away, off) — with article except 5:9; 12:23; —

1 place east of Jericho, where Israel lay encamped Josh 4:19-20, 5:9-10, 9:6; 10:7, 9; 14:6 compare also Micah 6:5 & Judg 3:19 אֶתהַֿגּ ׳אֲשֶׁר הַמְּסִילִים‎;? modern Tell Jeljul BdPal 169; perhaps also the place where sacrifices were offered 1Sam 10:8; 11:14-15, (twice in verse); 13:4, 7, 8, 12, 15 (in 13:15 insert also with other words, Dr compare We) 15:12, 21, 33; place of illicit sacrifice Hosea 4:15; 9:15; 12:12; Amos 4:4; 5:5 (twice in verse), named also Judg 2:1; 1Sam 7:16; 2Sam 19:16; 19:41; ? = הַגִּלְגָּל בֵּית‎ Neh 12:29.

2 place on border between Judah & Benjamin Josh 15:17 (= גְּלִילוֺת‎ 18:17 compare Di).

3 place in Northern Israel, dwelling of prophets 2Kin 2:1; 4:38, modern Jiljilie south from Nâbulus, southwest from Seilûn (Shiloh) compare Di RiHWB 518.

4 place in Northern Israel near Mt. Gerizim and Mt. Ebal Deut 11:30, identification with

3 by Ke RiHWB 518 SchenkelBL SS; but compare Di.

5 in phrase לְגִּלְגָּל גּוֺיִם מֶלֶךְ‎ Josh 12:23, in list of Canaanitish kings; probably name of a (northern) district compare Di (B βασιλέα Γεεὶ τῆς Γαλιλαίας — but ᵐ5‎L & Manuscripts Γοειμ τῆς Γελγελ); Di compare Isaiah 8:23.

גִּלְגָּל / הַגִּלְגָּל‎
原文音譯：lG"l.GI ghil-gawl’

詞類次數：專有名詞（4）

原文字根：旋轉（者）

字義溯源：吉甲；地名。此字與（H1536（גִּלְגָּל‎）=車輪）相似，出自（H1556（גָּלַל‎）*=運轉）字義：滾去（書5:9）

1）以色列人過了約但河，曾安營於吉甲（書4:19）

2）吉甲是猶大支派和便雅憫支派地業交界的一個地方（書15:7; 18:17）

3）以利亞升天之時，是從吉甲前往（王下2:1）

4）在基利心山與以巴路山相對的吉甲（申11:29,30）

5）約書亞率領以色列人戰敗諸王中，有一個是吉甲的戈印王（書12:23）以上五處經文所提到的吉甲，到底是一個還是多個不同的地方，解經家的看法並不一致
