H5411. nethinim

נְתִינִים‎ noun masculine plural Nethinim (properly those given to the service of the sanctuary, as Levites are called נְתוּנִים‎ Num 3:9; 8:16, 19); — only 1Chr 9:2 (where distinguished from priests and Levites) + 16 t. Ezra Nehemiah (often distinguished from priests, Levites and porters; Ezra 8:17 Kt has הנתונים‎, Qr הַנְּתִינִים‎), namely: 2:43, 58, 70 = Neh 7:46; 7:60; 7:72 (so Baer, Ginsb; van d. H. 7:73), Ezra 7:7; 8:17, 20 (appointment ascribed to David, compare 1Chr 9:2), 9:20; Neh 10:29; 11:3; living on the Ophel 3:26; 11:21; with their own overseers 11:21, having a house known (in part) by their name 3:31. — On ׳נ‎ see further BauPriesterthum 100, 142 f.
