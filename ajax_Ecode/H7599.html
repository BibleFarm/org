H7599. shaan

[שָׁאַן‎] verb Pa`lel (Ges§ 55d) be at ease or at peace, rest securely (Syriac pacify, Ethiopic peace); — Perfect3masculine singular שַׁאֲנַן‎ Jer 48:11, ׳וְשׁ‎ consecutive 30:10 +; 3 plural שַׁאֲנָ֑נוּ‎ Job 3:18; — be at ease, secure, undisturbed by ill fortune: מַחֲרִיד וְאֵין וְשׁ ׳וְשָׁקַט‎ Jer 30:10 = 46:27; Jeremiah 48:(of Moab); ׳שׁ רָעָה מִמַּחַד‎ Prov 1:33; of rest from trouble, etc., in grave Job 3:18.
