הָדַף‎ verb thrust, .push, drive (הֲדַף‎ in ᵑ7‎; Arabic is ingressus ad aliquem fuit, propinquus fuit, also of time prope accessit) —

Qal Perfect3masculine singular suffix הֲדָפוֺ‎ Num 35:22; Jer 46:15; 1singular suffix וַהֲדַפְתִּיךָ‎ consecutive Isa 22:19; Imperfect3masculine singularיֶהְדֹּף‎ Prov 10:3; suffix 3masculine singular יֶהְדֳּפֶנּוּ‎ Num 35:20; suffix 3masculine plural יֶהְדֳּפֵם‎ Josh 23:5; 3masculine plural suffix יֶהְדְּפֻהוּ‎ Job 18:18; 2masculine plural תֶּהְדֹּ֑פוּ‎ Ezek 34:21; Infinitive הֲדֹף‎ Deut 6:19; 9:4; suffix הָדְפָהּ‎ 2Kin 4:27; —

1 thrust, push, subject man, followed by accusative Num 35:20, 22 (here fatally); subject cattle, figurative of men, Ezek 34:21 (instrumental בְּכָתֵף‎; || נָגַח‎); push away 2Kin 4:27 (Gehazi, the Shunammite); thrust away (from oneself) figurative = reject, רְשָׁעִים הַוַּת‎ Prov 10:3 (subject ׳י‎).

2 thrust out, drive out, followed by מִן‎ of place whence & accusative of person; of ׳י‎ driving out enemies of Israel from Canaan Deut 6:19; 9:4; Josh 23:5; compare also indefinite subject אֶלחֿשֶׁךְ מֵאוֺר יֶהְדְּפֻהוּ‎ Job 18:18 (|| יְנִדֻּהוּ וּמִתֵּבֵל‎); & Jer 46:15 (where no plural whence). Hence

3 = depose Isa 22:19 of ׳י‎ deposing Shebna (followed byמִן‎ of station).
