H3411. yerekah

[יַרְכָה‎, or יְרֵכָה‎ Ol§ 167. g] flank, side, dual extreme parts, recesses; — suffix יַרְכָֿתוֺ‎ Gen 49:13; dual יַרְכָתַיִם‎ Exod 26:27; 36:32 + Ezek 46:19 Qr (Kt ירכתם‎); יַרְכָתָ֑יִם‎ Exod 26:23; 36:28; construct יַרְכְּתֵי‎ Judg 19:1 20t. + 1Kin 6:16 Qr (Kt ירכותי‎); —

1 side, i.e. further side of Zebulun, poetic for more distant border of his territory Gen 49:13.

2 elsewhere always dual the two things, i.e. figurative angle, recess, extreme parts: as recesses of Mt. Ephraim Judg 19:1, 18; of Lebanon 2Kin 19:23 = Isa 37:24; of recesses or innermost part of a cave 1Sam 24:4; a house Amos 6:10; Psa 128:3; the pit (בּוֺר‎) Isa 14:15 (|| שְׁאוֺל‎), Ezek 32:23; a ship Jonah 1:5; צָפוֺן יַרְכְּתֵי‎ remote parts of the north Isa 14:13; Ezek 38:6, 15; 39:2; Psa 48:3 (but here Lag proposes ׳יר רצון‎); אָ֑רֶץ יַרְכְּתֵי‎ remote parts of earth Jer 6:22; 25:32; 31:8; 50:41; of a long building, extreme or hinder part, so of tabernacle Exod 26:22-23, 27; 36:27-28, 32 (all P); of temple 1Kin 6:16; of Ezekiel's temple Ezek 46:19.
