H1025. Beth

Haemeq הָעֵ֫מֶק בֵּית‎ proper name, of a location on border of Asher (valley-house) Josh 19:27. Surveyi. 145 compare `Amka, 7 miles northeast of Akko (but see RobBR iii. 103, 108).
