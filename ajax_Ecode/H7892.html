H7892. shiyr

שִׁיר‎77 noun masculine song (Late Hebrew id.; so Ecclus 40:21 +); — ׳שׁ‎ absolute Judg 5:12 +, construct 2Chr 29:27 +; suffix שִׁירִי‎ Psa 28:7, etc.; plural שִׁירִים‎ 1Chr 13:8; Song 1:1; שִׁרִים‎ Gen 31:27; Prov 25:20; suffix שִׁרֶ֫יךָ‎ Amos 5:23, etc.; —

1 lyric song, + מָשָׁל‎ 1Kin 5:12, opposed to קִינָה‎ Amos 8:10; joyous Gen 31:27 (J) Isa 30:29; ׳שׁ עגבים‎ Ezek 33:32; triumphal Judg 5:12; love song Song 1:1 (twice in verse) (title הַשִּׁירִים שִׁיר‎), Psa 45:1 (יְדִידֹת שִׁיר‎); noisy Amos 5:23; Ezek 26:13, in drunken revels Isa 24:9, compare 23:16; ׳שׁ כסילים‎ Eccl 7:5; not for sorrow Prov 25:20; Psa 137:3 (twice in verse); הַשִּׁיר בְּנוֺת‎ Eccl 12:4 songstresses (probably birds).

2 religious song, in worship, || תפלה‎ Psa 42:9; usually praise, בשׁיר הלל‎ 69:31; אֲהוֺדֶנּוּ מִשִּׁירִי‎ 28:7; וְהוֺדוֺת תְּהִלָּה שִׁרִ‎ Neh 12:46; י ׳שׁיר‎ Psa 137:4; 2Chr 29:27; חדשׁ שׁיר‎ Isa 42:10 (fresh outburst), + 6 t., see חָדָשׁ‎; in Psalm-titles, שׁיר‎ 46:1, compare 26:1; המעלות שׁיר‎ pilgrim songs Psa 120:1 14t. (see II. מַעִַלָה‎ p.752); with מִמְוֺר‎ 48:1 12t. (see ׳מִז‎ p. 274); שִׁיר מַשְׁכִּיל‎ 45:1 (׳שׁ‎ originally alone in all except 30:1; 92:1; 108:1, see BrJBL xviii (1899), 138).

3 specifically song of Levitical choirs, with musical accompare, 1Chr 6:16; 6:17; 13:8 (so read also || 2Sam 6:5 for ᵐ5 בְּרוֺשִׁים,‎ Th and modern, compare ׳ב‎), 1Chr 25:6; 25:7; 2Chr 23:18; 29:28; Neh 12:27; (ה)שׁיר כלי‎ instrument to accompany song 1Chr 15:16 6t. (see כְּלִי‎ 2b); compare Amos 6:5.

שִׁירָה‎ noun feminine song (plural ירות‎[שׁ‎] Ecclus 39:15); — absolute ׳שׁ‎ Exod 15:1 9t.; construct שִׁירַת‎ Isa 5:1; 23:15; plural construct שִׁירוֺת‎ Amos 8:3 (read ᵐ5 שָׁרוֺת‎ HoffmZAW iii (1883) We and others); — song, ode, Exod 15:1 (E), 2Sam 22:1 = Psa 18:1; Deut 31:19 (twice in verse); 31:21-22, 30; 32:44; Num 21:17 (E) Isa 5:1; 23:15.
