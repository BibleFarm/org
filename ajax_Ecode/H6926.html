Derivation: feminine of קֶדֶם‎;
Strong's: the forward part (or relatively) East (often adverbially, on the east or in front)
KJV: east(-ward).

H6926. qidmah

[קִדְמָה‎] noun feminine front, East; — only construct קִדְמַת‎ as preposition:

1 in front of, over against (compare NöZMG xxxiii (1879), 532), Gen 2:14 (i.e. from standpoint of writer; really, on geographic grounds, = west of; yet see Died. 6), 4:16 (both J),

2 on the east of, Ezek 39:11; 1Sam 13:5 may be either.
