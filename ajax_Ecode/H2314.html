H2314. chadar

[חָדַר‎] verb surround, enclose (Phoenician in derivatives; Arabic II. conceal, curtain concealing a person, chamber, house, or tent as concealing some one; Ethiopic dwell; see also following) **Phoenician is only חדרת חדר,‎ (temple or sepulchral) chamber (Lzb271 Cooke126); Ezek 21:19 is best explained from Syriac go about, surround, Syriac around. It seems uncertain whether חֶדֶר‎, Phoenician חדר‎ chamber, Arabic curtain, chamber, tent, etc., are (ultimately) from this √ (as something surrounding); or (Buhl) from II. חדר‎ = II. conceal behind curtain, conceal, confine, IV. conceal oneself, also abide, stay, remain behind (Land707f.), Ethiopic abide, dwell (Driver, privately, Nov. 1905). — only
