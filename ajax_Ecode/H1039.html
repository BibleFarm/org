H1039. Beth

Nimrah נִמְרָה בֵּית‎ proper name, of a location east of Jordan, in Gad (place of leopard) Num 32:36 = Josh 13:27; ᵐ5‎ Ναμραμ, Βαιθαναβρα, etc., see also LagOnom. Sacr. 232, 2nd ed. 246; — modern Tel Nimrîn SurveyEP i. 337 BdPal 179, compare also נִמְרִים מֵי‎ Isa 15:6, & RobBR i. 551.
