H1196. Baanah

בַּעֲנָה‎ proper name, masculine (? id.) —

1 a Benjamite, one of the murderers of Ishbosheth 2Sam 4:2, 5, 6, 9.

2 father of one of David's heroes 2Sam 23:29 = 1Chr 11:30.

3 head of a family of returning exiles Ezra 2:2 = Neh 7:7; perhaps also = בַּעֲנָא‎ 3:4.

4 a chief of the people Neh 10:28.
