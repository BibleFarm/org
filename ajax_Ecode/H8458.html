H8458. tachbulah

[תַּחְבֻּלָה‎] noun feminine direction, counsel (probably originally of rope-pulling, i.e. steering, directing a ship, see חֹבֵּל‎, so Thes; see De Prov 1:5); — only plural and only Wisdom Literature. תַּחְבֻּלוֺת‎ absolute 1:5 3t.; construct 12:5; suffix תַּחבּוּלֹת֯ו‎ Job 37:12 — direction, guidance (i.e. by God) 37:12 (of thunder cloud); counsels of wicked (רשׁעים‎) Prov 12:5 (opposed to צדיקים מַחְשְׁבוֺת‎); Generally good, wise counsels 1:5; 11:14 (|| יועֵץ רֹב‎), 24:6 (|| id.); 20:18 (|| עֵצָה‎).
