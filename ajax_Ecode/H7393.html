[Heb] רֶכֶב‎ ETCBC: רֶכֶב‎ (subs｜chariot) OSHL: t.cq.ab TWOT: 2163a GK: H8207 Greek: ἵππος
Derivation: from רָכַב‎;
Strong's: a vehicle; by implication, a team; by extension, cavalry; by analogy a rider, i.e. the upper millstone
KJV: chariot, (upper) millstone, multitude (from the margin), wagon.
