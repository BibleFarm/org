1 body, self, only in phrase בְּגַמּוֺ (יֵצֵא) יָבאֹ‎) he shall come in (go out) by himself (with his body) Exod 21:3 (twice in verse); 21:4; in law of slave (compare Di).

2 height, elevation קָ֑רֶת מְרֹמֵי עַלגַּֿמֵּי‎ Prov 9:3.
