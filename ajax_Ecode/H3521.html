H3521. Kabul

כָּבוּל‎ proper name, of a location 1. city on border of Asher Josh 19:27, modern Kâbûl, 4 h. (9 miles) southeast from Akko, RobBR iii, 88 GuérinGal, i. 422 f.

2 כָּבוּל אֶרֶץ‎ district in Galilee 1Kin 9:13 containing 20 cities given by Solomon to Hiram (popular etymology possibly כּ‎ as + בֻּל‎ = בַּל‎ not, i.e. as good as nothing, compare 9:12, so Ew Th; Klo conjectures גָּלִיל ארץ‎), BuhlGeogr. § 116.
