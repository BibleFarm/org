II. עֲרֹעֵר‎10, עֲרוֺעֵר‎5, עַרְעֹר‎1

proper name, of a location Αροηρ, MI26 ערער‎; —

1 city on north bank of Arnon, southern limit of east Jordan Israel, עֲרֹעֵר‎ Num 32:34 (JE), Deut 2:36; 3:12; 4:48; Josh 12:2 (D) 2Kin 10:33; 1Chr 5:8; עֲרוֺעֵר‎ Josh 13:9 (D), 13:16 (P), 2Sam 24:5; Moabitish Jer 48:19 (compare MI26); once עַרְעֹר‎ Judg 11:26; modern `Ar`âir Buhl Geogr. 269, TristrMoab 129 ff.

2 city near Rabbah in Ammon, עֲרֹעֵר‎ Judg 11:33, ׳עֲרוֺ‎ Josh 13:25 (P), site unknown.

3 city in southern Judah, עֲרֹעֵר‎ 1Sam 30:28 (ᵐ5‎ Αροηρ + Αμμαδει, latter originally according to We, indicating form ערערה‎; ᵐ5‎L Παγουηλ); + Josh 15:22 reading ׳ע‎ (or עַרְעָרָה‎) for ᵐ5 עַדְעָרָה, ᵑ0‎B Αρουηλ, We1Sam 30:28 (compare Dr HPS), Dion the passage Benn Steuern; possibly `Ar`âra approximately 9 miles south of east from Beersheba, but compare BuhlGeogr. 183. — Isa 17:2 read ᵐ5 עַד עֲדֵי עָרֶיהָ‎ Lo LagSemitic i. 30 Che BuhlLex and others (for עֲרֹעֵר עָרֵי ᵑ0‎). — See also CheEncy. 3）在約但河之西，別示巴東南約十二哩處的亞羅珥，大衛曾送禮物給住在那裏的居民（撒上30:28）
