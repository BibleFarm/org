H8588. taanug

תַּעֲנוּג‎ noun [masculine] daintiness, luxury, exquisite delight; —

1 luxury: ׳ת‎ Prov 19:10; elsewhere plural; construct תַּעֲנֻגוֺת‎ Eccl 2:8.

2 suffix תַּעֲנוּנָ֑יִךְ בְּנֵי‎ Micah 1:16 thy dainty sons; תַּעֲנֻגֶיהָ בֵּית‎ 2:9 her (their) dainty house(s), > of tender love (reading בְּנֵי‎ 2:9 We Now.

3 delight of love, plural absolute בַּתַּעֲנוּגִים‎ Cant 7:7 (PerlesAnal. 22 f. conjecture עַמִּינָדָב בַּת‎).
