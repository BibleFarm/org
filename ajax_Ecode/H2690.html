חָצַר‎ (châtsar｜khaw-tsar'｜verb｜sound a clarion)
[Heb] חָצַר‎, חַצְצַר‎ ETCBC: חצר‎ (verb｜sound clarion) OSHL: h.fd.ac TWOT: 726b GK: H2955, H2957 Greek: σαλπίζω
Derivation: a primitive root; properly, to surround with a stockade, and thus separate from the open country; but used only in the reduplicated form חֲצֹצֵר‎; or (2 Chronicles 5:12) חֲצֹרֵר‎; as dem. from חֲצֹצְרָה‎;
Strong's: to trumpet, i.e. blow on that instrument
KJV: blow, sound, trumpeter.
