H5852. Ataroth

עֲטָרוֺת‎ and (Num 32:34) עֲטָרֹת‎ proper name, of a location Αταρωθ, etc.:

1 East of Jordan (MI10, 11); —

a. Num 32:3 (+ דִּיבוֺן‎, etc.) — modern A‰‰arûs, approximately 8 miles north-northwest from Dibon — built by Gad 32:34 (+ עֲרֹעֵר ׳ד,‎, and also)

b. שׁוֺפָן עַטְרֹת‎ Num 32:35 (Samaritan ׳ע שפים‎, see Di; site unknown).

2 west of Jordan, conjectures as to sites see in Di BuhlGeogr. 172:

a. on border between Ephraim and Benjamin Josh 16:2, = אַדָּר עַטְרוֺת‎ 16:5; 18:13.

b. on east border of Ephraim Josh 16:7.

c. יוֺאָב בֵּית עַטְרוֺת‎ 1Chr 2:54 in Judah; a 'son' of Salma (+ Bethlehem, al.).
