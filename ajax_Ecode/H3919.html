H3919. Layish

II. לַ֫יִשׁ‎ proper name, of a location (on meaning lion, compare RSSemitic i. 156 (om. 2nd ed.)); — former name of Dan, in extreme north of Canaan, Judg 18:7, 14, 27, 29 (see דָּן‎ b); ᵐ5‎ Λαισα; = לֶשֶׁם‎ q. v.; compare BuhlGeogr. § 124.

III. לַ֫יִשׁ‎ proper name, masculine father of Michal's second husband, 1Sam 25:44 + 2Sam 3:15 Qr (Kt לושׁ‎), ᵐ5‎ A Λμεις, Σελλης, ᵐ5‎L Ιωας, Σελλειμ.

לַ֫יְשָׁה‎ proper name, of a location North of Jerusalem Isa 10:30 (perhaps akin to III. לַיִשׁ‎, see Di); site unknown; see KasterenZPV xiii. 101 conjecture `Isawiye, village two miles northeast from Jerusalem (PEFMap; Mem. iii. 27 RobBR i. 437 compare BuhlGeogr. 175), ᵐ5‎ ἐν Σα, Λαισα.
