H8198. shiphchah

שִׁפְחָה‎ noun feminine maid, maid-servant (synonym אָמָה‎, q. v.; originally = concubine ? compare √); — absolute ׳שׁ‎ Gen 16:1 +; construct שִׁפְחַת‎ 16:2 +; suffix שִׁפְחָתִי‎ 16:2 +, etc.; plural absolute שְׁפָחוֺת‎ 12:16 +; suffix שִׁפְחֹתֶ֑ךָ‎ Ruth 2:13, etc.; —

1 literally, maid, maid-servant, as belonging to a mistress Gen 16:1, 3, 6, 8 (PJ) 29:24; 29:29 (P) Psa 123:2; Prov 30:23; Isa 24:2; even where concubine of master (compare אָמָה‎) Gen 16:2, 5 (PJ) 25:12; 35:25-26, (P) 30:4, 7, 9, 10, 12, 18 (all J E); less often reference to master 29:24, 29 (J), Ruth 2:13, of concubine Gen 32:23, compare 33:1-2, 6 (all J); marriageable Lev 19:20 (H, compare Exod 11:5; not elsewhere in legisl.); in General, especially of menial service (never אָמָה‎) 11:5 (J) 1Sam 25:41 (opposed to אָמָה‎) 2Sam 17:17 (|| עֶבֶד‎) Gen 12:16; 24:35(J) 20:14 (E) 30:43 (P) 32:6 (J; collective) Deut 28:68; 1Sam 8:16; 2Kin 5:26; Isa 14:2; Jer 34:9-10, 11 (twice in verse); 34:16 (twice in verse); Joel 3:2; Eccl 2:7; 2Chr 28:10; Est 7:4.

2 figurative in address, שִׁפְחָֽתְךָ‎, etc., of speaker, in token of humility (see אָמָה‎) Ruth 2:13 (|| אָמָה‎ 3:9), 1Sam 1:18 (|| id. 1:16), 25:27, (|| 25:24f.), 28:21-22, 2Sam 14:6-7, 12 (twice in verse); 14:15, 17, 19; (|| אָמָה‎ 14:15; 14:16); 2Kin 4:2, 16; not toward God; but see אָמָה‎.
