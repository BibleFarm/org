H419. Eldad

אֶלְדָּד‎ proper name, masculine (God has loved, compare Sabean ורראל‎ DHMZMG 1883. 15; see also יְדִידְיָה‎) one of the elders Num 11:26-27, probably = אֱלִידָד‎ 34:21 where called a prince of Benjamin. — On proper names with אֵל‎ compare those with אָב‎ & אָח‎, & especially Nöl.c.,, & (Sabean) DHMEpigr.
