מְנוּחָה‎ (mᵉnûwchâh｜men-oo-khaw'｜noun｜feminine｜resting-place)
[Heb] מְנוּחָה‎, מְנֻחֹות‎ ETCBC: מְנוּחָה‎ (subs｜resting place) OSHL: n.by.am TWOT: 1323f GK: H4957, H4967 Greek: κατάπαυσις
Derivation: or מְנֻחָה‎; feminine of מָנֹוחַ‎;
Strong's: repose or (adverbially) peacefully; figuratively, consolation (specifically, matrimony); hence (concretely) an abode
KJV: comfortable, ease, quiet, rest(-ing place), still.

H4496. menuchah

מְנֻחָה מְנוּחָה,‎ noun feminine resting-place, rest; — absolute ׳מ‎ Micah 2:10 +, מְנֻחָה‎ Gen 49:15 (but see below), 2Sam 14:17; suffix מְנוּחָתִי‎ Psa 95:11 2t., מְנוּחָתֶ֑ךָ‎ 132:8 2Chr 6:41 (where read as in Psalms, for anomalous לְנוּחֶ֑ךָ‎ MT), מְנֻחָתוֺ‎ Isa 11:10; Zech 9:1; plural מְנוּחֹת‎ Isa 32:18, מְנֻחוֺת‎ Psa 23:2; —

1 resting-place Micah 2:10; Num 10:33 (JE), Deut 12:9 (+ הַנַּחֲלָה‎) so Psa 95:11 (׳י‎s resting- plural), compare 132:8 2Chr 6:41 (see above), Psa 132:14; Isa 66:1 (|| לִי תִּבְנוּ אֲשֶׁר בַּיִת‎), compare מ ׳בֵּית וגו ׳לַאֲרוֺן‎ 1Chr 28:2; also Isa 11:10; probably also Gen 49:15 (|| הָאָרֶץ טוֺב‎ here is apparently substantive, ⅏‎ reads adjective טוֺבָה‎; but read perhaps מְנֻחהֹ‎, his resting-place, from מָנוֺחַ‎, BallHpt Holz after ᵑ6‎); שַׁאֲנַנּוֺת מְנוּחֹת‎ Isa 32:18 (+ מִבְטַחִים מִשְׁכְּנוֺת שָׁלוֺם, נְוֵה‎); of ׳י‎s word Zech 9:1; resting-place on journey, in מ ׳שַׂר‎ Jer 51:59 (Ew Gf Gie and others); figurative for security, assurance 2Sam 14:17 (taken from resting, equilibrium, of scale according to Klo).

2 rest, quietness, plural מְנֻחוֺת מֵי‎ Psa 23:2; = refreshment Isa 28:12 (|| מַרְגֵּעָה‎); rest (from enemies) = peace 1Kin 8:56 (compare 5:18), מ ׳אִישׁ‎ 1Chr 22:9 a man of peace (compare הֵנִיחַ‎ vb); = relief from sorrow Jer 45:3; condition of rest and security attained by marriage Ruth 1:9 (= מָנוֺחַ‎ 3:1). — ׳מ‎ Judg 20:43 is perhaps proper name, compare GFM.
