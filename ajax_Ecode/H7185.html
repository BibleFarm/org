H7185. qashah

I. [קָשָׁה‎] verb be hard, severe, fierce (Late Hebrew קָשָׁה‎ be severe; Arabic () be hard, dry, III. endure, struggle against, severity; Aramaic קְשָׁא‎, be hard, difficult; Jewish-Aramaic קַשְׁיָה קְשִׁי,‎ adjective hard); —

Qal Perfect3feminine singular קָֽשְׁתָה‎ 1Sam 5:7, קָשָׁ֑תָה‎ Gen 49:7; Imperfect3masculine singular יִקְשֶׁה‎ Deut 1:17; 15:18, וַיִּ֫קֶשׁ‎ 2 Samuel 19:44; —

1 be hard, difficult Deut 1:17 (of a legal case; מִן‎ comparative).

2 be hard, severe, עָלֵינוּ ׳ק יָדוֺ‎1Sam 5:7 (of ׳י‎, in judgement); ׳יִק בְעֵינֶ֑ךָ‎. Deut 15:18 it shall not be hard in thine eyes (seem a severe trial, an injustice); of wrath Gen 49:7 (poem in J; || עָ֑ז‎); of fierce words 2 Samuel 19:44.

Niph`al Participle נִקְשֶׁה‎ Isa 8:21 hardly bestead, hard pressed.

Pi`el Imperfect3feminine singular בְּלִדְתָּהּ וַתְּקַשׁ‎ Gen 35:16 (E) she made hard in her bearing (had severe labour).

Hiph`il Perfect3masculine singular הִקְשָׁה‎ Exod 13:15 +, etc.; Imperfect3masculine singular וַיֶּקֶשׁ‎2Chr 36:13; 3 masculine plural וַיַּקְשׁוּ‎ Jer 7:26 +, etc.; Infinitive construct suffix בְּהַקְשֹׁתָהּ‎ Gen 35:17; Participle מַקְשֶׁה‎ Prov 28:14; 29:1; —

1 make difficult, difficulty: לְשַׁלְּחֵנוּ הִקְשָׁה‎ Exod 13:15 Pharaoh made difficulty about sending us away; of travail Gen 35:17 (E; as

Pi`el); לִשְׁאוֺל הִקְשִׁיתָ‎ 2Kin 2:10 thou hast made hard to ask, asked a hard thing.

2 make severe, burdensome, yoke imposed by king 1Kin 12:4 2Chr 10:4.

3. a. make hard, stiff, stubborn, figurative of obstinacy: אֶתעָֿרְמָּם וַיַּקְשׁוּ‎ 2Kin 17:14 they stiffened their neck, so Jer 7:26; 17:23; 19:15; Deut 10:16; 2Chr 30:8; 36:13; Neh 9:16-17, 29; Prov 29:1; לְבַבְכֶם אַלתַּֿקְשׁוּ‎ Psa 95:8, compare Prov 28:14; subject פ אֶתלֵֿב ׳אַקְשֶׁה ׳י,‎ Exod 7:3 (P), accusative אֶתרֿוחוֺ‎ Deut 2:30,

b. shew stubborness Job 9:4 (with אֶל‎ person [׳י‎]).
