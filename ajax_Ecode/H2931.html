II. טָמֵא‎ adjective unclean; — ׳ט‎ Lev 5:2 58t.; construct טְמֵא‎ 22:4 3t.; feminine טְמֵאָה‎ 5:2 11t.; construct טְמֵאַת‎ Ezek 22:5, 10; plural טְמֵאִים‎ Lev 11:8 10t.; — unclean,

1 ethically and religiously ׳ט שׂפתים‎ Isa 6:5 (twice in verse) unclean of lips; ׳ט השׁם‎ Ezek 22:5 defiled of name, infamous; מִטּ ׳טהור‎ Job 14:4.

2 ritually:

a. of persons, || טהור‎ Deut 12:15, 22; 15:22; Eccl 9:2; ׳ט נפשׁ‎ Lev 22:4 (H); (אדם‎) ׳ט לנפשׁ‎ unclean for a (dead) person Num 5:2; 9:6-7, 10 (P) = ׳ט נפשׁ‎ Hag 2:13; elsewhere for various reasons Deut 26:14; Lev 5:2; 13:11, 16, 44, 45 (twice in verse); 13:46; 15:2, 25, 33; Num 19:13, 17, 19, 20, 22 (all P), 2Chr 23:19; Isa 64:5; Ezek 4:13; 22:10; Lam 4:15.

b. of animals Lev 5:2 (4 t. in verse); 7:21 (twice in verse); 11:4-5, 6, 7, 8, 26, 27, 28, 29, 31; 27:11, 27; Num 18:15 (all P), Lev 20:25 (twice in verse) (H), Deut 14:7-8, 10, 19.

c. of things in Gen. Lev 11:35 (twice in verse); 11:38; 15:26; Num 19:15 (all P), Isa 52:11; food Judg 13:4; Hosea 9:3; houses Jer 19:13; leprosy Lev 13:15, 51, 55; 14:44, 57 (P); offering Hag 2:14.

d. persons and things in General טמא כל‎ Lev 7:19, 21 (P); הט בין ׳הבדיל הטהור ובין‎ 10:10; 11:47; (ה)ט בין ׳הודיע לטהור‎ Ezek 22:26; 44:23; of aliens Isa 52:1 (|| עָרֵל‎), perhaps also 35:8.

3 specifically of places: ט ׳מקום‎ unclean place (place of refuse away from holy place and human habitation) Lev 14:40-41, 45 (P); ארץ טמאה‎ land on the east of the Jordan separated from the land of the tabernacle of ׳י‎ Josh 22:19 (P); so ט ׳אדמה‎ a foreign land Amos 7:17.
