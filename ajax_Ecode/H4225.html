H4225. machbereth

מַחְבֶּ֫רֶת‎ noun feminine thing joined, place of joining; — absolute ׳מ‎ Exod 26:4 3t.; מַחְבָּ֑רֶת‎ 36:11, 17; suffix מַחְבַּרְתּוֺ‎ 28:27; 39:20; —

1 thing joined, of curtain-pieces of tabernacle (compare חֹבֶרֶת‎), Exod 26:4-5, 36:11 (twice in verse); 36:12.

2 place of joining:

a. of the curtains Exod 36:17.

b. of shoulder-pieces of the ephod Exod 28:27; 39:20 (all P).
