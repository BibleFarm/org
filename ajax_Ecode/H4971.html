H4971. mathkoneth

or mathkuneth מַתְכֹּ֫נֶת‎ noun feminine measurement, tale, proportion; —

1 construct ׳מ הַלְּבֵנִים‎ Exod 5:3 (J) the tale of bricks (compare 1.תֹּכֶן‎).

2 suffix בֻּנְתּוֺ‎- measurement, proportion, of ephah and bath Ezek 45:11; of composition of sacred oil Exod 30:32, תָּהּ‎- 30:37 (P); of temple, its measurement, proportion תּוֺ‎-2Chr 24:13.
