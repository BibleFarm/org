[אֻרְיָה‎] noun feminine manger, crib (Aramaic אוּדְיָא‎, , Arabic stall, etc., Vulgar Arabic = manager Mohit19. 15 (GFM, privately), compare Lane51c; Assyrian urû DlBAS i. 211) — Plural אֻרָוֺת‎2Chr 32:28 (Aramaic form for אֳרָוֺת‎; compare LagBN 172); construct אֻרְוֺת‎ 1Kin 5:6, ֻארְיוֺת‎2Chr 9:25; crib of horses 1Kin 5:6 (app. in enumeration horses themselves, English head, or span, compare also 2Chr 9:25 with 1Kin 10:26; So Th MV, but text here dubious. compare Klo), hence 2Chr 9:25; of any animals (כלבֿהמה‎) 32:28 (on ׳לַאֲ‎ see Add.). **Also plural אֲוֵרוֺת‎32:28b (read probably ᵑ9 ᵐ5 לָעֲדָרִים‎ Kit Benz and others).
