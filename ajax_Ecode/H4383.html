H4383. mikshol

מִכְשׁוֺל‎ noun masculineJer 6:21 a stumbling, means or occasion of stumbling, stumbling-block; — ׳מ‎ absolute Isa 8:14 3t., מִכְשֹׁל‎ Lev 19:14; construct מִכְשׁוֺל‎ 1Sam 25:31 6t. Ezekiel; plural מִכְשֹׁלִים‎ Jer 6:21 + Ezek 21:20 ᵑ0‎ (Co and others מֻכְשָׁלִים‎, see below); —

1 stumbling, מ ׳צוּר‎ Isa 8:14 (fig) a rock of stumbling (i.e. over which one stumbles); literal הַמִּכְשֹׁלִים הַרְבֵּה‎ Ezek 21:20 an abundance of stumblings, falls (si vera lectio; ᵐ5‎. οἱ ἀσθενοῦντες, apparently reading participle, compare Sm; so Co Da (as a possibility), i.e. מֻכְשָׁלִים‎

Hoph`al Participle of כשׁל‎ = those who have stumbled or been overthrown).

2 means, or occasion of stumbling, stumbling-block; a literal Lev 19:14.

b. figurative of misfortune, calamity Paslm Lev 119:165; in divine judgment Jer 6:21; Ezek 3:20, as a hindrance to restoration of people Isa 57:14.

c. in ethical sense ׳מ עֲוֺנָם‎ Ezek 7:19 a stumbling-block of (i.e. occasioning) their iniquity, so 14:3-4, 7; 18:30; 44:12 (mostly with reference to idols.).

d. ׳מ לֵב‎ 1Sam 25:31 a stumbling-block of heart i.e. ground for remorse.
