רְכֻשׁ רְכוּשׁ,‎ noun masculineGen 13:6 property, goods; — absolute רְכוּשׁ‎ Num 16:32 +, רְכֻשׁ‎ Gen 14:16, 21; construct רְכוּשׁ‎2Chr 35:7, רְכֻשׁ‎ Gen 14:11; suffix רְכֻשׁוֺ‎ 14:12 +, רְכוּשָׁם‎ 12:5 +, etc.; — property, goods, Proverbs, Chronicles, Daniel, Genesis 14:

1 general term for movable possessions of all kinds (often specifically including cattle), Gen 12:5; 15:4; Num 16:32; 1Chr 27:31; 28:1; 2Chr 31:3; 32:29; Ezra 8:21; 10:8.

2 used specifically of cattle, flocks, etc. Gen 13:6; 31:18; 36:7; Num 35:3; 2Chr 35:7.

3 of stores, utensils, etc. Gen 46:6; Ezra 1:4, 6, as camp-baggage Dan 11:13, especially as booty Gen 14:11-12, 16 (twice in verse); 14:21; 2Chr 20:25; 21:14, 17; Dan 11:24, 28.
