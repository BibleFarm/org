H642. aphuddah

אֲפֻדָּה‎ noun feminine ephod (= אֵפוֺד‎, for which it supplies construct & suffix forms).

1 of high-priest's ephod, compare אֵפוֺד‎ 1b, only אֲפֻדָּתוֺ חֵשֶׁב‎Exod 28:8; 39:5 the cunningly-wrought band of his ephod.

2 of sheathing of idol-images, מַסֵּכַתוְהָכֶ֑ךָ אֲפֻדַּת‎Isa 30:22 (|| ךָ כַסְמֶּ פְסִילֵי צִפוּי‎) compare אֵפוֺר‎ 3a.
