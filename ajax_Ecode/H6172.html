עֶרְוָה‎54 noun feminine nakedness, pudenda; — absolute ׳ע‎ Exod 28:42; Lev 18:6; usually construct עֶרְוַת‎ Gen 9:22 +, suffix עֶרְוָתְךָ‎ Exod 20:23 (Ginsb), Lev 18:10; עֶרְוָתֵךְ‎ Isa 47:3 +; ת֯וֺ‎ Lev 20:17, תָ֯הּ‎ 18:7 +; suffix 3 feminine plural עֶרְוָתָן‎ 18:9; 18:10; —

1 pudenda, of man ע ׳רָאָה‎ implying shameful exposure Gen 9:22-23, (J); mostly of woman: figurative of Jerusalem (with רָאָה‎) Lam 1:3; Ezek 16:37; usually with נלה ׃‎ literal ע ׳תִּגָּלֶה‎ i.e. be exposed to view Exod 20:23 (Ginsb; van d. H. 20:26; E), so, as shameful punishment, figurative of Egypt Isa 20:4 (gloss according to Du Che Di-Kit), Babylonian 47:3, of Jerusalem Ezek 16:37; 23:10, 29 (זְנוּנַיִךְ עֶרְוַת‎; all three object of active verb); chiefly euphemism for cohabitation, ע ׳גִּלָּה‎ Lev 18:6 + (see גלה‎ Pi`el 1 a; figurative of Jerusalem (verb passive) Ezek 16:36; ע ׳רָאָה‎ in same meaning Lev 20:17 (twice in verse) (H; of both sexes); ׳ע‎ also 18:8, 10, 16 (H); ע ׳כִּסָּה‎ cover nakedness Gen 9:23 (J), Exod 28:42 (P; ע ׳בְּשַׂר‎), Hosea 2:11 (figurative of Israel), Ezek 16:8 (of Jerusalem); reviling words are ע ׳לְבשֶׁת אִמֶּ֑ךָ‎ 1Sam 20:30 (compare DoughtyArab. Deserta i. 269).

2 דָּבָר עֶרְוַת‎ nakedness of a thing, i.e. probably indecency, improper behaviour Deut 23:15; 24:1 (see Dr).

3 figurative ׳ע הָאָרֶץ‎ Gen 42:9, 12 (E), i.e. its exposed, undefended parts (Arabic ).
