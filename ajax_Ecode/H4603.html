מָעַל‎ (mâʻal｜maw-al'｜verb｜act unfaithfully)
[Heb] מָעַל‎ ETCBC: מעל‎ (verb｜be unfaithful) OSHL: m.cz.aa TWOT: 1230 GK: H5085 Greek: ἀδικέω, ἀθετέω, ἁμαρτάνω, ἀπειθέω, ἀφίστημι, λανθάνω, παραβαίνω, παραπίπτω, πλανάω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: properly, to cover up; used only figuratively, to act covertly, i.e. treacherously
KJV: transgress, (commit, do a) trespass(-ing).

H4603. maal

מָעַל‎ verb act unfaithfully, treacherously, a priestly word (P Ezra, Chronicles), chiefly late (Late Hebrew id., act unfaithfully; Arabic whisper, backbite, perfidy, fraud); —

Qal Perfect3masculine singular מָעַל‎ Josh 22:20 4t.; 3 feminine singular מָֽעֲלָה‎ Leviticus 5:21; Num 5:12-13t. Perfect; Imperfect יִמְעַל‎ Prov 16:10; 2Chr 26:16; 3 feminine singular תִּמְעֹל‎ Lev 5:15; Num 5:27; 3masculine plural יִמְעֲלוּ‎ Josh 7:1, וַיִּמְעֲלוּ‎ 1Chr 5:25; 2masculine plural תִּמְעָ֑לוּ‎ Neh 1:8; Infinitive construct לִמְעֹל‎, so read for HCT למסר‎ Num 31:16 Thes Di; — act unfaithfully, treacherously, usually

1 מַ֫עַל מָעַל‎:

a. absolute בַּחֵרֶם‎ in the matter of the devoted thing (the sin of Achan) Josh 7:1 (JE), 22:20 (P), compare Lev 5:15 (P), 2Chr 36:14; Ezek 14:13; 15:8; 18:24.

b. wife against (בְּ‎) husband Num 5:12, 27 (P; marital infidelity).

c. elsewhere against (בְּ‎) God, Lev 26:40 (H), Leviticus 5:21; Num 5:6; 31:16 (?) Josh 22:16, 31 (all P), 1Chr 10:13; 28:19; Ezek 17:20; 20:27; 39:26; Dan 9:7.

2 usage without מַעַל‎ (only late):

a. פיו ימעל לא במשׁפט‎ Prov 16:10 let not his (the king's) mouth act treacherously against justice (RV in judgment).

b. elsewhere against God, with בְּ‎, Deut 32:51 (P), 1Chr 5:25; 12:2; 26:22; 1Chron 30:7; Ezra 10:2; Neh 13:27; Ezek 39:23; without בְּ‎, 2Chr 26:18; 29:6; Ezra 10:10; Neh 1:8; בַּחֵרֶם‎ 1Chr 2:7.
