H3330. yatstsiyb

יַצִיב‎ adjective certain, true (ᵑ7‎ id., firm, native (= אֶוְרָת‎)); — absolute ׳י‎ Dan 2:8, 45, emphatic בָא‎- 3:24(K§ 59.g); feminine singular בָא‎- 6:13 7:16; —

1 certain, sure Dan 2:45; in exclamation 3:24 undoubtedly! מִןיַֿצִּיב‎ 3:8 of a surety.

2 TRUE Dan 6:13; feminine as substantive 7:16 the truth concerning, עַל‎ of thing.
