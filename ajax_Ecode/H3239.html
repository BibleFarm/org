H3239. Yanowach

יָנוֺחַ‎ proper name, of a location in extreme north of Israel 2Kin 15:29; site dubious, Yânû— (ConderLists 38) near Tyre is probably too far west; GuérinGal. ii. 371 f. proposes Hunîn, west of upper Jordan, compare BuhlGeogr. 237; ᵐ5‎ Ανιωχ, A ᵐ5‎L Ιανωχ.

יָנ֫וֺחָה‎ proper name, of a location on border of Ephraim Josh 16:6-7, identification with Yânun southeast from Shechem RobBR iii. 297 compare BuhlGeogr. 178; ᵐ5‎ Ιανωχα, Ιανωκα.

יָנׄוחַ / יָנׄוחָה‎
