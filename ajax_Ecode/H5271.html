Derivation: or נָעֻר‎; and (feminine) נְעֻרָה‎; properly, passive participle from נַעַר‎ as denominative;
Strong's: (only in plural collectively or emphatic form) youth, the state (juvenility) or the persons (young people)
KJV: childhood, youth.

H5271. naur

נְעוּרִים‎ noun [masculine] plural youth, early life; — absolute ׳נ‎ Isa 54:6; Psa 127:4; elsewhere suffix נְעֻרַי‎ 1Sam 12:2, נְעוּרַי‎ Job 31:18 +, נְעֻרֶיךָ‎ 2Sam 19:8, נְעוּרֶיךָ‎ Prov 5:18 +, נְעוּרַיִךְ‎ Jer 2:2 +, נְעוּרָ֑יְכִי‎ Psa 103:5, נְעוּרֵיהֶן‎ Ezek 23:3, etc.; — youth, early life, especially in phrase ׳מִנּ‎ from one's youth up Gen 8:21 (J), 1Sam 17:33; 1Kin 18:12; Jer 3:24; Zech 13:5; Job 31:18 (extreme youth; || אִמִּי מִבֶּטֶן‎); figurative of Babylon Isa 47:12, 15, Moab Jer 48:11, Judah 22:21 compare Psa 71:5; 71:17; 129:1; 129:2; ׳מִנּ וְעַדעַֿתָּה‎ Gen 46:34 (J), 2Sam 19:8; Ezek 4:14; ׳מִנּ הַזֶּה וְעַדהַֿיּוֺם‎ 1Sam 12:2; Jer 3:25; as time in or during which Lev 22:13 (H), Num 30:4; Numbers 30:17 (P), Lam 3:27; Psa 144:12, figurative of Judah and Samaria, personified, Ezek 23:2, 8; compare נ ׳יְמֵי‎ (in figurative of Israel) Hosea 2:17 (|| מִצְרָ֑יִם מֵאֶרֶץ עֲלוֺתָהּ יוֺם‎), Ezek 16:22, 43, 60; 23:19; other phrase are: נ ׳אַלּוּף‎ Jer 3:4 friend of my youth, compare Prov 2:17, נ ׳עֲוֺנוֺת‎ Job 13:26, נ ׳חַטּאֹות‎ Psa 25:7, נ ׳חֶסֶד‎ Jer 2:2, נ ׳חֶרְמַּת‎ 31:19, נ ׳זִמַּת‎ Ezek 23:21, נ ׳שְׁדֵי‎ 23:21, נ ׳בַּעַל‎ Joel 1:8, and especially נ ׳אֵשֶׁת‎ Prov 5:18; Mal 2:14-15, Isa 54:6 (figurative); נ ׳בְּנֵי‎ Psa 127:4 sons of (a man's) youth; = youthful vigour 103:5.

[נְעוּרוֺת‎] noun [feminine] plural id.; — only מִנְּעֻרֹתֵיהֶם‎ Jer 32:30 (figurative of nation).
