H3157. Yizreel

יִזְרְעֶאל‎ 1. proper name, of a location (God soweth; Sabean אלדֿרא‎ MordtZMG 1879, 489; see LagBN 131) — ׳י‎ Josh 15:56; 2Sam 2:9 +; יִזְרְעֶ֑אל‎ 1Sam 25:43; Josh 17:16 +; יִזְרְעֶ֫אלָה‎ 19:18 6t.:

a. city in the Negeb of Judah Josh 15:56; 1Sam 25:43 (home of Ahinoam, one of David's wives), site unknown; compare proper name, masculine 1Chr 4:3.

b. city in Issachar Josh 19:18 on northwest spur of Mt. Gilboa 2Sam 4:4 (compare 1Sam 31:1, 8) 1Kin 4:12 (compare 1Sam 31:10, 12), modern Zer`în (RobBR ii. 318 ff. BdPal 244), close to scene of great battle with Philistines 29:1 (בְיִז אשׁר ׳בְּעַיִן‎, compare RobBR ii. 323) 29:11; subject to Ishbosheth 2Sam 2:9 (yet see below), residence of Ahab and Jehoram of Israel 1Kin 18:45-46, 21:1 (Naboth) 2Kin 8:29 (twice in verse) = 2Chr 22:6 (twice in verse), 2Kin 9:15 (twice in verse); 9:16-17, 30; 10:11 compare ׳יִז דְּמֵי‎ Hosea 1:4, and symbolical name of Hosea's son 1:4 (compare Che); — in 2Kin 10:1 for יִז ׳שָׂרֵי‎ read שֹׁמְרוֺן שָׂרֵי‎ so ᵐ5‎. Used by Hosea as representing Israel, with play on etymology, Hosea 2:24 (compare בָּאָרֶץ לִּי וּזְרַעְתִּיהָ‎ Hosea 2:25), compare also יִז יוֺם ׳גָּדוֺל‎ Hosea 1:2. Territory immediately about the city is יִז ׳חֵלֶק‎ 2Kin 9:10, 36, 37; compare יִז ׳חֵל‎ 1Kin 21:23 (read probably חלק‎, see Th Klo after ᵑ9 ᵑ6‎; the adjacent plain is יִז ׳עֵמֶק‎ (compare Di Josh 17:16 BdPal 229) 17:16; Judg 6:33; Hosea 1:5 (perhaps also = ׳יִז‎ 2Sam 2:9 above, || Gilead, Ephraim, Benjamin, etc.)

2. proper name, masculine a. Hosea's son Hosea 1:4, compare 1b above

b. Judahite name 1Chr 4:3, compare 1a above
