H818. ashem

אָשֵׁם‎ adjective guilty, ׳א‎ absolute 2Sam 14:13; plural אֶלאָֿחִינוּ אֲשֵׁמִים‎ Gen 42:21 (E); guilty, and so bound to offer a trespass-offering Ezra 10:19; but read probably וַאֲשָׁמָם‎, so KueChronol. see h. Perzische Tijdvak, 1890, 43.

אָשֵׁם‎
