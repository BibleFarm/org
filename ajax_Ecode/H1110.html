H1110. balaq

[בָּלַק‎] verb waste, lay waste (Assyrian balâ‡u, Pi`el destroy, LyonSargon 61; compare Arabic a desert) —

Po`el Participle without מ‎, suffix בּוֺלְקָהּ‎ Isa 24:1 (|| בּוֺקֵק‎, subject ׳י‎, object הארץ‎); — on form (which might also be Qal Participle) compare Ol§ 254 LagGN 1882, 403; also בקק‎ Po. Jer 51:2, and intensive use of Infinitive absolute Niph`al Isa 24:3; further Punic (i.e. intensive) Participle Nahum 2:11 (this however perhaps largely influenced by assonance).

Pu`al Participle מְבֻלָּקָ֑ה‎ Nahum 2:11 (ומב וּמְבוּקָה ׳בּוּקָה‎), devastated, or as substantive a devastated city.
