H4384. makshelah

מַכְשֵׁלָה‎ noun feminine 1. overthrown mass.

2 stumbling-block; —

1 figurative of kingdom Isa 3:6.

2 plural הַמַּכְשֵׁלוֺת‎ Zeph 1:3 stumbling-blocks = idols (compare מִכְשׁוֺל‎ 2c), but rather dubious; We thinks a gloss. SchwZAW x. (1890), 169 reads וְכִשַּׁלְתִּי‎ and I will cause the wicked to stumble.

I. כשׁף‎ (√ of following; compare Arabic cut off, cut up, Syriac to pray (literally probably to cut oneself, see 1Kin 18:28): so RsJ Phil. xiv. 125, 126 NöZMG 1886, 723; according to RS כֶּשֶׁף‎ is properly herbs etc. shredded into a magic brew).
