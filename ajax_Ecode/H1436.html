H1436. Gdalyah

גְּדַלְיָה‎ proper name, masculine (Yah is great) —

1 governor of Judea appointed by Nebuchadnezzar Jer 40:5, 8; 41:16.

2 son of Amariah, a son of Hezekiah Zeph 1:1.

3 priest of the sons of Jeshua Ezra 10:18.

גְּדַלְיָ֫הוּ‎ proper name, masculine (Yah(u) is great) —

1 governor of Judea = גדליה‎2Kin 25:22-25 Jer 39:14; 40:6-16; Jeremiah 41; 43:6 (24 t.)

2 son of Pashur, one of the chiefs of Jerusalem in the time of Jeremiah Jer 38:1.

3 one of the sons of Jeduthun, in the time of David 1Chr 25:3, 9.
