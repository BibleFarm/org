1 father of a prince under Jehoiakim Jer 36:12.

4 a false prophet of Jeremiah's time Jer 28:1, 5, 10, 11, 12, 13, 15 (twice in verse); 28:17.

5 one of the three companions of Daniel Dan 1:6-7, 11, 19.

6 grandfather of an officer of the guard in Jeremiah's time Jer 37:13.

7 a son of Zerubbabel 1Chr 3:19, 21.

8 a Benjamite 1Chr 8:24.
