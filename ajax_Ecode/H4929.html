H4929. mishmar

מִשְׁמָר‎ noun [masculine] place of confinement, goal, prison; late, guard, watch, observance; — absolute ׳מ‎ Gen 42:17 +; construct מִשְׁמַר‎ 40:3 +; suffix מִשְׁמָרוֺ‎ Neh 7:3, etc.; plural suffix רָיו‎- 13:14; —

1 goal, prison, ׳מ הַטַּבָּחִים שַׂר בֵּית‎ Gen 40:3, compare 40:4 (absolute), 40:7; 41:10; 42:17 (absolute), + 42:30 (ᵐ5‎), מִשְׁמַרְכֶם בֵּית‎ 42:19 (all E); guard-house in camp Lev 24:12 (H), Num 15:34 (P).

2 guard, Jer 51:12; Neh 4:3; 4:16, הַמּ ׳אַנְשֵׁי‎ 4:17; guard&-post 7:3; figurative of ׳י‎ setting a guard, עָלַימ תָשִׂים ׳כִּי‎ Job 7:12; band of (Levitical) guards or functionaries 1Chr 26:16; Neh 12:24 (compare מִשְׁמֶרֶת‎); act of guarding, ׳מִכָּלמֿ‎ Prov 4:23 above all guarding watch thy heart. — ׳לְמ‎ Ezek 38:7 is doubtful, Hi Co Toy and others reserve; Krae לְמשֵׁל‎.

3 plural observances, services, of temple Neh 13:14.
