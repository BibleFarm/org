H3607. kala

[כָּלָא‎] verb shut up, restrain, withhold (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic כְּלָא‎, ; Ethiopic restrain, hinder; Assyrian Kâlû, id.; Arabic protect Qor 21:43 compare BaZMG 1887, 605 Anm. 4) —

Qal Perfect3masculine singular suffix כְּלָאוֺ‎ Jer 32:3; 3feminine singular כָּֽלְאָה‎ Hag 1:10; 2feminine singular suffix כְּלִתִ֫נִי‎ 1Sam 25:33; 1singular כָּלִאתִי‎ Psa 119:101; 3plural כָּֽלְאוּ‎ Hag 1:10, כָּלוּ‎ 1Sam 6:10; Imperfect3masculine singular יִכְלֶה‎ Gen 23:6; 2masculine singular תִּכְלָא‎ Psa 40:12; 2feminine singular תִּכְלָאִ֑י‎ Isa 43:6; 1singular אֶכְלָא‎ Psa 40:10; Imperative suffix כְּלָאֵם‎ Num 11:28; Infinitive construct לִכְלוֺא‎ Eccl 8:8; Passive participle כָּלוּא‎ Jer 32:2, כָּלֻא‎ Psa 88:9; (on the metaplastic forms, as if from כלה‎, see Ges§ 75, 21 c Köi. 611); —

1 shut up, with accusative 1Sam 6:10 (+ בְ‎ local), Jer 32:3; passive participle 32:2 (בְ‎ local), figurative Psa 88:9 (opposed to יצא‎).

2 restrain, with accusative 1Sam 25:33 (+ בְדָמִים מִבּוֺא‎), Psa 119:101 (+ מִכָּלֿ‎); כְּלָאֵם‎ (sc. מֵהִתְנַבֵּא‎) Num 11:28; ׳כ שְׂפָתַי‎ Psa 40:10; ׳כ אֶתהָֿרוּחַ‎ Eccl 8:8. On Psa 74:11 see כלה‎ Pi`el 2. 3. withhold, מֵתֶ֑ךָ מִקְּבֹר מִמְּךָ ׳כ אֶתקִֿבְרוֺ‎ Gen 23:6 (P), ׳כ מִמֶּ֑נּי רַחֲמֶיךָ‎ Psa 40:12; Hag 1:10 b, probably also va (read טָ֑ל‎ for מִטָּ֑ל ᵐ5 ᵑ0‎, We); absolute אַלתִּֿכְלָאִי‎ (that is, exiles) Isa 43:6 (|| תְּנִי‎).
