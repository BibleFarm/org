H469. Elitsaphan

or Eltsaphan אֱלִיצָפָן‎ proper name, masculine (God has protected, compare Phoenician צפנבעל‎)

a. chief of the Kohathites Num 3:30; 1Chr 15:8; 2Chr 29:13; = אֶלְצָפָן‎ Exod 6:22; Lev 10:4.

b. chief of Zebulun Num 34:25.
