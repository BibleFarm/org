H1006. Bayith

בַּ֫יִת‎ with article הַבַּיִת‎ Isa 15:2 according to Ew Brd Di and others proper name, of a location but abbreviated; perhaps for דִּבְלָתַיִם בֵּית‎ Jer 48:22 (so Ew and others) or בָּמוֺת בֵּית‎ compare MI27 (compare De Di); others (Ges De Che Or) take ׳הַבּ‎ here = the house, i.e. temple or shrine.
