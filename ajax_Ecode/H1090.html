H1090. Bilhah

I. בִּלְהָה‎ proper name, feminine (etymology dubious) handmaid of Rachel, concubine of Jacob Gen 29:29; 30:3-4, 5, 7; 35:22, 25 (sons Dan & Naphtali) 37:2 (probably gloss, Ol) 46:25; 1Chr 7:13.

II. בִּלהָה‎

proper name, of a location a city of Simeon 1Chr 4:29, probably = בַּעֲלָה‎ Josh 15:29, בָּלָה‎ 19:3 compare Di; site dubious
