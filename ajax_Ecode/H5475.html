סוֺד‎ noun [masculine] council, counsel; — ׳ס‎ absolute Prov 11:13 +; construct Jer 6:11 +; suffix סוֺדִי‎ 23:22; Job 19:19; סוֺדוֺ‎ Amos 3:7; Prov 3:32; סֹדָם‎ Gen 49:6; —

1 council, in familiar conversation; —

a. divan or circle of familiar friends, ׳ס בַּחוּרִים‎ Jer 6:11, ׳מ משׂחקים‎ 15:17; סוֺדִ ׳מְתֵי‎ Job 19:19 men of my intimate circle; (י בסוד ׅ׳עמד‎ Jer 23:18, 22 in the intimate circle of ׳י‎; אלוה בסוד‎ Job 15:8; in bad sense, ׳ס מְרֵעִים‎ Psa 64:3.

b. assembly, company, ׳ס יְשָׁרִים‎ Psa 111:1 (|| עֵדָה‎; עמי סוֺד‎ Ezek 13:9; ׳ס קדשׁים‎ Psa 89:8 (of angels); in bad sense, בא בסוד‎ Gen 49:6 (|| קהל‎).

2 counsel, taken by those in familiar conversation:

a. counsel itself, סוד באין מחשׁבות‎ Prov 15:22 thoughts without counsel; of intimate friendship, Psa 55:15 סוֺד נַמְתִּיק‎; in bad sense, of crafty plotting 83:4 סוֺד יַעֲרִימוּ‎

b. secret counsel, which may be revealed (גלה‎), Amos 3:7; Prov 11:13; 20:19; 25:9.

c. familiar converse with God, intimacy, ס ׳ליראיו ׳י‎ Psa 25:14 intimacy with ׳י‎ have those who fear him (|| ברית‎), סודוֺ אתיֿשׁרים‎ Prov 3:22 with the upright is his intimacy; here also Job 29:4 (si vera lectio) Di De and others; Siegf Du compare BuhlLex בְּסֹךְ‎ when Eloah sheltered my tent.
