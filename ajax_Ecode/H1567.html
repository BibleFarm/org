H1567. Galed

גַּלְעֵד‎ proper name, of a location (witness-pile) name of pile of stones erected by Jacob and his company (KitGeschichte.129; by Laban We Di) Gen 31:47-48, (see גַּל‎ 1c) apparently intended to explain גִּלְעָד‎ q. v.; Di conjecture also (as name of altar) Josh 22:34 (compare ᵑ6‎).

גַּלְעֵד‎
原文音譯：d[el.G: gal-ade’

詞類次數：專有名詞（2）

原文字根：旋轉（堆）-更遠的（見證）
