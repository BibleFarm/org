עֶשְׂרִים‎315 noun plural indeclinable (a) twenty (ancient plural of עֶשֶׂר‎ PräBAS i. 376; Late Hebrew = Biblical Hebrew; Arabic ; Assyrian ešrâ; Sabean עשרי‎ HomChrest. 48; Ethiopic Aramaic עשרין‎, ; Nabataean עשרין‎); —

I. as cardinal: 1 without other number:

a. usually followed by singular noun (especially אַמָּה אִישׁ, שָׁנָה,‎, etc.) Gen 31:38; 1Sam 14:14; Exod 38:18 64t., + אֶלֶף עֶשְׂרִים‎20, 000 1Chr 18:4 7t. + Ezek 45:1 (reading אָ֑לֶף עֶשְׂרִים‎, see עֶשֶׂר‎ 2b).

b. followed by plural noun 2Sam 3:20; Exod 36:23-24, 25 4t.

c. followed by singular noun collective 1Kin 5:3.

d. plural noun precedes Gen 32:15 (twice in verse); 32:16; 2Chr 3:3 6t.

e. noun omitted Gen 18:31 (twice in verse) (׳הָע‎), 1Kin 6:2 6t., as predicate Exod 27:10 (twice in verse); 27:11 (twice in verse) + 4 t.

f. + בָּאַמָּה‎ Zechariah 5:12.

2 + other number:

a. with units, usually 20 + (with וְ‎) unit + singular noun Judg 10:2-3, 1Kin 14:20 58t. (including 20 1* 1000, etc., 27 t.); noun omitted Josh 15:32 2t. predicate; less often unit + 20 +? Gen 11:24; Exod 38:24; Num 8:24 (all P) + 27 t.; unit + 20 + בָּאַמָּה‎ Ezek 40:21; Exod 26:2; 36:9 (both P).

b.20+? Num 7:8 (twice in verse) (P), 1Chr 2:22 3t. Chronicles

c. plural noun + 20 +? Josh 19:30 (twice in verse); 1Chr 12:29 (van d. H. v.12:28).

d. c. hundreds, 20 usually following; followed by singular noun 1Kin 9:14; Gen 6:3; Deut 31:2 7t.; noun omitted 2Chr 3:4; Ezra 2:32 = Neh 7:35; 20100(noun omitted) Num 7:86.

e. plural noun + 100 20 1Chr 15:5-6, — compare further מֵאָה‎.

II. as ordinal:

120alone, ע ׳בִּשְׁנַת‎ in the twentieth year 1Kin 15:9 4t.; noun omitted Num 10:11 3t.

2. a.20+? Ezek 29:17; 40:1.

b. ns. + 20 +? Hag 1:15; 2Chr 7:10 +; ns. + unit + 20 Exod 12:8; בִּשְׁנַת‎20+? 1Kin 16:10, etc. — See references below עֶשֶׂר‎.
