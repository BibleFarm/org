H634. Esarchaddon

אֵסַרְחַדֹּן‎ proper name, masculine Esarhaddon (Assyrian Ašura—-iddina, Ashur hath given a brother) king of Assyria B.C. 681-668, son & successor of Sennacherib Isa 37:38 = 2Kin 19:37 (van d. H. אֵסַרחַֿדֹּן‎); Ezra 4:2; compare COT.
