גֵּר‎ noun masculineExod 12:48 sojourner (Arabic , Ethiopic Aramaic , גִּיּוֺר‎, prostelyte, גַּיֵּר‎ proselytize, Phoenician גר‎ in proper name, & plural גרם‎) — גֵּר‎ Gen 15:16 74t.; suffix גֵּרְךָ‎ Exod 20:10 4t., גֵּרוֺ‎ Deut 1:16; plural גֵּרִים‎ Exod 22:20 9t., גֵּירִים‎2Chr 2:16; —

1 sojourner, temporary dweller, new-comer (no inherited rights), compare Exod 12:19; Lev 24:16; Num 15:30; Josh 8:33 (opposed to homeborn); of Abraham at Hebron Gen 23:4 (P; || תּוֺשָׁב‎); Moses in desert Exod 2:22 (J) 18:3 (E; here explanation of name Gershom, Moses' son); as claiming hospitality Job 31:32; perhaps in above cases, and certainly in General, with technical sense; figurative of Yahweh Jer 14:8; of Israel in Egypt Gen 15:13; Exod 22:20; 23:9 (all J E) Lev 19:34 (H) Deut 10:19; 23:8; גֵּרִים‎ with Yahweh Lev 25:23 (H) 1Chr 29:15; Psa 39:13 (in all || תּוֺשָׁב‎) compare 119:19.

2 usually of גֵּרים‎ in Israel 2Sam 1:13 (Amalekite) compare Josh 8:33, 35 (E) 20:9 (P) Isa 14:1; dwellers in Israel with certain conceded, not inherited right (compare RSOTJC 434; 2nd ed. 342. n.; K 42; Sem 75 f. StaGeschichte.i.400). The גֵּר‎ is to share in Sabbath rest Exod 20:10; 23:12 (both J E) Deut 5:14; otherwise he is have like obligations with Israel Exod 12:19, 48, 49; Lev 16:29 (all P) 17:8, 10, 12, 13, 15; 18:26; 20:2; 22:18; 24:16, 22 (all H) Num 9:14 (twice in verse); 15:14-15, (twice in verse); 15:16, 26, 29, 30; 19:10; 35:15 (all P) Ezek 14:7; similar rights Deut 1:16; Ezek 47:22-23, and like privileges Deut 16:11, 14; 26:11; 29:10; 31:12 compare 2Chr 30:25; very rarely any distinction made, in obligation Lev 25:47 (3 t. in verse) (H), in permissible food Deut 14:21; in future success 28:43; kindness to גֵּר‎ frequently enjoined: Lev 19:10 (|| עָנִי‎), 23:22 (|| id.) 19:34 (all H); Deut 10:18-19, 14:29; 24:19-20, 21; 26:12-13, (all || וְאַלְמָנָה יָתוֺם‎); oppression prohibited Lev 19:33 (H) Deut 24:14; Exod 22:20; 23:9 (twice in verse) (JE) Deut 24:17; 27:19; Jer 7:6; 22:3; Zech 7:10 (these eight || וְאַלְמָנָה יָתוֺם‎); object of care to ׳י‎ Psa 146:9 (|| id.); charge that גֵּר‎ has been oppressed Ezek 22:7; Mal 3:5 (both || id.); also Ezek 22:29 (|| וְאֶבְיוֺן עָנִי‎), Psa 94:6; compare also command that a poor brother be treated like גֵּר‎ i.e. kindly, Lev 25:35 (H).
