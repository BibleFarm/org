H513. Eltolad

אֶלְתּוֺלַד‎ proper name, of a location (compare תּוֺלַד‎ below ילד‎) city in southern Judah Josh 15:30; 19:4; compare also תּוֺלָ֑ד‎ (q. v.) 1Chr 4:29.
