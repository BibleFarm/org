מַשְׂאֵת‎ (masʼêth｜mas-ayth'｜noun｜feminine｜uprising)
[Heb] מַשְׂאֵת‎, מַשְׂאֹות‎ ETCBC: מַשְׂאֵת‎ (subs｜lifting up) OSHL: n.fm.aj TWOT: 1421h GK: H5367, H5368 Greek: δῶρον, μερίς, πύργος, σημεῖον, σύσσημον
Derivation: from נָשָׂא‎;
Strong's: properly, (abstractly) a raising (as of the hands in prayer), or rising (of flame); figuratively, an utterance; concretely, a beacon (as raised); a present (as taken), mess, or tribute; figuratively, a reproach (as a burden)
KJV: burden, collection, sign of fire, (great) flame, gift, lifting up, mess, oblation, reward.

H4864. maseth

מַשְׂאֵת‎ noun feminine uprising, utterance, burden, portion (on formation compare Ol§ 109 a Sta§ 272 b Köii. 1, 183; Phoenician (Pun) has משאת‎ = payment, tax see Lzb326); — absolute ׳מ‎ Judg 20:40 +; construct מַשְׂאַת‎ Gen 43:34 +; plural absolute מַשְׂאֹת‎ 43:34, + (probably) מַשְׂאוֺת‎ Lam 2:14 (see below); construct מַשְׂאֹת‎ Gen 43:34; —

1 that which rises, uprising, uplifting:

a. ׳מ הֶעָשָׁן‎ Judg 20:38 uprising of smoke, compare (׳הֶע‎ omitted) 20:40.

b. in General = signal Jer 6:1 (= נֵס‎ Gf.).

c. uplifting of hands (כַּמַּי‎) in prayer Psa 141:2 (|| תְּפִלָּה‎).

2 utterance, oracle (= III. מַשָּׂא‎), Lam 2:14 (Bu Löhr).

3 burden Zeph 3:18 (of reproach, figurative).

4. a. portion (carried to some one), especially from table of king or superior, Gen 43:34 (3 t. in verse) (J; on custom see Di and references), 2Sam 11:8; so read perhaps also 19:43 (for נִשֵּׂאת ᵑ0‎) Gr HPS.

b. perhaps also more Generally, present, largess from a superior Jer 40:5 ( + אֲרֻחָה‎ food-allowance), Est 2:18 (הַמֶּלֶךְ כְּיַד‎).

c. exacted or enforced gift, from inferior, ׳מ בַּר‎ Amos 5:11 (compare Phoenician above; see also מנחה‎).

d. sacred contribution, tax ׳מ משֶׁה‎ (׳י‎) עֶבֶד‎2Chr 24:6, 9.

e. offering to ׳י‎ Ezek 20:40.
