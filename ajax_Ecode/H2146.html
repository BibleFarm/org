H2146. zikkaron

זִכָּרֹן זִכָּרוֺן,‎ noun masculineEccl 1:11 memorial, remembrance (compare LgBN 199, 200) — absolute זִכָּרוֺן‎ Josh 4:7 +; זִכָּרֹן‎ Exod 28:12 (twice in verse); 28:29; construct זִכְרוֺן‎ Eccl 1:11 2t.; suffix זִכְרוֺנֵךְ‎ Isa 57:8; plural הַזִּכְרֹנוֺת‎ Est 6:1; suffix זִכְרֹנֵיכֶם‎ Job 13:12; —

1 memorial, reminder:

a. memorial-day Exod 12:14 (P).

b. memorial-usage Exod 13:9 (JE).

c. memorial-objects, altar-plates Num 17:5 (P); stones in Jordan Josh 4:7 (JE); crowns in temple Zech 6:14; ׳ז‎ in Isa 57:8 is symbol of strange god (Di), or perhaps phallus-image, as sign of harlot (Che), compare > Du (who proposes זְכָרוֺן‎, from זָכָר‎).

d. memorial-record; in a book Exod 17:14 (E); compare זִכָּרוֺן סֵפֶר‎ Mal 3:16, ׳ס הימים דברי הַזִּכְרֹנות‎ Est 6:1; memorial, as proof of citizenship Neh 2:20 (|| צְדָקָה חֵלֶק,‎); reminder of Israel, י ׳לִפְנֵי‎, of הַכִּמֻּרִים כֶּסֶף‎ Exod 30:16; spoils of war Num 31:54; inscribed stones of ephod Exod 28:12, 29, called ז ׳אַבְנֵי‎ 28:12 = 39:7; blowing of trumpets Num 10:10 (foregoing all P), compare תְּרוּעַה זִכְרוֺן‎ Lev 23:24 (H; where, however, no י ׳לפני‎, see Di); (ה)זכרון מִנְחַת‎ Num 5:15, 18 (P); memorial-sentence, apophthegm Job 13:12. **אֵפֶר מִשְׁלֵיֿ זִכְרֹנֵיכֶם‎ 13:12 your memorial words are ashen sayings (i.e. worthless), compare Bu Du; Margolis (privately) suggests that ᵐ5‎ seems to have read לְאֵפֶר יִמָּשֵׁל‎ [? < רָנְּכֶם‎] רָנְיְכֶם‎ your exultation shall be like ashes.

2 remembrance Eccl 1:11 (construct before preposition Ges§ 130, 1), 1:11; 2:16.
