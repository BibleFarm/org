[Heb] שָׂרַע‎ ETCBC: שׂרע‎ (verb｜deform) OSHL: u.ci.aa TWOT: 2291 GK: H8594
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to prolong, i.e. (reflex) be deformed by excess of members
KJV: stretch out self, (have any) superfluous thing.

H8311. sara

[שָׂרַע‎] verb extend (Arabic point directly at, also enter upon a path (Frä213 thinks originally divide, then stretch out, make straight), projecting roof, long-nosed (torto naso ᵑ9‎Lev 21:18); Ethiopic dispose, arrange; Sabean שרע‎ arrangement, security HomChr 124; — but Aramaic explanation as mutilated 21:18; 22:23 PS2744, compare ᵐ5‎ ὠτότμητος); —

Qal Passive participle שָׂרוּעַ‎ extended, i.e. too long (in a limb or member), of man Lev 21:18, beast 22:23 (see II. חרם‎).

Hithpa`el stretch oneself: Infinitive construct הִשְׂתָּרֵעַ‎ Isa 28:20 (see מַצַּע‎ p. 427:a).

שַׂרְעַמִּים‎ see below שׂעף‎.
