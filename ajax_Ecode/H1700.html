[דִּבְרָה‎] noun feminine cause, reason, manner — construct דִּבִרַת עַל‎ because of, for the sake of Eccl 3:18; 7:14; 8:2; מַלְכִּיצֶֿדֶק עַלדִּֿבְרָתִי‎ after the order, or manner, of Melchizedek Psa 110:4 (י‎ ancient Genitive ending, to soften transition in poetry, Ges§ 90 (3) a); suffix דִּבְרָתִי‎ my cause, suit Job 5:8.

[דִּבְרָה‎] noun feminine cause, reason (Biblical Hebrew id., √ דבר‎, compare דבר על‎ Egyptian Aramaic S-CPap. B 5.6.8.16); — construct in adverb phrase דִּי עַלדִּֿבְרַת‎ Dan 2:30 for the cause (to the intent) that, 4:14 (read עַל‎ for עַד‎ K§ ll, 2 Bev).
