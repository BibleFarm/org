H3939. laanah

לַעֲנָה‎ noun feminine wormwood (Late Hebrew id.; Arabic is curse (verb id.), so Nabataean לצנת‎ (verb לען‎) compare CookGlossary); — only figurative of bitter things: — of perverted justice, Amos 5:7; 6:12 (|| ראֹשׁ‎; of result of idolatry, Deut 29:17 (|| id.); of ׳י‎s chastisement Jer 9:14 (|| מֵ֯ירֿאֹשׁ‎, 23:15 (|| id.), Lam 3:15 (|| מְרוֺרִים‎), 3:19 (|| ראֹשׁ‎); simile of bitter (מָרָה‎) result of intercourse with strange woman Prov 5:4. — On wormwood (Artemisia absinthium, etc., Greek ἀψίθιον) compare Löw80f. 401, 421 TristrNat, Hist. Bib. 493.
