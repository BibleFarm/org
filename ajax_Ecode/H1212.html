H1212. Betsalel

בְּצַלְאֵל‎ proper name, masculine (in the shadow (protection) of El; compare cuneiform ‚il-Bêl, a king of Gaza, COT Josh 11:22) —

1 a skilled artisan of tribe of Judah Exod 31:2; 35:30; 36:1-2, 37:1; 38:22 (all P) 1Chr 2:20; 2Chr 1:5.

2 an Israelite Ezra 10:30.
