I. שֵׁן‎ noun feminine1Sam 2:13 (yet masculine 14:5 compare Dr) tooth, ivory; — ׳שׁ‎ absolute Exod 21:24 +, construct 21:27 +, ׳שֶׁן‎ Deut 32:24; suffix שִׁנּם‎ Exod 21:27; dual שִׁנַּיִם‎ Prov 10:26, construct שִׁנֵּי‎ Jer 31:29 +; suffix שִׁנֵּיהֶם‎ Psa 124:6, שִׁנֵּימוֺ‎ 58:7, etc.; —

1 tooth:

a. of man, Exod 21:27 (3 t. in verse) (E), Num 11:33 (J), Gen 49:12 (poem in J), Amos 4:6; Song 4:2; 6:6, ׳שׁ רֹעָה‎ Prov 25:19, see II. רעע‎

2; of lex talionis, ׳שׁ שׁ ׳תַּחַת‎ Exod 21:24 (E), Lev 24:20 (H), בְּשׁ ׳שֵׁן‎ Deut 19:21; irritated by acid Prov 10:26, dulled by it (in proverb of inherited guilt) Jer 31:29-30, Ezek 18:2; שׁ ׳חָרַק‎ Lam 2:16; Job 16:9; Psa 35:16; 112:10, see חָרַק‎; בְשִׁנָּ֑י בְשָׂרִי אֶשָּׂא‎ Job 13:14 = risk my life (strike out עַלמָֿה‎, dittograph; || נַפְשִׁי מִּי בְּכַּ אָשִׂים‎); שִׁנָּ֑י בְּעוֺר‎ 19:20, see מָלַט‎

Hithpa`el (other conjectures see Di Bi Bu Du).

b. of beasts : Deut 32:24, crocodile Job 41:6, locusts Joel 1:6 (teeth of lions); especially figurative of false prophets Micah 3:5, of foes Zech 9:7, oppressors Job 29:17, wicked 4:10; Psa 3:8; 58:7; 124:6; of their weapons Prov 30:14; Psa 57:5; of Israel shattered by ׳י‎.

c. tooth of a fork, tine, 1Sam 2:13 (compare DrSm. 291); of cliff, הַסֶּלַע שֵׁן‎14:4 (twice in verse), compare Job 39:28, הַשֵּׁן‎ 1Sam 14:5.

2 ivory, of commerce, שֵׁן קַרְנוֺת‎ Ezek 27:15 (i.e. tusks?); as material, שׁ ׳מִטּוֺת‎ Amos 6:4, ׳כִּסֵּאשֿׁ‎ 1Kin 10:18 2Chr 9:17, הַשּׁ ׳בֵּית‎ 1Kin 22:39, compare Amos 3:15, שׁ ׳הֵיכְלֵי‎ Psa 45:9, also Ezek 27:6; Song 5:15; 7:5.
