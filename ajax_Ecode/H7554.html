רָקַע‎ (râqaʻ｜raw-kah'｜verb｜beat)
[Heb] רָקַע‎ ETCBC: רקע‎ (verb｜stamp) OSHL: t.eo.aa TWOT: 2217 GK: H8392 Greek: στερεόω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to pound the earth (as a sign of passion); by analogy to expand (by hammering); by implication, to overlay (with thin sheets of metal)
KJV: beat, make broad, spread abroad (forth, over, out, into plates), stamp, stretch.
