H2977. Yoshiyyah

or Yoshiyyahu יאֹשִׁיָּה יאֹשִׁיָּ֫הוּ,‎ proper name, masculine (׳י‎ supporteth)

1 יאֹשִׁיָּהוּ‎ king of Judah, son of Amon 1Kin 13:2; 2Kin 21:24, 26 11t. Kings, + 19 t. Chronicles, + 17 t. Jeremiah + Zeph 1:1; also יאֹו֯שִׁיָּהוּ‎ Jer 27:1.
