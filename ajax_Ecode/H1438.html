H1438. gada

גָּדַע‎ verb hew, hew down or off (Late Hebrew id. (rare), Aramaic גְּדַע‎ Ithpe`el; Arabic cut off hand or other member, mutilate) —

Qal Perfect גָּדַע‎ Lam 2:3; וְגָדַעְתִּ֫י‎ 1Sam 2:31; Imperfect וָאָגְדַּע‎ Zech 11:10, 14; Participle passive גְּדוּעָה‎ Isa 15:2 (so many editions but) Baer גְּרוּעָה‎; compare below, גְּדֻעִים‎ 10:33; — hew, cut in two, a staff Zech 11:10, 14; metaphor hew off, an arm 1Sam 2:31; horns Lam 2:3; hew down, trees Isa 10:33; if in 15:2 then = shave off (object זקן‎, beard), but no other indication of this meaning, and true גְּרוּעָה ᵑ0‎, compare Baer's note & Jer 48:37; see גרע‎.

Niph`al Perfect נִגְדַּע‎ Judg 21:6 נֶגְדַּע‎ Jer 50:23; 3feminine singular נִגְדְּעָה‎ 48:25; וְנִגְדְּעָה‎ consecutive Isa 22:25; 2masculine singular נִגְדַּעְתָּ‎ 14:12; 3plural וְנִגְדְּעוּ‎ consecutive Ezek 6:6; Amos 3:14; — be hewn off, of altar-horns 3:14, of idols Ezek 6:6 (|| נשׁבר‎); of severance of a tribe from nation Judg 21:6; figurative of king of Babylon Isa 14:12; of Babylon as hammer Jer 50:23 (|| נשׁבר‎); of a minister, under figure of secure peg or pin Isa 22:25; of horn of Moab Jer 48:25 (|| נשׁבר‎).

Pi`el Perfect גִּדַּע‎2Chr 34:7, גִּדֵּ֑עַ‎34:4; Psa 107:16, וַיְגַדַּע‎2Chr 14:2, אֲגַדֵּ֑עַ‎ Psa 7:5; Isa 45:2, וַיְגַדְּעוּ‎2Chr 31:1; 3 masculine plural תְּגַדֵּע֑וּן‎ Deut 7:5; 12:3; — hew off, down, in two (compare Qal) of Asherîm 7:5; 2Chr 14:2; 31:1, of idols Deut 12:3 (מְּסִילִים‎) 2Chr 34:4, 7 (חַמָּנִים‎); figurative horns of wicked Psalm 75:11; bars of iron (i.e. of Babylon's gates) Isa 45:2, compare Psa 107:16.

Pu`al Perfect גֻּדָּ֑עוּ‎ Isa 9:9 hew down (of trees).
