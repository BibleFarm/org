קָרוֺב קָרֹב,‎78

adjective near; — absolute קָרֹב‎ Exod 12:4 +, וֺב‎- 2 Samuel 19:48 +; plural קְרֹבִים‎ 1Kin 8:59, קְרוֺבִים‎ Isa 33:13 +; feminine plural קְרֹבוֺת‎ Ezek 22:5, etc.; — near (often opposed to רָחוֺק‎) :

1 of city Gen 19:20 (+ שָׁ֫מָּה לָנוּס‎), Jer 48:24, ׳הַקּ‎ Deut 21:3 the nearest (+אֶל‎), compare (of elders) 21:6; of land 1Kin 8:46 2Chr 6:36; road Exod 13:17; = contiguous, כֶּרֶם‎ 1Kin 21:2 (+ בֵּיתִי אֵצֶל‎).

2 of person:

a. + אֶל‎ person Gen 45:10; Deut 13:8; 22:2; Josh 9:16; 1 Chronicles 12:41 (van d. H. v40); ׳הַקּ אֶל‎ Exod 12:4 the nearest to; with suffix קְרֹבוֺ‎ 32:27, i.e. his neighbour.

b. absolute (opposed to or + רחוק‎, oft,= near and far, i.e. all without distinction), Ezek 6:12; Isa 57:19; Prov 27:10; plural Isa 33:13; Jer 25:26; Dan 9:7; Est 9:20; Exod 22:5; ׳ק‎ of distress, personified, Psa 22:12.; — Ezek 23:5, 12 Co Berthol read קְרוּאִים‎ famous; Toy רַבִּים‎ (compare Jer 39:13); Hpt in Toy Krae conjectures קְרוֺדִים‎ (Assyrian ‡urâ du, warrior).

c. of near relationship, + אֶל‎ person2 Sam Jeremiah 19:43; Lev 21:2-3, 25:25 (all H), Num 27:11 (P); ל‎ pers Neh 13:4; Ruth 2:20; + מִן‎ compare 3:12; of intimacy, קְרֹנוֺ‎ Psa 15:3 (|| רֵעֵהוּ‎), קְרוֺבַי‎ Job 19:14 (|| מְיֻדָּעַי‎) Psa 38:12 (|| וְרֵעַי אֹהֲנַי‎); near, in office, Est 1:14.

d. near to י קְרֹבוֺ ׳עַם‎ Psa 148:14 (Ri Bae קְרכָו‎ the people of those near him).

e. near to ׳י‎ in ceremon function, with אֶל‎ Ezek 43:19, with ל‎ 42:13, suffix Lev 10:3.

f. of God (׳י‎), + אֶל‎ person Deut 4:7; 30:14; ל‎ pers Psa 34:19; 145:18; abs Jer 12:2; Isa 50:8; 55:6 Psa 119:151; מִקָּרֹב אֱלֹהֵי‎ Jer 23:23 a God from near by (opposed to ׳א מֵרָחוֺק‎ ); ׳ק‎ of י ׳צֶרֶק‎ Isa 51:5, י ׳יֶשַׁע‎ Psa 85:10 (+ ל‎ of person); — שִׁמְךָ ׳ק‎ 75:2, read בְּשׁ ׳קֹרְאֵי‎ Dy Che Gr Dr, compare Bae ᵐ5‎ SyeVer Du.

g. of word, + אֶל‎ person Deut 30:14; prayer, + ׳אֶליֿ‎ 1Kin 8:59.

3 of time : ׳ק אֵידָם יוֺם‎ Deut 32:35; especially of י ׳יוֺם‎ Zeph 1:7, 14 (twice in verse); Ezek 30:3 (perhaps del as ᵐ5‎ Co Krae), 30:3; Isa 13:6; Obadiah 15; Joel 1:15; 2:1; Joel 4:14, compare Ezek 7:7; + infinitive ׳ק לָבוֺא אֵירמֿוֺאָב‎ Jer 48:16, compare Isa 13:22; 56:1; ק ׳מְחִתָּה‎ Prov 10:14 imminent ruin; מִקָּרוֺכ‎ Deut 32:17 recently, so Ezek 11:3 ᵑ6 ᵐ5‎ Co Siegf Berthol (for Mt בְּקָרוֺב‎) 7:8 soon; Job 20:5 of the briefest; — ק ׳אוֺר חשֶׁח מִמְּנֵי‎ 17:12 is dubious; Thes (my) light is near to (prope abest a, as Arabic , but not proven in Hebrew) darkness; Di light (they say) is near (breaking away) from darkness, will soon leave it behind; But מֶּןיֶֿחְשַׁךְ קְרוֺבָם אוֺר‎ the light of their intimate friend shall not (?)grow dark.
