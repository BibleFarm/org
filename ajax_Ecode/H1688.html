H1688. Debir

II. דְּבִיר‎ 1. proper name, masculine king of Eglon Josh 10:3.

2. proper name, of a location a. דְּבִ֫רָה‎ with ָ  ה‎ locative, northern border Judah Josh 15:7 (westward Hup Psa 28:2).

b. לִדְבִר‎ town of the Gadites Josh 13:26, perhaps Lôdebâr 2Sam 9:4 see דְבָר לֹא‎ Di MV.

c. דְּבִיר‎ Judg 1:11 (twice in verse); 1Chr 6:43, דְּבִרָה‎ Josh 10:38-39, elsewhere דְּבִר‎ 15:15 +, a royal city of Canaanites anciently called סֵפֶר קִרְיַת‎ 15:15; Judg 1:11, סַנָּה קִרְיַת‎ Josh 15:49; on the mountains of Judah, in region of Hebron 11:21; 15:49, assigned to the Aaronite priests 21:15 as a city of refuge 1Chr 6:43; modern Dhoherîye, 5 hours southwest from Hebron, according to Kn, so Surveyiii. 402; other conjectures in Di Josh 10:39 RiHWB 265.
