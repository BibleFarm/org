H6780. tsemach

צֶ֫מַח‎ noun masculineJer 23:5 sprout, growth; — absolute ׳צ‎ Hosea 8:7 +, צֶ֑מַח‎ Zech 3:8; construct צֶ֫מַח‎ Gen 19:25+; suffix צִמְחָהּ‎ Ezek 17:9 +; —

1 collective sprouting, growth, ׳צ הָאֲדָמָה‎ Gen 19:25 (J), ׳צ הַשָּׂדֶה‎ Ezek 16:7 (simile), compare Isa 61:11; Psa 65:11; so ׳צ יהוה‎ Isa 4:2, ׳צ‎ absolute Hosea 8:7; growth (= foliage) of vine, צ ׳טַרְמֵּי‎ Ezek 17:9.

2 process of growth, of vine, צִמְתָהּ עֲרֻגֹת‎ Ezek 17:10 = the beds where it grew.

3 future ruler, under figure of sprout from Davidic tree (compare Psa 132:17) צ ׳לְדָוִר צַדִּיק וַהֲקִמֹתִי‎ Jer 23:5 a righteous sprout, shoot, = צ לְדָוִד ׳אַצְמִיחַ צְדָקָה‎ 33:15; hence (as proper name) צ ׳עַבְדִּי‎ Zech 3:8, of Zerubbabel צ ׳אִישׁ שְׁמוֺ‎ 6:12.
