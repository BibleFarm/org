H2250. chabburah

חַבּוּרָה‎, [חֲבֻרָה חַבֻּרָה,‎]

noun feminine stripe, blow; — absolute ׳הַבּ‎ Isa 1:6 2t.; suffix חַבֻּרָתִי‎ Gen 4:23; חֲבֻרָתוֺ‎ Isa 53:5; plural construct חַבֻּרוֺת‎ Prov 20:30; suffix חַבּוּרֹתָ֑י‎ Psa 38:6: — stripe, blow, stroke, Gen 4:23 my blow, i.e. for striking me (J), compare Exod 21:25 (twice in verse) (JE), also of injury to land of Judah (under figure of human body) Isa 1:6 (all || מֶּצַע‎, see below); of blows (singular collective) inflicted on suffering servant of ׳י‎ 53:5; (plural) Psa 38:6, מֶּצַע חַבֻּרוֺת‎ Prov 20:30, i.e. blows that cut in (|| מַכּוֺת‎).
