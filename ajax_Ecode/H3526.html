כָּבַס‎ (kâbaç｜kaw-bas'｜verb｜tread)
[Heb] כָּבַס‎ ETCBC: כבס‎ (verb｜wash) OSHL: k.aj.aa TWOT: 946 GK: H3891 Greek: ἀποπλύνω, γναφεύς, πλύνω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to trample; hence, to wash (properly, by stamping with the feet), whether literal (including the fulling process) or figurative
KJV: fuller, wash(-ing).

H3526. kabas

[כָּבַס‎]51 verb (tread), wash (Late Hebrew id.; Phoenician כבס‎ as participle; Assyrian kabâsu, tread (down), subjugate, kibšu, step, path, DlHWB) —

Qal Participle only כוֺבֵס שְׂדֵה מְסִלַּת‎ highway of fuller's (= treader's, washer's) field Isa 7:3; 36:2 = 2Kin 18:17.

Pi`el Perfect וְכִּבֶּס‎ consecutive Lev 13:6 11t.; כִּבֵּס‎ Gen 49:11; 2Sam 19:25, etc.; Imperfect יְכַבֵּס‎ Lev 11:25 16t.; 2 masculine singular suffix תְּכַבְּסֵנִי‎ Psa 51:9, etc.; Imperative suffix כַּבְּסֵנִי‎ 51:4; feminine singular כַּבְּסִי‎ Jer 4:14; Participle plural מְכַבְּסִים‎ Mal 3:2; —

1 wash garments (i.e. by treading) Exod 19:10, 14 (E), 2Sam 19:25; figurative of Judah Gen 49:11; elsewhere chiefly P: Lev 6:20; 11:25, 28; 13:6, 34; 15:11; Num 8:9 +; || רָחץ‎ (which = wash person) Lev 14:8-9, 15:5-6, 7, 8, 10, 11 + (׳כ‎ in Leviticus 27 t., || ׳ר‎15t.; in Numbers 8 t., || ׳ר‎3t.); Participle = fuller (compare Qal), only מְכ ׳בֹּרִית‎ Mal 3:2.

2 wash person, only in poetry and figurative בַּנֶּתֶר תְּכַבְּסִי אִם‎ Jer 2:22; לִבֵךְ מֵרָעָה כַּבְּסִי‎ 4:14; with suffix Psa 51:4 (|| טהר‎; + מֵעָוֺן‎); 51:9 (|| חִטֵּא‎).

Pu`al Perfect וְכֻבַּס‎ consecutive Lev 13:58; 15:17 be washed, of garment.

Hothpa`al (Ges§ 54, 3) Infinitive אֶתהַֿנֶּגַע הֻכַּבֵּס אַחֲרֵי‎ Lev 13:55 after the plague is washed out, so 13:56.
