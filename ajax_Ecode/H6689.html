II. צוּף‎ noun masculine Ephraim ancestor of Elkanah and Samuel, ׳בֶּןצֿ‎ 1Sam 1:1 (ἐν Νασειβ, ᵐ5‎L υιὁῦ Σωφ), 1Chr 6:20 Qr ᵑ9 ᵐ5‎ (Kt ציף‎); apparently = צוֺפַי‎ v.6:11 (Levite), Σουφ(ει): hence probably צוּף אֶרֶץ‎ 1Sam 9:5 (in Benjamin, τὴν(γὴν)Σ(ε)ιφ(α)).

צוּפִי‎ adjective, of a people Suphite; — so read probably for צוֺפִים‎ 1Sam 1:1 (We Klo Dr and most modern, after ᵐ5‎ Σ(ε)ιφα, A Σωφιμ).
