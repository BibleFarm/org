מָשַׁח‎ (mâshach｜maw-shakh'｜verb｜smear)
[Heb] מָשַׁח‎ ETCBC: משׁח‎ (verb｜smear) OSHL: m.dt.aa TWOT: 1255 GK: H5417 Greek: ἀλείφω, ἑτοιμάζω, καθίστημι, χρίω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to rub with oil, i.e. to anoint; by implication, to consecrate; also to paint
KJV: anoint, paint.
