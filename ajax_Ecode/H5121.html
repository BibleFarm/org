H5121. Navith

נוית‎ Kt (i.e. נָוִית‎, or נָָֽוְיַת‎, Ginsb), נָיוֺת‎ Qr; probably

proper name, of a location in Ramah (possibly = habitations, but dubious, see Dr1Sam 19:18 HPS); — adode of prophets 19:18-19, 22, 23 (twice in verse) and 20:1 (where Baer נוות‎ Kt); followed by בָּרָמָה‎ (except 19:18); ᵐ5‎ Ἀυαθ (ν dropped out after ἐν), so ᵐ5‎L A Ναυιωθ.
