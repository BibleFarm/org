H511. Elqanah

אֶלְקָנָה‎ proper name, masculine (God has created, or taken possession)

a. father of Samuel 1 Samuel 1-2 (8 t.) 1Chr 6:12; 6:19.

b. son of Korah Exod 6:24.

c. a ruler in Jerusalem in the time of Ahaz2Chr 28:7.

d. one of David's warriors 1Chr 12:6.

e. several Levites (a) 1Chr 6:8; 6:10; 6:21. (β) v.6:11; v.6:20. (γ) 9:16 (δ) 15:23.
