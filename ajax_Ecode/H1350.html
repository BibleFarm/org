גָּאַל‎ (gâʼal｜gaw-al'｜verb｜redeem｜redemption)
[Heb] גָּאַל‎, גְּאוּלִים‎ ETCBC: גאל‎ (verb｜redeem), גְּאוּלִים‎ (subs｜avenge) OSHL: c.ac.ab, c.ac.aa TWOT: 300 GK: H1453, H1457 Greek: ἀπωθέομαι, ἀφίστημι, βδελύσσω, ἐκλύω, ἐλεέω, ἐξαιρέω, λυτρόω, λυτρωτής, προσοχθίζω, ῥύομαι
Derivation: a primitive root, to redeem (according to the Oriental law of kinship), i.e.
Strong's: to be the next of kin (and as such to buy back a relative's property, marry his widow, etc.)
KJV: [idiom] in any wise, [idiom] at all, avenger, deliver, (do, perform the part of near, next) kinsfolk(-man), purchase, ransom, redeem(-er), revenger.

H1350. gaal

I. גָּאַל‎ verb redeem, act as kinsman (Late Hebrew, Niph`al be redeemed; also גְּאוּלָּה גּוֺאֵל,‎) —

Qal Perfect ׳ג‎, etc. Isa 44:23 14t.; Imperfect יִגְאַל‎, etc. Lev 25:33 18t.; Imperative גְּאַל‎, etc. Ruth 4:6 3t.; Infinitive absolute גָּאֹל‎ Lev 27:13 2t.; construct לִגְאוֺל‎ Ruth 4:4 2t.; suffix לְגָאֳלֵךְ‎ 3:13; Participle גֹּאֵל‎ Lev 25:26 24t., גּוֺאֵל‎ Isa 59:20; Psa 103:4; suffix גֹּאַלְךָ‎ Isa 48:17, גֹּאֲלֵךְ‎ 54:8 (suffix 19 t.); passive plural גְּאוּלִים‎, etc. 35:9 4t.; —

1 act as kinsman, do the part of next of kin (chiefly in D H P Ruth), גֹּאֵל‎ kinsman Lev 25:25 (H) Num 5:8; 35:12 (P) Ruth 2:20; 3:9, 12; 4:1, 3, 6, 8, 14; 1Kin 16:11.

a. in taking a kinsman's widow וגאלתיך לגאלך יחפץ לא ואם יגאל טוב יגאלך אם‎ if he will do thee the kinsman's part (raise up children by the widow) well, let him do the kinsman's part; but if he is not pleased to do thee the kinsman's part then I will do thee the kinsman's part Ruth 3:13;

b. in redeeming from bondage Lev 25:48-49, (H);

c. in redeeming a field Lev 25:26, 33 (H) Ruth 4:4, 6;

d. claim as kinsman Job 3:5;

e. הַדָּם גֹּאֵל‎ the avenger of blood Num 35:19, 21, 24, 25, 27 (twice in verse); Josh 20:3, 5 (?; not in ᵐ5‎) 20:9 (P). Deut 19:6, 12 (D) 2Sam 14:11.

2 redeem, by payment of value assessed, of consecrated things, by the original owner Lev 27:13, 15, 19, 20, 31 (P).

3 redeem, with God as subject implying personal relationship, chiefly in poetry: —

a. individuals, from death Psa 103:4; Lam 3:58; Hosea 13:14, רע מכל‎ Gen 48:16 (E poetry), נפשׁ‎ Psa 69:19; 72:14, orphans Prov 23:11; Jer 50:34, וגאלני ריבי ריבה‎ Psa 119:154, גֹּאֲלִי‎ Job 19:25, וְגֹאֲלִי צוּרִי‎ Psalm 19:15.

b. Israel, from Egyptian bondage Exod 6:6 (P?) 15:13 (song) Psa 74:2; 77:16; 78:35, אויב מיד‎ 106:10.

c. from exile (chiefly Isa3, the verb not in Isa1) Isa 43:1; 44:22-23, 48:20; 52:9; 63:9; Micah 4:10, מיד‎ Psa 107:2; Jer 31:11; Yahweh is גֹּאֵל‎ Isa 41:14; 43:14; 44:6, 24; 47:4; 48:17; 49:7, 26; 54:5, 8; 59:20; 60:16; 63:16; and the people גְּאוּלִים‎ 35:9; 51:10; 62:12; 63:4 (compare גְּאוּלַי‎ below), Psa 107:2.

Niph`al Perfect נִגְאָ֑ל‎ Lev 25:49; Imperfect יִגָּאֵל‎ 25:30 5t.; תִּגָּאֵ֑לוּ‎ Isa 52:3; —

1 reflexive redeem oneself Lev 25:49 (H).

2 pass. be redeemed,

a. field Lev 25:30 (H), slave 25:54 (H);

b. consecrated things Lev 27:20, 27, 28, 33 (P);

c. Jerusalem by Yahweh Isa 52:3.

גְּאוּלַי‎ Isa 63:4, in ג ׳שְׁנַת‎,
