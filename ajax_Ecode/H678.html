H678. atsil

אָצִיל‎ noun [masculine] side, corner, chief — plural construct אֲצִילֵי‎ Exod 24:11; suffix אֲצִילֶיהָ‎ Isa 41:9; —

1 sides (borders) of earth Isa 41:9 (|| קצות‎); compare א ׳ירכתי‎ Jer 6:22 etc.

2 figurative nobles (perhaps as sides, supports, compare below מִּנּוֺת‎; so Ew Di but perhaps = noble — from be rooted, root, metaphor origin, stock — properly a man having a (known) origin, sprung from an ancient and famous stock; so Ges, compare LagBN 68) Exod 24:11.
