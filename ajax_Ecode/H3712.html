H3712. kippah

כִּמָּה‎ noun feminine branch frond (probably of palm tree, compare כַּף‎ 3d); — ׳כ‎ Isa 9:13; 19:15 figurative of nobles, rulers (both opposed to אַגְמוֺן‎ rush, i.e. high and low; || וְזָנָב רֹאשׁ‎); with suffix כִּמָּתוֺ‎ Job 15:32 (of wicked under figure of palm-tree).
