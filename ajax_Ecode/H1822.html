H1822. dummah

דֻּמָּה‎ noun feminine one silenced, brought to silence (?; destroyed?) הַיָּ֑ם בְּתוֺךְ כְּדֻמָּה כְצוֺר מִי‎ Ezek 27:32 (form pecul. & sense dubious: Baer כְּדֻמָֿה‎; Co כְּבֻדָּה‎ from כָּבֹד‎ adjective, compare 23:41; Psa 45:14) AV What city is like Tyrus, like the destroyed (as though for מְדֻמָּה‎ compare ההללה‎ 26:1; but Pi`el Punic not elsewhere) in the midst of the sea? RV like her that is brought to silence. — DlBaer Ezech p.xi. & Pr 64 derived, hesitantly, from II. דמם‎.
