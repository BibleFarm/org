H1779. din

[דּוּן‎] noun [masculine] dubious; Qr Job 9:29 שַׁדּיּ֯ן‎; see דִּין‎ n.

דִּין‎ noun [masculine] judgment — Deut 17:8 16t.; suffix דִּינִי‎ Psa 9:5, דִּינֵךְ‎ Jer 30:13; —

1 דין כסא‎ throne of judgment Prov 20:8; דין השׁמעת משׁמים‎ from heaven thou didst cause judgment to be heard Psa 76:9.

2 cause, plea, לדין דין בין‎ between plea and plea Deut 17:8; דין דין‎ plead a cause Jer 5:28; 22:16; 30:13; דין עשׂה‎ maintain, the cause Psa 9:5; 140:13; דין ידע‎ consider the cause Prov 29:7; לפניו דין‎ the cause is before him Job 35:14; דין שׁנה‎ change the cause Prov 31:5; דַּלִּים מִדִּין לְהַטּוֺת‎ Isa 10:2; יתום דין‎ Jer 5:28; חֲלוֺף בני כל דין‎ Prov 31:8.

3 judgment, condemnation, רשׁע דין‎ judgment of the wicked Job 36:17; ומשׁפט דין‎ judgment and justice 36:17.

4 strife, וקלון דין‎ Prov 22:10 (legal strife, law-suit Str).

5 government, ודין דת‎ law and government Est 1:13.

Note. — שַׁדּיּ֯ן תֵּדְעוּן לְמַעַן‎ Job 19:29 is variously explained. (1) AV RV that ye may know there is a judgment follow Aq Symm Theod, so De Da, in interpreting שׁ‎ relative + דִּין‎ judgment, but שׁ‎ is unknown to the dialect of Job and דִּין‎ is used only in Elihu section. (2) ᵐ5‎ reads ὕλη, or ἴσχυς (i.e. שַׁדַּי‎ (compare 29:5 ᵐ5‎) ? or שֹׁד‎?), & Qr שַׁדּוּן‎, indicating ancient uncertainty and a corrupt text. (3) Ew Di read שַׁדַּי‎ the Almighty, which accords with usage of Job. (4) Siegf שֻׁדָּ֑דוּ כִּי‎ (doubtfully).
