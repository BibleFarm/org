H5465. sad

סַד‎ noun [masculine] stocks, for confining feet of culprits (probably loan-word from Aramaic סַדָּנָא סַדָּא,‎ ; Late Hebrew סַד‎, plural סַדִּין‎); = Latin nervus, Greek ποδοκάκη; — absolute בַּמּ רַגְלַיִם׳שִׂים‎ Job 13:27 (ᵐ5‎ κώλύμα), 33:11 (ᵑ6‎ ξύλό); (compare synonym מַהְמֶּכֶת‎, and Greek equivalent there cited).
