H992. botnim

בָּטְנִים‎ noun [masculine] plural pistachio, an oval nut (compare Assyrian bu‰nu COTGloss & SchrMBAK 1881, 419, Aramaic בּוּטְנָא‎, , Arabic ; on Punic Βουτνούμ BlauZMG 1873, 527) = pistacia terebinthus RobBR i. 208, ii. 222, PostPEF Oct. 1888, 218, No. 214 f; Tr. Vict. Inst. xxii. 271 etc. pist. vera LöwNo. 44); Gen 43:11 one of the articles carried from Canaan to Egypt by sons of Jacob as present to Joseph; still a delicacy in Egypt and Syria, compare Wetzst in Löwp.420.

בָּטְנָה‎
原文音譯：!j,Bo bo’-ten

詞類次數：名詞（1）

字義溯源：阿月渾子樹核，榧子；一種橢圓形的核果，當日雅各從迦南帶到埃及；今日仍是敘利亞與埃及的美味果食。此字源自（H990（בֶּטֶן‎）*=腹），可能此核果外形像人的肚腹
