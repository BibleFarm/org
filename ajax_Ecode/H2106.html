H2106. zavith

[זָוִית‎] noun feminine corner (late (Aramaic זָוִיתָא‎, (often for Hebrew מִקְצוֺעַ מִּנָּה,‎), hence, as loan-word (FräAramaic Fremdw. 11, 168) Arabic ); — Plural absolute זָוִיּוֺת‎ Psa 144:12; construct זָוִיֹּת‎ Zech 9:15; — מִוְבֵּךְַ כְּזָוִיֹּת כַמִּזְרָק וּמָֽלְאוּ‎ 9:15 simil. of warriors streaming with blood, see Lev 1:5, 11 etc.; ׳וגו כְּז ׳בְּנוֺתֵינוּ‎ Psa 144:12 out daughters like corner (-pillar)s, carved (so Che and others, but see II. חטב‎) in palace-fashion.
