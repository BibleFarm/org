II. סוּף‎ proper name, of a location (si vera lectio) named in defining location of Deuteronomic law-giving Deut 1:1 (סוּף מול‎, where מוֺל‎ by dissimil. for מוּל‎); but read perhaps סוּף מִיַּם‎ (ᵐ5‎ πλησίον ρῆς ἐρυθρᾶς, ᵐ5‎L + θαλάσσης, ᵑ9‎ contra mare rubrum), see I. סוּף‎.
