H5247. Nimrah

נִמְרָה‎ proper name, of a location place east of Jordan Num 32:3 (+ חֶשְׁבּוֺן דִּיבֹן, עֲטָרוֺת,‎, etc.), ᵐ5‎ Ναμβρα, A Αμβραμ, ᵐ5‎L Μαμβραν. Perhaps = Tel Nimrîn, approximately 6 miles east of Jordan, nearly opposite Jericho, compare BuhlGeogr. 264. — compare נ ׳בֵּית‎ (q. v.), also in Late Hebrew (compare נמרה‎ proper name, masculine Sinaitic Inscriptions SACook81 Lzb323; meaning dubious; NöZMG xxix (1875), 437 thinks from spotted or striped appearance of ground; place of leopard also GrayProp. Names 92 (on tribal names from leopard see RSK 201 also Sabean proper name אנמרם‎ Levy-Osl.c., etc.); WeHeid:2, 82 thinks of clear water [compare following]).
