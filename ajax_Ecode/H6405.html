H6405. pallet

מַּלֵּט‎ noun [masculine] deliverance (si vera lectio, properly Infinitive of foregoing); — פ ׳רָנֵּי‎ Psa 32:7 shouts of deliverance (dubious, see Commentaries); מַּלֶּטלָֿ֑מוֺ עַלאָֿוֶן‎ 56:8, read מַּלֶּסֿ‎ (Ew Hup and others) for (their) iniquity weigh to them (retribution), or מַּלֶּטֿ אֵין עַלאָֿוֶן‎ Bae.
