H3026. Yegar

Sahadutha [יְגַּר‎] noun [masculine] (stone-)heap (ᵑ7‎ id.; Syriac ; compare Ethiopic throw together, mound); — construct ׳י שָֽׂהֲדוּתָא‎ Gen 31:47 (= Hebrew גַּלעֵֿד‎).
