H1975. hallaz

הַלָּז‎ pronoun common this, a rare synonym of זֶה‎ or הַזֶּה‎, this (Arabic (also ), plural , who, which (compare זֶה‎, which is used also sometimes as relative), formed from , the definite article, and the pronoun זוּ זֶה,‎, with the insertion of the demonstrative element la, and thus in fact = הַזֶּה‎: WAGi. § 347, CG p. 117) — usually with a substantive defined by the article, Judg 6:20 הַלָּז הַסֶּלַע‎ this rock, 1Sam 14:1 הַלָּז מֵעֵבֶר‎ off this side-across, 17:26 הַלָּז אֶתהַֿמְּלִשְׁתִּי‎, (+ 20:19 ᵐ5‎ Th We Dr τὸ ἐργαβ ἐκεῖνο, i.e. (or הַלָּאז‎) הַלָּז הָאַרְגָּב‎ this mound (or cairn), for האזל האבן‎) 2Kin 23:17; Zech 2:8; with a feminine, 2Kin 4:25 הַלּז הַשּׁוּנַמִּית‎: without a substantive Dan 8:16 לְהַלָּז‎ to this one (= normal לָזֶה‎). The fuller form is
