H154. Edrei

אֶדְרֶ֫עִי‎ proper name, of a location a chief city of Bashan אֶדְרֶ֖עִי‎ Josh 19:37; אֶדְרֶ֑עִי‎ Num 21:33 = Deut 3:1, 10; בְּאֶדְרֶ֑עִי בְּעַשְׁתָּרֹת‎ 1:4; וּבְאֶדְרֶ֑עִי בְּעַשְׁתָּרֹת‎ Josh 12:4; 13:12; compare 13:31; — on identity of all these compare Di Deut 3:10; modern Der`ât, 7 2-Janhours west-northwest of Bosra; Euseb. Ἀδραά (LagOnom. 213, 86 2nd ed. 235) compare Di Num 21:33 WetzstHauran 47, 77, 123 BdPal 201.

2）分給拿弗他利的一個堅固城（書19:37）
