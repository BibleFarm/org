H1271. Barzillay

בַּרְזִלַּי‎ proper name, masculine (man of iron) **no adjective from names of metals; perhaps Aramaic, and בַּר‎ = son NesZPV xv.257; AJSL Apr. 1897. 173. —

1 a Gileadite 2Sam 17:27; 19:32; 19:33; 19:35; 19:40; 1Kin 2:7; בַּרְזִלָּי֑‎ 2Sam 19:34; Ezra 2:61 = Neh 7:63 עַלשְֿׁמָם וַיִּקָּרֵא הַגִּלְעָדִי בַּרְזִלַּי מִבְּנוֺת לָקַח אֲשֶׁר בַרְזִלַּי בְּנֵי‎, where 2nd Barzillai = above, & former is

2 a priest Ezra 2:61 = Neh 7:63, who adopted name בַּרְזִלַּי‎.

3 2Sam 21:8 a Meholathite.
