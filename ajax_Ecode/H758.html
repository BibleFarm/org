H758. Aram

אֲרָם‎149 proper name, masculine Aram (Assyrian Aramu, etc., see DlPa 257; Thes and others propose √ ארם‎ = רום‎ but compare Nö as below) —

1 5th son of Shem Gen 10:22-23, 1Chr 1:17.

2 grandson of Nahor Gen 22:21.

3 1Chr 2:23.

4 a descendant of Asher 1Chr 7:34. — Elsewhere only of AramÊan people & land (= 1 above), feminine2Sam 8:5 masculine10:14.

a. people, singular collective = the Aramoeans, a leading branch of the Shemitic stock inhabiting Mesopotamia & northern Syria, in many tribes & settlements; 2Sam 8:5 (twice in verse); 8:6 + 1Kin 20:20-21, + 1Chr 19:10, 12 + (64 t. Samuel Kings Chronicles) Amos 9:7; Isa 7:2, 4, 5, 8; 9:11; 17:3; Jer 35:11; so Ezek 16:57; 27:16, but Co in both אֲרָם עַם אדום‎ Amos 1:5; of particular divisions of Aram, ׳א רְחוֺב בית‎ 2Sam 10:6, ׳א צוֺבָא‎ 10:6, 8; Psa 60:2 (title), ׳א דַּמֶּשֶׂק‎ 2Sam 8:5 compare 1Chr 18:5, even ׳א נַהֲרַיִם‎ Psa 60:2 (title); (note that Assyrian never gives name Aramu to people west of Euphrates, but Chatti instead, with other particular names, COT Gen 10:22, also Dll.c.); on 2Sam 8:12-13, 1Chr 18:11 see אֱדוֺם‎.

b. less often clearly of land, Aram Num 23:7; 2Sam 15:8 2Chr 20:2 (read however here אדום‎ Thes Add and others), א ׳שְׂדֵה‎ Hosea 12:13; also of particular divisions of the territory ׳א נַהֲרַיִם‎ 'Mesopotamia,' i.e. probably land between Euphrates & Chaboras, so Di after Kiep, Gen 24:10; Deut 23:5; Judg 3:8 (compare Psa 60:2 above); compare א ׳מַּדַּן‎ Paddan-Aram Gen 25:20; 31:18; 33:18; 35:9, 26; 46:15, א ׳מַּדֶּ֫נָה‎ 28:2, 5, 6, 7 see פדן‎; ׳א דַּמֶּשֶׂק‎ 2Sam 8:6 compare 1Chr 18:6.

c. often indeterminate, especially in א ׳מֶלֶךְ‎ etc., perhaps primarily land but often including people: so Judg 2:11; 10:6 (א ׳אֱלֹהֵי‎) 1Kin 10:29 2Chr 1:17 + (41 t. Kings & Chronicles) Isa 7:1. — (compare especially NöSehenkel BL, ZMG 1871, 113; Hermes see 3, 443 f. DlPa 257.)

אֲרָם / אֲרַם מַעֲכָה‎
原文音譯：~r;a] arawm

詞類次數：專有名詞（133）

原文字根：高 （城堡）

字義溯源：亞蘭；人名，地名。字義：高地。有如（H759（אַרְמֹון‎）*=城堡）有三人名亞蘭：

1）閃的兒子亞蘭（創10:22）

2）拿鶴的孫子亞蘭（創22:21）

3）朔默的兒子亞蘭（代上7:34）地名：亞蘭就是敘利亞，舊約常說到亞蘭地，亞蘭人（撒下8:6），亞蘭王（王上20:23）註一：H758（אֲרָם‎ / אֲרַם מַעֲכָה‎）與H765（אֲרָן‎）兩原文編號均繙為亞蘭。註二：新約主耶穌的家譜中，猶大的曾孫亞蘭（太1:3），編號：G689（Ἀδμίν / Ἀράμ / Ἀρμίν）
