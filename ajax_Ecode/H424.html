H424. elah

I. אֵלָה‎ noun feminine terebinth (= אֵילָה‎ (?) see IV. איל‎ > StaGeschichte 455 who derives from אֵל‎ = divine; but compare ib. on lack of clear distinction between אֵלוֺן אֵלָה,‎ & אַלּוֺן‎) — Gen 35:4 15t. + אֵילָה‎ 49:21 (for אַיָּלָה ᵑ0‎ see below) — terebinth = Pistacia terebinthus, Linn., a deciduous tree with pinnate leaves & red berries; occasional in Palestine; grows to great age; always of single tree; near Shechem 35:4 (E) compare אַלָּה‎ Josh 24:26 (E, read אֵלָה‎?), Ophrah Judg 6:11, 19; in Jabesh 1Chr 10:12; tree in which Absalom was caught 2Sam 18:9 (twice in verse); 18:10, 14; see also 1Kin 13:14; expressly of idol-shrine Hosea 4:13 (|| לִבְנֶה אַלּוֺן,‎) Ezek 6:13; as fading, withering, simile of Judah Isa 1:30; as hewn down, simile id. 6:13 (|| אַלּוֺן‎); figurative of Naphtali שְׁלֻחָה אֵילָה‎ Gen 49:21 (ᵐ5‎ Ew Ol Di > אַיָּלָה ᵑ0‎ hind q. v.) a slender terebinth, see Di & compare II. אֵלָה‎; in topographic designation הָא ׳עֵמֶק‎ 1Sam 17:2, 19; 21:10 (see עֵמֶק‎).
