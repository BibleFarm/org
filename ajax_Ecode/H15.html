H15. abeh

[בַּי בָּיַי,‎] verb entreat (Arabic come as suppliant, entreat, still current in the Hauran: see Wetzst in DeJob 34:36), of which (probably) אָבִי‎ 34:36 is 1 singular imperfect (used dialectically): אִיּוֺב יִבְּחֵן אָבִי‎ would that (literally I entreat that) Job were tried!
