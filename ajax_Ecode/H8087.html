H8087. Shema

' II. שֶׁ֫מַע‎ proper name, masculine usually Σαμα(α), Σεμαα (compare Sabean proper name סמע‎ Hal509); —

1 in Judah 1Chr 2:44, שָׁ֑מַע‎ 2:43.

2 in Reuben 1Chr 5:8.

3 in Benjamin 1Chr 8:13 (= שִׁמְעִי‎ 8:21).

4 post-exilic Neh 8:4, Σαμαιας.
