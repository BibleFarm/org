אָזַן‎ (ʼâzan｜aw-zan'｜verb｜give ear)
[Heb] אָזַן‎ ETCBC: אזן‎ (verb｜listen) OSHL: a.cg.ac TWOT: 57 GK: H263 Greek: ἀκούω, εἰσακούω, ἐνωτίζομαι, ἐπακούω, προσέχω, ὑπακούω
Derivation: a primitive root; probably to expand; but used only as a denominative from אֹזֶן‎;
Strong's: to broaden out the ear (with the hand), i.e. (by implication) to listen
KJV: give (perceive by the) ear, hear(-ken). See אָזַן‎.
