H5732. iddan

עִדָּן‎ noun masculineDan 2:9 time (ᵑ7‎ id., Syriac , perhaps loan-word from Assyrian adannu, fixed, appointed, or definite, time; [Arabic probably Aramaic loan-word, SchwZMG liii (1899), 197]); — absolute ׳ע‎ 7:12 +; emphatic עִדָּנָא‎ 2:8 +; plural absolute עִדָּנִין‎ 4:13 +, emphatic עִדָּנַיָּא‎ 2:21; —

1 in General, time, as duration Dan 2:8; 7:12; involving specific conditions 2:9, 21; (point of) time 3:5, 15.

2 definite time, = year (as modern Greek χρόνος, see EASophocles1173): ע ׳שִׁבְעָה‎ = seven years, Dan 4:13; 4:20; 4:22; 4:29; עִדָּן וּפְלַג וְעִדָּנִין עִדָּן‎ 7:25 (i.e. 3 2-Janyears, see Dr; perhaps read dual for plural, compare Bev GunkSchöpf. 201).
