Sharetser שַׂרְאֶ֫צֶר נֵרְגַּל‎ proper name, masculine (so Baer Ginsb; van d. H. ׳שַׁר‎), chief soothsayer (רַבמָֿ֑ג‎) in train of Nebuchad. Jer 39:3a (strike out Gie, plausibly) 39:3b, 13 (compare SchrCOT on the passage).
