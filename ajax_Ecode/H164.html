H164. Ehud

אֵהוּד‎ proper name, masculine 1. a Benjamite, son of Gera, deliverer of Israel from Moab Judg 3:15-16, 20 (twice in verse); 3:21, 23, 26; 4:1.

2 a Benjamite, son of Bilhan (= fore-going ?) 1Chr 7:10.
