חוֺל‎ noun masculineHosea 2:1 sand (perhaps as turning or whirling; otherwise explained by Sta, see below חֲוִילָה‎; Late Hebrew id., Aramaic חָלָא‎, ) — ׳ח‎ absolute Gen 22:17 15t.; construct 32:13 6t.; — sand Exod 2:12 (E); set as bound for sea Jer 5:22; ח טמוני ׳שְׂפֻנֵי‎ Deut 33:19 hidden treasures of sand, poetic for glass (regarded as mysteriously produced out of sand, see Di and references);

a. usually sand of seashore, simile of numberlessness, vastness, so of Abraham's seed Gen 22:17 (J; compare 15:5); Jacob's 32:13 (E) of a host Josh 11:4 (JE), Judg 7:12; 1Sam 13:5; 2Sam 17:11; a people 1Kin 4:20; Hosea 2:1; Isa 10:22; 48:19 (|| כִּמְעֹתָ֑יו מֶעֶיךָ וְצֶאֱצָאֵי‎), Jer 33:22; Hab 1:9; of corn gathered by Joseph Gen 41:49; of days of Job Job 29:18 (on interpretation = phoenix, see Di); of birds כנף עוף‎ (i.e. quails) Psa 78:27 (|| כֶּעָפָר‎); of vast extent of Solomon's wisdom 1Kin 5:9.

b. in compare, more than the sand Jer 15:8 (widows of Judah); thoughts of God Psa 139:18.

c. of weight (in balances), metaphor of Job's vexation (כַּעַשׂ‎) Job 6:3 heavier than the sand, compare הַחוֺל וְנֵטֶל‎ Prov 27:3 (|| אֶבֶן כֹּבֶד‎), said of אֱוִיל כַּעַס‎. — Combinations are: הים שׂפת על אשׁר ׳כח‎ Gen 22:17; Josh 11:4; 1Sam 13:5; 1Kin 5:9; ׳כח הַיָּם‎ Gen 32:13; 41:49; Isa 10:22; Hosea 2:1 (+ יִסָּפֵר ולא יִמַּד לא אשׁר‎); ׳ח הַיָּם‎ Jer 33:22; ׳כח לרב הים שֶׁעַלשֿׂפת‎ Judg 7:12; ׳כח לרב אשׁרעֿלהֿים‎ 2Sam 17:11; 1Kin 4:20; יַמִּים (מ)חול‎ Job 6:3; Psa 78:27; Jer 15:8.
