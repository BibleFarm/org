H4719. Maqqedah

מַקֵּדָה‎ proper name, of a location in Judah Josh 15:41, near Beth Horon 10:10, and Libnah 10:29, where was a cave 10:16; 10:17, compare 10:21, captured by Joshua 10:28, with a king 10:28; 12:16. ᵐ5‎ Μακηδα. Site dubious, Warren;Survey Mem.ii. 411 ff., 427 compare El-mughâr, approximately 2 4-Marmiles southwest from Ekron.
