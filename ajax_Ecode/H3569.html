H3569. Kushi

I. כּוּשִׁי‎ adjective, of a people of I. כּוּשׁ‎; — masculine כּוּשִׁי‎ Jer 13:23 13t.; feminine כֻּשִׁית‎ Num 12:1 (twice in verse); plural כּוּשִׁים‎ Zeph 2:12 6t.; כֻּשִֿׁים‎ Dan 11:43; כֻּשִׁיִּים‎ Amos 9:7; —

a. singular agreeing with noun Num 12:1 (twice in verse) (E; only here feminine).

b. = substantive, a Cushite Jer 13:23.

c. id. with article, the Cushite, of Joab's adjutant 2Sam 18:21 (twice in verse) (where read ׳חַכּ‎, for ׳כ ᵑ0‎, We Dr Kit Bu), 18:22; 18:23; 18:31; 18:31; 18:32; 18:32; in apposition with proper name Jer 38:7, 10, 12; 39:16; 2Chr 14:8.

d. plural כּוּשִׁים‎ (הַ‎) = substantive Zeph 2:12; 2Chr 12:3; 14:11 (twice in verse); 14:12; 16:8; 21:16; so כֻּשִֿׁים‎ Dan 11:43, כֻּשִׁיִּים‎ (si vera lectio) Amos 9:7.
