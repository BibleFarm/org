II. [חָדָה‎] verb rejoice (Assyrian —adû DlPr 153; Aramaic , חֲדָא‎) —

Qal Imperfect3masculine singular וַיִּ֫חַדְּ‎ Exod 18:9 (E), and Jethro rejoiced over (עַל‎ of thing); jussive אַליִֿ֫חַדְּ‎ Job 3:6 let it not rejoice among (בְּ‎) the days of the year, i.e. not take its place joyfully among them (figurative of day of Job's birth). — Klo proposes חָדוּבְּ֗֗֗ וְלֹא‎ 1Sam 6:19 for וַיַּךְבְּ֗֗֗‎ compare also Dr
