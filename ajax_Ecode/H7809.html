H7809. shachad

[שָׁחַד‎] verb give a present, bribe (Ecclus 35:14; Aramaic שְׁחַד‎, bribe; perhaps also Assyrian šidê, gifts, DlWB 643); —

Qal Imperfect2feminine singular, accusative of person אוֺתָם וַתִּשְׁחֳדִי‎ Ezek 16:33 thou didst bribe them to come (לָבוֺא‎; || נְדָנִים נָתַן‎); read probably also Infinitive construct suffix שַׁחֲדָהּ‎ Isa 47:11 to buy it off (for שַׁחְרָהּ ᵑ0‎, so Kr Gr Buhl CheHpt Heb 142, compare Marti; see Prov 6:35); Imperative masculine plural בַעֲדִי שִׁחֲדוּ‎ (Ges§ 64a) Job 6:22 give a bribe for me.
