a. terr. land of Edom, south of Dead Sea, שׂ ׳אַ֫רְצָה‎ Gen 32:4 (J; || אֱדוֺם שְׂדֵה‎), compare 36:30 (P); ׳שׂ‎ alone Judg 5:4 (poem; || id.), Num 24:18 (J E; || אֱדוֺם‎), Deut 1:44; 2:4, 8, 12, 22, 29; 33:2; Josh 11:19 (D), Isa 21:11; 2Chr 20:23 b; with ה‎ locative שֵׂעִ֫ירָה‎ Gen 33:14, 16 (J), Josh 12:7 (D).

b. specific

mont. East of Arabah, ׳הַרשֿׂ‎ Josh 24:4 (E), Gen 36:8-9, (P), Deut 1:2; 2:1; 21:5; Ezek 35:2-3, 7, 15; 1Chr 4:42; 2Chr 20:10, 22, 23; compare שׂ ׳הַרֲרָם‎ Gen 14:6 (home of הַחֹרִי‎; on text see BuhlEdom. 28 GunkGn).
