מָחָה‎ (mâchâh｜maw-khaw'｜verb｜full of marrow｜strike｜wipe)
[Heb] מָחָה‎, מָחָא‎ ETCBC: מחה‎ (verb｜wipe), מחה‎ (verb｜strike), מחה‎ (verb｜fill with marrow) OSHL: m.bn.ad, m.bl.aa, m.bm.aa TWOT: 1178 1179 1181c GK: H4674, H4681, H4682, H4683 Greek: ἀπονίπτω, ἀφαιρέω, ἐξαλείφω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: properly, to stroke or rub; by implication, to erase; also to smooth (as if with oil), i.e. grease or make fat; also to touch, i.e. reach to
