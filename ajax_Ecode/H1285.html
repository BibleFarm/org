H1285. berith

בְּרִית‎285 noun feminine covenant (|| Aramaic קְיָם‎, διαθήκη; constitutio) — ׳ב‎ Gen 9:13 199t.; suffix בְּרִיתִי‎ 6:18 50t.; בְּרִיתְךָ‎ Deut 33:9; בְּרִיתֶ֑ךָ‎ Psa 44:18 11t.; בְּרִיתְכֶם‎ Isa 28:18; בְּרִיתֵךְ‎ Ezek 16:61; בְּרִיתוֺ‎ Exod 2:24 18t.; — pact, compact, covenant.

I. between men.

1 trety, alliance, league: Abraham and Amorites Gen 14:13; Edom and its allies Obadiah 7; with Philistines 21:27, 32 (E) 26:28 (J); Jacob and Laban 31:44 (J); Joshua and Gibeonites Josh 9:6-7, 11, 15, 16 (J); Israel and Canaanites Exod 23:32; 34:12, 15 (JE) Deut 7:2; Judg 2:2; Ammonites and Jabesh 1Sam 11:1; Solomon and Hiram 1 Kings 5:26; Ahab and Benhadad 1Kin 20:34; Syria and Israel 15:19 2Chr 16:3; Nebuchadnezzar and Zedekiah Ezek 17:13-19; nations against Israel Psa 83:6; nations with Egypt Ezek 30:5; Ephraim and Assyria Hosea 12:2; Judah and Israel Ezek 16:61; Judah and Tyre Amos 1:9; Assyria and Judah Isa 33:8; ברית נגיד‎ a prince in league (with him) Dan 11:22 (so He Ew; Hi Meinh Bev reference to high priest Onias III, & translate prince of covenant, compare AV RV); figurative, with death Isa 28:15, 18; with stones of the field Job 5:23.

2 constitution, ordinance, between monarch and subjects: David and Abner 2Sam 3:12-13, 21; David and the elders of Israel 5:3 = 1Chr 11:3; Zedekiah and his people Jer 34:8-18; hostile prince and Israelites Dan 9:27.

3 agreement, pledge: Jehoiada and captains 2Kin 11:4 2Chr 23:1; with onself Job 31:1; with Leviathan to be a servant Job 40:28; between man and man Hosea 10:4 compare ברית בעל‎ Judg 8:33; 9:4 = ברית אל‎ 9:46.

4 alliance of friendship between David and Jonathan 1Sam 18:3; 20:8; 23:18 compare Psa 55:21.

5 alliance of marriage Prov 2:17; Mal 2:14. — In all cases ברית כרת‎ is the technical phrase for making covenant except Jer 34:10 בברית באו‎; Dan 9:27 לְ ברית הגביר‎. Various prepositions are used, most often לְ‎ Exod 23:32 +, but also עִם‎ Gen 26:28 +, אֵת‎ 2Sam 3:12 +, בֵּין‎2Chr 16:3.

II. between God and man.

1 alliance of friendship (|| סוֺד‎) Psa 25:14.

2 covenant, as a divine constitution or ordinance with signs or pledges (see אוֺת‎).

a. with Noah Gen 9:9-17 (P) Isa 54:10; Jer 33:20, 25; a divine promise that there would be no other deluge.

b. with Abraham, Isaac and Jacob Gen 15:18 (J) 17:2-21 Exod 2:24; 6:4-5, Lev 26:42 (P) 2Kin 13:23; 1Chr 16:15 = Psa 105:8; 105:10; Neh 9:8; Jer 34:18; a promise to multiply their seed, give them the land of Canaan, and make them a blessing to the nations.

c. with Israel at Sinai = Horeb, with a covenant sacrifice Exod 19:5; 24:7-8, (E) 34:10, 27, 28 (J) 31:16; Lev 2:13 (P) 24:8; 26:9, 15, 25, 44, 45 (H) Deut 4:13; renewed in plains of Moab Deuteronomy 28:69; with blessings and curses 29:20; frequently referred to in other books 2Chr 34:32; Psa 25:10; 44:18; 50:5; 50:16; 74:20; 78:10; 78:37; 103:18; 106:45; 111:5; 111:9; Isa 56:4, 6; Jer 1:2-3, 6, 8, 10; 14:21; 22:9; 31:32; Ezek 16:8, 59, 60; 44:7; Dan 9:4; 11:28, 30, 32; Hosea 6:7; 8:1; Zech 9:11; 11:10; a divine constitution given to Israel with promises on condition of obedience and penalties for disobendience, in the form of tables of the covenant Deut 9:9, 11, 15, inscribed with the ten words, placed in ברית(י ׅ׳ארון‎ the ark of the covenant Num 10:33 40t. (see ארון‎; in 1Sam 4:3-4, (twice in verse); 4:5 omits ברית‎ after ᵐ5 ארון‎ We Dr); set forth in הברית דברי‎ words of the covenant Exod 34:28 (J) Deuteronomy 28:69; Deut 29:8; 2Kin 23:3 (= 2Chr 34:31) Jer 11:2-8; written in הברית ספר‎ the book of the covenant Exod 24:7 (E compare 34:27 J) 2Kin 23:2, 21 (compare 2Chr 34:30).

d. with Phinehas Num 25:12-13, (P), a constitution, establishing an everlasting priesthood in his line; compare הכהנים ברית‎ Neh 13:29 & הלוי ברית‎ Mal 2:4, 8.

e. with Joshua and Israel Josh 24:25 (E), an ordinance or constitutional agreement to serve Yahweh only.

f. with David Psa 89:4; 89:29; 89:34; 89:39; 132:12; Jer 33:21 (compare 2 Samuel 7 = 1 Chronicles 17); a divine promise to the seed of David of an everlasting kingdom, the relation of sonship, and the superintendence of the temple (compare Psalms 2..)

g. Jehoiada and the people 2Kin 11:17 2Chr 23:3, a constitutional agreement to be the people of Yahweh.

h. Hezekiah and the people2Chr 29:10, a constitutional agreement to reform the worship.

i. Josiah and the people 2Kin 23:3, a constitutional agreement to obey the book of the covenant.

j. Ezra and the people Ezra 10:3, a constitutional agreement to put away foreign wives and observe the Law.

k. the prophetic covenant, a divine promise through a series of prophets to establish a new constitution חדשׁה ברית‎ Jer 31:31, with new institutions and precepts Isa 42:6; 49:8; 55:3; 59:21; 61:8; Jer 31:31, 33; 32:40; 50:5; Ezek 16:60, 62; 20:37; 34:25; 37:26; Hosea 2:20. In Isa2 the Messianic servant is עם ברית‎ Isa 42:6; 49:8, compare הברית מלאך‎ Mal 3:1.

詞類次數：名詞（284）

原文字根：純；相當於G1242（διαθήκη）

字義溯源：契約，約，立約，合約，盟約，同盟，結盟；源自（H1262（בָּרָא‎ / בָּרָה‎）*=揀選，喫）註：喫，含有切割之意；而‘立約’，希伯來文是說‘切約’；也可能是因立約時，都有殺牲畜來表示約的完成，並示意約是不能變更，不可違犯的；否則，違約的也要像牲畜那樣被殺。如果神與人立約，人固然不可違約，神也要受約的約束而守約。神曾與挪亞，亞伯拉罕，以色列人立約；末了，耶利米宣告神要與以色列家另立新約（耶31:31-34），這就是指主耶穌用他自己的血所立的新約
