H550. Amnon

אַמְנוֺן‎ proper name, masculine (faithful)

1 eldest son of David 2Sam 3:2; 2 Samuel 13; = אֲמִינוֺן‎ 13:20; = אַמְנֹן‎ 1Chr 3:1.

2 a son of Shimon 1Chr 4:20.

אֲמִינוֺן‎ diminutive, so Ew§ 167 a compare Wi. § 269, or text error; compare Dr 2Sam 13:20, see אַמְנוֺן‎.
