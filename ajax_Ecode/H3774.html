[pn] כְּרֵתִי‎ ETCBC: כְּרֵתִי‎ (adjv｜Cherethite) OSHL: k.dh.ae GK: H4165
Derivation: probably from כָּרַת‎ in the sense of executioner; (compare טַבָּח‎) (only collectively in the singular as plural)
Strong's: a Kerethite or life-guardsman
KJV: Cherethims, Cherethites.

H3774. Kerethi

כְרֵתִי‎ adjective, of a people, always with article, or plural, as substantive, Kerethites, a name for Philistines or a part of them (perhaps = Cretans, compare ᵐ5‎ Zeph 2:5; Ezek 25:15; on Crete as original home of Philistines, see מְּלִשְׁתִּי כַּפְתֹּר,‎); — הַכְּרֵתִי‎ = substantive collective; — הַכּ ׳נֶגֶב‎ 1Sam 30:14 (compare Dr; ᵐ5‎ B Ξολθει, ᵐ5‎L Ξορρι A Ξερηθει); of soldiers of David's guard, ׳הַכּ וְהַמְּלֵתִי‎ 2Sam 8:18 (read ׳עַלהַֿכּ‎ Th We Dr and others) = 1Chr 18:17; 2Sam 15:18; 20:7 + 20:23 Qr ( > Kt הכרי‎), 1Kin 1:38, 44 (ᵐ5‎ Ξελεθθει, Ξερεθθει, etc.); plural כְּרֵתִים‎ Ezek 25:16 (|| מְּלִשְׁתִּים‎; ᵐ5‎. Κρῆτας); כְּרֵתִים גּוֺי‎ Zeph 2:5 (ᵐ5‎ Κρητῶν; || פלשׁתים ארץ‎): in 2:6 We reads כֶּרֶת‎ = Philistia (see [כָרָה‎], below I. כרה‎).

כִּשְׂבָּה כֶּשֶׂב,‎ see כֶבֶשׂ‎ etc.
