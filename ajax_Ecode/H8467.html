תְּחִנּה‎ noun feminine favour, supplication for favour; — ׳ת‎ Josh 11:20 4t.; construct תְּחִנַּת‎ 1Kin 8:30, 52 (twice in verse); תְּחִנָּתִי‎ Jer 37:20 5t.; תְּחִנֹּתֵיהֶם‎2Chr 6:39 etc. + 10 t. suffix; —

1 favour, shewn by Israel Joshua 11:29 (D); יהוה מאת‎ Ezra 9:8.

2 supplication for favour, from God 1Kin 8:52 (twice in verse); 2Chr 33:13; Psa 119:170; || תְּפִלָּה‎ 1Kin 8:28 2Chr 6:19; Psa 6:10; 55:2; (ו)תחנה תפלה‎ 1Kin 8:38, 45, 49, 54 2Chr 6:29; 6:35; 6:39; 1Kin 9:3; ׳ת התפלל אשׁר‎ 8:30 (= תַּחֲנוּן‎2Chr 6:21); ת ׳תִּמֹּלּ לִפְנֵי‎ let the supplication fall before, Yahweh Jer 36:7, the king 37:20, Jeremiah 42:2; ת ׳הִמִּיל לפני‎ present supplication before, Yahweh 42:9; Dan 9:20, the king Jer 38:26.
