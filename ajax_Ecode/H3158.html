H3158. Yizreeli

יִזְרְעֵאלִי‎ masculine, יִזְרְעֵאלִית‎

feminine, adjective, of a people 1. masculine from יִזְרְעֶאל‎ 1b; always הַיִּז ׳נָבוֺת‎ 1Kin 21:1, 4, 6, 7, 15, 16; 2Kin 9:21, 25.

2. feminine from יִזְרְעֶאל‎ 1a; always ׳הַיִּז אֲחִינֹעַם‎ a wife of David; ending יִ֯ת‎ 1Sam 30:5; 2Sam 2:2; 1Chr 3:1; ending תִ֯‎ — 1Sam 27:3; 2Sam 3:2 (= 1Chr 3:1).
