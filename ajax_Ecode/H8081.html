H8081. shemen

שֶׁ֫מֶן‎192 noun masculine 2Kin 20:13 (on apparently feminine Song 1:3 compare AlbrZAW xvi (1896), 99, 106) fat, oil; — ׳שׁ‎ absolute Genesis 28:28 +, construct Lev 8:2 +; ׳שָׁ֑‎ 2Kin 4:6 +; suffix שַׁמְנִי‎ Hosea 2:7 +, etc.; plural שְׁמָנִים‎ Amos 6:6 +; suffix שְׁמָנֶיךָ‎ Song 1:3, נַיִח‎- 4:10; —

1 fat, fatness: בֶּןשֿׁ ׳קֶרֶן‎ Isa 5:1 (of fertile slope); plural גֵּיאשְֿׁמָנִים‎ 28:1, 4; of food (in figurative) 25:6 (twice in verse); literal מִשָּׁ֑מֶן כָּחַשׁ כְּשָׂרִי‎ Psa 109:24 (compare צוֺם‎ va), compare שׁ ׳מִמְּנֵי‎ Isa 10:27 (in figurative, si vera lectio, meaning obscure; most now emend by conjecture; RSJPH xili (1885), 62 שֹׁרֵד מִצָּפוֺן עָלָה‎ [see also II. חבל‎], joining to 10:28; Du Marti has gone up from Pene-Rimmon [??], compare Di-Kit).

2. a. oil, specifically olive-oil, as rich product Deut 8:8 + (see זַיִת‎); hence שׁ ׳עֵץ‎ Isa 41:19 oil-tree, usually explained as wild olive, oleaster, so Neh 8:15 (|| וַיִת‎), שׁ ׳עֲצֵי‎ oleaster-wood 1Kin 6:23, 31, 32, 33; once ׳שׁ הַמֹּר‎ Est 2:2 (see מֹר‎ p. 600:b; ׳מ‎ also in holy anointing oil Exod 30:23).

b. ׳שׁ‎ as staple (codiment) Num 11:8 (J; see לשׁד‎), of value 1Kin 17:12, 14, 16; 2Kin 2:4, 6, 7 4t.; symbol of plenty Deut 32:13; 33:24; Job 29:6; ascribed (fassely) to gift of Baals Hosea 2:7; as royal treasure 2Kin 20:13 = Isa 39:2, compare 1 Kings 5:25 (׳שׁ תִית כָּ‎, see ׳כ‎) = 2Chr 2:9; 2:14 3t.; tribute Hosea 12:2, article of trade Ezek 27:17; odoriferous Prov 27:9; Song 1:3; 4:10, ׳שׁ רוֺקֵחַ‎ Eccl 10:1, so (figurative) Song 1:3; Eccl 7:1; Prov 27:16 is corrupt, see Toy; sign of luxury Ezek 16:13; Prov 21:17, of prosperity Ezek 16:20.

c. ׳שׁ‎ as medicament Isa 1:6; as unguent, for shield 2Sam 1:21, person Amos 6:6; Micah 6:15; Deut 28:40; Ezek 16:9; Esther; Psa 92:11; 104:15, not for mourner 2Sam 14:2; on head token of happiness Eccl 9:8, of hospitality, בַּמֹּ ׳דִּמַּנְתָּ ראֹשִׁי‎ Psa 23:5 (figurative), in simile 133:2; 141:5.

d. for anointing king, הַשּׂ ׳מַּח‎ 1Sam 10:1; 2Kin 9:1, 3, compare 9:6, with קֶרֶן‎ 1Sam 16:1, 13; 1Kin 1:39; compare ׳בְּשׁ מְשַׁחְתִּיו קָדְשִׁי‎ Psa 89:21.

e. in various figs., of joy ׳שׁ שָׂשׂוֺן‎ Isa 61:3; Psa 45:8; soft words 55:22; Prov 5:3; as penetrating Prov 109:18; as clear, transparent Ezek 32:14.

f. used in primitive worship, poured on stone Gen 28:18 (E), 35:14 (J).

g. in later ritual: for anointing priests, tabernacle, altar utensils, etc. (P), made of various ingred., ׳שׁ וַיִת‎ among them, Exod 30:24, ׳שׁ הַמִּשְׁתָה‎ 25:6; Lev 8:2, 10, 12 11t.; ׳שׁ מִשְׁחַתקֹֿדֶשׁ‎ Exod 30:25 (twice in verse) + 2t.; ׳שׁ ׳מ ׳י‎ Lev 10:7, ׳שׁ ׳מ אֱםהָיו‎ 21:12; ׳שׁ ׳הקּ‎ Num 35:25; ׳שׁ‎ alone Lev 14:10 12t. Leviticus 14; for lighting Exod 25:6 6t. + ׳שׁ חִית כָּ זָח זַיִת‎ 27:20; Lev 24:2 (see תִית כָּ‎); poured upon מִנְחָה‎ 2:1 5t., compare 5:11; Num 5:15; smeared (see מָשַׁח‎) upon wafers Exod 29:2; Lev 2:4 +, and cakes (or mixed in the making? compare בָּלַל‎) Exod 29:2; Lev 2:4-5, +, so בַּשׁ ׳סֹלֶת‎ 2:7 etc. (c. 45 t. in all), שׁ לֶחֶם ׳חַלַּת‎ Exod 29:23; Lev 8:26.

h. for use in temple 1Chr 9:29.

i. for תְּרוּמָה‎ in Ezekiel's temple Ezek 45:14 (twice in verse) + 7 t. 45, 46.

j. efficacy denied Micah 6:7

k. offered to idols Isa 57:9; Ezek 16:18-19, 23:41. — See MacalisterHast. DB OIL ARSKEncy. Bib. OIL.
