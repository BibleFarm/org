H5524. Sukkoth

Benoth בְּנוֺת סֻכּוֺת‎ proper name, of a divinity 2Kin 17:30, Assyrian-Babylonian deity, worshipped by Babylonians in Samaria; text of name corrupt; SchrCOT ad. location (after H. Rawlinson) proposes Zîr-bânit, or Zarpanituv, wife of Marduk (compare HptAR, May 1886 Muss-ArnJBL xi (1892), 167), rejecting Sakkut-binûtu = Marduk DlPar 215; JenZA iv. 352 makes בְּנוֺת‎ = banîtu, epithet of Ištar; all doubtful.
