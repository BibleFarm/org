אֲגֻדָּה‎ (ʼăguddâh｜ag-ood-daw'｜noun｜feminine｜band)
[Heb] אֲגֻדָּה‎ ETCBC: אֲגֻדָּה‎ (subs｜bundle) OSHL: a.at.ab TWOT: 15a GK: H99 Greek: δέσμη, ἐπαγγελία
Derivation: feminine passive participle of an unused root (meaning to bind);
Strong's: a band, bundle, knot, or arch
KJV: bunch, burden, troop.

H92. aguddah

אֲגֻדָּה‎ noun feminine band (Mishna אֲגוּדָּה‎ compare NHWB).

1 plural construct מוֺטָה אֲגֻדּוֺת‎ bands, thongs (fastening ox-bow) metaphor of fetters of slavery Isa 58:6.

2 אֵזוֺב אֲגֻדַּת‎ bunch of hyssop Exod 12:22.

3 ׳א‎ absolute band of men (compare חֶבֶל‎. English band) 2Sam 2:25.

4 אֲגֻדָּתוֺ‎ vault of the heavens (as fitted together, constructed, compare Arabic ) Amos 9:6.
