לוּלֵא‎ (lûwlêʼ｜loo-lay'｜conditional particle or conjuction｜if not)
[Heb] לוּלֵא‎ ETCBC: לוּלֵא‎ (conj｜unless) OSHL: l.ar.ab TWOT: 1085a GK: H4295
Derivation: or לוּלֵי‎; from לוּא‎ and לֹא‎;
Strong's: if not
KJV: except, had not, if (...not), unless, were it not that.

H3884. luwle

' לוּלֵא‎ Gen 43:10; Judg 14:18; 2Sam 2:27; Psa 27:13, elsewhere לוּלֵי‎10 if not, unless (from לוּ‎ if, and לֵא‎, by dissimilation (Köii. 236, 489) for לֹא‎ not; compare Arabic ), the negative of לוּ‎, and used similarly: —

a. followed by perfect, Judg 14:18 מצאתם לא ֗֗֗ חרשׁתם לולא‎ unless ye had ploughed with my heifer, ye would not have found out my riddle, 1Sam 25:34 (second כי‎ resumptive: כִּי‎ 1d), Psa 106:23; with apod, introd. by עַתָּה כִּי‎ Gen 31:42; 43:10; by אָז‎ 2Sam 2:27 (כי‎ resumptive); by כִּמְעַט‎ Isa 1:9; with an aposiop. Psa 27:13 if I had not believed ...!

b. followed by imperfect Deut 32:27 אגור לולי ֗֗֗ אמרתי‎ I should have said, &c . . . . except I dreaded, &c.

c. followed by participle, 2Kin 3:14.

d. without a verb, Psa 94:17 (apodosis כמעט‎), 119:92 (apodosis אָז‎). In the later language, 124:1; 124:2 -י ׳לוּלֵי שֶׁ‎ (apodosis אֲזַי‎ except that ... (compare Aramaic, 106:23, דִּי ֗֗֗ אִילּוּלֵי‎ 27:13 ᵑ7‎). — Read also לוּלֵי‎ for אוּלַי‎ in Num 22:33 (apodosis עַתָּה כִּי‎). See further on לוּ‎ and לוּלֵא‎ Dr§§ 139-145 Köiii. 487f; 565.
