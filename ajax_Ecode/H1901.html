H1901. hagig

הָגִיג‎ noun [masculine] murmuring (Che), whisper, musing (on form compare BaNB 136) — only suffix הֲגִיגִי‎

1 Psa 5:2 ה ׳בִּינָה‎ understand my murmuring (whispering, faint utterance), addressed to ׳י‎.

2 Psa 39:4 בַּהֲגִיגִי‎ in my musing i.e. while I was musing.
