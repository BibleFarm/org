H1041. Beth

-azmaveth בֵּיתעַֿזְמָ֫וֶת‎ proper name, of a location near Jerusalem Neh 7:28 = עַזְמָ֫וֶת‎ Ezra 2:24 & עַזְמָ֑וֶת‎ Neh 12:29; compare 1Chr 12:13 (where proper name, masculine); — modern El-„izmeh approximately 5 miles north-northeast of Jerusalem according to RitterGeogr. xvi. 519 Surveyiii. 9.
