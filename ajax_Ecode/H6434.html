מִּנָּה‎28 noun feminine corner; — absolute ׳פ‎ 2Kin 14:13 +, construct מִּנַּת‎ Jer 31:40 +; suffix מִּנָּתָהּ‎ Job 38:9, מִּנָּהּ‎ Prov 7:8 (Ges§ 91e); plural מִּנּוֺת‎ absolute Zeph 1:16; 2Chr 26:15, מִּנִּים‎ Zech 14:10; construct מִּנּוֺת‎ 1Kin 7:34 +, etc.; —

1. a. corner, of square objects 1Kin 7:34 + 7:30 (read מִּנּוֺתָיו‎ for מַּעֲמֹתָיו‎, compare Kit), Exod 27:2; 38:2; Ezek 43:19; of house Job 1:19, roof Prov 7:12; 21:9 = 25:24; לְפ ׳אֶבֶן‎ Jer 51:26 i.e. a corner-stone; פ ׳אֶבֶן‎ of earth Job 38:9; compare (figurative) Psa 118:22, and (אבן‎ omitted) Isa 28:16; ׳פ‎ = street-corner2Chr 28:24.

b. specifically of wall of Jerusalem, Neh 3:24 (in southeast), 3:31; 3:32 (northeast); הַמּ ׳שַׁעַד‎ 2Kin 14:13 + || 2Chr 25:23 ᵑ7 ᵑ6 ᵐ5‎ and most (for ׳שׁ הַמּוֺנֶה ᵑ0‎), + (see שַׁעַר‎); apparently with battlements 26:15; of cities in General ׳הַמּ הַנְּבֹהוֺת‎ Zeph 1:16; of the nations 3:6.

2 figurative of chief, ruler as corner (support or defence), Judg 20:2; 1Sam 14:38; Isa 19:13 (read perhaps plural); Zech 10:4 (+יָתֵד‎ q. v.).
