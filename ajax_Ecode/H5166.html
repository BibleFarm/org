H5166. Nechemyah

נְחֶמְיָה‎ proper name, masculine (Yah comforts, ᵐ5‎ Νεεμιας(+?), etc.; — proper name נחמיה(וׅ‎ on Israel scarab in BM, ClGannJas 1883, Fév.-Mars. 156 No. 42); —

1 the son of Hachaliah, governor of Judah under Artaxerxes Longimanus Neh 1:1; 8:9; 10:2; 12:26, 47.

2 ruler of half the district of Bethzur Neh 3:16.

3 one of twelve heads of people who returned with Zerubbabel Ezra 2:2 = Neh 7:7.
