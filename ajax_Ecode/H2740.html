H2740. charon

חָרוֺן‎ noun masculine (burning of) anger (compare LagBN 204 Anm. * *) — ׳ח‎ Ezek 7:12 2t.; construct חֲרוֺן‎ Num 25:4 33t.; suffix חֲרוֺנִי‎ Ezek 7:14 (Co חרון‎), חֲרוֺגוֺ‎ Psa 2:5; חֲרֹנְךָ‎ Exod 15:7; plural suffix חֲרוֺנֶי֑ךָ‎ Psa 88:17; — always of God's anger (see note, below), אף חֲרוֺן‎ Exod 32:12; Num 25:4; 32:14; Josh 7:26 (J), Deut 13:18; 1Sam 28:18; 2Kin 23:26; 2Chr 28:11, 13; 29:10; 30:8; Ezra 10:14; Hosea 11:9; Nahum 1:6; Zeph 2:2; 3:8; Isa 13:9, 13; Jer 4:8, 26; 12:13; 25:37-38, 30:24; 49:37; 51:45; Jonah 3:9; Psa 69:25; 78:49; 85:4; Job 20:23; Lam 1:12; 4:11; אף‎ omitted, Exod 15:7 (song), Neh 13:18; Psa 2:5; Ezek 7:12 (strike out Co), 7:14; plural bursts of burning anger Psa 88:17. —

Note: כְּמוֺחָֿרוֺן‎ Psa 58:10 burning anger ᵑ9 ᵐ5‎ Jerome AV Bae Che; but Thes MV SS DeW Ew Ol Pe, something burning, e.g. thorns, antith. green thorns; Hengst De cooked flesh, antith. raw; Jer 25:38 ח ׳מפני הַיּוֺנָה‎ because of the fierceness of the oppressor (? AV) would then be the only use of the word of other than God's anger; here ᵑ7 ᵐ5‎ Ew Hi Gf Ke Che Gie RV rightly read חרב‎ oppressing sword (as 46:16; 50:16). חרון‎ is not used in H P D2 or E (except song, Exod 15:7) of the Hexateuch; nor in Ezekiel except Ezek 7:12 (strike out Co), 7:14.
