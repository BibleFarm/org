H669. Ephrayim

אֶפְרַ֫יִם‎ proper name, masculine Ephraim (Gen 41:52 connected with הִפְרָה‎, Hiph`il of פרה‎, compare פרא‎ Hiph`il Hosea 13:15).

1 2nd son of Joseph Gen 41:52; 46:20; reckoned among sons of Jacob, blessed by him, and given preference over Manasseh 48:1, 5, 13, 14, 17 (twice in verse) (compare 48:19) 48:20; 48:20; 50:23; Num 1:10; 1Chr 7:20, 22.

2 אֶפְרַיִם בְּנֵי‎ (= descendants, tribe of east) Num 1:32; 2:18 (twice in verse); 2:24; 7:48; 10:22; 26:35, 37; Josh 16:5, 9 (boundaries of territory), 17:8; 1Chr 9:3; 12:31; 27:10, 14, 20; 2Chr 28:12; less often א ׳מַטֵּה‎ Num 1:33; 13:8; Josh 21:5; 1Chr 6:51; בְנֵיאֿ ׳מַטֵּה‎ Num 34:24; Josh 16:8; compare 14:4; א ׳שֵׁבֶט‎ Psa 78:67 (|| יוֺסֵף אֹהֶל‎); א ׳בֵּית‎ Judg 10:9; also ׳א‎ alone, tribe Ephraim Num 26:28; Deut 33:17; Josh 16:10 + often; א ׳אֶרֶין וּמְנַשֶּׁה‎ 17:17 compare Judg 12:15; 2Chr 30:10 especially

3 הַראֶֿפְרַיִם‎ the mountain-country of Ephraim, a ridge stretching from north to south through territory assigned to Ephraim, with fruitful land on both slopes, especially the western (compare Di Josh 16:1) 17:15; 19:50; 20:7; Judg 2:9; 3:27; 4:5 26t.

4 by Hosea & Isaiah (rarely later) אֶפְרַיִם‎ = kingdom of northern Israel (from Ephraim as largest & strongest tribe in it) Hosea 4:17; 5:3 (twice in verse); 5:5 (|| יִשְׂרָאֵל‎) 5:11; 5:12 30t. Hosea; Isa 7:2, 5, 8, 9, 17 9t. Isaiah; also Jer 7:15; 31:9, 18, 20; Ezek 37:16 (strike out Co) 37:19; Psa 78:9; 2Chr 25:7, 10; compare 30:1; Psa 60:9 = 108:9; א ׳עָרֵי‎2Chr 17:2; compare 34:6; א ׳שְׂדֵה‎ Obadiah 19; hence ׳א‎ alone in local sense 31:1; also Hosea 5:9, where feminine (& perhaps Isa 7:2).

5 אֶפְרַיִם יִעַר‎ 2Sam 18:6, east of Jordan; ᵐ5‎L Klo read מַחֲנַיִם יער‎ compare 17:24, 27.

6 name of a city near Baal-hazor 2Sam 13:23 (= Ἐφραϊμ John 11:54 & 1 Macc 11:34? Klo compare ᵐ5‎L Γοφραιμ & עפרון‎, Qr עֶפְרַיִן‎2Chr 13:9; so previously Bö Th Ke compare Dr).

7 אֶפְרַיִם שַׁעַר‎ a chief gate of Jerusalem 2Kin 14:13; 2Chr 25:23; Neh 8:16; 12:39; perhaps at northwest angle, near Holy Sepulchre, compare Schick-GutheZPV 1885.

אֶפְרַיִם‎
原文音譯：~yIr;p.a, ef-rah’-yim

詞類次數：專有名詞（180）

原文字根：（滿了）果實

字義溯源：以法蓮；人名，地名；這字是（H672（אֶפְרָת‎ / אֶפְרָתָה‎）=以法他，人名，地名，意為果實豐富）的雙數，陽性，出自（H6509（פָּרָה‎ / פֹּרָת‎）*=結果子）；字義：雙倍果實，因他是在埃及七個豐收年時所生。他本是約瑟次子，雅各祝福時成了長子。舊約說約瑟的兒子以法蓮14次；說到出於以法蓮的支派，和子民，多於40次（其中說以法蓮人14次）在曠野安營時，說以法蓮營3次；以法蓮支派的地方，多於40次。在北部十支派，以色列國中以法蓮成了領頭的支派（約50次）此外，約但河東有一樹林名以法蓮；耶路撒冷附近一城邑名以法蓮；耶路撒冷城北門名以法蓮（4次）
