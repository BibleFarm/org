H523. ummah

[אֻמָּה‎] noun feminine tribe, people (Arabic ; Assyrian ummatu compare JenKosmol. 336, Aramaic , אוּמָּא‎) only plural אֻמּוֺת‎ Num 25:15; אֻמִּים‎ Psa 117:1; suffix אֻמֹּתָם‎ Gen 25:16; — of tribes of Ishmaelites 25:16; of Midian (|| בֵּיתאָֿב‎) Num 25:15; || גּוֺיִם‎ Psa 117:1.

אֻמָּה‎ noun feminine nation (Biblical Hebrew id., late); — absolute ׳א‎ Dan 3:29; plural emphatic אֻמַּיָא‎ 3:4, 7; Daniel 3:31; 5:19; 6:26; 7:14; Ezra 4:10.
