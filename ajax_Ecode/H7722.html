[שׁוֺא‎] noun [masculine] ravage (?); — plural suffix שֹׁאֵיהֶם‎ Psa 35:17 rescue me from their ravages (si vera lectio); ᵐ5‎ κακουργία; Ol Dy Gr CheComm. plausibly שַׁאֲגָם‎ their roaring, compare 35:16; 35:17 c; We Du שֹׁאֲגִים‎.

שֹׁאָה שׁוֺאָה,‎ noun feminine devastation, ruin, waste; — absolute ׳שׁו‎ Isa 10:3 +, שֹׁאָה‎ 47:11; Zeph 1:15; Job 30:14 (+ 38:27 van d. H. Gi, but see Baer's note); construct שֹׁאַת‎ Prov 3:25; —

1 devastation, ruin, as coming on person, Isa 10:3; 47:11 (Babylon personified), Psa 35:8; a 63:10; רְשָׁעִים שֹׁאַת‎ Prov 3:25 (object Genitive; || מַּחַד‎); ׳(יוֺם)שׁ וּמְשׁוֺאָה‎ Zeph 1:15; probably = devastating storm, in simile Ezek 38:9 (|| עָנָן‎), Prov 1:27 (Qr; Kt שׁאוה‎; || סוּפָה‎). — Psa 35:8b read probably שַׁחְתֹּה‎ his pit, ᵑ6‎ We (compare 35:7).

2 concretely, ruin, waste, of the desert, Job 30:3 (reading אֶרֶץ‎ for אֶמֶשׁ‎ Ol; otherwise below

1), ׳שׁ וּמְשׁוֺאָה‎ Job 38:27 (|| מִדְבָּר‎ 38:26); = ruins 30:14.
