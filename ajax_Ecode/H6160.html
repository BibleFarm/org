עֲרָבָה‎ (ʻărâbâh｜ar-aw-baw'｜noun｜feminine｜desert-plain)
[Heb] עֲרָבָה‎ ETCBC: עֲרָבָה‎ (subs｜desert) OSHL: p.fe.af TWOT: 1688d GK: H6858 Greek: ἄπειρος, γῆ, διψάω, δυσμή, ἔρημος
Derivation: from עָרַב‎ (in the sense of sterility);
Strong's: a desert; especially (with the article prefix) the (generally) sterile valley of the Jordan and its continuation to the Red Sea
KJV: Arabah, champaign, desert, evening, heaven, plain, wilderness. See also בֵּית הָעֲרָבָה‎.

H6160. arabah

I. עֲרָבָה‎61 noun feminine desert-plain, steppe; — absolute ׳ע‎ 2Sam 4:7 +, suffix עַרְבָתָהּ‎ Isa 51:3; plural עֲרָבוֺת‎ Jer 5:6; Psa 68:5, construct עַרְבוֺת‎ 2Kin 25:5 +, עַרְבֹת‎ Jer 52:8; —

1 earliest use:

a. arid steppe west of Dead Sea (in southern Judah) 1Sam 23:24, also Ezek 47:8; Isa 51:3, whence name הָע ׳יָם‎ Josh 3:16 (JE) || הַמֶּלַח יָם‎), also 2Kin 14:25; Deut 4:49, and (|| id.) 3:17; Josh 12:3 (D); הָעֲרָבָה נַחַל‎ Amos 6:14 must be east of Dead Sea, si vera lectio, but read probably ׳נ מִצְרַיִם‎ see נַחַל‎.

b. Jordan-valley west of river + adjacent plain; near ford (opposite Jericho) 2Sam 2:29, compare Josh 8:14 (JE); also Deut 11:30; 2Kin 25:4 = Jer 39:4 = 52:7; — 2Sam 15:28; 17:16 see עֲבָרָה‎

c. Jordan-valley east of river 2Sam 4:7.

2 in D:

a. especially of east Jordan plain Deut 1:1 (probably) Josh 12:1, 3, east half of Jordan-valley Deut 3:17; 4:49; of entire Jordan-valley (between כִּנֶּרֶת‎ and Dead Sea, modern El-Ghôr, the Depression) 1:7; Josh 11:2, 16; west Jordan plain only 12:8; depression south of Dead Sea (modern Wady el-`Arabah) Deut 2:8.

3 in P always plural construct מוֺאָב עַרְבוֺת‎ (east Jordan) Num 22:1 11t. (see מוֺאָב‎); ׳ע עְרֵחוֺ‎ (West Jordan) Josh 4:13; 5:10, also 2Kin 25:5 = Jer 39:5 = 52:8.

4 apparently North Arabian desert Isa 40:3; 41:19.

5 in General the steppe (often || מִדְבָּר‎), Jer 17:6; 50:12; Isa 33:9; 35:1, 6; Job 24:5; 39:6; Psa 68:5; עֲרָבוֺת זְאֵב‎ Jer 5:6 (figurative of invader); compare description of Exodus וְשׁוּחָה עֲרָבָה אֶרֶץ‎ 2:6; in simile, of a plain, of future land of Judah Zech 14:10. — See DrDeut 1:1 and references BuhlGeogr. 111.
