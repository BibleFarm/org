a. of man ׳מ אדם‎ Psa 94:11; ׳מ לֵב‎ 33:11 thoughts of the mind; מ יצר ׳(כל) (לבׅ‎ Gen 6:5 (J), 1Chr 28:9; 29:18.

2 device, plan, purpose Isa 55:7; 59:7; 65:2; 66:18; Jer 6:19; 18:12; Psa 56:6; Prov 19:21; Job 21:27; Lam 3:60-61, Est 8:5; ׳מ חָרוּץ‎ Prov 21:5; ׳מ עֲרוּמִים‎ Job 5:12; ׳מ צדיקים‎ Prov 12:5; ׳מ עמים‎ Psa 33:10; ׳מ אֹון‎ Isa 59:7; Jer 4:14; Prov 6:18; ׳מ רע‎ 15:26; ׳מ שָׁלוֺם‎ Jer 21:11; מ ׳חשׁב‎ devise devices 2Sam 14:14; Jer 11:19; 18:11, 18; 29:11; 49:20, 30; 50:45; Ezek 38:10; Dan 11:24-25, Est 8:3; 9:25; ׳מ‎ as subject of נָכוֺן‎ be established Prov 16:3; 20:18; מ ׳הפר‎ break plans 15:22.
