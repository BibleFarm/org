H2680. chatsi

Hammanachti מָנַחְתִּי‎ adjective, of a people perhaps from foregoing, only with article as collective noun הַמּ ׳חֲצִי‎ 1Chr 2:54 of a Judaean tribe (ᵐ5‎ Μαλαθει, A ᵐ5‎L Μαναθ(ι)); so read also probably for הַמְּנֻחוֺת חֲצִי‎ see :52 (ᵐ5‎ Μωναιω, A Αμμανιθ).
