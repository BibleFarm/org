H5450. siblah

[סִבְלָה‎] noun feminine burden; — plural construct מִצְרַיִם סִבְלֹת‎ Exod 6:6 (P), of the heavy labours imposed on Israel by Egyptian, 6:7; suffix סִבְלֹתֵיכֶם‎ 5:4 (J), סִבְלֹתָם‎ 1:11; 5:5 (both J), 2:11 (E); (compare Psa 81:7, סֵבֶל‎).
