H6372. Pinechas

מִּינְחָס‎25 proper name, masculine (Egyptian Pe-nehasi, the negro, according to LauthMoses (1868). 71, ZMG xxv (1871), 130 f. compare NesEg 112, AJSL xiii (1897) 174 BaenExod 6:25, yet see Di); —

1 grandson of Aaron, Φ(ε)ινεες, Exod 6:25; Num 25:7 16t.

2 son of Eli 1Sam 1:3 ( מְִּנְחָס‎), 2:34; 4:4, 11, 17, 19; 14:3.
