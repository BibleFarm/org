H2260. chibbel

חִבֵּל‎ noun [masculine] probably mast (as corded or roped in place; denominative from חֶבֶל‎) — only in simile of drunken man ח בְּראֹשׁ ׳שֹׁכֵב‎ Prov 23:24 (opposed to בְּלֶביָֿם שֹׁכֵב‎) i.e. in the lookout-basket at the mast-head, compare Thes in carchesio. — ᵑ9 ᵐ5‎ read חֹבֵל‎.
