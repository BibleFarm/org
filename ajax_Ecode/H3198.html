H3198. yakach

[יָכַח‎] verb Hiph`il decide, adjudge, prove, (Late Hebrew Pi`el וִכַּח‎ argue with (עם‎), compare Hithpa`el, Hiph`il prove, correct; Aramaic Pa וַכַּח‎ Aph`el אוֺכַח‎, id.) —
