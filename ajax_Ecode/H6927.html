H6927. qadmah

[קַדְמָה‎] noun feminine antiquity, former state; —

1 antiquity, beginning, suffix קַדְמָתָהּ‎ Isa 23:7 (of Tyre).

2 former state, suffix קַדְמָתָן‎ Ezek 16:55 (twice in verse), קַדְמַתְכֶן‎ 16:55; plural suffix קַדְמוֺתֵיכֶם‎ 36:11. 3 construct singular =

conjunction before (temporal; Aramaic) שָׁלַף שֶׁקַּדְמַת‎ Psa 129:6.

[קַדְמָה‎] noun feminine former time (compare Biblical Hebrew קַדְמָה‎, Zinjirli קדמה‎ (Cooke177), former state); — construct Dan 6:11 דְּנָה מִןקַּֿדְמַת‎ = before this, formerly; so Ezra 5:11 דְּ ׳מִקַּדְמַת‎. compare Gen 28:19 ᵑ7‎J, Ezek 38:17 ᵑ7‎.
