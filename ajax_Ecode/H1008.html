H1008. Betheel

ֵבּיתְאֵל‎71 proper name, of a location Bethel (so read, not בֵּיתאֵֿל‎ as Josh 7:2 + according to see d. H; compare Baer Gen 12:8; house of God, or house of El) —

1 ancient place and seat of worship in Ephraim on border of Benjamin, identification with Luz (former name) Gen 28:19; 35:6; Josh 18:13; Judg 1:23; apparently distinguished from Luz Josh 16:2 (yet compare Di); name connected with vision of Jacob when journeying to Paddan-Aram (JE) Gen 28:19; 35:7 (where ֵבּיתְאֵל אֵל‎, but ᵑ9 ᵑ6 ᵐ5‎ Ol strike out אֵל‎; Di maintains); compare Hosea 12:5; when journeying from P.-A. Hos 35:15 (P); name apparently given first to a stone (Gen 28:18; 35:14) compare WeSkizzen iii. 70; ֵבּיתְאֵל הַר‎ 1Sam 13:2; later important place of worship 10:3 בֵּיתְאֵל הָאֱלֹהִים אֶלֿ עָלָה‎; abode of prophet 1Kin 13:11; 2Kin 2:3; Jeroboam set up one of the golden calves at Bethel 1Kin 12:29f. compare also 13:1f; 2Kin 10:29; 23:15; Jer 48:13 see further 2Kin 17:28; 23:15 Th proposes on internal grounds, אַל בֵּית‎ house of nothingness, or ׳בּ אָלָה‎ house of execration; ׳בּ אָלָה‎ also RenHist. iii. 185; in prophets Amos 3:14; 4:4; 5:5 (twice in verse); 5:6; 7:10, 13; Hosea 10:15; note especially Amos 5:5 לְאָ֑וֶן יִהְיֶה ביתאל‎, & אָוֶן בֵּית‎ (house of iniquity) as substitute for ביתאל‎ Hosea 4:15; 5:8; 10:5 (און בית עֶגְלוֺת‎); — modern Beitîn RobBR i. 448 f. GuérinJudée iii. 14-27 compare BdPal 215 Surveyii. 305.

2 place in south country of Judah, not far from Beersheba & Ziklag 1Sam 30:27 compare We Dr; = כסיל ᵑ0‎ Josh 15:30 (text error; ᵐ5‎ Βαιθηλ); also בְּתוּל‎ 19:4, בְּתוּאֵל‎ 1Chr 4:30.

בֵּית־אֵל‎
原文音譯：lae-tyBe bayth-ale’

詞類次數：專有名詞（71）

原文字根：在內（家）-屬於（神）

字義溯源：伯特利；城名，意為神的家，或神的殿；由（H1004（בַּיִת‎ / בַּת‎ / בֵּית אַשְׁבֵּעַ‎ / בֵּית הַגָּן‎ / בֵּית תֹּוגַרְמָה‎）=家，殿）與（H410（אֵל‎）=大能者）組成；其中H1004（בַּיִת‎ / בַּת‎ / בֵּית אַשְׁבֵּעַ‎ / בֵּית הַגָּן‎ / בֵּית תֹּוגַרְמָה‎）出自（H1129（בָּנָה‎）*=建造），H410（אֵל‎）出自（H193（אֱוִיל‎ / אוּל‎）*=有力）在以色列的歷史上這城佔有重要的地位，可說僅次於耶路撒冷。由於以色列人拜偶像，拜牛犢，先知何西阿就譏諷伯特利為伯亞文（何4:15; 10:5），意即：神的家竟成了空虛的家。另有一小城也叫伯特利（撒上30:27），所在地不詳
