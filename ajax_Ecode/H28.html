H28. Abida

אֲבִידָע‎ proper name, masculine (my father took knowledge) a son of Midian Gen 25:4; 1Chr 1:33. compare Sabean אבידע‎, HalMA 192, 202, also ידעאב‎, DHMZMG '83, 399.
