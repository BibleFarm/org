H1844. deah

דֵּעָה‎ noun feminine knowledge (strictly Infinitive of ידע‎) — דֵּעָה‎ Psa 73:11 3t.; plural דֵּעוֺת‎ 1Sam 2:3; Job 36:4; — ד ׳תְּמִים‎ 36:4 (of Elihu); of God's knowledge, ד ׳אֵל ׳י‎ 1Sam 2:3 a God of knowledge is ׳י‎ (on plural see Dr); בְעֶלְיוֺן דֵּעָה יֵשׁ‎ Psa 73:11 (|| יָדַעאֵֿל אֵיכָה‎); knowledge, with ׳י‎ as object Isa 11:9 (where verbal force of noun appears); as taught by prophets 28:9; וְהַשְׂכֵּיל דֵּעָה אֶתְכֶם וְרָעוּ‎ Jer 3:15 of ׳י‎s shepherds (i.e. future ideal rulers). Gr proposes (plausibly) רָעֹה‎, after ᵐ5‎, see also Gie.
