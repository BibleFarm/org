H2657. Chephtsi

-bah חֶפְצִיבָֿהּ‎ proper name, feminine (my delight is in her; compare Phoenician חפצבעל‎) —

1 mother of king Manasseh 2Kin 21:1.

2 figurative name of Zion Isa 62:4, here explained by בָּךְ יהוה חפץ כִּי‎ for Yahweh delighteth in thee.
