H4956. Misrephoth

Mayim מַיִם מִשְׂרְפוֺת‎ proper name, of a location names with Sidon Josh 11:8; 13:6 (apparently near coast); compare (dubious BuhlGeogr. 229) Mušêrfe, 14 miles south of Tyre, see Di and references, GuérinGal ii. 166 f.. — Μασερων, Μασρεφωθμαειμ, etc.
