שִׁכָּרוֺן‎ noun [masculine] drunkenness; — ׳שׁ‎ only figurative: Jer 13:13; Ezek 23:33 (Co Berthol Krae שַׁכָּרוֺן‎, not Toy), both of bewilderment and helplessness under calamity; from drinking blood 39:19. — See LagBN 202, compare 199.
