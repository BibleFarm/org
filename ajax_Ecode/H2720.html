[Heb] חָרֵב‎ ETCBC: חָרֵב‎ (adjv｜dry) OSHL: h.fi.ab, h.fj.ab TWOT: 731a GK: H2992

Strong's: parched or ruined
KJV: desolate, dry, waste.

H2720. chareb

I. [חָרֵב‎] adjective dry, only feminine singular חֲרֵבָה‎ Lev 7:10 of meal-offering not moistened (with oil; P); חֲרֵבָה מַּתֿ‎ Prov 17:1 a dry morsel.

II. חָרֵב‎ adjective waste, desolate; — masculine singular absolute ׳ח‎ Jer 33:10 3t.; feminine singular חֲרֵבָה‎ Neh 2:3 2t.; feminine plural הֶחֳרֵבוֺת‎ Ezek 36:35, 38 (see Köi. 241); — of a city, defined בְּהֵמָה וּמֵאֵין אָדָם מֵאֵין‎ Jer 33:10, compare 33:12; also Neh 2:3, 17; הֶחֳרֵבוֺת הֶעָרִים‎ Ezek 36:35 (+ הַנֶּהֱרָסוֺת הַנְשַׁמּוֺת,‎; opposed to יָשָׁב֑וּ בְּצוּרוֺת‎).
