KJV: labour, reward, wages, work.

H6468. peullah

[מְּעֻלָּה‎] noun feminine work, recompence (in poetry and late); — construct מְּעֻלַּת‎ Lev 19:13 +, suffix מְּעֻלָּתִי‎ Isa 49:4, etc.; plural construct מְּעֻלּוֺת‎ Psa 17:4, תֹ‎- 28:5; —

1 work:

a. plural deeds of ׳י‎ Psa 28:5.

b. of men, Psa 17:4 (plural); toil, suffering Jer 31:16; good action 2Chr 15:7, wicked Isa 65:7, ׳פ שָׁ֑קֶר‎ Prov 11:18.

2 wages (as earned by work) Lev 19:13; Prov 10:16 (|| תְּבוּאָה‎), Ezek 29:20; reward (from ׳י‎) Isa 49:4; 61:8; probably figurative of people won back by ׳י‎ in warfare 40:10; 62:11 (|| שָׂכָר‎); of punishment, Psa 109:20.
