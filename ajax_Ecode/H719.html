H719. Arvad

אַרְוָד‎ proper name, of a location city of Phenicia (Assyrian Aruada, etc. see COT Gen 10:18 DlPa 281; ᵐ5‎ Ἀραδιοι; modern Ruâd) on an island near main land, northward from Tripolis, mentioned with Sidon Ezek 27:8 compare 11.
