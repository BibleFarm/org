טוּב‎ noun masculineJob 20:21 good things, goods, goodness; — ׳ט‎ Gen 24:10 17t.; suffix טוּבִי‎ Exod 33:19; Jer 31:14; טוּבְךָ‎ Psa 25:7 4t.; טוּבוֺ‎ Hosea 3:5 2t.; טוּבָהּ‎ Neh 9:36; Jer 2:7; טוּבָם‎Job 21:15; — not in H P, Chronicles, Ezekiel or post-exilic prophets —

1 good things, collective, produce of the land to be eaten Gen 45:18, 20, 23 (E), Isa 1:19; Jer 2:7; Ezra 9:12; Neh 9:35-36, to be enjoyed; good things of ׳י‎ as given by him Hosea 3:5; Jer 31:12, 14; Psa 27:13; of house of ׳י‎ 65:5, figurative of spiritual blessings.

2 goods, property Gen 24:10 (J), Deut 6:11; 2Kin 8:9; Neh 9:25. 3 abstract:

a. fairness, beauty, of neck of heifer Hosea 10:11; of people of ׳י‎ Zech 9:17; of ׳י‎ himself Exod 33:19 (JE).

b. לב(בׅ טוּב‎ joy of heart Deut 28:47; Isa 65:14 (see I. טוֺב‎

2, II. ׳ט‎ 7).

c. prosperity Job 20:21; 21:16; of Jerusalem Psa 128:5; צַדִּיקִים בְּטוּב‎ Prov 11:10 in the prosperity of the righteous the city rejoiceth.

d. goodness of taste, discernment Psa 119:66.

4 abstract, goodness of God:

a. in bestowing good things Neh 9:25.

b. in the salvation of his people Isa 63:7; Psa 25:7; 145:7.

c. stored up for his saints Psalm Psa 31:20.
