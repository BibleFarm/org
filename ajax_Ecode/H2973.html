H2973. yaal

I. [יָאַל‎] verb be foolish (compare I. אול‎) —

Niph`al Perfect3masculine plural נוֺאֲלוּ‎ Isa 19:3; Jer 5:4; וְנֹאָ֑לוּ‎ 50:36; 1plural נוֺאַלְנוּ‎ Num 12:11; — do or act foolishly: —

1 shew wicked folly = sin חָטָאנוּ וַאֲשֶׁר נוֺאַלְנוּ אֲשֶׁר חַטָּאת‎ Num 12:11 (J); in Jer 5:4 an exhibition of this folly is ascribed to ignorance.

2 become fools, lacking insight and judgment: צֹעַן שְׂרֵי נוֺאֲלוּ‎ Isa 19:13 the princes of Zoan have become fools (|| נֹף שָׂרֵי נִשְּׁאוּ‎, and, in 19:11 צֹעַן שָׂרֵי אֱוִילִים‎, etc.); וְנֹאָ֑לוּ אֶלהַֿבַּדִּים חֶדֶב‎ Jer 50:36 a sword is against the praters, and they shall become fools — be shewn up as such (|| ׃ חָֽתּוּ‎).
