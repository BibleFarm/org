H6344. pachad

II. [מַּ֫הַד‎] noun [masculine] thigh (probably loan-word (through Aramaic, otherwise = ז‎) from Arabic thigh and (compare WeGGN, 1893, 479) sub-tribe, Palmyrene פחד‎ tribe); — פַחְדּוֺ גִּידֵי‎ Job 40:17 Kt the sinews of his thigh (Qr מְּחָדָיו‎), of hippopotamus.
