H4521. menath

[מְנָת‎] noun feminine portion ( = מָנָה‎ chiefly late; on form compare Ges§ 95n. Lag BN 81, 150); — construct מְנָ֫ת‎2Chr 31:3 5t.; Plural construct מְנָאוֺת‎ Neh 12:44; מְנָיוֺת‎ 12:47; 13:10; — portion of king, for sacrifice, 2Chr 31:3; priest 31:4; also Neh 12:44 ׳לַכּ הַתּוֺרָה מְנָאוֺת‎; of Levites 13:10; of singers 12:47; figurative מְנָת כּוֺסָם‎ Psa 11:6 (of judgment of wicked), compare ׳מ שֻׁעָלִים‎ 63:11; so מְנָֽתמִֿדַּיִךְ‎ Jer 13:25; portion of thy measure = thine allotted portion; read also מְנָתוֺ(הּֿׅ‎ for מִנֶּ֑הוּ‎ Psa 68:24 (so Ol Dy Bi Che We); in good sense, ׳י וְכוֺסִי חֶלְקִי מְנָת‎ 16:5 ׳י‎ is the portion of my lot and my cup.
