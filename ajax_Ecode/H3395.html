H3395. Yerocham

יְרֹחָם‎ proper name, masculine (may he be compassionated; compare LagBN 134); —

1 father of Elkanah, grandfather of Samuel, Ephraimite 1Sam 1:1 (Ιερεμε[ι]ηλ, A Ιεροαμ); Levite 1Chr 6:12; 6:19 (Ιδαερ, Ηααλ; A Ιεροβοαμ, Ιερεαμ; ᵐ5‎L Ιεραμαηλ).

2 Benjamites:

a. 1Chr 8:27 Ιρααμ, etc. (apparently = יְרֵמוֺת‎ 8:14).

b. 1Chr 9:8, ᵐ5‎ id. (= a ?).

3 priests:

a. 1Chr 9:12, Ι(ε)ρααμ, etc.

b. Neh 11:11, Ι(ε)ροαμ.

4 father of a hero of David 1Chr 12:8 (van d. H. v.12:7), Ρααμ, A ᵐ5‎L Ιεροαμ.

5 Danite 1Chr 27:22, Ιωραμ (ᵐ5‎L as 4).

6 father of a captain with Jehoiada, 2Chr 23:1, Ιωραμ.
