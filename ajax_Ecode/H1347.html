H1347. ga

'own גָּאוֺן‎ noun masculineHosea 5:5 exaltation — Job 40:10 5t.; construct גְּאוֺן‎ Lev 26:19 31t.; suffix גְּאוֺנְךָ‎ etc. Exod 15:7 9t.; plural suffix גְּאוֺנָ֑יִךְ‎ Ezek 16:56; —

1 exaltation, majesty, excellence,

a. of nations, their wealth, power, magnificence of buildings, e.g. Egypt Ezek 32:12, Chaldeans Isa 13:11, 19; 14:11, Philistines Zech 9:6, Assyria 10:11, Jacob Psa 47:5; Amos 6:8; 8:7; Nahum 2:3, Israel Hosea 5:5; 7:10 (probably appellation of ׳י‎), Nahum 2:3, Judah Jer 13:9, Jerusalem 13:9; Ezek 16:56; עֻזָּהּ גְּאוֺן‎ pride of her strength 30:6, 18; 33:28; עזים גאון‎ 7:24 (but ᵐ5‎ Ew Hi Co read עזם‎); עזכם גאון‎ Lev 26:19; Ezek 24:21; the fruit of land of Judah will become ולתפארת לגאון‎ majestic and beautiful Isa 4:2; צבי כל גאון‎ the majesty of all the splendour (of Tyre) 23:9; Zion is to become עולם גאון‎ an everlasting excellency 60:15.

b. of God Exod 15:7; Isa 24:14; Micah 5:3; גְּאֹנוֺ הֲדַר‎ Isa 2:10, 19, 21; גְּאוֺנוֺ בְּקוֺל יַרְעֵם‎ Job 37:4; וָגֹבַהּ גָּאוֺן נָא עֲדֵה‎ 40:10.

c. הירדן גאון‎ majesty of Jordan, referring to the green and shady banks, clothed with willows, tamarisks, and cane, in which the lions made their covert Jer 49:19; 50:44; Zech 11:3, and therefore dangerous Jer 12:5 (Ew thinks of the swelling of its agitated waters); גַּלֶּיךָ גְּאוֺן‎ majesty of thy waves Job 38:11.

2 pride (bad sense) Job 35:12; Psa 59:13; Prov 8:13; 16:18; Ezek 7:20; 16:49; Zeph 2:10; of Moab Isa 16:6 (twice in verse) = Jer 48:29 (twice in verse).
