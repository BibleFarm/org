H3276. yaal

I. [יָעַל‎] verb only

Hiph`il profit, avail, benefit; —

Hiph`il Perfect הוֺעִיל‎ Hab 2:18; Imperfect יוֺעִיל‎ Jer 2:11 2t.; אֹעִיל‎ Job 35:3; יוֺעִילוּ‎ 1Sam 12:21 6t.; יוֺעִ֫לוּ‎ Jer 2:8; 12:13; יוֺעִילוּךְ‎ Isa 57:12; נוֺעִיל‎ Job 21:15; Infinitive absolute הוֺעֵיל‎ Jer 23:22; construct הוֺעִיל‎ 7:8 4t.; Participle מוֺעִיל‎ 16:19; — profit, avail, benefit, always (except Job 30:13 where in bad sense, Isa 47:12) with negative, or in question implying negative; especially of idols or false gods (as unprofitable), so Hab 2:18; Isa 44:9-10, 57:12; Jer 2:8 הלבו יועילו לא אחרי‎, 2:11 יועיל בלוא כבידו המיר‎, 16:19 מועיל בם ואין‎, 1Sam 12:21, of vain confidences Jer 7:8, or promises 23:32 (twice in verse) (followed by לְ‎); of Egypt as ally Isa 30:5 (followed by לְ‎), 30:5; 30:6; of wickedness Prov 10:2; wealth 11:4; worthless men Job 30:13 (יועִילוּ לְהַוָּתִי‎ i.e. promote it); of words 15:3 (|| יִסְכּוֺן לֹא דָּבָר‎); in General gain profit Isa 47:12; 48:17; Jer 12:13; Job 21:15; 35:3.
