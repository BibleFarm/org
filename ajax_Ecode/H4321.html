H4321. Mikayehu

מִיכָֽיְהוּ‎ proper name, masculine (so on an Israel scarab, GanneauJAS 1883, 156, No. 42) —

1 an Ephraimite, Judg 17:1, 4 (in 17:5ff., 18:2ff called מִיכָה‎).

2 a prophet, the son of Imlah (ᵐ5‎ Μειχαίας), 1Kin 22:8-26 (9 t.), || 2Chr 18:7-25 (7 t.) + 18:8 Qr (Kt מיכהו‎).

3 a contemporary of Jeremiah, Jer 36:11 (מִכָֽיְהוּ‎), 36:13.
