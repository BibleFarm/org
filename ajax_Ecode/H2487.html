H2487. chaliphah

[חֲלִיפָה‎] noun feminine a change (from idea of replacing, changing, substituting, compare Arabic, Aramaic) — suffix חֲלִיפָתִי‎ Job 14:14; plural absolute חֲלִיפוֺת‎ Judg 14:19 3t.; construct id. 14:13; 2Kin 5:5; חֲלִפוֺת‎ Gen 45:22 2t.; חֲלִפֹת‎ 45:22; Judg 14:12; —

1 change of raiment ׳ח שׂמלת‎ Gen 45:22 (twice in verse), elsewhere ׳ח בגדים‎ Judg 14:12-13, 2Kin 5:5, 22, 23; so without בגדים‎ Judg 14:19.

2 relays 1 Kings 5:28; compare ׳ח וצבא‎ Job 10:17 (Di).

3 revival after death, under figure of relief from military service Job 14:14.

4 changing, varying, of course of life לָ֑מוֺ חֲלִיפוֺת אֵין אֲשֶׁר‎ Psa 55:20 of the wicked, with whom are no changings, i.e. they do evil incessantly, steadily (|| אלהים יָרְאוּ ולא‎) so Kmp Hu De Sch and others; but the expression is peculiar and obscure; Calv vicissitudes; Hengst discharges; text perhaps corrupt.
