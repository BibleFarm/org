H437. allon

אַלּוֺן‎ noun masculineGen 35:8 oak (ᵐ5‎ βάλανος, δρῦς, etc.) — ׳א‎ absolute 35:8 +; construct ib.; plural אַלּוֺנִים‎ Amos 2:9; Ezek 27:6; construct אַלּוֺנֵי‎ Isa 2:13; Zech 11:12; — as marking grave of Deborah, Rebekah's nurse Gen 35:8 (E); whence called oak of weeping, ׳א בָּכוּת‎ ib. (see Di on the passage, & below אֵלוֺן‎ p. 18); elsewhere only in prophets; as marking illicit shrines Hosea 4:13 (|| אֵלָה לִבְנֶה,‎); as felled Isa 6:13 (in simile; || אֵלָה‎); as furnishing material for making idols 44:14 (|| אֹרֶן תִּרְזָה, אֶרֶז,‎); as simile for strength (חסן‎) Amos 2:9; also הַבָּשָׁן אַלּוֺנֵי‎ Isa 2:13 (as lofty & majestic), compare Ezek 27:6 (as strong, for making oars); Zech 11:2, metaphor of prominent men.
