H1020. Beth

Hayshimoth הַיְשִׁימוֺת בֵּית‎ proper name, of a location east of Jordan (place of the desert, ישׁם‎) in Moab Ezek 25:9; ׳בּ הַיְשִׁמֹת‎ Num 33:49; Josh 12:3; given to Reuben ׳בּ הַיְשִׁמוֺת‎ 13:20; — perhaps modern es-Suweime, De SaulcyVoyage i. 315 f., compare MerrillPE Soc. iv. 1877 BdPal 172 SurveyEP i. 156.
