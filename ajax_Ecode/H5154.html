H5154. nechushah

נְחוּשָׁה‎ and (Lev 26:19; Job 40:18) נְחֻשָׁה‎ noun feminine copper, bronze (only in poetry; compare I. נְחשֶׁת‎); — ׳נ‎ always absolute; —

1 copper produced from ore by melting Job 28:2.

2 copper, doubtless hardened with alloy, = bronze, as material of bow 2Sam 22:35 = Psa 18:35; Job 20:24; gates Isa 45:2; figurative of strength Job 40:18 (bones of hippopotamus), compare 41:19, Micah 4:13 (hoofs of Zion); of obstinate brow Isa 48:4 (|| עָרְמֶּ֑ךָ בַּרְזֶל גִּיד‎); unproductive earth Lev 26:19.
