H4626. maar

[מַעֲרֶה‎], מַ֫עַר‎ noun [masculine] bare, naked place (hence Μαραθών, Marathon, according to LewyFremdw. 144); —

1 bare place, or space: construct מִמַּעֲרִהגָּֿ֑בַע‎ Judg 20:33 bare (open) space of Geba' (si vera 1.; but see II. מַעֲרָב‎); כְּמַעַראִֿישׁ‎ 1Kin 7:36 according to the clear space on each (plate, see לוּחַ‎).

2 suffix מַעְרֵח‎ Nahum 3:5 figurative of Nineveh (pudenda exposed in shameful punishment; compare עֶרְוָה‎).
