[מַמְלָכוּת‎] noun feminine kingdom, dominion, reign (on form compare We1Sam 15:28 Dron the passage id.); — construct מַמְלְכוּת‎ Josh 13:12 + 8t.;

1 kingdom of Og Josh 13:12, 30, 31; of Sihon 13:21; 13:27.

3 reign מַמ ׳בְּרֵאשִׁית‎ Jer 26:1 (compare מַמְלָכָה‎
