H5262. necek

נֶ֫סֶךְ‎ noun masculine drink-offering; — absolute ׳נ‎ Gen 35:14 6t.; נֵ֫סֶךְ‎ Exod 29:40; 30:9; Ezek 45:17; נָ֑סֶךְ‎ Num 4:7; Joel 1:13; construct נֶסֶךְ‎ Num 28:7; suffix נִסְכִּי‎ Isa 48:5, etc.; plural נְסָכִים‎ Jer 7:18 9t.; suffix נְסָכֶיהָ‎ Num 29:31, נִסְכֵּיכֶם‎ 29:39, etc.; —

1 drink-offering, with עַל‎ (on Jacob's מַצֵּבָה‎ at Bethel) Gen 35:14 (E); elsewhere in worship of ׳י‎ only P and related documents; once of vessels of holy table הַנָּ֑סֶךְ קְשׂוֺת‎ Num 4:7; elsewhere in classifications Lev 23:37; Num 29:39, usually offered with the עלה‎ Exod 29:40; Lev 23:13; 23:18; Num 15:5; 15:7; 15:10; 15:24; 28:7; 28:14; 28:31; 29:18; 28:21; 28:24; 28:27; 28:30; Numbers 28:33; Numbers 28:37, especially הַתָּמִיד עוֺלַת‎ 28:9-10, 15, 24; 29:6, 11, 16, 19, 22, 25, 28, 31, 34, 38; הַבֹּקֶר מִנְחַת‎ Exod 29:41; Num 28:8; but sometimes also with (שׁלמים‎) זבח‎ 6:15, 17; 15:15, 10; usually + מִנְחָה‎; elsewhere only 1Chr 29:21; 2Chr 29:35; Ezek 45:17; ׳וָנ מִנְחָה‎ Joel 1:9, 13; 2:14. Offered to other gods 2Kin 16:13, 15; Isa 57:6; Jer 7:18; 19:13; 32:29; 44:17-18, 19 (twice in verse); 44:25; Ezek 20:28; Psa 16:4.

2 molten images, Isa 41:29; 48:5; Jer 10:14; 51:17. — Combinations are: נ ׳הִסִּיךְ נ, ׳נָסַךְ‎ (see verb); נ ׳שָׁפַךְ‎ Isa 57:6.
