H368. Eymiym

אֵימִים‎ proper name, masculine plural Emim (terrors) ancient inhabitants of Moab Gen 14:5 (הָאֵימִים‎); Deut 2:10 (׳הָאֵמ‎); 2:11 (׳אֵמ‎).
