H2784. chartsubbah

[חַרְצֻבָּה‎] noun [feminine] bond, fetter, pang, only plural חַרְצֻבּוֺת‎; —

1 construct ׳ח רֶשַׁע‎ Isa 58:6 bonds of wickedness i.e. imposed by wicked men (|| מוֺטָה אֲגֻדּוֺת‎).

2 pangs (compare חֵבֶל חֶבֶל,‎), absolute למותם ח ׳אֵין‎ Psa 73:4 they have no pangs (read וגו תָּם ׳לָ֑מוֺ‎).
