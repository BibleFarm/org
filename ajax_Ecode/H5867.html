H5867. Eylam

I. עֵילָם‎ proper name, of a people et. terr. Elam, Αιλαμ, Ἐλαμεῖραι, well-known country and people northeast of Lower Tigris (Assyrian Elamtu DlPa 320 ff. COTGen 10:22); — as early invaders of Palestine 14:1, 9, allies of Assyr. Isa 22:6; foe of Babylon 21:2; abode of dispersed Israelites 11:11; prophesied against Jer 49:34-35, 36 (twice in verse) (Qr, Kt עולם‎), 49:37; 49:38; 49:39; Ezek 32:24, compare ע ׳כָּלמַֿלְכֵי‎ Jer 25:25; called 'son' of Shem Gen 10:22 (P) = 1Chr 1:17; purely local designation ׳ע הַמְּדִינָה‎ Dan 8:2.

II. עֵילָם‎ proper name, masculine Ηλαμ, Αιλαμ:

1 heads of families of returned exiles:

a. Ezra 2:7 = Neh 7:12.

b. Ezra 2:31 = Neh 7:34.

c. Ezra 8:7; 10:2 (Qr; Kt עולם‎), 10:26.

2. a chief of people Neh 10:15.

3 name in Benjamin 1Chr 8:24.

4 Levite name 1Chr 26:3.

5 a priest Neh 12:42.

עֵילָם‎
原文音譯：~l'y[e ay-lawm’or o-lawm’

詞類次數：專有名詞（28）

原文字根：字義溯源：以攔；人名，國名。字義：隱藏，意即：遙遠的。出自（H5956（עָלַם‎）*=隱藏）閃的兒子以攔，其後代為以攔人，他們的邦國也叫以攔。另外還有五人名以攔：

1）沙煞的兒子（代上8:24）

2）米施利米雅的五子（代上26:3）

3）被擄歸回的民中有以攔的子孫（拉2:7）

4）被擄歸回民的首領（尼10:14）

5）城牆修復，遊行歌唱的（尼12:42）
