H6896. qebah

קֵבָה‎, suffix קֳבָתָהּ‎ (Köl.c. compare Ges§ 10h)

noun feminine stomach, belly; —

1 absolute ׳הַקּ‎ fo sacrificial victim, assigned to priest Deut 18:3, ᵐ5‎ τὸ ἔνυστρον, i.e. fourth stomach of ruminants (compare Dr), ᵑ9‎ ventriculum.

2 suffix of woman, apparently more Generally, belly Num 25:8 (P).
