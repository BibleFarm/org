H5736. adaph

[עָדַף‎] verb remain over, be in excess, synonym of סרח‎ q. v., only P H (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic עָדִיף‎ superior (to); Arabic be profuse, IV. let down veil, or curtain, etc.; plentifulness); —

Qal Participle הָעֹדֵף סֶרַח‎ Exod 26:12 the surplus (of curtains) that remains over, so feminine הָעֹדֶ֫פֶת‎ 26:12, and הָעֹדֵף‎ as substantive 26:13 the excess in (ב‎) length; הָעֹדֵף‎ = the surplus of food 16:23, of price of field Lev 25:27; עַלֿ הָעֹדְפִים‎ those over and above, Num 3:46, 49, compare 3:48 (no עַלֿ‎).

Hiph`il Perfect3masculine singular הֶעְדִּיף‎ Exod 16:18 have a surplus (of manna; opposed to הֶחְסִיר‎).
