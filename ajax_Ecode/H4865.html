מִשְׁבְּצ(וֺ)ת‎ noun feminineExod 28:25

plural chequered (or plaited) work, usually of settings for gems; — ׳מ צָהָב‎ (i.e. plaited with gold-thread) for two gems on high priest's ephod (P): Exod 28:11, 13; 39:6, 13, 16; ׳מ‎ absolute 28:14, 25, 39; 39:18; ׳מִמּ ׳ז לְבוּשָׁהּ‎ Psa 45:14 her clothing is of chequer work in wrought with gold; < Krochm Gr Checomm. {abbrev}לְבוּשָׁהּ ז בְּמִשְׁבְּצוֺת מְּנִינִים‎ (see מְּנִימָה‎ above) pearls in plaited settings of gold (-thread).
