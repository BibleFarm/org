[רִיק‎] verb Hiph`il make empty, empty out (Arabic () pour out, forth, intransitive (of water, blood, etc.); Aramaic רִיק‎ empty, pour, in derivatives; Assyrian rê‡u, empty); — Perfect1singular הֲרִי֫קֹתִי‎Lev 26:33; Mal 3:10; 3plural חֵרִיקוּ‎ Ezek 28:7; 30:11; Imperfect3masculine singular יָרִיק‎ Hab 1:17, וַיָּ֫רֶק‎ Gen 14:14 (but see below); Imperative masculine singular הָרֵק‎ Psa 35:8; Infinitive construct הָרִיק‎ Isa 32:6; Participle plural מְרִיקִים‎ Gen 42:35; Zech 4:12; —
