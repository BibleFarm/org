אוּד‎ (ʼûwd｜ood｜noun｜masculine｜brand)
[Heb] אוּד‎ ETCBC: אוּד‎ (subs｜log) OSHL: a.bq.ab TWOT: 38a GK: H202
Derivation: from an unused root meaning to rake together;
Strong's: a poker (for turning or gathering embers)
KJV: (fire-) brand.

H181. ud

אוּד‎ noun masculine brand, fire-brand (originally perhaps bent stick used to stir fire) ׳א מִשְּׂרֵפָה מֻצָּל‎ Amos 4:11 = ׳א ׳מ מֵאֵשׁ‎ Zech 3:2, plural הָעֲשֵׁנִים הָאוּדִים זַנְבוֺת‎ Isa 7:4, stumps of smoking firebrands.
