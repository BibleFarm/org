H1808. dalith

[דָּלִיּוֺת‎] plural of [דָּלִית‎] noun feminine branch, bough, metaphor of Israel under figure of olive tree דָּֽלִיּוֺתָיו‎ Jer 11:16; Ezek 17:6-7, (figure of vine); 17:23. (under figure of cedar); 31:7, 9, 12 of Asshur as a cedar, דָּלִיֹּתָיו‎ 19:11 Israel as a vine (compare Löw65).
