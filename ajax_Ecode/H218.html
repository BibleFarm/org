H218. Uwr

II. אוּר‎ proper name, masculine (flame) father of one of David's heroes 1Chr 11:35 (III. אוּר‎ proper name, of a location see below)

III. אור‎ proper name, of a location Ur (Babylonian Uru; seat of moon-god worship; hence Eupolemos in EusebPraep.Ev.ix. 17 says Καμαρίνῃ ἥν τινας λέγειν πόλιν Οὐρίην), ancient city in Southern Babylonia; OT always כַּשְׂדִּים אור‎ i.e. Ur of the Kasdim (Chaldeans) see כַּשְׂדִּים‎ below כשׂד‎; home of Terah, Abram's father, & A.'s point of departure for Mesopotamia & Canaan Gen 11:28; 15:7 (both J), & hence Neh 9:7; also Gen 11:31 (P); — modern Muqayyar, south of Euphrates, approximately 150 miles southeast of Babylon; see KG94f DlPa 226 f COT on 11:28.
