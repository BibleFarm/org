חָלָק‎ adjective smooth —

1 literal חָלָק אִישׁ‎ Gen 27:11 (J), smooth man (opposed to שָׂעִר אישׁ‎); הֶחָלָק הָהָר‎ Josh 11:17; 12:7 the bald mountain (both D); southern limit of Josephus' conquest, identification dubious; (1) white cliffs 8 miles south of Dead Sea, and (2) Mt. 4) smooth (stones) of a brook-bed (ravine, wady), i.e. thou worshippest common round stones (contempt., see Che Di).

3 as substantive = flattery חָלָק מִקְסָם‎ Ezek 12:24 divination of flattery.
