H5678. ebrah

עֶבְרָה‎ noun feminine overflow, arrogance, fury; — absolute ׳ע‎ Isa 13:9 +; construct עֶבְרַת‎ Zeph 1:18 +; suffix עֶבְרָתִי‎ Isa 10:6 +, etc.; plural עֲבָרוֺת‎ Job 21:30; construct עַבְרוֺת‎ Psa 7:7; Job 40:11; —

1 overflow, excess, outburst, ׳ע זָדוֺן‎ Prov 21:24 excess of insolence; אַמֶּ֑ךָ עַבְרוֺת‎ Job 40:11 outbursts of thine anger.

2 arrogance, of Moab Isa 16:6 ( + גַּאֲוָה‎), גַּא׃ן‎ hence Jer 48:20 (+ id. 48:29).

3 overflowing rage, fury:

a. of men, Gen 49:7 (poem in J), Amos 1:11 (both || אַף‎), Isa 14:6; Prov 14:35, עֶבְרָתוֺ שֵׁבֶט‎ 22:8 (i. e. rod wielded by him in fury, see also Lam 3:1 below), צוֺרְרָ֑י עַבְרוֺת‎ Psa 7:7 the outbursts of fury of my foes

b. of ׳י‎ Hosea 5:10; 13:11 (|| אַף‎), Hab 3:8 (|| id.), Isa 9:18; Lam 2:2; Psa 90:9; 90:11; + אַף חֲרן‎ Isa 13:9, || id. 13:13; Psa 78:49 (+ זַעַם‎), 85:4; עֶבְרָתִי אֵשׁ‎ Ezekiel 21:36 (|| זַעַם‎), Ezek 22:31 (|| id.), 22:21; 38:19; עֶבְרָתוֺ שֵׁבֶט‎ Lam 3:1 (compare Prov 22:8 above); עֶבְרָתִי עַם‎ Isa 10:6 (i.e. object of my rage), compare עֶבְרָתוֺ דּוֺר‎ Jer 7:29; י עֶבְרַת ׳יוֺם‎ Zeph 1:18 day of ׳י‎'s fury (coming judgment), so Ezek 7:19 (strike out Co Berthol, after ᵐ5‎, as gloss from Zephaniah); so עֶבְרָה יוֺם‎ Zeph 1:15; Prov 11:4; עֲבָרוֺת יוֺם‎ Job 21:30; compare עֶבְרָה‎ Prov 11:23.
