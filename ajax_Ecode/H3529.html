H3529. Kebar

II. כְּבָר‎ proper name, of a river Kebar, a river (or perhaps a canal) of Babylonia, not at present identified, by which the exiles, among whom Ezekiel ministered, were settled; always in the phrase כְּבָר נְהַר‎ Ezek 1:1, 3; 3:15, 23; 10:15, 20, 22; 43:3 (compare Sm1:1 DelPar. 47 f., 184).

II. כבר‎ (√ of following; ? to intertwine, net; Late Hebrew כָּבַר‎ sift is denominative from כְּבָרָה‎).
