H1410. Gad

III. גָּד‎71 proper name, masculine (fortunatus? perhaps derived from foregoing divine name SiegfJPTh 1875, 364 StaGeschichte.i.148; but see also BaeRel 159f.) — שָגּד‎ Gen 35:26 +, as well as גָּד֑‎ 30:11 +; —

1 son of Jacob and Zilpah;

a. strictly as personal name Gen 30:11; 35:26; 46:16; Exod 1:4 compare Gen 49:19; 1Chr 2:2.

b. as name of tribe Num 1:14; Deut 27:13; 33:20 (twice in verse); Josh 18:7; Ezek 48:27-28, compare גָּד אֶרֶץ‎ 1Sam 13:7 see also Jer 49:1, גָּד שַׁעַד‎ Ezek 48:34, on הַגָּד הַנַּחַל‎ 2Sam 24:5 compare We Dr; explicitly גָּד מַטֵּה‎ Num 1:25; 2:14; 13:15; Josh 13:24; 20:8; 21:7, 36; 1Chr 6:48; 6:65; once, בְנֵיגָֿד מַטֵּה צְבָא‎ Num 10:20; גָד בְּנֵי‎ (MI10 גד אש‎) 1:24 12t. Numbers; Josh 4:12 14t. Joshua; 1Chr 5:11; 12:14.

2 a prophet in David's time, called נָבִיא‎ 1Sam 22:5, but דָוִד חֹזֶה הַנָּבִיא‎ 2Sam 24:11 & דָוִיד חֹזֵה‎ || 1Chr 21:9, הַחֹזֶה‎ 29:29 & חֹזֵההַֿמֶּלֶךְ‎2Chr 29:25.
