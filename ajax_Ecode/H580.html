H580. anu

אֲנוּ‎ Jer 42:6 Kt: see after אֲנִי‎.

אֲנוּ‎ pronoun 1 plural common we (common in Post-Biblical Hebrew; compare also Amharic §ñ¹) may be regarded as the plural of אֲנִי‎ (WSG 99), only Jer 42:6 Kt, for which Qr substitutes the normal אֲנַחְנוּ‎.
