H6089. etseb

I. עֶ֫צֶב‎ noun [masculine] pain, hurt, toil; —

1 ׳ע‎ pain Gen 3:16 (of travail), Prov 10:22.

2 hurt (of mind), ׳דְּבַרעֿ‎ Prov 15:1 a word that hurts (opposed to מַעֲנֶהרַֿךְ‎).

3 toil Prov 14:23; pluralהָעֲצָבִים לֶחֶם‎ Psa 127:2 bread of (gained by) toils; suffix עֲצָבֶיךָ‎ Prov 5:10.

II. עֶ֫צֶב‎ noun masculine vessel (as fashioned); — וגו ׳נִבְזֶה ׳י‎ Jer 22:28 a vessel despised, etc. (figurative of Coniah = Jehoiachin).
