H8088. shema

I. [שֶׁ֫מַע‎ noun [masculine] sound; — שָׁ֑מַע בְּצִלְצְלֵי הַלְלוּהוּ‎ Psa 150:5 (|| תְּרוּעָה‎).

שֵׁ֫מַע‎ noun [masculine] hearing, report (on from compare LagBN 143); — ׳שׁ‎ absolute Isa 23:5; construct 23:5 +; suffix שִׁמְעִי‎ 66:19 etc.; — report, usually with Genitive object: accusative with שְׁמַע‎ Gen 29:13 (J) the tidings about Jacob, Isa 23:5 +? Num 14:15 (JE), Deut 2:25; 1Kin 10:1= 2Chr 9:1; Nahum 3:19; Hab 3:2; Jer 37:5; 50:43; Isa 66:9; with ל‎ Genitive 23:5; לַעֲדָתָם‎ Hosea 7:12 usually according to the report (heard by) their congregation (!), but inexplicable and probably corrupt, We Now; Marti (compare Oettli) עַלרָֿעָתָם‎ (כְּשֵׁמַע‎ as gloss) according to their wickedness; שׁ ׳נָשָׂא שָׁוְא‎ Exod 23:1 (E) take up (utter) a false report; ׳לְשׁ אֹוָן‎ Psa 18:45 i.e. as soon as heard of, = mere report Job 42:5 (opposed to personal knowledge), compare שִׁמְעָהּ‎ 28:22.
