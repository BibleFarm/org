יָקָר‎ adjective precious, rare, splendid, weighty; — absolute יָקָר‎ 1Sam 3:1 9t.; construct יְקַרֿ‎ Prov 17:27 Qr (Kt וקר‎; see below); כִּיקַר‎ Psa 37:20; feminine יְקָרָה‎ 2Sam 12:30 14t.; construct יִקְרַת‎ Isa 28:16; masculine plural יְקָרִים‎ Lam 4:2; feminine plural יְקָדוֺת‎ 1 Kings 5:31 2t. + Zech 14:6 (see below); יְקָרֹת‎ 1Kin 7:9; suffix בְּיִקְּרוֺתֶ֑יךָ‎ Psa 45:10 (so Baer; van d. b. precious, highly valued: יְקָרָה נֶפֶשׁ‎ Prov 6:26 precious life; opposed to זוֺלֵל‎ Jer 15:19 (of choice elements of character); י בְּעֵינֵי ׳יָקָר לַחֲסִידָיו הַמָּֽוְתָה‎ Psa 116:15; מפניים הִיא יְקָרָה‎ Prov 3:15 she (wisdom) is more precious than rubies (see also

c. יְקָרָה אֶבֶן‎ collective precious stones, jewels 2Sam 12:30 = 1Chr 20:2; 1Kin 10:2, 10; 1:11 2Chr 9:1, 9, 10; 1Chr 29:3; 2Chr 3:6; 32:27; Ezek 27:22; 28:13 (list of precious stones follows), Dan 11:38; so also Lam 4:2, read הַיְקָרִים צִיּוֺן אַבְנֵי‎ (for וגו ׳בְּנֵי ᵑ0‎), see VB; יָקָר שֹׁהַם‎ Job 28:16 (see also Prov 3:15 below b).
