H3508. yothereth

יֹתֶ֫רֶת‎ noun feminine appendage (technical term of P); — most probably (fat) appendage, a (fatty) mass at opening of liver of sacrificial animal, extending to kidneys (see Di DrPolychr. Bible, Lev 3:4); **GFM (Dec. 28, 1899, privately points out that fat parts are always distinguished from this; compare now on יתדת‎ Id.SACRIFICE EB 4206 and especially Id.Oriental. Stud. für Th. Nöldeke (1906), 761, where it is shewn to be the caudate lobe of the liver. — עַלהַֿכָּבֵד הַיֹּתֶרֶת‎ Exod 29:13; Lev 3:4, 10, 15; 4:9; 7:4; ׳הַיּ מִןהַֿכָּבֵד‎ 9:10; construct הַכָּבֵד יֹתֶרֶת‎ Exod 29:22; Lev 8:16, 25; 9:19 (all P).
