H4962. math

[מַת‎] noun masculine male, man (מְתוּֿ‎ in proper name; Zinjirli מת‎ collective males, male offspring; Assyrian mutu, husband; Ethiopic id.; compare Egyptian m-t, phallus, male, Steind in BaNB § 2b, δ); — only plural מְתִים‎ Job 11:3 3t., מְתִם‎ Deut 2:34; 3:6 + Judg 20:48 (probably), for מְתֹם ᵑ0‎, so GFM; construct מְתֵי‎ Gen 34:30 13t.; suffix 2 feminine singular מְתַיִךְ‎; Isa 3:25; 3masculine singular מְתָיו‎ Deut 33:6; —

1 males, men, Isa 3:25 (|| גְּבוּרָתֵךְ‎); more distinctly, עִיר מְתִם (וְ)הַנָּשִׁים וְהַטָּף‎ Deut 2:34; 3:6 every city of males (male population, compare Dr), and the women, etc.; Judg 20:48 (see above; opposed to בְּהֵמָה‎).

2 usually less emphasis on sex, in prose only phrase:

a. מִסְמָּר מְתֵי‎ men of number (numerable, i.e. few) Gen 34:30 (J; apposition of אֲנִי‎ = Jacob, representing his family), Deut 4:27; Jer 44:28, also Psa 105:12 = 1Chr 16:19; compare מִסְמָּר מְתָיו וִיהִי‎ Deut 33:6 and (but) let his men be few.

b. מְעָ֑ט בִּמְתֵי‎ = consisting of a few men Deut 26:5; 28:62

3 elsewhere only in poetry, sex usually not emphasized:

a. Job 11:3; 24:12; Psa 17:14 (twice in verse) (but text probably corrupt, of. Bae We; at least omit ידך ממתים‎ Ol Bi Che, compare Hup-Now); ישׂראל מְתֵי‎ Isa 41:14.

b. poetic phrase: שָׁוְא מְתֵי‎ men of false-hood Job 11:11; Psa 26:4; ׳מ אָוֶן‎ 22:15 men of wickedness; ׳מ סוֺדִי‎ Psalm 19:19 men of my cycle; ׳מ אָהֳלִי‎ Job 31:31 men of my tent (household); ׳מ רָעָב‎ Isa 5:13 men of hunger, but read probably ר ׳מְזֵי‎ sucked out, empty from hunger (as Deut 32:24) Ew De Che Di Du (compare מָזֶה‎).
