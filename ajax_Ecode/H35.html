H35. abiyyonah

אֲבִיּוֺנָה‎ noun feminine caper-berry (as stimulating desire) Eccl 12:5 (see GFMJBL 1891, 55 ff.; so ᵑ9 ᵐ5‎, Mishna אביונות‎, compare NHWB; see also ᵑ6‎; i.e. capparis spinosa, compare RiHWB; so Thes, Ew De, etc.; but Wetzst in De (German ed. 1875) proposes אֶבְיוֺנָה‎ (as feminine of אביון‎) the poor soul in sense = הָאֶבְיוֺנָה נִשְׁמָתוֺ‎ compare Symm ᵑ6‎, where double translation).
