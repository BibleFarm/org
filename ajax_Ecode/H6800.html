H6800. tsana

[צָנַע‎] verb be modest, humble (perhaps properly be retired, Late Hebrew Hiph`il keep close, reserve, preserve, so אַצְנַע ᵑ7‎; צְנִיעַ‎ retiring, modest); —

Hiph`il Infinitive absolute לֶכֶת הַצְנֵעַ‎ Micah 6:8 a making humble to walk = shewing a humble walk (with God).

[צָנוּעַ‎]

adjective modest (on formation compare BaBN § 31 d); — plural צְנוּעִים‎ Prov 11:9 (see Toy; opposed to זָדוֺן‎).
