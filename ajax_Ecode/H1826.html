H1826. damam

I. [דָּמַם‎] verb be or grow dumb, silent, still (Late Hebrew id.; Ethiopic in II. stupefy; III., 1 & 2 be astounded, stupefied) —

Qal Perfect דַּ֫מּוּ‎ Job 30:27, דָּ֑מּוּ‎ Psa 35:15; Imperfect יִדֹּם‎ Amos 5:13 2t., וַיִּדֹּם‎ Lev 10:3; Josh 10:13; 3feminine singular תִּדֹּם‎ Lam 2:18; 2feminine singular תִּדֹּמִּי‎ Jer 48:2 (so Ki Ol & Kö327 q. v.; others Niph`al); וָאֶדֹּם‎ Job 31:34: יִדְּמוּ‎ Exod 15:16 3t.; 1 plural cohortative נִדְּמָה‎ Jer 8:14 (so Thes Ol Bö Kö327q. see; others Niph`al); —

1 be silent Psa 4:5; Psalm 30:13; 35:15 (probably, compare De Che), Ezek 24:17; Lev 10:3 (P) Amos 5:13; in grief Lam 2:10; 3:28.

2 be still (opposed to both speech and motion) Job 31:34; = perish Jer 8:14; 48:2; Psa 31:18 (׳ד לשׁאול‎); with לְ‎ be silent to i.e. be resigned to 37:7; 62:6, followed by עֲצָתִי לְמוֺ‎ Job 29:21 (|| שָֽׁמְעוּ לִי‎); be still, motionless, stand still Josh 10:12-13, (of sun || עמד‎); 1Sam 14:9 (men); Jer 47:6 (of sword || הֵרָֽגְעִי‎); so also of bowels, as seat of mental excitement Job 30:27; Lam 2:18 בַּתעֵֿינֵךְ אַלתִּֿדֹּם‎, i.e. cease not to weep (|| דִּמְעָה כַנַּחַל הוֺרִידִי‎).

3 be struck dumb, astounded, in amazement and fear Exod 15:16 (כאבן‎) Isa 23:2 (but compare II. דמם‎).

Niph`al Perfect3plural וְנָדַמּוּ‎ consecutive Jer 25:37; Imperfect יִדַּמּוּ‎ 49:26; 50:30, יִדָּ֑מּוּ‎ 1Sam 2:9; 2masculine plural תִּדַּמּוּ‎ Jer 51:6 — be made silent, i.e. destroyed; the wicked 1Sam 2:9 (בַּחשֶׁךְ‎), compare Jer 51:6; men of war 49:25; 50:30; dwellings 25:37.

Po. Perfect1singular נַפְשִׁי וְדוֺמַ֫מְתִּי שִׁוִּיתִי‎ Psa 131:2 'composed and quieted my soul Che (as a weaned child).

Hiph`il Perfect3masculine singular suffix הֲדִמָּנוּ‎ he (God) hath silenced us ( = caused to perish) Jer 8:14.

II. [דָּמַם‎] verb wail (?; compare Assyrian damâmu, groan, wail, lament, DlBaer's Ezech xi; pr 64) — only

Qal Imperative אִי ישְׁבֵי דֹּ֫מּוּ‎ Isa 23:2 wail, ye coast dwellers (compare Dll.c.), || הֵילִילוּ‎ 23:1, & אִי ישְׁבֵי הֵילִילוּ‎ 23:6; most, however, assign this to I.דמם‎ q. v. (3).
