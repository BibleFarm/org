H8059. shemittah

שְׁמִטָּה‎ noun feminine a letting drop of exactions, a (temporary) remitting: שׁ ׳תַּעֲשֶׂה‎ Deut 15:1(at end of seven years), 15:2 שׁ קָרָא ׳כִּי ׳לי‎; whence seventh year is הַשּׁ ׳שְׁנַת‎ 15:9; 31:10.
