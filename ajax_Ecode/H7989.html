H7989. shallit

שַׁלִּיט‎ adjective having mastery, domineering; —

1 having mastery: שׁ אָדָם ׳אֵין בָּרוּחַ‎ Eccl 8:8; elsewhere as substantive ׳הַשּׁ‎ the ruler 10:5, so (with עַלהָֿאָרֶץ‎) Gen 42:6 (probably late substitution for original word of E); plural as substantive שַׁלִּיטִים‎ Eccl 7:19 (specifically of Alexander's successors PerlesAnal. 42).

2 domineering, imperious, feminine singular (Kö ii. 10. 201) שַׁלָּ֑טֶת זוֺנָה אִשָּׁה‎ Ezek 16:30 (Jerusalem personified).
