[Heb] חָשַׁב‎, חֹשֵׁב‎ ETCBC: חשׁב‎ (verb｜account) OSHL: h.gr.aa TWOT: 767 GK: H3108, H3110 Greek: βουλεύω, διαλογίζομαι, δοκέω, ἐπιστρέφω, ἐπιχειρέω, εὐλαβέομαι, ἡγέομαι, κινδυνεύω, λαλέω, λογίζομαι, ποιέω, συλλογίζομαι, ὑπολαμβάνω, ὑφαντός, φροντίζω

חָשַׁב‎ verb think, account (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic חֲשַׁב‎, ; Arabic ; Ethiopic id.; Phoenician noun חשב‎ meaning dubious see CISi. 86) —

Qal Perfect ׳ח‎ Isa 33:8-9t.; חָשַׁבְתָּה‎ 2Sam 14:13 etc.; Imperfect יַחְשֹׁב‎ Isa 10:7 4t.; יַחֲשָׁבֿ‎ 2Sam 19:20; Psalm 40:18; suffix יַחְשְׁבֵנִי‎ Job 19:11; 33:10; plural יַחְשְׁבוּ‎ Psa 41:8; Dan 11:25; יַחְשֹׁ֑בוּ‎ Isa 13:17; יַחֲשֹׁבוּן‎ Psa 35:20; נַחְשְׁבָה‎ Jer 18:18, etc. + 9 t. Imperfect; Infinitive construct לַחְשֹׁב‎ Exod 31:4 3t.; Participle חשֵׁב‎ 26:1 15t., חוֺשֵׁב‎2Chr 26:15; חשְׁבִים‎ Neh 6:2-3t., חשְׁבֵי‎ Micah 2:1 3t.; —

3 charge, impute ׳ח ל ׳עון‎, 2Sam 19:20 impute iniquity to.

4 esteem, value, regard, silver Isa 13:17, a man 33:8, the servant of ׳י‎ 53:3.

5 invent ingenious and artistic things, ׳ח שׁיר כלי להם‎ Amos 6:5 invent for themselves instruments of music; ׳ח מחשׁבת (כל)‎ invent cunning work (of artistic devices in construction of tabernacle) Exod 31:4; 35:32, 35 (all P), so 2Chr 2:13; חשֵׁב מַעֲשֵׂה‎ work of the cunning (ingenious, inventive) workman (of artistic devices in weaving; see especially VB and Di) Exod 26:1, 31; 28:6, 15; 36:8, 35; 39:3, 8 (all P); חוֺשֵׁב מַחֲשֶׁבֶת‎2Chr 26:15 inventions of inventive men (of engines of war); וְחשֵׁב חָרָשׁ‎ craftsman and inventive workman (in construction of tabernacle, see above) Exod 35:35; 38:23 (P).
