H1151. Ben

-ammi בֶּןעַֿמִּי‎ proper name, masculine (son of my people) son of Lot by his younger daughter, and ancestor of the Ammonites (בְּנֵיעַֿמּוֺן‎ see עַמּוֺן‎) Gen 19:38.
