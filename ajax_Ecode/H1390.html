H1390. Gibah

II. גִּבְעָה‎ proper name, of a location (hill) — ׳ג‎ Josh 15:57 +; (הַ)גִּבְעָ֫תָה‎ Judg 20:4-5t.; גִּבְעַת‎ Josh 18:28; construct גִּבְעַת‎ 1Sam 11:4 9t.; absolute always with article except Josh 15:57; 18:28; Judg 19:12; 20:31; 1Sam 10:26; 2Chr 13:2; —

1 a city of Judah Josh 15:57 (perhaps one of two villages called Gabaa, Gabatha in Onomasticon see LagOnom. 246, 128; 2nd ed 255, 160).

2 city of Benjamin Judg 19:14, 16 20t. Judges; 1Sam 10:26; 14:2; 22:6; 26:1; 2Sam 23:29 = 1Chr 11:31; also Hosea 5:8; 9:9; 10:9 (twice in verse) (compare Judg 19:12ff. 13:2; perhaps also 1Sam 7:1; 10:10; 2Sam 6:3-4, read also (for גבע‎) 1Sam 13:16 Judg 20:10, 33; = גִּבְעַת‎ Josh 18:28; called also בִּנְיָמִין גִּבְעַת‎ 1Sam 13:2, 15; 14:16, & שָׁאוּל גִּבְעַת‎ 11:4; 15:34; Isa 10:29; 2Sam 21:6 read probably ᵐ5 גִּבְעוֺן‎ We Dr.

3 a city of Ephraim, called ִמּינְחָס גִּבְעַת‎ Josh 24:33.
