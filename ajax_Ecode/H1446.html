H1446. Gedor

גְּדֹר גְּדוֺר,‎ proper name (Arabic wall); —

1. proper name, of a location city of Judah גְּדוֺר‎ Josh 15:58; also as proper name, masculine, under figure of Genealogy גְּדֹר‎ son of Penuel 1Chr 4:4, גְּדוֺר‎ son of Yered 4:18; also הַגְּדוֺר‎ 12:7 see d. H.; הַגְּדוּד‎ Baer, compare his note.

2. proper name, of a location גְּדֹר‎ 1Chr 4:39, but read Gerar, according to ᵐ5‎ Ew Hi Be and others

3. proper name, masculine גְּדוֺר‎ 1Chr 8:31; 9:37 a Benjamite, of Gibeon.

I. גדשׁ‎ (Late Hebrew גָּדַשׁ‎, Aramaic גְּדַשׁ‎, heap up; compare also Arabic ).

1）毗努伊勒的子孫（代上4:4）

2）雅列之子孫（代上）

1）基突是猶大支派地業之一城（書15:58）

2）西緬支派有些宗族在基多附近牧羊（代上4:39）
