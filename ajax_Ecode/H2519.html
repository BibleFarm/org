חֲלַקְלַקָּה‎ (chălaqlaqqâh｜khal-ak-lak-kaw'｜noun｜feminine｜smoothness)
[Heb] חֲלַקְלַקָּה‎, חֲלַקְלַק‎ ETCBC: חֲלַקְלַקֹּות‎ (subs｜smoothness) OSHL: h.db.ag TWOT: 670f GK: H2761
Derivation: by reduplication from חָלַק‎;
Strong's: properly, something very smooth; i.e. a treacherous spot; figuratively, blandishment
KJV: flattery, slippery.

H2519. chalaqlaqqoth

חֲלַקְלַקּוֺת‎ noun plural feminine abstract smoothness, slipperiness, flattery, fine promises;

1 slipperiness וַחֲלַקְלַקֹּת חשׁך יהידֿרכם‎ Psa 35:6; כַּחֲלַקְלַקּוֺת להם דרכם יהיה‎ Jer 23:12.

2 fine promises בַּחֲלַקְלַקּוֺה מלכוּת והחזיק‎ Dan 11:21; compare 11:34.
