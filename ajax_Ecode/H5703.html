עַד‎ (ʻad｜ad｜noun｜masculine｜perpetuity)
[Heb] עַד‎ ETCBC: עַד‎ (subs｜future) OSHL: p.aq.ab TWOT: 1565a GK: H6329 Greek: αἰών
Derivation: from עָדָה‎;
Strong's: properly, a (peremptory) terminus, i.e. (by implication) duration, in the sense of advance or perpetuity (substantially as a noun, either with or without a preposition)
KJV: eternity, ever(-lasting, -more), old, perpetually, [phrase] world without end.

H5703. ad

I. עַד‎ and וָעֶ֑ד‎ (so always) noun masculine perpetuity (= advancing time, compare Assyrian adû, time at the present times); —

1 of past time: עַר מִנִּי‎ Job 20:4; עד הררי‎ Hab 3:6 ancient mountains (compare עולם‎ 1). **so Gen 49:26 (read עַד הַרְרֵי‎ for עַד הוֺרַ֔י‎).

2 of future time, לָעַד‎ (usually לָעַ֑ד‎) for ever:

a. during lifetime, of king Psa 21:7; Prov 29:14; of others Psa 9:19; 22:27; Psalm 61:9; Prov 12:19.

b. of things, יֵחָֽצֵב֑וּן בַּצּוּר לָעַד‎ Job 19:24.

c. of continuous existence, of nations, עַד גְּבֶרֶת‎ (of Babylon, compare עולם‎ 2c) Isa 47:7 (yet see III. עַד‎

II 3); anger, לעד‎ Amos 1:11; elsewhere עַד עֲדֵי‎ Psa 83:18; 92:8; Isa 26:4; 65:18.

d. of divine existence, עַד שֹׁכֵן‎ Isa 57:15; attributes, לָעַד‎ Psa 111:3; 111:10; 112:3; 112:9; residence in Zion, עַד עֲדֵי‎ 132:14; law of God לָעַד‎ 19:10; promise as to dynasty of David, לָעד‎ 89:30; עַד עֲדֵי‎ 132:12; inheritance of land, לָעַד‎ 37:29; continuous relations between God and his people 1Chr 28:9; Isa 64:8; Micah 7:18 לעד‎.

e. phrases (see עולם‎ 2m): וָעֶ֑ד (לְ)וְֺלָם‎ Psa 9:6; 10:16; 21:5; 45:7; Psalm 45:18; Psalm 48:15; Psalm 52:10; 104:5; 119:44; 145:1; 145:2; 145:21; Exod 15:18; Micah 4:5; Dan 12:3; לְעוֺלָם לָעַד‎ Psa 111:8; 148:6; עַד עַדעֿוֺלְמֵי‎ Isa 45:17. — 30:8 read (עַדעֿוֺלָם‎) לְעֵד‎ for a witness ᵑ7 ᵑ9 ᵑ6‎ Ges Ew Di Che Du) for לָעַד ᵑ0‎.
