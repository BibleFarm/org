H6722. Tsidoni

צִידֹנִי‎ adjective, of a people of foregoing; — ׳צ‎ Judg 3:3; Ezek 32:30; elsewhere plural צִידֹנִים‎ Deut 3:9 +, צִידוֺנִים‎ Judg 10:12 צִדֹנִים‎ 18:7; 3t.; feminine plural צֵדְנִיֹּת‎ 1Kin 11:1; — as substantive = Sidonians, coll with article Ezek 32:30; Judg 3:3; in earlier literature apparently = Phoenicians Deut 3:9; Josh 13:4, 6; Judg 3:3; 10:12; 18:7 (twice in verse); 1 Kings 5:20 (= Tyre v.1Kin 5:15), 16:31 (if, [JosAnt. viii. 13, 1] Ethb. was king of Tyre also), Ezek 32:30; also צ אְֶהֵֹי ׳עַשְׁתֹּרֶת‎ 1Kin 11:5, 33, compare 2Kin 23:13; named with Tyrians 1Chr 22:4; Ezra 3:7.
