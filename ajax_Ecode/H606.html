H606. enash

אֱנָשׁ‎ noun masculine man, mankind; — absolute ׳א‎ Dan 2:10 +; emphatic אֲנָשָׁא‎ 2:38 +, so Qr 4:13; 4:14 ( > Kt אנושׁא‎ K§ 57f); so Nabataean אנוש‎ often Lzb222 Cooke219 SAC22): plural אֲנָשָׁים‎ 4:14 (Hebraism, M53* proposes אֲנָשָׁא‎); —

1 man, human being, Dan 2:10; 3:10; 4:13; 5:5, 7 (twice in verse); 6:13 (twice in verse); 7:4 (twice in verse); 7:8; Ezra 6:11; so בַּראֱֿנָשׁ‎ Dan 7:13 a son of man, human being (see especially DrDan 102; DB SON OF MAN).

2 collective men, people, Ezra 4:11; Dan 4:14a; 4:22 (twice in verse); 4:29 (twice in verse); 4:30; 5:21, compare plural 4:14; א ׳ןְרַע‎ 2:43 i.e. human offspring; אֲנָשָׁא בְּנֵי‎ sons of men, = men, 2:38; 5:21.
