H2450. chakam

חָכָם‎ adjective wise, ׳ח‎ Deut 4:6 67t.; construct חֲכַם‎ Isa 3:3 9t.; plural חֲכָמִים‎ Deut 1:13 39t.; construct חַכְמֵי‎ Exod 28:3-4t.; suffix חֲכָמֶיךָ‎ Isa 19:12 etc. + 8 t.; feminine חֲכָמָה‎ 2Sam 14:2; 20:16; construct חַכְמַת‎ Exod 35:25; plural חֲכָמוֺת‎ Jer 9:16; construct חַכְמוֺת‎ Judg 5:29; Prov 14:1(but read חָכְמוֺת‎ De) —

1 skilful in technical work; artificers Isa 3:3; sailors Ezek 27:8, so (probably) 27:9; mourning women Jer 9:16; artisans of tabernacle and temple and their furniture Exod 28:3; 31:6; 35:10; 36:1-2, 4, 8 (P), 1Chr 22:15; 2Chr 2:6; 2:12; 2:13 (twice in verse); women in spinning Exod 35:25 (P); goldsmiths and other artisans Jer 10:9; makers of idol-images Isa 40:20.

2 wise in the administration of affairs: Joseph in Egypt Gen 41:33, 39 (E); heads of tribes Deut 1:13, 15; Judg 16:19; David 2Sam 14:20, of Solomon 1Kin 2:9; 3:12; 1 Kings 5:21 2Chr 2:11; the prince of Tyre, in satire Ezek 28:3; of kings in General Prov 20:26; class of political advisers of Judah Isa 29:14; Jer 18:18, apparently also Isa 5:21; of Egypt 19:11 (twice in verse); 19:12, Edom Obadiah 8, the nations Jer 10:7; so of God Isa 31:2.

3 shrewd, crafty, cunning: Jonadab 2Sam 13:3; the princesses Judg 5:29; חכמה אשׁה‎ wise woman 2Sam 14:2; 20:16; Generally, Jer 9:22; intelligent animals Prov 30:24; ח ׳לכד בערמם‎ taking the cunning in their craft Job 5:13; בעיני חכם‎ wise in one's own eyes Prov 3:7; 26:5, 12, 16; 28:11; לב חכמי‎ wise of mind (in their own mind) Job 37:24.

4 plural class of learned and shrewd men, including astrologers, magicians and the like, of Egypt Gen 41:8 (E), Exod 7:11 (P; compare Isa 19:11 (twice in verse); 19:12); Babylon 44:25; Jer 50:35; 51:57; Persia Est 1:13; 6:13.

5 prudent, towards king Prov 16:14; in controversies 11:29; 29:8-9, 11; religious affairs Hosea 13:13; Hosea 14:10; Jer 4:22; 8:8-9, Deut 4:6; 32:6; Psa 107:43; ׳ח אישׁ‎ Jer 9:11.

6 wise, ethically and religiously,. in Wisdom Literature:

a. as adjective, לֵב חֲכַם‎ wise of mind Prov 10:8; 16:21; חָכָם לֵב‎ Eccl 8:5; of the son Prov 10:1 = 15:20; 13:1; 23:24; Eccl 2:19; ח ׳ילד‎ 4:13 wise boy; ח ׳גבר‎ Job 34:34 wise man; ח ׳מוכיח‎ wise reprover Prov 25:12.

b. = substantive: (1) as a wise learner in school of wisdom, he fears God and departs from evil Prov 14:16; is silent 17:28; hearkens to counsel 12:15; hears and increases in learning 1:5; his ear seeketh knowledge 18:15; he receives it 21:11; and stores it up 10:14; his ears hearken to the reproof which giveth life 15:31; and he becomes wiser through it 9:8-9,. (2) as a wise teacher, a sage, he does not answer with windy knowledge Job 15:2; he tells the experience of the past Job 15:48; has knowledge 34:2; teaches it Eccl 12:9; disperses it Prov 15:7; his tongue is health 12:18; it utters knowledge aright 15:2; his mind instructs his mouth and adds learning to his lips 16:23; his words are gracious Eccl 10:12; it is good to hear his rebuke 7:5; his instruction is a fountain of life Prov 13:14; one walking with him becomes wiser 13:20. The חכמים‎ recall the Greek σόφοι, having their schools, pupils (בֵן‎), discipline (מוּסָר‎), principles and collections of wisdom, חכמים דברי‎ 1:6; 22:17 (compare 24:23), Eccl 9:17; 12:11; God himself is לבב חכם‎ Job 9:4. (3) the wise are prosperous: true, in sceptical view of Ecclesiastes, they have no advantage over the fool Eccl 6:8; but die as the fool 2:16 (twice in verse); Psa 49:11; they will not be able to find the works of God Eccl 8:17; like others are in the hands of God 9:1; they do not secure bread 9:11; but elsewhere reverse is true: precious treasure is in his dwelling Prov 21:20; his lips preserve him 14:3; they inherit glory 3:35; wealth is their crown 14:24; the wise man is strong 24:5; Eccl 7:19; his eyes are in his head 2:14; his mind is at his right hand 10:2; he interprets things 8:1 (4) the wise man is a blessing to others: he wins men Prov 11:30; scales the city of the mighty 21:22; though poor he delivers the city Eccl 9:15; is a reproof to scorners Prov 15:12; his mind is in the house of mourning Eccl 7:4; injustice makes him foolish 7:7; Job finds no wise men among his cruel friends Job 17:10.
