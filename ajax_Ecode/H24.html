אָבִיב‎ (ʼâbîyb｜aw-beeb'｜noun｜masculine｜green)
[Heb] אָבִיב‎ ETCBC: אָבִיב‎ (subs｜ears) OSHL: a.ab.ac TWOT: 1b GK: H26 Greek: νέος, παρίστημι
Derivation: from an unused root (meaning to be tender);
Strong's: green, i.e. a young ear of grain; hence, the name of the month Abib or Nisan
KJV: Abib, ear, green ears of corn (not maize).
