H3077. Yehoyada

יוֺיָדָע יְהוֺיָדָע,‎ proper name, masculine (׳י‎ knoweth; compare אֶלְיָדָע יְדַעְיָה,‎ & references, בְּעֶלְיָדָע‎) —

1 ׳יְהוֺ‎; ᵐ5‎ usually Ἰωδαε, A sometimes Ἰωιαδαε (2Sam 23:30 + ), ᵐ5‎L Ἰωαδ (20:23 ὠαδδαι), father of Benaiah (David's time), in combination בֶּןיְֿהוֺ ׳בְּנָיָהוּ‎ 8:18; 20:23; 23:20, 22; 1Kin 1:8, 26, 32, 36, 38, 44; 2:25, 29, 34, 35, 46; 4:4; 1Chr 11:22, 24; 18:17; 27:5; בֶּןבְּֿנָיָהוּ יְהוֺיָדָע‎ 27:34 is probably erroneous inversion of the usual order; ׳יְהוֺ‎ alone only 12:28 (where called לְאַחֲרֹן הַנָּגִיד‎).

2 ᵐ5 ׳יְהוֺ,‎ usually Ἰωδαε A sometimes Ἰωιαδαε etc., (chief) priest at Jerusalem in time of Joash 2Kin 11:4, 9 (twice in verse); 11:15, 17; 12:3; 12:8; 12:10; 2Chr 22:11; 23:1, 8 (twice in verse); 23:9, 11, 14, 16, 18; 24:2-3, 6, 12, 14 (twice in verse); 24:15; 24:17; 24:20; 24:22; 24:25; Jer 29:26.

3 יוֺיָדָע‎ a builder at wall of Jerusalem, with Nehemiah, Neh 3:6 (ᵐ5‎ Ἰοειαα, Ἰοειδα, Ἰωδαε etc.)

4 ׳יוֺ‎ son of high priest Eliashib Neh 12:10-11, 22; 13:28 (ᵐ5‎ Ἰωδα, Ἰωαδαε, Ἰωιαδα).
