H1749. donag

דּוֺנַג‎ noun masculine wax, דּוֺנַג‎ Micah 1:4 2t.; דּוֺנָג֑‎ Psa 22:15 — always in simile, of melting; at theophany hills melt like wax 97:5; like wax before fire Micah 1:4; wicked perish as wax melteth before fire Psa 68:3; heart melteth like wax 22:15 (simile of fear, despair). On ׳ד‎ in 118:12 compare Bae Checritical note.
