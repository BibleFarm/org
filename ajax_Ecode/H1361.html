H1361. gabahh

גָּבַהּ‎ verb be high, exalted (Late Hebrew id. (Hiph`il), Aramaic גְּבַהּ‎, compare Arabic forehead, prominence of forehead; compare perhaps also Assyrian gabâni, heights (?) LotzTP 133) —

Qal Perfect ׳ג‎2Chr 26:16 5t.; גָָּֽבְהָא֯‎ Ezek 31:5 (א‎ = ה‎); גָּבַהְתָּ‎ 31:10, גָּבְהוּ‎ Job 35:5 3t.; Imperfect יִגְבַּהּ‎ Prov 18:12 5t.; suffix יִגְבְּהוּ‎ Ezek 31:14, יִגְבָּ֑הוּ‎ Job 36:7, תִּגְבָּ֑הוּ‎ Jer 13:15, תִּגְבְּהֶינָה‎ Ezek 16:50; Infinitive כִּגְבֹהַּ‎ Psa 103:11, לְגָבְהָה‎ Zeph 3:11; —

1 be high, lofty, tall, e.g. tree Ezek 19:11; 31:5, 10, 14, heavens Job 35:5; Isa 55:9; Psa 103:11, man 1Sam 10:23.

2 be exalted, of man in dignity and honour Job 36:7, of servant of Yahweh Isa 52:13, God 5:16, God's ways 55:9.

3 lofty לִבּוֺ גָּבַהּ‎: —

a. in a good sense, encouraged in the ways of Yahweh 2Chr 17:6;

b. elsewhere in a bad sense, be haughty Psa 131:1; Prov 18:12; 2Chr 26:16; 32:25; Ezek 28:2, 5, 17, and so without לב‎ Isa 3:16; Jer 13:15; Ezek 16:50; Zeph 3:11.

Hiph`il Perfect הִגְבַּהְתִּי‎ Ezek 17:24; Imperfect יַגְבִּיהַּ‎ Job 39:27; Jer 49:16; Obadiah 4; וַיַּגְבִּיהֶהָ‎2Chr 33:14; יַגְבִּיהוּ‎ Job 5:7; Infinitive הַגְבֵּהַּ‎ Isa 7:11; Ezek 21:31; Participle מַגְבִּיהַּ‎ Prov 17:19; Psa 113:5; — make high, exalt, e.g. trees Ezek 17:24, wall 2Chr 33:14, gate Prov 17:19, nest Jer 49:16; Obadiah 4, dwelling Psa 103:5, a request Isa 7:11, the lowly Ezek 21:31; עוּף יַגְבִּיהוּ‎ make their flight high, soar aloft Job 5:7, without עוף‎ 39:27.
