H2030. harah

II. הָרָה‎ adjective feminine pregnant — absolute ׳ה‎ Gen 16:11 10t.; construct הֲרַת‎ Jer 20:17; plural construct הָרוֺת‎ Amos 1:13; suffix הָרוֺתֶיהָ‎ 2Kin 15:16; הָרֹתֵיהֶם‎ 8:12; — pregnant, as attributive, ה ׳אִשָּׁה‎ Exod 21:22; as predicate Gen 38:24 (לִזְנוּנִים‎), 38:25 (לְאִישׁ‎), 1Sam 4:19; 2Sam 11:5; Isa 7:14 (+ וְיֹלֶדֶת‎); in phrase וְיֹלַדְתְּ הָרָה הִנָּךְ‎ Gen 16:11 (on resemblance to Isa 7:14 compare PetersHebraica. Apr. 1885, 243; Apr. 1886, 175), Judg 13:5, 7; = substantive pregnant women, women with child Amos 1:13; 2Kin 8:12; 15:16; Jer 31:8, and, in simile, Isa 26:17; once of womb עוֺלָם הֲרַת וְרַחְמָהּ‎ Jer 20:17.

הֵרוֺן‎ see הֵרָיוֺן‎ below

[הָרִיָּה‎] adjective feminine pregnant, only plural as substantive וְהָרִיּוֺתָיו‎ Hosea 14:1 (compare Assyrian êriiâtê JägerBAS i. 473).
