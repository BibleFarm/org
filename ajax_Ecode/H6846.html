H6846. Tsephanyah

or Tsephanyahu צְפַנְיָה(וּׅ‎ proper name, masculine Σοφονιας (׳י‎ hath treasured; Old Hebrew צפניהו‎, Phoenician צפנבעל‎); —

1 priest, יָה‎- Jer 21:1; 22:25, 29; 52:24 = יָהוּ‎- 2Kin 25:18 (ᵐ5‎L Σαφανιας), Jer 37:3. Elsewhere יָה‎-:

2 the prophet Zeph 1:1.

3 a Judaean Zech 6:10, 14.

4 ancestor of Heman 1Chr 6:21 (Σαφανιας; = אוּרִיאֵל‎ v.6:9).
