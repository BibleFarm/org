H357. Ayyalon

אַיָּלוֺן‎ proper name, of a location (Deer-field) Aijalon — ׳א‎ Josh 10:12 8t.;

1 city in Dan Josh 19:42; 21:24 (Levitical city) Judg 1:35; 1Sam 14:31 doubtless same; so 1Chr 6:54 (where Dan omitted); apparently later in Benjamin 8:13; 2Chr 11:10; 28:18 (see Be); אַיָּלוֺן עֵמֶק‎ Josh 10:12 almost certainly named from same; = Epiph. Ἰαλω modern Jâlo RobBR ii. 253, Surveyiii. 19.

2 city in Zebulun Judg 12:12.
