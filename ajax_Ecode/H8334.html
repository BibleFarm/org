H8334. sharath

[שָׁרַת‎]97 verb Pi`el minister, serve (compare Late Hebrew שֵׁרוּת‎ (temple-)ministry, Phoenician משרת‎ ministry, and perhaps verb שרת‎ minister); —

Pi`el Perfect3masculine singular שֵׁרֵת‎ Num 8:26; Deut 18:7; 3masculine plural שֵׁרְתוּ‎ Num 3:6; Imperfect3masculine singular וַיְשָׁ֫רֶת‎ Gen 39:4, 3masculine plural יְשָֽׁרְתוּ‎ Num 3:31 +, etc.; Infinitive construct לְשָׁרֵת‎ Exod 28:35 +, שָׁם לְשָׁ֫רֶת‎ Deut 17:12, etc.; Participle מְשָׁרֵת‎ Num 11:28 + (2Sam 13:9 see מַשְׂרֵת‎, p. 602:a), feminine מְשָׁרַת‎ 1Kin 1:15, etc.; — serve:

1 of ministerial service (opposed tomenial, עבד‎):

a. higher domestic service: Joseph Gen 39:4; 40:4 (JE), Elisha's chief servant 2Kin 4:43; 6:15, royal domestics 2Sam 13:17-18, 1Kin 10:5; 2Chr 9:4; Est 2:2; 6:3, Abishag 1Kin 1:4, 15.

b. of royal officers (late) 1Chr 27:1; 28:1; 2Chr 17:19; 22:8; Est 1:10; Prov 29:12.

c. Joshua as chief assistant to Moses Exod 24:13; 33:11 (E), Num 11:28 (J), Josh 1:1 (D), Elisha to Elijah 1Kin 19:21.

d. of angels to ׳י‎ Psa 103:21, compare 104:4.

e. of kings of nations in prophets Isa 60:10.

2 usually of special service in worship:

a. of Levitical priests, absolute 1Kin 8:11 2Chr 5:14; with accusative ׳י‎ Deut 10:8; 17:12; 21:5; Jer 33:21 +?אֹתִי מְשָֽׁרְתֵי‎ 33:22; also Isa 60:7 of animals put figurative for those sacrificing them; of foreigners admitted to priesthood 61:6, compare 56:6; so probably Samuel conceived as priest 1Sam 2:11; 3:1, י פני ׳את‎ 2:18 (פני‎ probably accusative, compare ᵐ5‎ τῷ προσώπῳ 2:11), ׳שׁ י ׳בְּשֵׁם‎Deut 18:5, 7.

b. Levites (P, and late); with accusative of Aaronic priests Num 3:6; 8:26; 18:2, with accusative of ׳י‎ 1Chr 15:2; 2Chr 29:11 a; probably also in reference to Levitical singer Psa 101:6; with accusative tabernacle Num 1:50 (P), congregation 16:9 (P), people Ezek 44:11 + 44:12 (of idolatry); absolute 1Chr 16:4; 2Chr 23:6; 29:11; b 31:2; Ezra 8:17 (participle as substantive, ministers); י בְּרִית ׳אֲרוֺן ׳שׁ לִפְנֵי‎ 1Chr 16:37, ׳שׁ הַכֹּהֲנִים נֶגֶד‎2Chr 8:14; ׳שׁ‎ with ב‎ location 1Chr 26:12; especially with בְּ‎, of sacred vessels Num 3:31; 4:9, 12, 14; 2Kin 25:14 = Jer 52:18; בַּשִּׁיר‎ 1Chr 6:17.

c. Zadokite priests (Ezekiel), absolute Ezek 42:14 3t., with accusative ׳י‎ 40:48 4t., construct מְשָׁרֲתֵי‎ before sacred place 45:4-5, 46:24.

d. Aaronic priests, absolute Exod 28:35 4t. P; with accusative ׳י‎ 1Chr 23:13; ׳לי‎2Chr 13:10; י ׳מְשָֽׁרְתֵי‎ Joel 1:9, 13; 2:17, מזבח מְשָֽׁרְתֵי‎ 1:13, with בַּקֹּדֶשׁ‎ Exod 28:43 4t. P.

e. of idolatry, with accusative ואבן עץ‎ Ezek 20:32.
