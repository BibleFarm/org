צְדָקָה‎157 noun feminine righteousness; — absolute ׳צ‎ Gen 15:6+81 t.; construct צִדְקַת‎ Deut 33:21+5 t.; suffix צִדְקָתִי‎ Gen 30:33 +, etc.; plural צְדָקוֺת‎ Isa 33:15+3t.; construct צִדְקוֺת‎ Judg 5:11+; suffix צִדְקֹתֶיךָ‎ Dan 9:16, etc.; —

1 righteousness, in government:

a. of judge, ruler, king: || משׁפט‎ Isa 5:7; 9:6; Amos 5:7; 6:12; וצ ׳משׁפט‎ 2Sam 8:15, עשׂה‎ David executed justice and righteousness = 1Chr 18:14, compare 1Kin 10:9 2Chr 9:8; Jer 22:3, 15; 33:15; Ezek 45:9; ׳בּצ כִּסֵּא יִכּוֺן‎ Prov 16:12, compare Isa 54:14; הצ ׳מַעֲשֵׂה השׂ, ׳עֲבֹדַת‎ 32:17, compare V:16; צ ׳נֹגְשַׂיִךְ‎ 60:17.

b. of law, || י ׳צִדְקַת משׁפטים,‎ Deut 33:21.

c. of Davidic king, Messiah, || משׁפט‎, Psa 72:1; 72:3; Isa 9:6; Jer 23:5; 33:15.

2 God's attribute as sovereign Psa 36:7; 71:19; in government, וצ ׳משׁפט עשׂה‎ 99:4; Jer 9:23; administering justice Job 37:28; punishment Isa 1:27; 5:16; 10:22; 28:17; Dan 9:7; vindication of his people Micah 7:9.

3 righteousness, in a case or cause, החזקתי בצדקתי‎ Job 27:6 on my righteousness I hold fast; צ עוד לי ישׁ ׳מה‎ 2Sam 19:29 what right have I yet ? of God's judgments, צ ׳הגיד‎ Isa 57:12 (iron.); צ ׳השׁיב‎ 1Sam 26:23; Job 33:26; השׁיב ׳כּצ,‎ 2Sam 22:25, גמל‎ 22:21, נתן‎ 1Kin 8:52 2Chr 6:23.

4 righteousness = truthfulness, ובצ ׳באמת‎ Isa 48:1; Zech 8:8; in word Isaiah 45:28; Isa 63:1, oath Jer 4:2.

5 righteousness, as ethically right: Gen 30:33 (J) Deut 6:25; Isa 33:5; 41:18; Ezek 14:14, 20; Prov 10:2; 11:4-5, 18, 19+17 t., +(ה)צַדִּיק(יםׅ צִדְקַת‎ Isa 5:23; Ezek 18:20; 33:12; ׳צ ישׁרים‎ Prov 11:6; צ ׳אֹרַח‎ 8:20; 12:28; צ ׳דֶּרֶךְ‎ 16:31; צ ׳עשׂה‎ do righteousness Psa 106:3; Isa 56:1; 58:2; Ezek 18:22; וצ משׁפט ׳עשׂה‎ 18:5-6t. Ezekiel; צ ׳עשׂה ומשׁפט‎ Gen 18:10 (J) Prov 21:8; צ ׳רדף‎ 15:9; 21:21; ובצ באמת ׳הלך‎1k 3:6; (ל)צ לוֺ ׳חשׁב‎ Gen 15:6 (JE) imputed to him (for) righteousness, Psa 106:31; ׳צ וָחֶסֶד‎ Prov 21:21 (twice in verse) (strike out ᵐ5‎A B Toy).

6 righteousness as vindicated, justification, salvation, etc. (compare צֶדֶק‎ 6):

a. of God || יְשׁוּעָה תְּשׁוּעָה, יָשַׁע,‎ Isa 45:8; 46:13; 51:6-7t. Isa2; || בְּרָכָה‎ Psa 24:6; || נַחֲלָה‎ Isa 54:17; || חֶסֶד‎ Psa 36:11; 103:17; צ ׳שֶׁמֶשׁ‎ Malachi 3:20 sun of righteousness (with healing); ׳י ׳בִּצ,‎ delivers, guides, exalts his people 5:9; 31:2; 71:2; 89:17; 119:40; 143:1; 143:11; בְּצִדְקָתֶ֑ךָ אַליָֿבאֹוּ‎ 69:28 (of wicked); as accusative after verbs of declaring, etc., his saving (delivering) righteousness Psalm 22:32; 40:11; 51:16; 71:15; 71:16; 71:24; 98:2; 145:7; also צ ׳יִוָּדַע‎ 88:13; לָעַד ׳צ עֹמֶדֶת‎ his righteousness endureth for ever 111:3; compare 119:142.

b. of people, = prosperity, || עשׁר הון,‎ Prov 8:18; לצ ׳מוֺרֶה‎ early rain for prosperity Joel 2:23.

7 plural righteous acts:

a. of God Judg 5:11 (twice in verse); 1Sam 12:7; Micah 6:5; vindication of right Psa 103:6; redemptive Isa 45:24; Dan 9:16.

b. of man's moral conduct Isa 64:5; Jer 51:10, also probably Ezek 3:20; 18:24; 33:13 (Kt singular) Psa 11:7 (? gloss), Dan 9:18; as adverb accusative, צְדָקוֺת הֹלֵךְ‎ Isa 33:15.
