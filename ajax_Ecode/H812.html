H812. Eshkol

אֶשְׁכֹּל‎ proper name

1. masculine an Amorite, brother of Mamre, dwelling in neighbourhood of Hebron Gen 14:13, 24.

2 in combination אֶשְׁכֹּל נַחַל‎ Num 13:23; Deut 1:24; ׳נ אֶשְׁכּוֺל‎ Num 13:24; 32:9; — valley of Eshcol, = valley of a cluster, region of Hebron (compare 13:22); in 13:24 derived from ׳א‎ cluster, q. v.; see however Di on the passage
