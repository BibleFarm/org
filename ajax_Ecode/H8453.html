Derivation: or תֹּשָׁב‎; (1 Kings 17:1), from יָשַׁב‎; a dweller (but not outlandish (נׇכְרִי‎)); especially (as distinguished from a native citizen (active participle of יָשַׁב‎) and a temporary inmate (גֵּר‎) or mere lodger (לוּן‎))
Strong's: resident alien
KJV: foreigner, inhabitant, sojourner, stranger.
