H472. Elisheba

אֱלִישֶׁ֫בַע‎ proper name, feminine (God is an oath, by which one swears, compare Isa 19:18; Amos 8:14; Zeph 1:5) wife of Aaron Exod 6:23; = Ἐλεισαβεθ ᵐ5‎, compare Luke 1:7.
