H1803. dallah

I. דַּלָּה‎ noun feminine collective, hair, thrum (both front hanging down) — דַּלָּה‎ Isa 38:12; construct דַּלַּת‎ Cant 7:6; — hair, ׳ד ראשׁך‎ Cant 7:6; thrum (threads of warp hanging in loom) in simile of premature death יְבַצְּעֵנִי מִדַּלָּה‎ 38:12.

II. [דַּלָּה‎] noun feminine the poor — construct דַּלַּת‎ Jer 40:7 2t.; plural דַּלּוֺת‎ 52:15-16, — the poor (collective, weak, helpless ones) 40:7; 2Kin 24:14; 25:12 (in || Jer 52:16, as also 52:15, ᵑ0‎ has strangely the plural)
