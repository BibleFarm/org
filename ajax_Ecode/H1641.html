H1641. garar

[גָּרַר‎] verb drag, drag away (Late Hebrew id., Arabic Aramaic גְּרַר‎, ; Assyrian garâru, run, ZimBP 102; etc.) —

Qal Imperfect suffix יְנֹרֵהוּ‎ Hab 1:15, יְגוֺרֵם‎ Prov 21:7 — drag away, fishes in net, figurative Hab 1:15 (Chaldeans, their opponents); violence, the wicked Prov 21:7.

Niph`al (read

Qal ? Samaritan יָגוֺר‎ compare Di) Imperfect יִגָּ֑ר‎ Lev 11:7 with accusative of congnate meaning with verb לֹאיִֿגָּ֑ר גֵּרָה‎ = (the) cud he cheweth not, compare also Deut 14:8 ᵐ5 ⅏‎ (Di).

Po`al Participle (denominative; so Late Hebrew Qal & Niph`al) בַּמְּגֵרָה מִגֹרָרוֺת‎ 1Kin 7:9 sawn with the saw.

Hithpo`el מִתְגּוֺרֵ֑ר סַעַר‎ Jer 36:23 a (sweeping) roaring whirlwind (|| 23:19 has מִתְחוֺלֵל‎). On יִתְגּוֺרָרוּ‎ Hosea 7:14, see I.גדד גור,‎.
