H1409. gad

II. [גַּד‎] 1. noun [masculine] fortune, good fortune (Arabic id., Aramaic גַּדָּא‎, ) — Gen 30:11 בּגד‎ Kt, i.e. בְּגָד‎ (גָד֑ בָּא‎ Qr), ᵐ5‎ ἐν τύχῃ, by or with good fortune.

2. proper name, masculine god of fortune (Arabic WeSkizzen iii. 171; גד‎ named often in Phoenician & Aramaic inscriptions, & found in Phoenician & Aramaic proper name, BaeRel 76 f. NöZMG 1888, 479; see especially SiegfJPTh 1875, 356 ff.) — with לְ‎ + article לַגַּד‎ Isa 65:11 compare Che.
