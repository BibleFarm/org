H41. Abinadab

אֲבִינָדָב‎ proper name, masculine (my father is noble)

1 a man of Gibeah in whose house the ark tarried 1Sam 7:1; 2Sam 6:3 (twice in verse); 6:4; 1Chr 13:7.

2 a son of Jesse 1Sam 16:8; 17:13; 1Kin 4:11 (? perhaps otherwise unknown; Klo proposes בְּ אֲבִינֵר‎).

3 a son of Saul 1Sam 31:2; 1Chr 8:33; 9:39; 10:2.
