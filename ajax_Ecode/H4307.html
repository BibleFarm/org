H4307. mattara

or mattarah מַטָּרָה‎ and (Lam 3:12) מַטָּרָא‎ noun feminine 1. guard, ward, prison Neh 3:25.

2 target, mark (as kept in the eye, watched, compare נָצַר‎ Prov 23:26, and σκόπος (watcher, mark) from σκέπτομαι; perhaps Nabataean [רתא‎]מט‎ Lzb323); — always absolute: —

1 guard, ward, prison, usually הַמּ ׳חֲצַר‎ court of gaurd (see I. ׳חָצֵ‎) Jer 32:2, 8, 12; 33:1; 37:21 (twice in verse); 38:6, 13, 28; 39:14-15, הַמּ ׳שַׁעַר‎ Neh 12:39.

2 target, mark Job 16:12 (figurative of ׳י‎s chastisement), לַחֵץ כַּמַּטָּרָא‎ Lam 3:12 (simile id.); literally only 1Sam 20:20.
