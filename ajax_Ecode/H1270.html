בַּרְזֶל‎ noun masculineDeut 19:5 iron (with ל‎ afform.; from piercing ? Late Hebrew id., Aramaic id., מַּדְזֶל‎, , Phoenician ברזל‎, Assyrian parzillu COTGloss, (Arabic fetter is loan-word)) — always absolute ׳ב‎ Gen 4:22 74t. (בַּרְזֶל֑‎ 4:22 etc.); —

1 iron, literally:

a. iron-ore, stone containing iron, Deut 8:9; Job 28:2;

b. as raw material (to be worked) Gen 4:22; 1Chr 22:3, 14, 16; 29:2 (twice in verse); 29:7; 2Chr 2:6; 2:13; 24:12; Isa 44:12;

Cyrus Isa 45:2; compare 1Sam 17:7 & Isa 60:17 (twice in verse) (figurative)

2 tool of iron Deut 27:5; Josh 8:31; Prov 27:17 (twice in verse); Eccl 10:10; Isa 10:34 (metaphor); head of an axe Deut 19:5; 2Kin 6:5-6, so weapon 2Sam 23:7; Job 41:19; compare also Num 31:22; Josh 22:8 where iron as spoil of war.

3 iron in figure of unwatered earth Deut 28:23; of Egyptian bondage, הַבּ ׳כּוּר‎ 4:20; 1Kin 8:51; Jer 11:4; of oppression ב ׳עֹל‎ Deut 28:48; Jer 28:14 compare 28:13; of strength 15:12 (twice in verse); compare ׳ב מִנְעָלֶיךָ‎ Deut 33:25 & Micah 4:13 ב ׳קֶרֶן‎; of prophet, firm through Yahweh's might, ׳ב עַמּוּד‎ Jer 1:18; of distress, ב ׳בְּרִיחֵי‎ Psa 107:16 compare 107:10 of judgments of ׳י‎, & 105:18; ב ׳שֵׁבֶט‎ 2:9; of evil-doers, Jer 6:28; Ezek 22:18, 20; simile of scorching sky Lev 26:19; ב ׳מְטִיל‎ of bones of hippopotamus Job 40:18; ב ׳גִּיד‎ of obstinate neck of Israel Isa 48:4.
