H6238. ashar

[עָשַׁר‎] verb be or become rich (Late Hebrew in derived species, and derivatives; Aramaic עֲתַר‎, ; compare Arabic abound with herbage, abundance, of herbage, goods, etc.); —

Qal Perfect1singular עָשַׁרְתִּי‎ Hosea 12:9 I have become rich; Imperfect3masculine singular יֶעְשַׁר‎ Job 15:29 he shall not be rich. — 1Kin 22:49 read עָשָׂה‎
