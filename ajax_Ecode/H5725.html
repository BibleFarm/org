H5725. Adullam

עֲדֻלָּם‎ proper name, of a location old Canaanite city, ᵐ5‎ Οδολλαμ, with king Josh 12:15 (D), in the שְׁפֵלָה‎ 15:35 (P; ᵐ5‎L Αδαλαμ ), compare Micah 1:15; Neh 11:30; (re-)built by Rehod. according to 2Chr 11:17 (Οδολαμ); ׳ע מְעָרַת‎ (read מְצָדַת מְצֻדַת,‎, see מְעָרָה‎ below I. ערר‎) 1Sam 22:1; 2Sam 23:13; 1Chr 11:15; site probably I`d-el-Mîye (`Aid el-Ma), approximately 13 miles west-southwest from Bethlehem, see GASmGeogr.229 BuhlGeogr.193 and references.
