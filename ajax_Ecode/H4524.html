H4524. mesab

or mesibbim or mesibboth מֵסַב‎ noun [masculine] that which surrounds, or is round; —

1. a. plural surrounding places, ׳יְרוּשׁ מְסִבֵּי‎ 2Kin 23:5 places round about Jerusalem.

b. as adverb מֵסַב‎ round about 1Kin 6:29; feminine plural מְסִבּוֺת‎ on all sides, in all directions, Job 37:12 (of cloud Di < of lightning Bu Du).

2 suffix מְסִבּוֺ‎ Song 1:12 dubious: round table Ew De; cushion, divan Bae Kau; compare on all these Bu. — מְסִבָּ֑י‎ Psa 140:10 see סבב‎
