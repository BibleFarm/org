מַרְמֵּה מַרְמֵּא,‎ noun masculineEccl 10:4 healing, cure, health (usually figurative); — absolute מַרְמֵּא‎ Jer 14:19 +, מַרְמֵּה‎ 8:15; construct מַרְמֵּא‎ Prov 15:4; —

1 healing, cure, of national woes, Jer 8:15; 14:19b (|| שָׁלוֺם‎), 36:6 (+ אֲרֻכָה‎); especially מ לָנוּ ׳אֵין‎ 14:19a there is no cure for us, מ לְאֵין ׳עַד‎2Chr 36:16.

3 literally, of disease, מ ׳לְאֵין‎2Chr 21:18.
