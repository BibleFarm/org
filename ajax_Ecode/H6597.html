H6597. pithom

מִּתְאֹם‎, once Psa 64:8 (see Baer) מִּתְאוֺם‎ substantive suddenness, usually as

adverb accusative suddenly (from מֶּתַע‎, with the term. ם‎- (compare שִׁלְשֹׁם‎), and with ע‎ weakened to א‎: Sta§ 295 Köii. 255 f. BaNB § 216 b); — Num 12:4 י ׳ויאמר ׳פ אלטֿשׁה‎, Josh 10:9; 11:7; especially of calamity, invasion, etc., coming suddenly, Isa 47:11; 48:3; Jer 4:20 ׳בּ אֹהָלי שֻׁדְּדוּ‎, 6:26; 15:8; 18:22; 51:8; Psalms ׳פ יִירָ֑אוּ וְלאֹ יֹרֻהוּ‎, 51:8; Job 5:3; 9:23; Eccl 9:12; Prov 6:15; 24:22; also 7:22; Mal 3:1; thrice with מֶּתַע‎ (q. v.). As a Genitive Prov 3:25 פ מִמַּהַר ׳אלתֿירא‎ of the terror of suddenness, i.e. the sudden terror, Job 22:10; and with בְּ‎, in suddenness, 2Chr 29:36 ׳בפ הדבר היה‎.
