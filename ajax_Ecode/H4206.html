H4206. maziyach

מֵ֫זַח‎ noun masculine girdle (according to Bondi11 WMMAs.u.Eur.104 loan-word from Egypt mdµ, compare LagGGN 1889, 310); — absolute ׳מ‎: — in simile of curse enfolding the wicked Psa 109:19 (|| בֶּגֶד‎); figurative = restraint Isa 23:10 (but text dubious).

[מְזִיחַ‎] noun masculine id. (of like origin): — only construct ׳מ רִמָּה אֲפִיקִים‎ Job 12:21 loosen the girdle of mighty; i.e. weaken them, make them defenceless, by ungirding.
