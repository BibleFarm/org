H902. Bigvay

בִּגְוַי‎ proper name, masculine (compare Sanskrit bhagavân, happy) ?)

1 a companion of Zerubbabel Ezra 2:2 = Neh 7:7; perhaps = head of a family of returning exiles Ezra 2:14 (בִּגְוָ֑י‎) = Neh 7:19, compare Ezra 8:14.

2 a chief of the people in Nehemiah's time Neh 10:17; compare SmListen 13.
