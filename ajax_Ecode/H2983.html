H2983. Yebusi

יְבֻסִי יְבוּסִי,‎ 1. adjective, of a people Jebusite, Jebusites, singular הַיְבֻסִי אורנה‎ 2Sam 24:16, 18 compare 1Chr 21:15, 18, 28; 2Chr 3:1; as substantive a Jebusite Zech 9:7; usually with article ׳הַי‎ collective the Jebusites, in historical statement Gen 10:16 (J) = 1Chr 1:14; Num 13:29 (E) Josh 9:1; 11:3; 12:8; 24:11 (all D) Judg 3:5; Neh 9:8, all in list of Canaanitish peoples; so also 1Kin 9:20 2Chr 8:7, where remnant of these peoples referred to; also Ezra 9:1, which seems to shew that this list had become a standing expression for early inhabitants; compare further in promises (J E D) Gen 15:21; Exod 3:8, 17; 13:5; 23:23; 33:2; 34:11; Deut 7:1; 20:17; Josh 3:10; specifically defined as inhabitants of Jebus-Jerusalem 15:63 (twice in verse) (J) Judg 1:21 (twice in verse); 2Sam 5:6, 8 (on which compare Dr) 1Chr 11:4 (called also הָאָרֶץ יוֺשֵׁב‎ 2Sam 5:6 & הָאָרֶץ ישְׁבֵי‎ 1Chr 11:4); see also הַזּאֹת הַיְבוּסִי עִיר‎ Judg 19:11; whence

2 Jerusalem, Hexateuch only P, Josh 15:8 יְרוּשָׁלָ֑םִ הִיא מִנֶּגֶב היבוסי אֶלכֶּֿתֶף בֶןהִֿנֹּם גֵּי‎, compare 18:16, 28 יְרוּשָׁלַםִ הִיא וְהַיְבוּסִי‎. — compare כנען‎.
