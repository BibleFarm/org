1. a. as adjective joyful, shewing joy, 1Kin 1:45; Est 5:9 (+ לֵב טוֺב‎), 5:14; שְׂמֵחָה אֵם‎ Psa 113:9; שׂ ׳לֵב‎ Prov 15:13; 17:22; at feast of tabernacle Deut 16:15, in thankfulness to ׳י‎ Psa 126:3 (both predicate with הָיָה‎), 1Kin 8:66 (+ לֵב טוֺבֵי‎) = 2Chr 7:10.
