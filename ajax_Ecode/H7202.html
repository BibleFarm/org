H7202. raeh

[רָאֶה‎] adjective seeing, assumed by Thes and others to explain construct עָנְיִי וּרְאֵה‎ Job 10:15, but improbable; Masoretes apparently intended Imperative (unsuitable); Ew and others וְרֹאֵה‎; Di Bu Du (after Lag Gei) עֹ֑נִי רְוֵה‎ drenched (i.e. sated) with affliction.
