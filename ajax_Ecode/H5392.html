H5392. neshek

נֶ֫שֶׁךְ‎ noun [masculine] interest, usury (literally something bitten off); — ׳נ‎ absolute Exod 22:24 +; construct Deut 23:20; — with עַלֿ שִׂים‎ Exod 22:24 (E); with מִן לָקַח‎ Lev 25:36, מִן הֵשִׁיב‎ Ezek 18:17, לָקַח‎ 22:12 (no preposition), בְּנֶשֶׁךְ‎ Lev 25:37 at interest, so Psa 15:5; Ezek 18:8, 13; ׳בְּנ‎ Prov 28:8 by usury; as accusative of congnate meaning with verb Deut 23:20 (3 t. in verse).
