גֹּ֫מֶד‎ noun masculine cubit (from elbow to knuckles of clenched (contracted) hand; Aq on Ezek 27:11, Ra and others; Greek πυγμή; Late Hebrew גּוֺמֶד‎ cubit; so Aramaic גַּרְמִידָא‎ ChWB1. 155, (lexicons)) — Judg 3:16 of Ehud's sword ׳ג אָרְכָּהּ‎ (see GFM).
