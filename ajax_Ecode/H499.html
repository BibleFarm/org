H499. Elazar

אֶלְעָזָר‎ proper name, masculine (God has helped, compare אֱלִיעֶזֶר‎ above)

a. Eleazar the priest Exod 6:23 + (50 t. in Hexateuch) Judg 20:28; 1 Chronicles 5:29, 30; 1Chr 6:35; 9:20; 24:1-2, 3, 4 (twice in verse); 24:5-6, Ezra 7..

b. son of Abinadab 1Sam 7:1.

c. one of David's heroes 2Sam 23:9; 1Chr 11:12; insert also 27:4 compare DrSm 280.

d. a Levite 1Chr 23:21-22, 24:28.

e. priest of the time of Ezra Ezra 8:33; Neh 12:42. feminine one of the line of Parosh Ezra 10:25.
