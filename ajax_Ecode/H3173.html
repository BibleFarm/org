H3173. yachid

יָחִיד‎ adjective and substantive only, only one, solitary (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic יְחִידַי יָחִיד,‎, , , id.; Arabic solitary) —

1 only one, especially of an only son, Gen 22:2, 12, 16 יְחִידְךָ את בנך את‎ thy son, thine only one, יָחִיד אֵבֶל‎ Amos 8:10; Jer 6:26 mourning for an only son, היחיד על כמספד‎ Zech 12:10; Prov 4:3 אמי לפני וְיָחִיד רַךְ‎; so feminine יְחִידָה‎ Judg 11:34.

2 feminine יְחִידָה‎ as substantive Psa 22:21; 35:17 יְחִידָתִי‎ my only one, poetic for my life, as the one unique and priceless possession which can never be replaced (in each || נַפְשִׁי‎).

3 solitary, Psa 25:16 אני ועני יחיד כי‎; 68:7 בַּיְתָה יְחִידִים מוֺשִׁיב‎ causing solitary, isolated ones (i.e. friendless wanderers or exiles; || אֲסִירִים‎) to dwell at home (Lag Ch and others מֵשִׁיב‎ bringing back home).
