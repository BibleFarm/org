II. זֶ֫רַח‎ proper name, masculine (on form compare LgBN 77, and Anm.) —

1 ᵐ5‎ Ζαρα, son of Judah and Tamar Gen 38:30; 46:12 (both J), 1Chr 2:4 (all זָ֑רַח‎), 2:6; as head of a family Num 26:20; Josh 7:1 (both Psalms), 7:18; 7:24 (JE), 22:20 (P) 1Chr 9:6 (ᵐ5‎L Ζηρα), Neh 11:24 (compare SmListen 7).

2 Edomites:

a. ᵐ5‎ Ζαρε Gen 36:13, 17 (both P) = 1Chr 1:37.

b. ᵐ5‎ Ζαρα Gen 36:33 (P) = 1Chr 1:44 (b = a ?).

3 ᵐ5‎L Ζαρα, Levites:

a. (variant reading Ιααρα) 1Chr 6:6.

b. (variant reading Ζααραι) 1Chr 6:26.

4 head of a Simeonite family, ᵐ5‎ Ζαρα) Num 26:13 (P), 1Chr 4:24 (variant reading Ζαρες) (= ᵐ5 צֹחַר‎ Σααρ Gen 46:10; Exod 6:15, compare Di Num 26:13).

5 a Cushite, ᵐ5‎ Ζαρε, foe of king Asa 2Chr 14:8.
