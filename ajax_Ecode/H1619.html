H1619. Gareb

גָּרֵב‎ 1. proper name, masculine (compare Palmyrene proper name, masculine גריבא‎ Vog141) one of David's heroes 2Sam 23:38 = 1Chr 11:40.

2. proper name, of a location (compare Sabean proper name, of a location גרבם‎ HalNo. 379, 382) only in גָּרֵב גִּבְעַת‎ Jer 31:39 hill of Gareb, apparently close to Jerusalem on the southwest compare Gf; see also Hi Che.
