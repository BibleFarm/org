H1865. deror

I. דְּרוֺר‎ noun [masculine] a flowing; free run, liberty; —

1 מָרדְּֿרוֺר‎ Exod 30:23 (P) i.e. myrrh of flowing, fine-flowing myrrh compare מֹר‎ & Di.

2 with קרא‎ proclaim liberty, לְכָליֿשְׁבֶ֑יהָ בָּאָרֶץ דְּרוֺר וּקְרָאתֶם‎ Lev 25:10 (H) in Jubilee year, compare Isa 61:1 (|| מְּקַחקֿוֺחַ‎); Jer 34:8, 15, 17 all reference to liberty of Sabbatical year; 34:17 (iron.) lo ! I proclaim to you liberty unto the sword, unto pestilence & unto famine, i.e. liberty to be destroyed by sword, pestilence and famine, (a judgment of Yahweh); ׳שְׁנַתהַֿדּ‎ Ezek 46:17 i.e. year of Jubilee (cf Lev 25:10).
