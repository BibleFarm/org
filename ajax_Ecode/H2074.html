H2074. Zebulun

זְלֻבוּן זְבוּלֻן,‎ proper name, masculine Zebulun (on explanation of name given in E and J compare זבל זבד,‎); — זְבוּלֻן‎ Gen 49:13 25t.; זְבֻלוּן‎ Judg 5:18 17t.; זְבוּלוּן‎ 1:30; —

1 sixth son of Jacob and Leah Gen 30:20; 35:23; 46:14; 49:13; Exod 1:3; 1Chr 2:1.

2 the tribe of Zebulun Num 1:9; Deut 27:13; 33:18 (twice in verse); Judg 1:30; 4:10; 5:14, 18; Ezek 48:26; 1Chr 12:34; 1 Chronicles 12:41; 27:19; 2Chr 30:11, 18; compare ז ׳שָׂרֵי‎ Psa 68:28; ז ׳שַׁעַר‎ Ezek 48:33; ז ׳בְּנֵי‎ Num 1:30; 2:7; 7:24; 26:26; Josh 19:10, 16; Judg 4:6; ז ׳מַטֵּה‎ Num 1:31; 2:7; 13:10; ז בְּנֵי ׳מַטֵּה‎ 10:16; 34:25.

3 territory of the tribe ז ׳אֶרֶץ‎ Judg 12:12, ז ׳אַרְצָה‎ Isaiah 8:23, ז ׳גְּבוּל‎ Ezek 48:27; also ׳ז‎ alone Josh 19:27, 34; 2Chr 30:10, ז ׳מַטֵּה‎ Josh 21:7, 34; 1Chr 6:48; 6:62.
