H718. aru

אֲרוּ‎ interjection lo (of uncertain derivation: only Biblical Aramaic (? Nabataean, see CISii. 186); אֲלוּ‎, and Late Hebrew הֲרֵי‎ see! Here! Ab Dan 3:4 and elsewhere, are perhaps allied), וַאֲרוּ‎ 7:2 וַאֲרוּ ֗֗֗ הֲוֵית חָוֵי‎, 7:5; 7:6; 7:7; 7:13, always in description of a vision (compare אֲלוּ‎).
