H5959. almah

עַלְמָה‎ noun feminine young woman (ripe sexually; maid or newly married); — ׳ע‎ Gen 24:43 (J), Exod 2:8 (E), Prov 30:19; Isa 7:14; plural עֲלָמוֺת‎ Psa 68:26; Song 1:3; 6:8; עַלעֲֿלָמוֺת‎ to (the voice of) young women, either literally, or of soprano or falsetto of boys: 1Chr 15:20; Psa 9:1 (read לַבֵּן עַלעֲֿלָמוֺת‎ [for לַבֵּן עַלמֿוּת‎], 'voce virginea a pueris decantandum,' Thes), 46:1; Psalm 48:15 (read עַלעֲֿלָמוֺת‎ [for עַלמֿוּת‎]; translated probably to 49:1).
