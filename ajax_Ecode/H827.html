H827. ashpah

אַשְׁמָּה‎ noun feminine quiver for arrows (Assyrian išpatu DlK 29) ׳א‎ literal as part of warlike equipment Job 39:23; Isa 22:6; figurative in simile of sons of one's youth as arrows (weapons which the father may wield) Psa 127:5 מֵהֶם אֶתאַֿשְׁמָּתוֺ מִלֵּא אֲשֶׁר הַגֶּבֶר אַשְׁרֵי‎ the happiness of the man that hath filled his quiver with them ! of Yahweh's quiver, in metaphor of prophet as arrow of ׳י‎ Isa 49:2 הִסְתִּירָ֑נִי בְּאַשְׁמָּתוֺ בָּרוּר לְחַץ וַיְשִׂימֵנִי‎ and he made me into a polished arrow, in his quiver he hid me; also of quiver in which ׳י‎ has his arrows of chastisement, אַשְׁמָּתוֺ בְּנֵי‎, i.e. his arrows Lam 3:13 (|| חֵץ‎ 3:12).
