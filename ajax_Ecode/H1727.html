[דּוּב‎] verb pine away (compare דאבון דאב,‎) —

Hiph`il Participle feminine plural נפשׁ מְדִיבֹת‎ Lev 26:16 (diseases) causing to pine away the soul (life), compare Di; in 1Sam 2:33 read probably Infinitive נפשׁך את לְהָדִיב‎ (for לַאֲדִיב ᵑ0‎) so Dr; transitive to cause thy (al. his) soul to pine away (compare VB).
