[Heb] עֻמָּה‎ ETCBC: עֻמָּה‎ (subs｜side) OSHL: p.dj.ae TWOT: 1640f GK: H6645
Derivation: from עָמַם‎;
Strong's: conjunction, i.e. society; mostly adverb or preposition (with prepositional prefix), near, beside, along with
KJV: (over) against, at, beside, hard by, in points.

H5980. ummah

I. [עֻמָּה‎] noun feminine juxtaposition, but only in stative construct with force a

preposition close by, side by side with, parallel to, agreeing with, corresponding to; — stative with עֻמַּת‎, except Eccl 5:15 always with לְ‎, suffix לְעֻמָּתוֺ‎, once plural construct לְעֻמּוֺת‎ Ezek 45:7; —

a. close by, side by side with: Exod 25:27 (37:14), 28:27 (39:20), Lev 3:9 יְסִירֶנָּה הֻעָצֶה לְעֻמַּת‎ shall take it away close by the backbone; especially of what is parallel, Ezek 42:7 the wall alongside of the chambers; of the contiguous portions in Ezekiel's division of the land, alongside of each other, 45:6-7, 48:13, 18 (twice in verse); 48:21; of movement in parallel lines, 2Sam 16:13 Shimei was going along לְעֻמָּתוֺ‎ parallel with him, 16:13; Ezek 1:20 לְעֻמָּתָם יִנָָּֽשְׂאוּ וְהָאוֺפַנִּים‎, 1:21; 3:13; 10:19; 11:22; 3:8 מְּנֵיהֶם לְעֻמַּת חֲזָקִים פָנֶיךָ נָתַתִּי‎ by the side of their face (which, as antagonism is implied, = against, RV), 3:8.

b. agreeing with, corresponding to (a common result of juxtaposition), Exod 38:18 a screen five cubits high החצר קלעי לעמת‎ agreeing with (RV answerable unto) the hangings of the court (in height), Ezek 40:18; probably also 1Chr 26:16; Neh 12:24 ל ׳מִשְׁמָר מִשְׁמָר‎ ward corresponding to ward.

c. correspondingly to, 1Chr 24:31 they also cast lots אֲחֵיהֶם לְעֻמַּת‎ correspondingly to their brethren, vb the head, correspondinly to his younger brother, 26:12 to these . . . belonged charges correspondingly to (in common with) their brethren. **Eccl 7:14 side by side with, as well as.

d. before a sentence (Ges§ 155n), 1Chr 25:8 (strangely) כַּגָּדוֺל כַּקָּטֹן לְעֻמַּת‎ (see Ke) correspondingly to (the principle) of as the small so the great; Eccl 5:15 שֶׁכָּא כָּלעֻֿמַּת‎ quite exactly as he came, so shall he go (but LambertRŠJ xxxi. 47, RahlfsTh. Lt. -z. 1896, 587 כִּלְעֻמַּת‎).

e. מִלְּעֻמַּת‎ (see מִן‎

1c, 9 a) 1Kin 7:20 close beside.
