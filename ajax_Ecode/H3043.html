H3043. Yediael

יְדִיעֲאֵל‎ proper name, masculine (Palmyrene ידיבעל‎ DHM in MV10, 983 compare Sabean proper name with ידע‎ DHMZMG 1875, 612) —

1 a Benjamite 1Chr 7:6, 10, 11, ᵐ5‎ Αδειηλ, Ιεδιηλ, etc.

2 one of David's heroes according to 1Chr 11:45 apparently = the Manassite captain of 12:21 (Baer; van d. H v.12:20), ᵐ5‎ Ελθειηλ, Ιεδιηλ.

3 a Korahite porter 1Chr 26:2 ᵐ5‎ Ιδερηλ, Ιεδιηλ.
