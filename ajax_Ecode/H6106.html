H6106. etsem

I. עֶ֫צֶם‎125 noun femininePsa 35:10 (with pronoun masculine Ezek 37:5-6, etc., compare AlbrZAW xvi (1896). 73) bone, substance, self; — absolute ׳י‎ Gen 2:23 +; עָ֑צֶם‎; Prov 15:30; 16:24; construct עֶצֶם‎ Exod 24:10 +; suffix עַצְמִי‎ Job 19:20 +, etc.; plural עֲצָמוֺת‎ 2Kin 23:16 +; construct עַצְמוֺת‎ Josh 24:22 +; suffix עַצְמֹתַי‎ Job 4:14 +, etc.; also עֲצָמִים‎ Ezek 24:4 +, suffix עֲצָמַי‎ Gen 2:23 +, etc.; —

1 bone:

a. of living person Micah 3:2-3,(hyperbole), Job 10:11; 19:20; Lam 4:8; Psa 102:6; Eccl 11:5 +; figurative of close relationship מֵעֲצָמַי עֶצָם‎ Gen 2:23, compare 29:14 (both J), Judg 9:2; 2Sam 5:1 = 1Chr 11:1; 2Sam 19:13; 19:14 (all + בָּשָׂר‎).

b. ׳ע‎ + בָּשָׂר‎ = body, Job 2:5, plural = bodily frame Jer 20:9 (simile), limbs, members, לַעֲצָמֶיהָ‎ Judg 9:29 i.e. limb by limb (GFM); as seat of vigour Job 20:11, etc.

c. (poetry) as seat of disease and pain Job 30:17, 30 and (especially of personified Israel) Psa 22:15; 31:11; 102:4; Lam 1:13; Hab 3:16 +.

d. plural as representing entire person = one's whole being, (especially of personified Israel) Psa 6:3 (|| נֶפֶשׁ‎ 6:4), 35:10 (|| נֶפֶשׁ‎ 35:9), Isa 66:14; of individual (including physical and moral) Prov 3:8; 14:30; 15:30; 16:24.

e. singular = external body Lam 4:7 Ew Th and others, but dubious; Bi Bu bones (= branches) of coral (מְּנִינִים‎); Löhr עוֺרָם‎ their skin.

f. especially of dead Num 19:16, 18 (P), Ezek 37:1 +; often (plural) = remains Amos 6:10; Gen 50:25; Exod 13:19 (twice in verse); Josh 24:32 (all E) +.

2 bone of animal Exod 12:46; Num 9:12 (both P); Ezek 24:4-5, (symbolic); of (living) hippopotamus Job 40:18.

3 singular substance, self (compare גֶּרֶם‎

3): ׳כְּע הַשָּׁמַיִם‎ Exod 24:10 (JE), like the substance of the sky = the sky itself; ׳בְּע תֻּמּוֺ‎ Job 21:23 i.e. full prosperity; especially הַזֶּה הַיּוֺם ׳אֶתעֿ‎ Ezek 24:2 this selfsame day, ׳בְּע ׳וגו‎ 24:2; 40:1; Gen 7:13; 17:23, 26; Exod 12:17, 41, 51; Lev 23:21, 28, 29, 30; Deut 32:48; Josh 5:11 (all P), ׳עַדעֿ ׳וגו‎ Ezek 2:3; Lev 23:14 (P), Josh 10:27 (R). — Ezek 24:5a read הָעֵצִים‎ (Bö Ew and others); 24:10 strike out הָעֲצָמוֺת‎ (Co Toy); Job 7:15 read מֵעַצְּבוֺתָ֑י‎ (Reiske Me Bu Du and others); Psa 53:6 read perhaps מֵעֲצַת‎ (compare || 14:6 Bae; see Hup Che and others); 69:5 read מֵעַצְמוֺתַי‎ (for מַצְמִיתַי ᵑ0‎), ᵑ6‎ Ol Che Bae and others
