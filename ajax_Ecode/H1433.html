H1433. godel

גֹּ֫דֶל‎ noun masculine greatness — Deut 32:3 5t.; suffix גָּדְלוֺ‎ 5:21 5t.; גֻּדְלוֺ‎ Psa 150:2; —

1 greatness, magnitude, tree Ezek 31:7, arm of God Psa 79:11; גְּדֹל‎ Exod 15:16, mercy of God Num 14:19.

2 magnificence,

a. king Ezek 31:2, 18;

b. God Deut 3:24; 5:21; 9:26; 11:2; 32:3; Psa 150:2.

3 in a bad sense, לֵבָב גֹּדֶל‎ = pride, insolence of heart Isa 9:8; 10:12.
