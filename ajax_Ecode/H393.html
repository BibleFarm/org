[Heb] אַכְזָר‎ ETCBC: אַכְזָר‎ (adjv｜cruel) OSHL: k.bb.ab TWOT: 971a GK: H425 Greek: ἀνελεήμων
Derivation: from an unused root (apparently meaning to act harshly);
Strong's: violent; by implication deadly; also (in a good sense) brave
KJV: cruel, fierce.

H393. akzar

אַכְזָר‎ adjective cruel, fierce, of poison (ראֹשׁ‎) Deut 32:33; elsewhere of men, fierce Job 41:2; as substantive = a cruel one 30:21 (of God), Lam 4:3.
