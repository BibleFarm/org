H1364. gaboah

גָּבֹהַּ‎ adjective high, exalted — 1Sam 9:2 15t.; גָּבוֺהַּ‎ Psa 138:6; construct גְּבֹהַּ‎ 1Sam 16:7; גְּבַהּ‎ (Ew§ 213 d) Psa 101:5 3t.; plural גְּבֹהִים‎ Eccl 5:7 5t.; feminine גְּבֹהָה‎ Deut 3:5-6t.; plural גְּבֹהוֺת‎ Dan 8:3 2t.; גְּבֹהֹת‎ Deut 28:52; —

1 high, lofty, tall, e.g. tree Ezek 17:24, tower Isa 2:15; Zeph 1:16, mountain Gen 7:19; Psa 104:18; Isa 40:9; 57:7; Ezek 17:22; 40:2; compare phrases גבהה גבעה כל על‎ upon every high hill 1Kin 14:23; 2Kin 17:10; Jer 2:20; הגבהות גבעות על‎ 17:2; גָּבֹהַּ עַלכָּֿלהַֿר‎ Isa 30:25; Jer 3:6; man 1Sam 9:2; tree קוֺמָה גְּבַהּ‎ Ezek 31:3; horns Dan 8:3; walls Deut 3:5; 28:52; gallows Est 5:14; 7:9; gate Jer 51:58; altar Ezek 41:22; high things Job 41:26; Eccl 12:5.

2 exalted in station Ezek 21:31; עֲלֵיהֶם וּגְבֹהִים שֹׁמֵר גָּבֹהַּ מֵעַל גָּבֹהַּ כִּי‎ for high one above high one is watching, & the Most High over them Eccl 5:7 so Ew Zö and others, but Vrss De Now and others higher (earthly), potentates over them.

3 haughty Psa 138:6; Isa 5:15; 10:33; 1Sam 2:3; גְּבַהּעֵֿינַיִם‎ Psa 101:5; ׳ג לֵב‎ Prov 16:5; ׳ג רוּחַ‎ Eccl 7:8.

4. noun [masculine] loftiness, קוֺמַתוֺ גִּבֹהַּ‎ 1Sam 16:7; compare גָּדוֺל‎ 10, p. 153.
