H3923. Lachish

לָכִישׁ‎ proper name, of a location — so Josh 10:3 +; with ה‎ locative לֶכִ֫ישָׁה‎ 10:31 5t.; — Canaanitish (Amorite) city, with king, 10:3, 5, 23 (all J E), 10:31; 12:11 (both D); (= Tel el-Amarna Lakiša, Lakisi WklTA Tablets, Nos. 217, 218, 180, 181); captured by 10:32-33, 34, 35 (all D); reckoned to Judah Micah 1:13; Jer 34:7; Josh 15:39 (P), compare 2Kin 14:19 (twice in verse) = 2Chr 25:27 (twice in verse), 11:9; Neh 11:30; base of Assyrian operations 2Kin 18:14, compare 2Chr 32:9, also 2Kin 18:17; 19:8 = Isa 36:2; 37:8 ( = Assyrian Lakisi SchrCOT 2Kin 18:13); probably = modern Tell el-Hesy, approximately 33 miles southwest from Jerusalem FJBMound of Many Cities, 1893 BuhlGeogr. § 103 GASmGeogr. 234; > Umm el-Lâkis vd VeldeMem. 320 GuérinJudeé; ii. 299 ff.; Um Lâkis opposed to by RobBR ii. 47. — ᵐ5‎ Λαχεις.
