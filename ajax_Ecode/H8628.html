[Heb] תָּקַע‎, תֹּוקְעִים‎ ETCBC: תקע‎ (verb｜blow), תֹּוקְעִים‎ (subs｜handshakes) OSHL: w.bp.aa TWOT: 2541 GK: H9364, H9546 Greek: βάλλω, ἐμβάλλω, ἦχος, ἵστημι, παραδίδωμι, πήγνυμι, σαλπίζω, σημαίνω, στηρίζω, συνδέω, τίθημι, φωνέω
Derivation: a primitive root;
Strong's: to clatter, i.e. slap (the hands together), clang (an instrument); by analogy, to drive (a nail or tent-pin, a dart, etc.); by implication, to become bondsman by handclasping)
KJV: blow (a trumpet), cast, clap, fasten, pitch (tent), smite, sound, strike, [idiom] suretiship, thrust.

H8628. taqa

תָּקַע‎ verb thrust, clap, give a blow, blast (Late Hebrew id.; Jewish-Aramaic תְּקַע‎ strike, blow horn; Ethiopic blow trumpet); —

Qal Perfect3masculine singular ׳ת‎ Gen 31:25 +, 1 singular וְתָקַעְתִּ֫י‎ Jer 7:18, etc.; Imperfect3masculine singular תִּקְעוּ‎ Jeremiah 3:37 +, suffix וַיִּתְקָעֵהוּ‎ Exod 10:19, etc.; Imperative masculine plural תִּקְעוּ‎ Hosea 5:8 +; Infinitive absolute תָּקוֺעַ‎ Josh 6:9, 13; construct לִתְקוֺעַ‎ Judg 7:12, ׳כִּת‎ Isa 18:3; Participle active תִּקֵעַ‎ Prov 17:18 +, ect.; passive feminine תְּקוּעָה‎ Isa 22:25; —

1 thrust, drive, weapon (accusative) into (ב‎) a person Judg 3:21; 4:21; 2Sam 18:14, peg into (ב‎) something Isa 22:23, 25 (compare Judg 4:21); hence pitch a tent (accusative), i.e. drive its pegs: Gen 31:25 a Jer 6:3 ( + עַל‎ against, in figure), accusative omitted Gen 31:25b; thrust, drive, beat (strands of hair together) בַּיָּחֵד‎ Judg 16:4, insert a; so 16:13 (so ᵐ5‎; compare GFMComm. and PAOS Oct, 1889, 176 ff.); thrust, drive locusts seaward (ה‎ locative), Exod 10:19 (J); compare ׳ת בְּ‎ 1Sam 31:10 of fastening bodies to wall, and similarly 1Chr 10:10 (but read in both הוֺקִיעוּ‎ √ יקע‎ Lag We Dr and modern).

2 give a blast, blow (German 'stossen'), with ב‎ of instrument (usually horn or clarion) Josh 6:4, 8, 9, 13 (twice in verse); 6:16, 20 (JE), Judg 3:27; 6:34; 7:18 (twice in verse); 7:19-20, 14t., + Num 10:3-4, 8, 10 (P; see חֲצֹצְרָה‎), also Ezek 7:14 (see תָּקוֺעַ‎ below); ׳ת‎ alone Judg 7:20; Num 10:7 (P; opposed to חֵרִיעַ‎ sound an alarm), with accusative תְּרוּעָה‎ 10:5; 10:6; 10:6 (P; see חֲצֹצְרָה‎); with accusative of instrument Judg 7:22; Hosea 5:8; Isa 18:3; Jer 4:5; 6:1; 51:27; Joel 2:1, 15; Psa 81:4, + תקעו‎ Josh 6:9 (Kt; Qr תֹּקְעֵי‎).

3 strike, clap hands; in triumph, ׳ת עָלֶיךָ כַּף‎ Nahum 3:19, ׳ת כָ֑ף‎ Psa 47:2; also (late) of gesture ratifying a bargain, specifically pledging oneself to become surety, only Proverbs (all || עַרַב‎): ׳ת כַּמֶּיךָ לַזָּר‎ Prov 17:18; 22:26; absolute 11:15.
