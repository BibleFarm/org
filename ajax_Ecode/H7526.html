H7526. Retsin

רְצִין‎ proper name, masculine 1. king of Aram, Ahaz's time: 2Kin 15:37; 16:5-6, 9; Isa 7:1, 4, 8; 8:6; 9:10; Ρα(α)ς(ς)ων, Ρασειν. **Assyrian has Raƒunnu, hence read perhaps רצון‎, compare WklKAT 3. 135.

2 Ezra 2:48 = Neh 7:50, Ρα(α)σων.
