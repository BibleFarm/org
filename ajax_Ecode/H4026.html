מִגְדָּל‎ (migdâl｜mig-dawl'｜noun｜masculine｜tower)
[Heb] מִגְדָּל‎ ETCBC: מִגְדָּל‎ (subs｜tower) OSHL: c.aq.am TWOT: 315f 315g GK: H4463 Greek: βῆμα, πύργος, Μαγδαλά
Derivation: also (in plural) feminine מִגְדָּלָה‎; from גָּדַל‎;
Strong's: a tower (from its size or height); by analogy, a rostrum; figuratively, a (pyramidal) bed of flowers
KJV: castle, flower, tower. Compare the names following.

H4026. migdal
