H2656. chephets

חֵ֫פֶץ‎ noun masculineProv 3:15 delight, pleasure; — ׳ח‎ Isa 54:12 20t.; suffix חֶפְצְךָ‎ 58:13 etc. + 13 t. suffixes; plural חֲפָצִים‎ Prov 8:11; suffix חֲפָצֶיךָ‎ 3:15; חֲפָצֶ֖ךָ‎ Isa 58:13 —

1 delight ח ׳אבני‎ Isa 54:12 delightful stones; ח ׳אֶרֶץ‎ Mal 3:12 delightsome land; ח ׳דִּבְּרֵי‎ Eccl 12:10; so perhaps also ח ׳בִּגְדֵי‎ Ezek 27:20 garments of delight, i.e. of beauty and luxury (Gr; חֹפֶשׁ ᵑ0‎ q. v.); with בְּ‎ of persons Psa 16:3; Eccl 5:3; Mal 1:10; of things 1Sam 15:22; 18:25; Psa 1:2; Job 21:21; Eccl 12:1; בּוֺ חֵפֶץ אֵין כְּלִי‎ vessel wherein is no pleasure Jer 22:28; 48:38; Hosea 8:8; כִּי לְשַׁדַּי הַחֵפֶץ‎ Job 22:3 is it a pleasure to Shadday that ?

2 desire, longing מח ׳מנע דלים‎ Job 31:16 withhold the poor from (their) desire; חֶפְצָם מְחוֺז‎ the city of their desire Psa 107:30; חפץ כל‎ all (one's) desire 2Sam 23:5; 1 Kings 5:22; 1 Kings 5:23; 1 Kings 5:24; 1Kin 9:11; 10:13 2Chr 9:12; חפצים כל‎ all things to be desired Prov 3:15; 8:11.

3 the good pleasure, will, purpose, of Yahweh Isa 44:28; 46:10; 48:14.

4 that in which one takes delight, his business (late), or matter (very late, compare in Mishna = thing) י ׳חֵפֶץ יִצְלַח בְּיָדוֺ‎ the good pleasure (cause, business) of Yahweh will prosper in his hands Isa 53:10; ח ׳מצא‎ 58:3, 13; חֲפָצֶךָ עֲשׂוֺת‎ doing thy affairs 58:13 (see De on the passage); כַּמֶּיהָ בְּחֵפֶץ‎ in the business of her hands Prov 31:13; לְכָלחֵֿפֶץ עֵתּ‎ time for every matter, affair Eccl 3:1, 17; 8:6; עַלהַֿחֵפֶץ תִּתְּמַהּ אַלֿ‎ marvel not at the matter 5:7.

Note. — חפץ‎ is not used in any of its forms in E D2 H P of the Hexateuch.
