H3645. Kemosh

or Kemish כְּמוֺשׁ‎ proper name, of a divinity Chemosh (כמש‎ MI3. 5. 9. 12. 13. 14. 18. 19. 32. 23 also כמש עשתר‎ Jer 1:17 and proper name, masculine כמשמלך‎ 1:1; Assyrian Kammusunadbi, a king of Moab SchrCOT i, 281; = KAT2, 288; compare further BaeRel 13 f. 238, 256 NöZMG 1888, 471; ᵐ5‎ Ξαμώς); — god of the Moabites to whom Solomon erected a high place 1Kin 11:7, 33; 2Kin 23:13; Jer 48:7 (Kt כמישׁ‎), 48:13. Moab is ׳עַםכֿ‎ Num 21:29 (ode) people of Chemosh, and Moabites his sons and daughters, compare Jer 48:46. He is said to be also the God of the Ammonites Judg 11:25 (probably an error BaeRel 15 GFM).
