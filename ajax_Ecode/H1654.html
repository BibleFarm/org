H1654. Geshem

or Gashmu I. גֶּ֫שֶׁם‎ proper name, masculine an opponent of Nehemiah Neh 2:19; 6:1 (in both called הָעַרְבִּי‎); 6:2; also גַּשְׁמוּ‎ 6:6; וּ‎ as in Nabataean proper name compare Nö in EutNab 73.
