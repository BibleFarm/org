H8435. toledoth

[תּוֺלֵדוֺת‎]39 noun feminine plural generations, especially in Genealogies = account of a man and his descendants; — construct תּוֺלְדוֺת‎ Gen 2:4; Ruth 4:18; תּוֺלְדֹת‎ Gen 5:1 6t.; תֹּלְדוֺת‎ 36:1-2t.; תֹּלְדֹת‎ 25:12; suffix תֹּלְדֹתָיו‎ 1Chr 26:31; תּוֺלְדֹתָם‎ Exod 28:10 16t.; תֹּלְדוֺתָם‎ 1Chr 5:7 5t.; תֹּלְדֹתָם‎ Exod 6:16, 19; —

a. account of men and their descendants Gen 5:1; 6:9; 10:1; 11:10, 27; 25:12, 19; 36:1, 9; 37:2; Num 3:1; Ruth 4:18; 1Chr 1:29; successive Generations (in) of families (מִשְׁמָּחֹת‎) Gen 10:32; compare 25:13, Exod 6:16, 19; 28:10; genealogical divisions, by parentage Num 1:20 (׳ת לְמִשְׁמְּחֹתָם‎) + 11 t. Numbers 1; לתולדוֺתם בְּהִתְיִחֶשׂ לְמִשְׁמְּחֹתָיו וְאֶחָיו‎ 1Chr 5:7; compare 7:9 and probably also 7:2 see Be, 7:4; 8:28; 9:9, 34; 26:31.

b. metaph. וְהָאָרֶץ הַשָּׁמַיִם תּוֺלְדוֺת‎ Gen 2:4 literally begettings of heaven and earth, i.e. account of heaven and earth and that which proceeded from them (compare DrIntr. 5 n.) In Hexateuch always P.
