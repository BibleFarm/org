H3789. kathab

כָּתַב‎223 verb write (Late Hebrew id.; Phoenician כתב‎; Aramaic כְּתַב‎, Nabataean כתב‎, see especially Nö in EutNab. p. 43; thence Arabic Ethiopic book, DiLex. 852 (as loan-words, Frä249 DHMVOJ i (1887), 29); MV Buhl compare Arabic draw or sew together, conjoin (letters), etc., compare FlZMG 1873, 427) —

Qal Perfect ׳כ‎ Josh 8:32 +, כָּתַבְתָּ‎ Jer 36:6 +, suffix וּכְתַבְתָּם‎ consecutive Deut 6:9; 11:20, etc. (Perfect 27 t.); Imperfect יִכְתֹּב‎ Isa 44:5, וַיִּכְתֹּב‎ Exod 24:4 +, etc. (Imperfect 39 t.); Imperative כְּתֹב‎ Isa 8:1 5t., כְּתָבֿ‎ Exod 34:27 2t.; suffix כָּתְבָהּ‎ Isa 30:8, כָּתְבֵם‎ Prov 3:3; 7:3; plural כִּתְבוּ‎ Deut 31:9 3t .; Infinitive absolute כָּתוֺכ‎ Jer 32:44; cstr כְּתוֺב‎ Psalm 87:8, לִכְתֹּב‎ Deut 31:24; Josh 18:8; suffix כָּתְבוֺ‎ Jer 45:1; Participle active כֹּתֵב‎ 36:18, plural כֹּתְבִים‎ 32:12; Neh 10:1; passive (113 t.), כָּתוּב‎ Josh 1:8 +, feminine כְּתוּבָה‎ 2Sam 1:18 +, plural כְּתוּבִים‎ 1Kin 15:7 +, כְּתֻבִים‎ 11:41 +; feminine plural כְּתוּבוֺת‎2Chr 34:24; —

1 write:

a. with accusative, words Exod 34:27, commandments 24:12, etc.; accusative of congnate meaning with verb מכתב‎ 39:30, compare כַּמִּכְתָּב‎ Deut 10:4; with כְּ‎, giving purport of writing, also Est 8:8; but object also a book, סֵפֶר‎ Exod 32:32; Deut 24:1; מְגִלָּה‎ Jer 38:6, compare participle pass, וְאָחוֺר מָּנִים כְּתוּבָה וְהִיא‎ Ezek 2:10 ( of מגלה‎; i.e. written on both sides); a letter, סֵפָר‎ 2Sam 11:14; 2Kin 10:1 4t., אִגֶּרֶת‎2Chr 30:1; a divorce-certificate כְּרִיתֻת סֵפֶר‎ Deut 24:1, etc.; even כְּתֻבִים לֻחֹת‎ tablets inscribed by the finger of God Exod 31:18 compare Deut 9:10; ׳ל ׳כ עֶבְרֵיהֶם מִשְּׁנֵי‎ Exod 32:15 tables inscribed on both sides (compare Ezek 2:10 above) Here belongs (probably) לי יָדוֺ יִכְתֹּב ׳וְזֶה‎ Isa 44:5 and this one shall inscribe his hand, 'Yahweh's', so ᵐ5‎ Hi Kn Che Br Du Di, Ges Ew De, shall sign (with) his hand unto ׳י‎.

b. more often write something on, or in (עַלֿ‎85t., בְ‎44t., אֶלֿ‎3t.) a tablet, roll, book, etc. (object various, as above): — (I) ׳כ עַלֿ‎ followed by סֵפֶר‎ = book Deut 17:18 55t., especially in phrase וגו דברי עלסֿפר ׳כתובים‎ 1Kin 11:41 + often Kings Chronicles; ׳כ עַלמְֿגִּלָּה‎ Jer 36:2; 36:28; 36:32; Jeremiah 38:29; ׳כ עַללֿוּח(ותׅ‎ Exod 34:1 7t., עַלאֶֿבֶן‎ Deut 27:3 2t., עַלגּֿלָּיוֺן‎ Isa 8:1; בַּיִת עַלמְֿזֻזוֺת‎ Deut 6:9; 11:20; עַלמַֿטֶּה‎ on a rod Numbers 17:17; Numbers 17:18; עַלעֵֿץ‎ Ezek 37:16 (twice in verse), עַלצִֿיץ‎ (high priest's plate) Exod 39:30; even (only Chronicles) עַלֿ‎ followed by name of book or its contents: — ׳כ נָתָן עַלדִּֿבְרֵי‎2Chr 9:29; compare 33:19; וגו ׳עַלמִֿדְרַשׁ‎24:27; עַלהַֿקִּינוֺת‎35:25; figurative, ׳כ עַללִֿבָּם‎ Jeremiah 31:83 I will write them upon their heart; compare ׳כ לִבֶּ֑ךָ עַללֿוּחַ‎ Prov 3:3. (2) ׳כ אֶלֿ‎ appears for ׳כ עַלֿ‎ only Jer 36:2 compare Ezek 2:10; Jer 51:60. (3) ׳כ בְּ‎ followed by ספר‎ = book Josh 24:26 (E) + 35 t.; = letter, bill, deed, 1Kin 21:9 5t.; עָלָ֑י כָּתוּב סֵפֶר בִּמְגִלַּת‎ Psa 40:8 it is prescribed to me, על‎ as 2Kin 22:13 (N.B. ׳כ עלֿ‎ not used with סֵפֶר‎ = letter, and ׳כ בְּ‎ not used with אבן לוּחַ,‎ or עֵץ‎).

c. other uses of perp. with ׳כ‎ are: — בְּ‎ instrumental, ׳כ אֱנוֺשׁ בְּחֶרֶט‎ Isa 8:1, ׳כ בַּרְזֶל בְּעֵט‎ Jer 17:1; אֱלֹהִים בְּאֶצְבַּע כְּתֻבִים‎ Deut 9:10; Exod 31:18; ׳כ אֶלֿ‎ unto, of person to whom letter, etc., is addressed 2Sam 11:14; 2Kin 10:6; Est 9:13; עַלֿ‎ substantive for אֶלֿ‎ (late) 2Chr 30:1; Ezra 4:7; Est 8:8; the fuller construction is ׳כ (אֶלֿׅ וישׁלח ספר‎ 2Kin 10:1; Est 8:10; 9:20 +; ׳כ אֶלֿ‎ write down for the benefit or use of some one, Judg 8:14; לְ‎ to or for, Deut 17:18; 24:1 +; note especially תּוֺרָתִי רֻבֵּו֯ אֶכְתָּו֯בלֿוֺ‎ Hosea 8:12, i.e. either, I write (keep writing) for them ever so many (We, רֹב‎, compare Ew and most רִבּוֺ(אׅ‎ a myriad) of my directions, or 'Though I write,' 'Were I to write,' etc.; ׳כ‎ followed by ל‎ with infinitive of purpose 2Chr 32:17; Est 8:5; with מִן‎ in phrase ׳כ פ ׳מִמִּי‎ write from the mouth of any one i.e. from dictation, Jer 36:2, 32; 38:6, 17, 27; 45:1.

2 = write down describe in writing, ׳כ אֶתהָֿאָרֶץ‎ Judg 18:4, 6, 8 (3 t. in verse); 18:9.

3 = register enroll Isa 10:19 (= record the number), 1Chr 24:6; especially passive הַכְּתוּבִים‎ Num 11:26 those enrolled compare Neh 12:22; ׳הַכּ בְּשֵׁמוֺת‎ 1Chr 4:41 those recorded by name; in prediction, עֲרִירִי הַזֶּה ׳כ אֶתהָֿאִישׁ‎ Jer 22:30 register this man as childless; with eschatological reference, לַחַיִּים כָּלהַֿכָּתוּב‎ Isa 4:3 all those enrolled (i.e. appointed) unto life; and, more explicitly, בַּסֵּפֶר כָּתוּב כָּלהַֿנִּמְצָא‎ Dan 12:1; ׳י עַמִּים בִּכְתוֺבּ יִסְמֹּר‎ Psa 87:6 ׳י‎ shall reckon, when he registers (note absence of suffix) the peoples, etc.

4 = decree, מְרֹרוֺת עָלַי תִּכְתֹּב‎ Job 13:26 thou (׳י‎) decrest against me bitter things.
