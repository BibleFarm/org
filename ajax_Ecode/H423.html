H423. alah

אָלָה‎ noun feminine oath Gen 26:28 +; suffix אָלָתוֺ אָלָתִי,‎ 24:11 + (4 t.); plural אָלוֺת‎ Deut 29:20 + (4 t).

1 oath in testimony Lev 5:1; Num 5:21 (twice in verse) (P) Prov 29:23; באלה בא‎ come into an oath Neh 10:30; באלה הביא‎ bring into an oath Ezek 17:13; אלה נשׂא‎ 1Kin 8:31 ( = 2Chr 6:22).

2 oath of convenant Gen 24:41 (twice in verse); 26:28 (J) Deut 29:11; 29:13; אלה בזה‎ despise an oath Ezek 16:59; 17:16, 18, 19.

3 curse (a) from God Num 5:23 (P) Deut 29:18; 29:19; 29:20; 30:7; 2Chr 34:24; Isa 24:6; Jer 23:10; Dan 9:11; Zech 5:3; (b) from men Job 31:30; Psa 10:7; 59:13.

4 execration in the phrase לאלה היה‎ become an execration Num 5:27 (P) Jer 29:18; 42:18; 44:12.
