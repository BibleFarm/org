מֵעֶה‎ (mêʻeh｜may-aw'｜noun｜masculine｜internal organs)
[Heb] מֵעֶה‎ ETCBC: מֵעִים‎ (subs｜bowels) OSHL: m.cv.ab TWOT: 1227a GK: H5055 Greek: κοιλία
Typo: xlit mêʻâh corrected to mêʻeh
Derivation: from an unused root probably meaning to be soft;
Strong's: used only in plural the intestines, or (collectively) the abdomen, figuratively, sympathy; by implication, a vest; by extension the stomach, the uterus (or of men, the seat of generation), the heart (figuratively)
KJV: belly, bowels, [idiom] heart, womb.

H4578. meeh

[מֵעֶה‎] noun masculineIsa 16:11 only

plural internal organs, inward parts (intestines, bowels), belly (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic מַעֲיָא מְעָא,‎, , ; Arabic ; Ethiopic ; — on form compare LagBN 156 BaZMG xiii. 1888, 345); — plural [מֵעִים‎] construct מְעֵי‎ Psa 71:6 3t.; suffix מֵעַי‎ 2Sam 16:11 9t., מֵעָ֑י‎ Psa 22:15; 40:9; מֵעֶיךָ‎ Gen 15:4 6t.; מֵעַיִךְ‎ 25:23; Num 5:22; מֵעָיו‎ 2Sam 20:10 5t.; מֵעֵיהֶם‎ Ezek 7:19; —

1 literally:

a. אַ֫רְצָה מֵעָיו וַיִּשְׁמֹּךְ‎ 2Sam 20:10 and he poured out his inward parts on the ground (through a wound); compare מ ׳מַחֲלֵה‎2Chr 21:15, 18, מ ׳יָָֽצְאוּ‎ 21:15; 21:19.

b. as digestive organs, but without precision, nearly = stomach, belly, Job 20:14; Ezek 3:3 (|| בֶּטֶן‎), 7:19 (|| נֶפֶשׁ‎; both with מָלֵא‎), Num 5:22; so of fish that swallowed Jonah Jonah 2:1; 2:2.

2 source of procreation: ׳מִמּ יצא אשׁר‎ (said of offspring) Gen 15:4 (JE), 2Sam 7:12 (|| זַרְעֲךָ‎), 16:11; מ ׳צֶאֱצָאֵי‎ Isa 48:19 (|| id.); מ ׳מִיצִיאֵי‎2Chr 32:21.

3 = womb, יִמָּרֵ֑דוּ מִמֵּעַיִח לְאֻמִּים שְׁנֵי‎ Gen 25:23 (J; || בֶּטֶן‎), compare Isa 49:1 (|| id.), Psa 71:6 (|| id.), Ruth 1:11.

4 in General = inwards, inward part, מ בְּתוֺךְ נָמֵס ֗֗֗ ׳לִבִּי‎ Psa 22:15, compare מ בְּתוֺךְ ׳תּוֺרָֽתְךָ‎ 40:9.

5 figurative = seat of emotions: pity, Isa 16:11 (|| קֶרֶב‎); of God's compassion, Jer 31:20 (|| רחם‎; in both subject of verb הָמוּ‎); מֵעֶיךָ הֲמוֺן‎ Isa 63:15 (|| רַחֲמֶיךָ‎); distress, Jer 4:19 (twice in verse) (|| חֳמַרְמָרוּ מֵעַי לֵב,‎ Lam 1:20 (|| לֵב‎), 2:11 (|| כָּבֵד‎ liver); רֻתְּחוּ מֵעַי‎ Job 30:27 (dubious, see Bu); love, עָלָיו הָמוּ מֵעַי‎ Song 5:4.

6 the external belly, שֵׁן עֶשֶׁת מֵעָיו‎ Song 5:14.
