2 take and carry along with oneself, בְיֶדְכֶם קְחוּ כֶּסֶף‎ Gen 43:12 (J), Josh 9:11 (JE), 1Kin 14:3; 2Kin 5:5; 8:8-9, 9:1 all Prov 7:20 (all with בְּיַד‎); Gen 43:15; Josh 9:4 (both J E), +.

3. a. take from, or out of, followed by active of thing or person, + מִן‎ local: Gen 2:22 (rib from man), 3:22 (man from ground; both J); Deut 19:12; 1Sam 17:49; 1Kin 17:19 +; יִמִינוֺ יֶרֶךְ מֵעַל אֶתהַֿחֶרֶב ח וַיִּקַּ‎ Judg 3:21 (i.e. drew it); with ב‎ instrumental ׳ל הַמִּזְבֵּחַ מֵעַל בְּמֶלְקָחַיִם‎ Isa 6:6; followed by מִן‎ partitive 2Sam 12:4.

b. take, carry away: Ezek 3:14 sprit lifited me up and carried me away; figurativeof passion carrying one away Job 15:12.

c. take away from, so as to deprive of, followed by accusative Gen 42:36 (E); followed by accusative+ מִן‎, 44:29 (מֵעִם‎; J), 1Kin 11:34; Psa 51:13; ראֹשֶׁ֑ךָ מֵעַל אֲדֹנֶיךָ ׳י אֶתֿ לֹקֵחַ‎ 2Kin 2:3 ׳י‎ is about to take away thy lord from over thy head, so 2:5; compare absolute ׳כִּילֿ אלהים אֹתוֺ אֵינֶנּוּ‎ Gen 5:24 (P); more often without מִן‎, 27:36 (twice in verse); 30:15 (twice in verse); 31:1 (all J), Judg 11:13, 15; 15:6; 18:24; 1Sam 12:3 (twice in verse); 2Sam 12:4; 1Kin 11:34; Job 12:20; absolute 1:21; בְּעֶבְרָתִי וְאֶקַּח בְּאַמִּי מֶלֶךְ אֶתֶּןלְֿךָ‎ Hosea 13:11; of the sword, taking away its victims Ezek 33:4, 6; figurative of taking away understanding Hosea 4:11.

d. especially take away life 1Kin 19:10, 14; Psa 31:14; Prov 1:19; Jonah 4:3.

4 take to or for a person:

a. for oneself, followed by accusative + לְ‎, = appropriate to oneself Deut 7:25; Num 8:16 (P), Deut 22:7; 2Kin 12:6; with לְ‎ as dativus commodi [dative of benefit], Zech 11:7; ׳ל לַעֲבָדִים לוֺ יְלָדַי אֶתשְֿׁנֵי‎ 2Kin 4:1 to take my two sons for himself for slaves; so (without לְ‎ reflexive), עוֺלָם לְעֶבֶד תִּקָּחֶנּוּ‎ Job 40:28 wilt thou take him as slave for ever?

b. = procure, get, followed by accusative + תֶּבֶן לָכֶם קְחוּ לְ,‎Exod 5:11 (J) get you straw; קָלוֺן לוֺ לֹקֵחַ‎ Prov 9:7 getteth to himself shame; so often, especially לְךָ קַח‎, etc., Gen 6:21; 12:19; Genesis 16:21; 45:19 +; without לְ‎, Neh 5:2 let us procure corn, and eat and live; of acquiring a field Prov 31:16.

c. take possession of, followed by accusative, of land Deut 29:7; Josh 11:16, 23 (all D), 2Chr 16:5; compare אַחֵר יִקַּח מְּקֻדָּתוֺ‎ Psa 109:8 his office let another take; figurative אֹפֶל יִקָּחֵהוּ הַהוּא הַלַּיְלָה‎ Job 3:6 that night — let darkness take possession of it!

d. = select, choose, ל ׳וְאֶתְכֶם ׳י‎Deut 4:20, compare 4:34 ( + לוֺ‎), 1Kin 11:37; of men Deut 1:15; Josh 3:12 (J E; + לָכֶם‎); + מִן‎ Deut 1:23; Josh 4:2 (JE).

e. especially take in marriage: (I) for another, especially a son, with לִבְנִי אִשָּׁה וְלָקַחְתָּ֫ לְ,‎, Gen 24:4 and thou shalt take a wifefor my son, so 24:7; 24:38; 24:40; 24:48 (all J), 21:21 (E), Jer 29:6b; (2) more often for oneself, usually with לְ‎ reflexive (sometimes + לְאִשָּׁה‎), Gen 4:19; 6:2; 11:29; 12:19 (all J), + often; without לְ‎ 20:2-3,(E), etc.

f. = receive, accept, especially a bribe, gift, ransom, etc., 1Sam 8:3; 1 Samuel 12: 12:4 (both followed by מִן‎), Amos 5:12; 2Kin 5:15-16, 20, 23, 26 (twice in verse); Psa 15:5; interest-money Ezek 18:13, 17; 22:12; inheritance Josh 13:8; 18:7(both D), Num 34:14 (twice in verse); 34:15 (P); of earth, receiving Abel's blood Gen 4:11(J); chastisement Isa 40:2; of perceiving a sound, Job 4:11 mine ear received a whisper of it; receive mentally: בְּעָלָיו וְלָקַח‎ Exod 22:10 (E) i.e. shall accept the oath as satisfactory; לָקַחְתִּי בָּרֵךְ‎ Num 23:20 (JE), i.e. I have received (commandment by revelation) to bless; receive instruction Prov 24:32; Jer 2:30 +; entanglements of temptation Prov 22:25.

g. take, as preliminary to further action: Gen 31:34(E), she took them and put them; Judg 3:25 take key and open; Josh 2:4 she took the two men and hid them; 1Kin 18:4 Obadiah took100prophets and hid them; 19:21 Elisha took the yoke of oxen and slew them, compare 2Sam 17:19; 18:18; sometimes wholly redundant, חֲדָשָׁה עֲגָלָה וַעֲשׂוּ קְחוּ‎ 1Sam 6:7 take and prepare a new cart, etc.; somewhat different is נְאֻם וַיִּנְאֲמוּ לְשׁוֺנָם הַלֹּקְחִים‎ Jer 23:31, i.e. they take (= make use of) their own tongues, and talk oracles.

5 take up, upon =put upon, followed by עַלרֿאֹשָׁהּ אֵפֶר תָּמָר וַתִּקַּת ׃עַל‎ 2Sam 13:19 and Tamar put ashes upon her head; עַלהַֿחֲמוֺר וַיִּקָּחֶהָ‎ Judg 19:28; compare יִקָּחֵהוּ כְּנָפָיו יִפְרשֹׁ‎ Deut 32:11 he spreadeth his wings, he taketh him up.

6 = fetch: מְעַטמַֿיִם לִי קְחִינָֿא‎ 1Kin 17:10 fetch me, pray, a little water, compare 17:11; 17:11, 2Kin 2:20; 4:41; עִוִּים גְּדָיֵי שְׁנֵי מִשָּׁם וְקַחלִֿי‎ Gen 27:9 and fetch me thence two kids, compare 27:13 (J); + בְּיָדִי‎ 1Sam 21:9; an offering Lev 12:8 (P; || הֵבִיא‎ 12:6); with human object often nearly = summon, לְקַחְתִּיךָ אֹיְבַי לָקֹב‎ Num 33:11 (JE) to curse mine enemies did I summon thee, Judg 11:5; 1Sam 16:11; אֵלַי אֹתוֺ וְקַח שְׁלַח‎ 20:31 send and bring him unto me; 2Kin 3:15; 6:13 +; of ׳י‎, summoning his people from exile, יְקָּחֶ֑ךָ מִשָּׁם‎ Deut 30:4.

7 take=lead, conduct (with or without contact): עִמּוֺ בָנָיו אֶתשְֿׁנֵי וַיִּקַּח‎ Gen 48:1; קְחוּ בְּקַרְכֶם גַּם‎ Exod 12:32 (both E); עִמּוֺ לָקַח וְאֶתעַֿמּוֺ‎ 14:6 (J) and his people he took with him; compare 14:7 (J), Josh 8:1 (JE), Judg 4:6; אַחֵר אֶלמָֿקוֺם אֶקָּֽחֲךָ‎ Num 23:27 I will take thee unto another place, compare 23:14; 23:28 (all J E); ׳י‎ subject, אֶתאֲֿבִיכֶם וָאֶקַּח‎ Josh 24:3 (E) and I took your father Abraham from beyond the river; לְקֻחִים הַצֵּל לַמָּוֶת‎ rescue those led to death.

8 take = capture, seize: בְחָזְקָה לָקַחְתִּי‎ 1Sam 2:16 I will take by force, that is, flesh; of spoils, ל לֹא כֶּסֶף ׳בֶּצַע‎ Judg 5:19 gain of silver they took not; of capture of ark 1Sam 5:1; bronze 2Sam 8:3, etc.; territory Gen 48:22 (E), Deut 3:14; cities Num 21:25 (JE),.Deut 3:14; Josh 11:19 (D), 1Sam 7:14; 2Sam 8:1; of capture of hippopotamus Job 40:24; of seductions of woman, בְּעַפְעַמֶּיהָ וְאַלתִּֿקָּֽחֲךָ‎ Prov 6:25 and let her not capture thee with her eyelids.

9 take = carry off:

a. as booty Gen 14:11; 1Sam 27:9; 30:16, 18, 19, 20; 1Kin 14:26 (twice in verse).

b. as prisoners Gen 14:12; 2Kin 18:32 = Isa 36:17; 2Kin 23:34 2Chr 36:4.

10 inphrase take vengeance: מִמֶּנּוּ נִקְמָתֵנוּ וְנִקְחָה‎ Jer 20:10 and we will take our vengeance upon him; absolute אֶקַּח נָקָם‎ Isa 47:3 vengeance will I take.
