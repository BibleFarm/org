H2789. cheres

חֶ֫רֶשׂ‎ noun [masculine] earthenware, earthen vessel, sherd, potsherd, P and late (Late Hebrew חֶרֶס‎, Aramaic חֲרַס‎) — absolute ׳ח‎ Lev 6:21 9t., חָ֑רֶשׂ‎ Num 5:17 4t.; plural construct חַרְשֵׂי‎ Isa 45:9; suffix חֲרָשֶׂיהָ‎ Ezek 23:34. —

1 earthenware: ׳כְּלִיאח‎ earthen vessel Jer 32:14; especially P Lev 6:21; 11:33; 14:5, 50; 15:12; Num 5:17; without כְּלִי‎ Prov 26:23; compare ח יוֺצֵר ׳בַּקְבֻּק‎ Jer 19:1; ח ׳לְנִבְלֵי‎ Lam 4:2 they are reckoned as earthen vessels, simile of sons of Zion; אֲדָמָה אֶתחַֿרְשֵׂי חֶרֶשׂ‎ Isa 45:9 a potsherd (perhaps = earthen vessel, see Prov 26:23 above) among earthen potsherds (of men, over against ׳י‎ their potter); simile of dryness Psa 22:16.

2 a fragment of earthenware, sherd Isa 30:14; Ezek 23:34; ׳ח לְהִתְגָּרֵד‎ Job 2:8 a sherd to scrape himself; ח ׳חַדּוּדֵי‎ 41:22 sharpest potsherds, figurative of sharp scales on belly of crocodile.

חֲרֶשֶׂת‎ see קִירחֲֿרֶשֶׂת‎.
