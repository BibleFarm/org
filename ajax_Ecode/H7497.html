H7497. Rapha

II. רְפָאִים‎ proper name, of a people old race of giants (perhaps = I. ׳ר‎, as extinct and powerless; see especially WRS in DrDeut 2:11; or as shadowy, vaguely known, SchwZAW xviii (1898), 127 ff. see also Stal.c.); — ancient inhabitants of Canaan [west of Jordan?], Gen 15:20; Josh 17:15 (JE), compare 1Chr 20:4 (see II. רָפָה‎ above); hence ר ׳עֶמֶמק‎, plain south of Jerusalem 2Sam 5:18, 22; 23:13; 1Chr 11:15; 14:9; Isa 17:5; Josh 15:8; 18:16 (P); ׳ר‎ east of Jordan Deut 2:11 (|| עֲנָקִים‎; tall, compare 2:10), 2:20 (|| id.; tall; = Zamzummim); in Bashan, Gen 14:5, Og the last of them Deut 3:11 (of huge size), Josh 12:4; 13:12 (D); ר ׳אֶרֶץ‎ Deut 2:20; 3:13. ᵐ5‎ Ραφαειν[μ]; οἱ γίγαντες Gen 14:5 4t.; τῶν Τιτάνων 2Sam 5:18, 22 + (ᵐ5‎L) 23:13.

רִפוֺת‎ see רִיפָה‎ below ריף‎. p. 937.
