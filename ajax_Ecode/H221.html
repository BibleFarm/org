H221. Uri

אוּרִי‎ proper name, masculine (fiery, or contracted for אוּרִיָּה‎)

1 prince of Judah Exod 31:2; 35:30; 38:22; 1Chr 2:20; 2Chr 1:5;

2 a porter Ezra 10:24;

3 father of an officer of Solomon 1Kin 4:19.
