H7186. qasheh

קָשֶׁה‎ adjective hard, severe; — absolute ׳ק‎ Exod 18:26 +; construct 32:9 +; feminine singular קָשָׁה‎ Deut 26:6 +; construct קְשַׁת‎ 1Sam 1:15; masculine plural קָשִׁים‎ 2Sam 3:39; construct קְשֵׁי‎ Ezek 2:4; 3:7; feminine plural קָשׁוֺת‎ Gen 42:7, 30; —

1 hard, difficult, of a legal question (דָּבָר‎) Exod 18:26 (E).

2. a. severe, of battle 2Sam 2:17, wind Isa 27:8, servitude (עֲבֹדָה‎) 1Kin 12:4 2Chr 10:4; Deut 26:6; Isa 14:3; Exod 1:14; 6:9 (both P); as n.collective קָשָׁה עַמְּךָ הִרְאִיתָ‎ Psa 60:5 thou hast made thy people see severe things; יוֺם קְשֵׁה‎ Job 30:25 i.e. one whose time (life) is hard, so 1Sam 1:15 (reading יוֺם קְשַׁת‎ for ᵐ5 ׳ק, רוּחַ‎ Th We Dr and others); severe, rough, of lord (אֲדֹנִים‎) Isa 19:4, compare 2Sam 3:39 (with מִן‎ compare [מִמֶּנּוּ‎? see HPS]); עַלֿ וְקָשָׁה הָלוֺח‎ Judg 4:24 the hand of the sons of Israel went on being more and more severe upon Jabin; fierce, relentless, of ׳י‎'s sword Isa 27:1 (in figurative); rough, rude 1Sam 25:3; severe things, of language Gen 42:7, 30 (E; with דִּדֶּר‎), 1Sam 20:10; 1Kin 12:13 2Chr 10:13 (all with עָנָה‎); קָשָׁה אֵלַיִח שָׁלוּחַ וְאָנֹכִי‎ 1Kin 14:6 I am commissioned with a severe (message) unto thee (Ges§ 121d. N.2 DaSynt. §§ 75 (d), 80); קָשָׁה חָזוּת‎ Isa 21:2.

b. fierce, intense, vehement, of קִנְאָה‎ Song 8:6 (|| עַזָּה‎; compare Gen 49:9).

3 עַםקְֿשֵׁהעֹֿרֶף‎ a people stiff of neck, stubborn, Exod 32:9; 33:3, 5; 34:9 (all J E), Deut 9:6, 13; הַקָּשֶׁה עָרְמְּךָ‎ 31:27; compare הַקָּשָׁה דַּרְכָּם‎ Judg 2:19; פָנִים קְשֵׁי‎ Ezek 2:4 (+ חִזְקֵילֵֿב‎), קְשֵׁאלֵֿב‎ 3:7 (+ חִזְקֵימְֿצַח‎); ׳ק‎ alone = stubborn Isa 48:4.
