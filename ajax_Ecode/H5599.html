H5599. caphiyach

I. [סָפִיחַ‎] noun [masculine] outpouring ?; — plural suffix הָאָרֶץ עֲפַר תִּשְׁטֹףסְֿפִיחֶיהָ‎ Job 14:19 (si vera lectio) its outpourings sweep away the dust of the earth, so most, but dubious; Bu proposes *סְחִיפָה‎, compare סֹחֵף מָטָר‎ Prov 28:3.

II. סָפִיחַ‎ noun [masculine] growth from spilled kernels ((kernels) poured out, accidentally, in harvesting, according to most; BuhlLex 13 and others think of additional growth, √ I. ספח‎); — it is what springs up of itself in second year, and serves as food when no grain could be sown: absolute ׳ס‎ 2Kin 19:29 = Isa 37:30; construct קְצִירְךָ סְפִיחַ‎ Lev 25:5; suffix אֶתסְֿפִיחֶיהַ תִּקְצְרוּ‎ 25:11.
