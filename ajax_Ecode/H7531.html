H7531. ritspah

ִרצְפָה‎ noun feminine pavement; — absolute ׳ר‎ Ezek 40:17 (twice in verse) +, construct ִרצְפַת‎ Est 1:6 (van d. H. מַּת רִעצמָּה,‎-); — pavement, in Ezekiel's temple, Ezek 40:17 (twice in verse); 40:18 (twice in verse); 42:3; Solomon's temple 2Chr 7:3; in a palace Est 1:6.

I. רִצְמָּה‎ noun feminine glowing stone (or coal) (WeIsrael u. Jüd. Geschichte. (3) 83); — ׳ר‎ absolute Isa 6:6; plural רְצָפִים עֻגַת‎ 1Kin 19:6 (compare Syriac above).
