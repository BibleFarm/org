[מֵחַ‎] noun [masculine] fatling (Phoenician מח‎ fat (adjective ); Late Hebrew מֵיחָא‎ figurative of choice flour); — only plural absolute: מֵיחִים עֹלוֺת‎ Psa 66:15 burnt offerings of fatlings; so מֵחִים‎ Isa 5:17 (|| כְּבָשִׂים‎) according to Hi Du CheHpt (read חֳרָבוֺת‎ and strike out גָּרִים‎); most, fat ones, figurative for rich men.
