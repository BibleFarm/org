H977. bachar

בָּחַר‎ verb choose (compare Aramaic בְּחַר‎, Syriac || בחן‎, Assyrian bêru (√ באר‎):3 DlPr 76) —

Qal Perfect בָּחַר‎ Deut 7:6 66t.; יִבְחַר‎ Isa 41:24 60t.; אֶבֲחַר‎ Job 29:25 (Baer); Imperative בְּחַר‎ Exod 17:9 5t.; Infinitive absolute בָּחוֺר‎ 1Sam 2:28; Isa 7:15-16, Infinitive construct suffix בָּחֳרִי‎ Ezek 20:5; Participle בֹּחֵר‎ 1Sam 20:30; Zech 3:2; passive בָּחוּר‎ Exod 14:7 18t.; —

a. divine choice, of Abraham Neh 9:7; Israel Deut 7:7; Isa 44:1; Ezek 20:5; to become his people Deut 7:6; 14:2; Jeshurun Isa 44:2; the seed of the patriarchs Deut 4:37; 10:15; Levites 18:5; 21:5; 1Chr 15:2; 2Chr 29:11; Aaron Psa 105:26; Judah 1Chr 28:4 not Ephraim Psa 78:67; Levi and Judah Jer 33:24; the king Deut 17:15, especially David 1Sam 10:24; 16:8-9, 10; 2Sam 6:21; 1Kin 8:16 (= 2Chr 6:6) 1Chr 28:4-5, 29:1; 2Chr 6:5; Psa 78:70; others Num 16:5; Numbers 17:20 (P) Hag 2:23; place of sacrifice Deut 12:18; 14:25; 16:7, 15; 17:8; 2Chr 7:12; the city 1Kin 8:16, 44 (= 2Chr 6:5, 34) 1Kin 11:32; Jerusalem 2Chr 6:6; Isa 14:1; Zech 1:17; Zechariah 2:16; 3:2; Zion Psa 132:13; delusions Isa 66:4.

a. divine choice, Israel Isa 41:8 בְּחַרְתִּיךָ אֲשֶׁר יַעֲקֹב‎, 43:10; the people 1Kin 3:8; Psa 33:12; men Num 16:7 (P) Psa 65:5; king 2Sam 16:18; 1Kin 11:34; place of sacrifice Deut 12:14, 26; 15:20; 17:10; 31:11 (D) Josh 9:27 (P), especially שָׁם שְׁמוֺ לָשׂוּם‎ Deut 12:5, 21; 14:24, שָׁם שְׁמוֺ לְשַׁכֵּן‎ 12:11; 14:23; 16:2, 6, 11; Neh 1:9; the city 1Kin 8:48 (= 2Chr 6:38), שׁם שׁמי לשׂום‎ 1Kin 11:36; 14:21 (= 2Chr 12:13); Jerusalem 1Kin 11:13; 2Kin 21:7 (= 2Chr 33:7) 2Kin 23:27; fast Isa 58:5-6, way Psa 25:12.

b. man's choice, place to dwell in Deut 23:7; gardens Isa 1:29; king 1Sam 12:13; wives Gen 6:2 (J); what to do 2Sam 15:15.

3 with accusative & לְ‎, choose some one or something for:

a. divine choice, of Levi 1Sam 2:28; Jacob Psa 135:4; 135:5; inheritance 47:5.

b. human choice persons Exod 17:9; Josh 24:15, 22 (E) 1Sam 8:18; 13:12; 2Sam 24:12 (= 1Chr 21:20); things Gen 13:11 (J) 1Sam 17:40; 1Kin 18:23, 25; Job 34:4.

4 with accusative and מִן‎, choose, select from 2Sam 10:9 (= 1Chr 19:10).

5 accusative

a. divine choice, temple 2Chr 7:16; Judah Psa 78:68; servant Isa 41:9; 49:7.

b. man's choice, persons Exodus 18:28 (E) Judg 5:8; Josh 8:3 (J) 2Sam 17:1; things Job 7:15; 9:14; 15:5; 29:25; 34:33; Psa 119:30; 119:173; Prov 1:29; Isa 40:20.

7 with לְ‎ of accusative 1Sam 20:30 (many MSS. have בְּ‎; but ᵐ5‎ We Dr read חָבֵר‎ companion). The participle בָּחוּר‎ chosen, of a ruler Psa 89:20, warrior Jer 49:19 (= 50:44); as cedars Song 5:15; collective בחור רכב‎ chosen chariots Exod 14:7 (E); בחור אישׁ‎ chosen men, warriors Judg 20:15-16, 34; 1Sam 24:3; 2Chr 13:3, 17, for which בָּחוּר‎ alone 1Kin 12:21 2Chr 11:1; 25:5; ישׂראל בחורי‎ 1Sam 26:2; Psa 78:31; 2Sam 10:9 בישׂראל בחורי‎ (|| 1Chr 19:10 בישׂראל בחור‎, doubtless the true reading, as 2Sam 6:1).

8 test, try (Aramaic = בחן‎) עֹ֑נִי בְּכוּר בְּחַרְתִּיךָ‎ Isa 48:10 I have tested thee in the furnace of affliction ᵑ7 ᵑ6‎ Ges Hi Ew De Che Dr, but chosen ᵑ9‎ Rab Calv AV. Niph`al נִבְחַר‎ Jer 8:3; Participle נִבְחָר‎ Prov 8:10 5t.; — to be chosen.

a. absolute chosen, choice Prov 8:10, 19; 10:20.

b. construct מחיים מות וְנִבְחַר מִן,‎ death will be chosen rather than life Jer 8:3; choicer than Prov 16:16; 22:1; מזבח ליהוה נבחר‎ choicer (more acceptable) to Yahweh than peace-offering 21:3.
