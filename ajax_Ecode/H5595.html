H5595. saphah

[סָפָה‎] verb sweep away or snatch away, catch up (Late Hebrew סָפָא ׳ספ,‎ collect (rare), ׳סְפִ ᵑ7‎ (rare), Syriac collect, pick up; Arabic of wind, raise dust and carry it away Lane1377); —

Qal 1. intransitive: Perfect3feminine singular סָֽפְתָה‎ Jer 12:4 be snatched away (read possibly סָ֫פָה‎ [סוף‎]).

2 transitive: Imperfect3feminine singular תִּסְמֶּה‎ Isa 7:20 sweep away beard; 2 masculine singular תִּסְמֶּה‎ Gen 18:23-24, sweep away indiscriminately (good and bad); Infinitive construct סְפוֺת‎ Deut 29:18 to snatch away the moist with the dry (proverb. expression, compare Dr), suffix לִסְמּוֺתָהּ‎ Psa 40:15 to snatch it away (i.e. my life). — וְסָפ֫וּ‎ Amos 3:15 see סוף‎.

Niph`al Pf .3masculine singular וְנִסְמָּה‎ consecutive 1Sam 26:10; Imperfect2masculine singularתִּסָּפֶה‎ Gen 19:15, 17; 1singular אֶסָּפֶה‎ 1Sam 27:1-2;masculine plural תִּסָּפוּ‎ Num 16:26, תִּסָּפ֑וּ‎ 1Sam 12:25; Participle נִסְמֶּה‎ Isa 13:15; Prov 13:23 (1Chr 21:12 see below); —

1 be swept away, destroyed, Gen 19:15 (הָעִיר בַּעֲוֺן‎ by reason of the iniquity of the city), 19:17 (both J), Num 16:26 (JE) 1Sam 12:25; 26:10 (in battle), + בְּיַד‎ of agent 27:1; compare מִשְׁמָּט בְּלאֹ נִסְמֶּה וְיֵשׁ‎ Prov 13:23 and there is that is swept away for lack of justice. — 1Chr 21:12 read נוּסְךָ‎ (as || 2Sam 24:13, see נוּס‎).

2 be caught up, captured, Isa 13:15 (|| נִמְצָא‎).

Hiph`il Imperfect1singular רָעוֺת עָלֵימוֺ אֵסְמֶּה‎ Deut 32:23 I will catch up against them calamities, but read אֹסְפָה‎ I will gather (√ אסף‎), or < אֹסִפָה‎ (Di Dr BuhlLex 13) I will add, i.e.multiply (√ יסף‎; see Ges§ 69h, note).
