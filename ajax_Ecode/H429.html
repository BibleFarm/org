H429. elleh

אֵלֶּה‎ demonstrative pronoun plural these (Nabataean אלה‎ (Lzb264 Cooke241), Cappadocian and Egyptian Aramaic אלה‎ (LzbEph. i. 323 RÉS361A 3 S-CPap. E.13, Ki 13): probably therefore not, as used to be supposed, a Hebraism, but a peculiarity of the dialects of Jer 10:11 and of Ezra (in which the usually Aramaic form אִלֵּין‎ does not occur) : see further אִלֵּין‎); — these, 10:11; Ezra 5:15 Kt.
