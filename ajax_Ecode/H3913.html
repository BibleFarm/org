H3913. latash

[לָטַשׁ‎] verb hammer, sharpen, whet (Late Hebrew id., hammer, tap; Aramaic לְטַשׁ‎, sharpen; Arabic strike, tap); —

Qal Imperfect יִלְטוֺשׁ‎ Job 16:9; Psa 7:13; Infinitive construct לֵלְטוֺשׁ‎ 1Sam 13:20; Participle לֹטֵשׁ‎; —

1 hammer, only Participle = hammerer followed by כָּלתֹֿרֵשׁ‎ Gen 4:22 (J).

2 sharpen sword חֶרֶב‎ (accusative) Psa 7:13; compare 1Sam 13:20, object מַחֲרֵשָׁה קַרְדֹּם, אֵת, מַחֲרֶשֶׁת,‎; figurative ׳ל לִי עֵינַיִם‎ Job 16:9 my foe whets his eye for me

Pu`al Participle מְלֻטָּשׁ תַּעַר‎ Psa 52:4 a sharpened razor (in simile of evil tongue).
