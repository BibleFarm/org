H2504. chalats

[חָלָץ‎] noun [feminine] only dual, loins (as seat of strength, vigour; compare Aramaic חַרְצָא‎ hip, loin, Syriac , NöZMG 1886, 741) — חֲלָצָ֑יִם‎ Isa 32:11; suffix חֲלָצֶיךָ‎ Gen 35:11 4t.; חֲלָצָיו‎ Isa 5:27 2t.; חלצו‎ Kt Job 31:20, Qr חֲלָצָיו‎; — loins:

1 as seat of virility ח מן ׳יצא‎ Gen 35:11 (P), 1Kin 8:19; 2Chr 6:9.

2 as girded ח כְּגֶבֶר ׳נָא אֱזָרֿ‎ Job 38:3; 40:7; חֲלָצָיו אֵזוֺר‎ Isa 5:27 (see אֵזוֺר‎), חֲלָצָ֑יִם עַל וַחֲג֫וֺרָה‎ 32:11, compare Job 31:20 where the clothed loins are conceived as blessing charitable giver; metaphor Isa 11:5 faithfulness waist-cloth of his loins (|| מתניו‎).

3 as seat of pains, like a woman's in travail, Jer 30:6.

[חֲרַץ‎] noun [masculine] loin (see Biblical Hebrew חָלָץ‎, √ II. [חָלַץ‎]); — suffix חַרְצֵהּ‎ Dan 5:6 the joints of his loin (see קְטַר‎).
