H4031. Magog

מָגוֺג‎ proper name, of a territory (= land of Gog? compare DlPa 246 LenOr ii. 1, 465) — Ezek 38:2; 39:6; in Gen 10:2 = 1Chr 1:5 a son of Japhet, וְתִירָס וּמֶשֶׁךְ וְתֻבָ֑ל וְיָוָן וּמָדַי וּמָגוֺג גֹּמֶר יֶפֶת בְּנֵי‎; = Scythians? compare JosAnt. i. 6. 1; LagGes. Abh. 158 refers name to mountainous region between Cappadocia and Media; compare Di Gen 10:2, KiepMBAk Feb. 1889, 207 (North & East Armenia), Len (Southeast Armenia), see especially LenOr ii. 1, 412-476.
