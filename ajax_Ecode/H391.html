H391. akzab

אַכְזָב‎ adjective deceptive, disappointing (elative; opposed to אֵיתָן‎ q. v. below יתן‎) — only absolute singular: — Micah 1:14 (with play on proper name אַכְזִיב‎); of a deceptive, disappointing stream (compare Isa 58:11) Jer 15:18 (|| נֶאְמָ֑נוּ לֹא מַיִם‎; on meaning compare Job 6:15ff.)
