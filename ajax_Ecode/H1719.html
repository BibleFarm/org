H1719. Dedan

or Dedaneh דְּדָן‎ proper name, of a location & a people; — ׳ד‎ Gen 10:7-8t. + Ezek 27:20 (but see below) with ה‎ locative דְּדָ֫נֶה‎ 25:13 compare Ges§ 90, 2 near the end; —

1 under figure of son of רַעֲמָה‎ and grandson of כּוּשׁ‎ (see these arts.) Gen 10:7 (brother of שְׁבָא‎) = 1Chr 1:9; Ezek 27:20; compare ד ׳בְּנֵי‎ 27:15 (but read here perhaps ᵐ5 רדן‎ υιὁὶ Ῥοδίων, compare StaDe Pop. Javan. 11, also ᵑ6‎ Co; see below דֹּדָנִים‎ below) compare also 38:13, וּד ׳שְׁבָא‎; south-Arabian tribe on Persian Gulf, = Sabean דדן בנו‎ according to DHMZMG 1876, 122; Sab. Denkm. 28 (dubit. Mordt); but in northwest of Arabian peninsula, GlaserSkizze ii. 391 ff.; perhaps originally further south compare Di Gen 10:7.

2 as son of יָקְשָׁן‎ father of אַשּׁוּרִם‎ etc., Gen 25:3 (twice in verse) (here also brother of שְׁבָא‎) = 1Chr 1:32; Jer 25:23; 49:8 ד ׳ישְׁבֵי‎. This is apparently a northern branch of 1, compare Di Gen 25:3. — On דדן‎ in MI31, 31 compare Sm & SoMI p. 29 NöLCB Jan. 8, 1887 ClGannJAssyrian Jan. 1887. 107.

1）先知書中論以東時，說到底但的居民（耶49:7）

2）其次，講論推羅時，也說底但人（結27:15,
