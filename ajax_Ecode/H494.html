H494. Elnathan

אֶלְנָתָן‎ (God has given, compare נְתַנְאֵל‎, & Assyrian Ilu-iddin DlPr 207, Phoenician יתנבעל בעליתן,‎, Palmyrene והבלת עתנתן,‎, VogPal 30 & p. 31 f, Nabataean קסנתן והבאל,‎ EutNab 12, 1, Sabean אלוהב והבאל,‎ DHMZMG 1883, 15. 361. 388 CISiv.1, 1.2.6, Theodore, Diodate).

a. the grandfather of Jehoiakim 2Kin 24:8; compare Jer 26:22; 36:12, 25 (?).

b. Levites of the time of Ezra Ezra 8:16 (3 t. in verse).
