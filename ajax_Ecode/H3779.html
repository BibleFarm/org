כַּשְׂדָּ֑י‎ proper name, of a people Chaldean (see Biblical Hebrew כַשְׂדִּים‎); — absolute ׳כ‎ Dan 2:10; emphatic כשדיא‎ 5:30 Kt (Qr כַּשְׂדָּאָה‎), כסדיא‎ Ezra 5:12 Kt (Qr כַּסְדָּאָה‎); plural absolute כַּשְׂדָּאִין‎ 3:8; 5:11, emphatic כשדיא‎ Kt, כַּשְׂדָּאֵי‎ Qr, Dan 2:5, 10; 4:4; 5:7; —

1 Chaldean By race Dan 3:8; 5:30; Ezra 5:12.

2 as learned, of the class of Magi (Biblical Hebrew ׳כ‎ 1c), Dan 2:5, 10 (twice in verse); 4:4; 5:7, 11.
