H6925. qodam

קֳדָם‎42 preposition before (so Old Aramaic, Nabataean, Palmyrene, etc. (Lzb360f.), קָדָם ᵑ7‎, Syriac ; properly a substantive the front, compare Arabic the front; for √ see Biblical Hebrew קדם‎); — suffix קָדָמַי‎ Dan 2:9 +, מָ֑י‎- 2:6; קדמיך‎ Kt, קֳדָמָךְ‎ Qr, 5:23; 6:23; קֳדָמוֺהִי‎ 4:5 + (compare Nerab קדמוה‎, Cooke189); 3 feminine singular קדמיה‎ Kt, קֳדָמַהּ‎ Qr, 7:7-8, 20; קֳדָמֵיהוֺן‎ 4:4; —

1 before (= Hebrew לפְנֵי‎), especially in the phrases to answer, pray, say, etc., before a superior (as more respectful than to), Dan 2:9 קֳדָמַי לְמֵאמַד‎, 2:10; 2:11; 2:27; 6:11 (see Dr), 6:12; 6:13; 6:14; ק ׳קֱרִי‎ Ezra 4:18; 4:23; Ezra 5:23 (twice in verse); with שְׁפַר‎ to seem fair Daniel 3:32; Dan 6:2; after verbs of motion, in before, 2:24-25, 3:13; 4:3 +; of time 7:7.

2 מִןקֳֿדָם‎ from before ( = מִלִּפְנֵי‎, but as used in late Hebrew, Est 1:19; 4:8), with קַבֵּל‎ Dan 2:6, בְּעָא‎ 2:18; of a decree, 6:27 טְעֵם שִֹׁים מִןקֳֿדָמִי‎ (|| מִנִּי‎ 3:29 +), compare 2:15; Ezra 7:14; of God, Dan 5:24 דַּייְֿדָא מַּסָּא שְׁלִיחַ מִןקֳֿדָמוֺהִי‎, 7:10 (so very often in ᵑ7‎, DrSm lxx. f. lxxiii). Also = Hebrew מִמְּנֵי‎, with to fear 5:19; 6:27, be rooted up 7:8, fall 7:20.
