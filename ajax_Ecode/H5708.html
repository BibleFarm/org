עֵד‎ (ʻêd｜ayd｜noun｜feminine｜menstruation)
[Heb] עֵד‎, עִדָּה‎ ETCBC: עִדָּה‎ (subs｜menstruation) OSHL: p.ap.ab TWOT: 1564a GK: H6340
Derivation: from an unused root meaning to set a period (compare עָדָה‎, עוּד‎);
Strong's: the menstrual flux (as periodical); by implication (in plural) soiling
KJV: filthy.

H5708. ed

[עִדָּה‎] noun feminine menstruation, so Vrss (properly time, period); — plural absolute עִדִּים בֶּגֶד‎ Isa 64:5 i.e. stained garment (figurative of best deeds of guilty people; || טָמֵא‎).
