H4585. meonah

מְעֹנָה‎ noun feminine id.; —

1 den, lair of wild beasts: of מְעֹנָתוֺ כְּפִיר,‎ Amos 3:4, so plural absolute מְעוֺנוֺת‎ Job 38:40 (|| סֻכָּה‎), suffix מְעוֺנֹתָם‎ Psa 104:22; construct אֲרָיוֺת מְעֹנוֺת‎ Song 4:8; מְעֹנֹתָיו‎ Nahum 2:13 (|| חֹרָיו‎; of אַרְיֵה‎, figurative of Nineveh); of חַיָּה‎ in General, תִשְׁכֹּן בִּמְעוֺנֹתֶיהָ‎ Job 37:8 (|| אָ֑רֶב‎); so apparently figurative of men, hunted by מְעוֺנוֺתֵינוּ ׳י,‎ Jer 21:13, figurative also of ׳י‎ dwelling in temple, singular suffix, בְּצִיּוֺן מְעוֺנָתוֺ‎ Psa 76:3 (|| סוּכּוֺ‎).

2 figurative קֶדֶם אֱלֹהֵי מְעֹנָה‎ Deut 33:27 the ancient God is a dwelling-place (for his people; compare I. מָעוֺן‎ 3).
