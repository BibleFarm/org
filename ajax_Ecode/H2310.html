חָדֵל‎ (châdêl｜khaw-dale'｜adjective｜forbearing)
[Heb] חָדֵל‎ ETCBC: חָדֵל‎ (adjv｜ceasing) OSHL: h.at.ab TWOT: 609b GK: H2534 Greek: παρίημι
Derivation: from חָדַל‎;
Strong's: vacant, i.e. ceasing or destitute
KJV: he that forbeareth, frail, rejected.

H2310. chadel

חָדֵל‎ adjective forbearing, lacking, הֶחָדֵל‎ as substantive Ezek 3:27 he that forbeareth (opposed to שׁמע‎, compare חָדֵל‎

Qal near the end); construct אִישִׁים חֲדַל‎ Isa 53:3 lacking men (i.e. forsaken by them); — ceasing, transient אָ֑נִי מֶהחָֿדֵל‎ Psa 39:5, but read rather חֶלֶד‎ see Checritical note.
