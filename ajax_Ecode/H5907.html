H5907. Akbor

עַכְבֹּר עַכְבּוֺר,‎ proper name, masculine (mouse, compare עַכְבָּר‎, see GrayProp. N. 93; עכבר‎ Old Hebrew and Phoenician as proper name Lzb304 (compare also on similar Latin proper name ClGannJAs 1883, Fev.- Mar. 128)); — ᵐ5‎ Αχοβωρ:

1 Edomite Gen 36:38-39, (P) = עַכְבֹּר‎ 1Chr 1:49 (Baer Ginsb).

2 courtier of Josiah 2Kin 22:12, 14, perhaps also Jer 26:22; 36:12 (compare Gie and Ency. Bib.).
