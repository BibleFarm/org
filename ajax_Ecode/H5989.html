H5989. Ammihud

עַמִּיהוּד‎ proper name, masculine (my kinsman is majesty); — ᵐ5‎ (Σ)εμιουδ, Αμιουδ, etc.:

1 a Geshurite 2Sam 13:37 Qr (> Kt עַמִּיחוּר‎, compare Dr and others, GrayProp. N. 43).

2 an Ephraimite Num 1:10; 2:18; 7:48, 53; 10:22 (all P), 1Chr 7:26.

3 a Simeonite Num 34:20.

4 a Naphtalite Num 34:28.

5 a Judahite 1Chr 9:4.
