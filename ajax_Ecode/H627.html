H627. asuppah

[אֲסֻמָּה‎] noun feminine collection (compare Phoenician אספת‎ assembly) only אֲסֻמּוֺת בַּעֲלֵי‎ Eccl 12:11 (חֲכָמִים דִּבְרֵי‎) compared to driven nails; members of learned assemblies Thes MV and others; so Post-Biblical Hebrew NHWBi. 127; but Hi-Now and others refer to the wise utterances, called lords (possessors) of collection because of their well-connected grouping.
