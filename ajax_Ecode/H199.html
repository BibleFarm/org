III. אוּלָם‎ and (Job 17:10, perhaps for sake of assonance with following כֻּלָּם‎) אֻלָּם‎

adverb but, but indeed, a strong adversative Job 2:5; 5:8; 13:3 (where ᵐ5‎ excellently οὐ μὴν ἀλλά). More usually with וְאוּלָם וְ,‎ Gen 28:19 (compare Judg 18:29) Gen 48:19 howbeit his younger brother shall be greater than he, Exod 9:16 but in very deed, Num 14:21 (compare 1Sam 20:3; 25:34) 1Kin 20:23; Micah 3:8; Job 1:11; 11:5; 12:7; 13:4; 14:18; 17:10; 33:1.
