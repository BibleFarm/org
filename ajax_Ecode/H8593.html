H8593. taar

תַּ֫עַר‎ noun masculinePsa 52:4 and (Isa 7:20) feminine razor, sheath; —

1. a. razor, absolute בְּת ׳יְגַלַּח‎ Isa 7:20 (figurative); subject of עַלרֿאֹשׁ יַעֲבֹר לֹא‎ Num 6:5, object of עַלֿ הֶעֱבִיר‎ 8:7 (P), compare construct ׳ת הַגַּלָּבִים‎ Ezek 5:1; absolute ׳כְּת מְלֻטָּשׁ‎ Psa 52:4 (simile of tongue).

b. construct ׳ת הַסֹּפֵר‎ Jer 36:23 = penknife.

2 sheath, always suffix of sword: בְּתַעְרָהּ‎ 2Sam 20:8; of drawing sword, מִתּ ׳שָׁלַף‎ 1Sam 17:51, מִתּ ׳הוֺצִיא‎ Ezek 21:8; 21:10, מִתּ ׳תֵּצֵא‎ 21:9; of sheathing, אֶלתַּֿרְעֵךְ הֵאָֽסְפִי‎ Jer 47:6; אֶלתַּֿעְרָהּ הָשַׁב‎ Ezekiel 21:35.
