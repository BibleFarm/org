H1508. gizrah

גִּזְרָה‎ noun feminine cutting, separation — גִּזְרָה‎ Ezek 41:12 6t.; suffix גִּזְרָתָם‎ Lam 4:7; —

1 cutting, i.e. polishing (AV RV or carving, i.e. beauty of from, shape, compare VB) גִּזְרָתָם סַמִּיר‎ Lam 4:7; their polishing, (or beauty of form) was as sapphire.

2 separation, הַגִּזְרָה‎ Ezek 41:12-13, 14, 15; 42:1, 10, 13, separate place AV RV compare Da; i.e. yard, or space adjoining temple on three sides.
