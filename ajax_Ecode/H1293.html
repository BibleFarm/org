H1293. berakah

I. בְּרָכָה‎ noun feminine blessing (Arabic ; Ethiopic Aramaic בִּרְכָּא‎ ; Late Hebrew as Hebrew) — ׳ב‎ Gen 12:2 39t.; construct בִּרְכַּת‎ 28:4 9t.; suffix בִּרְכָתוֺ‎ 49:28 7t.; plural בְּרָכוֺת‎ Psa 21:7 4t.; construct בִּרְכֹת בִּרְכוֺת,‎ Gen 49:25 5t.; suffix בִּרְכוֺתֵיכֶם‎ Mal 2:2; —

1 blessing:

a. of parent Gen 27:12, 41; 49:28 (JE), of Moses Deut 33:1.

b. of God Exod 32:29 (E) Lev 25:21 (P) Deut 11:26-27, 29; 23:6 (= Neh 13:2) Deut 28:2; 28:8; 30:1; 30:19; Josh 8:34 (D) 2Sam 7:29; Psalm 3:9; Psa 21:4; 133:3; Isa 44:3; Ezek 34:26; 44:30; Joel 2:14; Mal 3:10; יהוה ברכת‎ Gen 39:5 (J) Deut 12:15; 16:17; 33:23; Psa 129:8; Prov 10:22; י מאת ׳ברכה‎ Psa 24:5; אברהם ברכת‎ Gen 28:4 (P), the blessing given to Abraham.

c. of the people, in recognition of good men Psa 109:17; Prov 10:6; 11:26; 24:25; 28:20.

d. of a poor man, in recognition of benefits Job 29:13.

2 source of blessing: Abraham Gen 12:2 (J); Israel Isa 19:24; Ezek 34:26; Zech 8:13; seed of the righteous Psa 37:26; the king 21:7; memory of the righteous Prov 10:7; new wine Isa 65:8.

3 blessing, prosperity: ישׁרים בברכת‎ by the prosperity of the upright (the city is exalted) Prov 11:11; מורה יעטה ברכות גם‎ yea, the early rain covereth with blessings Psa 84:7 compare Gen 49:25-26, ברכותיכם את וארותי‎ and I will curse your prosperity Mal 2:2.

4 blessing, praise of God Neh 9:5.

5 a gift, present Gen 33:11 (E) Josh 15:19 (J) Judg 1:15; 1Sam 25:27; 30:26; 2Kin 5:15; בְּרָכָה נֶפֶשׁ‎ a liberal person Prov 11:25 (compare Syriac , Ethiopic ).

6 treaty of peace 2Kin 18:31 = Isa 36:16.
