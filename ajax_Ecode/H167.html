H167. ahal

[אָהַל‎] verb denominative tent, move tent from place to place (compare Assyrian â'ilu (?) DlW No. 4 & Agl) וַיֶּאֱהַל‎ Gen 13:12, 18 (J), compare אֹהֶל ישֵׁב‎ (see אֹהֶל‎);

Pi`el Imperfect יַהֵל‎ (contracted for יְאַהֵל‎) pitch one's tent like nomad Isa 13:20.
