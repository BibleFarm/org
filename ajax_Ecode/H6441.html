H6441. penimah

לִפְנִים‎ 1Kin 6:29 see below מָּנִי֫מָה‎.

מְּנִ֫ימָה‎ adverb (ה‎ locative)

1 towards the (in-)side.

2 within (literally faceward, from point of view of one entering by opposite door); — of a building, usually temple:

1 after verb of motion Lev 10:18; 2Chr 29:18, and (after הִגִּיד‎) 2Kin 7:11, also ׳לִפ‎ Ezek 41:3; 2Chr 29:16.

2 ׳פ‎ 1Kin 6:18, also ׳לִפ‎ 6:30, so read also 6:29 (for מִלִּפְנִים ᵑ0‎, see KmpKau Kit Benz), Ezek 40:16 (twice in verse), also Psa 45:14 ᵑ0‎, i.e. within the house, but Krochm Gr Che and others (plausibly) מְּנִינִים‎, q. v.; ׳מִמּ‎ 1Kin 6:19, 21.= 2Chr 3:4.
