אַלּוּף‎ (ʼallûwph｜al-loof'｜adjective｜chief｜tame)
[Heb] אַלּוּף‎ ETCBC: אַלּוּף‎ (subs｜chief), אַלּוּף‎ (adjv｜familiar) OSHL: a.ds.ac, a.dt.ac TWOT: 109b GK: H476, H477 Greek: ἄκακος, ἀρχηγός, βοῦς, διδασκαλία, ἡγεμονία, ἡγεμών, ἡγέομαι, οἰκεῖος, φίλος, χιλίαρχος
Derivation: or (shortened) אַלֻּף‎; from אָלַף‎;
Strong's: familiar; a friend, also gentle; hence, a bullock (as being tame; applied, although masculine, to a cow); and so, a chieftain (as notable, like neat cattle)
KJV: captain, duke, (chief) friend, governor, guide, ox.

H441. alluwph

I. אַלּוּף‎ adjective tame — ׳א‎ absolute Micah 7:5 +; construct Prov 2:17 +; suffix אַלּוּפֵי‎ Psa 55:14; plural אַלֻּפִים‎ Jer 13:21; suffix אַלֻּפֵינוּ‎ Psa 144:14, etc.; —

1 tame, docile, אַלּוּף כֶּבֶשׂ‎ a docile (gentle) lamb Jer 11:19.

2. noun masculine friend, intimate Psa 55:14 (|| מְיֻדָּע‎) Micah 7:5 (|| רֵעַ‎) Prov 16:28; 17:9; Jer 13:21; of a woman's husband ׳א נְעוּרֶיהָ‎ Prov 2:17; figurative of ׳י‎ as husband of Judah, id., Jer 3:4.

3 i. q. I. אֶלֶף‎; מְסֻבָּלִים אַלּוּפֵינוּ‎ Psa 144:14 (i.e. cows).

II. אַלּוּף‎ noun masculine1Chr 1:51 chief, chiliarch (denominative from II. אֶלֶף‎ see Di Gen 36:15) — ׳א‎ absolute 36:15 +; plural construct אַלּוּפֵי‎ (אַלֻּפֵי‎) Exod 15:15 +; suffix אַלּוּפֵיהֶם‎ Gen 36:19, 30 — chief (tribal) of Edom 36:15 (5 t.) + 38 t. Genesis 36 (P); Exod 15:15 (E) 1Chr 1:51 (4 t.) + 9 t.; of Judah Zech 12:5-6, so אַלֻּף‎ 9:7 (in simile)
