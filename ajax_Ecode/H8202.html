H8202. Shaphat

שָׁפָט‎ proper name, masculine (he hath judged); — usually Σαφατ[θ]:

1 father of Elilsha 1Kin 19:16, 19; 2Kin 3:11; 6:31.

2 prince of Simeon Num 13:5.

3 grandson of Zerubbabel 1Chr 3:22.

4 Gaddite chief 1Chr 5:12 (Σαβατ, Σαφαμ [ν]).

5 herdsman of David 1Chr 27:29 (Σωφαν, A Σωφατ, ᵐ5‎L Σαφατ).
