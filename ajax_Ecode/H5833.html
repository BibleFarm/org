H5833. ezrah

or ezrath I. עֶזְרָ֫תָה עֶזְרָת, עֶזְרָה,‎

noun feminine help, succour, assistance; — absolute ׳ע‎ Isa 10:3 +, עֶזְרָת‎ Psalm 60:13; Psa 108:13 (Ges§ 80g), עֶזְרָ֫תָה‎ 63:8 2t.; construct עֶזְרַת‎ Isa 31:2 +; suffix עֶזְרָתִי‎ Psa 22:20 +, etc.; —

1 help, succour Isa 10:3; 20:6 (+ לְהִנָּצֵל‎), 31:1; Jer 37:7; Lam 4:17; Job 6:13; 2Chr 28:21; ׳ע אָ֑וֶן מֹּעֲלֵי‎ Isa 31:2 help from, etc.; ׳ע ׳י‎ Judg 5:23 (twice in verse) help of (for) ׳י‎; from ׳י‎ Psa 22:20; Psalm 38:23; 40:14 (|| לְהַצִּילֵנִי‎), 70:2 (|| id.), 71:12 + מִצָּ֑ר‎ Psalm 60:13 (|| תְּשׁוּעָה‎) =108:13.

2 concrete, embodied help, one who helps (compare 1 עֵזֶר‎ 2):

a. collective Job 31:21 when I saw my help (assistance, support) in the gate; ׳בֵּע‎ (see I. עֵזֶר‎ 2) Nahum 3:9.

b. of ׳י‎ Psa 27:9; Psalm 40:18 (+ מְפַלֵּט‎), 46:2 (|| עֹז מַחֲסֶה,‎) Psalm 44:27; 63:8; 94:17; ׳בְּע‎ 35:2.
