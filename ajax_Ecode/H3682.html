כְּסוּת‎ noun feminine covering — ׳כ‎ Gen 20:16 3t.; suffix כְּסוּתְךָ‎ Deut 22:12 3t. suffixes; —

1 covering, clothing Exod 21:10; 22:26 (Covt. code), Deut 22:12; Job 24:7; 31:19; of שַׂק‎ as clothing of heavens Isa 50:3 in figure

2 covering for concealment, of Abaddon, the subterranean abode of the dead Job 26:6; ׳כ עֵינַיִם‎, covering of the eyes Gen 20:16 (so that they cannot see the wrong, figurative of a present offered in compensation for it; E).
