H5417. Nethanel

נְתַנְאֵל‎ proper name, masculine (see GrayProp. N. 181, 192, 205; on proper names of this kind compare RSSem i. 100 n., 2nd ed. 108, GrayProp. N. 264); — ᵐ5‎ Ναθαναηλ; —

1 a chief of Issachar Num 1:8; 2:5; 7:18, 23; 10:15.

2 4th son of Jesse 1Chr 2:14.

3 name of priests:

a. 1Chr 15:24.

b. Neh 12:21.

4 priest's son (who had strange wife) Ezra 10:22, perhaps = Neh 12:36.

5 Levite name:

a. 1Chr 24:6.

b. 1Chr 26:4.

c.2Chr 35:9.

6 a prince of Jehoshaphat2Chr 17:7.
