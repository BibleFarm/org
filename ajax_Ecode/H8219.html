H8219. shephelah

שְׁפֵלָה‎ noun feminine lowland; — always absolute ׳שׁ‎ with article, except suffix שְׁפֵלָתֹה‎ Josh 11:16; — lowland:

1 usually technical term of strip west of Judaean mountains (on exact limits see GASmGeogr. 201 ff.; Expos. Dec. 1896, 404 f. BuhlGeogr. 104 DrHast. DB iii. 892 f.): almost always distinguished from הָהָר‎ and הַנֶּגֶב‎, sometimes from הָעֲרָבָה‎, in Deut 1:7; Josh 9:1 (RD), also from הַיָּם חוֺף‎, compare 10:40; 12:8 (both RD), Judg 1:9 (perhaps RD, compare Bu GFM), Josh 15:33 (P), Jer 17:26; 32:44; 33:13; Obadiah 19; Zech 7:7; 2Chr 26:10 (distinguished from הַמִּישׁוֺר‎, table-land east of Jordan); region of sycomores 1Kin 10:27 2Chr 1:15; 9:27; 1Chr 27:28.

2 of lowland (near coast) north of Carmel Josh 11:2; west of mountains of Ephraim 11:16 (both RD).
