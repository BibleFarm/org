[תָּמַהּ‎] verb be astounded, dumbfounded (Late Hebrew id.; so Aramaic תְּמַהּ‎, ); —

Qal Perfect3plural תָּמָ֑הוּ‎ Psa 48:6; Imperfect2masculine singular תִּתְמֶהּ‎ Eccl 5:7; 3masculine plural יִתְמְהוּ‎ Job 26:11, etc.; Imperative masculine plural תְּמָ֑הוּ‎ Isa 29:9; Hab 1:5; — be astounded Isa 29:9; Hab 1:5 (see Hithpa`el), Jer 4:9 (|| שׁמם‎, Niph`al), Psa 48:6; + מִן‎ causative Job 26:11; + על‎ of thing at which Exod 5:7; look in astonishment at ׳ת אֶלרֵֿעֵהוּ אִישׁ‎ Gen 43:33 (J), Isa 13:8.
