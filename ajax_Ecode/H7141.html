H7141. Qorach

קֹ֫רַח‎37 proper name, masculine Κορε (baldness ?); —

1 Edomite name:

a. Gen 36:5, 14, 18; 1Chr 1:35.

b. Gen 36:16 (not ⅏‎; probably gloss from 36:18 Di Kau Ball and others; Holz hesitates).

2 Levite, rebel ag. Moses, Num 16:1 10t. 16, 17, 26:9-10, 27:3; in Geneal. Lists Exod 6:21, 24; 1Chr 6:7; 6:22; ׳בֶּןקֿ‎ 9:19; ׳בְּנֵיקֿ‎ Num 26:11; especially ׳לִבְנֵיקֿ‎ as company of Psalm-collectors (or singers), in titles: Psa 42:1; 44:1; 45:1; 46:1; 47:1; 48:1; 49:1; 84:1; 85:1; 87:1; 88:1.

3 a 'son' of Hebron, i.e. Judahite clan 1Chr 2:43 Κορεε, etc.
