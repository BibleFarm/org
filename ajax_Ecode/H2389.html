H2389. chazaq

חָזָק‎ adjective strong, stout, mighty; — ׳ח‎ Exod 10:19 18t.; feminine חֲזָקָה‎ 3:19 31t. + Ezek 3:14 (Co for ᵑ0‎ verb 3feminine singular); plural חֲזָקִים‎ Judg 18:25 2t.; construct חִזְקֵי‎ Ezek 2:4 (strike out Co. see below) 3:7; —

1 strong:

a. of men, rarely predicate Num 13:18 (opposed to רָפֶה‎), 13:31 (comparative with מִן‎), Josh 14:11; 17:18 (all J E), Judg 18:26 (comparative with מִן‎); also of גֹּאֲלָם‎ Jer 50:34; Prov 23:11; of hand of ׳י‎ Josh 4:24 (D), so Ezek 3:14 ᵐ5‎ Co (see above and חָזַק‎ Qal 3); compare of Tyre בַּיָּם חֲזָקָה הָֽיְתָה‎ 26:17 (strike out B Co).

b. usually attributive; of ram of Pharaoh Ezek 30:22 (opposed to נִשְׁבָּ֑רֶת‎); especially of hand of ׳י‎ in delivering Israel from Egypt, Exod 3:19 (gloss ? see Di), 6:1 (twice in verse); 13:9 (all J), Deut 6:21; 7:8; 9:26; Dan 9:15; in wonders done by agency of Moses Deut 34:12; also חֲזָקָה וּבְיָד גָּדוֺל בְּכֹחַ‎ Exod 31:11(JE), compare Neh 1:10, but oftener נְטוּיָה וּבִזְרוֺעַ חזקה ביד‎ Deut 4:34; 5:15; 7:19; 11:2; 26:6; Jer 32:21; Psa 136:12; of control of enemies and deliverance from exile Ezek 20:33-34, more Generally Deut 3:24 (|| גָּדְלְךָ‎), 1Kin 8:42 2Chr 6:32; of ׳י‎s opposition to Zedekiah Jer 21:5; once of Edom, opposed to Israel חזקה וביד כבד בעם‎ Num 20:20 (JE).

c. of sword of ׳י‎ Isa 27:1 (|| וְהַגְּדוֺלָה הַקָּשָׁה‎), of wind Exod 10:19 (J), 1Kin 19:11; sound of trumpet (= loud) Exod 19:16 (E).

d severe, sharp, hot, of war 1Sam 14:52 (predicate), battle 2Sam 11:15, sickness 1Kin 17:17 (predicate), famine 18:2.

e. firm, hard, of face, פנים‎ Ezek 3:8, forehead, מֵצַח‎ 3:8; adamant, simile of forehead 3:9 (comparative, with מִן‎); of sky Job 37:18 (predicate) in plural construct ׳ח לֵב‎ Ezek 2:4 hard of heart (|| פָנִים קְשֵׁי‎) but omitted by B Co, מֵֿצַח׳ח‎ 3:7 hard (= impudent) of forehead (|| קְשֵׁילֵֿב‎).

2 as substantive, a strong one, of ׳י‎ (בְּחָזָק‎, as, in the character of, a strong one) Isa 40:10; of ׳י‎s agent 28:2 (|| אַמִּץ‎); see also כֹחוֺ יְאַמֵּץ לאֹ וְחָזָק‎ Amos 2:14; with מִן‎ compare= one too strong for Jer 31:11; Psa 35:10. In Ezek 34:16 ᵑ0‎ uses ׳הח‎ in bad sense (|| הִשְּׁמֵנָה‎), ᵐ5‎ Co, with different text, in good sense (see VB).
