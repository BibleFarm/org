1

a. inward part of human body, physical sense, ׳בְּק‎ within one's body Gen 25:22 (J), compare 18:12 (J), 1Sam 25:37; Jer 23:9; Job 20:14 (|| בְּמֵעָיו‎), compare Isa 19:1 (of nation personified) into his body Psa 109:18 (simile); as seat of life עַלקִֿרְבּוֺ נֶפֶשׁהַֿיּלֶד (וַ)תָּשָׁב‎ 1Kin 17:21-22, compare (of idol) Hab 2:19 (see also 2).

d of house Psa 101:2; 101:7; temple 48:10; ׳בְּק דֶ֑ךָ מוֺעֲ‎ 74:4.

e. of land (earth) Gen 45:6; 48:16; Amos 2:3; Isa 5:8; 24:13 (|| הָעַמִּים בְּתוֺח‎), + 10 t. (׳מִקּ ׳בְּק‎).

(3) ׳בְּק אֶחָיו‎ Deut 18:2; 1Sam 16:13, and (׳מִק‎) Deut 17:15; 18:18. (4) ׳מִקּ עַמֶּיהָ‎ Exod 31:14 (II.עַם‎) (5) ׳בְּק אֹיְכֶיךָ‎ Psa 110:2 compare (of wicked) 55:16 ׳בְּק חֲכָמִים‎ Prov 15:31. (6) ׳בְּק יִשְׁמֹּט אֱלֹהִים‎ Psa 82:1 -7׳בְּק הַמִּלְחָמָה‎ 1Kin 20:39 into the midst of the battle, the thick of the fight; ׳בְּק צָרָה‎ Palam 1Ki 138:7. (8) of nations, ׳בְּק הַגּוֺיִם‎ Deut 29:15, הָעַמִּים‎ Lam 3:45, compare Josh 24:17; Micah 5:6; 5:7.

g. ׳מִקּ חוקך‎ Psa 74:11 (Qr חֵיקְךָ‎) thy hand from within thy bosom, (anthropomorphism of ׳י‎; see I כלה‎ Pi`el 2 c).

h. of a period of time,׳בְּק שָׁנִים‎ Hab 3:2 (twice in verse)

2 of inward part of man;

a. as seat of thought and emotion : ׳בְּק‎ 1Kin 3:28; Jer 4:14; 9:7; Prov 26:24; Psa 62:5; 94:19; || בְלֵב‎ Prov 14:33, עַללֵֿב‎ Jer 31:33; seat of לֵב‎ Psa 39:4; 55:5; 109:22; Lam 1:20; of רוּחַ‎ Ezek 11:19; 36:26-27, Zech 12:1; Isa 26:6; Psa 51:12, compare Isa 19:3, 14; ׳בְּק לִבִּי‎ Psa 36:2 (read לִבּוֺ‎ Vrss Hup Now Che Bae and others)
