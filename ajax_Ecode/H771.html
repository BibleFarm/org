H771. Ornan

אָרְנָן‎ proper name, masculine a Jebusite, whose threshing-floor was bought by David to erect an altar 1Chr 21:15, 18, 20 (twice in verse); 21:21 (twice in verse); 21:22-23, 24, 25, 28, & according to 2Chr 3:1 became site of temple; called אֲרַוְנָה‎ 2Sam 24:16f. q. v.
