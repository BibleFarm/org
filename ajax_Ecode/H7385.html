רִיק‎ noun [masculine] emptiness, vanity; — chiefly in adverb phrase לְרִיק‎ Isa 46:4; Job 39:16 in vain (of labour without benefit), so ׳לָר‎ Lev 26:16, 20 (H), Isa 65:23; and as adverb accusative רִיק‎ 30:7; Psa 73:13, probably also 2:1 (most = empty scheme as רֵיק‎ 2) compare 4:3 (read probably רֵיק‎); דִיק בְּדֵי‎ Jer 51:58 = Hab 2:13. — Jer 51:34 see רֵיק‎ 1 above
