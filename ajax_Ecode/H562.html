אֹמֶר‎ noun masculinePsa 77:9 utterance, speech, word, only in poetry, & Josh 24:27 exalted style; — absolute ׳א‎ Job 22:28 5t.; suffix אִמְרוֺ‎ 20:29; Plural absolute אֲמָרִים‎ Prov 19:7; 22:21; construct אִמְרֵי‎ Num 24:4 +; אֲמָרָיו‎ Job 22:22; אִמְרֵיכֶם‎ 32:14; Isa 41:26 (on derivation from אֹמֶר‎ compare Di Job 15:33) etc.; —

1 utterance, word, especially plural Job 6:26 (|| מִלִּים‎) 32:12, 14 (|| מִלִּין‎) 33:3; 34:37; Psa 5:2 (|| הָגִיג‎), 141:6 (on this see compare Che); Prov 2:1 (|| מִצְוָה‎), 2:16; 4:10, 20 (|| דָּבָר‎), 7:1 (|| מִצְוָה‎), 7:5; 19:7; Isa 41:26; words of God Num 24:4, 16 compare Josh 24:27; Job 6:10; 22:22; Psa 107:11; figurative of day 19:3 (singular); compare 19:4 (|| דְּבָרִים‎) but see Che on see; of wisdom (personified) Prov 1:21 (|| קוֺל‎); often in phrase אִמְרֵיפִֿי‎ Deut 32:1; Psalm 19:15 (|| הֶגְיוֺן‎); Psa 54:4 (|| תְּפִלָּה‎); 78:1 (|| תּוֺרָה‎); Prov 4:5; 5:7; 7:24; 8:8; compare Job 8:2; Prov 6:2 (twice in verse); of ׳י‎Job 23:12 (|| שְׂפתָיו מִצְוַת‎); Psa 138:4; Hosea 6:5 (of Yahweh's words as weapon); in phrases אִמְרֵיישֶׁר‎ Job 6:25; ׳א בִינָה‎ Prov 1:2; ׳א נֹעַם‎ 15:26; 16:24; ׳א דָ֫עַת‎ 19:27; 23:12; ׳א אֱמֶת‎ 22:21, compare אֱמֶת אֲמָרִים הֵשִׁיב‎ 22:21 return answer; לָהּ אֲמָרֶיהָ תָּשִׁיב‎ Judg 5:29 she returneth (i.e. repeateth) her words to herself; א ׳חָשַׁךְ‎ spare (i.e. refrain from) words Prov 17:27.

2 singular promise Psa 77:9; appointment, decree Job 20:29; command Psa 68:12 (but see Che); plan, purpose Job 22:28 א ׳גָּזַרֿ‎; Hab 3:9 Thes sworn are the rods of appointment (i.e. the chastisements decreed), but passage dubious, see Commentaries; — Gen 49:21 read אֲמִרֵי‎ compare אָמִיד‎; see Di.
