H8444. totsaah

[תּוֺצָאָה‎] noun feminine outgoing, extremity, source (?),escape; only plural; chiefly P and late; — absolute תוֺצָאוֺת‎ Psa 68:21; construct תוֺצְאוֺת‎ Prov 4:23 + Josh 18:19 Qr (Kt תוצאותיו‎); תּוֺצְאֹת‎ Num 34:8; Ezek 48:30; תֹּצְאוֺת‎ Josh 15:4 2t.; suffix תּוֺצְאֹתָיו‎ Num 34:4 3t.; תֹּצְאֹתָיו‎ Josh 15:7-8t. + 16:3 Qr (Kt תצאתו‎); תּוֺצְאוֺתָם‎ 1Chr 5:16; —

1 outgoing, extremity of border of territory Josh 16:3; 17:18 (both J E), elsewhere P: Num 34:4-5, 8, 9, 12; Josh 15:4, 7, 11; 16:8; 17:9; 18:12, 14, 19; 19:14, 22, 20, 33; outskirts of city Ezek 48:20, compare 1Chr 5:16.

2 ׳ת חַיִּים‎ Prov 4:23 (? read מוֺצְאוֺת‎) sources of life.

3 ת ׳לַמָּוֶת‎ Psa 68:21 escapes from (literally for, i.e. in view of) death.
